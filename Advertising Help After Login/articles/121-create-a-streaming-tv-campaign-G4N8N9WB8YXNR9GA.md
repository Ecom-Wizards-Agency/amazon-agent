# Create a Streaming TV Campaign

This article explains how to create and launch a Streaming TV campaign.

## Key Points

- Streaming TV uses Amazon shopping and streaming data to reach new customers.
- It can complement Sponsored Brands, Display, and Sponsored Products campaigns.
- Advertisers must meet eligibility requirements.

## Workflow

1. In the navigation side menu, click **Create campaign**.
2. Select **Streaming TV**.
3. Name the campaign.
4. Set start and end dates or choose no end date.
5. Enter a daily budget.
6. Name the ad.
7. Choose a landing page: Brand Store or product detail page.
8. Upload a video or generate one with AI.
9. Name the ad group.
10. Enter the maximum average CPM.
11. Choose dynamic or manual audiences.
12. Set location targeting.
13. Submit for review.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before submitting for review.
- Confirm eligibility, budget, landing page, audience, CPM, and location targeting.
- Moderation may take 24 hours to 3 business days.
- Metrics can take up to 12 hours to populate.
