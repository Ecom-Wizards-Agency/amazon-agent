# How to Pay by Invoice

This article explains invoice payment by wire transfer or check, including documentation and processing expectations.

## Key Points

- Invoice payments are generally due within 30 days.
- Wire transfers can be applied faster than check payments.
- Invoice numbers must be included in payment notes.
- Payment details should be checked on the official Amazon Ads page before sending money.

## Workflow

1. Review the invoice and payment due date.
2. For wire transfer, include all invoice numbers in the bank payment notes.
3. After making a wire payment, email Amazon Ads receivables with invoice/payment details.
4. For check payment, include invoice numbers on the check or a separate sheet when paying multiple invoices.
5. Monitor invoice status after payment processing.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before initiating any payment.
- Confirm the official remittance details on Amazon Ads; do not rely on copied bank details.
- Confirm invoice numbers, amount, currency, and entity.
- Confirm finance approval before wire/check payment.
