# Plan your budget with Ads Planner

Learn how to generate and view campaign forecasts in Ads planner.

> Requirement: you must be signed in to a Manager account to use Ads planner.

## Plan your budget

Ads planner can help plan campaign budget for an audience opportunity. After entering a budget amount, Ads planner calculates key metrics for each ad, including reach percentage, reach, impressions, clicks, and detail page views.

![Ads planner budget planning](https://d369o5h5zn8mv7.cloudfront.net/images/2024/CrossChannelPlanner/plan_budgets.gif)

Start by opening an existing opportunity in Ads planner.

1. At the top-left corner of the audience opportunity, click **Select ad products and formats**.
2. Review suggested ad products and formats aligned with the marketing goal.
3. Select ad products and formats to include.
4. Click **Apply** in the top-right corner.
5. After choosing ad products, enter a budget for each selected ad product row.
6. Click **Apply** to generate key metric forecasts.
7. Use the forecast to decide what budget is most likely to achieve the desired results.
8. Click **See details** to view reach curves and edit additional metrics.

## Explore campaign forecasts

For each ad product or format added to the plan, click **See details** to expose the reach curve and forecast settings. A summary row shows combined totals for all selected ad products.

To generate a forecast from new inputs:

1. Set the budget by entering it directly or clicking a value on the curve.
2. Set preferred CPC/CPM. By default, it is set to an average value based on account performance history in the specific format.
3. Set preferred CTR, DPVR, and Frequency for DSP formats.
4. Click **Apply** to reforecast.
5. Click **Save changes** to apply budget and settings to the plan output table.
