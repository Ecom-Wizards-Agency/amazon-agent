# Create Online Grocery Campaigns on Amazon DSP

This article explains how to create Amazon DSP online grocery campaigns for Amazon Fresh and Whole Foods Market.

## Key Points

- Online grocery campaigns can target Prime members for same-day grocery delivery and pickup.
- Campaign setup includes ad formats such as online video, Streaming TV, and display/REC.
- ASIN availability and logo guidelines matter for final approval.

## Workflow

1. Click **Campaigns** in the navigation menu.
2. Select the **Video, Audio & Display ads** tab.
3. Create the relevant ad.
4. Choose the correct ad setup for the campaign type and format.

## Format Routing

| Ad group | Ad format | Setup path |
| --- | --- | --- |
| OLV | Online video | Video tab, then continue next to Standard Video. |
| Streaming TV | Streaming TV | Use the Streaming TV setup path. |
| Display | REC for Amazon Fresh and Whole Foods Market | Component-based tab, then continue next to Component-based ad. |

## Checkpoints

- Stop before launching or submitting the campaign.
- Confirm all ASINs are in stock for REC ads.
- Verify logo and creative guidelines before finalizing ads.
