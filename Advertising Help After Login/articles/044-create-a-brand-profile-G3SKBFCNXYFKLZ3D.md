# Create a brand profile

Brand profiles are available to vendors and professional sellers enrolled in Amazon Brand Registry and meeting eligibility requirements.

## Create a brand profile

1. Click the Brand home icon in the advertising console.

   ![Brand home icon](https://d369o5h5zn8mv7.cloudfront.net/images/Icons/Brand_Content.png)

2. Click **Brand profiles**.
3. Enter **Brand name**. Amazon recommends using the exact Brand Registry name because the field is case-sensitive.
4. Upload **Brand logo** or choose from Ad assets if the logo was previously uploaded. The logo appears in a circle crop on Amazon.
5. Select **Brand color** or add the hex color.
6. Click **Submit for review**.

Moderation review usually completes within 24 hours but may take up to 3 business days. Amazon emails or sends an in-app notification when the profile is approved or needs changes.
