# Manage Your Brand and Products

This article explains how vendors and advertisers manage brands and products within an Amazon Ads account.

## Key Points

- Vendors and Amazon DSP advertisers can manage brands and products.
- Sellers, authors, and book vendors may be limited to registering one brand.
- Changes to brands can affect current and future advertising campaigns.
- Brand Registry and the right user permissions may be required.

## Workflow

1. Click **Administration**.
2. Select **Account access and settings**.
3. Click **Brands**.
4. To add or remove a brand, search by brand name, select it, and choose the relevant action.
5. To add products, click the brand name, click **Add products**, search for products or ASINs, and submit for approval.
6. To remove products, click the brand name, select the products, and request removal.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before adding or removing brands/products.
- Confirm Brand Registry enrollment and permissions first.
- Consider impact on active and future campaigns before changing brand/product associations.
