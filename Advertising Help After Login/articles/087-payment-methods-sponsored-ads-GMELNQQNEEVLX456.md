# Payment Methods for Sponsored Ads

This article explains payment options for sponsored ads.

## Key Points

- Available payment methods depend on account type and setup.
- The default payment method is visible in the payment profile.
- Upgraded Advertiser Accounts may need to pay by invoice for all campaigns.
- Payment failure can pause campaigns after repeated attempts.
- Adding a backup card can help prevent campaign suspension.

## Payment Options

| Option | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Debit or credit card | Card is charged according to billing cycle and credit limit behavior. |
| Seller account funds | Charges are deducted from Funds Available, not Total Balance. |
| Invoice | Monthly invoice payment for eligible accounts. |

## Checkpoints

- Stop before adding, removing, or changing payment methods.
- Confirm the correct account and billing profile.
- Do not store or write private payment identifiers in local notes.
- If a payment failure is fixed, account restoration can take up to 24 hours.
