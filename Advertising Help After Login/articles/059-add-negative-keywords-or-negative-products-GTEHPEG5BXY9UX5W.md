# Add negative keywords or negative products

Negative keywords and negative products can be added during campaign creation or later in Campaign manager.

## Negative keywords

Negative keywords can use phrase and exact match types:

- **Negative phrase:** ads do not show on shopping queries containing the complete phrase or close variations. Maximum 4 words and 80 characters.
- **Negative exact:** ads do not show on shopping queries containing the exact phrase or close variation. Maximum 10 words and 80 characters.

The match type of an existing negative keyword cannot be changed.

> Note: Amazon sellers and vendors can upload negative keywords to a campaign in bulk through **Bulk Operations**.

## Add negative keywords to an existing campaign

1. Go to Campaign manager and find the campaign and ad group.
2. Select the campaign and ad group.
3. Click **Negative targeting**.
4. Click **Add Negative keywords**.
5. Enter negative keywords and set the match type.
6. Click **Save**.

## Negative products

Negative product targets prevent product-targeted or automatically targeted ads from displaying when the ASIN of a product page matches the negative product target.

- Ad group-level negative product targets affect product attribute targets or automatic targets in the same ad group.
- Campaign-level negative product targets affect product attribute targets or automatic targets in any ad group within the campaign.

Buffer periods:

- Negative keywords: 72 hours.
- Negative products/ASINs: 96 hours.

## Add negative products to an existing campaign

1. Go to Campaign manager and find the campaign or ad group.
2. Select the campaign and ad group.
3. Click **Negative targeting**.
4. Click **Add negative product targets**.
5. Exclude products by brand or product using search or a list.
6. Click **Add negative product targets**.
