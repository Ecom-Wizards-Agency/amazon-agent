# Understand Campaign Spend

This article explains advertising spend, credit limits, billing frequency, and invoice differences.

## Key Points

- Sponsored ads use a cost-per-click model: charges happen when customers click ads.
- Cost is total spend on clicks, subject to traffic validation adjustments.
- Total amount billed is what is owed at the end of a billing cycle.
- Accumulating charges show real-time outstanding amounts.
- Campaign manager costs and invoices can differ because of invalid traffic adjustments and billing latency.

## Workflow

1. Click **Administration**.
2. Open **Billing**.
3. Review current amounts, invoices, and accumulating charges.
4. Compare budget, campaign costs, and invoice totals.

## Definitions

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Cost | Total amount spent on clicks, subject to adjustments. |
| Total amount billed | Total owed at the end of the billing cycle, including unpaid charges. |
| Accumulating charges | Current amount owed, updating with transactions. |
