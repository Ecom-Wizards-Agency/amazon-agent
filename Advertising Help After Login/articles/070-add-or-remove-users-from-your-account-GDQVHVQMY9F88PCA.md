# Add or Remove Users From Your Account

This article explains how admins invite, update, or remove users from Amazon DSP, sponsored ads, and Manager accounts.

## Key Points

- Only admins can invite, update, or remove users.
- Access levels include Admin, Editor, Viewer, and Custom.
- The workflow differs slightly between seller accounts and Manager accounts.
- A user may not be able to leave an account if they are the only admin.

## Workflow

1. Open the left menu.
2. Click **Administration**.
3. Select **Account access and settings**.
4. Open the **Users** tab.
5. To invite someone, click **Invite users** and choose the right access.
6. To update or remove someone, find the user and adjust their role or access.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before inviting, removing, or changing a user role.
- Confirm the target email address and account context.
- Avoid leaving an account without at least one trusted admin.
