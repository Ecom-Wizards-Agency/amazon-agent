# Create and manage ads in Creative Studio

Learn how to use Creative Studio to create image and video ads using AI.

Creative Studio provides multiple ways to create AI-powered ad assets. All ads created using Creative Studio require moderation and approval before use in campaigns.

## Access Creative Studio

1. In the left navigation, click **Creative Tools**.
2. Click **Creative Studio**.
3. Choose the creative method and follow the workflow.

## Collaborate with Creative Agent to create ads

> Note: Creative Agent is only available to advertisers in the US.

Use Creative Agent to plan and strategize campaigns, create videos and images, and write scripts.

1. Type a message in the chat box or click a suggestion button to start talking to Creative Agent.
2. Follow the conversation to create campaign ideas and materials such as video ads, concepts, storyboards, video, and audio.
3. To get better results, click the **+** button to upload your own materials.
4. Only use materials you have permission to use.
5. Follow the upload window requirements.
6. Click **Done** to save uploads.

## Create images

1. Click **Generate images** under **Other ways to create**.
2. Choose how to make the image:
   - Click **Add a product** to pick one of your products.
   - Click **Describe an image** to tell AI what you want.
   - Click **Upload an image** at the top of the window to use files from your computer.
3. Click an image thumbnail to preview, edit, or delete it.
4. Save the image by clicking **Save**.

## Create videos

1. Click **Generate videos** under **Other ways to create**.
2. Search for products by name or ASIN.
3. Click **Add** next to the product to use.
4. Click **Generate video**.
5. To improve videos, click **Assets > Update assets** so AI can use them as examples.
6. Click **+ Logo** at the bottom navigation bar to add a logo.
7. Click a video thumbnail to watch the video.
8. Optionally click **Generate more** to make more video options.
9. Optionally hover over a thumbnail and click **Edit** to edit logo and text. Changes save automatically.
10. Save videos by hovering over a thumbnail and clicking **Save**.

## Create a Sponsored Brands campaign from a generated video

1. In the top-left corner of the video thumbnail, click **Select**.
2. Click **Create Sponsored Brands campaign**.
3. Campaign settings prepopulate with the relevant products and generated video.

## Manage saved AI-generated assets

After creating images and videos in Creative Studio, find and manage them in the Creative Assets library. They are also available when creating campaigns in Campaign manager.
