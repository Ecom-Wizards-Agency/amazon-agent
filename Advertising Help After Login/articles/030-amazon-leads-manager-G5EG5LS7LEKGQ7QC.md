# Amazon Leads Manager

Amazon Leads Manager (ALM) is a self-service database for accessing lead information from lead generation campaigns.

Lead generation ads help advertisers that sell products and services outside the Amazon store capture customer leads directly through ads. After a customer submits lead information, the information is sent to ALM for self-service access.

Links to the ALM portal appear in multiple locations inside the ads console. An account can be registered using the email associated with the Amazon Ads account.

![Amazon Leads Manager dashboard](https://d369o5h5zn8mv7.cloudfront.net/HMH/Images/Lead+Generation+07.2023/ALM+Main.png)

## Manage leads

After signing in, advertisers can:

- Download CSV files.
- Integrate a CRM with ALM through the **CRM Profiles** tab.
- Use Salesforce or a custom CRM with a webhook URL.
- Grant agency partners access if the privacy policy allows it.
- Register an agency partner email in the **Users** tab.
- Grant lead information access to one or more accounts.
- Revoke access at any time.

To protect customer information, leads are auto-deleted after 14 days and cannot be accessed by Amazon.

## Access

Go to [Amazon Leads Manager](http://leadsmanager.amazon.com/) and sign in with Amazon Ads account credentials.

Use of ALM is subject to the [Amazon Lead Generation Ads and Leads Manager Agreement](https://advertising.amazon.com/leadgenerationterms).
