# Create an Amazon Brand Lift Study

This article explains how to create an Amazon Brand Lift study.

## Key Points

- Avoid measuring campaigns that target only branded keywords.
- Select a benchmark category for comparison.
- Choose a study goal such as Awareness or Consideration.
- Campaign eligibility and budget requirements must be checked before submission.

## Workflow

1. Go to **Measurement & reporting**.
2. Select **Brand lift studies**.
3. Click **Create study**.
4. Configure study name, brand, benchmark category, and goal.
5. Verify campaign eligibility.
6. Submit the study.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before submitting the study.
- Review the Eligibility card for campaign requirements.
- Confirm benchmark category, goal, and campaign selection.
- Confirm budget requirements are met.
