# Supported Currencies

This article lists supported currencies by invoice country for Amazon Ads.

## Key Points

- Supported currency depends on invoice country.
- Currency availability can vary by region and account setup.
- Currency matters for billing, invoices, and payment profile setup.

## Checkpoints

- Confirm the invoice country before choosing or troubleshooting currency.
- Stop before changing billing profile details that affect invoice country or currency.
- Use current Amazon Ads billing settings as the source of truth for active accounts.

## Routing Use

Use this page when the user asks what currency an Amazon Ads account can use, why a currency is unavailable, or how invoice country affects billing currency.
