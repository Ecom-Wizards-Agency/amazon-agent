# Get started with Partner Network

Amazon Ads Partner Network is a self-service hub for agencies and tech partners to manage business relationships with Amazon Ads.

## Getting started

Partner Network is designed for companies that serve advertisers on Amazon. Companies can register to access marketing resources, learning resources, and upcoming events.

After business registration is approved, Amazon recommends completing account setup by adding users and advertisers supported by the company.

Through Partner Network, companies can submit a directory listing that appears in the partner directory.

The registering representative must be authorized to accept [Amazon Ads Terms and Conditions](https://advertising.amazon.com/partner-network/terms) and create/operate the organization's Partner Network account.

## Partner Network assets

If a company is registered on Partner Network, account admins can download the Amazon Ads logo from Partner Network and view marketing guidelines for using those assets.
