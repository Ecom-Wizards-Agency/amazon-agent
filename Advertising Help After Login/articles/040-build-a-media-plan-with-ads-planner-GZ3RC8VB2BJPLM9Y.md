# Build a media plan with Ads planner

Learn how to build media plans in Ads planner to get campaign forecasts and budget insights.

> Requirement: you must be signed in to a Manager account to use Ads planner.

## 1. Create your plan

Navigation:

1. In the left menu, click **Insights and planning**.
2. Click **Ads planner**.
3. Select a Manager account and choose a country if prompted.
4. Click **Create plan**.

Plan details:

1. Enter account information or choose the account to create a plan for.
2. Optionally choose a brand category to get suggested categories.
3. Select an industry that best matches the advertised products or services.
4. Click **Done**.
5. Choose a media plan objective:
   - Achieve multiple marketing goals.
   - Build brand awareness.
   - Optimize streaming TV campaigns.
6. Enter a name, time period, and budget.
7. Choose whether to advertise products sold on Amazon or products/services not sold on Amazon.
8. For products sold on Amazon, click **Add** to select up to 10 categories for shopper traffic data.
9. Click **Next**.

## 2. Choose your goals

1. Select one or more audience types:
   - Awareness: learn who is not aware of the brand or products.
   - Consideration: learn who is not engaging with the brand.
   - Conversion: learn who is viewing and purchasing.
   - Loyalty: engage customers who have already purchased products. Only available for products sold on Amazon.
2. For each goal, review the default KPI.
3. To change a KPI, click **Change**, choose a new KPI, and click **Apply**.
4. Click **Next**.

## 3. Refine audience opportunity

1. For **Audience**, click **Change** to add or exclude audiences.
2. For **Location**, click **Change** to add or exclude locations.
3. For **Consideration opportunity**, click **Change** to select retail categories or audiences.
4. For Amazon DSP, browse, select, and create combinations of audience segments. Third-party audiences are not supported.
5. For Sponsored ads, select categories where the brand lists products. Related or adjacent categories can also be selected.
6. Click **Create plan**.

## 4. View insights

After saving, Ads planner generates audience size, demographic, and behavioral insights. Then it can help plan budget and explore forecasts.
