# Pay for Ads in Your Card Currency

This article explains how to pay Amazon Ads charges in the card currency to simplify currency conversion.

## Key Points

- Paying in card currency can reduce conversion complexity.
- Amazon may charge a service fee for card-currency payment.
- The advertiser should still check possible foreign transaction fees with the bank.

## Workflow

1. Go to **Payment Settings**.
2. Select the card’s active country.
3. Click **Change Payment Profile**.
4. Choose and edit the preferred card.
5. Select the payment currency.

## Eligible Currency Examples

AED, BGN, CAD, CHF, CNY, CZK, DKK, EUR, GBP, HKD, HUF, ILS, INR, JPY, KRW, NZD, PLN, RON, SEK, TRY, TWD, USD, and VND were listed as eligible card currencies.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before changing payment currency.
- Verify the card currency is eligible.
- Ask the bank about foreign transaction fees before deciding.
- Confirm the fee impact before saving.

## Routing Use

Use this page when the user asks about paying ads in local/card currency, avoiding conversion confusion, or card currency eligibility.
