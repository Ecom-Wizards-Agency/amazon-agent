# Understand brands on Amazon Ads

On Amazon, a brand is a name that represents a product or set of products. A brand usually has a name, logo, or other signals that appear on product lists and/or packaging.

Brand assets can be managed with a brand profile, and brands can be promoted with Sponsored Brands campaigns and Brand Stores. Amazon Brand Registry enrollment is required to use these features.

## Brand Registry requirements

To register, the advertiser must be the brand owner and provide:

1. Brand name. The brand name must have an active registered trademark.
2. Associated government-registered trademark number.
3. Product categories where the brand should be listed.

Brand profiles must also meet [Brand profile content guidelines and acceptance policies](https://advertising.amazon.com/help/GDHA8LUYDVTCJJSC) to pass moderation review.
