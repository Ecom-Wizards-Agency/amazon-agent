# Live Shopping Best Practices

This article explains best practices for Amazon Live reach, engagement, stream management, followers, and performance measurement.

## Key Points

- Amazon Live is an interactive shopping ad format.
- Advertising campaigns can drive traffic to the livestream.
- Encouraging customers to follow during the stream can improve engagement.
- Campaigns should stay active after the livestream to continue traffic momentum.

## Workflow

1. Create advertising campaigns to drive traffic to the livestream.
2. Manage the product carousel during the stream.
3. Encourage customers to follow the brand.
4. Keep campaigns active for a post-stream period.
5. Review performance after the event.

## Video Quality Reference

| Requirement | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Resolution | 1280x720p |
| Framerate | 29.97 |
| Bitrate | Max 4 Mbps |

## Checkpoints

- Check internet speed before going live.
- Confirm video quality settings.
- Stop before launching paid campaigns or changing livestream promotion settings.
