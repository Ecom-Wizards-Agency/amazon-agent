# Update Billing Details

This article explains how to update the company address shown on Amazon Ads invoices.

## Key Points

- Billing address should be updated before the end of the month for accurate invoicing.
- VAT/GST handling depends on account information at invoicing time.

## Workflow

1. In the left menu, click **Administration**.
2. Under **Billing and Payment**, select **Billing**.
3. Select **Payment Settings**.
4. Click **Change billing profile**.
5. Click **Edit**.
6. Update the company name and physical billing address.
7. Review tax information if applicable.
8. Save the billing profile.

## Visual Reference

![Administration icon](https://d369o5h5zn8mv7.cloudfront.net/images/Icons/Administration.png)

## Checkpoints

- Stop before saving billing address or tax changes.
- Confirm company legal name, address, VAT/GST details, and invoice country.
- Make changes before month-end if the change should affect that month’s invoice.

## Routing Use

Use this page when the user asks to update invoice address, company billing information, VAT/GST details, or billing profile details.
