# Sponsored ads translation

Amazon supports multiple languages for customers shopping on Amazon.

> Note: This feature is currently available only in AE, CA, DE, IN, JP, SA, and US marketplaces.

Automatic translation helps reach customers in their preferred language across supported marketplaces. Campaigns are automatically translated within 24 hours after going live.

## Supported languages

| Marketplace | Secondary language supported |
| --- | --- |
| AE | Arabic |
| CA | English, French |
| DE | German, English, Turkish, Polish |
| IN | Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, English |
| JP | Japanese, English |
| SA | Arabic |
| US | English, Spanish |

## Supported ad products

| Topic | Sponsored Brands | Display |
| --- | --- | --- |
| What's translated | Headlines, custom text, and video subtitles | Headlines and product titles |
| Timeline | Within 24 hours after default language campaign goes live | Within 24 hours after ad approval |
| Your control | Edit translations using the Creative page and **Edit Translations**, or submit your own translations | Not specified |
| Video support | Videos automatically include translated subtitles | Video translations not supported |
| Eligibility | Not specified | Only available to advertisers selling products/services on Amazon; not available for non-endemic advertisers |
| Restrictions | JP marketplace excludes Pharmaceutical and Grocery categories; translated ads cannot be turned off | Onsite placements only; excluded categories include media, food/beverages, prescription products, and certain personal care products; non-endemic advertisers cannot use this feature |

If Amazon's ad translation service cannot provide a translation that meets quality and ad policy standards, it will not provide a translation. In that case, the ad will not be shown to customers shopping in that secondary language.
