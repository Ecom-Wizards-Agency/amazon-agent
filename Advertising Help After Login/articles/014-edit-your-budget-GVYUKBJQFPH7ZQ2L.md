# Edit your budget

Daily budgets can be changed at any time and adjustments take effect immediately. For lifetime budgets, only increases are allowed once the campaign is live.

## How to edit your budget

1. In the navigation side menu, click **Campaigns**.
2. Click the amount in the budget column.
3. Enter the new amount.

If the budget changes midmonth, monthly campaign spend will not exceed the new daily budget multiplied by the month's remaining days, plus spend already used.

Example: daily budget is `$50`, and `$800` has been spent by the 20th. On the 20th, the daily budget changes to `$10`. The maximum monthly charge will be `$900`: `$800` spent so far plus `$100`, calculated as `$10 x 10` remaining days.

## How to apply budget recommendations

Budget recommendations are found on the Budgets page within Campaign manager. The page can show estimated missed impressions, clicks, sales, performance insights, and optimization recommendations.

1. In the navigation side menu, click **Campaigns**.
2. Next to the campaign name, in the **Recommended budget** column, click **Apply** for one campaign or **Apply all** to implement all recommendations together.
3. Click **Export** to export all data.
