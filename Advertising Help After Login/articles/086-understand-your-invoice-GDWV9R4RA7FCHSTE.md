# Understand Your Invoice

This article explains Amazon Ads invoice timing, statuses, and invoice adjustments.

## Key Points

- Invoice dates are determined by credit limit or the second day of the month.
- Invoice statuses include Issued, Failed, Written Off, Paid in Part, and Paid in Full.
- Overlapping invoices can happen after reaching credit limits or invalid-click adjustments.
- Credits and debits appear as invoice adjustments.

## Workflow

1. Open invoices from the ads console.
2. Review invoice date, invoice number, amount, and status.
3. Download the invoice if needed.
4. Check whether payment is required or if there are invoice issues.
5. Monitor invoice charges to keep campaigns active.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before making payment or billing profile changes.
- Ensure payment settings are complete to avoid invoice failures.
- Contact Advertiser Support for written-off invoice reissue questions.
