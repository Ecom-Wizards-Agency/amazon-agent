# Streaming TV

Streaming TV is a self-service ad solution for growing a brand by connecting with new and existing customers, including advertisers new to TV advertising.

Powered by Amazon shopping and streaming signals, Streaming TV can complement Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Display, Sponsored Products, and Stores efforts.

Streaming TV can run on services like Twitch and other Streaming TV publishers. Campaign owners control ad spend, with no minimum spend requirements and daily budget caps.

If advertisers do not have TV-quality creative, Amazon Ads can help produce TV-quality video ads through creative services.

## Interactive elements

All Streaming TV ads are eligible for interactive overlays and burnt-in elements. For example, viewers can scan a QR code in the ad to go to a product detail page or Brand Store on a mobile device.

QR code interaction allows brands to review ad value in real time, including viewer interaction insights.

## Availability

Streaming TV is available for vendors and brand owners, including book vendors. It is not available for Health and Personal Care advertisers at this time.

## Benefits

- Reach viewers likely to be interested in specific content types, including video games and TV genres.
- Reach viewers likely to be interested in product categories sold on Amazon.
- Build awareness with sight, sound, motion, and interactive ad formats.
- Measure awareness impact with metrics such as Branded Searches and % of orders New-To-Brand.
- Drive consideration using interactive ad formats.
- Measure consideration impact with metrics such as Detail Page Views and Add to Carts.
