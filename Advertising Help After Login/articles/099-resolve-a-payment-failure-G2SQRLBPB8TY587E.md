# Resolve a Payment Failure

This article explains how to resolve Amazon Ads payment failure notifications.

## Key Points

- Campaigns can pause when the default payment method fails.
- Common causes include insufficient funds, expired card, incorrect payment details, or card issuer restrictions.
- Updating payment information can take up to 24 hours to restore campaign status.

## Workflow

1. Check whether the payment method is expired or incorrect.
2. If using Deduct from payment, confirm enough funds are available in the relevant vendor codes.
3. Compare the entered card details against the actual card inside Amazon Ads.
4. Check whether the card issuer blocks electronic or online advertising purchases.
5. Submit invoice payments on time if the account pays by invoice.
6. Wait up to 24 hours after updating payment details before confirming campaign restoration.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before reattempting payment or changing payment details.
- Do not record private payment details.
- Confirm payment method validity before retrying.
- If campaigns remain paused after 24 hours, prepare a support case with screenshots and invoice/payment status.
