# Understand Advertising Promotions

This article explains promotional ad credits, eligibility, redemption, and usage.

## Key Points

- Promotional credits apply only to future advertising costs after credits are issued.
- Eligibility can come by invitation or promo code.
- Credits are applied automatically after qualifying actions are completed.
- Credits must be used before expiration.

## Promotion Flow

1. Receive promotion notification or promo code terms.
2. Complete the qualifying action during the promotional period.
3. Amazon issues ad credits after qualification.
4. Use ad credits on eligible campaigns before expiration.

## Definitions

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Ad product eligibility | Campaign/ad product types that qualify for the promotion. |
| Qualifying action | Required action to receive credits. |
| Promotional period | Timeframe to complete the required action. |
| Offer amount | Amount of ad credits offered. |
| Award date | Date credits are received. |
| Expiration date | Date credits expire. |
