# Edit a campaign

Campaigns can be modified by clicking editable fields in Campaign manager, or campaign status can be changed by modifying start and end date parameters.

## Edit campaign settings

To edit settings or details such as budget allocation, targeting, or ad groups:

1. Navigate to **Campaign manager**.
2. Locate the campaign to edit.
3. Some fields, such as campaign end date, daily budget allocation, and bid adjustment percentage, can be edited directly in the table. Editable fields are surrounded by a dotted line.
4. Click the campaign name for additional editing options, including keyword and product targeting, bids, and negative keywords.
5. For Sponsored Brands, click **Edit creative** in the **Creative** tab to edit products, ad headline, and logo.

## Managing campaign status

Campaigns cannot be enabled or paused after they are archived. Archived campaigns remain available for future reference. If an ad may be used later, set the campaign to **Paused** instead of **Archived**.

The Amazon page includes expandable status workflows for:

- Stop a campaign.
- Restart a paused campaign.
- Extend the duration of an active campaign.
- Extend the duration of a campaign that has already ended.

Large-scale campaign changes can also be made using a bulk file.
