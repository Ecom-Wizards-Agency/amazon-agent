# Set up product targeting

Product targeting lets advertisers choose specific products, categories, brands, or product features relevant to the advertised product.

Use product targeting when:

- Broad reach is desired instead of specific keyword targeting.
- Launching in a foreign marketplace where keyword translations are not desirable for research.

## Add targeting to an existing campaign

1. In the navigation side menu, click **Campaigns**.
2. Select the campaign.
3. Select the ad group.
4. Click **Targeting** in the left-hand menu.
5. Click **Add products**.
6. Choose categories or individual products.
7. Browse suggested products, search, or upload a list.
8. Click **Add** next to each product.
9. Click **Add product targets**.

## Categories

Target suggested categories based on relevance to the product. Categories can be refined by:

- Brand
- Price range
- Review star ratings
- Prime shipping eligibility for Sponsored Products and Display
- Age range for Toys & Games
- Genre for Books and eBooks

## Individual products

Target suggested individual products that are similar to the advertised product. Specific products can also be searched.

## Expanded product targeting

Expanded product targeting helps discover additional products related to already-targeted items. It is the default setting for campaigns; the **Expanded** box next to the targeted product is automatically selected during setup.

Expanded product targeting:

- Chooses closely related products based on past interactions and views.
- Expands each product to a limited number of products with the highest performance potential.
- Automatically updates underlying products daily.
- Shows expanded product targets in reports with a **Product-expanded** prefix in the targeting column.
- Lets advertisers identify expanded targets meeting performance criteria and promote them as individual product targets.
- Allows negative targeting to exclude individual products or brands from the expanded list.
