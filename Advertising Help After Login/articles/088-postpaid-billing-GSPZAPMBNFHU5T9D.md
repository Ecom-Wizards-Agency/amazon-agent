# Postpaid Billing

This article explains postpaid billing for Amazon Ads and how advertisers can manage ad spending under an approved spending limit.

## Key Points

- Postpaid billing allows ads to run first and payment to happen later.
- Eligibility can depend on spending history and account/payment behavior.
- A consolidated monthly invoice can cover all campaigns for an account.
- Spending limit and optional monthly budget help control spend.

## Workflow

1. Check eligibility for postpaid billing.
2. Review the max spending limit.
3. Set an optional monthly budget.
4. Monitor spend and credit availability in Campaign Manager.
5. Pay monthly invoices according to the billing terms.

## Definitions

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Postpaid billing | Run ads and pay after the billing period. |
| Spending limit | Maximum approved spend before payment or restriction. |
| Monthly budget | Optional advertiser-controlled budget cap. |

## Checkpoints

- Stop before enabling or changing postpaid billing.
- Confirm eligibility, spending limit, and monthly budget.
- Review invoice ownership and payment responsibility.
