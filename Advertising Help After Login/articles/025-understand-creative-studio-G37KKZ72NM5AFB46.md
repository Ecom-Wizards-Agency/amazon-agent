# Understand Creative Studio

Creative Studio is a central place in Amazon Ads Console for AI-powered creative tools, including Creative Agent, image creation, and video generation.

## What is Creative Studio?

Creative Studio gives advertisers access to generative AI and agentic AI creative tools for developing professional-level content. It brings together different ways to create ads, including chat-based Creative Agent, image creation, and video generation.

Creative Studio includes a tutorial for first-time users. AI-generated content goes through Amazon Ads moderation review before use.

## Benefits

Creative Studio puts AI-powered creative tools in one place. Advertisers can choose the ad creation method that best fits the workflow, such as chatting with Creative Agent or creating images directly.

## How it works with campaigns

Generated assets can be saved to the Creative Assets library and selected when building campaigns in Campaign manager. Assets can be used across multiple ad types and campaigns.

## Ways to create ads

- **Creative Agent:** chat with AI for guided help. It can create ad concepts, storyboards, video, images, and audio while using audience insights from Amazon and marketplace data.
- **Image generation:** generate images using product ASINs or custom ideas. Upload and edit photos with different styles.
- **Video generation:** transform a product image or video and ASIN into professional-quality video ads with AI.

> Note: Creative Agent is only available to advertisers in the US.
