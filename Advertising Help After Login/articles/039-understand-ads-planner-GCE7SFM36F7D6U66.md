# Understand Ads planner

Ads planner uses media plans to manage advertising activity.

> Requirement: you must be signed in to a Manager account to use Ads planner.

## What is Ads planner?

Ads planner uses Amazon insights and algorithms to build media plans for single or multiple-format ad campaigns. It helps create the optimal mix of audiences, channels, and ad formats for advertising objectives.

With Ads planner, advertisers can:

- Create a cohesive strategy and forecast reach across ad products and formats on and off Amazon.
- View audience opportunity size at each conversion stage based on Amazon first-party, third-party, and custom audiences.
- Access demographic, shopping, and viewing behavior insights.
- Plan and optimize budget.

Ads planner is available in US, CA, MX, BR, UK, DE, FR, IT, ES, NL, SE, TR, AU, JP, IN, KSA, and UAE.

## Media plan structure

1. Define the audience by selecting products/categories to promote for Sponsored ads or choosing audience segments for Amazon DSP.
2. Choose audience types:
   - Awareness: build brand recognition and reach new customers.
   - Consideration: drive traffic by reaching customers who viewed similar products.
   - Conversion: reach customers who viewed product detail pages but have not purchased.
   - Loyalty: engage customers who have already purchased.
3. Receive customer opportunity, demographic, and behavioral insights.
4. View suggested ad products and formats aligned with the marketing goal.

## Ads planner insights

Ads planner organizes potential customer opportunity into distinct purchase-path groups:

- Customers who purchased products in the last 12 months: loyalty.
- Customers who researched but did not purchase: consideration.
- Customers shopping for competitive products in the aisle: conversion.
- Customers who have not yet shopped in the aisle: awareness.
