# Update Your Payment Method

This article explains where and how to update the payment method used for Amazon Ads.

## Key Points

- Only advertising console admins or Seller Central primary account owners can update payment methods.
- Changes usually take effect within 1 hour.
- If updating after payment failure, restoration can take up to 24 hours.

## Workflow

1. In the left menu, click **Administration**.
2. Under **Billing and Payment**, select **Billing**.
3. Open **Payment Settings**.
4. Click **Change payment profile**.
5. Click **Edit** next to the current default payment method.
6. Select the payment method to use.
7. Enter required payment information inside Amazon Ads.
8. Click **Save**.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before entering or saving payment information.
- Confirm admin/primary-owner access.
- Do not record payment card details in local notes.
- Confirm the correct account and billing profile before saving.

Use this page when the user asks to update the card/payment method, recover from payment failure, or check who can change payments.
