# Add a Sponsored Products Video

This article explains how to add Sponsored Products video assets to highlight product features and increase shopper engagement.

## Key Points

- Sponsored Products video is listed as available in the US and not available for Books.
- Each video must be at least 7 seconds long.
- Each video should demonstrate one unique product feature.
- Up to five product feature videos can be uploaded per product.
- Videos go through an approval process that can take 48 to 72 hours.

## Workflow

1. In the left menu, click **Sponsored ads**.
2. Open **Campaign manager**.
3. Open the campaign where Sponsored Products videos should be added.
4. Choose an ad group.
5. Go to **Ads**.
6. Select the **Video** tab.
7. Click **Add video**.
8. Upload a video from the computer or add one from creative assets.

## Checkpoints

- Stop before uploading or submitting video assets.
- Confirm product/category eligibility.
- Confirm the video follows Sponsored Products video guidelines.
- Remember approval can take 48 to 72 hours.
