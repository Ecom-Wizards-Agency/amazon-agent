# Manage Your Partner Network Account

This article explains Amazon Ads Partner Network account management for agencies and tool providers.

## Key Points

- Partner Network is a self-service hub for agencies and tool providers.
- It provides marketing and learning resources.
- Partners can showcase offerings to prospective clients.
- Users, advertisers, and directory listings can be managed inside the Partner Network account.

## Workflow

1. Register with the Amazon Ads Partner Network.
2. Manage users and advertiser relationships.
3. Create and manage directory listings.

## Definition

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Amazon Ads Partner Network | Platform for agencies and tool providers to manage their relationship with Amazon Ads and showcase services. |

## Checkpoints

- Confirm business registration before attempting partner account setup.
- Stop before changing directory listings or user access.
- Check user permissions before managing advertiser relationships.
