# Apply Schedule bid rules for Sponsored Products

Learn how to apply schedule bid rules for Sponsored Products campaigns and specific promotional events.

Schedule bid rules are available for Sponsored Products campaigns and allow you to schedule bid increases for specific times of day or days of week. You can also apply schedule bid rules around recommended promotional events.

This helps you take advantage of high-impact time windows to drive more sales.

This rule can be applied to:

- Dynamic bids - up and down
- Down only
- Fixed bidding strategies

## Apply Schedule bid rules

1. Sign in to Amazon Ads Console, then in the left menu, click **Sponsored Ads**.
2. Select **Campaign manager**, then locate the campaign you want to schedule bid rules for.
3. Next to the campaign name, click **More**, then select **Edit settings**.
4. Next to **Campaign bidding strategy**, click **Add schedule rule**.
5. Below **Settings**, complete all requirements, then click **Add rule**.

## Apply Schedule bid rules for specific promotional events

1. Sign in to Amazon Ads Console, then in the left menu, click **Sponsored Ads**.
2. Select **Campaign manager**, then locate the campaign you want to schedule bid rules for.
3. Next to the campaign name, click **More**, then select **Edit settings**.
4. Next to **Campaign bidding strategy**, click **Add schedule rule**.
5. Click the circle next to **Events**, then click the **drop-down arrow** to see the list of recommended promotional events.
6. Choose from the list of individual events.
7. Once you select an event from the dropdown menu, complete all requirements, then click **Add bid rule**.

Not all events will be available in the drop-down menu. Amazon also provides a retail holiday calendar and a guide to advertising for international events.
