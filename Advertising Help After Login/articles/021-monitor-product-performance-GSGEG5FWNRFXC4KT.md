# Monitor product performance

Monitor product performance and use recommendations to improve product listings.

The Products page displays all advertised products in one place and shows advertising performance metrics. It aggregates metrics from campaign and ad group levels, including impressions, clicks, sales, return on advertising spend (ROAS), new-to-brand (NTB) sales, and other metrics.

## To access

1. In the navigation side menu, click **Campaigns**.
2. At the top of the **Campaigns** page, click the **Sponsored Products** tab.

## Product views

Amazon lists two product views:

- Advertised Products
- All sold products

**Advertised Products** helps view and analyze product-level performance for all advertised products. It also shows listing improvement recommendations.

You can:

- Analyze performance by campaign and ad group.
- Review listing improvement recommendations.
- Search by campaign name or ASIN.
- Create new campaigns with selected ASINs.
- Add ASINs to existing campaigns.

## Available metrics

- Impressions
- Clicks
- Sales
- Return on advertising spend (ROAS)
- New-to-brand (NTB) sales
