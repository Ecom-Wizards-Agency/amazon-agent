# Third-party ad tools

Learn how to create high-performing ad creatives using third-party creative tools.

Integrated tools such as Canva and Adobe Express can be used directly within an Amazon Ads workflow to create, edit, and manage creative assets without leaving the advertising console.

## Benefits

- Design and upload creatives directly in the advertising console.
- Generate ads with AI-optimized templates.
- Keep brand consistency using the Creative Assets library, including logos, colors, and elements.
- Launch faster with Amazon-ready templates that meet specs automatically.
- Collaborate with shared designs, comments, and version tracking.

## Supported ad products

- Sponsored Brands
- Display
- Stores
- Amazon DSP, where applicable

## To access third-party tools

1. Sign in to an [Adobe Express](https://www.adobe.com/express/) or [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) account.
2. In the navigation menu, click **Apps**.
3. Search for "Amazon Ads".
4. Select **Use in existing design** or **Use in new design**.
5. Follow the prompts to authorize the connection between the Amazon Ads account and the third-party tool.
6. For Adobe, review webinar and training articles on [Adobe Learn](https://www.adobe.com/learn/express).

## Canva workflow

1. To reuse assets, select an advertiser profile in the Amazon Ads account.
2. Search existing assets in the Search tab.
3. Drag assets into a Canva template.
4. To upload and export a design, in **Check Design**, select **Upload Design**.
5. Preview the design.
6. Choose PNG for images or MP4 for videos.
7. Click **Export**.
8. To save the design, enter **Asset Name** and optional Tag/ASIN information.
9. Complete required fields.
10. Click **Save** design to publish to Amazon assets.

Template designs are based on Amazon Ads creative best practices, but conversion rates also depend on factors such as campaign budget, targeting, and keyword relevance.
