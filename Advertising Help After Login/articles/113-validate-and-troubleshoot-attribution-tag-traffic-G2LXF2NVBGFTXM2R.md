# Validate and Troubleshoot Attribution Tag Traffic

This article explains how to troubleshoot reporting metrics for attribution tags in non-Amazon campaigns.

## Key Points

- Discrepancies of 10-20% can be common between Amazon Attribution and publisher metrics.
- Date ranges must match for accurate comparison.
- Attribution tags should be implemented correctly and not reused incorrectly.

## Workflow

1. Verify date ranges in Amazon Attribution and publisher reporting.
2. Confirm attribution tags are not shared across creatives or URLs.
3. Confirm product ASINs are assigned to campaigns.
4. Compare publisher and Amazon Attribution reporting with methodology differences in mind.

## Checkpoints

- Ensure at least 10 clicks before expecting data to appear in Amazon Attribution.
- Check Stores and Amazon Attribution methodology differences before escalating discrepancies.
- Capture tag URLs, date ranges, and screenshots before opening a support case.

## Routing Use

Use this page when the user asks why Amazon Attribution clicks/conversions differ from publisher data or why tag traffic is not showing.
