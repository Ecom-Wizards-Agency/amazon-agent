# Get started with sponsored ads

This guide helps prepare an account for advertising and choose a campaign type.

## 1. Basic terms

- **Bid:** amount willing to pay when shoppers click or view an ad.
- **Conversions:** customer actions such as adding to cart or completing a purchase.
- **Featured Offer:** the product detail page section with **Add to Cart** or **Buy Now**.
- **Impressions:** number of times an ad was rendered on a page or device.
- **Keyword:** words or phrases used to target ads to relevant shopping queries.
- **Placement:** where an ad appears.
- **Reach:** total number of users shown the same ad.
- **Shopping Query:** words or phrases Amazon customers use to find products.

## 2. Sponsored ads eligibility

To advertise with Amazon, the advertiser must:

- Be a professional seller, vendor, author, or advertising agency.
- Have an active account in good standing.
- Be able to ship to the country being advertised in.
- Set up a valid payment method.

## 3. Decide which Amazon Ads product to use

Choose an advertising product based on goals. For sales and product engagement, consider Sponsored Products.

Display campaigns use auto-generated display ads with an Amazon look and feel. Ads can run on Amazon desktop and mobile, on detail pages and other Amazon pages, and can also reach audiences off Amazon across third-party publishers and Amazon Business.

## 4. Get product detail pages ready

Product setup is a key part of campaign success.

![Product detail page annotated](https://d369o5h5zn8mv7.cloudfront.net/images/2023/product+detail+page_annotated.jpg)

Amazon lists six top tips:

1. Create a strong product title: informative, easy to read, approximately 60 characters.
2. Feature at least four high-quality product images.
3. Select products with a rating of 3.5 stars or more and at least 15 customer reviews.
4. Price products competitively to improve Featured Offer chances.
5. Keep products in stock.
6. Include at least three bullet points in the description covering key features.

## 5. Link accounts if applicable

Vendors can link a Vendor Central account to an advertising account to find products more easily and receive better product recommendations.

Path: **Access and Settings > Selling Account > Link your Vendor Central account**.

For Sponsored Brands or Stores, enroll in Amazon Brand Registry.

## 6. Create your first campaign

In the navigation side menu, click **Create campaign** and choose a campaign type. During campaign setup, use workflow help links or the question mark in the upper-right to access Support Center.
