# Optimize products for advertising

Optimize advertised products to increase the chance of product clicks.

When grouping products in an ad group, select products that:

- Share the same category.
- Have related functionalities.
- Fall within similar price ranges.

This structure helps track campaign performance in Campaign Manager.

## Product optimization

Campaign manager shows tailored listing improvement recommendations for products in both active and inactive Sponsored Products campaigns.

## Product title

- Create informative, scannable titles of approximately 60 characters.
- Include key product details that help customers make quick decisions.

## Visual elements

- Include four or more high-quality images, minimum 1,000 pixels in height or width.
- Enable zoom functionality to show product details.
- Use multiple angles to highlight key features.

## Product information

Add three or more focused bullet points covering:

- Key features
- Contents
- Dimensions
- Usage instructions
- Age ratings
- Country of origin

Write detailed product descriptions explaining benefits and value proposition. Include relevant search terms for discoverability.

## Pricing and availability

- Maintain competitive pricing.
- Make sure products are in stock.
- Choose products that display the Featured Offer.

## A+ content

For vendors and sellers enrolled in Amazon Brand Registry, A+ content can describe product features using enhanced images, text placements, and stories.

## Prime badge

Advertise products that offer Prime Shipping. Eligible products can get the Prime badge through Fulfillment by Amazon.

## Reviews

Amazon recommends advertising products with five or more customer reviews and a rating of 3.5 stars or higher.
