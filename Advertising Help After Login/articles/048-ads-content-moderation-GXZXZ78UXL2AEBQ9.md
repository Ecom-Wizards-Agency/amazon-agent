# Ads content moderation

Amazon Ads uses automation and expert human review to monitor ads and respond to reports of ads that do not comply with Amazon Ads policies.

## Ads moderation

Amazon Ads reviews ads for infringing and noncompliant content, and responds to reports of illegal ads or ads that violate Amazon Ads policies. Review tools use natural language processing and machine learning.

If an ad does not meet policies, Amazon sends an email notification with the rejection reason and guidance on updates.

Beginning February 2026, Amazon no longer sends email notifications when an ad is approved or under review. Ad status can be checked in the ad console. Review is usually within 24 hours but can take up to 3 business days. Review times may vary by ad product and can be extended during high submission volume.

## Appeals process

If the advertiser believes the ad is compliant and the rejection is incorrect, an appeal can be submitted using the link in Amazon's decision communication. Amazon notifies the advertiser after review.

## Account suspension and reinstatement

Repeated policy violations may result in account suspension. The suspension duration and possibility of reinstatement correspond to violation severity.

If the advertiser disagrees or believes there was an error, an appeal can be submitted using the link in Amazon's suspension communication.
