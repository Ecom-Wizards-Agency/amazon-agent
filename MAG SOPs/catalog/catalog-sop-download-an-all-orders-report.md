# Catalog SOP: Download an All Orders Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-an-all-orders-report

## **Download an All Orders Report**

**Who is this for :** For individuals who are responsible for monitoring and analyzing inventory levels, tracking order fulfillment, and making informed business decisions based on inventory data.

**Objective :** To provide instructions on how to download an All Orders Report for effective inventory management and analysis.

1. From the Seller Central home page, click the 3-line bars located on the left side. Then, select "Reports" and choose "Fulfillment.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684343940349.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/In2image.png)

2. Under the "Sales" header on the left of the page, click on the "All Orders" link. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684344071582.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bNuimage.png)

3. Choose the type of report you want based on either Order Date or Last Updated Date. Select the date from the drop-down menu or choose "Exact Dates" and enter the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icon.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684344305835.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/lXiimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684344330289.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/JlUimage.png)

4. Click the "Request Download" button. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684344449728.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Yfuimage.png)

5. In the table below, the "Report Status" column will show the status as "In Progress".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684344505892.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4NVimage.png)

6. Once the report is ready, the status will change to "Download".

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rZHimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684344529792.png)

7. Click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column to download your report.

FAQs

1. Where can I find the All Orders Report in Seller Central?

You can find the report by clicking the 3-line menu on the left side of the Seller Central home page, selecting Reports > Fulfillment, and then navigating to Sales > All Orders.

2. What is the difference between selecting "Order Date" and "Last Updated Date"?

- Order Date: Filters reports based on when the order was originally placed.
- Last Updated Date: Shows orders that have had changes, such as status updates or modifications, within the selected time frame.

3. Can I choose a custom date range for my report?

Yes, you can select "Exact Dates" from the drop-down menu and manually enter the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icon.

4. What format will the report be downloaded in?

The report is typically downloaded in .csv format, which can be opened in Excel or other spreadsheet programs for analysis.

5. How do I track the progress of my report request?

After clicking "Request Download," check the "Report Status" column. The status will initially show "In Progress" and will update to "Download" when the report is ready.

6. How long does it take for the report to be generated?

The processing time varies based on data volume, but most reports are available within a few minutes.

7. What should I do if my report does not generate or download properly?

If your report is not generating or downloading:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for further assistance.
