# Catalog SOP: Creating a Brand Performance Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-creating-a-brand-performance-report

## Creating a Brand Performance Report

**Who is this for :** This is designed for sellers on Seller Central who want to generate a Brand Performance Report to track their brand's performance on the platform. It is intended for individuals or teams responsible for analyzing business data and making informed decisions to optimize their brand's performance.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions on how to download a Brand Performance Report from Seller Central. By following these steps, sellers will be able to access valuable insights and metrics related to their brand's performance, enabling them to make data-driven decisions to enhance their business strategies.

This SOP outlines the step-by-step process to download a Brand Performance Report on Seller Central. The report provides comprehensive data and performance metrics that are essential for evaluating and optimizing a brand's performance on the platform. By following these instructions, sellers will be able to generate a customized report tailored to their specific requirements and gain valuable insights into their brand's performance trends, sales data, and other key metrics. These insights can then be used to make informed decisions and implement effective strategies to drive growth and success on Seller Central.

Instructions:

1. To generate a report on your Brand's Performance, first, select "Business Reports" from the Drop-Down Menu under the "Reports" Tab in Seller Central's Top Menu.

2. On the next page, find the "Brand Performance" link in the left side menu under the sub-heading "By ASIN" and click on it.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-creating-a-brand-performance-report-image2.png)

3. Click on the "Columns" button located on the left side of the page to reveal the available Column Fields. From here, you can select or deselect which columns and information you would like included in your report.

FAQs

1. Who can access the Brand Performance Report?

The report is available to sellers who are enrolled in Amazon Brand Registry. Users must have the appropriate permissions (such as Brand Owner, Admin, or Business Analyst) to access Business Reports in Seller Central.

2. What kind of data does the Brand Performance Report provide?

The report includes insights on sales performance, customer engagement, conversion rates, and traffic trends for products under a registered brand. It helps sellers track brand health, identify high-performing ASINs, and optimize their business strategies.

3. Can I filter the report by ASIN, category, or marketplace?

Yes, you can customize the report by selecting specific ASINs, product categories, or marketplaces where your brand is active. Use the column selection feature to tailor the report to your needs.

4. How often is the Brand Performance Report updated?

The data in the Brand Performance Report is typically updated daily, but some metrics may have a delay of up to 48 hours. It is recommended to check the report regularly for the most accurate insights.

5. What should I do if I don’t see the Brand Performance Report in my account?

Ensure that:

- Your brand is enrolled in Amazon Brand Registry.
- Your user role has the necessary permissions to access Business Reports.
- There are active ASINs under your brand.
  If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache, using a different browser, or contacting Amazon Seller Support.

6. Can I download the Brand Performance Report in Excel format?

No, Amazon currently only allows downloads in CSV format. However, you can convert the CSV file into an Excel file by opening it in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.

7. Is there an API or automated way to retrieve this report?

Yes, sellers with API access can retrieve similar brand performance data via Amazon’s Selling Partner API (SP-API). This is useful for automating data collection and integrating it into business intelligence tools.

8. How can I use this report to improve my brand’s performance?

You can analyze traffic trends, conversion rates, and sales metrics to:

- Identify high-converting products.
- Optimize listings and keywords for better visibility.
- Improve inventory forecasting.
- Adjust pricing strategies based on sales trends.

9. How frequently should I generate this report?

It is recommended to generate and review the Brand Performance Report at least once a week to monitor sales trends and make data-driven decisions in a timely manner.

10. Can I access historical data in the Brand Performance Report?

Yes, you can adjust the date range to view historical data. However, Amazon may have limitations on how far back you can retrieve data, typically up to 12 months.
