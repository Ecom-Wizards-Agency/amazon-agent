## PRODUCT CONDITION CUSTOMER COMPLAINTS (DAMAGED / DEFECTIVE)

**Who is this for :** This SOP is for the customer service team and warehouse staff who are responsible for handling customer complaints and ensuring that products are shipped to customers in good condition.

**Objective :** To provide a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) to handle customer complaints about damaged or defective products and to take preventive measures to ensure that the issue does not occur again.

This issue is caused by customer complaints who received damaged/defective products. The complaint will show up on the account health page:

**DAMAGED/DEFECTIVE (3 parts)**

1. We have not checked all products we received from the manufacturer. Some products arrive damaged because of the lack of proper protective shipping equipment.

2. They did not package each item properly as we shipped it to customers causing damages to its inner parts or tools during shipment to the customers.

3. Lack of proper packaging and protective shipping material caused items to arrive damaged.

4. This lack of quality control process and care led to the products arriving damaged or defective.

**Preventive actions we have taken to prevent future complaints:**

1. We responded to all customer complaints and issued a full refund or free replacement.

2. We audited our catalog to ensure all listings are solid and in the proper condition and all of them have proper packaging.

3. We have had a warehouse-wide meeting with the warehouse division informing the packagers that there were complaints about products being damaged or defective due to potential defects caused by the shipping process while being shipped from our warehouse to the buyer.

4. We reviewed their shipping and packaging processes and reinforced our shipping policies to ensure that these issues will not happen again. We restocked protective materials such as bubble wrap, and scratch-resistant papers which go over our products before shipping them to customers.

5. We as a company have reviewed all of Amazon’s policies, paying special attention to the item condition policies to ensure our policies match and exceed Amazon’s guidelines.

**The steps you have taken to prevent the issue going forward**

1. We have taken the extra step to order fragile stickers to put on our packages to ensure our postal carrier does not damage the products, and we have communicated this to our carrier representative as well. This will ensure the products will not be damaged during the shipment to warehouses or customers.

2. Management will routinely check and audit the shipment process to ensure all in-house rules and Amazon rules and regulations are being followed.

3. We will check every unit we receive from the manufacturer and make sure it is not damaged or defective. We have a better quality control process with the manufacturer that will ensure any of our products are not defective. The manufacturer will check every item prior to shipping them out to us.

4. We take all responsibility for this incident and we have learned from this issue. We confirm this will never happen again in the future.

Dear Amazon Team,

We are confident that the information provided is sufficient in addressing the issue which caused the complaint and addressing our plan to correct the issue and make sure it does not happen again. We appreciate your support to help us keep selling on Amazon. We offer a great level of shopping experience to our customers and try to do our best on Amazon. Please review our appeal and reinstate our listing.

FAQs

#### 1. How do I check for Product Condition Complaints in Amazon’s Account Health Page?

To view complaints about damaged or defective products:

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central.
2. Go to Performance > Account Health.
3. Look for the section labeled Product Condition Customer Complaints.
4. Click on it to view affected ASINs and customer feedback.
5. Download the report if necessary to analyze trends in complaints.

#### 2. What should I do if multiple complaints are received for the same product?

If a single ASIN receives multiple damaged/defective complaints:

1. Temporarily deactivate the listing if issues persist.
2. Conduct an immediate quality control check on current inventory.
3. Escalate to management if the defect rate exceeds 1% of sales.
4. Contact the manufacturer to discuss corrective actions, such as improving packaging or inspecting before shipment.
5. If necessary, remove defective stock from FBA or warehouse inventory.

#### 3. How can we ensure the manufacturer follows quality control standards?

Require pre-shipment inspection reports from the manufacturer before products are sent. Implement a defective product penalty clause in supplier agreements. Request sample inspections for each batch before accepting large shipments.

#### 4. What is the escalation process if a product gets flagged multiple times?

First complaint: Investigate and resolve with refund/replacement.
Second complaint: Check inventory and verify packaging compliance.
Third complaint or more: Conduct a full audit, report findings, and consider removing/relisting the product after resolution.

#### 5. How often should quality control checks be conducted?

Warehouse checks should be done weekly. Manufacturer compliance checks should be done quarterly or after every major shipment. Amazon order fulfillment checks should be conducted monthly to verify packaging integrity.

#### 6. How does Amazon measure product condition issues?

Amazon tracks product complaints under Order Defect Rate (ODR), which consists of:

- Negative Feedback Rate (customer complaints)
- A-to-Z Guarantee Claims (refund requests due to defective items)
- Chargeback Rate (customer disputes)

A defect rate above 1% can lead to listing suspensions or account restrictions.

#### 7. What packaging materials are recommended to reduce damage complaints?

- Bubble wrap for fragile items like electronics, glass, or ceramics
- Scratch-resistant paper to prevent surface damage
- Fragile stickers to ensure proper handling by carriers
- Double-boxing for extra protection on high-value or breakable items

#### 8. What should I do if a customer complaint is inaccurate or false?

Gather evidence such as photos of the product, customer messages, and tracking details. Respond professionally and offer a resolution, such as a refund or replacement. If the complaint is misleading or abusive, report it to Amazon through Seller Support > Performance Notifications.
