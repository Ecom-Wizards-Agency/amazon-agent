# Catalog SOP: Finding a Refund Report on Amazon Seller Central

## Finding a Refund Report on Amazon Seller Central

**Who is this for :** Sellers who are registered on Amazon Seller Central and are looking to find a refund report on their account.

**Objectives :**

- Knowing the overall amount of refunds issued over a specific time period.
- Examining the justifications for returns to find any possible problems with product quality or customer support.
- Tracking the performance of the company by keeping track of the quantity and value of refunds over time and making necessary adjustments.

**Instructions**

1. Log in to your Seller Central Account.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/SS(1).png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Wmqimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Wmqimage.png)

2. Click on the "Payments" tab at the left part of the page. Select "Payments".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1719525145603.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/xkDimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/xkDimage.png)

3. Once you've landed on the Payments page, click on the Transaction View Tab.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/SS%24.jpg)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SWTimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/SWTimage.png)

4. Once you're on this page click the drop-down tab labeled "Transaction Type" then select "Refund."

5. Choose a date ranges (Statement Period, Past number of days or Custom date range) and then click on the "Update" button.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1683931637062.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Tz2image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Tz2image.png)

6. Proceed to download your report by clicking the "Download" button.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1683931928006.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9fdimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/9fdimage.png)

9. After you click download, Amazon will generate a text file for you to work with. Simply copy and paste everything in the text file into an Excel sheet to read the report. Here you will be able to sort your information even further, allowing you to find exactly the information you're looking for.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/amazon_-finding-a-refund-report-on-amazon-seller-central-__-template-__-flowster-image-bvo9tgu6.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/nGZimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/nGZimage.png)

10. Copy and Paste into Excel.

11. From here you can easily format the table as needed. You can sort by refund amount, the order's date, the order number, and any other column in the table.

Using this exact method there are a number of other reports that you can generate using this tool. Other reports available here include:

• Adhoc Disbursements • Order Payment • Refund • Chargeback Refund • Liquidations • Liquidations Adjustments • A-to-Z Guarantee Refund • Service Fees • Shipping services purchased through Amazon • Paid to Amazon • Previous statement's unavailable balance

FAQs

#### 1. What should I expect in the refund report from Amazon Seller Central?

- The refund report from Amazon will typically include details such as the refund amount, order date, order number, and the reason for refund. You can download it in a text file format, which can then be copied to Excel for easier analysis. If you're using Excel, you can further sort the data by different columns like refund amount, order date, and customer details to understand patterns in your refunds.

#### 2. How can I filter specific refund data in the report?

- After copying the report into Excel, you can filter the data based on various parameters like refund amount, order date, or refund reason. Simply use Excel's "Filter" feature by selecting the column header and applying the filter criteria. This will allow you to focus on specific periods or refund reasons, making it easier to analyze the data.

#### 3. How do I interpret the data in the refund report?

- The refund report will show you the amount refunded, and the reason for the refund. Look for patterns in the reasons for refunds (e.g., product quality issues, shipping problems) to identify any recurring issues. If a specific product has multiple refunds, it could indicate a quality or customer service issue. This allows you to take action, such as improving the product quality or enhancing your customer support services.

#### 4. Can I expect any recent changes to Amazon’s refund report features?

- Amazon regularly updates its platform, so it’s always a good idea to check for any new features or changes to the refund report section. Periodically check Amazon Seller Central’s "Payments" page for updates, or subscribe to Amazon’s release notes to stay informed about any enhancements that could improve how you analyze refund data.

#### 5. What other reports can I generate in Seller Central to track refunds or related financial data?

- In addition to the refund report, you can also generate reports like:

  - Adhoc Disbursements
  - Order Payment
  - Chargeback Refund
  - Liquidations
  - A-to-Z Guarantee Refund
  - Service Fees
  - Shipping services purchased through Amazon
  - Paid to Amazon
  - Previous statement's unavailable balance
- These reports can give you a more comprehensive view of your financials and help identify any further patterns or discrepancies.
