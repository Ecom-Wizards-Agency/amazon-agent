## **How to Submit a Ticket to Brand Registry**

**Who is this for?** This is for individuals who need to file a case or submit a ticket through the brand registry

**Objective:**

To efficiently raise a ticket to the Brand Registry department by the Account Associates, ensuring prompt resolution of any issues related to the brand registry.

**Instructions:**

1. Log in to [https://brandregistry.amazon.com/](https://brandregistry.amazon.com/)

2. Go to Support on the upper right-hand side of the page. Then, click on ‘Contact Brand Support.’

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4qKimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4qKimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-how-to-submit-a-ticket-to-brand-registry-1.png)

3. You will be directed to the 'Brand Registry Support' page where you can select the type of problem you want to raise with the team.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-how-to-submit-a-ticket-to-brand-registry-2.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gs4image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/gs4image.png)

4. In this example, we will choose ‘Technical Issue'

5. Update the short description and describe your issue, providing as many details as you can, and indicating your merchant ID or merchant token. Add in yours including all email addresses that need to be looped into the case under the CC field to receive notifications for Amazon Brand Registry response. **Note:** Go to settings and click account info to access the merchant token.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Q3fimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Q3fimage.png)

6. Once done, you can click the **Send**button.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/MABimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/MABimage.png)

7. It will generate a unique CASE ID, and you can check its status and follow up as needed.

FAQs

1. What should I do before submitting a ticket to Brand Registry?

Before submitting a ticket, ensure you have gathered all necessary details, such as a clear description of the issue, your merchant ID or merchant token, and email addresses that need to be CC’d. This will help Amazon process your request more efficiently.

2. Where can I find my merchant token?

You can find your merchant token by navigating to Settings > Account Info in your Amazon Seller Central account.

3. Can I submit a ticket if I don’t have Brand Registry access?

No, only individuals with Brand Registry access can submit tickets. If you don’t have access, coordinate with an authorized person on your team who can file the case on your behalf.

4. What happens after I submit a ticket?

Once you submit a ticket, Amazon generates a unique Case ID. You can track its status and follow up as needed within the Brand Registry Support section.

5. How do I follow up on an open Brand Registry case?

To follow up, log in to Brand Registry, navigate to Support > Case Log, and search for your Case ID. You can add additional details or request an update from Amazon.

6. How can I ensure that all necessary people receive updates on the ticket?

When submitting the ticket, add all relevant email addresses under the CC field so they receive notifications regarding Amazon’s responses.

7. What type of issues can I raise under Brand Registry Support?

Brand Registry Support handles issues related to trademark disputes, unauthorized sellers, technical issues, listing errors, and brand protection concerns. Select the most relevant category when filing your ticket.

8. Can I edit my ticket after submission?

No, you cannot edit a submitted ticket. However, you can provide additional details by responding within the case log.

9. What should I do if I don’t get a response from Amazon?

If you don’t receive a response within the expected timeframe, follow up using the Case ID in the Brand Registry Support portal. If necessary, submit a new ticket referencing the original Case ID.

10. What if I selected the wrong issue type when submitting a ticket?

If you selected the wrong issue type, you cannot change it. Instead, you should close the current ticket and submit a new one under the correct category.
