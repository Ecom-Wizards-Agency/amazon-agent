# Catalog SOP: How to Generate an FBA Customer Returns Report

## How to Generate an FBA Customer Returns Report

**Who is this for :** For sellers using Amazon Seller Central who need guidance on generating an FBA Customer Returns Report, downloading it in either CSV or TXT format, and setting up automated report generation for this specific report.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions on how to generate an FBA Customer Returns Report on Amazon Seller Central. It aims to guide users in selecting the desired event date range, downloading the report in the preferred format, and setting up automated report generation at a specified frequency.

**Introduction :** The FBA Customer Returns Report is a valuable tool for sellers on Amazon Seller Central to track and analyze customer returns. This SOP will walk you through the step-by-step process of generating this report, including selecting the event date range and specifying additional filters for a more specific report. Additionally, it will provide instructions on how to download the report in either CSV or TXT format. Furthermore, the SOP will outline the process for automating the report generation at your preferred frequency, ensuring you have the latest data available for analysis.

To generate an FBA Customer Returns Report, follow these steps:

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684334242881.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dAsimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/dAsimage.png)
2. Under the "Customer Concessions" header on the left-hand side of the page, click on the "FBA Customer Returns" link.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684334364866.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7ZYimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/7ZYimage.png)

3. On the following page, choose the event date by selecting from the drop-down menu or selecting "Exact Dates" and entering the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icons. You can also enter your Merchant SKU, Amazon Order ID, FNSKU, ASIN, or LPN to generate a more specific report. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684334543410.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/x4Cimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/x4Cimage.png)

**To download a Txt or a CSV copy of the report, follow the instructions below:**

1. On the same page, click "Download".

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eWximage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/eWximage.png)
2. Select the event date by choosing from the drop-down menu or selecting "Exact Dates" and entering the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icons.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684334684583.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/55Uimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/55Uimage.png)
3. Click the "Request .csv Download" button to download the report in Excel format, or click the "Request .txt Download" button to download it in text format.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684334776615.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fteimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/fteimage.png)
4. The "Report Status" column in the table below will show the status as "In Progress".![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684334862088.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0Isimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/0Isimage.png)
5. Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to "Download". Click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column to download your report.

**To automate report generation at your preferred frequency, follow the steps outlined below.**

1. Select the Schedule tab under the FBA Customer Returns page.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684335528888.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dFdimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/dFdimage.png)
2. Choose the desired frequency, start date (which must be in the future), and report format from the provided drop-down menus.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684335847746.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ymoimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/ymoimage.png)
3. Click on "Create scheduled report".

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3Ltimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/3Ltimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684335897963.png)

**Notes:**Your schedule will be displayed in the "My Schedule" section of the page. To make changes to the schedule, delete the current scheduled report and create a new one. Only one schedule is allowed per report.

The report will be generated around midnight in the time zone of your store, based on the selected frequency. Each time a scheduled report is generated, it will appear in the "Scheduled Reports" section. This section can hold a maximum of 30 reports, arranged chronologically.

FAQs

### 1. Where can I find the FBA Customer Returns Report in Seller Central?

To find the report, navigate to the Reports tab at the top of Seller Central, select Fulfillment from the drop-down menu, and then click on the "FBA Customer Returns" link under the "Customer Concessions" header on the left-hand side.

### 2. How do I select the event date for the FBA Customer Returns Report?

You can select the event date by choosing from the available options in the drop-down menu or select "Exact Dates" and manually enter the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icons provided on the page.

### 3. Can I filter the FBA Customer Returns Report by specific items or orders?

Yes, you can filter the report by entering specific criteria such as Merchant SKU, Amazon Order ID, FNSKU, ASIN, or LPN to generate a more specific report related to certain items or returns.

### 4. What formats are available for downloading the FBA Customer Returns Report?

You can download the report in either CSV format (for Excel) by clicking "Request .csv Download" or in TXT format by clicking "Request .txt Download".

### 5. How do I track the progress of my report request?

Once you request the report, check the "Report Status" column. Initially, it will show as "In Progress". Once the report is ready, the status will change to "Download", and you can then click the Download button to retrieve your report.

### 6. How long does it take for the FBA Customer Returns Report to generate?

The processing time typically takes a few minutes. However, this can vary based on the size of the data. The status will show as "In Progress" until the report is ready for download.

### 7. What should I do if my FBA Customer Returns Report is not generating or downloading correctly?

If the report fails to generate or download:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Clear your browser cache or try using a different browser.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for further assistance.

### 8. How can I automate the generation of the FBA Customer Returns Report?

To automate the report generation, follow these steps:

1. Select the Schedule tab under the FBA Customer Returns page.
2. Choose your desired frequency, start date (which must be in the future), and report format (CSV or TXT) from the drop-down menus.
3. Click "Create scheduled report".

### 9. How can I view and manage my scheduled FBA Customer Returns Reports?

Your scheduled reports will be listed in the "My Schedule" section of the page. You can view and manage them here. If you need to make changes to an existing schedule, delete the current one and create a new one. Note that only one schedule is allowed per report.

### 10. When will the scheduled FBA Customer Returns Report be generated?

The scheduled report will be generated around midnight in the time zone of your store, based on the selected frequency. The report will appear in the "Scheduled Reports" section, and you can keep up to 30 reports in that section, arranged chronologically.

### 11. How can I ensure that I don’t miss important customer return data from my scheduled reports?

Ensure that your report schedule is set to a frequency that matches your needs (e.g., daily, weekly) and that the start date is in the future. Check the "Scheduled Reports" section regularly to review newly generated reports.

### 12. How do I delete or update a scheduled FBA Customer Returns Report?

To update your schedule, you need to delete the existing scheduled report and create a new one. Navigate to "My Schedule" under the FBA Customer Returns page, and click Delete next to the existing schedule. After that, follow the same steps to create a new schedule.
