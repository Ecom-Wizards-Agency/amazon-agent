## **Feed Processing Error Code - 5665**

**Who is this for :** This is for All individuals who are encountering 5665 errors.

**Objective :** To ensure that all are able to address the 5665 error whenever it appears in the processing report.

**Error Code: 5665**

**Error Message:**

- This type of error usually appears when the brand name you have entered on your listing has not been approved by Amazon yet.

FAQs

**1. What does Error Code: 5665 mean?**

- Error Code: 5665 appears when the brand name entered in the listing has not been approved by Amazon. This occurs if the brand is not yet registered with Amazon’s Brand Registry or if additional verification is required.

**2. Why does Amazon reject unapproved brand names?**

- Amazon enforces brand approval to maintain brand integrity and prevent unauthorized sellers from listing products under established brand names. This policy protects both brands and customers.

**3. How can I resolve Error Code: 5665?**

- To resolve this error:

  - Remove the brand name from the brand_name field in the flat file.
  - Leave the brand_name field blank.
  - Re-upload the corrected file through Seller Central.

**4. What should I do if the listing upload fails after removing the brand name?**

- If the issue persists:

  - Confirm that no other fields reference the unapproved brand name.
  - Ensure the flat file format adheres to the category template.
  - Check the error report for additional details.

**5. How can I get my brand name approved by Amazon?**

- To gain approval for your brand:

  - Enroll your brand in Amazon’s Brand Registry.
  - Submit the required documentation (e.g., trademark certificate, brand logo).
  - Wait for Amazon’s approval before reattempting the listing upload with the brand name.

**6. Can I still list products without an approved brand name?**

- Yes, you can list the products by leaving the brand_name field blank. However, doing so may affect how your product appears in search results and could limit branding opportunities.

**7. Will leaving the brand name blank affect my product listing?**

- Leaving the brand name blank means your product will not display a specific brand name. This may reduce visibility and customer trust for branded items. Resolving the brand approval issue is recommended for optimal results.

**8. How can I confirm if my listing was successfully uploaded?**

- After re-uploading the corrected flat file:

  - Check the upload status in Seller Central.
  - Review the processing report for any additional errors.
  - Verify that the listing appears in your inventory.

**9. What happens if the unapproved brand name remains in the flat file?**

- The listing upload will fail, and Error Code: 5665 will continue to appear in the processing report. Ensure that the brand_name field is blank before re-uploading.

**10. Can this error occur with other fields referencing the brand name?**

- Yes, if the unapproved brand name appears in other fields (e.g., manufacturer, description, or title), the upload may fail. Remove all references to the unapproved brand name to avoid additional errors.

**11. How long does it take for Amazon to approve a brand name?**

- The approval process may vary depending on the documentation submitted and Amazon’s review timeline. Typically, it takes 2–10 business days after enrolling in Brand Registry.

**12. What if my brand is already approved but I still get this error?**

- If your brand is approved in Brand Registry but you encounter Error Code: 5665:

  - Verify that the brand name in the flat file matches the registered brand name exactly (case-sensitive).
  - Check for typos or inconsistencies in the brand_name field.
  - Contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance if the issue persists.

**13. Are there specific product categories more prone to this error?**

- This error can occur across all categories, especially for new brands or products being listed for the first time. Categories requiring strict compliance, such as electronics or beauty, may experience this error more frequently.

**14. Can I escalate the issue to Seller Support?**

- Yes, if you’re unable to resolve Error Code: 5665, file a case with Amazon Seller Support. Provide the error details, flat file, and evidence of your brand’s registration for faster resolution.
