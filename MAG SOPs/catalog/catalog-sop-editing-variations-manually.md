# Catalog SOP: Editing Variations Manually

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-editing-variations-manually

## Editing Variations Manually

**Who is this for :** Individuals who want to manually update the variations of their listings.

**Objective:** To update product information and attributes, such as size, color, and style, for each product variation on Amazon, in order to enhance the customer's shopping experience, improve search results, and increase sales through consistent information, optimized listings, and accurate product descriptions and images.

**Instructions:**

Note: When making changes to variations, we are referring to the specific attributes of the product, such as size, color, and material.

1. To make manual changes to variations, go to the “Inventory” tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select “Manage All Inventory.”

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682086310214.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Vnlimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Vnlimage.png)

2. Click the arrow next to the parent ASIN to view its variations.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-10-02%20at%203.08.35%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/u07image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/u07image.png)

3. Select the “Edit” button to the right of the variation you wish to modify.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682087067366.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/JVIimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/JVIimage.png)

4. Proceed to modify the desired fields by navigating through the tabs at the top of the page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682087422747.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Pliimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Pliimage.png)

5. Note that some fields may not be editable for child ASINs, as they are linked to the parent ASIN.

6. If a field cannot be edited in the variation table, you will need to click on the child ASIN and make the changes in the Product Listing Edit Page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682091776025.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/nGyimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/nGyimage.png)

7. Changing the UPC may not allow you to modify the variation (e.g. color or size), so it is advisable to use the ASIN instead.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682091968730.png)

8. The Variation Theme cannot be manually altered.

9. After making changes, click “Save and Finish” at the bottom of the page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682092466550.png)

10. If you encounter issues, contact Seller Support. You may refer to this SOP: [How to file a ticket in Seller Central for reference](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-file-a-ticket-in-seller-central).

FAQs

#### 1. What are variations, and why should I update them?

Variations refer to product attributes such as size, color, material, or style, which are linked to a parent ASIN. Updating variations helps enhance the shopping experience, improve search results, and increase sales by providing consistent and accurate product information and optimized listings.

#### 2. How do I edit variations on Amazon Seller Central?

To edit product variations manually:

1. Go to the "Inventory" tab in Seller Central and select "Manage All Inventory."
2. Click the arrow next to the parent ASIN to reveal the variations.
3. Select the "Edit" button next to the variation you wish to modify.
4. Make changes to the required fields by navigating through the tabs at the top of the page.

#### 3. Can I edit all variation fields?

Not all fields can be edited directly in the variation table. Some fields, such as size, color, and style, can be edited, but other fields may need to be updated via the Product Listing Edit Page.

#### 4. What should I do if I can’t edit a field in the variation table?

If a field cannot be edited in the variation table, click on the child ASIN to make changes in the Product Listing Edit Page.

#### 5. Can I change the UPC for a variation?

Changing the UPC may prevent you from modifying the variation (e.g., color or size). It’s recommended to use the ASIN instead of the UPC to make changes to variations.

#### 6. Can I change the Variation Theme manually?

No, the Variation Theme cannot be manually altered. The variation theme (such as size, color, or style) is pre-defined and cannot be changed directly in Seller Central.

#### 7. What do I do after editing variations?

After making the desired changes, click "Save and Finish" at the bottom of the page to save your updates.

#### 8. What should I do if I encounter issues while editing variations?

If you encounter issues, contact Seller Support for assistance. You can refer to this SOP: How to File a Ticket in Seller Central for further help.
