# Catalog SOP: Managing Listing Enhancements

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-managing-listing-enhancements

## **Managing Listing Enhancements**

**Who is this for :** This is intended for individuals who aim to enhance the search and browsing experience for customers on Amazon by providing specific product details.

**Objectives:**

- To enhance the visibility and effectiveness of your products by verifying the accuracy and comprehensiveness of your listing information.
- Utilizing the Listing Enhancements tool to improve product discoverability and conversion rates.

**Overview:** The term "Listing Enhancements" refers to information that is missing from your product listings, which can negatively impact performance. Examples of missing information include product descriptions, bullet features, and proper images.

**Instructions:**

1. To manage your Listing Enhancements, navigate to the "Manage Inventory" page in Seller Central by selecting "Manage All Inventory" from the Menu options: Inventory > Manage All Inventory Inventory.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.14.02%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YHFimage.png)

2. On the following page, click the "Listing Enhancements" in the horizontal menu link located at the top of your Seller Central page.

**REMINDER:** Please take note that the visibility of the Listing Enhancements feature is visible only upon the presence of identified enhancement opportunities. If there are no enhancement opportunities available, you will not be able to see this link.

3. Once you are on the page, you will see a table displaying all of your SKUs and the enhancements required for each one.If possible, add the missing information to the "Enhancement" field.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.14.12%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6hsimage.png)

5. If not, click the "Edit" button to the right of the SKU to access the product listing edit page, where you can add the necessary information.

6. SKUs requiring information will be highlighted by an exclamation mark within a triangle.

7. You can also filter your listings by issue type using the "Refine by" block on the left-hand side of the SKU table.

8. Be sure to click the "Save All" button at the top right of the SKU table to save any changes you make.

**NOTE:** Any irrelevant or suggested options will not appear in the top menu.

FAQs

1. What is the "Listing Enhancements" feature, and why is it important?

Listing Enhancements refers to missing or incomplete information in your product listings, which can negatively affect their performance on Amazon. These enhancements include details like product descriptions, bullet points, and images. Completing these fields can improve your listing's visibility, customer engagement, and conversion rates.

2. How do I access the Listing Enhancements page?

To access the Listing Enhancements page:

1. Go to Inventory > Manage All Inventory in Seller Central.
2. If there are enhancement opportunities, you will see a "Listing Enhancements" link at the top of the page. Click on it to view your SKUs that need updates.

3. What if I don’t see the Listing Enhancements link?

The Listing Enhancements link will only appear if there are identified opportunities to improve your listings. If no issues are found, the link will not be visible.

4. What should I do if a SKU requires enhancements?

If a SKU requires enhancements, it will be displayed in the table with missing or incomplete details. You can either:

- Add missing information directly in the "Enhancement" field if available.
- Click "Edit" next to the SKU to access the product listing edit page and make the necessary updates.

5. What does it mean if an SKU is highlighted with an exclamation mark?

An SKU highlighted with an exclamation mark inside a triangle indicates that it requires your attention and has missing or incomplete information that needs to be updated for better listing performance.

6. How can I filter my SKUs by issue type?

You can filter your listings by issue type using the "Refine by" block on the left-hand side of the SKU table. This allows you to focus on specific types of enhancements, such as missing descriptions, images, or bullet points.

7. How do I save changes to my listings?

After making the necessary enhancements, make sure to click the "Save All" button at the top right of the SKU table to save your changes.

8. What if the suggestions in Listing Enhancements are not relevant to my product?

If you see irrelevant suggestions, those options will not appear in the top menu, ensuring that only applicable enhancements are listed. If you believe a suggested enhancement is irrelevant, you can skip or ignore it.

9. How long does it take for changes made in Listing Enhancements to take effect?

Changes made in Listing Enhancements are usually reflected within 24–48 hours, depending on Amazon’s processing time. However, it’s always best to check after a day or two to verify that the updates were applied correctly.

10. Can I use Listing Enhancements to improve product discoverability?

Yes, by adding missing details (like descriptions, bullet points, and images), you improve the discoverability of your product on Amazon, making it more likely to show up in relevant searches. Well-enhanced listings tend to have better visibility and customer engagement, which can drive more sales.
