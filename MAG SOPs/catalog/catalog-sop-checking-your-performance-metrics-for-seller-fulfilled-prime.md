## **Checking Your Performance Metrics for Seller-Fulfilled Prime**

**Who is this for :** This is for sellers who have activated Seller-Fulfilled Prime and want to review their performance metrics on Seller Central.

**Objective :** This provides instructions on how to check your performance metrics for Seller-Fulfilled Prime on Seller Central.

1. To review your Performance Metrics for Seller Fulfilled Prime, go to the "Performance" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Account Health" from the drop-down menu.

FAQs

1. How do I access my Seller-Fulfilled Prime performance metrics?

To check your Seller-Fulfilled Prime performance metrics:

1. Go to the "Performance" tab in Seller Central.
2. Select "Account Health" from the drop-down menu.
3. Navigate to the "Eligibilities" tab at the top.
4. Scroll down to the "Premium Shipping Eligibility" section and click the "Go to the Premium Shipping Eligibility Page" button.
5. In the new window, click the link under the "Seller Fulfilled Prime Performance" section.

2. What will I see on the Program Eligibility Scorecard?

The Program Eligibility Scorecard shows an overview of your eligibility for Seller Fulfilled Prime, including your performance metrics, such as on-time shipping rate, cancellation rate, and other relevant performance indicators.

3. What other metrics are included in the performance review?

In addition to the Program Eligibility Scorecard, you will also see:

- Delivery Speed Metrics: Showing how fast your Prime orders are delivered to customers.
- Delivery Performance Metrics: Displaying the performance of your delivery process and whether you are meeting the required service levels.

4. What does the "Eligibilities" tab show?

The "Eligibilities" tab shows the status of your Seller-Fulfilled Prime eligibility. Here, you can track whether you are meeting the necessary requirements to keep your Prime status.

5. How can I use these metrics to improve my performance?

Review these metrics regularly to ensure you meet the key performance requirements, such as:

- On-time shipment rate of 99% or higher.
- A cancellation rate below 0.5%.
- Using Buy Shipping Services for at least 98% of orders.

You can make adjustments to your fulfillment process, shipping times, and inventory management to improve these scores.

6. How often should I check my performance metrics?

It’s a good practice to check your performance metrics regularly (at least once a week) to stay on top of your Seller-Fulfilled Prime eligibility and identify any issues before they impact your status.
