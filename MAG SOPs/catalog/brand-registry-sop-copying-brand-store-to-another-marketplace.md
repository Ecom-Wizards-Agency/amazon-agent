## Copying Brand Store to Another Marketplace

**Who is this for :**

Individuals who want to copy their brand store from one marketplace to another.

**Objective:**

This document aims to guide sellers in creating stores in multiple marketplaces.

An Amazon Brand Store allows merchants to set up an online storefront for their brands for free. In this example, we are going to duplicate the brand store that's already built in the US marketplace to CA.

*Note: To create a brand store on Amazon, it is essential to either register your brand through Amazon Brand Registry or acquire brand rights if you are a registered reseller.

**Copy the Brand store for another Marketplace**

In this part, you're going to copy the brand store from another marketplace using image assets and duplicating the format/pattern/store settings.

1. Open the marketplace where you’ll duplicate the brand store. 2. Do the same process on how to open the store. 3. Once open, you can now create or modify the store. Including the store settings/pages/sub-pages/sections/tile

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680803675911.png)

4. If you want to add a page just click “Add a page” 5. Then the settings will appear, such as:

- The page title(required)
- Page meta (required)
- For the template, just duplicate the format from the brand store you’re duplicating.
- Then click Create a page.

6. You can also create a sub-page just click the page you created then click “Add page” so it will create a sub-page under that. 7. Inside the page and sub-page it has a section, that’s where you can add images and products using tiles. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop_-copy-brand-store---one-marketplace-to-another-image-0kxmi4sa.png)

- From there you can choose what tile type you want.
- Just copy what’s the format or tile on the brand store you’re duplicating.

FAQs

**1. What are the prerequisites for copying a brand store to another marketplace?**

Before copying a brand store, ensure that:

- Your brand is registered through **Amazon Brand Registry** or you have acquired **brand rights** as a registered reseller.
- You have **Storefront access** in the target marketplace.
- The **target marketplace supports Amazon Stores**, as some regions may have restrictions.

**2. Can I copy my brand store to any marketplace?**

No, you can only copy a brand store to **marketplaces where Amazon Stores are available** and where your brand is registered. Some marketplaces may have different requirements or limitations.

**3. Will my store design and assets automatically transfer to the new marketplace?**

No, the transfer is **not automatic**. You must manually **retrieve creative assets**, **duplicate the store layout**, and **rebuild the pages and sub-pages** following the existing format.

**4. Where can I find the images and assets from my original store?**

You can find all image assets in **Creative Assets** under **Stores > Manage Stores** in Seller Central. This ensures high-quality images instead of downloading them from the live store, which may reduce quality.

**5. How do I ensure that the new brand store matches the original store’s layout?**

- Open the original brand store and **note the store settings, pages, sub-pages, sections, and tile formats**.
- When creating the store in the new marketplace, follow the same **page structure and design elements**.
- Use the **same images and branding elements** from the Creative Assets library.

**6. Can I modify the brand store while copying it to another marketplace?**

Yes, you can modify elements like **text, images, and layout** to better fit the target marketplace’s audience. However, **major design changes should be approved** by the brand owner to maintain consistency.

**7. How do I add a new page while setting up the brand store in another marketplace?**

- Click **"Add a Page"** and fill in the required fields:

  - **Page Title**
  - **Page Meta Description**
  - **Template (should match the original store format)**
- Click **"Create a Page"** to finalize.

**8. What happens if I leave a tile empty?**

If a tile is left empty (without an image or product), **Amazon will reject the brand store submission**. Always ensure every tile contains relevant content before submitting the store for approval.

**9. Can I bulk-download image assets from Creative Assets?**

Yes, you can bulk-download assets by selecting multiple images and clicking **Download** under the **Request Moderation** dropdown.

**10. How do I check if my duplicated store was successfully published?**

After submitting, regularly check the **Store Status** in Seller Central. If Amazon rejects the store, review the feedback, **fix any issues (such as missing content or policy violations), and resubmit**.
