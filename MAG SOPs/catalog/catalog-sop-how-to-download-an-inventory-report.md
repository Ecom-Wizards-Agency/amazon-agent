# Catalog SOP: How to Download an Inventory Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-download-an-inventory-report

## **How to download an Inventory Report**

**Who is this for :** Applies to all employees involved in the inventory management process using Amazon Seller Central.

**Objective :** This SOP outlines the process for creating an inventory report using Amazon Seller Central to ensure accurate tracking of inventory levels and to inform decision-making about inventory management.

Determine the frequency of inventory reporting. Inventory reports should be generated regularly to ensure that inventory levels are accurately tracked. Determine the frequency of inventory reporting based on the needs of the organization, but at minimum, reports should be generated monthly.

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central. Click on the three-line bar located on the top left side, then select "Reports," and choose "Inventory Reports."

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.56.30%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/AkGimage.png)

2. From the Inventory reports page, select the “Inventory Report” from the “Select Report Type” drop-down menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.56.37%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2mVimage.png)

3. If you wish to customize your report, click the “Customize the columns for this report” link and follow the steps on the next page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.56.45%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6vLimage.png)

4. Click the “Request Report” button to generate your inventory report. The report will appear in the table below with a “Request Submitted” status.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.56.52%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hb7image.png)

5. Once the report is ready, click on the “Download” link and download the report to your computer as needed.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.57.01%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pCjimage.png)

Review the inventory report to ensure that inventory levels are accurate and up-to-date. Utilize the data to make informed decisions regarding inventory management. Based on the findings of the inventory report, update inventory levels and locations as necessary. Repeat this process regularly to maintain accurate inventory tracking and to inform ongoing decisions regarding inventory management.

FAQs

1. What is the purpose of downloading an Inventory Report?

The Inventory Report helps you accurately track inventory levels, ensuring that stock is properly managed and up-to-date. Regular inventory reports inform decision-making about inventory management, enabling timely updates to stock levels and locations.

2. How often should I generate an Inventory Report?

Reports should be generated regularly to ensure accurate inventory tracking. At a minimum, you should generate an inventory report monthly. You may choose to generate it more frequently based on your organization’s needs.

3. How do I access the Inventory Report in Amazon Seller Central?

To download an Inventory Report:

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central.
2. Click on the three-line menu (located at the top left).
3. Select “Reports”, then choose “Inventory Reports” from the menu.

4. How do I choose the report type in Seller Central?

On the Inventory Reports page, use the “Select Report Type” drop-down menu to choose the Inventory Report.

5. Can I customize my Inventory Report?

Yes, you can customize the columns in your report:

1. Click the “Customize the columns for this report” link.
2. Follow the steps on the next page to adjust the columns to your preferences.

6. How do I generate the Inventory Report?

Once you’ve selected and customized the report, click the “Request Report” button. The report will be generated, and the status will show as “Request Submitted” in the table.

7. How do I download the Inventory Report after it’s ready?

Once the report is ready, the status will change to “Download”. Click the “Download” link in the Report Status column to save the report to your computer.

8. What should I do after downloading the Inventory Report?

After downloading the report, review the data to ensure that inventory levels are accurate and up-to-date. Use this data to make informed decisions about inventory management, including updating stock levels and locations.

9. Can I download the report in different formats?

Currently, Amazon Seller Central provides the report in CSV format, which can be opened in Excel or other data management tools.

10. What should I do if I notice discrepancies in my inventory report?

If discrepancies are found, investigate further by checking stock levels, locations, and possible issues like unfulfilled orders, returns, or miscounted inventory. After identifying the cause, update the inventory records as needed to maintain accuracy.

11. Can I automate inventory report downloads?

While Amazon Seller Central doesn’t support fully automated downloads, you can use tools or third-party software to schedule and automate reports if you need to generate them more frequently or have other custom requirements.
