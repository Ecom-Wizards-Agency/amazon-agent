# Catalog SOP: How to Change FBM Shipping Cut-Off Times

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-change-fbm-shipping-cut-off-times

## How to Change FBM Shipping Cut-off Times

**Who is this for :**Intended for the use of employees and management personnel who are responsible for managing the FBM shipping process on Amazon.

**Objective :**To provide clear instructions on how to change FBM (Fulfillment by Merchant) shipping cut-off times and ensure accurate and timely delivery of customer orders.

> **IMPORTANT REMINDER:**
>
> **(Effective July 30, 2025):** Amazon now offers **Premium Shipping** for 1-day and 2-day regional deliveries.
>
> To enable it, sellers must update their shipping template and meet specific performance thresholds.
>
> See [Premium Shipping program policy](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/external/G201503640) for full details.

The Order Fulfillment Settings page allows you to define the operating days, delivery days, and order cut-off times for your products. You will need to review and edit your Order Fulfillment Settings for both Prime and non-Prime products. The settings defined on this page will influence the Delivery Promise shown to customers, as well as the date you are expected to ship your orders.

**Steps on how to change the FBM shipping cut-off times:**

1. In Seller Central, go to Settings and click Shipping Settings.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/how-to-change-fbm-shipping-cut-off-times-image-b2hm94sk.png)

2. Under General Shipping Settings, identify the Order Fulfillment Settings section and click View Locations.

1. Under Locations, click on Edit.

4. On Locations, look for Fulfillment Capabilities and click Edit.

1. Under Order Fulfillment Settings, look for the Operation Settings and select Saturday and/or Sunday operations.

1. Click on Save Changes.

FAQs

#### 1. What are the consequences of changing my FBM shipping cut-off times?

- Changing your FBM shipping cut-off times affects when your customers will receive their orders. It updates the delivery promise shown to customers, ensuring that orders are shipped within the specified timeframe. This helps manage customer expectations and ensures timely delivery based on your updated cut-off times.

#### 2. Can I set different cut-off times for Prime and non-Prime products?

- Yes, you can define separate shipping cut-off times for Prime and non-Prime products within your Order Fulfillment Settings. This allows you to maintain different shipping expectations and timelines based on product eligibility for Prime delivery.

#### 3. Will changing my shipping cut-off times affect my shipping performance metrics?

- Yes, changing your shipping cut-off times can influence your shipping performance metrics. If orders are not shipped on time based on your updated cut-off times, it may impact metrics like Late Shipment Rate, which can affect your account health and customer satisfaction.

#### 4. Can I set operations for weekends (Saturday/Sunday) in my shipping cut-off settings?

- Yes, within your Order Fulfillment Settings, you have the option to select Saturday and/or Sunday as operation days, allowing you to fulfill orders during weekends if applicable. This is especially helpful for maintaining timely deliveries, particularly for customers in urgent need of their products.

#### 5. How can I ensure my cut-off times are accurate for customers in different time zones?

- When setting your FBM shipping cut-off times, ensure that the times reflect your local business hours and align with your fulfillment capabilities. If your operations cover different time zones, consider adjusting your cut-off times accordingly to avoid delays and discrepancies in delivery promises.

#### 6. Is there any impact on my customer’s delivery promise if I change the cut-off time?

- Yes, changing your shipping cut-off time will update the Delivery Promise displayed to your customers. It directly influences the expected shipping and delivery dates, so be mindful of any changes to ensure that customers are given realistic delivery expectations.

#### 7. How often should I review my FBM shipping cut-off times?

- It's a good practice to review and update your FBM shipping cut-off times periodically, especially during busy seasons (e.g., holidays) or if there are any changes to your operational hours or fulfillment capabilities. This ensures that your shipping settings align with your current business operations.
