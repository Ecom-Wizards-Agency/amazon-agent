# Catalog SOP: Download a Referral Fee Preview Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-referral-fee-preview-report

## **Download a Referral Fee Preview Report**

**Who is this for :** It is for individuals or teams actively involved in online selling, enabling them to estimate referral fees based on listed item prices and make informed pricing decisions within the Amazon marketplace.

**Objective :** The objective of this SOP is to guide users in creating a Referral Fee Preview Report that estimates the referral fee based on the listed item price, excluding shipping or gift wrap charges at the time of purchase. By following this SOP, users can assess estimated referral fees for better cost management and informed pricing strategies.

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central. Click on the three-line bar located on the top left side, then select "Reports," and choose "Inventory Reports."

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-VPFPTM94.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/aUiimage.png)

2. On the next page, choose "Referral Fee Preview Report" from the provided drop-down menu for "Select Report Type".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-27%20at%201.25.51%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ed4image.png)

3. Click on the "Request Report" button to generate the report. The report will appear in the table below with a "Request Submitted" status.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-27%20at%201.25.59%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ohoimage.png)

4. Once the report is ready to be downloaded, click on the "Download" link to save the report to your computer as needed.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-27%20at%201.26.06%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

FAQs

1. What is the purpose of the Referral Fee Preview Report?

The Referral Fee Preview Report estimates the referral fees based on the listed item price (excluding shipping and gift wrap charges). It helps sellers assess the estimated referral fees and make informed pricing decisions in the Amazon marketplace.

2. Where can I access the Referral Fee Preview Report in Seller Central?

To access the Referral Fee Preview Report, log in to Amazon Seller Central, click on the three-line menu bar at the top left, select "Reports," and then choose "Inventory Reports" from the options.

3. How do I generate the Referral Fee Preview Report?

1. In the "Inventory Reports" section, select "Referral Fee Preview Report" from the "Select Report Type" drop-down menu.
2. Click on the "Request Report" button to generate the report.

4. What happens after I request the Referral Fee Preview Report?

Once you request the report, the status will initially show as "Request Submitted." It usually takes a few moments for the report to be processed and become available for download.

5. How do I download the Referral Fee Preview Report once it's ready?

Once the report is ready, the status will change to "Ready," and a "Download" link will appear. Click the "Download" link to save the report to your computer.

6. What information is included in the Referral Fee Preview Report?

The report provides estimates of the referral fees for the listed item price. It does not include shipping or gift wrap charges in the fee calculation.

7. Can I use the Referral Fee Preview Report to set my prices?

Yes, the Referral Fee Preview Report helps you understand the estimated fees for your products, allowing you to adjust your pricing strategies accordingly to ensure profitability.

8. How often can I download the Referral Fee Preview Report?

You can download the Referral Fee Preview Report as needed. It is generated in real-time, so you can request and download the report whenever necessary to review your pricing and fee estimates.

9. What file format is the Referral Fee Preview Report available in?

The Referral Fee Preview Report is available in a text format, typically provided as a .txt file. You may need to open it in a text editor or spreadsheet program for better analysis.

10. Why is the Referral Fee Preview Report important for sellers?

This report is essential for sellers who want to understand the estimated referral fees on their products, allowing them to adjust their prices accordingly, optimize profitability, and make better-informed pricing decisions.
