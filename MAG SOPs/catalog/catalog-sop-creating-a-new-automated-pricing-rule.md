## **Creating a New Automated Pricing Rule**

**Who is this for:** For Amazon sellers who are responsible for pricing strategies and operations in a business or organization that seeks to automate its pricing process.

**Objective:** To outline the steps and guidelines for implementing a new automated pricing rule. The automated pricing rule aims to enhance pricing outcomes and increase competitiveness in the market.

The Automate Pricing tool allows you to create rules that automatically adjust your prices for selected listings. Changes can happen instantly without any further action by you once you have automated pricing. However, you remain responsible for your prices.

**To access Automate Pricing tool :**

1. Go to "Pricing" tab and then select "Automate Pricing" in Seller Central.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-J6P51PFT.png)

2. Create a customized pricing rule by clicking the "What type of rule do you want to create" of the page. Also name the rule you want to create. Make it easy to identify.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-10-02%20at%202.28.15%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/IWaimage.png)

3. Choose the marketplace you want to create the rule for.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-10-02%20at%202.29.42%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0Vximage.png)

4. After selecting the correct marketplace, Click the drop down menu under the “Which pricing action do you want Amazon to take” and choose the correct option.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-10-02%20at%202.30.14%E2%80%AFAM.png)

5. Complete all the required action then click on “Save and exit”.

FAQs

1. What is the purpose of creating a new Automated Pricing rule?

Creating an Automated Pricing rule helps you set up pricing strategies that adjust your product prices automatically, ensuring your prices remain competitive in the marketplace. This tool allows you to maintain price changes in real-time, increasing the likelihood of winning the Buy Box without manual intervention.

2. How do I access the Automate Pricing tool?

To access the Automated Pricing tool:

1. Go to the Pricing tab in Seller Central.
2. Select Automate Pricing from the drop-down menu.

3. What is the first step to create a new Automated Pricing rule?

To create a new rule:

1. Click on “What type of rule do you want to create?”
2. Enter a name for the rule to help you easily identify it later.

4. How do I select the marketplace for my Automated Pricing rule?

After entering a rule name, you will be prompted to select the marketplace you want to create the rule for. Choose the relevant marketplace from the available options.

5. How do I choose the pricing action for my rule?

Once you have selected the marketplace:

1. Open the drop-down menu under “Which pricing action do you want Amazon to take?”
2. Choose the appropriate option that aligns with your pricing strategy (e.g., match the Buy Box price, adjust prices based on competitor pricing, etc.).

6. What should I do after selecting the pricing action?

After selecting the desired pricing action, complete all the required fields based on your chosen rule type. Once done, click “Save and exit” to activate the rule.

7. Do I need to get approval before implementing an Automated Pricing rule?

Yes, before implementing an Automated Pricing rule, it’s important to inform and get approval from your client. Include proof of approval (e.g., screenshot or Fireflies call link) in the Task Details or as a comment.

8. How does Automated Pricing help in maintaining price competitiveness?

The Automated Pricing tool ensures that your product prices are consistently updated based on market conditions. This helps maintain competitive pricing, which increases your chances of winning the Buy Box and improving visibility on Amazon.

9. What happens once I have set up an Automated Pricing rule?

Once the Automated Pricing rule is set up, prices will adjust automatically according to the rule you’ve created. There is no need for further action, but it’s your responsibility to regularly monitor pricing performance.
