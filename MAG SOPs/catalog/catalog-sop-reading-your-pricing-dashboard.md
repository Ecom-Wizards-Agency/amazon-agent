## **Reading Your Pricing Dashboard**

**Who is this for :** Intended for individual who are responsible for setting and managing pricing strategies within an organization. It is especially useful for individuals who need to utilize a pricing dashboard as part of their daily activities.

**Objective :** To provide guidance on how to effectively read and interpret a pricing dashboard. It aims to help users understand the key metrics and insights provided by the dashboard, enabling them to make informed pricing decisions and optimize pricing strategies for their products or services.

**The Pricing Dashboard is a feature in Amazon Seller Central designed to help sellers manage and optimize their pricing strategies.**

1. From the top menu of Seller Central, click on the "Pricing" tab. The drop down menu will show "Pricing Health", "Manage Pricing", "Automate Pricing", and "Negotiated Pricing".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/reading-your-pricing-dashboard%201.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/E86image.png)

2. The Dashboard page will display several sections:

- **Pricing Health -** You are responsible for setting your own prices in Amazon’s store. Amazon then decides which (if any) offer to highlight for customers by selecting that offer as the Featured Offer and will not feature offers that do not meet its criteria for a great customer experience. Amazon provides the Pricing Health tool to help you make informed pricing decisions. Pricing Health provides an overview of inactive offers with potential pricing errors, offers that are currently ineligible to become the Featured Offer, offers that are close to the Featured Offer price, and sales conversion opportunities from a pricing point of view.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/reading-your-pricing-dashboard%202.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/cHhimage.png)

- **Manage Pricing -** The Manage Inventory page provides you with tools for searching, viewing, and updating your product and inventory listing information.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/reading-your-pricing-dashboard%203.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fUfimage.png)

- **Automate Pricing -** Automate Pricing allows you to automatically adjust prices on SKUs in your catalog in response to events such as the Featured Offer (Buy Box) price, without having to revisit the SKU every time you want to change your price.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/reading-your-pricing-dashboard%204.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/q7Simage.png)

- **Negotiated Pricing -** it allows Amazon Business sellers to offer pre-negotiated prices to business customers they have already established a business relationship with. In order for business customers to take advantage of negotiated pricing, they must have an Amazon Business account and be able to provide you, the business seller, with their Amazon Business ID. The Amazon Business ID will allow you to add your business customer to your Negotiated pricing customer list. Once added, you can upload and manage your pre-negotiated prices for each business customer on the negotiated pricing page.

FAQs

1. What is the Pricing Dashboard, and how can it help me manage pricing?

The Pricing Dashboard in Amazon Seller Central helps you manage and optimize pricing strategies by providing insights into your pricing performance. It offers tools like Pricing Health, Manage Pricing, Automate Pricing, and Negotiated Pricing, allowing you to monitor and adjust your pricing strategies for better customer engagement and sales.

2. How do I access the Pricing Dashboard?

To access the Pricing Dashboard in Seller Central:

1. Click on the "Pricing" tab in the top menu.
2. From the drop-down menu, select from the available options: Pricing Health, Manage Pricing, Automate Pricing, or Negotiated Pricing.

3. What is Pricing Health, and how does it benefit me?

Pricing Health provides a snapshot of your pricing status, including:

- Inactive offers with potential errors.
- Offers ineligible to become the Featured Offer.
- Offers close to the Featured Offer price.
- Sales conversion opportunities from a pricing perspective.

By using Pricing Health, you can identify areas to optimize your prices, ensuring your products meet Amazon’s criteria and improve visibility.

4. What is the Manage Pricing tool?

Manage Pricing allows you to view, search, and update product listings within your inventory. You can make adjustments to pricing and ensure your listings are competitive in Amazon's marketplace, helping you take action if a pricing error is identified.

5. What does Automate Pricing do, and why should I use it?

Automate Pricing helps you automatically adjust prices for your SKUs based on factors such as the Featured Offer (Buy Box) price. This ensures your prices stay competitive without manual adjustments, allowing you to remain in the Buy Box and improve sales.

6. What is Negotiated Pricing, and who can use it?

Negotiated Pricing allows Amazon Business sellers to offer pre-negotiated prices to business customers. To use this feature:

- Your business customers must have an Amazon Business account and provide their Amazon Business ID.
- You can add the customer to your Negotiated Pricing list and manage the pre-negotiated prices for each customer.

7. How can I make informed pricing decisions using the Pricing Dashboard?

By reviewing the Pricing Health section, you can identify pricing issues, such as offers that are not competitive or ineligible for the Featured Offer. Automating pricing with the Automate Pricing tool can help you stay competitive, while Negotiated Pricing ensures that you offer the best deals to business customers.

8. What are the steps to update my pricing using the Pricing Dashboard?

1. Access the Pricing Dashboard from the "Pricing" tab in Seller Central.
2. Review the Pricing Health section to identify any issues or opportunities.
3. Use the Manage Pricing tool to update prices for specific SKUs.
4. If you want to automate price changes, use the Automate Pricing feature.
5. For business customers, set up Negotiated Pricing to provide special pricing to your Amazon Business customers.

9. How do I set up Automated Pricing for my listings?

To set up Automate Pricing:

1. Go to the Pricing Dashboard and select Automate Pricing.
2. Choose the rules that suit your pricing strategy (e.g., adjusting to the Buy Box price).
3. Select the SKUs you want to automate and save your settings. Your prices will now adjust automatically based on the criteria you set.

10. What should I do if I don't see any opportunities in Pricing Health?

If you don’t see any identified opportunities in Pricing Health, your pricing might already be optimized. Keep an eye on it regularly to ensure that your pricing stays competitive and that you don’t miss future opportunities for improvement.
