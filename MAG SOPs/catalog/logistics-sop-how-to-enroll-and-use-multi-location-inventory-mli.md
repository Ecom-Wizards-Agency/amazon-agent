# Logistics SOP: How to Enroll and Use Multi-Location Inventory (MLI)

## How to Enroll and Use Multi-Location Inventory (MLI)

**Who is this for**?
This SOP is for sellers with multiple fulfillment locations who wish to use Multi-Location Inventory (MLI) to streamline inventory management and improve shipping accuracy.

**Objective**: The objective of this SOP is to provide sellers with a clear, step-by-step guide on how to integrate and start using Multi-Location Inventory (MLI) in Seller Central. This process will help sellers synchronize inventory across multiple locations in real time, improve the accuracy of delivery promises, and simplify order fulfillment.

**Enrollment and Account Preparation**

**Step 1**: **Enroll via the**[**Automated Onboarding Tool**](https://amazonexteu.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_4IY1OG3g51fE3L8)

- Access the

  MLI automated onboarding tool

  and follow the prompts to enroll your account.

**Step 2**: **Complete the Multi-Location Inventory Sign-up Form**

- Fill out the sign-up form to initiate the preparation of your selling account to use MLI.
- You will be notified within 15 business days when your account is ready to start integrating with MLI.

**Setting Up Locations and Synchronizing Inventory**

**Step 1**: **Create Locations (Supply Sources)**

- Follow the instructions provided in the

  MLI API integration documentation

  to create locations (also referred to as Supply Sources).
- These locations represent your fulfillment centers or inventory storage points.

**Step 2**: **Synchronize Inventory by Location**

- Once locations are created, synchronize your inventory per location using the MLI integration setup.
- You can sync inventory for as many ASINs as you wish, ensuring your stock levels are accurately reflected at each location.

**Step 3**: **Review MLI Integration Instructions**

- Access the

  MLI API integration instructions

  to learn how to configure locations and sync inventory.

  Save Template

**Assigning ASINs to Shipping Templates**

**Step 1**: **Assign MLI SKUs to Shipping Template**

- Once you’ve created and saved your shipping template with SSA enabled, assign your MLI SKUs to the template.
- You can add ASINs synced with MLI as well as those not synced with MLI to the same shipping template, but all locations in the template should match your inventory locations.

**Step 2**: **Ensure SSA Is Enabled**

- If SSA is disabled on a shipping template with ASINs synced to MLI, delivery promises will revert to manual estimation, and you won’t benefit from MLI’s real-time inventory updates and automated delivery promises.

**Finalizing Setup and Integration**

**Step 1**: **Review Integration with MLI**

- Double-check that all ASINs are correctly synced with MLI and that your inventory settings are properly configured for each location.

**Step 2**: **Use MLI for Seller Fulfilled Prime**

- MLI can be used with

  Seller Fulfilled Prime

  for as many shipping templates as you need. Ensure your MLI-enabled SKUs are assigned to the appropriate templates.

**Step 3**: **Confirm Multi-Channel Integrator (Optional)**

- For multi-channel integrations, you can continue to add ASINs synced with MLI through a Multi-Channel Integrator or via SP-API, Feeds API, or Listing Loader feed file upload.

FAQs

1. **Who can participate?**

  MLI is available for sellers with multiple locations who provide their inventory via SP-API, Feeds API, Seller Central Template Upload, or using a third-party integrator.
2. **Do I need to use Multi-Location Inventory for all of my ASINs?**

  No, you can choose which ASINs to add. However, to fully benefit from MLI, it’s recommended to add all of your ASINs.
3. **Is there a limit to the number of locations I can use with MLI?**

  Yes, currently, Shipping Templates are limited to 10 locations.
4. **Do I need to put my ASINs synced with MLI on a separate Shipping Template?**

  No, you can use the same Shipping Template for both MLI-synced ASINs and non-MLI ASINs, as long as the template is enabled with Shipping Settings Automation (SSA) and includes all your inventory locations.
5. **Do I need to have all of my MLI inventory on the same Shipping Template?**

  No, you can have your MLI inventory on multiple Shipping Templates if needed.
6. **Can I use MLI for Seller Fulfilled Prime?**

  Yes, MLI can be used with Seller Fulfilled Prime.
7. **What happens if I accidentally disable SSA on a Shipping Template that has ASINs synced with MLI?**

  Customers can still place orders, but the delivery promise will be based on your manual estimation, and you won’t benefit from MLI or SSA features.
8. **Where can I receive support?**

  For support, contact the MLI team at **multilocationteam@amazon.com**.
