# Catalog SOP: Editing an Enrolled Vine Product on Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-editing-an-enrolled-vine-product-on-vendor-central

## **Editing an Enrolled Vine Product on Vendor Central**

**Who is this for :** All individuals who want to edit current Amazon Vendor Central Vine enrollment.

**Objective:** To be able to successfully edit the current Amazon Vendor Central Vine enrollment.

**To modify a product enrolled in the Vine Program, follow these steps:**

1. Go to Vendor Central and click on the "**Merchandising**" tab in the top menu and Select "**Amazon Vine**" from the dropdown menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-10-16%20at%2011.51.45%E2%80%AFPM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gV0image.png)
2. Click on the "Scheduled" link to view the list of products enrolled in Vine, along with their enrollment and fulfillment center status.
3. Find the product you wish to modify and click on the "**Edit Enrollments**" option from the "**Actions**" column dropdown menu.
4. Make any necessary changes and click on the "**Save Changes**" button.
5. You can modify your Vine product before it is delivered to an Amazon fulfillment center and added to the Vine Voices queue.
6. However, once your Vine product is in the Vine Voices queue, you cannot modify it, though you may be able to cancel it.

FAQs

**1. What can I edit in an enrolled Vine product?**

- Before the product is shipped to an Amazon fulfillment center, you can edit:

  - The number of units enrolled
  - Product details (title, description, images)
  - Billing vendor code
  - Once the product is in the Vine Voices queue, no further edits can be made.

**2. Why would I need to edit my Vine enrollment?**

- You may need to edit an enrolled Vine product if:

  - There was an error in the product details.
  - You want to increase or decrease the number of units available.
  - The wrong ASIN was enrolled.
  - You need to update images or descriptions for better clarity.

**3. How long do edits take to apply after saving changes?**

- Edits usually take a few hours to 24 hours to reflect in Vendor Central. If changes are not showing after 24 hours, clear cache or try a different browser, and if the issue persists, contact Vendor Support.

**4. What if I can’t edit my Vine product?**

- If the "Edit Enrollments" option is missing or grayed out, it means:

  - The product has already been shipped to an Amazon fulfillment center.
  - The product is in the Vine Voices queue and can no longer be modified.
  - The enrollment status has changed, restricting edits.
  - At this point, you may be able to cancel the enrollment (if allowed).

**5. How do I cancel an enrollment if editing is not possible?**

- Go to Vendor Central > Merchandising > Amazon Vine.

  - Click on Scheduled to find the enrolled ASIN.
  - If cancellation is available, select Cancel Enrollment under the "Actions" column.
  - If the cancellation option is unavailable, the product is already in the Vine Voices queue and cannot be withdrawn.

**6. Does editing an enrollment affect the review timeline?**

- If the product has not yet reached a fulfillment center, changes should not impact the timeline.
- If a product has already been claimed by Vine Voices, editing is no longer possible, and reviews will continue as scheduled.

**7. How can I confirm that my edits were successfully applied?**

- Go back to Vendor Central > Merchandising > Amazon Vine > Scheduled.
- Find the ASIN and check if the updated details are reflected.
- If the changes are missing after 24 hours, retry editing or contact Vendor Support.

**8. What if I need to change a Vine product but can’t edit or cancel it?**

- If modifications are needed but the enrollment is locked:

  - Create a new Vine enrollment with the correct details.
  - If the incorrect enrollment is causing issues, contact Vendor Support for assistance.
