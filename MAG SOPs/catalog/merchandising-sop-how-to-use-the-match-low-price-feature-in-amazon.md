# Merchandising SOP: How to Use the Match Low Price Feature in Amazon

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/merchandising-sop-how-to-use-the-match-low-price-feature-in-amazon

## **How to Use the Match Low Price Feature in Amazon**

**Who is this for?**This SOP is for our team members who are responsible for managing and updating product pricing using the Match Low Price feature on Amazon.

**Objective**: This SOP outlines the steps to apply the "Match Low Price" feature on Amazon, enabling sellers to quickly and efficiently adjust their prices to match the current lowest price available for a product, ensuring competitive pricing.

**I. Apply Match Low Price to Single Listings**

**Step 1**: **Navigate to Manage Pricing**

- From the **Pricing** menu, select **Manage Pricing**.

**Step 2**: **Select the Listing to Match Price**

- Locate the listing you want to adjust.
- In the **Lowest Price + Shipping** column, click **Match Price** for the listing, or alternatively, click the **Match Low Price** button.

**Step 3**: **Apply the Price Change**

- The price in the **Price + Shipping** column will automatically update to match the lowest price.
- Click the **Save** button to complete the price update.

**Step 4**: **Reverting the Change (If Needed)**

- To revert the change, manually adjust the price in the **Price + Shipping** box.
- Click **Save** to finalize the revert.

**II. Apply Match Low Price to Multiple Listings**

**Step 1**: **Navigate to Manage All Inventory**

- From the **Inventory** menu, select **Manage All Inventory**.

**Step 2**: **Select Multiple Listings**

- Optionally, use the **Search tool** or **Filters** to narrow down the listings to apply the match.
- Click the checkbox in the heading row to select all listings on the current page.
- Review and deselect any listings you do not wish to apply the match to.

**Step 3**: **Match the Prices**

- Click the **Action on [number] selected** button above the inventory table.
- From the drop-down menu, select **Match Low Price**.
- A confirmation window will appear with the selected listings.
- Click **Yes, continue**.

**Step 4**: **Apply the Changes**

- The price in the **Price + Shipping** column will update to match the lowest price for each selected listing.
- Click **Save all** to apply the price changes.

**Step 5**: **Reverting Changes**

- To revert changes for individual listings, manually adjust the price and click **Save** for each listing.
- For bulk price adjustments, use inventory files.

**III. Troubleshoot Match Low Price**

If the **Match Low Price** feature is unavailable, consider the following troubleshooting steps:

- **Shipping Charge is Equal or Greater than Lowest Price**:
  If your shipping charge equals or exceeds the lowest price, you cannot match the price. Adjust your shipping charges accordingly.
- **Active Sale Event**:
  The Match Low Price feature does not apply to listings with an active sale. You must remove the sale or wait for it to end before applying the match.
- **Inactive Listings or Missing Shipping Charges**:
  Listings with no active status or no shipping charges will not be eligible for price matching.
- **Shipping Settings Affecting Price Comparison**:
  If you’ve configured shipping settings to hide the shipping charge or use price bands, Match Low Price will only consider the item price and not the total price including shipping.

**IV. How Match Low Price Works**

**Example Scenario**:
You have a product listed in "Used-Like New" condition, with a seller-fulfilled price of $26.00 + $5.50 shipping. There are three other listings with varying prices:

| Listing | Condition | Sub-condition | Fulfillment Method | Item Price + Shipping | Total Price |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | New | -- | FBA | $28.00 + $1.00 | $29.00 |
| 2 | Used | Like New | Seller | $26.00 + $5.50 | $31.50 |
| 3 | Used | Very Good | FBA | $24.00 + $5.50 | $29.50 |
| 4 | Used | Like New | Seller | $25.00 + $5.00 | $30.00 |

- If **Low Price Comparison preferences** are set to compare all conditions and fulfillment methods, **Listing 1 ($29.00)** will be selected as the lowest price.
- If set to compare only the same condition and fulfillment method, **Listing 3 ($29.50)** will be selected.
- If the comparison is restricted to the same sub-condition and fulfillment method, **Listing 4 ($30.00)** will be selected.

**Important Notes**

- Ensure all changes are tracked and saved properly.
- Regularly check for any price adjustments or competitor changes to maintain competitive pricing.
- If there are discrepancies in pricing, ensure that shipping charges, sales events, and other factors are accounted for.
