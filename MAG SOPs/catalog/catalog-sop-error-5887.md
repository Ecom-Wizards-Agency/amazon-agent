# Catalog SOP: Error 5887

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-error-5887

## **Handling 5887 error**

**Who is this for :** All individuals who are encountering 5887 errors.

**Objective :** To ensure that all are equipped to resolve error code 5887 and they need to understand how to troubleshoot it.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PrWimage.png)

**Description:**

Error 5887 means that you are uploading a generic product. It doesn't have a brand name, you may not have control over the brand or it could also mean that it doesn't have an identifier UPC, EAN, or GTIN exemption type.

If you have a 5887 error on a specific column attribute on a flat file upload, you can often just delete the column of that attribute and re-submit and get a successful upload.

**Instructions:**

1. Try uploading with the brand name field blank

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1edimage.png)
2. Delete the brand name field column and try uploading

FAQs

1. What does error 5887 mean?

Error 5887 occurs when uploading a generic product without a brand name, or when the product lacks a valid identifier such as UPC, EAN, or GTIN exemption. It may also happen if the seller does not have control over the brand.

2. How can I resolve error 5887?

Try the following troubleshooting steps:

- Leave the brand name field blank and re-upload.
- Delete the brand name column in your flat file and submit again.
- Check if the ASIN qualifies for GTIN exemption and ensure it's correctly applied.
- If you have a brand name but no trademark (TM), try uploading with that name in the brand field.

3. Can I list a product under a brand without a registered trademark?

Yes, but you may encounter brand-related errors. If Amazon does not recognize the brand, you might need to request GTIN exemption or use the generic brand field.

4. How do I check if my ASIN qualifies for GTIN exemption?

Go to Seller Central > Inventory > Add a Product > Check for GTIN Exemption and verify if your category and brand are eligible.

5. What if I still can’t upload the product after trying these fixes?

- Check for listing restrictions in your category.
- Ensure the product does not require a UPC, EAN, or GTIN.
- Contact Amazon Seller Support for further assistance if the error persists.
