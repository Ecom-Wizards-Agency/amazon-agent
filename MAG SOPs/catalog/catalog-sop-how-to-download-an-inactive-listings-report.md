# Catalog SOP: How to Download an Inactive Listings Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-download-an-inactive-listings-report

## **How to download an Inactive Listings Report**

**Who is this for :** An Amazon seller may request an Inactive Listings Report to gain a comprehensive list of their inactive listings, which can help them to manage their inventory, analyze sales performance, and meet Amazon's requirements.

**Objective :** To help Amazon sellers manage their inventory, analyze sales performance, meet Amazon's requirements, and optimize their product listings.

**Instructions**

1. Access the “Report” tab from the top menu of Seller Central and select “Inventory Reports” from the selection Menu.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/wu9image.png)

2. On the next page, select “Inactive Listings Report” from the “Select Report Type” Drop-Down.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/isIimage.png)

3. If you wish to customize your report, click the “Customize the columns for this report” link and follow the steps on the next page.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YE7image.png)

4. You can customize the report and just select the attributes that you want or you need. Click “Save” once done with the customization.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/N4mimage.png)

5. Return to your Inventory Reports page by clicking the “Inventory Reports” link at the top left of the page. Click the “Request Report” button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oAtimage.png)

6. The report will appear in the table below, with a “Request Submitted” status. It usually takes only a minute or two to have the report ready.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rcyimage.png)

7. Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to “Ready,” and a “Download” link will appear. Click the “Download” link to download the report.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/uX9image.png)

8. The report will be available in text format.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YHIimage.png)

9. It is advisable to open the report via Excel for easy audit. Sample of Inactive Listings Report below is in Excel format.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ijhimage.png)

FAQs

1. What is the Inactive Listings Report, and why is it important?

The Inactive Listings Report provides a comprehensive list of listings that are inactive on Amazon. This can include products that are temporarily unavailable, listings that have been suppressed, or those that are out of stock. The report helps sellers manage their inventory, analyze sales performance, meet Amazon's requirements, and optimize their product listings to avoid potential issues.

2. How do I access the Inactive Listings Report in Seller Central?

To download the Inactive Listings Report:

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central.
2. Click on the “Reports” tab in the top menu.
3. Select “Inventory Reports” from the drop-down menu.

3. How do I select the Inactive Listings Report type?

On the Inventory Reports page:

1. In the “Select Report Type” drop-down menu, choose “Inactive Listings Report”.

4. Can I customize the Inactive Listings Report?

Yes, you can customize the report to include only the relevant attributes:

1. Click the “Customize the columns for this report” link.
2. Follow the instructions on the next page to select the columns you need.
3. Once done with customization, click “Save” to save your settings.

5. How do I generate the Inactive Listings Report?

After selecting and customizing the report:

1. Click on the “Request Report” button to generate the report.

6. How long does it take for the Inactive Listings Report to be ready?

The report typically takes just a minute or two to generate, and the status will show as “Request Submitted” during this time.

7. How do I download the Inactive Listings Report once it’s ready?

Once the report is ready:

1. The status will change to “Ready”.
2. A “Download” link will appear.
3. Click the “Download” link to save the report to your computer.

8. In what format will the Inactive Listings Report be available?

The Inactive Listings Report is available in text format. It’s recommended to open it in Excel for easier auditing and analysis.

9. Why should I open the Inactive Listings Report in Excel?

Opening the Inactive Listings Report in Excel makes it easier to audit, filter, and sort the data. Excel allows you to quickly identify issues, track inactive listings, and take action to either reactivate listings or adjust inventory management strategies.

10. What actions should I take after downloading the Inactive Listings Report?

Once you have the report:

- Review the inactive listings to understand why they are inactive (e.g., out of stock, suppressed, or policy violations).
- Take appropriate actions to update or reactivate listings, fix any issues, and optimize your product offerings.
- Regularly download and analyze this report to stay on top of your listings’ statuses.

11. Can I automate the download of the Inactive Listings Report?

Currently, Amazon Seller Central does not offer an option to automatically schedule the Inactive Listings Report. You will need to manually request and download it as needed.
