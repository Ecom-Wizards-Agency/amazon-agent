# Catalog SOP: Managing Pricing

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-managing-pricing

## **Managing Pricing**

**Who is this for?**

All individuals who need to update their Pricing.

**Objective:**

To adjust your prices quickly and automatically against your competition to increase your chance of becoming the Featured Offer.

**Prior any actual price change implementation, it is important to inform and get client’s approval. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.**

**To Manage Pricing for your listings on Amazon Seller Central, follow these steps:**

1. Click the Menu icon on the upper left side to access “Manage Pricing".

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/TvBimage.png)

2. Click Pricing → Manage Pricing

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oHEimage.png)

3. On the next page, you can view all your SKUs and their pricing, which includes the “Price + Shipping”, “Business Price + Shipping” (for B2B sellers), “Lowest Price + Shipping” (the lowest price currently on Amazon), and “Buy Box Price + Shipping” (the current winning price for the Buy Box).

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VfCimage.png)

4. Click on any blue text to obtain additional information, such as the “Calculate revenue” that provides a breakdown of your net proceeds for that SKU.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6VUimage.png)

5. Take various actions from this page, such as matching the lowest price, selecting multiple SKUs for bulk action, or typing in the Buy Box price to win it.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/NzDimage.png)

6. Remember to click the “Save All” button at the top right of the table to save your changes.

FAQs

1. Why should I get client approval before making price changes?

Client approval is necessary to ensure that pricing adjustments align with their strategy or goals. Always request approval and document it (via screenshots or call links) as Task Details or Comments before implementing changes.

2. How do I access the "Manage Pricing" page?

To access the **Manage Pricing** page, click the **Menu icon** on the upper left of Seller Central, then select **Pricing > Manage Pricing** from the drop-down menu.

3. What pricing information can I view on the "Manage Pricing" page?

On this page, you can view various pricing details for your SKUs:

- **Price + Shipping**: The total price of the item, including shipping.
- **Business Price + Shipping**: Price specific to B2B sellers, including shipping.
- **Lowest Price + Shipping**: The lowest price available for the SKU on Amazon, including shipping.
- **Buy Box Price + Shipping**: The price of the SKU currently winning the Buy Box, including shipping.

4. How can I calculate my net proceeds for an SKU?

Click on the **blue "Calculate revenue" text** next to any SKU to view a detailed breakdown of your **net proceeds**, factoring in Amazon fees, shipping costs, and other expenses.

5. Can I update prices for multiple SKUs at once?

Yes, you can select multiple SKUs for **bulk actions**, such as matching the lowest price or adjusting prices in bulk. Simply select the SKUs you want to update and apply the desired pricing changes.

6. How do I adjust the Buy Box price for an SKU?

To adjust the Buy Box price, you can directly **enter the price** in the **Buy Box Price + Shipping** field. This action may help you win the Buy Box, provided other conditions (like shipping speed and feedback score) are met.

7. Is there a way to automatically adjust prices to match the lowest price on Amazon?

Yes, you can use the option to **match the lowest price** available on Amazon for an SKU. This can help you stay competitive and increase the chances of winning the Buy Box.

8. How do I ensure my changes are saved?

After making your pricing adjustments, click the **“Save All”** button at the top right of the table to apply and save the changes. Without saving, your updates will not be implemented.

9. How long does it take for price changes to reflect on my product listings?

Price changes are usually reflected within **15-30 minutes** on Amazon, but it may take longer during high traffic periods or if there are technical delays.

10. Can I track the performance of my pricing changes?

Yes, you can monitor your **pricing performance** by checking the **Buy Box Price + Shipping** field, tracking any changes to your **Buy Box status**. Additionally, the **"Calculate revenue"** tool will show how price changes affect your proceeds.
