# Catalog SOP: Creating a Referral Fee Discounts Report

## **Creating a Referral Fee Discounts Report**

**Who is this for :** This report is intended for the company's management team, including executives, marketing managers, and finance personnel. It provides them with valuable insights and data to assess the impact of referral fee discounts on the company's overall performance and make informed decisions regarding the referral program.

**Objective :** The objective of creating a Referral Fee Discounts Report is to track and analyze the referral fee discounts offered by a company in order to evaluate the effectiveness of its referral program and identify opportunities for improvement.

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Inventory Reports" from the Drop-Down Menu.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-JN3SSURO.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sC9image.png)

2. Choose "Referral Fee Discounts Report" from the "Select Report Type" Drop-Down Menu on the next page. Click on the "Request Report" button to start generating the report. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712340618368.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fN8image.png)

3. Your report will appear in the table below with an "In Progress" status.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712340622149.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/k0Iimage.png)

4. Once it is ready, the status will change to "Ready" and the "Download" link will become live.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712340625753.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oawimage.png)

5. Download the report to your computer by clicking on the "Download" link as needed.

FAQs

1. Who can generate the Referral Fee Discounts Report?

Only users with the necessary account permissions, such as administrators or those with access to financial reports in Seller Central, can generate this report. If you do not see the report option, check with your account administrator.

2. How often is the Referral Fee Discounts Report updated?

The report is updated periodically, but users must manually request it each time they need the latest data. It does not auto-refresh.

3. In what format is the Referral Fee Discounts Report downloaded?

The report is typically downloaded in CSV format, which can be opened using spreadsheet software such as Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.

4. How long is the report available for download?

Once generated, the report remains available for download for a limited time, usually 7 days. After that, a new request must be made.

5. What data is included in the Referral Fee Discounts Report?

The report provides insights on referral fee discounts applied to different transactions, including product categories, discount percentages, and the total discount amount.

6. What should I do if the report does not generate or download correctly?

- Refresh the page and check if the report status updates.
- Ensure that pop-up blockers or browser extensions are not preventing the download.
- If the issue persists, try generating the report in a different browser or clearing your cache.
- Contact Seller Central support if you continue to experience issues.
