# Logistics SOP: Remove Inventory from a Fulfillment Center

## **How to Remove Inventory from a Fulfillment Center**

**Who is this for :** Everyone in MAG

**Objective :** To help understand the removal order process for Amazon, including the steps involved in creating and fulfilling a removal order.

Video: [How to Create a Removal Order on Amazon and What to do About Overstocked Inventory in 2020](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HJca_mY01BE)

**Removal Order** is an order created by an Amazon seller to have Amazon remove inventory from its fulfillment centers. This order is typically used when a seller needs to return or dispose of excess or unwanted inventory. Per-item removal fees apply. For more information, go to help pages [FBA removal order fees](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/200685050) and [FBA disposal order fees](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/5FKTA8LXU4TZPD5).

**Follow these steps, to remove inventory from fulfillment centers.**

1. Inventory → Manage All Inventory.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.29.46%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/C5Uimage.png)

2. Select the items you want to remove or enter the ASIN/SKU in the search box.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.29.53%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dKkimage.png)

3. On the Select Group Action, select Create Removal Order.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.30.00%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RlZimage.png)

4. On the Create removal order page, under Method of removal, do one of the following:

- Select Return-to address to have your inventory returned to your designated recipient—for example, your own warehouse, the manufacturer, or a recycling center. Enter the address where you want the inventory shipped.
- Select Dispose to have your inventory disposed of at the fulfillment center.
- Select Liquidations to liquidate and recover value from your inventory. For more information, go to FBA Liquidations.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.30.07%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/HbPimage.png)

5. Enter a custom order ID or leave the field blank and an order ID will be created.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.30.14%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rgoimage.png)

6. If you want to add inventory to the list, enter a Title, SKU or ASIN in the “Search and add items” text box at the bottom of the page and then click Search. Select the products you want to remove, and click Add to list.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.30.22%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6l7image.png)

7. In Fulfillable/Unfulfillable quantity for removal, enter the number of fulfillable/unfulfillable units to remove. Review the total inventory available for removal, and total inventory not available for removal.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.30.29%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2N9image.png)

**Note:** Under Total inventory not available for removal, click the legend to see the reason codes and a brief description of why inventory is not available for removal.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.30.37%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Q85image.png)

8. Click Review (Review the order ID, method of removal, ship-to address, and the details of the selected items for removal.)

9. Click Confirm to complete the removal order. (If you decide not to complete the removal order, click Cancel order).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.30.45%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/8u2image.png)

10. A removal order creation is successful once you receive a pop-up message that reads, “Removal order has been placed. Your order has been placed. To view the status of your order, see the [Removal order detail report](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/reportcentral/REMOVAL_ORDER_DETAIL/0).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-14%20at%201.30.52%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3mTimage.png)

**Removals can take 90 days or more to process and the shipment to leave the fulfillment center.**

**NOTE:**A removal order cannot have items with duplicate fulfillment network SKUs and dispositions. This error in the creation of the removal order is just a mirror offer of the original SKU.

Since both SKUs use manufacturer barcodes, the available units on one SKU are also reflected on the other SKU and are shared.

To fix this, we must remove the other SKU to avoid confusion and place a removal order on the original SKU where the units were replenished.

Check the Received Inventory Report (Reports - Fulfillment - Received Inventory) to see if the SKU has shipment in the past.

FAQs

1. How are removal fees calculated, and when will I be charged?

Amazon charges per-item removal fees based on size, weight, and the selected removal method. Fees are deducted from your seller account balance. You can review the latest fee structure on the [FBA removal order fees](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/external/200280650) and [FBA disposal order fees](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/external/201436320) help pages.

2. Can I cancel or modify a removal order after submitting it?

Once a removal order is submitted, it cannot be canceled or modified. However, you can create a new removal order for additional items if needed. To check if an order has already started processing, go to Reports > Fulfillment > Removal Order Detail Report.

3. Why is my removal order not processing?

Common reasons include:

- The inventory is under an unavailable status (damaged, reserved, or pending removal).
- The SKU has duplicate fulfillment network SKUs, causing a barcode conflict.
- The item is a restricted product and cannot be removed.
  To resolve these, check the Received Inventory Report under Reports > Fulfillment to identify issues.

4. How can I track the status of my removal order?

You can track your order in Reports > Fulfillment > Removal Order Detail Report. The report provides details on processing status, shipment tracking, and expected delivery times.

5. What should I do if I receive an error related to duplicate SKUs?

If you get an error stating that a removal order cannot include duplicate fulfillment network SKUs and dispositions, follow these steps:

1. Identify which SKU the units were originally replenished under by checking the Received Inventory Report.
2. Remove the duplicate SKU from your inventory.
3. Submit the removal order for the original SKU that was used for replenishment.

6. What happens if I don’t create a removal order for excess inventory?

Amazon may dispose of excess inventory at your expense if it exceeds storage limits. If you receive an overstock warning, consider liquidation or discounted removals to minimize costs.

7. Can I use my own shipping carrier for removals?

No, Amazon only ships removals via their partnered carriers. You must enter a valid return address where they will send the inventory.

8. How long does it take for inventory to be removed?

Amazon states that removals can take 90 days or more to process. However, delays may occur due to warehouse congestion, high removal volumes, or inventory verification processes.

9. What should I do if my removal shipment never arrives?

If your removal order has not arrived within 90 days, check the Removal Order Detail Report for tracking information. If it shows as "shipped" but not received, contact Amazon Seller Support and provide your order ID for further assistance.

10. What is the difference between “Dispose,” “Return,” and “Liquidate” options?

- Return-to Address – Amazon sends inventory back to your specified address.
- Dispose – Amazon discards the inventory at their fulfillment center.
- Liquidate – Amazon attempts to sell your inventory through wholesale channels, and you receive a portion of the recovered value.
