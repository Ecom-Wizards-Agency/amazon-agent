## **Updating Tracking for your Merchant Fulfilled Orders via Seller Central**

**Who is this for :**Intended for Amazon sellers who need to update the order shipment or tracking details for Merchant Fulfilled Orders to ensure that the shipment is delivered on time and that the tracking information is accurate and up-to-date.

**Objective :**Update tracking information for your merchant-fulfilled orders is an important part of ensuring timely delivery and customer satisfaction. By keeping the buyer informed and updated, you can help avoid any misunderstandings or disputes and ensure a positive customer experience.

**Instructions:**

1. Go to the "Orders" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Manage Orders" from the dropdown menu.

FAQs

**1. How do I update tracking for multiple shipments in a single order?**

**Answer:**If an order requires multiple shipments (e.g., partial shipments or split orders), you will need to update tracking information for each shipment separately. Here's what you can do:

- Go to "Manage Orders" and find the relevant order.
- For each shipment, click "Edit Shipment".
- Enter the tracking details for that specific shipment and click Re-confirm Shipment.
- Each shipment in the order will have its own tracking entry. Make sure that you update the tracking for all shipments individually, even if they are part of the same order.

**2. What should I do if I made a mistake while updating tracking information?**

**Answer:**If you realize that you made a mistake after updating tracking (e.g., incorrect tracking number or carrier), here’s how to correct it:

- Go to "Manage Orders" in Seller Central.
- Find the order with the incorrect tracking information.
- Click "Edit Shipment".
- Update the tracking details (carrier, tracking number, etc.) with the correct information.
- Click Re-confirm Shipment to save the changes.
- This will overwrite the previous tracking details. If you encounter any issues with updating tracking, you can reach out to Amazon Seller Support for further assistance.

**3. What if my shipment is delayed and I can't provide tracking information right away?**

**Answer:**If your shipment is delayed or you don't have tracking information immediately available, you can still update the order once the tracking details are ready. If you need to update tracking after a delay:

- Go to "Manage Orders" and find the order.
- Click "Edit Shipment" and enter the tracking details as soon as you have them.
- If you don't have tracking yet, you may want to update the shipment as "Shipped" without tracking and then update it once the tracking is available.
- It’s important to ensure that your tracking information is updated as soon as possible to avoid customer complaints or potential issues with the order status.

**4. Are there any specific format requirements for tracking numbers?**

**Answer:**Yes, there are certain format requirements for tracking numbers. While Amazon accepts tracking numbers from most carriers, they should be in a standardized format and match what the carrier provides. Some carriers have specific formats for their tracking numbers, and you should enter the exact tracking number without adding any extra spaces or characters.

If you’re unsure whether the format is correct, you can cross-check it with the carrier or consult Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

**5. What if I don't have a tracking number yet but still need to update the order as shipped?**

**Answer:**If you don’t have a tracking number ready but need to update the order as shipped:

- You can mark the order as "Shipped" but leave the tracking field empty.
- Once you have the tracking details, you can update them later by going to the "Manage Orders" section and clicking "Edit Shipment" to input the tracking information.
- This will ensure that your shipment status is updated, but it’s crucial to enter tracking information as soon as possible to avoid customer confusion.

**6. Does Amazon notify the buyer when I update the tracking information?**

**Answer:**Yes, Amazon automatically notifies the buyer when tracking information is updated for an order. Once you update the tracking information, the buyer will receive an email notification with the new details. There’s no need for you to manually contact the buyer, but it’s always a good idea to check your order status and ensure everything is updated correctly.

If for any reason the buyer doesn't receive the notification, you can always reach out to them via Buyer-Seller Messaging to confirm the tracking details.

**7. How do I update tracking for orders that have already shipped but I forgot to update the tracking information?**

**Answer:**If you forgot to update tracking information before shipping but have already shipped the order, you can still update the tracking by:

- Going to "Manage Orders" and finding the order in question.
- Clicking "Edit Shipment" for the order.
- Entering the correct tracking details and clicking Re-confirm Shipment.
- Even if the order has already shipped, updating the tracking is crucial for Amazon’s order processing and customer satisfaction. It also ensures that the tracking info is visible to both you and the buyer.

**8. Can I change the carrier or shipping service after I've already updated tracking?**

**Answer:**Yes, you can edit the carrier or shipping service after updating tracking if necessary. To do so:

- Go to "Manage Orders" and find the order.
- Click "Edit Shipment".
- You can then select a different carrier or shipping service and enter the correct tracking number.
- After making the changes, click Re-confirm Shipment to save your updates.
- Remember that any changes to the carrier or shipping service should reflect the actual method you used to ship the order to avoid confusion.
