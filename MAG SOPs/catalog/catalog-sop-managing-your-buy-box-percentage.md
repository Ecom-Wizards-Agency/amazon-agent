## **Managing Your Buy Box Percentage**

**Who is this for :** All individuals who need to manage their Featured Offer (Buy Box Percentage).

**Objective :** To manage the Featured Offer on Amazon, which highlight a specific seller's product as the primary or most prominent option for customers.

**Requirements:**

- Sellers must have a Professional Selling Account and their product should be in “New” condition
- Seller must set a competitive price for the product.
- Good Customer Service that is Measured in the Order Defect Rate and Shipment Performance

**Checking on Order Defect Rate (ODR)**

1. Go to the Menu and click on “Performance” then “Account Health”.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/l9Dimage.png)
2. You can check your “Order Defect Rate” (ODR) from the Customer Service Performance. To get a better understanding on what affected your ODR, you can click on the metrics below. Note: To be eligible your ODR must be under 1%.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GuMimage.png)

**Checking on Shipping Performance**

1. From the “Pricing Health” Menu, look at the “Shipping Performance” section on the right most side. There are 3 factors to consider :

  - Late Shipment Rate - Must be under 4%
  - Pre-fulfillment Cancel Rate - Must be under 2.5%
  - Valid Tracking Rate- Must be over 95%

FAQs

1. What is the Buy Box, and why is it important?

The Buy Box is the section on an Amazon product detail page where customers can easily add items to their shopping cart. Being featured in the Buy Box is critical because it increases your chances of making a sale, as it highlights your product as the primary option for customers.

2. What are the requirements for winning the Buy Box?

To win the Buy Box, sellers must meet the following criteria:

- Have a Professional Selling Account.
- The product must be in “New” condition.
- Set a competitive price for the product.
- Maintain good customer service, measured by metrics like the Order Defect Rate (ODR) and Shipping Performance.

3. How can I check my Order Defect Rate (ODR)?

To check your ODR:

1. Go to the Performance tab in Seller Central.
2. Click on Account Health.
3. Under Customer Service Performance, you can view your ODR. Ensure your ODR is below 1% to remain eligible for the Buy Box.

4. What factors affect Shipping Performance?

To be eligible for the Buy Box, consider the following shipping performance factors:

- Late Shipment Rate: Must be below 4%.
- Pre-fulfillment Cancel Rate: Must be below 2.5%.
- Valid Tracking Rate: Must be above 95%.

5. How do I ensure my price is competitive for the Buy Box?

To remain competitive for the Buy Box:

- Match the Featured Offer price for the same ASIN and condition.
- Compare with prices from external retailers.
- Use Automate Pricing to adjust prices automatically in real-time based on the Buy Box price.

6. What is Automate Pricing, and how can it help me win the Buy Box?

Automate Pricing helps you stay competitive by adjusting your prices in real-time based on factors like the Buy Box price. By automating your pricing strategy, you increase your chances of securing the Buy Box without manually updating your prices every time a change is required.

7. How can I check my Featured Offer (Buy Box) price?

To check the Featured Offer (Buy Box) price:

- Go to Pricing > Pricing Health from the Menu in Seller Central.
- In the Pricing Opportunities section, you’ll find eligible ASINs for the Buy Box and can download a report.

8. Is there an alternative way to check the Featured Offer price?

Yes, you can also check the Featured Offer price by:

1. Going to Inventory > Manage All Inventory.
2. Selecting the listing and checking the Price and Shipping Cost column.
  Note: This method won’t show the Winning Rate for the Buy Box. For the winning rate, click the SKU, and scroll down to the Pricing section.

9. How do I check the Buy Box status using the Help Hub?

To check the Buy Box status via the Help Hub:

- Enter the relevant ASIN in the dialogue box provided in the Help Hub.
- The results will provide insights into your Buy Box status and other relevant pricing opportunities.

10. What should I do if I am not winning the Buy Box?

If you’re not winning the Buy Box, consider:

- Adjusting your price to be more competitive.
- Improving your customer service metrics (ODR, Shipping Performance).
- Using Automate Pricing to stay competitive.
- Ensuring your inventory is fully stocked and available for fast shipping.
