## Adding, Editing and Removing Videos

**Who is this for:** This is intended for Amazon users who want to enhance their product listings by adding videos or need to edit or remove existing videos.

**Objective:** To guide Amazon users on adding, editing, and removing videos for their product listings.

- Videos are a great way to increase confidence in buyers on Amazon. Adding video content can help boost sales, increase conversion rate, and decrease returns. Currently, both brand-registered and non-brand-registered sellers can add videos in the image stack but Brand Registry is required for videos to appear in Ads or on the Brand Store.
- Before commencing with any changes, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval first. Include the proof of approval(screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.

FAQs

#### 1. How do I add a video to my product listing on Amazon?

To add a video:

1. Go to the "Catalog" tab in Seller Central and select "Upload & Manage Videos" from the dropdown menu.
2. Click "Upload video".
3. On the upload page, enter your video title, relevant ASINs, upload your thumbnail, and then upload the video file.
4. Once everything is set, click "Submit" in the bottom right corner.

#### 2. What are the benefits of adding videos to my product listings?

Adding videos to your listings can increase buyer confidence, boost sales, improve conversion rates, and reduce returns by giving customers a clearer idea of the product’s features and benefits.

#### 3. Do I need Brand Registry to upload videos to my listings?

No, both brand-registered and non-brand-registered sellers can upload videos to the image stack. However, Brand Registry is required for videos to appear in Ads or on the Brand Store.

#### 4. Can I edit a video after uploading it?

Yes, to edit a video:

1. Go to "Upload & Manage Videos" from the "Catalog" dropdown menu.
2. Select "Manage" or the Trash Bin icon next to the video you want to edit.
3. You can change the video title, add more ASINs, or update the thumbnail image.
4. Once you’ve made your changes, click "Submit".

#### 5. How do I remove a video from my product listing?

To remove a video:

1. Go to "Upload & Manage Videos" in the "Catalog" dropdown menu.
2. Click the Trash Bin icon next to the video you want to remove.
3. Confirm the removal to delete the video from your listing.

#### 6. How does Amazon review the video content after uploading?

After submitting the video, Amazon will review it to ensure it complies with their content policies. If the video meets their guidelines, it will be published to the product detail page.

#### 7. Will the video always appear on the product detail page (PDP)?

If there are more than 6 images in the image stack, the video may not be immediately visible on the PDP unless the image stack is clicked to “pop out” and reveal the video.

#### 8. What information should I include when uploading a video?

You should include:

- A relevant video title.
- The appropriate ASINs.
- An appropriate thumbnail for the video.

#### 9. How do I ensure the video displays correctly on the PDP?

Ensure that the video is part of the image stack. If there are more than 6 images in the stack, the video may require users to "pop out" the stack to view it.

#### 10. Do I need approval from the client before uploading or editing videos?

Yes, always communicate with your client and get approval before uploading or editing videos. Include proof of approval (e.g., a screenshot or Fireflies call link) in the Task Details or as a Comment.
