# Catalog SOP: Negative Feedback Removal

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-negative-feedback-removal

## **Negative Feedback Removal**

**Who is this for :** This is for individuals who would like to request the removal of negative feedback.

**Objective:** This SOP aims to provide a step-by-step guide on how to remove negative feedback. While Amazon usually does not remove buyer feedback, it's crucial to understand the situations where feedback may be removed or strike-through. You may find these in the “[**Can Amazon remove buyer feedback?**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G20231?locale=en-US)” help page.

**Here are some steps that you can take to submit a request for the removal of negative feedback**

1. Login to your Seller Central account.
2. Click on the "burger" icon located on the top left part of the Seller Central homepage.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/negative-feedback-removal-image-1.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/C6limage.png)
3. Hover your mouse over to the "Performance" tab, and then select "Feedback Manager".

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vuPimage.png)
4. Once you're on the Feedback Manager page, you'll see how much Positive, Neutral, and Negative feedback there has been. Please note that we're only concerned with feedback that was given within the last 30-90 days. We cannot remove feedback older than 90 days.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/negative-feedback-removal-image-3.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/86yimage.png)
5. To filter only negative feedback, click on the "Negative" button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/negative-feedback-removal-image-4.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DwRimage.png)
6. Since we’ve already been removing feedback from this account, there aren’t any new ones to remove at the moment. I’ll show you how to do it as if there was feedback to remove. The top feedback has already been removed (that’s why they’re crossed out). The bottom feedback has not been removed. Click on Choose One and then click on Request Removal. Amazon may or may not allow us to remove the feedback.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/N4Kimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/negative-feedback-removal-image-5.png)
7. If they don’t allow us to remove the feedback, that’s okay. If they do, they will ask if the feedback meets the criteria. Click Yes.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/negative-feedback-removal-image-6.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/86wimage.png)

**Please note the following:**

- Amazon will reply later through a ticket if they remove it.
- Do this for all Negative Feedback within 90 days.
- If denied, follow up with a case on Amazon.

**Use the template below as a starting point, and adjust it to fit your specific scenario:**

Dear Amazon,

I am writing to report an issue with a recent order that was fulfilled by Amazon. Our product received a negative review that I believe violates Amazon's feedback policy.

The feedback in question [describe the content of the review, including any language or behavior that violates Amazon's feedback policy]. I believe this feedback should be removed as per Amazon's policy.

We believe that Amazon will remove feedback only in the following cases:

- The feedback includes words commonly understood to be obscene or profane.
- The feedback includes seller-specific, personally identifiable information, including email addresses, full names, or telephone numbers.
- The entire feedback comment is a product review. For example, “The Acme Super-Widget lacks the sharpness and speed of the Acme Ultra Widget.”

We also know that Amazon might strike through feedback in the following cases:

- The order was fulfilled by Amazon
- The order was shipped using the Buy Shipping service

Given the circumstances, I kindly request that you take the necessary steps to remove this feedback from my product listing. I believe that this action would be in line with Amazon's policy and would help to ensure that feedback is fair and unbiased for all sellers. Thank you

FAQs

1. What are Amazon’s official guidelines for removing buyer feedback?

Amazon will remove feedback only if it meets one of the following criteria:

- The feedback contains obscene or profane language.
- The feedback includes personally identifiable information (e.g., phone numbers, email addresses, full names).
- The entire comment is a product review instead of a seller review.

Amazon may strike through feedback instead of removing it if:

- The order was fulfilled by Amazon (FBA).
- The order was shipped using Amazon’s Buy Shipping service.

You can review Amazon’s official policy here.

2. What is the difference between feedback removal and a strike-through?

- Feedback removal – The negative feedback is completely deleted from your account.
- Strike-through – The feedback remains visible but with a note stating: This item was fulfilled by Amazon, and we take responsibility for this fulfillment experience. This prevents the feedback from affecting your metrics.

3. How long does Amazon take to review a feedback removal request?

Amazon typically takes 24–72 hours to review and respond to feedback removal requests. If there is no response within 5 business days, follow up by opening a case in Seller Central > Get Support.

4. What should I do if my feedback removal request is denied?

If Amazon denies the request:

- Double-check whether the feedback meets the removal criteria.
- If you believe the decision was incorrect, reopen the case and provide additional context.
- If the feedback is about shipping delays caused by Amazon, reference Amazon’s policy and request a strike-through instead of removal.

5. Can I contact the buyer to request feedback removal?

Yes, but you cannot offer incentives for removal.

- Go to Performance > Feedback Manager and find the feedback.
- Click Contact Buyer and politely ask if they would consider revising or removing the feedback.
- If they agree, guide them to Performance > Feedback Manager > Remove Feedback.

6. How does negative feedback impact my seller account?

Negative feedback affects:

- Order Defect Rate (ODR) – Too many negative feedback instances can lead to account suspension.
- Buy Box eligibility – A high percentage of negative feedback reduces your chances of winning the Buy Box.
- Seller rating and credibility – Poor feedback can discourage potential buyers.

7. Is there a character limit for feedback removal requests?

Yes, Amazon’s case submission tool has a 4,000-character limit. Keep your request concise and factual while explaining why the feedback violates Amazon’s policies.

8. Can I submit multiple feedback removal requests in one case?

No, each feedback removal request should be submitted individually. Bulk removal requests may be ignored or delayed.

9. How can I prevent negative feedback in the future?

- Ship on time and use tracking with signature confirmation.
- Ensure product descriptions are accurate to prevent complaints about quality.
- Respond to customer inquiries within 24 hours to resolve issues before they escalate.
- Offer hassle-free returns to reduce disputes and A-to-Z claims.

10. Can I appeal Amazon’s decision if my feedback removal request is denied?

Yes. If Amazon denies a removal request:

1. Reopen the case and provide additional clarification.
2. If still denied, contact Amazon Account Health Support and explain why the feedback meets removal criteria.
3. If all else fails, focus on increasing positive feedback to offset the negative one.
