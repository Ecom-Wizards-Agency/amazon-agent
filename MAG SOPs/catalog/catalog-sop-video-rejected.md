# Catalog SOP: Video Rejected

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-video-rejected

## Video Rejected

**Who is this for:** This is for all individuals and sellers for those who upload videos on their listing but Amazon rejects them for some reason.

**Objective:** This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) will help you understand and fix the Rejected Videos by Amazon.

1. Access the “Catalog” tab from the top menu of Seller Central and select “Upload and Manage Videos” from the selection Menu.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sW2image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/sW2image.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-26%20at%203.42.28%20AM.png)
2. Locate the rejected video and see the rejection reason. Identify the specific issue(s) mentioned in the rejection notice, such as technical problems, content violations, or formatting errors. Cross-reference the rejection reasons with Amazon's video guidelines and policies to gain a clear understanding of the violations.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/R3oimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/R3oimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685067461885.png)
3. Resolve content violations:

  1. In case the rejection is due to content violations, identify the specific content elements that violate Amazon's guidelines.
  2. Modify the video content to remove any prohibited elements, such as explicit language, misleading claims, or copyright infringement. Refer to [community guidelines](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G1701) in seller central help page for additional details .
  3. If required, consult with Amazon's Seller Support for clarification on any specific content concerns.
4. Resubmit the video for review once the necessary changes have been made.
5. If you think that the video has been rejected in error, you may [contact seller support](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-file-a-ticket-in-seller-central) and provide the video identifier (Video ID).
6. In order to find the video identifier (Video ID):

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop_-video-rejected-image-6byyeqsn.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7U7image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/7U7image.png)

Note: It is important to adhere to Amazon's guidelines and policies when creating and uploading videos. Regularly reviewing and updating your videos based on Amazon's guidelines will help increase the chances of approval and improve the overall quality of your product listings on the platform.

FAQs

#### 1. Why was my video rejected on Amazon?

- Videos can be rejected for various reasons including technical issues, content violations (e.g., explicit language, misleading claims, or copyright infringement), or formatting errors. You can view the specific rejection reason on the rejection notice, which will guide you in addressing the issue.

#### 2. How can I identify the rejection reason for my video?

- To identify the reason, go to the "Upload and Manage Videos" section under the "Catalog" tab in Seller Central. Locate the rejected video and check the rejection message. This will specify the violation, whether it's technical, content-related, or formatting-related.

#### 3. What are common content violations that lead to video rejection?

- Common content violations include:

  - Explicit language or inappropriate content.
  - Misleading claims or exaggerated product benefits.
  - Copyright infringement or use of unlicensed materials.
  - Violations of Amazon's product guidelines (e.g., promoting restricted products).
  - Content that violates community standards.

#### 4. How do I fix content violations in my video?

- To resolve content violations, edit the video to remove prohibited elements. For example, remove explicit language, correct misleading claims, or use copyright-free material. After making the necessary changes, resubmit the video for review.

#### 5. What if I believe my video was rejected by mistake?

- If you believe the rejection was an error, contact Amazon Seller Support. Provide them with the Video ID (which you can find in the "Upload and Manage Videos" section) and explain why you think the rejection was incorrect. Amazon will review your case and provide further guidance.

#### 6. How can I find my video’s identifier (Video ID)?

- To find your video identifier (Video ID), go to the "Upload and Manage Videos" section in Seller Central. Locate the rejected video, and the Video ID will be displayed with the rejection notice. This ID is crucial if you need to contact Seller Support.

#### 7. How can I prevent future video rejections?

- To prevent future rejections, make sure that all videos follow Amazon’s video guidelines and policies. Regularly review Amazon’s community guidelines and ensure your video content aligns with these standards. Additionally, double-check your video formatting, size, and technical specifications before uploading.

#### 8. Can I resubmit a video after fixing the rejection issue?

- Yes, once you've addressed the issues that caused the rejection, you can resubmit the video for review. Be sure to follow Amazon’s guidelines to improve the chances of approval on the resubmission.
