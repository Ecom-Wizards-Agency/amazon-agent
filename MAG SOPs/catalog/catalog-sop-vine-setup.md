# Catalog SOP: Vine Setup

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-vine-setup

## **Vine Setup**

**Who is this for :** All individuals who want to join Amazon Vine.

**Objective :** To be able to set up Amazon Vine successfully.

Amazon Vine invites the most trusted reviewers on Amazon to post opinions about new products to help their fellow customers make informed purchase decisions. Amazon invites buyers to become Vine reviewers, also known as Vine Voices, based on the insightfulness of the reviews they published on their Amazon purchases. If you participate, you can provide free units of your products for this selected group of Vine Voices to post customer reviews for the products you submit. You can build awareness of your product, boost the sales of your slow and cold start ASINs, and help customers make informed decisions about your new products by participating in Vine.

> **IMPORTANT UPDATE:**
>
> - You can now get authentic product reviews before launch when you enroll eligible FBA products in the Vine program after creating listings.
> - Customers enrolled in Vine Voices can review your products pre-launch, which allows you to launch a product with up to 30 genuine customer reviews on the first day it’s available for sale.
>
> For more information on how to launch, go to our [Vine Seller Guide](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/bx-marketing/Vine_Seller_Guide.pdf).

**Before implementing, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.**

Here are some prerequisites to participate in Vine:

- You must be a Professional Selling Partner
- You must have registered a brand in the Amazon Brand Registry
- You must be identified as a brand owner
- You must have eligible FBA offers

**REMINDER**

Once your enrollment is complete, Vine Voices will have the opportunity to request your enrolled products for review. After the first Vine review is published, you will be charged a**$200 enrollment fee** once per parent ASIN. You will not be charged a fee if the enrolled product does not receive any reviews within **90 days of enrollment.**

**UPDATE : Vine gives you even more flexibility when it comes to selecting an enrollment tier. You can move up to a higher tier within the first 30 days by enrolling more units for the same ASIN. To get started, select Update enrollment in Amazon Vine for currently enrolled ASINs.**

**To be eligible, the items you enroll in must meet the following criteria:**

- Be brand registered in the Amazon Brand Registry
- Have fewer than 30 reviews on the product detail page
- Have a buyable FBA offer in "New" condition
- Not be an adult product
- Have already launched at the time of enrollment
- Have available inventory
- Have an image and a description

**Clarification of Amazon Vine Review Policy**

When you merge two or more separate parent ASINs under a new parent ASIN, only the maximum number of reviews for your highest enrollment tier will be retained. For example, if you merge two products that are enrolled in the top tier with 30 reviews each, your new parent ASIN will retain only the 30 highest-quality reviews.

**Retention limits by tier:**

- Top Tier: Enroll 11–30 units → up to 30 reviews
- Middle Tier: Enroll 3–10 units → up to 10 reviews
- Free Tier: Enroll 1–2 units → up to 2 reviews

VINE ENROLLMENT FEE FOR SELLERS

### VINE ENROLLMENT FEE FOR SELLERS

Vine enrollment fee is charged once per parent ASIN enrollment.

**FEE DETAILS**

| Units Enrolled | Maximum number of units available to be ordered by Vine reviewers (minimum 1 and maximum 30) |
| --- | --- |
| Time of Charge | Seven days after the publication date of the first Vine review for the ASIN enrollment |
| Fee structure | Rate is based on the number of units enrolled per ASIN enrollment |

Rate card (until October 18, 2023)

Enrollments created before October 19, 2023, are subject to $200.

**Rate card (effective October 19, 2023)**

Vine enrollment fee will be charged once per parent ASIN depending on the number of units enrolled. The fees will be charged seven days after the first Vine review is published.

| Units Enrolled | Vine Enrollment Fee |
| --- | --- |
| 1-2 | 0 |
| 3-10 | $75 |
| 11-30 | $200 |

**Fee example**

**Example 1:** Seller enrolls three units for a parent ASIN with no child ASINs and receives one Vine review within 90 days.

- Seller will be charged $75 seven days after the publication date of the first Vine review.

**Example 2:** Seller enrolls two units for a parent ASIN with no child ASINs and receives two Vine reviews within 90 days.

- Seller will not be charged for the enrollment as they enrolled two units at parent-ASIN level.

**Example 3:** Seller enrolls two units for the parent ASIN with five child ASINs and receives two reviews for the enrollment.

- Seller will not be charged for the enrollment as they enrolled two units at parent-ASIN level.

Note: In Example 3, Vine reviewers can place up to two orders on any of the five child ASINs enrolled under the parent-ASIN and submit up to two reviews on the enrollment.

FAQs

**1. What is Amazon Vine, and how does it benefit sellers?**

- Amazon Vine allows trusted reviewers (Vine Voices) to review new or slow-moving products. By participating, sellers can:

  - Build product awareness.
  - Generate early reviews.
  - Boost sales of slow-start ASINs.

**2. What are the prerequisites for enrolling in Amazon Vine?**

- To participate, you must:

  - Be a Professional Selling Partner.
  - Be brand registered in Amazon Brand Registry.
  - Be identified as the brand owner.
  - Have eligible FBA offers for your products.

**3. How do I know if my product is eligible for Vine?**

- Eligible products must:

  - Have fewer than 30 reviews on the product detail page.
  - Have an active FBA offer in “New” condition.
  - Be brand registered in Amazon Brand Registry.
  - Not be classified as an adult product.
  - Have available inventory.
  - Include images and a description on the product detail page.

**4. Can I enroll the same ASIN in Vine more than once?**

- No, an ASIN can only be enrolled in Vine once.

**5. How many units can I enroll per ASIN?**

- You can enroll a minimum of 1 unit and a maximum of 30 units per parent ASIN.

**6. What happens if I enroll more units than required?**

- You’ll be charged the Vine enrollment fee based on the number of units enrolled, regardless of how many units are reviewed.

**7. When will I be charged the Vine enrollment fee?**

- You will be charged seven days after the first Vine review is published for the enrolled ASIN.

**8. What is the fee structure for Vine enrollment?**

- The fees are based on the number of units enrolled:

  - 1-2 units: $0
  - 3-10 units: $75
  - 11-30 units: $200
  - For enrollments before October 19, 2023, the fee is a flat $200.

**9. Will I be charged if no reviews are published within 90 days?**

- No, you will not be charged if the enrolled product does not receive any reviews within 90 days of enrollment.

**10. How can I monitor the status of my Vine enrollment?**

- After enrolling an ASIN, you can check the enrollment status on the Vine page in Seller Central.

**11. Can I withdraw an ASIN from Vine after enrolling?**

- No, once an ASIN is enrolled in Vine, it cannot be withdrawn. Ensure you meet the prerequisites and have client approval before enrolling.

**12. What happens if my ASIN is out of stock during the Vine program?**

- If your ASIN runs out of stock, Vine Voices will not be able to request units for review. This could affect your ability to generate reviews within the 90-day window.

**13. Do I need client approval before enrolling in Vine?**

- Yes, always obtain client approval before enrolling in Vine. Include proof of approval (e.g., screenshots or call notes) in task details or comments.

**14. Can I enroll child ASINs in Vine separately from the parent ASIN?**

- No, Vine enrollment is at the parent ASIN level, and Vine reviewers can select from any child ASINs under that parent.

**15. What should I do if the "Vine" option is not available in Seller Central?**

- If the Vine option isn’t visible:
- Confirm you meet all prerequisites (Professional Seller, Brand Registered, FBA offer).
- Contact Amazon Seller Support to enable Vine in your account.

**16. How do I create a public link for the required product description and image?**

- Ensure your product detail page contains all necessary information (e.g., images, bullet points, description) before enrolling. Public links are not required for Vine enrollment.

**17. Will Vine reviews indicate that they came from the program?**

- Yes, all reviews from Vine Voices will be labeled with the “Vine Customer Review of Free Product” tag to maintain transparency.

**18. Can Vine help improve sales for all products?**

- Vine is most effective for:

  - Newly launched products with minimal visibility.
  - Slow-moving ASINs with low review counts.
  - However, results depend on product quality and reviewer feedback.

**19. Can I provide additional incentives to Vine reviewers?**

- No, offering incentives beyond the free product violates Amazon’s policies. Reviews must be unbiased and based solely on the reviewer’s experience.

**20. How can I resolve issues with Vine enrollment?**

- If you encounter problems:

  - Verify the ASIN meets all eligibility criteria.
  - Double-check your Vine setup steps in Seller Central.
  - Contact Amazon Seller Support for further assistance.
