# Catalog SOP: How to Generate a Replacements Report

## How to Generate a Replacements Report

**Who is this for :** For sellers using Amazon Seller Central who need guidance on generating a Replacements Report to track and analyze replacements issued to customers for completed orders.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions on how to generate a Replacements Report on Amazon Seller Central. It aims to guide users in selecting the desired event date range, downloading the report in either CSV or TXT format, and monitoring the report status for a successful download.

The Replacements Report is a valuable resource for sellers on Amazon Seller Central to monitor and analyze replacements issued to customers. Please expect the report to be updated within 24 hours.

**Instructions**

1. To generate a Replacements Report, navigate to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684336468858.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1R0image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/1R0image.png)
2. Under the "Customer Concessions" header located on the left side of the page, click on "Replacements."

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/FpCimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/FpCimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684336550318.png)

On the following page, choose the event date by selecting from the drop-down menu or selecting "Exact Dates" and entering the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icons. You can also enter your Merchant SKU or Amazon Order ID to generate a more specific report.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684336669448.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6Qnimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/6Qnimage.png)

**To download a Txt or a CSV copy of the report, follow the instructions below:**

1. On the same page, click the "Download" tab.

1. Select the event date by choosing from the drop-down menu or selecting "Exact Dates" and entering the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icons.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jEKimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/jEKimage.png)

1. The "Report Status" column in the table below will show the status as "In Progress".

FAQs

1. Where can I find the Replacements Report in Seller Central?

To access the Replacements Report, go to the Reports tab at the top of Seller Central, select Fulfillment from the drop-down menu, and then click on "Replacements" under the "Customer Concessions" header on the left side of the page.

2. How do I select the event date for the Replacements Report?

You can choose the event date by selecting from the drop-down menu or selecting "Exact Dates" and entering the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icons provided on the page.

3. Can I filter the Replacements Report by specific items or orders?

Yes, you can filter the report by entering specific values such as Merchant SKU or Amazon Order ID to generate a more targeted report focused on particular items or orders.

4. What file formats are available for downloading the Replacements Report?

You can download the report in CSV format (for Excel) by clicking "Request .csv Download" or in TXT format by clicking "Request .txt Download".

5. How do I track the progress of my report request?

Once you request the report, the "Report Status" column will show the status as "In Progress". Once the report is ready, the status will change to "Download", and you can click the Download button to retrieve the report.

6. How long does it take for the Replacements Report to generate?

Typically, the report is updated within 24 hours. However, the actual generation time may vary depending on the amount of data. The "Report Status" will show "In Progress" until the report is ready for download.

7. What should I do if my Replacements Report does not generate or download correctly?

If the report fails to generate or download:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

8. Can I automate the generation of the Replacements Report?

Currently, Amazon Seller Central does not provide an option to automate the generation of this specific report. You will need to manually generate and download it when needed.

9. How can I ensure the accuracy of the report data?

Make sure to use the correct filters (such as Merchant SKU or Amazon Order ID) and select the appropriate date range to ensure you’re pulling the most relevant data. If needed, verify with Amazon Seller Support if the data appears incorrect.

10. Does the Replacements Report include all types of replacements or just specific ones?

The Replacements Report includes details on all replacements issued to customers for completed orders. If you need more specific data, consider using the filtering options for Merchant SKU or Amazon Order ID to narrow the results.

11. How often is the Replacements Report updated?

The report is updated within 24 hours, so there may be a delay in seeing the most recent returns or replacements processed.

12. Can I download the Replacements Report multiple times for different date ranges?

Yes, you can download the report multiple times for different date ranges. You simply need to adjust the "From" and "To" dates and follow the same steps to download the report.
