# Troubleshooting SOP: How to Fix Search Suppressed

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/troubleshooting-sop-how-to-fix-search-suppressed

## **How to Fix Search Suppressed**

**Who is this for :** Individuals who are trying to fix their Search Suppressed listings.

**Objective :** To rectify search-suppressed listings and remove the suppression.

When Amazon suppresses a listing, it is removed from visibility in both search and browse functions until the issues and policy breaches within it are fixed.

**You might find your product listing suppressed if:**

- Missing the main image
- Inadequate product description
- Invalid product information
- Product title over 200 characters
- Clothing or accessory title over 80 characters
- Missing product category
- Prohibited product for sale on Amazon
- Suspected counterfeit product

In order to detect and fix Amazon-suppressed listings, check the following steps below.

**Find your suppressed listings:**

**Step 1.** Log in to your seller account, and select Manage Inventory.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-01%20at%205.27.47%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ZZgimage.png)

**Step 2**. Select Suppressed from the horizontal menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-01%20at%205.27.55%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/xkximage.png)

**Step 3.** View your suppressed listings on the Fix your products page, or click on Attribute to filter by issue.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-01%20at%205.28.07%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vCdimage.png)

**How to fix your Amazon-suppressed listings:**

**Option A:**

**Step 1.** Fix suppressed listings in the Issue(s) to fix column by entering the missing values in the editable cells or selecting missing values from the drop-down list

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-01%20at%205.28.30%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/kG2image.png)

**Step 2**. Click Save.

**Option B:**

**Step 1.** Fix suppressed listings that are not fixable in the editable grid (Option A) by clicking Edit and going to the Edit product info page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-01%20at%205.28.37%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Fhfimage.png)

**Step 2.** In Edit product info, the missing product details will be highlighted and you will see informational messages that further detail the issue.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-01%20at%205.28.45%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ukximage.png)

**Step 3.** For non-image issues, enter the missing value and click Save and Finish.

- For image issues, follow the guidelines on the Product image requirements page and then upload valid product images.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/8ACimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-01%20at%205.28.54%E2%80%AFAM.png)

**Option C:**

To fix suppressed listings, you may upload a feed file to provide the missing information.

By uploading a feed file, you can update the missing or incorrect information that is causing your listings to be suppressed. This can be particularly useful if you are dealing with a large number of suppressed listings, as it allows you to make changes in bulk.

Download the correct flat file template. Go to Inventory > Add Products via Upload > Download an Inventory File.

FAQs

**1. What happens when my listing is search suppressed?**

- When Amazon search suppresses a listing, it disappears from search results and browsing, meaning customers can’t find or buy your product until you fix the issue. This can lead to lost sales and lower ranking in Amazon’s search algorithm.

**2. How do I know why my listing was suppressed?**

- You can check the reason for suppression by:

  - Going to Manage Inventory > Suppressed Listings and looking at the Issue(s) to fix column.
  - Clicking Edit on the product page to see highlighted missing or incorrect details.
  - Checking the Account Health dashboard for compliance-related suppression issues.

**3. What if my listing is suppressed but doesn’t appear under ‘Suppressed Listings’?**

- If your listing doesn’t show in the Suppressed Listings tab:

  - Search for the ASIN in Manage Inventory and look for error messages.
  - Check Account Health > Policy Compliance to see if Amazon flagged your product.
  - Look for suppression notices in Performance Notifications.

**4. What happens if I don’t fix my suppressed listing?**

- If a listing stays suppressed, Amazon may:

  - Remove the listing permanently.
  - Reduce your account’s health rating, which can lead to selling restrictions.
  - Suspend your account if the suppression is due to policy violations.

**5. Can I fix multiple suppressed listings at once?**

- Yes! If you have several suppressed listings, it’s faster to use a feed file:

  - Go to Inventory > Add Products via Upload > Download an Inventory File.
  - Fill in missing product details.
  - Re-upload the file and wait for Amazon to process the updates.

**6. How long does it take for my listing to be reinstated after fixing it?**

- Listings typically get reinstated within 24-48 hours after changes are made. If your listing is still suppressed after this timeframe:

  - Check your case log for updates.
  - Open a case with Seller Support to investigate further.

**7. Why did my listing get suppressed again after I fixed it?**

- Recurring suppression can happen if:

  - Amazon updates its policies, making previous fixes outdated.
  - New errors or missing attributes are detected.
  - Restricted words or claims trigger suppression again.
  - To prevent this, regularly review Amazon’s policy updates and monitor your listings.

**8. How can I prevent search suppression in the future?**

- To reduce the risk of suppression:

  - Keep product titles under 200 characters (80 for clothing/accessories).
  - Ensure your listing has a main image and meets Amazon’s image guidelines.
  - Provide a complete product description, category, and relevant attributes.
  - Regularly check Account Health and fix warnings before they escalate.
