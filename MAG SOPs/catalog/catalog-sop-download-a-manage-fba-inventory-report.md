# Catalog SOP: Download a Manage FBA Inventory Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-manage-fba-inventory-report

## Download a Manage FBA Inventory Report

**Who is this for :** Intended for individuals within an organization who are responsible for managing inventory through the FBA program on Amazon.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions on how to download and manage the FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) inventory report. This SOP aims to ensure accurate and up-to-date inventory information, streamline inventory management processes, and facilitate effective decision-making for the designated individuals or teams responsible for managing FBA inventory.

**To generate a Manage FBA Inventory Report:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-NX3C0VHG.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rGaimage.png)

2. Under the "Inventory" header on the left-hand side of the page, click on the "Manage FBA Inventory" link.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685144312994.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/JjMimage.png)

3. On the following page, choose the "Request .csv Download" button to download the report in an Excel format or the "Request .txt Download" button to download it in a text format.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685144382080.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zK2image.png)

4. In the table below, the "Report Status" column will initially show the status as "In Progress".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685144439561.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zIQimage.png)

5. Once the report is ready for download, click the "Download" button located in the "Report Status" column to download your report.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685144506246.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/k6bimage.png)

FAQs

1. Who can generate the Manage FBA Inventory Report?

Only users with the necessary permissions, such as administrators or those with inventory management access in Seller Central, can generate this report. If you do not see the report option, check with your account administrator.

2. How often is the Manage FBA Inventory Report updated?

The report is not real-time; it is updated periodically based on Amazon’s system updates. The exact refresh rate depends on Amazon's schedule, but typically, it is updated at least once a day.

3. How long is the report available for download?

Once requested, the report remains available for download for up to 7 days. After that, you must generate a new report.

4. What is the difference between the .csv and .txt file formats?

- .csv (Comma-Separated Values): Best for opening in spreadsheet software like Excel or Google Sheets.
- .txt (Text File): Plain text format, useful for custom scripts or database imports.

5. What should I do if the report does not generate or download?

- Refresh the page and check the report status again.
- Ensure you are using a compatible browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox).
- Disable pop-up blockers or browser extensions that may interfere with the download.
- Try clearing your browser cache and cookies.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for further assistance.
