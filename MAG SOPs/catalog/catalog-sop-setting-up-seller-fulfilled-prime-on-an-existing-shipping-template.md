# Catalog SOP: Setting up Seller Fulfilled Prime on an Existing Shipping Template

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-setting-up-seller-fulfilled-prime-on-an-existing-shipping-template

## **Setting up Seller Fulfilled Prime on an Existing Shipping Template**

**Who is this for:** This can be performed by the seller or their authorized representative who has access to the seller account on the e-commerce platform where their products are listed.

O**bjectives:**

1. Provide customers with fast and reliable delivery options to improve their overall shopping experience and foster loyalty.
2. Leverage efficient fulfillment strategies to boost customer retention and drive higher sales figures for your business.
3. Integrate Seller Fulfilled Prime into your shipping template to access Amazon Prime benefits while maintaining control over your order fulfillment process.

**Instructions:**

1. Go to your Seller Central Dashboard and select "Shipping ettings" from the drop-down menu under the "Settings" tab in the top right corner.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0Hoimage.png)

2. From the Shipping Templates List, choose the template that you want to enable for shipping of Seller Fulfilled Prime items.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0Csimage.png)

3. Click the "Edit Template" link located on the top-right side of the page.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2DYimage.png)

4. Tick the Prime Delivery box to enable Prime Delivery.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Hvsimage.png)

5. Select your preferred carriers and Shipping address in the pop-up window.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iHeimage.png)

6. You must select a preferred carrier to enable the "Next" button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/WW3image.png)

7. Select Fulfillment Type Preferences and click Next.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/62yimage.png)

8. Select the appropriate or your preferred shipping services and click Next.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/14Uimage.png)

9. If necessary, select the "Delivery zone limit" option to manage your shipping costs and limit Prime regions.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/L9Bimage.png)

10. Review your "Prime shipping settings" and click "Confirm" to save the changes.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/V5Rimage.png)

11. Review the whole Prime Template, enter any shipping rates for non-Prime customers according to the guidelines provided on the "Seller Fulfilled Prime shipping requirements" and click the "Save" button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/BaZimage.png)

12. Your shipping template is now modified.

FAQs

1. Can I enable Seller Fulfilled Prime on multiple shipping templates?

- Yes, you can enable Seller Fulfilled Prime on multiple templates. Just repeat the steps for each shipping template you want to modify.

2. What happens if I don’t select a preferred carrier?

- The "Next" button will remain disabled until you select a preferred carrier. This is required to proceed with setting up Seller Fulfilled Prime.

3. Can I disable Prime delivery for certain regions?

- Yes, you can manage Prime availability by selecting the "Delivery zone limit" option to restrict Prime shipping to specific areas.

4. Will non-Prime customers be affected by this change?

- No, non-Prime customers will still see the shipping options you set for them. You can update non-Prime shipping rates in the template before saving.

5. How do I know if my shipping template has been successfully updated?

- After clicking "Save," check your shipping templates list to confirm that Seller Fulfilled Prime is enabled. You may also receive a confirmation email from Amazon.

6. Can I switch back to a standard shipping template after enabling Seller Fulfilled Prime?

- Yes, you can edit the template anytime to disable Prime delivery if needed. However, this may impact your eligibility for Seller Fulfilled Prime.

7. What if I don't meet Amazon's Seller Fulfilled Prime shipping requirements?

- You must comply with Amazon’s delivery speed and performance standards to maintain Seller Fulfilled Prime eligibility. Failure to meet these requirements may result in losing Prime status.

8. Do I need to enter separate shipping rates for Prime and non-Prime orders?

- Yes, Prime orders will follow Prime shipping settings, but you must manually enter rates for non-Prime customers based on Amazon’s guidelines.
