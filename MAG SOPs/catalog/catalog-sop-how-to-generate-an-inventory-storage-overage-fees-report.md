# Catalog SOP: How to Generate an Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-generate-an-inventory-storage-overage-fees-report

## **How to Generate an Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report**

**Who is this for :** This is intended for individuals or teams responsible for inventory management and financial analysis on Seller Central. It is suitable for users who have access to Seller Central and need to generate an Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report to track and evaluate any additional fees related to excess inventory storage. This SOP will assist users in navigating to the appropriate section, selecting the desired report format, and downloading the report for review, analysis, and decision-making purposes related to inventory management and cost optimization.

**Objective :** To provide instructions for generating an Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report. This SOP aims to guide users in accessing and downloading a report that provides information on any overage fees incurred due to excess inventory storage. It enables users to monitor and analyze overage fees to optimize inventory management and minimize additional storage costs.

**Instructions:**

1. To generate an Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report, go to the “Reports” Tab on the Top Menu of Seller Central and select “Fulfillment” from the Drop-Down Menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684262350130.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Xy1image.png)
2. Under the “Payments” Header on the left side of the page, click on the “Inventory Storage Overage Fees” link.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684262449826.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/e5timage.png)
3. On the next page, choose the desired “Event Month” and Year from the available Drop-Down Menus.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684262631801.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yGjgmYimage.png)
4. If you prefer to download the report in an Excel format, click the “Request .csv Download” button. If you prefer to download the report in a text format, click the “Request .txt Download” button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684262708499.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/TENimage.png)
5. The “Report Status” Column in the table below will show the status as “In Progress”.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684262746909.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/mvCimage.png)
6. Once the report is ready for download, this status will change to “Download”. To download your report, click the “Download” button in the “Report Status” Column.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684262899771.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zFsimage.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I find the Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report in Seller Central?

You can access the report by navigating to Reports > Fulfillment, then selecting Payments > Inventory Storage Overage Fees on the left-hand side of the page.

2. What information does the Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report include?

The report provides details on additional storage fees incurred due to exceeding Amazon’s inventory storage limits. It includes fee amounts, storage volume, overage duration, and affected SKUs.

3. Can I select a custom date range for the Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report?

No, Amazon provides the report based on monthly storage periods. You can select the “Event Month” and Year from the drop-down menus to retrieve the corresponding data.

4. What file formats are available for downloading the report?

You can download the report in Excel (.csv) format by clicking "Request .csv Download" or in text (.txt) format by selecting "Request .txt Download."

5. How do I track the progress of my report request?

After requesting the download, check the "Report Status" column. The status will first show "In Progress" and update to "Download" when the report is ready.

6. How long does it take for the Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report to be generated?

Processing time varies based on the amount of data, but most reports are available within a few minutes.

7. What should I do if my Inventory Storage Overage Fees Report does not generate or download properly?

If the report does not generate or download successfully:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Try clearing your browser cache or switching to a different browser.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

8. Does this report include all storage fees, or just overage fees?

This report specifically covers overage fees related to exceeding storage limits. If you need a full breakdown of all storage fees, you may need to generate an Inventory Storage Fees Report separately.

9. How can I minimize inventory storage overage fees?

To reduce overage fees, consider:

- Monitoring inventory levels regularly to stay within Amazon’s storage limits.
- Using automated restock and removal strategies to prevent excess stock.
- Participating in Amazon's inventory cleanup programs, such as FBA Liquidations or Removals.

10. Can I automate or schedule this report for regular downloads?

Currently, Amazon does not offer an option to schedule automated downloads for this report. You will need to manually generate and download the report each month.
