# Logistics SOP: Reserved Inventory Report Generation and Management

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/logistics-sop-reserved-inventory-report-generation-and-management

## Reserved Inventory Report Generation and Management

**Who is this for?**

This SOP is for **Amazon Specialists**, **Brand Managers**, and **Project Managers** responsible for monitoring and managing inventory in Amazon’s Fulfillment Centers.

**Objective:**

Provide clear guidelines for generating, downloading, and managing the Reserved Inventory Report in Amazon Seller Central.

> **Inventory Overview Column Update**
>
> **What's changing:**
>
> •The "Available" category has been replaced by "On-hand", which includes both Available and FC transfer units. These units are considered customer-purchasable.
>
> •As a result, FC transfer units have been removed from the "Reserved" category. Reserved now reflects only FC processing and Customer order units.
>
> These changes apply to all FBA sellers in the US store. No action is required on your part.
>
> **Please note:**
>
> •These updates will be fully reflected in FBA reports by mid-2026.
>
> •Until then, inventory reports may differ from the new Inventory Overview column, as they will continue to classify FC transfer units as Reserved.
>
> We understand this temporary discrepancy may cause some confusion and appreciate your patience as the transition is completed across all systems.
>
> **For more details on managing your FBA inventory, please visit the Inventory Performance page.** [**https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G202174810**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G202174810)

**Overview of Reserved Inventory Report**

The [**Reserved Inventory Report**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/reportcentral/ReserveBreakdown/1) gives a detailed breakdown of inventory in a "**reserved**" status within Amazon's Fulfillment Centers. These units are typically reserved for customer orders, being transferred between FCs, or pending additional processing. Understanding the reserved status helps ensure effective inventory management and timely fulfillment of customer orders.

**Reasons for Reserved Status**

Units in reserved status might fall under the following categories:

- **Customer Order:** Units reserved for fulfilling a customer order.
- **FC Transfer:** Units being transferred from one fulfillment center to another, but still available for purchase.
- **FC Processing:** Units set aside at the fulfillment center for additional processing, such as verification or pending investigations.

Field Definitions

Below are the key fields you will encounter in the Reserved Inventory Report:

| Online Header | Download Header | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Merchant SKU | sku | Unique identifier assigned by you as the seller. |
| Fulfillment Network SKU | fnsku | Unique identifier assigned by Amazon for items in FCs. |
| ASIN | asin | Amazon’s unique product identifier. |
| Title | product-name | Name or description of the product. |
| Reserved Quantity | reserved_qty | Total number of units in reserved status. |
| Reserved - Customer Order | reserved_customerorders | Number of units reserved for customer orders. |
| Reserved - FC Transfer | reserved_fc-transfers | Units being transferred between FCs. |
| Reserved - FC Processing | reserved_fc-processing | Units pending additional processing at the FC. |

**Steps to Download Reserved Inventory Report**

To **download the Reserved Inventory Report** immediately:

1. **Navigate to the Report Page:**

  - Go to the [**Reserved Inventory report**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/reportcentral/ReserveBreakdown/1) in your Amazon Seller Central account.
2. **Download the Report:**

  - On the **Download tab**, click on either **Request .csv download** or **Request .txt download** depending on your preferred file format.

**Scheduling Reserved Inventory Reports**

You can schedule automatic reports at the frequency of your choice. These reports will be generated based on your selected time frame (e.g., every 7 days).

**Steps to Schedule a Report:**

1. **Go to the Schedule Tab:**

  - From the [**Reserved Inventory Report page**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/reportcentral/ReserveBreakdown/2), select the **Schedule tab**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-reserved-inventory-report-generation-and-management-image-a3g9wl1q.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Xkjimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Xkjimage.png)

1. **Select Report Frequency and Start Date:**

  - Choose the frequency (e.g., Every 7 days, Every 30 days).
  - Set a **start date** for the scheduled report. The start date must be in the future.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-reserved-inventory-report-generation-and-management-image-d50jwvck.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4Vyimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4Vyimage.png)

1. **Choose Report Format:**

  - Select your preferred report format (CSV or TXT).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-reserved-inventory-report-generation-and-management-image-occkf71o.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3b8image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/3b8image.png)

1. **Create the Schedule:**

  - Click on **Create scheduled report** to save your settings.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-reserved-inventory-report-generation-and-management-image-9s8g1cnu.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SCZimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/SCZimage.png)

1. **Check Your Scheduled Reports:**

  - Your scheduled reports will appear under the **My schedule** section on the page.
  - The report will be generated approximately at midnight in your store’s time zone.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-reserved-inventory-report-generation-and-management-image-nmxtuog6.png)

1. **Modify or Delete Scheduled Reports:**

  - To modify your schedule, delete the existing report and create a new one. Only one schedule can be created per report.

**Note:** Each time a scheduled report is generated, it will appear under the **Scheduled reports** section. You can have a **maximum of 30 reports** listed here. Any manually requested reports will not appear in this section.

**Key Considerations**

- **Reserved Quantity**: Remember that **reserved inventory is not included** in available or unsellable inventory quantities. Always account for it separately when assessing available stock.
- **FC Transfers**: Transfers between fulfillment centers can take up to **22-25 days** to complete, with some delays possible due to weather or other factors.
- **FC Processing**: If your inventory is in FC processing (e.g., awaiting verification or removal), it may be unavailable for immediate fulfillment.

FAQs

**Q1: What is the Reserved Inventory Report?**
**A1**: It provides a detailed breakdown of inventory in a "reserved" status within Amazon’s Fulfillment Centers. This includes inventory reserved for customer orders, transfers between Fulfillment Centers, and units pending processing.

**Q2: How can I download the Reserved Inventory Report? A2:**

- Navigate to the Reserved Inventory Report page in Seller Central.
- Click on the Download tab and choose between .csv or .txt formats.

**Q3: Can I schedule automatic Reserved Inventory Reports?**
**A3:** Yes, you can schedule reports to be generated automatically at your preferred frequency (e.g., every 7 or 30 days). Set the start date and choose the format (CSV or TXT).

**Q4: What are the key fields in the Reserved Inventory Report? A4:**

- Merchant SKU: Unique identifier by the seller.
- Fulfillment Network SKU (FNSKU): Amazon’s product identifier.
- Reserved Quantity: Total number of reserved units.
- Reserved - Customer Order: Units reserved for customer orders.
- Reserved - FC Transfer: Units being transferred between FCs.
- Reserved - FC Processing: Units pending processing at FCs.

**Q5: What should I do if I want to modify or delete a scheduled report?**
**A5**: To modify, delete the current schedule and create a new one. Only one schedule can be created per report.

**Q6: What factors should I consider when reviewing the Reserved Inventory Report? A6:**

- Reserved Quantity: It’s not included in available or unsellable inventory.
- FC Transfers: Transfers may take up to 22-25 days.
- FC Processing: Units pending processing may not be available for immediate fulfillment.

**Q7: How do I access my scheduled reports in Seller Central?**
**A7**: Your scheduled reports appear under the My Schedule section on the Reserved Inventory Report page. These are generated at midnight in your store’s time zone.

**Q8: How often is the Reserved Inventory Report generated when scheduled?**
**A8**: Scheduled reports are generated approximately at midnight in your store's time zone based on the frequency you selected (e.g., every 7 or 30 days).
