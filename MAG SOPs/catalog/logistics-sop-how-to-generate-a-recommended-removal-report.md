# Logistics SOP: How to Generate a Recommended Removal Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/logistics-sop-how-to-generate-a-recommended-removal-report

## **How to Generate a Recommended Removal Report**

**Who is this for?** For sellers using Amazon Seller Central who need guidance on generating a Recommended Removal Report. This report provides valuable information about inventory that may be subject to Long-Term Storage Fees (LTSF) at the time of the next Inventory Cleanup.

**Objective:** To provide clear instructions on how to generate a Recommended Removal Report on Amazon Seller Central. It aims to guide users in entering the necessary information, such as Merchant SKU or ASIN, to generate a specific report. Additionally, it will provide instructions on how to download the report in either CSV or TXT format and monitor the report status for a successful download.

**To create a Recommended Removal Report, follow these steps:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684338318582.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/WGyimage.png)
2. Under the "Removals" header on the left side of the page, click on the "Recommended Removal" link.
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684338400071.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/imtimage.png)
3. On the following page, enter your Merchant SKU or ASIN to generate a more specific report.
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684338749839.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eIqimage.png)

  **To download a Txt or a CSV copy of the report, follow the instructions below:**

  1. On the same page, click "Download".
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684338833030.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1sUimage.png)

  2. Click the "Request .csv Download" button to download the report in Excel format, or click the "Request .txt Download" button to download it in text format.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RhYimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684338918367.png)

  3. The "Report Status" column in the table below will show the status as "In Progress".

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/juLimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684338978875.png)

  4. Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to "Download". Click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column to download your report.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684339020926.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Ra8image.png)
