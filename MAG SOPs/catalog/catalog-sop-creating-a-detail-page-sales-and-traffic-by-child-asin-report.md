## Creating a Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN Report

**Who is this for :** This is intended for individuals who have access to Seller Central and need to generate a Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN Report. It is designed for users who are responsible for analyzing business data and require a step-by-step guide on how to download this specific report.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions on how to generate a Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN Report on Seller Central. By following these steps, users will be able to access the necessary menu options, customize the report's columns and date range, and successfully download the report in CSV format.

The Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN Report is a valuable tool for businesses to analyze the performance of their products on Amazon's Seller Central platform. This report provides comprehensive data on sales and traffic generated by each individual ASIN (Amazon Standard Identification Number). By generating and downloading this report, users can gain insights into the performance of specific products, make data-driven decisions, and optimize their overall business strategy.

**To generate a Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN Report, follow these steps:**

1. Access the "Reports" Tab on the Top Menu of Seller Central and select "Business Reports" from the Drop-Down Menu

2. In the left side menu under the Sub-Heading "By ASIN," click on "Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN."

3. Click the "Columns" button on the right side of the page to view the available Columns Fields. Choose which columns and information you want to include in your report by selecting or deselecting them.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-12%20at%201.20.56%E2%80%AFAM.png)

FAQs

1. How far back can I retrieve data in the Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN Report?

The report typically allows users to retrieve data for the past 90 days. For older data, users may need to check other Amazon reports or manually track performance over time.

2. Why don’t I see the 'Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN' option in Seller Central?

If you cannot access the report, ensure:

- Your Seller Central role includes access to Business Reports.
- Your account type (Individual vs. Professional) supports report generation.
- The reporting feature is enabled for your seller account. If access issues persist, contact Amazon Seller Support.

3. Are there limits on how many columns I can select in the report?

There are no strict limits on selecting columns, but choosing too many may result in a large file that can be difficult to manage. Key recommended columns for insights include:

- Sessions (Number of visits to the product page)
- Unit Session Percentage (Conversion rate)
- Ordered Product Sales (Revenue generated)
- Total Order Items (Total units sold)

4. How long does it take for the report to generate?

The report is usually generated instantly, but during high-traffic periods, it may take a few minutes. If the download button is unavailable or the report doesn’t generate, try refreshing the page or selecting a smaller date range.

5. What should I do if my report download fails?

If you experience download failures:

- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
- Clear your cache and cookies, then reload the page.
- Reduce the date range to see if a smaller dataset works.
- Contact Amazon Support if issues persist.

6. Can I download the report in a format other than CSV?

Currently, Amazon only provides the report in CSV format. However, you can open it in Excel, Google Sheets, or Power BI for further analysis.
