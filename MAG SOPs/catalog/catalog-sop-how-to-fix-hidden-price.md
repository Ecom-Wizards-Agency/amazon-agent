# Catalog SOP: How to Fix Hidden Price

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-fix-hidden-price

## How to Fix Hidden Price

**Who is this for :** This is intended for everyone who comes across hidden pricing errors on the Amazon detail page.

**Objective :** The objective is to provide guidance and solutions for hidden pricing.

- Walkthrough Video by Steven Pope

**Overview:**

In most cases, the reason for the hidden price is the MAP (minimum advertised pricing) policy. This bug typically occurs when you exceed the minimum price threshold or when the minimum price is higher than the listing's price rate. The solution here is to lower the minimum price which is below your regular price and sale price (if applicable).

Here’s a sample screenshot of what a listing would look like on the Amazon detail page when the price is hidden. Although the item is still available for purchase, buyers may find it challenging to confirm the price as they must proceed to the "add to cart" function to view it.

  - Pesticide Registration Status

FAQs

**1. What causes the price to be hidden on the Amazon detail page?**

- Hidden pricing typically occurs due to the Minimum Advertised Price (MAP) policy. This issue arises when the minimum price is set incorrectly—either higher than the listing's price or not appropriately aligned with the sale price and regular price.

**2. How do I confirm if the issue is related to MAP pricing?**

- Check the "Your Minimum Price" column in the Manage All Inventory page. Ensure the Minimum Price < Sale Price < Regular Price hierarchy is followed. If this is misconfigured, it is likely the cause of the hidden price.

**3. What should I do if the manual update does not fix the hidden price issue?**

- If the manual update fails to resolve the issue:

  - Ensure all duplicate listings (e.g., FBM and FBA SKUs) are updated.
  - Use a flat file update (Category Listings Report) to make bulk changes.
  - If the issue persists, escalate the case to Amazon Seller Support with screenshots of the error and the steps already taken.

**4. How can I identify duplicate listings (FBM and FBA)?**

- Check your Manage All Inventory page for multiple SKUs under the same ASIN. Update the pricing details for both SKUs to ensure consistency across fulfillment types (FBM and FBA).

**5. Can I fix hidden prices for multiple SKUs at once?**

- Yes, use a flat file upload to update the minimum advertised price (MAP) for multiple SKUs in bulk. Ensure all irrelevant rows are removed from the file to avoid processing errors.

**6. What happens if the uploaded flat file shows errors?**

- Download the Processing Report for details on why the upload failed. Common issues include:

  - Missing or incorrect fulfillment-center-id.
  - Missing compliance details for FBA listings (e.g., "batteries_required" or "pesticide marking").
  - Address the errors in the flat file, re-upload, and monitor the status.

**7. Are there any additional requirements for FBA listings when fixing MAP pricing?**

- Yes, for FBA listings, ensure the following compliance columns are filled out in the flat file:

  - batteries_required
  - supplier_declared_dg_hz_regulation1
  - Pesticide Marking (if applicable)
  - Missing these fields may cause processing errors.

**8. How long does it take for pricing changes to reflect on the detail page?**

- Changes typically reflect within 10–15 minutes, but in some cases, it may take up to an hour. If changes do not reflect within this timeframe, double-check for duplicate listings or upload errors.

**9. What should I do if Amazon’s processing still doesn’t resolve the hidden price issue?**

- Escalate the issue to Amazon Seller Support by filing a ticket. Provide:

  - The ASIN or SKU.
  - Screenshots of the hidden price issue.
  - Details of all steps you’ve already taken (manual updates, flat file uploads, etc.).

**10. How do I prevent hidden price issues in the future?**

- Follow these best practices:

  - Always set the minimum price lower than the sale price and regular price.
  - Regularly update your Category Listings Report to ensure accurate pricing information.
  - Check for duplicate listings to avoid discrepancies between FBM and FBA SKUs.

**11. Does the MAP price impact Buy Box eligibility?**

- Yes, if the MAP price is set incorrectly (e.g., higher than the listing's regular or sale price), it can affect your Buy Box eligibility and visibility to customers.

**12. Can hidden prices occur for specific categories or regions?**

- Yes, certain categories (e.g., electronics) and regions with stricter MAP policies are more prone to hidden pricing issues. Ensure all pricing rules comply with regional and category-specific policies.

**13. How do I confirm if the price is fixed after making updates?**

- Check the product’s detail page by navigating to [amazon.com/dp/ASIN](http://amazon.com/dp/ASIN) after applying changes. Ensure the price is visible without needing to add the product to the cart.
