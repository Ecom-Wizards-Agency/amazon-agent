## Scheduling a New Orders Report

**Who is this for :** All individuals who need to schedule a new orders report.

**Objective :** To schedule a new orders report that enables businesses to monitor new orders as they come in, which is essential for understanding demand, customer behavior, and sales trends.

**Instructions:**

1. To schedule a New Orders Report, navigate to the "Orders" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Order Reports" from the drop-down menu.

2. Under the "New Orders" tab, go to the "Scheduled Report Settings" heading and select the desired schedule for your report, then click the "Schedule" button.

3. If you choose the "Daily at" option, select a specific time from the available drop-down menu.

FAQs

1. Where can I schedule the New Orders Report in Seller Central?

To schedule the New Orders Report, navigate to the Orders tab at the top of Seller Central, then select Order Reports from the drop-down menu. On the New Orders tab, you will find the options for scheduling the report.

2. How do I choose the schedule for my New Orders Report?

Under the New Orders tab, go to the "Scheduled Report Settings" section. From there, select the desired schedule for your report and click the "Schedule" button to set it up.

3. What scheduling options are available for the New Orders Report?

You can choose from several scheduling options, including:

- Daily at: Allows you to set the report to be generated daily at a specific time.
- If you choose "Daily at", you will need to select a specific time from the available drop-down menu.

4. How do I include a sales channel column in the New Orders Report?

If you'd like to include a sales channel column in your New Orders Report, simply tick the box labeled "Include sales channel" in the Scheduled Report Settings section before scheduling the report.

5. How can I customize the columns in the New Orders Report?

Before scheduling the report, you can customize the columns by clicking the "Add or remove order report columns" link located in the top-right corner of the page. You can then select or deselect the columns based on your preferences and click "Save" to apply the changes.

6. Can I schedule multiple New Orders Reports at once?

Currently, you can only schedule one report at a time. However, you can customize the frequency and the details of the report (e.g., daily or other specific criteria).

7. Can I edit the schedule once it’s been set?

Yes, after scheduling the report, you can edit the schedule if needed. To make changes, simply go back to the Scheduled Report Settings and adjust your selections.

8. What happens if I don’t select a specific time when choosing the "Daily at" option?

If you choose the "Daily at" option without specifying a time, the system may default to a set time for generating the report, or it may prompt you to select a time. Be sure to specify a time to ensure the report is generated at your preferred hour.

9. Will I receive the New Orders Report automatically once it's scheduled?

Yes, once the New Orders Report is scheduled, it will be generated according to your selected time and frequency. The report will be available for download as per the schedule you’ve set.

10. How can I confirm that my New Orders Report has been scheduled successfully?

Once you’ve set your schedule and clicked "Schedule", the report will be scheduled, and you will see it listed under your Scheduled Reports section. You will receive the report based on the schedule you have configured.

11. Can I schedule the report to be generated weekly or monthly?

At this time, the SOP mentions a daily schedule, but you can set specific times for daily generation. If you're looking for a weekly or monthly schedule, check for any updates in the interface or refer to Seller Central help for advanced options.

12. What if I need to cancel or stop the scheduled report?

To cancel or stop a scheduled report, navigate to your Scheduled Reports section and either delete or adjust the schedule for the New Orders Report.

13. Can I schedule the New Orders Report for past dates?

No, you can only schedule the report to start from the current day onward. If you need historical data, you will need to manually download the report for those specific periods.
