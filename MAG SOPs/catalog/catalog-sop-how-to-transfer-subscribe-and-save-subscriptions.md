# Catalog SOP: How to Transfer Subscribe and Save Subscriptions

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-transfer-subscribe-and-save-subscriptions

## **How to Transfer Subscribe and Save Subscriptions**

**Who is this for :** Individuals who need to transfer Subscribe and Save subscriptions.

**Objective :** The purpose of this document is to present the updated process for transferring subscriptions from old to new listings. Subscribe and Save allows clients to secure repeat sales and build brand awareness among highly engaged customers. These subscriptions are tied to SKUs as opposed to ASINs. This means that if an SKU is discontinued, the subscriptions will need to be transferred to the new SKU to remain active.

**Steps:**

1. Go to this [page](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/sns/manage)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-29%20at%201.40.01%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/8M4image.png)

2. Click Actions next to the product with the subscriptions that you want to transfer.

3. Select Request to transfer subscriptions to process the request through a self-service tool.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-29%20at%201.40.09%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/MNDimage.png)

4. Choose the new SKU from the list that appears, then click Submit.

**Allow up to 72 hours** for a successful transfer to be reflected on the Manage Products Page.

To request other types of Subscribe & Save subscription transfers (for example, transfers across ASINs or seller accounts), contact your Amazon account manager or get support on the [Get Help page](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/center). You must provide the following information:

- Serial number
- Existing merchant token
- Existing ASIN
- Existing SKU
- New merchant token
- New ASIN
- New SKU

If a Subscribe & Save offer is unavailable (for example, out of stock) for more than 14 days, we'll automatically transfer associated subscriptions to an identical and available Subscribe & Save offer with the same seller. This affects sellers who have opted in to automatic enrollment for Subscribe & Save.

FAQs

**1. What is the purpose of transferring Subscribe and Save subscriptions?**

- Subscriptions are tied to SKUs rather than ASINs. When a SKU is discontinued or replaced, transferring the subscriptions ensures customers continue to receive their products, maintaining repeat sales and customer satisfaction.

**2. How do I transfer Subscribe and Save subscriptions to a new SKU?**

- Follow these steps:

  - Go to the subscription management page in Seller Central.
  - Click Actions next to the product with subscriptions to be transferred.
  - Select Request to transfer subscriptions using the self-service tool.
  - Choose the new SKU from the list and click Submit.
  - Allow up to 72 hours for the transfer to reflect.

**3. Can subscriptions be transferred between different ASINs or seller accounts?**

- Yes, but this requires contacting Amazon Seller Support or your account manager. Provide the following details for such requests:

  - Serial number
  - Existing merchant token
  - Existing ASIN and SKU
  - New merchant token
  - New ASIN and SKU

**4. How long does it take for a subscription transfer to be processed?**

- Transfers typically take up to 72 hours to reflect on the Manage Products Page after submission.

**5. What happens if a Subscribe and Save offer is out of stock?**

- If the offer is unavailable for more than 14 days, Amazon automatically transfers the subscriptions to an identical and available offer with the same seller. This applies to sellers who have opted in to automatic enrollment for Subscribe and Save.

**6. Is there a way to transfer subscriptions automatically without manual intervention?**

- Automatic subscription transfers occur only when a seller has opted into Amazon's automatic enrollment for Subscribe and Save. Otherwise, manual transfers must be performed using the self-service tool or by contacting Seller Support.

**7. What information do I need to provide for manual subscription transfers involving ASINs or seller accounts?**

- You must provide the following:

  - Serial number
  - Existing merchant token
  - Existing ASIN and SKU
  - New merchant token
  - New ASIN and SKU

**8. What should I do if the new SKU does not appear in the list when transferring subscriptions?**

- Ensure that the new SKU is active, in stock, and enrolled in Subscribe and Save. If the SKU still doesn’t appear, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

**9. How can I confirm that subscriptions have been successfully transferred?**

- Check the Manage Products Page in Seller Central after 72 hours to confirm the subscription transfer. If the transfer doesn’t appear, file a case with Amazon Seller Support.

**10. Can I transfer subscriptions if the product is no longer eligible for Subscribe and Save?**

- No, subscriptions can only be transferred to SKUs that are currently eligible for Subscribe and Save. Ensure the new SKU meets eligibility requirements before initiating the transfer.

**11. What happens if the subscription transfer fails?**

- If a transfer request fails:

  - Double-check that the new SKU is eligible and properly set up for Subscribe and Save.
  - Review the details provided during the transfer request for accuracy.
  - File a case with Amazon Seller Support if the issue persists.

**12. Can customers be notified of subscription transfers?**

- Amazon typically notifies customers if their subscription is transferred to a new SKU. This ensures transparency and prevents confusion.

**13. Are there restrictions on the number of subscriptions that can be transferred?**

- There are no stated restrictions in the SOP regarding the number of subscriptions that can be transferred at once. However, ensure all necessary details are provided during the transfer request for successful processing.

**14. What does “merchant token” refer to in the transfer process?**

- The merchant token is a unique identifier for a seller’s account. It is required for transferring subscriptions across seller accounts to ensure proper authorization and tracking.

**15. What should I do if automatic enrollment is not enabled for my account?**

- If automatic enrollment is not enabled, manually transfer subscriptions through the self-service tool or by contacting Amazon Seller Support.
