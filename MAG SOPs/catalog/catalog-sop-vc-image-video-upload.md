# Catalog SOP: VC Image/Video Upload

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-vc-imagevideo-upload

## How to Upload Images on Vendor Central

**Who is this for :** Everyone who is uploading listing images and videos on Vendor Central.

**Objective:** This SOP will walk you through the process of uploading images on Amazon Vendor Central.

- Amazon Vendor Central is the web interface manufacturers, and distributors use. If you sell via Vendor Central, you’re called a first-party seller – acting as a supplier and selling in bulk to Amazon. Registration on Vendor Central is by invitation only; as such, VC contributions weigh heavier than SC in most cases. When making changes to the account, and the product is listed in both the Seller and Vendor Central accounts, it is encouraged to update both as this increases the chances of pushing the changes on live detail pages.
- **Before uploading any images, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.**

**NOTE :** If we have ASINs selling in different marketplaces and the language on the images is country-specific, we must upload them through the country-specific upload to avoid issues

- Rename it to ASIN.Variant

  - i.e. ASIN.MAIN, ASIN.PT01, ASIN.PT02, ASIN.PT03, etc.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-vc-image-video-upload-image4.jpg)

- Once all images have been resized and renamed, compress the folder into a .zip file.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-vc-image-video-upload-image5.jpg)

- Go back again to the [VC home page](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/). Go to Items, then Upload Images.

- Click the Bulk image upload tab.

  - Drag or browse the image zip file created earlier.
  - Select the correct Vendor Code from the dropdown. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-vc-image-video-upload-image7.jpg)

- Click Submit Images. A progress bar will appear in the box where the zip file was uploaded.

**VC Video Upload**

The process here is the same as with image upload.

- Go to Items, then Upload Images.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-09-14%20at%2010.44.49%E2%80%AFPM(2).png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/z9dimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/z9dimage.png)

- Click Videos tab.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-09-14%20at%2010.47.11%E2%80%AFPM.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/UZyimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/UZyimage.png)

- Fill out the necessary information.

  - Click Upload video and browse the video to be uploaded.
  - Title should be less than 60 characters long.
  - ASINs where the video should be displayed on the PDP.

    - You can add up to 1500 ASINs in a single upload.
    - Video will automatically take up the 7th slot in the Image Stack.
    - If after ASIN input it came up with a RED font, you need to remove that particular ASIN and check its Brand Name. The issue with it is Amazon is not recognizing the Brand Name of that particular ASIN.
  - If no thumbnail is available, Amazon will automatically create one that will be displayed on the PDP
- Once all fields are filled, click Submit.

**NOTES:**

- Avoid uploading videos that is showing product/website URLs.
- Avoid uploading videos that display firearms/ammunition.
- Avoid uploading videos that are too close to sensual/seductive.
- You can upload multiple videos to display on the PDP. The pecking order will be based on which video was uploaded. The most recent video uploaded will be the first to display.

FAQs

#### 1. How do I upload images to my ASIN on Vendor Central?

To upload images to an ASIN on Vendor Central:

1. Log in to Amazon Vendor Central.
2. Go to Items and select Upload Images.
3. Search for the ASIN you want to upload images to.
4. On the right side, browse for the images you want to upload.
5. Select which variant/sequence the images will display under the PDP (Product Detail Page).

  - For variations, codes range from PT01 to PT15.
6. Once you browse the images, they will be uploaded immediately.

#### 2. Do I need client approval before uploading images?

Yes, always communicate with your client and get their approval before uploading images. Include proof of approval (e.g., a screenshot or Fireflies call link) in the Task Details or as a Comment.

#### 3. How do I upload images for ASINs selling in multiple marketplaces?

If the images are country-specific (due to language differences), you must upload them through the country-specific upload to avoid issues. Ensure you select the correct marketplace when uploading.

#### 4. Can I replace images once they’re uploaded?

Yes, you can replace images anytime after they’re uploaded. There is no confirmation button, so once you browse the images and they appear, they’re already uploaded.

#### 5. How do I upload multiple images via a bulk file?

1. Download all the images to be uploaded and place them in a single folder.
2. Rename the images to follow this format: ASIN.Variant (e.g., ASIN.MAIN, ASIN.PT01, ASIN.PT02, etc.).
3. Resize and compress the folder into a .zip file.
4. Go to Items > Upload Images and click the Bulk Image Upload tab.
5. Drag or browse the .zip file.
6. Select the correct Vendor Code from the dropdown.
7. Click Submit Images and monitor the progress with the status report.

#### 6. How do I check if my bulk image upload was successful?

Once the upload is complete, you can check the status report. If the status shows SUCCESS - APPROVED, the images were uploaded successfully.

#### 7. How do I upload videos to Vendor Central?

1. Go to Items > Upload Images and click the Videos tab.
2. Fill in the necessary information, such as the video title (less than 60 characters).
3. Browse for the video file to upload.
4. You can add up to 1500 ASINs for a single video upload.
5. Once completed, click Submit.

#### 8. What if my video has a red font after entering the ASINs?

If you see a red font, it indicates an issue with the Brand Name for the ASIN. Ensure the ASIN’s brand name matches the one registered with Amazon.

#### 9. What happens if no video thumbnail is available?

If no thumbnail is available for your video, Amazon will automatically generate one and display it on the PDP.

#### 10. Can I upload multiple videos for a single ASIN?

Yes, you can upload multiple videos. The most recent video uploaded will be the first to display on the PDP.

#### 11. Are there any video content restrictions?

Yes, avoid uploading videos that:

- Show product/website URLs.
- Display firearms or ammunition.
- Are too close to sensual or seductive themes.
