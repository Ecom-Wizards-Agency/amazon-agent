# Supplemental Article: Maximizing Sales Insights with the Amazon Sales Dashboard

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/supplemental-article-maximizing-sales-insights-with-the-amazon-sales-dashboard

## **Maximizing Sales Insights with the Amazon Sales Dashboard**

The [**Sales Dashboard**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/business-reports/ref=xx_sitemetric_dnav_xx#/dashboard), found in the Business Reports section of your Amazon Seller account, is a powerful tool designed to help Professional sellers track, analyze, and compare their sales performance. Available exclusively to sellers with a Professional selling plan, this feature provides essential insights into sales metrics, customer behavior, and product performance.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DVpimage.png)

**Key Features of the Sales Dashboard**

The Sales Dashboard consists of three main features that help sellers monitor and assess their sales performance:

1. **Sales Snapshot**

  The **Sales Snapshot** is a real-time overview of your sales activity, displaying key statistics such as total sales, order metrics, and other important figures. This data is updated approximately once per hour to reflect the most current trends. The information displayed in the Sales Snapshot aligns with the definitions in the [Business Reports Glossary](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/27691), providing consistent and standardized metrics for easy interpretation.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iZIimage.png)

1. **Compare Sales**

The **Compare Sales** feature offers an interactive way to evaluate sales performance over different time periods. This tool allows you to filter your sales data by date and fulfillment channel (such as FBA or FBM), providing a detailed view of your sales across various dimensions.

- **Date Filters**: Use the Date filter to choose the specific time period for comparison, whether it's for a single day, week, month, or custom range.
- **Fulfillment Channel Filters**: Narrow your data further by selecting the fulfillment method that applies to your sales.
- **Sales Breakdown Filters**: This option allows you to see a breakdown of sales by Marketplace Total, Business Buyers, and Non-Business Buyers, providing a deeper understanding of your customer base.

The **Compare Sales** feature also allows you to toggle between viewing your data in graph format (the default view) or as a table. You can easily switch between these views using the buttons located at the upper right of the page for a more tailored experience.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/05Ximage.png)

**Comparison Options**: Once you've set your Date filter, the system will present several comparison options. For example, if you select "Today" as your filter, the system will allow you to compare your sales with:

- Yesterday
- The same day last week
- The same day last year Alternatively, if you select a "Year to Date" filter, you can compare the current year to the previous one.

This flexibility enables you to see how your sales are trending over time and identify patterns in your performance.

1. **Sales by Category**

  The **Sales by Category** feature allows you to dive deeper into the performance of specific product categories. By understanding how different categories perform, sellers can adjust their strategies to focus on high-performing categories or adjust inventory in areas that need improvement.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/N90image.png)

**4. Business Performance Insights (Beta)**

The **Business Performance Insights** feature, currently in beta, offers a deeper, more personalized analysis of your overall business performance. It provides key metrics such as:

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0vzimage.png)

- **Sales Growth**: An overview of how your ordered product sales have evolved over a given period, including year-over-year comparisons to assess overall growth.
- **Units Sold**: Insights into the number of units sold, including trends and percentage changes compared to previous periods.
- **Average Selling Price**: Information on how the average price of your products has remained consistent or changed over time.
- **Traffic**: Metrics on the volume of page views or visits to your store, highlighting trends in visibility and customer interest.
- **Feature Offer Percentage**: This metric shows the percentage of time your offers are featured, helping you understand your competitive positioning within Amazon's marketplace.

Business Performance Insights helps you track long-term trends and patterns, identifying areas where your business is excelling and where adjustments may be needed. This feature is particularly valuable for sellers aiming to maintain consistent growth and improve their competitive standing on Amazon.

**Understanding Sales Data Trends**

It's important to note that the data displayed in the Sales Dashboard is updated in near real-time, but the most recent data can sometimes show minor fluctuations early in the day. For instance, sales numbers from the current day may appear lower compared to the same time frame in previous days, particularly in the early hours of the day when sales are still ramping up. This pattern becomes less pronounced as the day progresses and more data is collected.

**Benefits of Using the Sales Dashboard**

The Sales Dashboard empowers sellers by providing detailed and actionable insights into sales performance, enabling data-driven decision-making. With tools to compare sales over various time periods, filter by buyer type and fulfillment method, and view category-specific data, sellers can identify opportunities to optimize their product offerings, marketing strategies, and inventory management.

By using the Sales Dashboard regularly, sellers can stay on top of performance trends, spot potential issues before they escalate, and make informed adjustments to maximize sales and improve business outcomes.
