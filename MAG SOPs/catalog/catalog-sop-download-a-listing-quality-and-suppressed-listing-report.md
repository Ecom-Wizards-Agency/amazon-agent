# Catalog SOP: Download a Listing Quality and Suppressed Listing Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-listing-quality-and-suppressed-listing-report

## **Download a Listing Quality and Suppressed Listing Report**

**Who is this for :** This SOP is intended for sellers using an online marketplace platform who want to assess and improve the quality of their product listings. It is suitable for sellers who wish to identify and rectify any listing quality issues or suppressed listings that may be hindering their visibility and sales.

**Objective :** To guide sellers on the process of accessing the report in Seller Central. By generating these reports, sellers can improve the quality of their listings, increase visibility, and resolve any problems causing listings to be suppressed.

**Suppressed listing**

1. Access the “Inventory” tab from the top menu of Seller Central and select “Manage All Inventory” from the Drop-Down Menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684900322980.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/A1Timage.png)

2. In the Manage Inventory page, choose “Search Suppressed and Inactive Listings”.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-24%20at%2011.52.51%20AM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LpLimage.png)

3. On the next page, click "Search Suppressed" to filter the report to only include suppressed ASINS.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-24%20at%2012.00.24%20PM.png)

4. Click "Download Suppressed Listings Report" on the top right corner of the page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-24%20at%2012.02.09%20PM.png)

**Listing Quality**

1. Access the “Catalog” tab from the top menu of Seller Central and select “Improve Listing Quality” from the Drop-Down Menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-24%20at%206.33.56%20PM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6OJimage.png)

2. In the Listing Quality Dashboard, choose "Add missing Attributes in Bulk"

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3Kbimage.png)

3. On the next page, click "Generate Report".

FAQs

1. How do I access the Suppressed Listings Report in Seller Central?

To access the Suppressed Listings Report, go to the "Inventory" tab in Seller Central and select "Manage All Inventory." Then, choose "Search Suppressed and Inactive Listings." On the next page, click "Search Suppressed" to filter the report, then click "Download Suppressed Listings Report" at the top right corner of the page.

2. Where can I find the Listing Quality Report?

To access the Listing Quality Report, go to the "Catalog" tab in Seller Central and select "Improve Listing Quality." From the Listing Quality Dashboard, select "Add missing Attributes in Bulk," and then click "Generate Report" on the next page.

3. What does the Suppressed Listings Report include?

The Suppressed Listings Report includes all ASINs that are suppressed, meaning they are not visible to potential buyers due to issues such as missing or incorrect information.

4. How can I filter the Suppressed Listings Report to only show suppressed ASINs?

After navigating to the "Manage Inventory" page and selecting "Search Suppressed and Inactive Listings," click the "Search Suppressed" button to filter the report and show only the suppressed listings.

5. What type of issues does the Listing Quality Report help identify?

The Listing Quality Report helps identify missing attributes or other quality issues with product listings that may affect visibility, search rankings, and sales performance on Amazon.

6. Can I download both the Listing Quality and Suppressed Listings Reports at the same time?

No, these reports are accessed separately. To get the Suppressed Listings Report, you follow steps in the "Inventory" section, while the Listing Quality Report is accessed through the "Catalog" section.

7. How do I resolve issues with suppressed listings?

To resolve suppressed listings, download the report and review the identified issues. Follow the instructions in Seller Central to address each issue, such as adding missing attributes or correcting errors in the listing details.

8. What should I do if the Listing Quality Report identifies missing attributes?

If the report identifies missing attributes, you can use the "Add missing Attributes in Bulk" option to update multiple listings at once. This will help improve the completeness and quality of your listings.

9. How often should I download these reports?

It is recommended to download and review these reports regularly to ensure that your product listings are in good standing, not suppressed, and that all required attributes are added to maintain visibility and improve sales performance.

10. Can I access the Listing Quality Report for a specific product or ASIN?

The Listing Quality Report does not allow you to generate a report for a single ASIN but instead provides an overview of listings needing quality improvements. However, you can filter the report to view specific products if needed.
