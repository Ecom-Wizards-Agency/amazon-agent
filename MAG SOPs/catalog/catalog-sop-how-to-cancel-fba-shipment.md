# Catalog SOP: How to Cancel FBA Shipment

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-cancel-fba-shipment

## How to Cancel FBA Shipment

**Who is this for :** Intended for Amazon sellers who need to cancel a previously created FBA shipment

**Objective :** Provide steps and guidance on how to cancel an FBA shipment and ensure that the correct inventory levels are reflected in the seller's account. This can prevent potential fulfillment errors in receiving the shipment.

You can cancel the shipment by clicking Cancel Shipment at the bottom of the workflow page. This will cancel all shipments that you created and move them into Canceled status in your Shipping Queue.

**To cancel a shipment, follow these steps:**

Log in to your account and look for the FBA shipment that you would like to cancel.

1. Go to Inventory → Shipments

2. Look for the FBA Shipment ID in the shipping queue page or search the ID on the search box.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%202.12.15%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1bUimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/1bUimage.png)
3. Click the Shipment ID link

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%202.12.22%E2%80%AFAM.png)

**NOTE: Applicable for Amazon-partnered carrier charges**

Small parcel shipments have 24 hours to void Amazon-partnered carrier charges and receive a full refund after you confirm the shipping selections and accept the charges.

For pallet shipments, after you have confirmed your shipping selections and accepted the charges, you have one hour to void Amazon-partnered carrier charges and get a full refund.

If the shipments go beyond the time frame above, you can still cancel the shipment and request a refund for the unused shipping label by contacting seller support.

For more information about Send to Amazon: Cancel your shipment, see [here](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/GP29SYECJZGJ9XMR)

FAQs

1. **Why would I need to cancel an FBA shipment?**

- You may need to cancel an FBA shipment if:

  - You entered incorrect inventory quantities or wrong product details.
  - You selected the wrong fulfillment center.
  - You changed your mind about sending inventory to FBA.
  - You want to switch to a different carrier or shipping method.
  - Your supplier or warehouse is delaying shipment and you need to replan logistics.

1. **What happens to my inventory after I cancel an FBA shipment?**

- Canceling a shipment does not automatically return inventory to "Available" in Seller Central. You may need to:
- Manually adjust inventory levels if the shipment was never sent.
- Wait for Amazon to update the status (this usually happens within 24 hours).

1. **Can I cancel a shipment if I’ve already sent part of it?**

- No, once a portion of the shipment has been shipped or received by Amazon, you cannot cancel it. You can:

  - Remove remaining, unshipped units from the shipment.
  - Create a removal order for units already in transit or received at Amazon's fulfillment center.

1. **Does canceling an FBA shipment affect my account health or performance metrics?**

- No, canceling a shipment does not negatively impact your account health. However, if you frequently create and cancel shipments, Amazon may flag this as unusual behavior.

1. **What if the "Cancel Shipments" button is missing or not working?**

- If you cannot find the Cancel Shipments button:

  - Ensure you are in the correct shipment workflow (e.g., "Send to Amazon").
  - Check if the shipment is already in transit—if so, it cannot be canceled.
  - Try clearing your browser cache or switching browsers.
  - Contact Seller Support if the button is missing or grayed out.

1. **What can I do if the Amazon-partnered carrier charge refund window has passed?**

- If you missed the 24-hour refund window for small parcels or 1-hour window for pallets, you can:

  - Still cancel the shipment, but you may not receive a full refund.
  - Contact Seller Support to request a refund on the unused shipping label.
  - Use the shipping label for a different shipment if Amazon allows it.

1. **What happens if my shipment is already in transit or received by Amazon?**

- If the shipment is:

  - In transit: You cannot cancel it, but you can create a removal order once it arrives at Amazon.
  - Received at Amazon: Your inventory will be checked in and processed. If you don’t want the products to be sold, you must create a removal order.

1. **How long does it take for Amazon to process a canceled shipment?**

- Once you cancel a shipment:

  - It typically updates in Seller Central within a few minutes to an hour.
  - Inventory status may take 24 hours to reflect correctly.
  - Refunds for Amazon-partnered carrier charges (if applicable) may take 3–5 business days.
