# Catalog SOP: How to Fix Errors in the Vine Program on Seller Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-fix-errors-in-the-vine-program-on-seller-central

## **How to Fix Errors in the Vine Program on Seller Central**

**Who is this for:** This is for Amazon sellers who have enrolled their products in the Vine Program on Seller Central and need to fix any errors that may occur during the enrollment process.

**Objective:** To provide instructions on how to fix errors that may occur with enrolled products in the Vine Program on Seller Central for Amazon sellers.

- If you are a seller on Amazon, you may have enrolled your products in the Vine Program on Seller Central, which allows Vine Voices to review your products. However, there may be times when errors occur with your enrolled products.

**Follow the steps below to fix these errors:**

1. Go to the top menu of Seller Central and select "Advertising" from the drop-down menu, then click on "Vine".

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Z0Aimage.png)

2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the "Get Started With Vine" button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1719948689785.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4Fdimage.png)

3. On the next page, scroll down to the bottom, and you will see the details of the product you enrolled in the Vine Program, including the enrollment date, the number of units available for Vine Voices, and the number of Vine reviews published for your recent enrollments.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-05-26_9-24-21(3).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dA1image.png)

4. If the status of your enrolled ASIN is "Action Required", click on the link to be taken to the error page. Amazon will bring any enrollment with action required to the top of your dashboard for you to take appropriate action as quickly as possible.

5. There are different types of errors you may encounter:

- **Too many reviews:** This occurs when the enrolled product reaches the 30-review limit to be eligible for Vine. At this point, Amazon will stop offering the product to Vine Voices. You do not need to take any action, and the enrollment will automatically be concluded.
- **No FBA offer:** This occurs when the SKU loses its FBA offer. You can fix it by converting your offer to FBA again.
- **No image:** This occurs when the product does not have a compliant image. You can fix it by uploading a new high-quality image to your listing.
- **Associate SKU with your brand:** This occurs when the SKU is not associated with a brand that you are the registered agent for in Brand Registry. Visit the [Brand Registry help page](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G200955930) for more information on how to associate your SKUs with your brand.
- **Not associated with the enrolled parent:**This occurs when the enrolled product is no longer part of the same product variation family. To fix this, cancel your enrollment and re-enroll in the product family.

6. By following these steps, you can quickly fix any errors with your enrolled products in the Vine Program on Seller Central.

FAQs

**1. Can all sellers fix Vine enrollment errors, or do some require Amazon support?**

- Most common errors (e.g., No Image, No FBA Offer) can be fixed by the seller. However, issues like "Associate SKU with your brand" may require Amazon Seller Support intervention.

**2. How can I track unresolved Vine enrollment errors?**

- Go to Seller Central > Advertising > Vine.
- Look for "Action Required" next to enrolled products.
- Click on the error link to see required fixes.

**3. How long do I have to fix an error before Vine enrollment is canceled?**

- There is no official deadline, but unresolved errors may prevent Vine Voices from claiming units, which could lead to Amazon automatically closing the enrollment.

**4. What should I do if I can’t fix an error?**

- First, double-check the error message in Vine and try the recommended solution.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support through Seller Central > Help > Contact Us > Advertising & Stores > Vine Program Issues.

**5. How do I confirm that an error has been successfully fixed?**

- Refresh the Vine enrollment dashboard after applying the fix.
- If the error no longer appears, the ASIN is back in the program.
- If the status remains "Action Required" after 24 hours, check for additional issues or contact Amazon.

**6. Can I re-enroll an ASIN after fixing an error?**

- Yes, once an issue is resolved, you can re-enroll the ASIN in Vine as long as it still meets the eligibility criteria.

**7. What happens if a product with errors has already been claimed by Vine Voices?**

- If units have been claimed before the error occurred, Vine reviews will continue as normal.
- If the error prevents additional claims, fixing the issue may allow Vine Voices to claim remaining units.

**8. Who should I contact if fixing an error does not work?**

- If the issue persists, open a case with Amazon Seller Support:

  - Seller Central > Help > Contact Us > Advertising & Stores > Vine Program Issues.
  - Provide the ASIN, error message, and steps already taken to fix it.
