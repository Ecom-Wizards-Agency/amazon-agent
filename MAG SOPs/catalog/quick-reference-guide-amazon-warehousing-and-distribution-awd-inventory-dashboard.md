Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/quick-reference-guide-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-inventory-dashboard

## Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD) Inventory Dashboard

**Accessing the AWD Inventory Dashboard:**

1. **Login to Seller Central.**
2. Navigate to the **Inventory** drop-down menu.
3. Select **Warehousing and Distribution (AWD)**.

**Actions You Can Take on the Dashboard:**

1. **Send Inventory to AWD**: Use the action menu on the right to send inventory to AWD.
2. **Manual Replenishment**: If auto-replenishment isn’t sufficient, manually move inventory by clicking **Move from AWD**.
3. **Track Add-On Shipments**: Create and track replenishment shipments for products opted out of automatic replenishment.

- #### AWD Inventory Overview
- Real-Time Status: The AWD inventory dashboard shows the status of inventory stored in AWD facilities, in transit to AWD, and available in FBA.
- Inventory Management: Review data on your SKUs and track add-on or manual replenishment shipments, including inventory opted out of automated replenishment.

| Section | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Key Features of the Dashboard | Product Information:
Product Title: The name of your product.
SKU: Unique identifier you assign to your product.
ASIN: Amazon’s unique identifier for products.
FNSKU: Amazon’s identifier for individual seller offers for a specific ASIN.
Inventory Data:
Inbound to AWD: Units in shipments sent to AWD but not yet received. Click Details for shipment tracking info.
Available in AWD: Inventory present at AWD warehouses available for FBA replenishment. Data shows whether it’s out of stock, low stock, or adequate.
Reserved in AWD: Inventory tied to orders, in transit, or set aside for processing. Includes FBA orders, Off-Amazon orders, and DC transfers.
Undistributable Inventory: Items with issues (e.g., damaged, lost, or expired) that cannot be distributed.
Outbound to FBA: Units in transit from AWD to FBA. Click Details to track shipments.
Available in FBA: Units available in FBA for immediate buyer orders, excluding pending removals. Shows how many days the current FBA inventory will meet demand.
Reserved in FBA: Inventory reserved for orders, transfers, or processing at FBA fulfillment centers. |
| AWD Inventory Performance Metrics | FBA Replenishment:
Active: Replenishment is active.
Failed: Replenishment is inactive due to an error. Fix the issue to reactivate.
Manual: You are manually managing the replenishment process.
Replenishment Quantity: Shows the number of units planned for replenishment within the next week, with options to view details for the next two weeks. |
| AWD Discounted Fee Metric | To qualify for discounted rates (effective April 1, 2025), your SKU must meet two criteria:
Automated Replenishment Ratio: At least 70% of your SKU’s inventory must be sent to fulfillment centers via AWD automated replenishment in the last 90 days.
Combined Historical Days of Supply: The combined inventory level in AWD and FBA must meet or exceed 70 days relative to historical demand. The higher value from either the last 30 or 90 days is used to assess this metric. |
