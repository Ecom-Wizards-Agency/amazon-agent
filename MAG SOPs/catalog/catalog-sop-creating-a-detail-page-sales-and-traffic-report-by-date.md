## Creating a Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report by Date

**Who is this for:**This is for individuals who have access to Seller Central and need to download a business report on the platform. It is specifically targeted towards users who want to create a Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report by Date.

**Objective:** The objective of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to download a Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report on Seller Central. By following these instructions, users will be able to access and customize the report according to their requirements.

The Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report is a valuable tool for sellers on Seller Central, as it provides insights into the performance of their products over a specified time period. By following the steps outlined in this SOP, users will be able to access this report and tailor it to their needs, including selecting specific columns and customizing the date range. The report can then be downloaded for further analysis, helping sellers make informed decisions to optimize their business strategies.

**To create a Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report, follow these steps:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Business Reports" from the Drop-Down Menu.

2. Under the sub-heading "By Date", click on the "Detail Page Sales and Traffic" link in the left side menu.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ScNimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/ScNimage.png)

3. Click the "SHOW/HIDE COLUMNS" button on the left side of the page to open up the available column fields. Choose which columns and information you want to include in your report by selecting or deselecting them.

4. Customize the dates by clicking the calendar icons for the "From" date and "To" date" respectively on the right side of the page above the table.

FAQs

1. What is the Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report used for?

The report provides insights into product performance over a selected time period, helping sellers track page views, conversion rates, and sales data. It is useful for making informed business decisions.

2. Who can access the Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report?

Only users with the appropriate permissions in Seller Central can access and download this report. If you don’t see the option, check with your account administrator.

3. Can I download the report in different formats?

Yes, the report is typically available in CSV format, which can be opened in spreadsheet programs like Excel or Google Sheets for further analysis.

4. Why don’t I see the “Detail Page Sales and Traffic” option in Business Reports?

This may be due to insufficient account permissions, report availability for your marketplace, or temporary technical issues. Try refreshing the page or checking your user permissions.

5. How often is the Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report updated?

The data in the report is updated daily, but there may be slight delays in reflecting the most recent sales and traffic data.

6. Can I schedule automatic downloads of this report?

Seller Central currently does not offer an automated scheduling feature for this specific report. However, you can manually download it as needed.

7. What columns should I select for the most important insights?

Key columns to consider include Page Views, Sessions, Unit Session Percentage, Buy Box Percentage, Ordered Product Sales, and Units Ordered. The best columns depend on your specific analysis goals.

8. Why is my downloaded report missing some data?

Ensure you have selected the correct date range and columns before downloading. Also, note that historical data may not be available for all time periods.

9. Can I generate reports for multiple ASINs at once?

Yes, the report includes data for all ASINs in your account by default. You can filter and sort the data after downloading it.

10. How can I analyze the report effectively?

Use spreadsheet tools to filter, sort, and create pivot tables for deeper insights. Look at trends in sessions, conversion rates, and sales to optimize product listings and marketing efforts.
