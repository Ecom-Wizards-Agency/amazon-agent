# Catalog SOP: Duplicate Bullet Points Audit and Troubleshooting

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-duplicate-bullet-points-audit-and-troubleshooting

## **Duplicate Bullet Points: Audit and Troubleshooting**

**Who is this for :** This is intended for Amazon sellers who need help locating and fixing technical issues that result in duplicate bullet points in their product listings.

**Objective :** To accurately detect and effectively address any instances of duplicated bullet points within a particular ASIN.

**Instructions:**

1. Open your Amazon Seller Central account and log in.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712243284585.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/68fimage.png)

2. Download a CLR or Category Listing Report. You may view the [How to Download a Category Listings Report (CLR) SOP](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-category-listings-report) for reference.

3. Open the CLR or Category Listing Report then, create your Google sheet tracker and copy all bullet points columns (BULLET 1 - 10) together with the SKU, Fulfilment Center, and Quantity. (so you can go ahead and perform a partial update if needed).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712243292654.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/29simage.png)

4. The usual number of bullet points is five. Check if there are copy values in bullet_6, bullet_7, bullet_8, bullet_9, and bullet_10 per SKU. Here is an example screenshot that will help you quickly determine whether the listing contains repeated bullet points.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712243301276.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dV6image.png)

5. Confirm the repeated bullet points on the Amazon detail page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-04-09%20at%201.18.23%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zbXimage.png)

6. Once confirmed, you can update and remove the redundant bullet points manually or by utilizing a flat file.

If you want to fix one listing via bulk updating, click on the link to the individual SOPs below.

- [Listing Update (Manual) SOP](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-update-product-listings-manually)
- [Listing Update (Flat File) SOP](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-listing-partial-update-flat-file)

7. Perform a partial update via a flat file containing only the first five (5) bullets and the bare minimum requirements.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712243317048.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Zqsimage.png)

8. After an hour or 24 hours, revisit the detail page of the Amazon listings to check if the bullet copy has been rectified.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712243324926.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dXhimage.png)

9. In case the duplicate bullet points persist despite following the above instructions, it is recommended to file a case with Amazon. You can refer to this [Case Creation SOP](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-file-a-ticket-in-seller-central).

FAQs

1. How can I identify duplicate bullet points in my listing?

You can identify duplicate bullet points by downloading the Category Listing Report (CLR) and reviewing the bullet points for each SKU. Check bullet_6 to bullet_10 columns for any duplicate values. You can also visit the Amazon product detail page to confirm if duplicates appear there.

2. What is the usual number of bullet points for a listing?

Most listings have five bullet points. If there are more than five (bullet_6, bullet_7, etc.), check those extra bullet points for repetition.

3. What should I do if I find duplicate bullet points in my listing?

Once you confirm the duplicate bullet points, you can either manually remove the duplicates or use a flat file for bulk updating. Follow the relevant SOPs to update the listing.

4. How do I remove duplicate bullet points manually?

You can manually edit your listing by going to the Manage Inventory section in Seller Central, selecting the product, and updating the bullet points. Make sure to remove the redundant ones.

5. How can I perform a bulk update to fix duplicate bullet points?

To fix multiple listings at once, download the flat file containing the first five bullet points and only the necessary attributes. Follow the Listing Update (Flat File) SOP for instructions on performing the bulk update.

6. How do I check if the update fixed the duplicate bullet points?

Wait an hour or up to 24 hours after making the changes, then revisit the product detail page to ensure the duplicates have been removed.

7. What should I do if the duplicate bullet points still persist after following the instructions?

If the issue remains unresolved, you should file a case with Amazon Seller Support for assistance. Refer to the Case Creation SOP for guidance on how to file the case.

8. How can I track the progress of fixing duplicate bullet points?

After downloading the CLR and copying the necessary bullet point columns to a Google sheet, you can track the progress of your updates by comparing the corrected data with what appears on the Amazon detail page.

9. How often should I perform a duplicate bullet point audit?

It’s good practice to periodically audit your listings, especially after making bulk updates or changes to ensure no duplicate content is introduced.
