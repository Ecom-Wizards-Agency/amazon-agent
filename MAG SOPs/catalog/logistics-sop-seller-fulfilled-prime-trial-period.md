## **Seller Fulfilled Prime Trial Period**

**Who is this for :** Seller Fulfilled Prime Trial Period can be a great way for sellers to test the program's benefits and determine if it is a good fit for their business, without incurring significant upfront costs or long-term commitments. Amazon sellers who can meet Amazon's performance requirements can enroll in the Seller Fulfilled Prime program.

**Objective :** To provide sellers with a low-risk way to test the program and determine if it is a good fit for their business. By offering Prime-eligible products, sellers can potentially increase their sales and reach a larger customer base, while also providing Amazon Prime members with access to a wider selection of products.

**Requirements to Qualify During the Trial Period:**

1. You must meet specific performance requirements for a minimum of 50 Prime trial orders. You can check your Prime unit sold by going to [**HERE**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-fulfilled-prime/analytics#SFP_DASHBOARD)

FAQs

1. What is the Seller Fulfilled Prime Trial Period?

The Seller Fulfilled Prime (SFP) Trial Period allows sellers to test the program’s benefits without long-term commitments or upfront costs. During the trial, you can offer Prime-eligible products, gain access to a larger customer base, and potentially increase sales.

2. What are the requirements to qualify during the trial period?

To qualify during the trial period, you must:

- Complete a minimum of 50 Prime trial orders.
- Maintain an On-Time Shipment Rate of 99% or higher.
- Use Buy Shipping Services for at least 98% of Prime trial orders.
- Keep the cancellation rate below 0.5%.

3. How can I track my Prime unit sales during the trial period?

You can track your Prime unit sales by visiting the Seller Fulfilled Prime Analytics Dashboard at this link: [Prime Dashboard](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-fulfilled-prime/analytics#SFP_DASHBOARD).

4. How do I ensure my On-Time Shipment Rate meets the 99% requirement?

To maintain a 99% on-time shipment rate, ship Prime trial orders on the same day they are received, provided the order is placed before your cutoff time.

5. How can I meet the Buy Shipping requirement for Prime trial orders?

You must use Amazon’s Buy Shipping Services for at least 98% of Prime trial orders. This ensures that your shipping is compliant with Amazon’s requirements.

6. How can I keep my cancellation rate below 0.5%?

Ensure that your inventory levels are accurate and that you fulfill orders promptly to avoid cancellations. Double-check your stock before listing products and remove any unavailable items.

7. How do I add SKUs to the Prime Shipping template?

To add SKUs to your Prime Shipping template:

1. Select the SKUs you want to assign to the template.
2. Click Change shipping template.
3. Choose the Prime template from the dropdown menu.

8. When will the Prime badge appear on my products?

The Prime badge will not appear on your products until the trial period is complete and you have met the performance requirements for the trial orders.

9. What happens if I don’t meet the trial requirements?

If your metrics do not meet the requirements during the 50 trial orders, you will need to restart the trial process from the beginning with another 50 trial orders.

10. How can I check my Program Eligibility Scorecard?

You can check your Program Eligibility Scorecard by visiting: [Seller Fulfilled Prime Program Eligibility](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-fulfilled-prime/seller-performance).
