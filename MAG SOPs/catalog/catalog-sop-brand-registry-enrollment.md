# Catalog SOP: Brand Registry Enrollment

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-brand-registry-enrollment

## **How to Enroll a Brand in Amazon Brand Registry**

**Who is this for?**Trademark owners who want to enroll their trademarks in the Amazon Brand Registry Program.

**Objective:** This document provides step-by-step guidance on the correct process of enrolling your brand on Amazon.

1. Access [**Amazon Brand Registry Page**](https://sell.amazon.com/brand-registry)

2. Click on either of the two: **Enroll a new brand** or **Manage / Enroll a brand**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712168013748.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/V28image.png)

3. A page like shown below will open and all you need to do is click on **Enroll your brand.**

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712168019174.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oHpimage.png)

4. Insert all the information required. You will find a guide below the next image.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712168024648.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/NcTimage.png)

- **What is your brand name?**

This should be the **WORD MARK**indicated in the trademark registration website

- **Select a Trademark Office**

Select the trademark office where you have registered your trademark.

- **Enter the registration or a serial number**

This is found on the trademark receipt.

**⚠️ *Important note*:** *Be mindful of any separation between the words.*

- **URL to the brand’s official website**

This information is not mandatory and it can be left blank.

However, it is recommended to fill it in, because it will increase the odds of your request being accepted.

- **URL to the storefront of other e-commerce sites**

This information is also optional. If you have this information, fill it in. However, if you are not sure, this field should be left blank.

- **Product Images**

**⚠️ This is the most important information.**

Images should be real-life photographs (not edited professional photos) showcasing the permanently affixed branding (not a sticker with a logo).

*Images of the product or packaging, which shows branding on either. The images can show the product and packaging when either held in hand or placed on a table. The branding must be permanently affixed.*

Provide as many as you can and make sure that none of them exceeds 5MG.

Sample:

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KReimage.png)

When all is filled out, click Next.

This will lead you to the following page :

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712168041185.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/BZQimage.png)

- **Are you a seller or a vendor for the brand you’re enrolling in?**

Choose the appropriate selling relation.

- **Categories that best describe the brand**

You will need to choose the correct option from the dropdown list. You may refer to the **"GOODS AND SERVICES"**section on the trademark registration website to verify the scope of service of the trademark/brand.

**⚠️**Important note: It is important to mark all relevant categories (it could be more than one category).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712168046836.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/AwOimage.png)

- Top-selling ASINs for each product category where your brand is sold

Add all ASINs relevant to the brand or best-sellers.

**⚠️**Important note: Make sure that the brand information in the product detail page on Amazon matches**fully and correctly**the brand name you are enrolling in. In some cases, the listing title can state one brand (the one you are enrolling in, while in the Vital info, there is a completely different brand name).

Once finished filling out all the information you may click the button Next.

FAQs

- **Q : What information do I need to provide for my brand’s enrollment?**

  - A: You will need:
  - Brand Name: The WORD MARK from your trademark registration.
  - Trademark Office: The office where your trademark is registered.
  - Registration/Serial Number: Found on your trademark receipt.
  - Product Images: Real-life photographs showing permanently affixed branding.
  - Optional Information: URL to the brand’s official website and e-commerce storefronts (recommended but not mandatory).

- **Q : Are product images mandatory for enrollment?**

  - A: Yes, product images are essential. They must be real-life photographs (not edited) showing the branding permanently affixed on the product or packaging. Images must not exceed 5MB.

- **Q : What should I include in the product images?**

  - A: The images should showcase the product or packaging with the branding either held in hand or placed on a table. Stickers with logos are not acceptable; the branding must be permanently affixed.

- **Q : What should I do if I don’t have a URL for my brand’s website or storefront?**

  - A: These fields are optional. If you don’t have the information, you can leave them blank. However, providing this information increases the likelihood of your request being accepted.

- **Q : How do I select the categories for my brand?**

  - A: Choose the categories that best describe your brand from the dropdown list. Refer to the "GOODS AND SERVICES" section on your trademark registration to verify the scope of your trademark.

- **Q : What are top-selling ASINs, and why are they needed?**

  - A: Top-selling ASINs are the best-performing products for your brand on Amazon. These are needed to associate your brand with its products during the enrollment process. Ensure the brand name in the product details matches the brand you are enrolling.

- **Q : What happens after submitting the application?**

  - A: Once submitted, Amazon will review the application, usually within 24 hours. They will send a verification code to the contact email associated with the trademark.

- **Q : What should I do if I don’t have access to the trademark email for the verification code?**

  - A: You can reach out to the contact email's administrator and request the verification code sent by Amazon. Once you receive it, provide the code to Amazon to complete the verification process.

- **Q : How will I know if my brand has been successfully enrolled?**

  - A: After Amazon verifies the provided information and the verification code, you will receive a confirmation that your request has been approved, and your brand will be successfully enrolled in the Amazon Brand Registry.

- **Q : How long does the entire process take?**

  - A: Amazon typically responds within 24 hours after the application is submitted. The duration may vary depending on the verification process and response times.
