## Set up European Fulfillment Network (EFN)

**Who is this for :** For Amazon seller who is planning to expand their business internationally.

**Objective :** To successfully set up and implement the European Fulfillment Network (EFN) in order to efficiently manage and fulfill customer orders across the European region, while optimizing costs and improving overall customer satisfaction. European Fulfillment Network (EFN) allow sellers with an Amazon Europe Stores account who are also registered for Fulfillment by Amazon to store their inventory in their local fulfillment center and fulfill orders coming from other European stores from the same local inventory pool, maximizing the control and flexibility that sellers have over their inventory.

**Here are the 3 steps to expand your business internationally with the European Fulfillment Network between the UK and the EU.**

1. Enable the European Fulfillment Network between the UK and the EU
2. List your offers using the same SKU
3. Check the pricing of your offers

**Enable the European Fulfillment Network between the UK and the EU**

FAQs

#### 1. What is the European Fulfillment Network (EFN)?

The European Fulfillment Network (EFN) allows sellers with an Amazon Europe Stores account, who are also registered for Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA), to store inventory in a local fulfillment center and fulfill orders across other European Amazon marketplaces from the same inventory pool. This setup helps maximize control, flexibility, and cost efficiency for sellers expanding into the European market.

#### 2. Who can use EFN?

Sellers who have an Amazon Europe Stores account and are enrolled in Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) can use the European Fulfillment Network to expand their business and fulfill orders across Europe from their local inventory.

#### 3. How do I enable the European Fulfillment Network (EFN) between the UK and the EU?

To enable EFN between the UK and the EU, follow these steps:

- Go to Settings and click on Fulfillment by Amazon.
- In the Fulfillment by Amazon Settings page, find the Cross-Border Fulfillment Settings and click Edit.
- In the EFN between the UK and the EU section, select or unselect the regions where you want to fulfill cross-border orders.
- Click Update to store your preferences.

#### 4. How do I list my offers using the same SKU for EFN?

To list your offers across multiple European regions, ensure that you are using the same SKU for each offer. This will allow your inventory to be shared across the eligible European Fulfillment Network regions.

#### 5. How can I check the pricing of my offers for EFN?

Make sure to review and adjust the pricing of your offers to ensure they are competitive in each European marketplace. Adjusting your pricing may be necessary to accommodate shipping, storage, and fulfillment costs when expanding across different countries.

#### 6. How do I check the status of my offers after enabling EFN?

After enabling EFN, you can check the status of your offers and manage enrollment by downloading the ASIN Eligibility Report on the Pan-European FBA Inventory page.

- Go to the Inventory tab and select Pan-European FBA Inventory.
- In the Pan-European FBA Inventory page, click on Reports and look for Manage your European Fulfillment Network settings across UK and EU.
- Download the report and confirm your offers' status by adding or removing them as needed.

#### 7. How do I add or remove offers for each EU store using EFN?

To add or remove offers for each EU store:

- Download the European Fulfillment Network ASIN Eligibility Report.
- Edit the report by adding or removing your offers.
- Upload the updated report to confirm the changes and ensure the offers are eligible for cross-border fulfillment.

#### 8. What if I need more information about EFN?

For more detailed information about EFN, you can refer to the following help pages:

- European Fulfillment Network
- Pan-European FBA product eligibility
- Pan-European FBA ASIN Enrollment
