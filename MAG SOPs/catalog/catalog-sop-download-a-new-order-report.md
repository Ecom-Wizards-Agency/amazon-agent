# Catalog SOP: Download a New Order Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-new-order-report

## Download a New Order Report

**Who is this for :** Intended for individuals who are responsible for order management and fulfillment within an e-commerce business. It can be used by order processors, inventory managers, or any personnel involved in processing and fulfilling customer orders.

**Objective :** The objective of the SOP for downloading a new orders report is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to retrieve the latest report containing new orders from an e-commerce platform. The SOP aims to ensure that the process of obtaining the new orders report is efficient, accurate, and accessible to relevant personnel for further order processing and fulfillment.

To generate a New Orders Report:

1. Navigate to the “Orders” Tab on the Top Menu of Seller Central and select “Order Reports” from the Drop-Down Menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-NSUM50L3.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1qrimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/1qrimage.png)

2. From the Order Reports page, click the "New Orders" tab.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1716149383247.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/k8zimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/k8zimage.png)

3. Under the “New Orders” tab, select a date range from the available Drop-Down Menu under the “Request Report” heading and click the “Request” button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1716149419154.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/NZBimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/NZBimage.png)

4. You can choose to include a sales channel column by ticking the relevant box. If you want to add or remove columns, click the “Add or remove order report columns” link in the top right side of the page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1716149504290.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/me5image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/me5image.png)

5. Select or deselect the fields, and then click the “Save changes” button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1716149526235.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vPwimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/vPwimage.png)

6. Your requested report will appear in the “Download Report” table below with a Report Status of “Request Submitted” and a Download Action of “Not Ready”.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1716149540508.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/FZBimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/FZBimage.png)

7. Keep refreshing the page by clicking the “Refresh” button and a clickable button appears in the Download Action column, allowing you to download the report in .csv format.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1716149555378.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I find the "New Orders" report in Seller Central?

To access the New Orders report, navigate to the Orders tab at the top of Seller Central, then select Order Reports from the drop-down menu. On the next page, click the "New Orders" tab to proceed.

2. How do I select the date range for the New Orders report?

Under the "New Orders" tab, you will see the "Request Report" heading. From here, select your desired date range from the available drop-down menu and then click the "Request" button to generate the report for that period.

3. Can I customize the columns in the New Orders report?

Yes, you can customize the columns in the report. To do so, click the "Add or remove order report columns" link in the top right corner of the page. This will allow you to select or deselect the fields you want to include or remove, and then click "Save changes" to apply your preferences.

4. Is it possible to include the sales channel in the New Orders report?

Yes, you can include the sales channel column in the report by ticking the relevant box under the "Request Report" heading. This will add the sales channel information to your report.

5. What is the "Report Status" and how does it work?

The Report Status will initially show as "Request Submitted", indicating that your report is being generated. Once the report is ready, the status will change, and a clickable Download button will appear in the Download Action column.

6. How can I download the New Orders report?

After clicking "Request" and refreshing the page, you will see the Download Action column change to a clickable button once the report is ready. Click the Download button to download the report in .csv format.

7. How do I know when the New Orders report is ready for download?

You will need to refresh the page by clicking the "Refresh" button until a clickable Download button appears in the Download Action column. Once this button appears, the report is ready to be downloaded.

8. What should I do if the New Orders report is not ready after refreshing?

If the report is not ready after refreshing, it may take additional time to generate. Ensure that you are checking the page periodically. If the issue persists, you may want to contact Seller Support for assistance.

9. Can I request multiple New Orders reports at once?

Currently, only one report can be requested at a time. However, you can adjust the date range or customize the columns before requesting the next report.

10. What file format is the New Orders report downloaded in?

The report is available for download in the .csv file format, which can be opened in applications like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets for further analysis.

11. Is there a limit to how many orders are included in the New Orders report?

The report will include all new orders within the selected date range. There is no specific limit on the number of orders, but the system may impose limits based on performance or system capabilities. If you encounter issues with very large orders, you may need to adjust the date range.

12. What happens if I accidentally click "Save Changes" without modifying the columns?

If you click "Save Changes" without making any modifications to the columns, the report will remain unchanged. You can continue with your report generation process without any issues.

13. How can I troubleshoot if the New Orders report is not generating?

If the report is not generating, ensure you have selected a valid date range and that the system is not experiencing delays. You may also want to check the Order Reports page for any notifications or updates regarding system issues. If the problem persists, contact Seller Support.
