## **How to Report Patent Infringement**

**Who is this for:**

This is for all individuals and sellers who want to report any third-party Amazon seller that is selling our patents without the proper license.

**Objective:**

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) will help you to report all Violations for Patent Infringements.

1. Browse the brand registry console: [https://brandregistry.amazon.com/](https://brandregistry.amazon.com/)

2. Go to Protect > Report a violation
 ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/troubleshooting-sop-how-report-patent-infringement-image1(3).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4pmimage.png)

3. Select the correct marketplace and enter ASIN.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/troubleshooting-sop-how-report-patent-infringement-image2(2).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dbfimage.png)

4. Select the hijackers on the list. **Make sure you do not select your products.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/troubleshooting-sop-how-report-patent-infringement-image3(1).png)**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/CBBimage.png)

5. Click on “Patent Infringement” under the “Select issue type” dropdown menu.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/troubleshooting-sop-how-report-patent-infringement-image4.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Kfbimage.png)

6. Select the option according to your patent Design or Utility.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/troubleshooting-sop-how-report-patent-infringement-image5.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SOPimage.png)

*“We have a design or utility patent for my brand [Name of Brand] and we believe [Competitor] with ASIN [NUMBER] is violating our patent as their products have the same characteristics as our products & patent. Please remove the product infringing the patent.”*

9. Enter your CONTACT information![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/troubleshooting-sop-how-report-patent-infringement-image7.png)

FAQs

1. How do I know if my product is being infringed upon?

You can check if your patent is being violated by reviewing competitors' products on Amazon. If you notice any similarities to your patented product (design or utility), it may be infringing on your patent.

2. What should I do if I don’t have a Brand Registry account?

You must register for Amazon Brand Registry first. You can do so by visiting [Brand Registry](https://brandregistry.amazon.com/) and following the steps to enroll your brand. Only registered brands can report intellectual property violations.

3. What is the difference between a design and utility patent?

- Design patents cover the ornamental design of a product.
- Utility patents cover the functional aspects of a product or process.

Make sure to select the correct patent type when reporting the violation.

4. How do I identify the hijacker or competitor on the list?

Browse through the list provided in the Brand Registry report form, ensuring you only select the competitor who is infringing on your patent. Do not select your own products by mistake.

5. How do I describe the patent violation to Amazon?

In the description box, clearly explain how the competitor’s product replicates your patented product’s characteristics. You can use the following template:
“We have a design or utility patent for my brand [Brand Name], and we believe [Competitor] with ASIN [NUMBER] is violating our patent as their products have the same characteristics as our products & patent. Please remove the product infringing the patent.”

6. What happens after I submit the infringement report?

Once you submit the report, Amazon will review the claim. They may remove the infringing product if they confirm the violation. You will receive updates on the status through the Brand Registry console.

7. Can I report a violation if I don’t have a patent number?

No, you must have a valid patent number to report patent infringement. Ensure you have registered your patent before reporting.

8. How long does it take for Amazon to process a patent infringement report?

Typically, Amazon will review and respond to your infringement report within a few days, but it could take longer depending on the complexity of the case.

9. What if my report is rejected or I don’t see any action?

If Amazon rejects your report, or you don’t see action taken, you can follow up with them by providing additional evidence or clarification. Consider contacting Amazon Seller Support for further assistance if necessary.

10. What should I do if I find another seller violating my patent outside of Amazon?

If another seller is violating your patent outside of Amazon, you may need to seek legal action or contact the seller directly. Amazon only handles violations that occur on its platform.
