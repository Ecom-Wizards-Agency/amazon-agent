# Catalog SOP: Export Reviews per ASIN Using Helium 10 Extension

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-export-reviews-per-asin-using-helium-10-extension

## **Export Reviews per ASIN Using Helium 10 Extension**

**Who is this for :** All individuals who need to export reviews.

**Objective:** To extract and collect customer reviews for a specific ASIN and save the data in a preferred format for further analysis or use.

**Instructions:**

To begin, make sure you have the Helium10 extension installed in your browser.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/export-reviews-per-asin-using-helium-10-extension-image-w3vwrf9z.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gmYimage.png)

1. Visit the product detail page of the listing that needs review collection.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Untitled%20design%20(5)(6).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9Llimage.png)

2. Click the Helium 10 extension -> Review Insights

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/JpNimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Untitled%20design%20(6)(4).png)3. Go to All Reviews -> Export Reviews

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-10-04%20at%201.22.23%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PrSimage.png)

4. A file containing all the product review details will be downloaded:

- Date (date where the review was submitted)
- Author (customer name)
- Verified (yes or no)
- Helpful (yes or no)
- Title
- Body (how the customer described the product)
- Rating ( 1 - 5 )
- Images (if the customer sent product images)
- Videos (if a customer sent product video)
- URL (review link)
- Variation (if variated, specific ASIN of the child)
- Style (variation)

FAQs

**1. What is the purpose of exporting reviews using Helium 10?**

- Exporting reviews helps analyze customer feedback, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions about product improvements, marketing strategies, or addressing customer concerns.

**2. Do I need any special tools to export reviews?**

- Yes, you need the Helium 10 browser extension installed. The extension must also be logged into an active Helium 10 account for full functionality.

**3. How can I install the Helium 10 extension?**

- Go to your browser’s extension store (e.g., Chrome Web Store).
- Search for Helium 10.
- Click Add to Browser and follow the prompts.
- Log in using your Helium 10 account credentials.

**4. What happens if the Helium 10 extension doesn’t appear in my browser toolbar?**

- If the extension is not visible:

  - Check if it’s installed and enabled under your browser’s extensions settings.
  - Pin it to the toolbar for easy access.
  - Ensure you’re logged into your Helium 10 account.

**5. Can I export reviews for any ASIN?**

- Yes, reviews can be exported for any ASIN that has reviews available on its product detail page. However, ensure you have the necessary permissions and are compliant with Amazon's guidelines when using the data.

**6. What file format is the exported review data saved in?**

- The exported reviews are typically saved as a CSV file. This format can be opened in spreadsheet programs like Excel or Google Sheets for further analysis.

**7. What review details are included in the exported file?**

- The exported file contains the following information:

  - Date of review submission
  - Author (customer name)
  - Verified purchase status
  - Helpful votes
  - Review title and body
  - Star rating (1–5)
  - Images or videos submitted with the review
  - URL to the review
  - Variation details (e.g., ASIN, style)

**8. Can I filter reviews before exporting?**

- Yes, in the Helium 10 Review Insights tool, you can filter reviews by:

  - Star rating
  - Review date range
  - Verified purchase status
  - Keywords or phrases in the review text
  - These filters allow you to customize the data you export.

**9. How do I handle reviews with images or videos in the exported file?**

- Reviews with images or videos will include links to the media in the exported file. These links can be accessed directly to view the content.

**10. Can I export reviews for multiple ASINs at once?**

- No, reviews must be exported one ASIN at a time by visiting each product detail page and following the steps to export reviews using Helium 10.

**11. How long does it take to export reviews?**

- The time required depends on the number of reviews associated with the ASIN. For listings with a large volume of reviews, the process may take a few minutes.

**12. What should I do if the review export fails or the file is incomplete?**

- If the export fails:

  - Check your internet connection.
  - Ensure the Helium 10 extension is logged in and updated.
  - Refresh the product detail page and retry.
  - If the issue persists, contact Helium 10 support.

**13. Are there limitations on the number of reviews I can export?**

- The number of reviews you can export depends on Helium 10’s capabilities and Amazon's review visibility settings. Typically, all visible reviews on the product detail page can be exported.

**14. Can I use the exported review data for marketing purposes?**

- While you can use the data for internal analysis and strategy development, ensure compliance with Amazon’s policies and data usage guidelines when leveraging customer reviews.

**15. How can I analyze the exported review data effectively?**

- You can use tools like Excel or Google Sheets to:

  - Sort reviews by rating to identify trends.
  - Filter by keywords to pinpoint recurring issues or praises.
  - Analyze variations (e.g., colors or sizes) to assess performance differences.

**16. What are the common issues customers highlight in exported reviews?**

- By analyzing reviews, you may identify:

  - Product defects or areas for improvement.
  - Commonly appreciated features.
  - Packaging issues or delivery concerns.
  - Insights into customer preferences for variations.

**17. How often should I export and review customer feedback?**

- Regularly exporting and reviewing feedback—weekly, biweekly, or monthly—can help stay updated on customer sentiment and address issues promptly.

**18. Can I share the exported file with team members?**

- Yes, you can share the exported file, but ensure sensitive customer data is handled responsibly and in compliance with Amazon’s data usage policies.

**19. Is there a way to automate the review export process?**

- Helium 10 does not currently support automated review exports. Each export must be done manually through the extension.

**20. What should I do if the exported file contains irrelevant or duplicate data?**

- Use spreadsheet tools to:

  - Remove duplicate rows.
  - Filter out irrelevant data based on your analysis needs (e.g., certain keywords or variations).
