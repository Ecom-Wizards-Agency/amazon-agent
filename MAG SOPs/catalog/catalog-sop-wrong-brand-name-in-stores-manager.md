Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-wrong-brand-name-in-stores-manager

## Wrong Brand Name in Stores Manager

**Who is this for?**For Amazon sellers who have added the wrong brand name in their store and need to have it removed.

**Objective**: To provide a step-by-step guide for Amazon sellers who need to remove a brand name from their Store Manager.

**Follow the steps below to resolve the issue of an incorrect brand name being added to the store**

**Check and verify brands in the store:**

1. Go to Seller Central, navigate to "Stores," and then select "Manage Stores."

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680292020331(1).png)

2. Check if the brands listed are the ones you own. If the listed brands are correct, no action is needed. However, if an incorrect brand name appears in the store, follow the next steps.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680292281106(1).png)

3. **File a Case to Amazon**

- To file a case, use the seller's central primary email address and contact Amazon Support. Report the unexpected brand on the brand store that was not owned by you and request that it be removed. Ensure to provide the **MERCHANT TOKEN** and request that the case be escalated to the **brand registry**.

4. **Escalation and Follow-Up**

- There may be several back-and-forth messages between you and Amazon Support. Remain firm on the reason for the request and reiterate that the brand should be removed as it is not owned by you. As of the moment, only Amazon can resolve this issue.

FAQs

**1. Why is there an incorrect brand name in my Store Manager?**

- This can happen due to Amazon system errors, incorrect brand approvals, or another seller mistakenly associating their brand with your account. It may also occur if multiple brands are linked to your seller account incorrectly.

**2. How long does it take for Amazon to remove an incorrect brand name?**

- Amazon’s response time varies, but most cases take 7–14 business days. If there’s no progress after a week, escalate the case and request urgent resolution.

**3. What should I do if Amazon refuses to remove the incorrect brand name?**

- If Amazon denies your request:
- Reopen the case and ask for clarification on why the request was rejected.
- Provide additional proof that the brand does not belong to you (e.g., brand registry evidence, store ownership details).
- Request escalation by asking for a senior representative or Account Health team intervention.

**4. Does having the wrong brand name affect my listings or Buy Box eligibility?**

- In most cases, it won’t affect existing product listings or Buy Box performance. However, it may cause issues if you need to edit your store branding or apply for Brand Registry benefits.

**5. I can’t find my Merchant Token or Admin Email. What should I do?**

- For the Merchant Token, go to Seller Central > Settings > Account Info > Merchant Token. If it's missing, contact Amazon Support.
- For the Admin Email, go to Settings > User Permissions. If you're not the admin, check with the account owner.

**6. Can I fix this issue by creating a new store instead of removing the incorrect brand?**

- No. Creating a new store won’t override the incorrect brand name in your current store. The issue needs to be resolved directly through Amazon Support.

**7. What should I do if Amazon Support is unresponsive or keeps giving generic replies?**

- Follow up regularly (every 48 hours) and keep the case active.
- Politely demand escalation to the Brand Registry or Internal Teams.
- Use the “Call Me” option in Seller Support for direct communication.
- Post in the Amazon Seller Forums to get visibility and possible moderator intervention.
