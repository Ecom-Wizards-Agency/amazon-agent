# Catalog SOP: How to Download an All Listings Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-download-an-all-listings-report

## **How to Download an All Listings Report**

**Who is this for :** An Amazon seller may request an All Listings Report to gain a comprehensive overview of all the products they are currently selling on the platform. This report provides detailed information about all of the seller's active listings, including the product title, ASIN, SKU, listing status, price, and other relevant details.

**Objective :** To provide sellers with a valuable tool for managing their product catalog and optimizing their performance on the Amazon platform. By using this report to gain a complete overview of their listings, sellers can make informed decisions that drive sales and increase profitability over time.

**Instructions:**

1. Access the “Report” tab from the top menu of Seller Central and select “Inventory Reports” from the selection Menu.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hTKimage.png)

2. On the next page, select “All Listings Report” from the “Select Report Type” Drop-Down Menu provided.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/g8uimage.png)

3. If you wish to customize your report, click the “Customize the columns for this report” link and follow the steps on the next page.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/8scimage.png)

4. You can customize the report and just select the attributes that you want or you need. Click “Save” once done with the customization.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/h5vimage.png)

5. Return to your Inventory Reports page by clicking the “Inventory Reports” link at the top left of the page. Click the “Request Report” button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/tu9image.png)

6. Your report will appear in the table below with an “In Progress” status.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/W8kimage.png)

7.Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to “Ready,” and a “Download” link will appear. Click the “Download” link to download the report.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hVyimage.png)

8. The report will be available in text format.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sqHimage.png)

9. It is advisable to open the report via Excel for easy audit. Sample of All Listings Report below is in Excel format.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9L1image.png)

FAQs

1. What is the All Listings Report, and why is it important?

The All Listings Report provides a comprehensive overview of all the active listings on Amazon. It includes details such as the product title, ASIN, SKU, listing status, price, and other relevant information. This report is essential for sellers to manage their product catalog efficiently and make informed decisions that can enhance sales and profitability.

2. How do I access the All Listings Report in Seller Central?

To download the All Listings Report:

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central.
2. Click on the “Reports” tab in the top menu.
3. Select “Inventory Reports” from the drop-down menu.

3. How do I select the All Listings Report type?

Once you're on the Inventory Reports page:

1. From the “Select Report Type” drop-down menu, choose “All Listings Report”.

4. Can I customize the All Listings Report?

Yes, you can customize the report by selecting specific attributes to include:

1. Click the “Customize the columns for this report” link.
2. Follow the steps on the next page to select the columns you need.
3. After customization, click “Save” to save your settings.

5. How do I generate the All Listings Report?

Once you’ve selected and customized the report (if necessary):

1. Click on the “Request Report” button to generate the report.

6. How long does it take for the All Listings Report to be ready?

The report typically takes a few minutes to generate, and its status will be displayed as “In Progress” until ready.

7. How do I download the All Listings Report once it’s ready?

Once the report is ready, the status will change to “Ready”.

1. A “Download” link will appear.
2. Click the “Download” link to download the report to your computer.

8. In what format will the All Listings Report be available?

The All Listings Report will be available in text format. To make the audit process easier, it is recommended to open the report in Excel.

9. Why should I open the All Listings Report in Excel?

Opening the All Listings Report in Excel makes it easier to filter, sort, and audit the data. Excel’s capabilities help you quickly identify performance trends, product issues, and opportunities for optimization in your product listings.

10. What actions should I take after downloading the All Listings Report?

After downloading the report:

- Review your listings to ensure that all relevant details are accurate and up to date.
- Take action to optimize your listings, such as adjusting prices, improving product titles, or correcting missing information.
- Use the report as a tool for inventory management and performance analysis to drive sales.

11. Can I automate the download of the All Listings Report?

Currently, Amazon Seller Central does not offer an option to automate the downloading of the All Listings Report. You will need to manually download the report as required.
