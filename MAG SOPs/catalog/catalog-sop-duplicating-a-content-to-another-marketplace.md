# Catalog SOP: Duplicating A+ Content to Another Marketplace

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-duplicating-a-content-to-another-marketplace

## **Duplicating A+ to Another Marketplace**

**Who is this for :** Individuals who wanted to duplicate current A+ content to another Amazon marketplace.

**Objective :** To duplicate A+ content from the US to a different Amazon marketplace in an efficient manner.

Amazon A+ Content is the tool that enables brand owners to describe product features in an advanced way: add detailed descriptions, charts, videos, high-quality images, and custom copy (e.g. brand stories). A+ Content feature is designed to help sellers and vendors increase traffic, conversion rate, and sales.

**Duplicating A+ content to Another Marketplace**

- Log in to the marketplace where the A+ content will be duplicated.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-10-09%20at%203.02.25%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/uLkimage.png)
- Go to Advertising, then A+ Content Manager.

  Direct Seller Central link: <https://sellercentral.amazon.com/enhanced-content/content-manager>

  Default to Premium A+ whenever it is available. Premium A+ uses separate desktop and mobile image placements; verify both asset sets before recreating or editing content. Use Basic A+ only if Premium is unavailable or the operator explicitly asks for Basic.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/g33image.png)
- On the A+ Content Manager page click the box “Show auto-created content” then choose the A+ content which you need to work on.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Catalog-SOP-Duplicating-A-Content-to-Another-Marketplace-1.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/kAcimage.png)

- You may now select the A+ content where the ASIN will be applied. After that follow the steps in the SOP below

  - [Add ASINs to Brand Store, Brand Story, and A+ Content](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/brand-registry-sop-add-asins-to-brand-store-brand-story-and-a-content)[![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/duplicating-a-to-another-marketplace-image-o3xu1vhe.png)](https://myamazonguy.document360.io/v1/docs/add-asins-to-brand-store-brand-story-and-a-content)
- If in case the duplicate A+ content is still not listed, you will need to re-create the content. Follow the steps in the SOP below.

  - [A+ Content Design](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/design-XyS/page/creative-sop-a-content-design)

FAQs

- **Q : What is the purpose of duplicating A+ content to another marketplace?**

  - A: The goal is to replicate existing A+ content from one Amazon marketplace (e.g., the US) to another marketplace to maintain consistency and improve traffic, conversion rates, and sales across regions.

- **Q : Can I duplicate A+ content to any marketplace?**

  - A: Yes, you can duplicate A+ content to other Amazon marketplaces where your brand is registered, provided you have access to those marketplaces through Amazon’s Brand Registry.

- **Q : Why is the “Show auto-created content” option important?**

  - A: The "Show auto-created content" option allows you to see existing A+ content linked to your brand that can be duplicated or modified for another marketplace, saving time compared to recreating the content from scratch.

- **Q : What should I do if the A+ content I want to duplicate is not listed in the target marketplace?**

  - A: If the duplicate A+ content is not listed, you will need to recreate it manually. Refer to the A+ Content Design SOP for detailed steps.

- **Q : Do I need to manually add ASINs when duplicating A+ content?**

  - A: Yes, you must add the relevant ASINs to the brand store, brand story, and A+ content in the target marketplace.

- **Q : Are there any regional restrictions or formatting requirements when duplicating A+ content?**

  - A: Yes, some elements (e.g., text, images, or videos) may need adjustments to meet the specific requirements or preferences of the target marketplace, such as language translations, cultural nuances, or regional compliance guidelines.

- **Q : Can I use the same images and videos for the duplicated A+ content?**

  - A: In most cases, you can reuse the same images and videos. However, ensure that the media complies with the guidelines and cultural context of the target marketplace.

- **Q : How do I verify that the duplicated A+ content is live in the new marketplace?**

  - A: After duplication, navigate to the product detail pages in the target marketplace and verify that the A+ content is displayed correctly. You can also monitor the status in the A+ Content Manager.

- **Q : Is it possible to edit duplicated A+ content after it goes live in the target marketplace?**

  - A: Yes, you can edit A+ content in the target marketplace if adjustments are needed. Changes made in the A+ Content Manager will reflect on the product detail pages once approved by Amazon.

- **Q : Do I need to duplicate A+ content individually for each product in the target marketplace?**

  - A: No, A+ content can be linked to multiple ASINs within the same brand in the target marketplace, provided they are eligible.

- **Q : What are common issues encountered when duplicating A+ content?**

  - A: Common issues include:
  - Missing “Show auto-created content” option.
  - ASIN eligibility errors.
  - Region-specific compliance requirements.
  - Media files not adhering to marketplace guidelines.
