# Catalog SOP: Download a Dangerous Goods Status Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-dangerous-goods-status-report

## **Download a Dangerous Goods Status Report**

**Who is this for :** This is for all individuals and sellers who want to know how to download a Dangerous Goods Status Report.

**Objective :** This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) will help you understand how you can download a Dangerous Goods Status Report. Your dangerous goods status report gives you current information on the status of your FBA listings that are classified as dangerous goods (DG), including listings currently under DG review. Dangerous goods are also known as hazardous materials or hazmat. See examples of products that may be regulated as dangerous goods. The information in this report is refreshed once every day. Products that are not DG are not included in this report.

**To generate a Dangerous Goods Status Report on Seller Central, follow these steps:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-DEMSAP6I.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GjQimage.png)
2. Click the "Dangerous Goods Status" link under the "Inventory" header on the left-hand side of the page.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-dangerous-goods-status-report-image-2.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/X5zimage.png)
3. On the next page, you can download the report in Excel format by clicking the "Request .csv Download" button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-dangerous-goods-status-report-image-3.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/i8Himage.png)
4. If you prefer a text format, click the "Request .txt Download" button.
5. The "Report Status" column in the table below will show "In Progress" until the report is ready.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-dangerous-goods-status-report-image-4.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Dq3image.png)
6. Once the report is ready, the status will change to "Download".
7. To download the report, click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-dangerous-goods-status-report-image-5.png)

FAQs

1. What is the purpose of the Dangerous Goods Status Report?

The Dangerous Goods Status Report provides information on the status of your FBA listings that are classified as dangerous goods (DG), including those currently under DG review. It helps you track hazardous materials or hazmat products and their current status on Amazon.

2. Where can I find the Dangerous Goods Status Report in Seller Central?

To find the Dangerous Goods Status Report, go to the "Reports" tab in Seller Central, select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu, and then click the "Dangerous Goods Status" link under the "Inventory" section on the left-hand side of the page.

3. What formats can I download the Dangerous Goods Status Report in?

You can download the Dangerous Goods Status Report in either Excel (.csv) format or text (.txt) format. To download in Excel format, click the "Request .csv Download" button, and to download in text format, click the "Request .txt Download" button.

4. What does the "Report Status" column indicate in the Dangerous Goods Status Report?

The "Report Status" column shows the current progress of the report. Initially, it will display "In Progress," and once the report is ready, the status will change to "Download."

5. How do I download the report once it's ready?

Once the report is ready, the "Report Status" column will change to "Download." Click the "Download" button in this column to access the report.

6. How often is the Dangerous Goods Status Report refreshed?

The Dangerous Goods Status Report is updated once every day. You can check back periodically for the most recent information regarding your listings.

7. What types of products are included in the Dangerous Goods Status Report?

The report includes products that are classified as dangerous goods, which may include hazardous materials or items subject to specific regulations. Non-DG (non-dangerous goods) products will not be included in the report.

8. Why would my product be classified as a dangerous good?

A product may be classified as a dangerous good if it contains hazardous materials, such as flammable liquids, aerosols, or products that pose risks to health or safety during transportation or handling. The report will help you identify and manage these listings effectively.

9. Can I download the Dangerous Goods Status Report for specific products or ASINs?

The report provides a summary of all your FBA listings that are classified as dangerous goods. It does not allow you to download the report for specific ASINs but instead provides an overview of the DG status of all applicable listings.

10. What should I do if my product is under DG review?

If your product is listed as "under DG review" in the report, you should monitor its status and take the necessary actions to address any regulatory requirements. You may need to provide additional information or take corrective measures to resolve any issues that arise.
