## Logistics SOP: How to Set Order Handling Capacity in Amazon Seller Central

**Who is this for?**

This SOP is intended for **sellers** on Amazon who want to manage their order fulfillment capabilities, particularly during busy periods or unexpected spikes in orders. This is especially useful for sellers who want to ensure timely deliveries, avoid late shipment penalties, and maintain accurate delivery promises.

**Objective:**

This SOP provides a step-by-step guide to setting the Order Handling Capacity feature on Amazon Seller Central to manage the number of orders you can fulfill within your default handling time.

**IMPORTANT NOTE:**Please refer to this article to know more about **Order Handling Capacity, its benefits and how it works (**[**Supplemental Article: Understanding Order Handling Capacity on Amazon Seller Central**](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/supplemental-article-understanding-order-handling-capacity-on-amazon-seller-central)**)**

**Effective February 24, 2025:**

Amazon will update the Order Handling Capacity (OHC) feature to automatically set a minimum threshold for sellers' order handling capacity. This threshold will be recalculated weekly based on your average daily orders over the past 30 days, ensuring that your OHC limit is more accurate and aligned with your actual fulfillment capacity.

This update helps ensure that your handling times reflect your true capacity, protecting you from late deliveries and improving the overall customer experience. If your OHC falls below the minimum threshold, the system will automatically adjust it to the new value.

**Key points:**

- The minimum threshold applies only to Standard and Free Economy shipping options.
- You can adjust your OHC above the minimum threshold at any time.
- If you plan to take time off, you can enable Vacation Mode to prevent automatic updates to your OHC while you're away.

**Step 2: Access the Settings Menu**

1. Once logged in, locate the **Settings Gear Icon** in the upper-right corner of the homepage.
2. Click on the gear icon to open the drop-down menu.

**Step 3: Open Shipping Settings**

1. In the drop-down menu, select **Shipping Settings**.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/JFSimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/JFSimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-set-order-handling-capacity-in-amazon-seller-central-image-yvwmelba.png)
2. The **Shipping Settings** page will open. From there, click on the **General Shipping Settings** tab.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-set-order-handling-capacity-in-amazon-seller-central-image-drx7y62z.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Sbnimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Sbnimage.png)

**Step 4: Modify Handling Time**

1. Scroll down to the **Handling Time** section.
2. Click the **Edit** button next to the Handling Time settings.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-set-order-handling-capacity-in-amazon-seller-central-image-7nta4oaz.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zARimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/zARimage.png)

**Step 5: Set Order Handling Capacity**

1. On the editing screen, scroll down until you see the **Order Handling Capacity** field.
2. Enter the number of orders you can handle within your default handling time.

  - For example, if you can manage 100 orders per day, input **100**.
3. Click **Save** to apply your changes.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-set-order-handling-capacity-in-amazon-seller-central-image-x0yg26jw.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/68Qimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/68Qimage.png)

**Step 6: Verify and Adjust (If Necessary)**

1. After saving, confirm that your **Order Handling Capacity** is correctly set.
2. If your capacity changes (due to increased or decreased order volume), repeat the process and update the capacity accordingly.

**Important Notes:**

- **Eligibility:** Only sellers with a **Professional Selling Plan** can set an order handling capacity.
- **Exclusions:** The **Order Handling Capacity** feature does not apply to **Seller Fulfilled Prime (SFP)** orders or **Premium Shipping** option orders.
- **Vacation Mode:** Enable **Vacation Mode** if you're taking time off to prevent automatic adjustments to your OHC during your absence.

**Conclusion:**

Setting an order handling capacity on Amazon Seller Central helps you manage spikes in order volume, maintain your account health, and provide accurate delivery promises to customers. By following this SOP, you can ensure smooth operations during busy periods and protect your account from late shipment penalties.
