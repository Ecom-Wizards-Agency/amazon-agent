# Catalog SOP: How to Cancel FBA Removal Order

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-cancel-fba-removal-order

## **How to Cancel FBA Removal Order**

**Who is this for :** Intended for Amazon sellers who need to cancel a previously requested FBA removal order.

**Objective :** To provide guidance on the process of canceling a removal order, which will outline the necessary steps to successfully complete the cancellation process. By following these steps, individuals will gain a better understanding of the requirements and procedures involved in canceling a removal order and will be more equipped to navigate the process with confidence.

A removal order created not long ago can be canceled before Amazon disposes or ships the items or if the order's status reads "Pending, Planning, or Processing."

**IMPORTANT:** Clicking on the cancel option doesn't guarantee the cancellation of the order. Your removal order may have moved to Process/Pending status before the cancellation request was received. Check back later to confirm that the order was canceled.

**To cancel a removal order, follow these steps:**

1. Go to Reports → Fulfillment → Removals → Removal Order Detail

Sometimes Processing status orders cannot be canceled during peak periods such as Prime Day, Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and the holidays as the removal orders can stay in Processing status for several weeks. Sellers are not able to cancel a removal order for a required removal. For more information, go to [Required Removals](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/202000820).

FAQs

**1. Can I cancel an FBA removal order after it has been created?**

- Yes, you can cancel a removal order as long as the status of the order is either "Pending," "Planning," or "Processing." However, orders that have moved past "Processing" or have been shipped/disposed cannot be canceled.

**2. How do I check the status of my removal order?**

- To check the status:

  - Navigate to Reports → Fulfillment → Removals → Removal Order Detail.
  - Enter or search for the Removal Order ID.
  - The status will be displayed as "Pending," "Planning," or "Processing."

**3. What happens if I cancel an order in "Processing" status?**

- While it is possible to attempt cancellation during the "Processing" phase, Amazon may already have begun the shipping/disposal process. During peak periods (e.g., Prime Day, Black Friday, Cyber Monday), orders in "Processing" may remain in that status for weeks and might not be eligible for cancellation.

**4. Can I cancel a removal order for a required removal?**

- No, removal orders designated as "Required Removals" cannot be canceled. For more details, consult Amazon's Required Removals policy in Seller Central.

**5. Does clicking the “Cancel” button guarantee that my removal order will be canceled?**

- No, clicking “Cancel this order” does not guarantee cancellation. If the removal order has already moved beyond the cancelable stages, it will proceed as planned. Check back in the "Removal Order Detail" section later to confirm whether the cancellation was successful.

**6. Can I cancel removal orders during peak periods?**

- It may be more challenging to cancel removal orders during high-demand periods, such as Prime Day or the holiday season, as Amazon prioritizes processing large volumes of orders. Orders in "Processing" status are especially unlikely to be canceled during these times.

**7. How long does it take to process a cancellation request?**

- Amazon typically processes cancellation requests promptly for orders in "Pending" or "Planning" stages. For "Processing" stage orders, cancellations may take longer or may not be possible depending on the stage of fulfillment.

**8. What should I do if I’m unable to cancel my removal order?**

- If you cannot cancel your removal order, you can:

  - Wait for the order to complete processing and review the results in the "Removal Order Detail" section.
  - Contact Amazon Seller Support for clarification or further assistance.
