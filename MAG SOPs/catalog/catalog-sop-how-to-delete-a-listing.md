# Catalog SOP: How to Delete a Listing

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-delete-a-listing

## **How to Delete a Listing**

**Who is this for :** This is intended for Account Specialists who need to remove or delete a listing.

**Objective :** To ensure that Account Specialists are equipped with the knowledge and skills to correctly remove a listing from an Amazon account.

**Instructions:**

1. Backup data:

- Before deleting a listing, ensure that you download the latest Category Listings Report (CLR) and obtain approval from both the client and BM. You can view the [SOP here](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-category-listings-report) for reference.

2. Delete the listing Manually

- Open the Inventory tab and select Manage All Inventory.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%203.06.08%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hf3image.png)
- Locate the SKU you wish to remove and choose the Edit drop-down menu. There are times that it can be found on the far-right side of the listing.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%203.06.15%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/OWtimage.png)
- Select Delete Product and Listing.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%203.06.21%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oVtimage.png)
- Click on the Ok button when Amazon asks if you really want to delete the listing to complete the process.

3. via Flat File

NOTE: You can use the reuse [Category Listings Report](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-category-listings-report) or generate a [Category Specific Template](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-category-specific-template) flat file in deleting the listing.

- a. Input only the bare minimum information in deleting the listing - these are your SKUs and the update_delete column.

Here is a sample preview of how your flat file will appear.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%203.06.31%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yNIimage.png)

4. Wait for 10-15 minutes and go back to the Manage Inventory page to verify if the SKU was successfully deleted.

**IMPORTANT NOTE :** While the listing may disappear from your product catalog, the product detail page may still exist on Amazon. If you wish to sell the same item in the future, add the product ASIN to ‘reclaim’ the listing.

FAQs

1. What happens when I delete a listing from my inventory?

Deleting a listing removes it from your Manage Inventory page but does not remove the product detail page from Amazon. If you want to sell the item again, you can add the ASIN back to your inventory.

2. How do I back up a listing before deleting it?

Before deleting a listing, download the latest Category Listings Report (CLR) to retain product data. Ensure approval from both the client and BM before proceeding.

3. How do I delete a listing manually?

1. Go to Inventory > Manage All Inventory in Seller Central.
2. Locate the SKU you want to remove.
3. Click the Edit drop-down menu on the right side of the listing.
4. Select Delete Product and Listing and confirm the deletion.

4. Can I delete a listing using a flat file?

Yes, follow these steps:

1. Use the Category Listings Report (CLR) or generate a Category-Specific Template flat file.
2. Fill in only the SKU and the update_delete column.
3. Upload the flat file to Amazon’s Inventory Loader Tool.
4. Wait 10–15 minutes and check if the SKU has been removed.

5. What if the deleted listing still appears on Amazon?

Even after deleting a listing, the product detail page may still exist. If you wish to remove the detail page, you must submit a Brand Registry or IP claim (if applicable).

6. Can I recover a deleted listing?

Yes, if you want to sell the item again, simply add the ASIN back to your inventory through the Add a Product tool in Seller Central.

7. How long does it take for a listing to be deleted?

Listings typically disappear within 10–15 minutes, but in some cases, they may take longer to reflect in the Manage Inventory page.
