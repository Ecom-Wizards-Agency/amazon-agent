## **How to Generate a Monthly Storage Fees Report**

**Who is this for :**

This is intended for individuals or teams responsible for inventory management, financial analysis, and cost optimization on Seller Central. It is suitable for users who have access to Seller Central and need to generate a Monthly Storage Fees Report to track and evaluate their monthly storage expenses. This SOP will assist users in navigating to the appropriate section, selecting the desired report format, and downloading the report for review, analysis, and decision-making purposes related to inventory management and cost optimization. It is particularly relevant for sellers seeking to understand and control their storage costs, improve profitability, and streamline their inventory management processes.

**Objective:**

To provide instructions for generating a Monthly Storage Fees Report. This SOP aims to guide users in accessing and downloading a report that provides information on the monthly storage fees incurred for their inventory on Seller Central. It enables users to monitor and analyze storage costs, optimize inventory management, and make informed financial decisions.

**To create a Monthly Storage Fees Report, follow these steps:**

1. Go to the "Reports" Tab on the Top Menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the Drop-Down Menu.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7VKimage.png)
2. Under the "Payments" header on the left-hand side of the page, click on "Monthly Storage Fees".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Catalog-SOP-How-to-Generate-a-Monthly-Storage-Fees-Report-2.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/X1Bimage.png)
3. On the next page, choose "Event Month" and Year from the Drop-Down Menus Available.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Catalog-SOP-How-to-Generate-a-Monthly-Storage-Fees-Report-3.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eeVimage.png)
4. Click on the "Request .csv Download" button to download the report in an Excel format or the "Request .txt Download" button to download it in a text format.

5. Check the "Report Status" Column in the table below to see if the report is still "In Progress".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Catalog-SOP-How-to-Generate-a-Monthly-Storage-Fees-Report-5.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I find the Monthly Storage Fees Report in Seller Central?

You can access the report by navigating to Reports > Fulfillment, then selecting Payments > Monthly Storage Fees on the left-hand side of the page.

2. What information does the Monthly Storage Fees Report include?

The report provides details about the monthly storage fees incurred for your inventory, including storage duration, inventory volume, fee amounts, and associated SKUs that have been stored in Amazon’s fulfillment centers.

3. Can I select a custom date range for the Monthly Storage Fees Report?

No, the Monthly Storage Fees Report is generated based on calendar months. You can select the "Event Month" and Year from the drop-down menus to retrieve data for the desired month.

4. What file formats are available for downloading the report?

You can download the report in Excel (.csv) format by clicking "Request .csv Download" or in text (.txt) format by selecting "Request .txt Download."

5. How do I track the progress of my report request?

After requesting the download, check the "Report Status" column in the table. The status will first show "In Progress" and will update to "Download" when the report is ready.

6. How long does it take for the Monthly Storage Fees Report to be generated?

The processing time varies depending on the amount of data, but reports are typically available within a few minutes.

7. What should I do if my Monthly Storage Fees Report does not generate or download properly?

If the report does not generate or download successfully:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

8. Does this report include all storage fees, or just monthly fees?

The Monthly Storage Fees Report only includes fees incurred for that specific month. For more detailed insights into storage fees across multiple periods, you may need to generate additional reports or review other fee-related reports in Seller Central.

9. Can I automate or schedule this report for regular downloads?

Currently, Amazon does not offer an option to schedule automated downloads for this report. You will need to manually generate and download the report each month.

10. How can I reduce my monthly storage fees?

To reduce your storage fees, consider:

- Regularly monitoring inventory levels and removing or liquidating excess inventory.
- Participating in Amazon's FBA cleanup programs, such as FBA Liquidations or Removals.
- Optimizing your inventory restocking process to keep inventory levels balanced and avoid excess storage.
