# Catalog SOP: Advertising a Social Media Promo Code

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-advertising-a-social-media-promo-code

### Advertising a Social Media Promo Code

After creating a Social Media Promo Code, you'll receive a unique landing page URL containing a list of eligible products for the promotion and the details of the offer. The landing page showcases your offers exclusively, eliminating the need for customers to search for them in the Buy Box.

Use this landing page to direct customers to your offers via social media or marketing channels such as paid search, and drive product sales. Customers can easily add items to their cart from this landing page, and the discount will be applied automatically.

Once the promotion is scheduled, check the landing page link to ensure that the offer is active and items are in stock. Share this link with your social media followers to promote your offers.

It's a best practice to indicate "limited time only" or "while supplies last" to notify customers that the offer may sell out. Please note that this feature is currently only available in the US marketplace.

FAQS

1. How do I create a Social Media Promo Code and generate the unique landing page URL?

To create a Social Media Promo Code:

1. Log into Seller Central: Navigate to the Advertising tab.
2. Create a New Promo Code: Under the Promotions section, select Create a New Promotion and choose the Social Media Promo Code option.
3. Set the Promotion Details: Enter the promotion name, details, and eligible products.
4. Generate the Landing Page URL: Once the promotion is created, you’ll receive a unique landing page URL where the promo code will be applied automatically to eligible products.

2. How can I track the performance of my Social Media Promo Code?

To track the effectiveness of your promo code campaign:

- Use Amazon’s Reporting Tools: Under Advertising > Promotions, view detailed performance reports on the promo code usage, redemption rates, and sales generated from the landing page link.
- Monitor Social Media Metrics: Track engagement through your social media platform’s analytics (e.g., click-through rates, likes, shares, and comments) to assess how well the promo code is resonating with your audience.
- Use URL Tracking: Use UTM parameters in your landing page URL to track traffic sources and conversions coming specifically from social media channels.

3. How do I manage the expiry of my Social Media Promo Code?

Once a promotion expires, it can no longer be used. However, if you need to extend the promotion or modify the end date:

- Modify Promo Settings: Go to Advertising > Promotions, select your promo code, and update the end date.
- Post-Promotion Actions: If a promotion ends unexpectedly due to stock issues, manually turn off the promotion in Seller Central or update the landing page with a message notifying customers that the offer has ended.

4. What are some best practices for running a Social Media Promo Code campaign?

To optimize your Social Media Promo Code campaign:

- Create Clear, Engaging Posts: Use eye-catching graphics and a compelling call-to-action (CTA), such as “Shop Now” or “Limited Time Only!”
- Use Urgency and Scarcity: Phrases like “while supplies last” or “limited-time offer” encourage customers to act quickly.
- Leverage Influencers or Partnerships: Collaborate with influencers or affiliates to increase reach and credibility.
- Offer Extra Incentives: Consider offering bonus deals, like free shipping or bundle discounts, to increase interest.

5. How can I integrate the Social Media Promo Code with other marketing channels?

To integrate your promo code with other marketing channels:

- Email Campaigns: Include the promo code and landing page link in your email marketing campaigns for direct targeting.
- Affiliate Marketing: Share the landing page link with affiliate marketers to drive additional traffic and sales.
- Paid Search Ads: Use Google Ads or Amazon’s own Sponsored Products with your promo code to further boost visibility.

6. What should I do if my stock is running low during the promotion?

If you run low on stock during your promotion:

- Update the Landing Page: Modify the landing page to reflect the out-of-stock status for certain products or display a message about limited availability.
- Monitor Inventory Levels: Regularly check your inventory to ensure you’re not overselling, especially if your promotion runs for a prolonged period.
- Pause the Promotion: If stock is exhausted, you may need to pause the promo code or adjust the promotion details until inventory is replenished.

7. Are there any restrictions or limitations on using Social Media Promo Codes?

Yes, there are some restrictions:

- Geographical Limits: Some promo codes may only be valid in certain countries or regions. Make sure to check the specific terms for each promotion.
- One-Time Use: Promo codes are often limited to one-time use per customer, though this can be adjusted depending on the promotion type.
- Stock Limitations: If your stock runs out, the promotion may no longer be valid, and Amazon will not automatically refund any redeemed coupons.
