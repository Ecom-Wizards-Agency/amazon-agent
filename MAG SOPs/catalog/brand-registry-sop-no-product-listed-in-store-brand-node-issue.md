## Store No Products Listed / Brand Node

**Who is this for :** Individuals who have issues with the accessibility of the Brand Store.

**Objective :** To guide individuals on how to fix issues related to Brand Node.

This problem occurs when the brand name is registered for the first time. In order to check the status of the store, go to seller central >> Stores >> Manage Stores.

**If it says:**No products listed. If you recently linked products, it may take up to 72 hours to take effect. Please contact [ads support](https://advertising.amazon.com/contactus) if you need further assistance., check the inventory and see if there are any listings under that brand name. If not, the client should create a listing. If there is at least one listing, proceed with the following steps:

- Go to Stores >> Manage Stores >> (on the top right corner) Click on the question mark button >> Support Center

FAQs

1. What does "No products listed" mean in the Brand Store?

This message appears when the store cannot detect products under the brand name. It could be due to:

- The brand name being newly registered and requiring up to 72 hours to update.
- No active listings under the brand name.
- A missing brand node association in Amazon’s system.

2. How do I check if my brand store has linked products?

- Go to Seller Central > Stores > Manage Stores.
- If you see the message "No products listed", check if there are any active listings under your brand name in Inventory Management.
- If there are no listings, create at least one active listing under the brand.

3. How long does it take for products to appear in the store after linking them?

It may take up to 72 hours for newly linked products to reflect in the brand store. If the issue persists beyond this period, follow the escalation process by contacting Amazon Advertiser Support.

4. What is a Brand Node, and why do I need it?

A Brand Node is an internal classification that links your brand with the correct product catalog in Amazon’s system. Without a brand node, your products may not appear in the brand store, even if they are listed correctly.

5. How do I request Amazon to create a Brand Node for my brand?

You need to submit a support request via Amazon Advertiser Support:

1. Go to Stores > Manage Stores.
2. Click the question mark button (top right corner) and open the Support Center.
3. Scroll down in the Getting Started Guide and click Contact Us.
4. Choose Store > Creating a Store > Email Options.
5. Submit the request using this template:

  - Subject: Store - No products listed: [ENTER_brand_name]
  - Description: Include the brand name and request Amazon to create a Brand Node for your brand.

6. How long does it take for Amazon to resolve a missing Brand Node issue?

It depends on Amazon’s response time, but it typically takes 5-7 business days for Amazon to verify and create the brand node.

7. Can I create a Brand Node myself?

No, only Amazon can create a Brand Node. However, you can expedite the process by ensuring your brand is correctly registered under Amazon Brand Registry and has at least one active listing under that brand name.

8. What should I do if Amazon does not respond or resolve my request?

- Follow up on your support ticket through Seller Central > Case Log.
- If you don’t get a response within 7 business days, reopen the case and request an update.
- If the issue persists, escalate the case through Amazon Brand Registry Support or your Amazon account manager (if applicable).
