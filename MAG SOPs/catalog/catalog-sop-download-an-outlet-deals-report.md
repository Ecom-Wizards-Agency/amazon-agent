# Catalog SOP: Download an Outlet Deals Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-an-outlet-deals-report

## **Download an Outlet Deals Report**

**Who is this for :** For individuals who are responsible for managing the outlet channel or analyzing sales data related to discounted or clearance products.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions on how to download an Outlet Deals report. This SOP aims to enable the designated individuals or teams to access and analyze the Outlet Deals report efficiently, ensuring effective monitoring and decision-making regarding discounted or clearance products available through the outlet channel.

**To generate an Outlet Deals Report:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-creating-an-outlet-deals-report-image-1.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/l8yimage.png)

2. Under the "Sales" header on the left of the page, click on the "Outlet Deals" link.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-creating-an-outlet-deals-report-image-2.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/v17image.png)

3. On the next page, choose the event date range by selecting it from the drop-down menu or entering the "From" and "To" dates from the calendar icons on the right. Then, click the "Request Download" button to generate the report.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-creating-an-outlet-deals-report-image-3.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ybIimage.png)

4. In the table below, the "Report Status" column will indicate that the report is "In Progress".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-creating-an-outlet-deals-report-image-4.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SnBimage.png)

5. Once the report is ready, click on the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column to download your report.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-creating-an-outlet-deals-report-image-5.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hKGimage.png)

FAQs

1. How long does it take for the Outlet Deals report to be generated?

The processing time varies based on the volume of data in the selected date range. Typically, reports are available within a few minutes to an hour. If the report status remains "In Progress" for an extended period, refresh the page or try requesting it again.

2. In what format is the Outlet Deals report downloaded?

The report is downloaded as a CSV (Comma-Separated Values) file, which can be opened in spreadsheet programs like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.

3. What should I do if the report request fails or does not appear in the download section?

If your report does not generate or is missing from the "Report Status" table:

- Ensure that you selected the correct date range.
- Refresh the page and check again.
- Clear your browser cache and try again.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support.

4. Can all Seller Central users access the Outlet Deals report?

No, access to the Outlet Deals report depends on user permissions. Only users with the necessary reporting or sales analysis permissions will be able to generate and download the report. If you cannot access it, check with your account administrator.

5. How long are Outlet Deals reports available for download?

Reports remain available for a limited period after generation. It is recommended to download and save them immediately, as Amazon may remove older reports after a certain time.

6. Can I request multiple Outlet Deals reports at the same time?

Yes, you can request multiple reports for different date ranges, but each request must be submitted separately. There is no option to generate a report covering multiple date ranges in a single request.

7. Can I filter the Outlet Deals report by product category, SKU, or discount level?

Currently, the report only allows filtering by date range. Any additional filtering must be done manually in a spreadsheet program after downloading the report.

8. When should I generate an Outlet Deals report?

You may need to generate an Outlet Deals report for several reasons, such as:

- Monitoring discounted product performance to assess sales trends.
- Tracking inventory movement to determine if clearance items are selling as expected.
- Analyzing pricing strategies to evaluate the effectiveness of outlet discounts.
- Preparing financial reports by reviewing outlet sales data for accounting purposes.
