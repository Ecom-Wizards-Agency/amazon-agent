## **Locate the Brand Store in VC**

**Who is this for :** For those who need to set up or update a Brand Store on Amazon.

**Objective :**

- To enable brand owners to create and manage a dedicated storefront for their brand on Amazon. The brand store allows brand owners to showcase their products, enhance their brand identity, and provide a more personalized shopping experience for customers.
- Amazon Brand Store allows merchants to set up an online storefront for their brands for free. In just a few clicks, sellers can level up their customers’ experience by experimenting with custom layouts and pre-made templates.

**Instructions**:

1. Access the Amazon Vendor Central page by clicking the [URL](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/)

FAQs

**1. Who is eligible to access and manage the Brand Store in Vendor Central?**

- Only brand-registered vendors can create and manage a Brand Store.
- If you do not see the Store option in Vendor Central, ensure:
- You are the brand owner or an authorized representative.
- Your brand is registered with Amazon Brand Registry.
- Your Vendor Central account has admin or marketing permissions.

**2. What should I do if I can’t find the Brand Store in Vendor Central?**

- If the Brand Store is missing from your account:

  - Ensure you have the correct account selected in Vendor Central.
  - Confirm that your brand is enrolled in Amazon Brand Registry.
  - If still missing, open a case with Amazon Vendor Support > Brand Store Issues.

**3. How do I verify that I’m working on the correct Brand Store?**

- Go to Stores > Manage Stores and check the brand name associated with the storefront.
- If you manage multiple brands, switch accounts by clicking See All Accounts and selecting the correct one.

**4. How long does it take for Brand Store updates to go live?**

- After submitting edits, Amazon reviews changes within 24-72 hours before publishing.
- If updates are not showing live after approval, clear your browser cache or check from a different device.

**5. Can I give other team members access to manage the Brand Store?**

- Yes, you can grant permissions by:

  - Navigating to Vendor Central > Settings > User Permissions.
  - Assigning the "Advertising & Stores" role to the team member.

**6. What should I do if my edits are not saving in the Brand Store?**

- Ensure that all required fields are completed before submitting.
- Avoid using prohibited words or images, as Amazon may flag them.
- If issues persist, contact Amazon Vendor Support > Brand Store Editing Issues.
