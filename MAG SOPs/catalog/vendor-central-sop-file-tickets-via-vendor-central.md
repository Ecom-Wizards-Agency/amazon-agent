# Vendor Central SOP: File Tickets Via Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/vendor-central-sop-file-tickets-via-vendor-central

## File Tickets Via Vendor Central

**Who is this for :** The vendor may file a ticket to report an issue or seek help from Amazon's support team regarding various aspects of their account, such as product listings, order management, payments, and performance metrics.

**Objective:** To ensure that the product information on Amazon is accurate, optimized, and up-to-date.

**Instructions:**

1. Access the account by going to [https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/).

FAQs

1. **What should I do after I submit a support case?**

- After submitting your case, you will typically receive a case ID that can be used to track the status. Amazon Vendor Support usually responds within an hour or so. You can check your case status in Vendor Central, and you will be notified via email when a response is available. If your issue requires additional clarification, be prepared to respond to further inquiries from Amazon.

1. **What should I do if my issue isn't resolved after the first response from Amazon Vendor Support?**

- If your issue isn't fully resolved after Amazon’s initial response, you can respond directly within the case, providing additional details or clarifying the problem. If the issue persists, you can escalate it by filing a follow-up case or contacting Amazon support again with a more detailed explanation of the problem.

1. **Are there specific categories for different types of issues when filing a case?**

- Yes, when filing a case, you can choose from various categories, depending on the type of issue you are experiencing. For example, if you're dealing with listing or catalog problems, you should select Manage My Catalog -> Detail Page Issues. If your issue is related to payments, performance metrics, or other account-related concerns, you may need to select a different category. Always pick the most relevant category for your issue to ensure that the right team addresses it.

1. **What if I can’t find my account in the list when selecting an account?**

- If you can’t find the correct account in the dropdown list after logging into Vendor Central, try refreshing the page or logging out and logging back in. If the issue persists, contact Amazon Vendor Support for assistance in selecting the right account or troubleshooting access issues.

1. **What should I do if the "Contact Us" button is missing?**

- If the "Contact Us" button isn’t visible in your Vendor Central account, ensure that you are logged in correctly and that you have the necessary permissions to access support. If the issue continues, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If the button still isn’t showing, you can reach out to Amazon Vendor Support via other available methods or check their help documentation for troubleshooting steps.

1. **What if I encounter technical issues while submitting a case?**

- If you run into technical problems while submitting a case (e.g., page not loading, form submission errors), try refreshing the page or submitting the case again after clearing your browser cache. You can also try using a different browser. If the issue continues, contact Amazon Vendor Support for help with submitting your case.
