# Logistics SOP: Download a Removal Shipment Detail Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/logistics-sop-download-a-removal-shipment-detail-report

## **Download a Removal Shipment Detail Report**

**Who is this for :**Intended for individuals who are responsible for inventory management, logistics, and supply chain operations within an organization using Amazon's fulfillment services.

**Objective :** To outline the process for downloading a Removal Shipment Detail Report. This report provides detailed information on removal shipments from an Amazon fulfillment center, including the SKU, quantity, reason for removal, and disposition of the inventory. The objective is to enable effective tracking and management of removal shipments, ensuring accurate inventory control and decision-making.

**To download a Removal Shipment Detail Report, follow these steps:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.07.37%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VBuimage.png)
2. Under the "Removals" header on the left side of the page, click on the "Removal Shipment Detail" link

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.07.43%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/EKXimage.png)
3. On the next page, select the "Event Date" from the drop-down menu available or choose "Exact Dates" from the drop-down menu and enter the "From" and "To" dates from the calendar icon to the right of each.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.07.50%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Xo6image.png)
4. Click on the "Request .csv Download" or "Request .txt Download" button if you want to download the report in an excel or text format.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.07.58%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/5onimage.png)
5. In the table below, check the "Report Status" column for the status of the report. It will show "In Progress".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.08.04%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PySimage.png)
6. When the report is ready for download, the status will change to "Download".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.08.11%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4mkimage.png)
7. Click on the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column to download your report.

FAQs

1. What information does the Removal Shipment Detail Report include?

The report contains details such as SKU, quantity, reason for removal, and disposition of the inventory. It helps sellers track and manage removal shipments from Amazon fulfillment centers.

2. How long does it take for the report to be generated?

The time to generate the report varies depending on the volume of data. Typically, it should be available within a few minutes, but in cases of large datasets, it may take longer.

3. Can I automate the download of the Removal Shipment Detail Report?

Yes, you can automate report downloads using Amazon's Reports API or by setting up scheduled reports in Seller Central.

4. What should I do if my report status remains "In Progress" for a long time?

If the status remains "In Progress" for an extended period, try refreshing the page or requesting the report again. If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support.

5. Can I download reports for multiple removal requests at once?

Yes, you can specify a date range in the "Exact Dates" option to download removal shipment details for multiple requests within that period.

6. How far back can I retrieve Removal Shipment Detail Reports?

Amazon typically allows access to reports for the last 90 days. For older reports, you may need to contact Amazon Seller Support.

7. What formats are available for downloading the report?

The Removal Shipment Detail Report can be downloaded in .csv (Excel format) or .txt format, depending on your preference.

8. Can I download the report on a mobile device?

While Seller Central can be accessed on a mobile device, some report downloads may not work correctly. It is recommended to use a desktop or laptop for best results.

9. Why am I unable to find the "Removal Shipment Detail" link?

Ensure that you have the appropriate permissions in Seller Central. If the link is missing, check your account settings or contact your account administrator.

10. How do I interpret the "Disposition" column in the report?

The disposition column indicates what happened to the removed inventory (e.g., Returned to Seller, Liquidated, Discarded). This helps in understanding how Amazon processed the removal request.
