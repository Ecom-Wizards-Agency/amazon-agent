# Catalog SOP: How to Generate an Aged Inventory Surcharge Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-generate-an-aged-inventory-surcharge-report

## **How to Generate an Aged Inventory Surcharge Report**

**Who is this for :** This is intended for individuals or teams responsible for inventory management, financial analysis, and cost optimization on Seller Central. It is suitable for users who have access to Seller Central and need to generate an Aged Inventory Surcharge Report to monitor and evaluate surcharges related to aged inventory. This SOP will assist users in navigating to the appropriate section, selecting the desired report parameters, and generating the report for review, analysis, and decision-making purposes. It is particularly relevant for sellers looking to control costs, reduce aged inventory, and optimize their overall inventory management practices.

**Objective :** To provide instructions for generating an Aged Inventory Surcharge Report. This SOP aims to guide users in accessing and downloading a report that provides information on surcharges associated with aged inventory in Seller Central. By generating this report, users can analyze and manage their aged inventory effectively, identify potential cost-saving opportunities, and make informed decisions to optimize their inventory management strategy.

**Instructions:**

1. To create a report on Long-Term Storage Fee Charges, navigate to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the Drop-Down Menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684264630660.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/NlGimage.png)
2. Under the "Payments" header on the left-hand side, click on the "Aged Inventory Surcharge report" link.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684264876779.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Ny5image.png)
3. On the next page, select "Event Month" and Year from the available Drop-Down Menus.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684264921437.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/M2kimage.png)
4. Click the "Generate Report" button in the "Report Status" column to download the report.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684264976250.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I find the Aged Inventory Surcharge Report in Seller Central?

You can access the report by navigating to Reports > Fulfillment, then selecting Payments > Aged Inventory Surcharge Report on the left-hand side of the page.

2. What information does the Aged Inventory Surcharge Report include?

The report provides details on surcharges related to aged inventory, including inventory age, surcharge amounts, SKU data, and specific fees associated with long-term storage.

3. Can I select a custom date range for the Aged Inventory Surcharge Report?

No, the report is based on calendar months. You can select the "Event Month" and Year from the drop-down menus to generate the report for the desired period.

4. How do I track the progress of my report request?

After clicking "Generate Report," check the "Report Status" column. The status will first show "In Progress" and will update to "Download" when the report is ready.

5. How long does it take for the Aged Inventory Surcharge Report to be generated?

Processing time typically takes a few minutes, depending on the volume of data. However, this can vary depending on the amount of inventory and surcharge data to be processed.

6. What should I do if my report does not generate or download properly?

If the report fails to generate or download:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Clear your browser cache or try using a different browser.
- If the issue persists, reach out to Amazon Seller Support for further assistance.

7. Can I automate or schedule the generation of the Aged Inventory Surcharge Report?

Currently, Amazon does not offer an option to automate or schedule the generation of the Aged Inventory Surcharge Report. You will need to manually generate and download the report each month.

8. How can I reduce or avoid aged inventory surcharges?

To minimize aged inventory surcharges:

- Monitor inventory levels regularly to avoid excess stock that could incur long-term storage fees.
- Use Amazon’s removal or liquidation programs to clear excess inventory.
- Optimize inventory restocking to align with current sales trends and avoid overstocking.

9. Does this report include all types of storage fees or just aged inventory fees?

The Aged Inventory Surcharge Report only includes surcharges associated with aged inventory. For a full breakdown of all storage fees, you may need to generate additional reports, such as the Monthly Storage Fees Report.

10. Is the Aged Inventory Surcharge Report available in different formats?

The SOP doesn't specify multiple formats, but based on other similar reports, the Aged Inventory Surcharge Report is likely available in .csv or .txt formats. Check the available options when generating the report.
