# Catalog SOP: Cancelling your Vine Enrolled Products on Seller Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-cancelling-your-vine-enrolled-products-on-seller-central

## **Cancelling your Vine Enrolled Products on Seller Central**

**Who is this for:** This is for sellers on Amazon who have enrolled their products in the Vine Program and want to cancel their enrollment.

**Objective:** To guide sellers on Amazon on how to remove their products from the Vine Program on Seller Central.

**If you want to remove your products from the Vine Program on Seller Central, follow these steps:**

1. Go to Seller Central and click on the "Advertising" tab in the top menu and select "Vine" from the drop-down menu.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SMzimage.png)

2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the "Start Enrolling Products into Vine today!" button.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-cancelling-your-vine-enrolled-products-on-seller-central-image2.jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eg1image.png)

3. Look for the product you want to remove and click on its ASIN link.

4. On the next page, click "Stop enrollment."![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-cancelling-your-vine-enrolled-products-on-seller-central-image3(1).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ukvimage.png)

5. Save your changes by clicking the "Save Changes" button.

After completing these steps, your product will no longer be enrolled in the Vine Program. On the enrollment status page, you can also view important details about your previous enrollments, including the number of units enrolled, available, claimed by Vine Voices, and the number of Vine reviews published.

FAQs

**1. Can I cancel Vine enrollment at any tim**e?

- You can cancel a product’s Vine enrollment at any time, but if Vine Voices have already claimed or received units, those reviews will still be published.

**2. Will Vine reviews disappear after I cancel enrollment?**

- No, Vine reviews will remain on your product listing even after cancellation. Vine reviews that are pending will still be published.

**3. How long does it take for cancellation to be processed?**

- Once you click "Stop enrollment", the product is typically removed from Vine immediately. However, pending reviews may still be published even after removal.

**4. What happens if Vine Voices have already claimed units before cancellation?**

- If a Vine Voice has already claimed or received a unit, the review process continues.
- Cancellation will prevent additional units from being claimed, but previously claimed units will still be reviewed.

**5. Can I cancel Vine enrollment if the "Stop Enrollment" button is missing?**

- If the option to cancel is unavailable, you may need to contact Amazon Seller Support by going to:
- Seller Central > Help > Get Support > Advertising & Stores > Vine Enrollment Issues.

**6. How do I confirm that my Vine cancellation was successful?**

- After cancelling, go to the Vine Enrollment Status page in Seller Central. If your product is successfully removed, it will no longer appear as "Enrolled".

**7. Will I get a refund for Vine fees if I cancel?**

- No, Amazon does not issue refunds for Vine fees, even if you cancel before any reviews are posted.
