## Explore Brand Selection Tool

If you’re a brand owner selling on Amazon, managing your catalog can sometimes feel like a big lift—especially as your product line grows. That’s where **Explore Brand Selection** comes in. This tool was created to help brand representatives like you discover new selection opportunities and stay in control of how your brand shows up on Amazon.

**Who Can Use It?**

This tool is only available to **selling partners who have a Brand Representative role** assigned through **Brand Registry**. That means you need to be:

- Officially registered as a representative of the brand,
- Internal to the brand (not a third-party agency),
- And responsible for selling that brand’s products on Amazon.

**What You Can Do with Explore Brand Selection?**

**1. New Selection Opportunities**

This is the core feature of the tool and is where most of the magic happens.
When you open this tab, you’ll see a list of products related to your brand that either:

- Aren’t yet listed on Amazon at all, or
- Are listed, but without an offer from your brand.

Amazon uses publicly available info (like product names, images, and descriptions from your website) to create draft listings for you. All you need to do is review, make any necessary edits, and submit. It’s fast, it’s clean, and it cuts down on a lot of repetitive work.

**Here’s what you get:**

- **Faster listing**: Pre-filled product attributes mean less manual entry.
- **Fewer errors**: Your data is automatically formatted to match Amazon’s requirements.
- **Listing improvements**: Fill in missing details on existing listings to make them more complete and polished.
- **More selection**: Identify and add products that might otherwise get overlooked.
- **No cost**: It’s free to enroll and use—no hidden fees or charges.
- **You stay in control**: You decide what to list, and you can edit everything before it goes live.

**How**[**Enrollment**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/selectiongaps/enrollment)**Works:**

- The feature is **optional**, but recommended if you're looking to expand your catalog.
- Amazon will invite you to enroll when recommendations for your brand are available. You can go here to enroll
- After enrolling, expect about **30 days** for initial recommendations to populate.
- The **New to Amazon** suggestions refresh **twice a year**.
- If you choose to **unenroll**, that tab won’t update anymore—but the **Has other offers** tab will still refresh.

**Heads-up**: You’re responsible for reviewing and confirming that any proposed content is accurate, complete, and compliant with Amazon policies and local laws. Think of the drafts as a helpful starting point—not a final version.

**2. Review Listing Updates**

This tab gives you visibility into content changes across your brand’s catalog. You can see what’s been updated, keep an eye on accuracy, and make sure your brand is being represented the way you want it to be.

**Why It’s Worth Using?**

Explore Brand Selection is especially useful for brand owners looking to grow their presence on Amazon without spending hours manually building out listings. It takes some of the heavy lifting off your plate—while still giving you full control over what gets published.

Whether you're launching a new product line, optimizing existing listings, or just trying to stay organized, this tool is a smart (and free) way to manage your brand’s catalog more efficiently.

Explore Brand Selection isn’t required, but if you're serious about expanding and improving your brand’s catalog on Amazon, it's absolutely worth a look. You’ll get faster listings, fewer errors, and more visibility—without giving up control over your content.
