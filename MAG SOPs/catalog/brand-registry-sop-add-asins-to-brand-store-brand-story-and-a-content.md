# Brand Registry SOP: Add ASINs to Brand Store, Brand Story, and A+ Content

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/brand-registry-sop-add-asins-to-brand-store-brand-story-and-a-content

## Add ASINs to Brand Store, Brand Story, and A+ Content

**Who is this for:**

An individual who is adding ASINs to a brand store, brand story, and A+ content.

**Objective:**

To enhance brand storytelling and product presentation on Amazon by adding ASINs to the brand store, brand story, and A+ content. By doing so, we aim to create a cohesive and compelling brand narrative that resonates with customers and differentiates the brand from competitors.

Brand Story and A+ Content (EBC)

- Determine which ASINs need to be added to the A+ content, and check to see if the ASIN is part of a parentage. If it’s a standalone listing proceed with the given methods below, otherwise examine which listings in the parentage are appropriate to add to the A+ content using the same steps below.

1. Go to the Advertising tab, then A+ Content Manager.

   Direct Seller Central link: <https://sellercentral.amazon.com/enhanced-content/content-manager>

   Default to Premium A+ whenever it is available. Premium A+ has separate desktop and mobile image placements, so confirm both asset sizes are present before building or updating the content. Use Basic A+ only if Premium is unavailable or Victor explicitly asks for Basic.

2. Check the content type. This will help you verify if it's a Brand Story or an EBC. Click the **content name** to be published.

3. Click the **edit** button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680273226697.png)

4. Click **Next: Apply ASINs**

5. From here, you can add the list of ASINs you want to add. You may also want to opt to bulk-upload should there be a long list of ASINs to add.

6. Review that everything is done correctly, then click **Submit for approval.**

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-03-31_22-41-50.jpg)

7. Amazon will review the content. Check the status daily for any errors.
8. Once approved, check if the A+ content/Brand Story shows up on the PDP of every ASIN that is applied to it. If the content is not displayed, republishing it usually fixes the problem. Check for backend issues as well and troubleshoot as needed, then republish.

NOTE: You may experience delays in getting the A+ approved. It would normally take 24 hours for Amazon to approve the A+ especially if it doesn't violate any Amazon Policy. File an Amazon ticket if it has not been approved after 48 hrs of publishing.

**Brand Store**

Adding ASINs to a brand store is important to boost sales. When a customer visits the brand store and clicks on a tile with an associated ASIN, they are taken to the product's live listing, where they may quickly purchase the item.

Here are the steps to add ASINs to the brand store:

1. Go to **Stores**, then **Manage Stores**.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/blvimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/blvimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-03-31_20-54-48(1).jpg)
2. Click **Edit Store**.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1681839464014.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/QTTimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/QTTimage.png)
3. Check the brand store pages and click on the one that an ASIN needs to link to.
4. On the right-hand side of the page, you’ll see the “add a link or product” where you can either add an ASIN or a page inside the brand store. Paste the ASIN and add. It is recommended to add ASINs to as many tiles as you can, making sure that the tile graphic relates to the product.
5. Click **Done**.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-03-31_23-10-09.jpg)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gOvimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/gOvimage.png)
6. Review and click**Submit for publishing**. Monitor until the brand store goes live.

FAQs

1. Why should I add ASINs to my Brand Store, Brand Story, and A+ Content?

Adding ASINs improves product visibility, enhances brand storytelling, and provides a seamless shopping experience. It also helps customers explore more of your brand’s products, increasing conversions and sales.

2. How do I know which ASINs to add to A+ Content?

Check if the ASIN is standalone or part of a parent listing. If it has parent-child variations, determine which variations are relevant for A+ Content. Ensure that all ASINs comply with Amazon's policies before adding them.

3. What should I do if an ASIN I added to A+ Content is not displaying?

First, try republishing the content. If the issue persists, check for backend errors, such as suppressed listings or compliance violations. If the problem remains unresolved after troubleshooting, contact Amazon Support.

4. How long does Amazon take to approve A+ Content?

Amazon typically reviews and approves A+ Content within 24 hours, provided there are no policy violations. If approval takes longer than 48 hours, consider filing a support ticket.

5. Can I bulk upload ASINs to A+ Content?

Yes. When applying ASINs in the A+ Content Manager, you have the option to bulk-upload a list of ASINs instead of adding them one by one.

6. What’s the difference between a Brand Story and A+ Content?

A Brand Story appears as a scrollable banner above the A+ Content on a product detail page (PDP), offering a broader brand narrative. A+ Content (formerly known as Enhanced Brand Content) provides more detailed product descriptions with rich media elements.

7. How do I add ASINs to my Brand Store?

Go to Stores > Manage Stores > Edit Store. Navigate to the desired page, then use the "add a link or product" feature to input the ASIN. Make sure the tile image matches the product.

8. Why aren’t my ASINs showing up in my Brand Store?

Ensure that the ASIN is active, properly linked, and not suppressed. If issues persist, try republishing the Brand Store. If still unresolved, file a case with Amazon Support.

9. Can I add multiple ASINs to a single tile in my Brand Store?

No, each tile can be linked to only one ASIN or an internal page. To showcase multiple ASINs, create additional tiles and assign different ASINs to each one.

10. How do I monitor the approval status of ASINs in A+ Content and Brand Store?

For A+ Content, check the A+ Content Manager regularly for status updates. For the Brand Store, monitor the Store’s status in the Manage Stores section. If there are delays or issues, contact Amazon Support.
