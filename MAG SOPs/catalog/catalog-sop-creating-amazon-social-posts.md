## **Creating Amazon Social Posts**

**Who is this for :** Individuals who wanted to register and start with Amazon social posts.

**Objective:** The purpose of this document is to present a step-by-step guide for registering and creating Amazon social posts.

Amazon social posting is an option for Amazon sellers wishing to share Facebook, IG, or Twitter-like posts on Amazon detail pages. While it hasn't increased sales or conversion significantly yet, it can still be a good value-add for a client and is worth setting up. This SOP will walk you through the steps to both get started with Amazon Profiles (the social posting name) and how to begin making posts.

**Note:**

- To enable social posts, the client will need to have both the Brand Registry on their account and a Brand Store made. If either of these is missing, speak with the client's POC (Brand Manager).
- Amazon Social Posts is available only to the US Marketplace as of this writing.

> **🛈 IMPORTANT REMINDER (as of June 3, 2025):**
> The Amazon **Posts** program has been **deprecated as of June 3, 2025**. Due to declining impressions and upcoming redesigns of Search and Detail pages, Amazon will no longer support new Posts API requests.
>
> - New post creation will be discontinued after **June 16, 2025**.
> - Full shut-off of the program will occur on **July 31, 2025**.
>   We recommend exploring other Sponsored Advertising formats to continue engaging customers.

**Images**

Click **Upload image** and browse the image to be used. There are a few requirements when uploading an image:

- **Image size:** 640 x 320 px or larger
- **Aspect ratio:** Between 9:16 and 16:9
- **File format:** JPG or PNG
- **Color format:** RGB

Here are a few tips when selecting your image to be posted:

- Use lifestyle images to attract more attention.
- Avoid duplicate images. Images posted already cannot be used again.
- Avoid using images with alcohol, blades, ammunition, and firearms.
- Avoid using collages and images with heavy text.
- Avoid using images with vulgar words in them.

**Caption**

The caption text will allow you to highlight important product features and include a call to action. Keep in mind that you are limited to 300 characters so make sure you keep the most important information.

- You can refer to the bullet points from the product listing and then work out a catching caption from it.
- Avoid using the word keto, weight loss, alcohol, firearms, ammunition, cannabis

**ASIN**

Add **up to 5 ASINs** that the brand owns or is permitted to sell.

- Add ASINs based on the image you uploaded
- You can add relevant products and/or variations of the uploaded image

FAQs

**1. What are Amazon Social Posts?**

- Amazon Social Posts allow sellers to share Facebook-, Instagram-, or Twitter-like posts directly on Amazon detail pages. These posts help enhance product visibility and engagement, though they may not significantly impact sales or conversions.

**2. Who is eligible to create Amazon Social Posts?**

- To create Amazon Social Posts, a seller must:

  - Have an active Brand Registry.
  - Own a Brand Store in the US Marketplace.
  - If either of these is missing, contact the brand’s point of contact (POC) or Brand Manager for assistance.

**3. Is Amazon Social Posts available in all marketplaces?**

- No, as of now, Amazon Social Posts is only available in the US Marketplace.

**4. Can I post for multiple brands if I manage several Brand Stores?**

- Yes, you can create posts for multiple brands. If a registered brand is not listed in your account, go to Profiles > Manage Your Account to ensure the brand is correctly registered.

**5. What are the image requirements for Amazon Social Posts?**

- Images must meet the following criteria:

  - Size: At least 640 x 320 px.
  - Aspect Ratio: Between 9:16 and 16:9.
  - File Format: JPG or PNG.
  - Color Format: RGB.

    - Additionally:

      - Use lifestyle images for better engagement.
      - Avoid duplicate images, heavy text, and collages.
      - Do not use images with alcohol, blades, firearms, or vulgar content.

**6. How can I make my caption more effective?**

- Keep the caption under 300 characters.
- Highlight key product features and include a call to action.
- Refer to the product listing’s bullet points for inspiration.
- Avoid restricted words like keto, weight loss, alcohol, firearms, ammunition, and cannabis.

**7. How many ASINs can I add to a single post?**

- You can add up to 5 ASINs that the brand owns or is permitted to sell. Ensure the ASINs are relevant to the image and caption.

**8. Can I reuse an image that I’ve already posted?**

- No, duplicate images are not allowed. Ensure that each post uses a unique image to maintain compliance and engagement.

**9. How often should I post on Amazon Social Posts?**

- Amazon encourages frequent posting to maximize visibility and engagement. Aim for:

  - 1 post per day, or
  - 1 post every 1–2 days.

**10. Can I schedule posts to go live at specific times?**

- Yes, Amazon allows you to schedule posts in advance. There is no limit to the number of posts you can create or schedule.

**11. What should I do if my post is rejected?**

- If a post is rejected:

  - Review Amazon’s guidelines for content, images, and captions.
  - Make necessary edits (e.g., replace restricted words or images).
  - Resubmit the post for review.

**12. How long does it take for a post to go live after submission?**

- Posts must be reviewed by Amazon before they go live. The review process typically takes 24–48 hours. Plan your posts accordingly.

**13. Can I edit a post after it’s been submitted?**

- No, posts cannot be edited once submitted. If changes are needed, you’ll need to wait for the post to be reviewed, rejected, or delete it and start over.

**14. Where will my Amazon Social Posts appear?**

- Posts will appear on:

  - Product detail pages (PDP) of the attached ASINs.
  - Amazon’s “Related Posts” feed for browsing customers.

**15. What types of products cannot be promoted via Amazon Social Posts?**

- Amazon restricts posts promoting:

  - Alcohol
  - Firearms, ammunition, and blades
  - Cannabis or related products
  - Products with vulgar or inappropriate content
  - Always review Amazon’s content guidelines before creating a post.

**16. How can I track the performance of my social posts?**

- Amazon currently does not provide detailed analytics for social posts. However, you can monitor product engagement metrics such as traffic and sales from your Amazon dashboard.

**17. Can I use promotional offers or discounts in my post?**

- Yes, you can mention ongoing promotions, provided they comply with Amazon’s guidelines and do not include restricted words or content.

**18. How do I access the Amazon Social Posts page?**

- Visit the Amazon Social Posts page at [posts.amazon.com/profiles](http://posts.amazon.com/profiles). Ensure you’re logged into the correct Seller Central account.

**19. Are there restrictions on collages or heavily edited images?**

- Yes, avoid using collages or images with heavy text overlays. Stick to high-quality lifestyle images for better engagement and compliance.

**20. What should I do if I can’t find the “Create Post” button?**

- If the Create Post button is unavailable, ensure that:

  - Your brand is registered and linked to your account.
  - You are logged into the correct Seller Central account.
  - Contact Seller Support if the issue persists.
