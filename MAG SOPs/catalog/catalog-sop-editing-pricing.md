# Catalog SOP: Editing Pricing

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-editing-pricing

## **Editing Pricing**

**Who is this for :** This is for All individuals who want to edit their product pricing on Seller Central.

**Objective :** To ensure that all know how to edit their product pricing on Seller Central.

Before editing prices, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.

**Instructions:**

To edit pricing on Seller Central for your product listings, there are several ways available:

1. One way is to select “Manage Inventory” from the Drop Down Menu of the “Inventory” tab on the top menu of Seller Central.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682362246006.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PYZimage.png)

2. You can also manually edit the pricing of each listing in the “Price + Shipping” or “Business Price + Shipping” columns.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682362899672.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2Zeimage.png)

3. Make sure to click the “Save All” button at the top left side of the table to save any changes made.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682363081885.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hmNimage.png)

4. Another option is to click the “Manage Pricing” link at the top of the page and make pricing adjustments there.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682363201687.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pnXimage.png)

5. The third alternative is to use the pricing update template by searching "price and quantity template". A pop-up window will appear where you can download the “Price and Quantity File Template”.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682364094783.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/wBgimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682364151579.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Qtvimage.png)

6. While completing the Template, make sure to refer to the “Data Definitions” Worksheet.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682364222951.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iBLimage.png)

7. Enter your SKU and the relevant pricing as described in the “Data Definitions” Worksheet.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682364366104.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/a59image.png)

8. After completing all the pricing updates, save your file and upload it to Seller Central by going to the “Add Products via Upload” option from the Drop Down Menu of the “Category” tab on the top menu of Seller Central.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682364438968.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/R5Nimage.png)

9. Under the Upload your spreadsheet tab, choose your file by clicking on the “Browse files” button. You can also enter an email address to be notified once your file is uploaded.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/f59image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682364492681.png)

10. Finally, click the “Upload” button, and your changes will be uploaded to your catalog.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1682364742985.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sMfimage.png)

FAQs

1. How do I edit product pricing on Seller Central?

You can edit product pricing in several ways:

- Manually in the “Manage Inventory” section under “Price + Shipping” or “Business Price + Shipping” columns.
- Use the “Manage Pricing” link at the top of the page.
- Bulk edit using the “Price and Quantity Template” by downloading it from the “Add Products via Upload” section.

2. Do I need approval before editing prices?

Yes, always communicate with your client and get approval before making any pricing changes. Include the approval proof (e.g., a screenshot or Fireflies call link) as Task Details or a Comment for reference.

3. How do I manually edit pricing in Seller Central?

1. Go to Inventory > Manage Inventory.
2. Find the listing you want to edit and adjust the pricing in the “Price + Shipping” or “Business Price + Shipping” columns.
3. Click “Save All” at the top left to confirm and save the changes.

4. How can I make bulk pricing adjustments?

You can use the “Price and Quantity Template” to make bulk pricing updates.

1. Search for the “Price and Quantity Template” in Seller Central.
2. Download the file and complete it, referring to the “Data Definitions” Worksheet.
3. Upload the completed file via the “Add Products via Upload” section in the “Category” menu.

5. What should I do after completing the pricing template?

After completing the template, save your file and upload it by going to “Add Products via Upload” in the “Category” tab. Click “Browse files” to select your file, and hit “Upload” to upload the changes to your catalog.

6. Will I get notified when my pricing changes are uploaded?

Yes, you can enter your email address when uploading the file to receive notifications once your file has been processed.

7. How do I ensure the changes are successfully uploaded?

After uploading, check the processing report in Seller Central to ensure that the changes have been applied without any errors.

8. Can I edit pricing for multiple listings at once?

Yes, using the “Price and Quantity Template”, you can edit the pricing for multiple listings at once by following the bulk update process outlined above.
