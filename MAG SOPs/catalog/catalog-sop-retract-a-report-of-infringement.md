# Catalog SOP: Retract a Report of Infringement

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-retract-a-report-of-infringement

## Retract a Report of Infringement

**Who is this for :** This is for brand owners who have submitted a report of infringement in error on the Amazon Brand Registry and want to retract it.

**Objective :** To guide users on how to retract a report of infringement that was submitted in error or if the brand reported asked for complaint retraction.

**2 Ways to Retract**

1. Through Brand Registry
2. Through [https://www.amazon.com/report/infringement](https://www.amazon.com/report/infringement)

**Pre-requisites for filing a retraction**

1. Complaint ID
2. Request from the client or formal request from the brand reported
3. ASIN of the product
4. Name of the brand reported for retraction

**First Way (Through Brand Registry)**

To retract a report of infringement that was submitted in error on the Amazon Brand Registry, follow these steps:

1. Hover to "Monitor" tab on the Amazon Brand Registry homepage.
2. Click on "Submission History."

**Second Way (Through direct Report Infringement)**

To retract a report of infringement that was submitted in through Report Infringement, follow these steps:

1. Sign in to [https://www.amazon.com/report/infringement](https://www.amazon.com/report/infringement)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%201.53.38%E2%80%AFAM.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KwCimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/KwCimage.png)

2. Once signed in, click on "Retract a Report"

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%201.53.46%E2%80%AFAM.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/tZGimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/tZGimage.png)

3. On the Notice Retraction Form, Provide the Complaint ID of your infringement report. Then, click Search.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%201.53.53%E2%80%AFAM.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LzRimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/LzRimage.png)

4. Below the Complaint ID, you will see a selection where you can choose which ASINs or companies you would like to retract the complaint. Select all that applies.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%201.54.01%E2%80%AFAM.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VSAimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/VSAimage.png)

5. Then, Select a "Reason for Retraction" depending on your case. If you cannot choose from the first 2, you may select "Other"

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%201.54.07%E2%80%AFAM.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bwWimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/bwWimage.png)

6. Once the reason has been selected, tick the "I acknowledge that retracting the complaint against the above will lead to the reinstatement of the ASIN/allow the selected sellers to list on the ASIN(s)."
Then, submit.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%201.54.15%E2%80%AFAM.png)

7. Submission review will take around 2-3 days. The email used for complaint retraction will receive an email saying

"Hello,

Thank you for informing us that you retracted your complaint. We reviewed the information you provided and reinstated the following content:

ASIN: [ASIN reported]

Complaint ID: [complaint number]

Sincerely,

Seller Performance Team

Amazon.com

http://www.amazon.com"

FAQs

1. **Why would I need to retract a report of infringement?**

- You might need to retract an infringement report if:

  - You reported a seller by mistake.
  - The issue has been resolved between both parties.
  - The brand you reported requested a retraction.
  - You misunderstood the listing's compliance with your intellectual property.

1. **Does Amazon always approve retraction requests?**

- Amazon typically processes retractions, but they do not guarantee automatic approval. If Amazon believes the original report was valid, they may deny the retraction.

1. **What happens after I retract an infringement report?**

- The reported ASIN may be reinstated (if previously taken down).
- The reported seller may regain selling privileges for that ASIN.
- Amazon may notify the affected seller about the retraction.
- The complaint ID remains in your submission history, but its status changes.

1. **How long does it take for a retraction to be processed?**

- Retraction review typically takes 2–3 days, but in some cases, it may take up to a week.
- If you do not receive confirmation within 5 business days, follow up with Amazon.

1. **What should I do if the "Retract a Complaint" button is missing?**

- If you don’t see the Retract a Complaint option in Brand Registry or Report Infringement:

  - Ensure you’re logged into the correct account that submitted the complaint.
  - Check if the complaint is still active (Amazon may have already resolved it).
  - Try using the alternate method (Brand Registry or Report Infringement form).
  - If still unavailable, contact Amazon Seller Support or Brand Registry Support.

1. **What if the seller I reported doesn’t receive confirmation of the retraction?**

- If the seller claims they haven’t been notified:

  - Confirm with Amazon that the retraction was processed.
  - Ask the seller to check their Performance Notifications in Seller Central.
  - If necessary, provide the seller with a copy of the retraction confirmation email.

1. **Does retracting a complaint affect my brand protection status on Amazon?**

- No, retracting a single complaint does not impact your Brand Registry protections. However, frequent erroneous reports may lead Amazon to scrutinize future infringement claims.

1. **What should I do if Amazon does not process my retraction properly?**

- If Amazon does not process your retraction within 5 business days:

  - Reopen the case in Brand Registry or Seller Support and provide your Complaint ID.
  - Ask for escalation to a senior representative.
  - Follow up every 48 hours until the retraction is completed.
