## **Delivery Document - FBA Restock**

**Who is this for:** This document is to be used by brand managers, Amazon specialists, project managers, or any member of the brand management team who directly communicates with clients

**Objective:** To provide a template when sending a **Weekly Restock Recommendation Report** to clients.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sntimage.png)

**SUBJECT:** Weekly Restock Recommendation Report (60 days)

Hello,

Attached is a shipment recommendation from My Amazon Guy.

**How do we get suggested restock number?**

MAG suggests a 2-month Supply for inventory at FBA. Our numbers subtract the two month supply (based on sales), from currently inbound inventory, and current inventory at FBA.

**Why a 2-Month Supply?**

Stocking inventory is a balancing act between having enough inventory to not sell out and to not overstock and pay large fees within Amazon.

**What do we need from you?**

Please review the suggested numbers on our worksheet. If you would like us to assist in setting up and processing your shipment, please let your MAG Specialist know.

Thank you,
My Amazon Guy
