# Catalog SOP: Switching Fulfillment Methods between FBA and FBM

## Switching Fulfillment Methods between FBA and FBM

**Who is this for :** This is intended for all Amazon Sellers and other individuals who want to change their Seller-Fulfilled Listings to Amazon-fulfilled Listings or Amazon-fulfilled Listings to Seller-Fulfilled Listings.

**Objective :** To provide a step-by-step guide for Amazon Sellers to change their FBA listings to FBM or FBM listings to FBA.

1. To switch between Fulfilled by Merchant (FBM) and Fulfilled by Amazon (FBA) for your products, go to the "Inventory" tab in Seller Central and select "Manage Inventory".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot_44(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4tHimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4tHimage.png)
2. Choose the products you want to modify, click on the "Actions on X selected" button in the top left corner, and select "Convert to Fulfilled by Amazon" or "Change to Fulfilled by Merchant" as needed.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-09-12%20at%2012.11.03%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/IJDimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/IJDimage.png)
3. Keep in mind that you can't convert Parent ASINs to FBA.
4. If you want to convert Child ASINs to FBA, select them and click "Change to Fulfilled by Amazon".
5. Clicking "List as FBA and Send to Amazon" will take you to the Shipment Plan page for the selected ASINs.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1681750681395.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/E61image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/E61image.png)
6. Clicking "List as FBA" will prompt you to specify if the ASINs require batteries or hazmat certifications.
7. If you answer "yes" to either question, additional options will appear. For this tutorial, we'll assume the answer is "no" to both.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1681751423151.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/aalimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/aalimage.png)
8. Click "List as FBA" to complete the conversion and see the ASINs on the FBA Inventory Page.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1681750928032.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4uaimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4uaimage.png)
9. To switch from FBA to FBM, select the Child ASINs and choose "Change to Fulfilled by Merchant" from the "Actions on X Selected" button.
10. Click "Convert" to confirm your changes.

FAQs

#### 1. How do I switch from FBA to FBM or vice versa?

To switch between Fulfilled by Merchant (FBM) and Fulfilled by Amazon (FBA):

1. Go to Inventory > Manage Inventory in Seller Central.
2. Select the products you want to modify.
3. Click “Actions on X selected” and choose either “Convert to Fulfilled by Amazon” or “Change to Fulfilled by Merchant”.

#### 2. Can I convert Parent ASINs to FBA?

No, you cannot convert Parent ASINs to FBA. You can only convert Child ASINs to FBA.

#### 3. How do I convert Child ASINs to FBA?

To convert Child ASINs to FBA:

1. Select the Child ASINs you want to convert.
2. Click “Change to Fulfilled by Amazon” under “Actions on X selected”.
3. Follow the steps to list as FBA, including specifying if the products need batteries or hazmat certifications.

#### 4. What happens when I click "List as FBA"?

When you click “List as FBA”, you'll be prompted to specify if the ASINs need batteries or hazmat certifications. If you select “no”, the process will continue to the FBA Inventory Page.

#### 5. What if I want to convert FBA ASINs back to FBM?

To switch from FBA to FBM, select the Child ASINs and choose “Change to Fulfilled by Merchant” from the “Actions on X selected” dropdown. Then click “Convert” to confirm the changes.

#### 6. Do I need to send products to Amazon if I switch from FBM to FBA?

Yes, when switching from FBM to FBA, you'll need to create a Shipment Plan and send the products to Amazon's fulfillment center.

#### 7. Can I change my fulfillment method for multiple ASINs at once?

Yes, you can select multiple ASINs and use the “Actions on X selected” dropdown to change their fulfillment method in bulk.

#### 8. How do I know if my products are successfully converted?

After completing the conversion, check the FBA Inventory Page in Seller Central to verify that the products are now listed under FBA. If you converted to FBM, they will remain in your regular Manage Inventory section.
