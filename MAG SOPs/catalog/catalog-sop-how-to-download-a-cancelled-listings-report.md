# Catalog SOP: How to Download a Cancelled Listings Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-download-a-cancelled-listings-report

## **How to download a Cancelled Listings Report**

**Who is this for :** An Amazon seller may request a Cancelled Listings Report to gain valuable information about their cancelled listings on the platform. Cancelled listings are products that were listed on Amazon but were later cancelled by the seller, either voluntarily or due to policy violations.

**Objective:**To identify and manage cancelled listings. By downloading the report, sellers can quickly identify all their cancelled listings in one place. They can then manage these listings by either re-listing them or taking necessary steps to prevent future cancellations.

**Instructions:**

1. Access the “Report” tab from the top menu of Seller Central and select “Inventory Reports” from the selection Menu.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/5BPimage.png)

2. On the next page, select “Cancelled Listings Report” from the “Select Report Type” Drop-Down.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/AuTimage.png)

3. If you wish to customize your report, click the “Customize the columns for this report” link and follow the steps on the next page.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/UhOimage.png)

4. You can customize the report and just select the attributes that you want or you need. Click “Save” once done with the customization.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/qM1image.png)

5. Return to your Inventory Reports page by clicking the “Inventory Reports” link at the top left of the page. Click the “Request Report” button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/e4fimage.png)

6. Your report will appear in the table below with an “In Progress” status.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6mUimage.png)

7. Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to “Ready,” and a “Download” link will appear. Click the “Download” link to download the report.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rjiimage.png)

8. The report will be available in text format.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sTgimage.png)

9. It is advisable to open the report via Excel for easy audit. Sample of Cancelled Listings Report below is in Excel format.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Ukfimage.png)

FAQs

1. What is the Cancelled Listings Report, and why is it important?

The Cancelled Listings Report provides a detailed list of products that were previously listed on Amazon but later cancelled, either by the seller or due to policy violations. This report is useful for sellers to manage and track these listings, identify the cause of cancellations, and take corrective actions, such as relisting products or preventing future cancellations.

2. How do I access the Cancelled Listings Report in Seller Central?

To access the Cancelled Listings Report:

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central.
2. Click on the “Reports” tab from the top menu.
3. Select “Inventory Reports” from the drop-down menu.

3. How do I select the Cancelled Listings Report type?

Once on the Inventory Reports page:

1. In the “Select Report Type” drop-down menu, choose “Cancelled Listings Report”.

4. Can I customize the Cancelled Listings Report?

Yes, you can customize the report by selecting specific attributes you want to include:

1. Click the “Customize the columns for this report” link.
2. Follow the instructions on the next page to select the relevant columns.
3. Once you’re done customizing, click “Save” to save your settings.

5. How do I generate the Cancelled Listings Report?

After customizing your report (if needed):

1. Return to the Inventory Reports page by clicking the “Inventory Reports” link at the top left.
2. Click the “Request Report” button to generate the report.

6. How long does it take for the Cancelled Listings Report to be generated?

The report typically takes a few minutes to generate. The status of the report will show as “In Progress” while it’s being generated.

7. How do I download the Cancelled Listings Report once it’s ready?

Once the report is ready:

1. The status will change to “Ready”.
2. A “Download” link will appear.
3. Click the “Download” link to save the report to your computer.

8. In what format will the Cancelled Listings Report be available?

The Cancelled Listings Report is available in text format. To make analysis easier, it’s recommended to open the report in Excel.

9. Why should I open the Cancelled Listings Report in Excel?

Opening the Cancelled Listings Report in Excel makes it easier to audit and analyze the data. Excel offers sorting, filtering, and formula options that simplify the process of reviewing and managing your cancelled listings.

10. What should I do after downloading the Cancelled Listings Report?

After downloading the report, review the listings to understand why they were cancelled. You can either:

- Relist the products if the cancellation was temporary or due to a minor issue.
- Take appropriate actions to prevent future cancellations, such as addressing policy violations or improving product listings.

11. Can I schedule this report to be generated automatically?

Currently, Amazon Seller Central does not offer an option to automatically schedule the Cancelled Listings Report. However, you can manually download it as needed to stay on top of your listings.
