# Catalog SOP: FBM Late Shipment

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-fbm-late-shipment

## **FBM Late Shipments**

**Who is this for :**Individuals who are responsible for managing or checking late shipments for merchant-fulfilled orders.

**Objective:** This SOP will guide you on how to check late shipments

**Note:**• Only accounts with merchant-fulfilled listings are applicable for this task. • Ensure your computer's time zone is set to EST only when checking for the Daily Late Shipment for data accuracy. • Attach a full screenshot for the unshipped orders. See the sample image below: ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-fbm-late-shipment-image1.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/L8aimage.png)

You may download the chrome extension [GoFullPage - Full Page Screen](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/gofullpage-full-page-scre/fdpohaocaechififmbbbbbknoalclacl) Capture to obtain the full screen of the order page and save it as a PNG file.

**HOW TO CHECK LATE SHIPMENT/S:**

1. Log in to the Seller Central Account

2. Go to the Orders tab and choose Manage Orders
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-fbm-late-shipment-image2.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/qgeimage.png)

3. On the left navigation portion, please select Ship by Today ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-fbm-late-shipment-image3.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RTJimage.png)

4. Select Date Range: Custom date range

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-fbm-late-shipment-image4(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Qvoimage.png)

5. On the lower right of the page, select "**Results per page**" and select "**100**" so we can see all the orders. Check if there's a 2nd page on the order page. If there is, take a picture of it to show our client what we see when we check their orders, but this doesn't happen often.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-fbm-late-shipment-image5.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/W89image.png)

6. Take a screenshot of the order page and ensure that this is included in the email as an attachment. We will only send late shipment notifications if any late orders are found.

**NOTE:** If the date for today is Jan. 16 and the ship-by date shows Jan.16 - Jan. 17 please do not include it on your Daily Late shipment report for Jan 16, 2023

FBM Late Shipment Delivery Email Sample

### FBM Late Shipment Delivery Email Sample

**Don’t forget to attach the full screenshot of the order page in the delivery docs and edit the date on the subject.**

**Subject:** *****HIGH PRIORITY*** Daily Late Shipment Check: Orders to be shipped on (Date Today)**

**To:** Client’s email CC: POC/BM and AS

**Body of the email (please include the MAG logo):**

Hello Team [Client’s Business Name],

The orders in the attached screenshot must be shipped and confirmed on Amazon; otherwise, they will become late shipments.

Please visit the link below to view your order:

[insert the order page link here]

Consequences of Late Shipments:

- Amazon allows a “late shipment rate” of 4% or lower for 10 and 30 days.
- Anything over this rate can put the account at risk of suspension

All Amazon Orders must ship by the “Ship by Date” which can be found on your “manage orders” tab as shown below.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/HDJimage.png)

**What if I cannot ship my items on time?**

- If you cannot ship 1 or 2 orders on time, this should not be a major issue and can be ignored. Just be careful to not make it a pattern.
- If you cannot ship a large number of items, (over 4% of your total orders), you will need to “confirm the shipments” manually. (We can help you do this). This means that Amazon will not count your shipment as late, but you must remember to ship the order and input shipping information at a later date (ASAP).
- If this is a recurring issue, you will need to consider FBA (if applicable) or extend your handling time to a time period in which you can confirm the orders.

My Amazon Guy has you covered and will help you through the process.

As of now, we just need you to confirm and ship the orders above. Let your MAG Specialist know if you have any questions!

Kind regards,

FAQs

- **Q : Which accounts are applicable for this task?**

  - A: Only accounts with merchant-fulfilled listings are applicable for this task.

- **Q : Why does my computer's time zone need to be set to EST?**

  - A: The time zone must be set to EST to ensure data accuracy when checking and reporting late shipments.

- **Q : What should I do if there are multiple pages of orders?**

  - A: If there are additional pages, take screenshots of each page to provide a complete record. Include these screenshots in the email.

- **Q : When should I exclude an order from the Daily Late Shipment report?**

  - A: Exclude orders where the ship-by date overlaps with today. For example, if today's date is Jan. 16 and the ship-by date shows Jan. 16 - Jan. 17, do not include it in the report for Jan. 16.

- **Q : What format should the screenshots be saved in?**

  - A: Save the screenshots as PNG files and ensure they are included in the email as attachments.

- **Q : How often should I send late shipment notifications?**

  - A: Notifications should only be sent if any late orders are found during the daily checks.
