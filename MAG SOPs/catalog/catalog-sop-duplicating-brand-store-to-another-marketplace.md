## **Duplicating Brand Store to Another Marketplace**

**Who is this for :** Individuals who wanted to duplicate their current Amazon brand store from one marketplace to another.

**Objective :** To expand the reach of the brand and increase sales by duplicating the existing Amazon brand store to another marketplace.

An Amazon Brand Store allows merchants to set up an online storefront for their brands for free. In this case, we are going to copy the brand store from one marketplace to another. For example, the brand store in the US marketplace is already built and it just needs to be duplicated for CA.

**NOTE:**To have a brand store, you must be a brand owner and be brand registered. Or if you are a registered reseller, you must have brand rights. There is no direct process to duplicate a brand store other than rebuilding it.

In this part, you're going to duplicate the brand store to another marketplace using image assets and duplicate the format/pattern/store settings.

FAQs

**1. Are there any restrictions when duplicating a brand store to another marketplace?**

Yes, some elements may need adjustments due to marketplace-specific rules. Restrictions may include:

- **Different product availability** – Some ASINs may not be eligible in the new marketplace.
- **Content guidelines** – Certain claims or images may need to comply with regional regulations.
- **Video formats** – Some marketplaces have different video specifications or approval processes.

**2. Do internal links in the brand store automatically update when duplicating to another marketplace?**

No, internal links do not automatically adjust. You will need to manually update links to ensure they direct to the correct product pages and category pages in the new marketplace.

**3. How long does it take for Amazon to approve and publish the duplicated brand store?**

Amazon typically reviews and approves a brand store within **24 to 72 hours**, but it may take longer if additional moderation is required. If the store is under review for more than 5 days, contact Amazon Seller Support.

**4. Will duplicating a brand store affect my existing store in the original marketplace?**

No, duplicating a store to another marketplace does not change the original brand store. However, be cautious when modifying shared assets, as changes made to images or templates might impact multiple marketplaces.

**5. Do I need to translate or localize the content when duplicating a brand store?**

Yes, if the target marketplace operates in a different language, you should:

- **Translate product descriptions and brand messaging** for clarity and compliance.
- **Adjust currency, measurements, and regional terminology** to match local standards.
- **Ensure compliance with local advertising guidelines** for text and images.

**6. What are common issues when duplicating a brand store, and how can I fix them?**

Some common problems include:

- **Missing assets** – Ensure all creative assets are properly downloaded and re-uploaded.
- **Formatting errors** – Double-check tile layouts, image placements, and mobile compatibility.
- **Approval delays** – If the store remains in review for an extended period, verify all elements comply with Amazon’s brand store guidelines and contact Seller Support if necessary.
