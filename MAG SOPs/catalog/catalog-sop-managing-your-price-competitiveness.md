## **Managing Your Price Competitiveness**

**Who is this for :** Amazon sellers or brand owners have the primary responsibility for managing Amazon listing price competitiveness.

**Objective :** To ensure that your products are priced competitively within the Amazon marketplace. This objective is important because pricing plays a significant role in determining your product's visibility, sales, and overall success on the platform.

**Note :** For these items, you have several options.

- Match the Featured Offer price, on all offers for the same ASIN and condition. Also compare with prices from retailers outside Amazon. Continue automated pricing after updating prices elsewhere.
- Automate Pricing helps you stay price competitive by automatically adjusting your prices in real-time, which may increase your likelihood of being the Featured Offer (Buy Box).
- Reference Prices may refer to the Featured Offer (Buy Box) price, Price derived from the per unit Featured Offer price based on smaller packs of this product, 60-day average selling price, or list price.

FAQs

1. What is the importance of managing price competitiveness on Amazon?

Price competitiveness is crucial for determining your product’s visibility, sales, and overall success on Amazon. Products with competitive pricing are more likely to be featured in the Buy Box, which drives higher customer traffic and improves sales performance.

2. How do I get client approval before implementing price changes?

Before making price changes, always inform the client and obtain their approval. Ensure to include proof of approval, such as a screenshot or a Fireflies call link, as Task Details or in the Comments section.

3. What is the Featured Offer (Buy Box) price, and how does it impact my pricing?

The Featured Offer (Buy Box) price is the price of the product that is selected as the primary option for customers to purchase. Matching the Featured Offer price increases your chances of being featured in the Buy Box, which significantly boosts product visibility and sales.

4. What options do I have to stay competitive with my pricing?

You can:

- Match the Featured Offer price for the same ASIN and condition.
- Compare your price with prices from external retailers to ensure you remain competitive.
- Use Automated Pricing to automatically adjust your prices in real-time, based on changes in the Buy Box price or market conditions.

5. What is Automated Pricing, and how does it help with price competitiveness?

Automated Pricing adjusts your product’s price automatically based on specific rules you set, such as matching the Buy Box price. This helps you remain competitive without manually adjusting your prices every time your competitors make a change, increasing your likelihood of securing the Featured Offer.

6. How do I access the Pricing Opportunities section?

To access Pricing Opportunities:

1. Go to the Pricing tab in the Menu.
2. Select Pricing Health.
3. Navigate to the Pricing Opportunities section, where you can view any eligible ASINs and download the relevant report to help optimize your prices.

7. How can I check the Featured Offer price for my listings?

You have several ways to check the Featured Offer price:

- Via the Manage All Inventory page: Go to Inventory > Manage All Inventory, select the listing, and view the Price and Shipping Cost column to see the Featured Offer price.
- Check the SKU’s winning rate: For more details, click on the SKU, then scroll down to the Pricing section to view your winning rate.
- Via the Help Hub: Enter the ASIN in the search box to get more detailed pricing information.

8. What is the Reference Price, and how is it determined?

The Reference Price can refer to several different pricing benchmarks, including:

- The Featured Offer price (Buy Box).
- A price derived from smaller pack sizes of the product.
- The 60-day average selling price of the product.
- The List Price (MSRP) set by the manufacturer or seller.

9. Can I track my Buy Box eligibility and status using these tools?

Yes, by using the Pricing Opportunities section and reviewing the Featured Offer price information, you can track whether your listings are eligible for the Buy Box and identify actions you can take to increase your chances of winning it.

10. How long does it take for price changes to reflect on the Featured Offer?

Price changes are usually reflected within 30 minutes to 24 hours. However, it may take longer if there are delays in processing or if your changes are not competitive enough to win the Buy Box.
