# Catalog SOP: Creating an End of Day Form

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-creating-an-end-of-day-form

## Creating an End of Day Form

**Who is this for :** This is specifically designed for Amazon Sellers who wish to download an End of Day Form.

**Objective:** The purpose of this document is to provide a comprehensive guide on generating and downloading the End of Day Form from the Amazon Seller Central page. This report serves as a valuable resource for Amazon Sellers, offering a convenient tool to access a detailed summary of their daily sales and order information.

This form assists sellers in reconciling their transactions, managing inventory, and maintaining accurate records for accounting and business analysis purposes.

**Instructions:**

1. To generate a report on the End of Day Form, navigate to the main menu of Seller Central. From there, choose "Orders" and then select "Order Reports."

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685237022429.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jowimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/jowimage.png)

2. On the next page, go to the "End of Day Forms" tab and click the "Generate the End of Day Form" heading. Choose USPS as the option, select your relevant warehouse from the Drop-Down Menu, and click the "Request" button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685237627790.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bfyimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/bfyimage.png)

3. Under the "Download Reports" heading, your requested report will appear at the top of the table with a Report Status of "Request Submitted" and a Download Action of "Not Ready".

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/TRdimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/TRdimage.png)

4. Click the "Refresh" button located at the top right of the table until the Report Status changes to "Processing Complete" and the Download Action displays a clickable link to download the report.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685237929026.png)

FAQs

### 1. Where can I find the "End of Day Form" in Seller Central?

To access the End of Day Form, navigate to the Orders tab in the top menu of Seller Central. From there, select Order Reports. On the next page, go to the "End of Day Forms" tab and click on the "Generate the End of Day Form" heading.

### 2. How do I choose the shipping method for the End of Day Form?

When generating the End of Day Form, you will be prompted to select the shipping method. Choose USPS from the drop-down menu under the "Generate the End of Day Form" heading.

### 3. Can I choose a specific warehouse for the End of Day Form?

Yes, you can select your relevant warehouse from the drop-down menu provided during the generation process. Choose the warehouse that corresponds to your transactions for that day.

### 4. How do I request the End of Day Form?

Once you've selected USPS and your warehouse, click the "Request" button to generate the report. This will submit the request and start the report generation process.

### 5. How do I track the progress of my End of Day Form report?

After requesting the report, the Report Status will initially show as "Request Submitted", and the Download Action will be marked as "Not Ready". Click the "Refresh" button at the top right of the table until the status changes to "Processing Complete" and a clickable Download link appears.

### 6. How can I download the End of Day Form after it’s generated?

Once the Report Status changes to "Processing Complete", a clickable link will appear in the Download Action column. Click the link to download the End of Day Form in your preferred format.

### 7. What file format will the End of Day Form be available in?

The End of Day Form is typically available for download in CSV or similar formats. Check the downloaded file for compatibility with your preferred accounting or inventory tools.

### 8. How long does it take for the End of Day Form report to be processed?

The processing time can vary depending on the volume of data. After clicking the Request button, refresh the page periodically to check for the "Processing Complete" status. Typically, the report should be ready within a short time, but it may take longer if there is a high volume of transactions.

### 9. Can I generate the End of Day Form for previous days?

No, the End of Day Form is generated for the current day’s transactions. You can only generate the form for the latest day. If you need reports for previous days, check the Order Reports section for other available reports.

### 10. What should I do if the End of Day Form is not generated after refreshing?

If the report is not generated after refreshing, check if the correct shipping method and warehouse have been selected. If everything seems correct and the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

### 11. Can I generate multiple End of Day Forms at once?

No, you can only generate one End of Day Form at a time. However, you can adjust the warehouse or shipping method and generate a new report if needed for different sets of transactions.

### 12. How can I use the End of Day Form for accounting or business analysis?

The End of Day Form provides a summary of your daily sales, order details, and shipment information. It can be used for:

- Reconciling transactions with your sales records.
- Tracking inventory based on shipped orders.
- Accounting and business analysis to assess daily sales performance.
