## Downloading a Statement View Report

**Who is this for :** For sellers using Seller Central who need to download a Statement View Report, which offers an overview of payment information, including balance, available funds, recent payouts, and a breakdown of account transactions.

**Objective :** To guide sellers in downloading a Statement View Report in Seller Central. By following the outlined steps, sellers can access and print the report to gain insights into their financial transactions and account status. The Statement View Report provides an overview of your payment information, including your current total balance, available funds, and recent payouts for all account types. It also offers a graphical breakdown of your balance in each account, showing the following details:

- **Beginning Balance:** The amount carried over from the previous settlement period, including account-level reserves and failed disbursements (if any).
- **Sales:** The total amount generated from your orders, consisting of:

  - **Product Charges:**The price multiplied by the quantity sold.
  - **Shipping:**Amount collected from customers to cover order shipment.
  - **Tax:** Sales tax and regulatory fee amounts paid by customers, remitted to you for tax purposes.
  - **Promo Rebates:** Charges for any promotional rebates.
  - **Other:** Includes various items like FBA inventory reimbursements and gift wrap credits.
- **Refunds:** The amount refunded, is categorized as:

  - **Refunded Expenses:** Fees charged by Amazon and promo rebate reimbursements.
  - **Refunded Sales:** Customer order payments for products, shipping, gift wrap, concessions, and tax.
- **Expenses:** Charges made to your account, including:

  - **Amazon Fees:** Referral fees, monthly professional selling fee (if applicable), FBA fulfillment fees, chargebacks for shipping, and gift wrap.
  - **FBA Fees:** Non-order fees such as inventory storage, removal orders, and reimbursement and balance adjustments.
  - **Cost of Advertising:** Advertising expenses.
  - **Other:** Miscellaneous charges like coupon redemption fees and Amazon Lending payments.
- **Account Level Reserves:** Reserved funds to ensure sufficient coverage for claims or chargebacks.

FAQs

1. Where can I find the "Statement View" report in Seller Central?

To find the Statement View report, navigate to the Payments tab at the top of Seller Central, then select Payments from the drop-down menu. On the next page, under the Statement View tab, you can select the relevant period for the statement you wish to view.

2. How do I select the date range for the Statement View report?

In the Statement View tab, you can choose the specific Settlement Period by selecting the dates from the drop-down menu next to the "Settlement Period" field. This allows you to view a report for a specific date range based on your desired settlement period.

3. What information is included in the Statement View report?

The Statement View report provides an overview of your payment information, including:

Beginning Balance: Carried over from the previous period.

Sales: Total from orders, including product charges, shipping, tax, promotional rebates, and others.

Refunds: Both refunded expenses (Amazon fees) and refunded sales (customer orders).

Expenses: Charges like Amazon fees, FBA fees, advertising costs, and other miscellaneous expenses.

Account Level Reserves: Reserved funds for claims or chargebacks.

4. How do I download or print the Statement View report?

After selecting your desired period in the Settlement Period field, scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the "Print this Page" link to print your statement. For downloading, you can print the report to a PDF.

5. How do I view the detailed transactions for the selected period?

To view the transactions for the selected period, click on the "View Transactions for this Period" link located beneath the statement view. This will show a detailed breakdown of all transactions within the selected timeframe.

6. Can I customize the date range for the Statement View report?

The Statement View report allows you to select specific settlement periods, but the range options are predefined in the drop-down menu based on Amazon's settlement cycle. Custom date ranges outside of the available settlement periods are not supported.

7. What should I do if the statement does not match my expectations?

If there are discrepancies in your Statement View report, you should:

Verify that the correct settlement period was selected.

Ensure that all transactions were accounted for in the period you’re reviewing.

Check for any discrepancies in refunds, expenses, or sales data.

If issues persist, contact Amazon Seller Support for clarification or investigation.

8. Can I save the Statement View report for future reference?

Yes, you can download the Statement View report by printing it to a PDF file or take a screenshot of the page. Since the report is available in a web-based format, saving the page as a PDF is a common method for future reference.

9. How often is the Statement View report updated?

The Statement View report is typically updated at the end of each settlement period. This report includes all relevant transactions and balances from that period and should be available soon after the period ends.

10. Can I access older statement periods?

Yes, you can view and download previous Statement View reports for past settlement periods by selecting the appropriate date range in the Settlement Period field. However, older reports may have limitations on availability, so it’s a good idea to download them as needed.

11. Is there a limit to how many statement periods I can view at once?

No, there is no specific limit to how many Statement View reports you can access, but you can only view one period at a time. If you need to access multiple reports, you would need to select different settlement periods one by one.
