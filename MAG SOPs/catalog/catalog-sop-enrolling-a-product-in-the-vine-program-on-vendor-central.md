# Catalog SOP: Enrolling a Product in the Vine Program on Vendor Central

## **Enrolling a Product in the Vine Program on Vendor Central**

**Who is this for:** This SOP is for sellers who want to enroll their products in the Vine Program on Vendor Central.

**Objective:** The objective of this Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is to guide users on how to enroll a product in the Vine Program on Vendor Central.

**Steps to Enroll a Product in the Vine Program Using Vendor Central**

1. From the top menu of Vendor Central, go to the "Merchandising" tab and select "Amazon Vine" from the drop-down menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%204.56.23%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ciDimage.png)
2. Enter the ASIN to be enrolled and click "Begin enrollment".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%204.56.32%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oZmimage.png)
3. Type in how many units will be enrolled in the Vine program. Click "Enroll".![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%204.56.41%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Ur3image.png)
4. Choose the Billing Vendor Code from the relevant drop-down menu.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/nvsimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%204.56.50%E2%80%AFAM.png)
5. Put a tick mark in the "Terms and Conditions" to accept.
6. Click "Enroll".

FAQs

**1. What is the Amazon Vine Program, and why should I enroll my product?**

- Amazon Vine allows trusted reviewers (Vine Voices) to receive free products in exchange for honest and unbiased reviews. It helps:

  - Increase product visibility with early, high-quality reviews.
  - Boost conversion rates with more customer feedback.
  - Build credibility for new product launches.

**2. What are the eligibility requirements for enrolling in Vine?**

- To enroll in Vine, your product must:

  - Be a brand-new ASIN (not refurbished or used).
  - Have fewer than 30 reviews at the time of enrollment.
  - Be FBA-fulfilled and have available inventory.
  - Be priced competitively (highly expensive products may be rejected).
  - Not fall under restricted product categories (e.g., adult content, certain supplements, digital goods).

**3. How many units can I enroll in Vine?**

- Amazon allows sellers to enroll up to 30 units per ASIN in the Vine Program. The more units available, the greater the chance of receiving multiple reviews.

**4. Does Amazon charge a fee for Vine enrollment?**

- Yes, Vine enrollment is not free for most sellers.
- Fees vary by marketplace and product category.
- Some new sellers or product categories may have temporary promotions offering free enrollment.
- Fees are charged per ASIN, not per unit.

**5. How long does it take for reviews to appear after enrolling in Vine?**

- Once enrolled, Vine reviewers can start requesting your product immediately.
- Reviews typically start appearing within 2–6 weeks after products are received.
- Not all Vine participants leave reviews, but most do within 30–90 days.

**6. How do I track my product’s Vine enrollment status?**

- Go to Vendor Central > Merchandising > Amazon Vine.
- Locate the ASIN and check the status (e.g., enrolled, pending, completed).
- Monitor how many units have been claimed and how many reviews have been left.

**7. What if I don’t see the enrollment button or cannot enroll my ASIN?**

- If the option to enroll is missing:

  - Check product eligibility (must meet the Vine requirements listed above).
  - Ensure your Vendor Central account is in good standing.
  - Try a different browser or clear cache.
  - If the issue persists, contact Amazon Vendor Support for assistance.

**8. Can I edit or cancel my Vine enrollment after submitting it?**

- No, once an ASIN is successfully enrolled, you cannot edit or cancel the enrollment. However, if inventory runs out, Vine Voices will no longer be able to claim the product.
