# Catalog SOP: Download a High Volumes Listing Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-high-volumes-listing-report

## **Download a High Volumes Listing Report**

**Who is this for :** This is for Amazon sellers who are generating High Volumes Listings Reports.

**Objective :** To generate High Volumes Listings Report.

1. To obtain the report, access the “Reports” tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select “Inventory Reports” from the Drop-Down Menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-ZMQI6I0Z.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Sytimage.png)

2. On the next page, select “High Volumes Listing Report” from the “Select Report Type” Drop-Down Menu.

3. Click on the “Request Report” button to initiate the report generation process.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712260687753.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/M8qimage.png)

4. Your report will be displayed in the table with a “Request Submitted” status.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712260692648.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/C3yimage.png)

5. Once the report is ready, the status will change to “Ready” and the “Download” link will become available.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712260700317.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gd9image.png)

6. Download the report to your computer by clicking on the “Download” link as needed.

FAQs

1. What is the High Volumes Listing Report?

The High Volumes Listing Report provides detailed information about listings with high sales volumes, including sales metrics and performance data. This report helps Amazon sellers identify high-performing products to optimize their sales strategy.

2. Who should use this SOP?

This SOP is designed for Amazon sellers who need to generate a High Volumes Listing Report to analyze their top-selling listings and better manage their inventory and sales performance.

3. How do I access the High Volumes Listing Report in Seller Central?

1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central.
2. Click on the “Reports” tab from the top menu.
3. Select “Inventory Reports” from the dropdown menu.

4. How do I generate the High Volumes Listing Report?

1. On the “Inventory Reports” page, select “High Volumes Listing Report” from the “Select Report Type” dropdown menu.
2. Click the “Request Report” button to begin generating the report.

5. What happens after I request the report?

After you request the report, the status will appear as "Request Submitted." The system will process your request and generate the report, which usually takes a few minutes.

6. How do I download the High Volumes Listing Report?

Once the report is ready, the status will change to “Ready,” and a “Download” link will become available. Click on the “Download” link to save the report to your computer.

7. What file format is the High Volumes Listing Report available in?

The report is typically provided in a text (.txt) format. You can open the file using a text editor or a spreadsheet program like Excel to make the data easier to read and analyze.

8. Why is the High Volumes Listing Report important for sellers?

This report helps sellers identify which of their listings are generating high sales volumes. By analyzing this data, sellers can adjust their pricing, inventory, and marketing strategies to focus on high-performing products.

9. Can I request the High Volumes Listing Report regularly?

Yes, you can request this report as often as needed to stay up to date on your high-performing listings and monitor sales performance.

10. What information is included in the High Volumes Listing Report?

The report includes detailed metrics on listings with high sales volumes, including the number of units sold, sales revenue, and performance data, helping you to optimize inventory and sales strategies for high-performing products.
