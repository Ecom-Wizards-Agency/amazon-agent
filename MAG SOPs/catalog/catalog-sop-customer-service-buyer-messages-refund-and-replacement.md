## Customer Service - Buyer Messages, Refund and Replacement.

**Who is this for :**Applies to all customer service representatives that is responsible for responding to buyer messages, processing refunds, and issuing replacements.

**Objective:** Facilitate learning and understanding on how to process refunds, replacements and handle buyer messages.

**Customer service**typically involves providing support to buyers in regards to their purchases, including resolving issues with orders, answering questions about products, handling returns and exchanges, and addressing any concerns or complaints. This is intended to help ensure a positive shopping experience for the buyer, which can lead to increased customer satisfaction and repeat business.

**Fulfillment by Merchant (FBM)** is a fulfillment option on Amazon where the seller is responsible for storing and shipping their own products to buyers. The seller is in charge of handling customer service and returns for their items. This option allows the seller to have control over their inventory and shipping process, but it also requires them to handle all the logistics and storage costs associated with fulfilling orders. FBM is a popular choice for sellers who want to have more control over the customer experience and the management of their products.

**Note:** Amazon will handle fulfillment and customer service for items Fulfilled by Amazon. Any form of communication with customers has to be aligned with [Amazon Communication Guidelines.](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ZBnglmF94FDpBLNODwVyO_BtIPyQQUfZ/view?usp=sharing)

FAQs

#### 1. How do I check customer messages in Amazon Seller Central?

To check customer messages, go to Seller Central and select Messages. New messages will appear under the Messages Response Needed section. Review each message carefully and follow Amazon's Communication Guidelines to address the customer's query.

#### 2. What should I do if a customer claims their item hasn’t arrived?

Before issuing a refund, check the order’s tracking number in the order details page. If the item has not been delivered, follow your store's policies to issue a refund or replacement, and seek approval from your Brand Manager before proceeding.

#### 3. How do I process a refund for a customer?

To process a refund:

- Go to Messages and click on the Order ID to open the Order Details page.
- Click the Refund Order button and select General Adjustment for the reason.
- Enter the refund amount or issue a full refund.
- Notify the customer that the refund has been processed using the provided email template.

#### 4. When should I seek approval from the Brand Manager before processing a refund?

Always seek approval from the Brand Manager (BM) before processing any refund. This ensures alignment with company policies and helps manage customer expectations.

#### 5. What should I do if a customer requests a replacement for a damaged item?

Ask the customer for a photo of the damaged item and their complete shipping information. Once you have this, follow the order replacement instructions to process a replacement.

#### 6. How do I process a replacement order for a customer?

To process an order replacement:

- Go to Messages and click the Order ID.
- Copy the SKU of the damaged item.
- Go to Manage Inventory and search for the SKU.
- Select Create Fulfillment Order from the dropdown menu and fill in the necessary fields.
- Place the order and provide the customer with the replacement order details.

#### 7. How do I inform the customer about their order replacement?

Once the replacement is processed, reply to the customer with the estimated delivery date and order summary. You can use the provided response template to notify them about the order replacement status and expected delivery.

#### 8. What are the common scenarios for issuing refunds or replacements?

- Refunds: When a customer reports that their item never arrived or is unsatisfactory (e.g., incorrect item or no longer needed).
- Replacements: When the item is damaged or defective upon arrival, and the customer prefers to receive a new one.

#### 9. Can I handle refunds or replacements if the item is fulfilled by Amazon (FBA)?

If the item is fulfilled by Amazon, Amazon handles the customer service, including refunds and replacements. You should only handle customer service for Fulfillment by Merchant (FBM) items.
