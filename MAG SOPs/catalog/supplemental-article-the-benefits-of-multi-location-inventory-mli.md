## The Benefits of Multi-Location Inventory (MLI)

In eCommerce, delivering products on time and keeping customers happy is crucial. **Multi-Location Inventory (MLI)** is a feature that helps businesses manage inventory across different locations in real time. By syncing inventory data, MLI improves delivery accuracy, streamlines order fulfillment, and boosts customer satisfaction. Let’s explore how MLI works and its key benefits.

**What is Multi-Location Inventory (MLI)?**

MLI allows businesses to manage their inventory across multiple locations. It updates inventory counts in real time and helps calculate more accurate delivery dates by considering the nearest fulfillment center to the customer. This feature makes it easier to fulfill orders on time and provide reliable delivery promises.

**Key Benefits of Multi-Location Inventory**

**1. Accurate and Automated Deliveries**

Without MLI, businesses often set delivery dates manually, which can lead to mistakes, such as overestimating the delivery time. MLI automatically calculates the closest fulfillment center with available stock, ensuring more accurate delivery times. This results in a more reliable and automated delivery process.

For example, instead of promising a 10-day delivery, MLI helps promise a realistic 4-day delivery based on the closest location.

**2. Simplified Shipping Templates**

Managing multiple shipping templates for each location can be complicated. With MLI, you can use one shipping template for all your locations. This simplifies your shipping process and reduces the chance of errors.

**3. More Accurate Inventory Availability**

Without MLI, products may appear out of stock at one location, even though they’re available at another. MLI helps show customers the most accurate stock levels from the nearest location, preventing missed sales and confusion.

**4. Automatic Inventory Updates**

MLI automatically updates inventory counts, eliminating the need for manual adjustments. This keeps your inventory accurate and up to date in real time, ensuring that customers always see the correct stock information.

**The Challenge Without MLI: Overestimating Delivery Times**

Without MLI, businesses may overestimate delivery times, leading to disappointed customers. For instance, a product might be delivered in 4 days from a nearby location, but the system may promise a 10-day delivery time because it doesn’t consider the distance between the customer and available inventory.

**The Advantage of MLI: Accurate Delivery Promises**

With MLI, your delivery promises are more accurate. The system automatically chooses the closest inventory location, ensuring that the delivery time shown to customers matches the actual time it takes to fulfill the order.

**Notes on MLI Availability**

Currently, MLI is only available through SP-API, Feeds API, and Listing Loader feed file uploads in Seller Central. It is also available only for orders Fulfilled by Merchant (FBM), not for Fulfilled by Amazon (FBA) orders.

Multi-Location Inventory (MLI) helps businesses offer accurate delivery promises, simplify shipping, and improve inventory management. By using MLI, you can provide a better customer experience, streamline your operations, and avoid common shipping mistakes. In today’s competitive eCommerce world, MLI is a powerful tool for ensuring your business runs smoothly and your customers stay happy.
