# Catalog SOP: How to Download an Open Listings Report

## **How to download an Open Listings Report**

**Who is this for :** An Amazon seller may request an Open Listings Report to gain to obtain detailed data and insights about the open listings on Amazon.

**Objective:**To analyze a wide range of data related to their open listings, such as sales performance, customer feedback, pricing information, inventory levels, and advertising metrics. This analysis can help identify patterns, trends, and areas for improvement.

**Instructions:**

1. Access the “Report” tab from the top menu of Seller Central and select “Inventory Reports” from the selection Menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%204.04.35%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/n9Iimage.png)

2. On the next page, select “Open Listings Report” from the “Select Report Type” Drop-Down.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%204.04.42%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sSfimage.png)

3. If you wish to customize your report, click the “Customize the columns for this report” link and follow the steps on the next page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%204.04.48%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/CiPimage.png)

4. You can customize the report and just select the attributes that you want or you need. Click “Save” once done with the customization.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%204.04.56%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gVgimage.png)

5. Return to your Inventory Reports page by clicking the “Inventory Reports” link at the top left of the page. Click the “Request Report” button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%204.05.05%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gg9image.png)

6. The report will appear in the table below, with a “Request Submitted” status. It usually takes only a minute or two to have the report ready.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%204.05.14%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/P4Rimage.png)

7. Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to “Ready,” and a “Download” link will appear. Click the “Download” link to download the report.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%204.05.21%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2Vrimage.png)

8. The report will be available in text format.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%204.05.29%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VsXimage.png)

9. It is advisable to open the report via Excel for easy audit. Sample of Sold Listings Report below is in Excel format.

FAQs

1. What is the Open Listings Report, and why is it important?

The Open Listings Report provides detailed data and insights about the open listings on Amazon, including sales performance, customer feedback, pricing information, inventory levels, and advertising metrics. Analyzing this data helps sellers identify patterns, trends, and areas for improvement in their listings, contributing to better inventory and sales management.

2. How do I access the Open Listings Report in Seller Central?

To access the Open Listings Report:

1. Go to the “Reports” tab in the top menu of Seller Central.
2. Select “Inventory Reports” from the drop-down menu.

3. How do I select the Open Listings Report type?

After accessing the Inventory Reports page:

1. In the “Select Report Type” drop-down menu, choose “Open Listings Report”.

4. Can I customize the Open Listings Report?

Yes, you can customize the report by selecting the attributes you want or need. To do this:

1. Click the “Customize the columns for this report” link.
2. Follow the steps on the next page to select your desired attributes.
3. Once done with the customization, click “Save”.

5. How do I generate the Open Listings Report?

After customizing the report (if needed):

1. Return to the Inventory Reports page.
2. Click the “Request Report” button to generate the report.

6. How long does it take for the Open Listings Report to be ready?

The report typically takes just a minute or two to generate and is displayed with a “Request Submitted” status until it’s ready.

7. How do I download the Open Listings Report?

Once the report is ready:

1. The status will change to “Ready”.
2. A “Download” link will appear.
3. Click the “Download” link to save the report to your computer.

8. In what format will the Open Listings Report be available?

The Open Listings Report is available in text format. It’s advisable to open the report in Excel for easier audit and analysis.

9. Why is it recommended to open the report in Excel?

Opening the Open Listings Report in Excel makes it easier to audit and analyze the data due to its structure. Excel’s filtering, sorting, and formula capabilities allow for more efficient data analysis.

10. What should I do if the report is not generating or is delayed?

If the report takes longer than expected to generate:

- Try refreshing the page.
- Check for any system delays from Amazon.
- If the issue persists, you can contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.
