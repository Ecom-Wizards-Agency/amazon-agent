# Catalog SOP: Download a Stranded Inventory Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-stranded-inventory-report

## Download a Stranded Inventory Report

**Who is this for :** This is for all individuals and sellers who want to know how to download a Stranded Inventory Report.

**Objective :** This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) will help you understand how you can download a Stranded Inventory Report. The stranded inventory report shows a breakdown of units in your inventory that are in stranded status.

**To generate a Stranded Inventory Report on Seller Central, follow these steps:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684323352741.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/MkSimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/MkSimage.png)
2. Click the "Stranded Inventory" link under the "Inventory" header on the left-hand side of the page.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684323664702.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/siGimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/siGimage.png)
3. On the next page, you can download the report in Excel format by clicking the "Request .csv Download" button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684323835233.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4kZimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4kZimage.png)
4. If you prefer a text format, click the "Request .txt Download" button.
5. The "Report Status" column in the table below will show "In Progress" until the report is ready.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684323913486.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/i1Qimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/i1Qimage.png)
6. Once the report is ready, the status will change to "Download".
7. To download the report, click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column. If you don't have any Stranded ASIN, you will see "No Data Available" under the "Report Status" column.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684324064182.png)

FAQs

1. How long does it take for the Stranded Inventory Report to be generated?

The time varies, but reports are typically available within a few minutes. If the report remains "In Progress" for an extended period, refreshing the page or retrying later may help.

2. Can all Seller Central users download the Stranded Inventory Report?

No, only users with the appropriate permissions can access and download this report. If you cannot see the "Stranded Inventory" link, check with your account administrator to ensure you have inventory management permissions.

3. Why does inventory become stranded?

Inventory can become stranded for several reasons, including:

- Listing errors (e.g., missing product details or SKU mismatches)
- Policy violations or restrictions on certain products
- Pricing errors that suppress the listing
- Changes in fulfillment settings or ASIN status (e.g., ASIN delisted)
- Expired or unfillable inventory in fulfillment centers

4. What should I do after downloading the Stranded Inventory Report?

After downloading the report, review the stranded ASINs and identify the reason for each issue. Depending on the cause, you may need to:

- Fix listing errors in Seller Central
- Adjust pricing to reactivate suppressed listings
- Create a removal order for unfillable inventory
- Contact Seller Support for restrictions or policy violations

5. Can I access the Stranded Inventory Report using the Amazon Seller App or API?

No, the report is only available through Seller Central on a desktop browser. However, you can use the "Fix Stranded Inventory" tool in the Seller App to address some issues without downloading the report.

6. What data is included in the Stranded Inventory Report?

The report typically includes:

- ASIN and FNSKU (Fulfillment Network Stock Keeping Unit)
- SKU and Product Name
- Current Inventory Quantity
- Reason for Stranded Status
- Suggested Actions to Fix the Issue
