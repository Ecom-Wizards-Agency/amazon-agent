## Editing a Lightning Deal in Seller Central

**Who is this for:** This is intended for sellers on the Seller Central platform who are responsible for managing Lightning Deals.

**Objective:** To guide sellers through the process of editing a Lightning Deal on Seller Central. To ensure that sellers can efficiently and accurately update their Lightning Deals to meet their business needs and promotional strategies.

Before any planned changes for running Lightning Deals, it is important to inform, brief details and get client’s approval. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.

**To edit a Lightning Deal on Seller Central, follow these steps:**

1. Click on the "Advertising" tab on the top menu and select "Deals" from the Drop-Down Menu.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712171608229.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/XX4image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/XX4image.png)

2. On the next page, use the search bar to locate your Lightning Deal by entering the ASIN or SKU and selecting the status, type, and date options from the respective Drop-Down Menus.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712171616766.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/aAyimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/aAyimage.png)

3. Once you find your deal in one of the "Running", "Upcoming", "Needs Attention" or "Draft" status modes, click "Edit" button from the right side of the page.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712171621366.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Klgimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Klgimage.png)

4. Locate the details of the Lightning Deal you want to edit. Then "Submit edits".
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712171625349.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/wgGimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/wgGimage.png)

5. Alternatively, you can edit your Lightning Deal by clicking the "View" button on the right of the deal to access the Lightning Deal page and then clicking the "Edit" button at the top right side of the page.

FAQs

1. Can all Lightning Deals be edited?

No, not all Lightning Deals can be edited. Once a deal has started running, certain changes like pricing and duration may be locked. Ensure you make necessary edits before the deal goes live.

2. What aspects of a Lightning Deal can be edited?

You can typically edit deal inventory, pricing, scheduling, and product variations. However, some attributes may become unchangeable after submission or approval by Amazon.

3. How far in advance can I edit a Lightning Deal?

Edits should be made at least 24 hours before the deal starts to ensure Amazon processes them in time. Some changes may not be allowed if the deal is too close to its start time.

4. Will editing a Lightning Deal affect its approval status?

Yes, major changes like pricing adjustments may trigger a review process, which could cause a delay or rejection. If your deal is already approved, ensure any edits comply with Amazon’s pricing and discount requirements.

5. How do I track or document the approval for changes?

Before making edits, obtain client approval via email or a recorded call (e.g., Fireflies link) and attach the proof as a Task Detail or Comment in your tracking system. Amazon does not always provide a history of modifications, so internal documentation is crucial.

6. What should I do if the “Edit” button is missing?

If you do not see the “Edit” button:

- Check if the deal is already live, as active deals may have limited edit options.
- Ensure your user role has permissions to manage deals.
- Try accessing the deal through the "View" button and look for an alternative edit option.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support.

7. Can I edit a Lightning Deal through bulk uploads or API?

No, Lightning Deals must be edited manually in Seller Central. There is currently no bulk upload or API option for modifying deals.

8. What happens after I submit an edit?

Once submitted, Amazon may review and approve the changes. You may receive a confirmation email, but it's best to check the "Deals" section in Seller Central for the updated status.

9. Will editing a Lightning Deal affect its ranking or visibility?

Yes, changes like pricing or inventory reductions can impact the deal’s competitiveness. Sudden price increases or stock limitations may lower the likelihood of being featured prominently.

10. Can I cancel a Lightning Deal instead of editing it?

Yes, you can cancel an upcoming deal by selecting it in Seller Central and clicking “Cancel Deal”. However, cancellation fees may apply if Amazon has already allocated promotional slots.
