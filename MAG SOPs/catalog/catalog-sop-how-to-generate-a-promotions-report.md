# Catalog SOP: How to Generate a Promotions Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-generate-a-promotions-report

## **How to Generate a Promotions Report**

**Who is this for :** Intended for individuals who are responsible for planning, executing, and analyzing promotional activities.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions on how to generate a promotions report. This SOP aims to enable the designated individuals or teams to effectively track and evaluate the performance of promotional activities within a business. By generating comprehensive promotions reports, this SOP facilitates data-driven decision-making, identifies successful promotion strategies, and helps optimize future promotional campaigns.

**To generate a Promotions Report:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab in Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the Drop-Down Menu. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-26%20at%202.56.00%20PM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2g6iCLimage.png)

2. Click the "Promotions" link under the "Sales" header on the left-hand side of the page then click "View downloads" button. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-26%20at%202.56.33%20PM(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yqOimage.png)

3. On the next page, choose the "Event Date" from the Drop-Down Menu Available or select "Exact Dates" and enter the "From" and "To" dates from the calendar icon.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oCWimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-26%20at%202.57.27%20PM.png)

4. If you want to download the report in an excel format, click the "Request .csv Download" button. Alternatively, click the "Request .txt Download" button to download the report in a text format.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/TOZimage.png)

5. The "Report Status" Column in the table below will display the status as "In Progress".

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Ux8image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-26%20at%202.58.50%20PM.png)

6. Once the report is ready for download, click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" Column to download your report.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Dxmimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202023-05-26%20at%202.59.13%20PM.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I find the Promotions Report in Seller Central?

You can access the Promotions Report by navigating to Reports > Fulfillment, then selecting Sales > Promotions on the left-hand side of the page.

2. What information does the Promotions Report include?

The report contains key promotional data such as promotion types, discount details, order volume, customer engagement metrics, and performance insights to help evaluate promotional effectiveness.

3. Can I select a custom date range for the Promotions Report?

Yes, you can select "Exact Dates" from the drop-down menu and manually enter the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icon.

4. What file formats are available for downloading the report?

You can download the report in Excel (.csv) format by clicking "Request .csv Download" or in text (.txt) format by selecting "Request .txt Download."

5. How do I track the progress of my Promotions Report request?

After requesting the download, check the "Report Status" column. The status will first show "In Progress" and update to "Download" when the report is ready.

6. How long does it take for the Promotions Report to be generated?

Processing time varies based on the amount of data, but most reports are available within a few minutes.

7. What should I do if my Promotions Report does not generate or download properly?

If the report does not generate or download successfully:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Try clearing your browser cache or switching to a different browser.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for further assistance.
