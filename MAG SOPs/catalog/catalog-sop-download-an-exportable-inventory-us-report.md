# Catalog SOP: Download an Exportable Inventory (US) Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-an-exportable-inventory-us-report

## **Download an Exportable Inventory (US) Report**

**Who is this for :** Intended for individuals or teams responsible for managing inventory.

**Objective :** To provide a set of clear and concise instructions to help users download an inventory report that contains a comprehensive list of items that can be exported from the United States. The report will include relevant information such as the name of the item, its description, quantity, location, and other relevant details necessary for the purpose of exporting.

1. To generate an Exportable Inventory Report, go to “Reports” and then select "Fulfillment". ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1683934943292.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2npimage.png)

2. From the Amazon Fulfillment Reports page, go to Inventory ➡ Show more ➡ Exportable Inventory (US)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1683935488015.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9kRimage.png)

3. Click the "Request Download" button to download the report. You can check the "Report Status" column to track the progress of the report.

4. Once the report is ready, click the "Download".

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/FiFimage.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I find the Exportable Inventory (US) Report?

You can access the report by navigating to Reports > Fulfillment, then selecting Inventory > Show more > Exportable Inventory (US).

2. What information is included in the Exportable Inventory (US) Report?

The report contains details about inventory that can be exported from the United States, including item name, description, quantity, location, and other relevant export-related information.

3. How do I track the progress of my report request?

After clicking "Request Download," you can check the "Report Status" column to monitor whether the report is still in progress or ready for download.

4. How long does it take for the report to be generated?

The time varies based on the volume of inventory data. The "Report Status" will initially show "In Progress" and update to "Ready" once the report is available for download.

5. How do I download the completed report?

Once the report status changes to "Ready," click the "Download" button to retrieve the file.
