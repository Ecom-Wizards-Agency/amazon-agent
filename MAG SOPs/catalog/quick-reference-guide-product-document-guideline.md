# Quick Reference Guide: Product Document Guideline

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/quick-reference-guide-product-document-guideline

## **Product Document Guidelines**

To ensure clarity and usefulness of documents for customers, please adhere to the following guidelines:

- **File Type:** Upload documents in PDF format with a direct download URL.
- **File Size:** Maximum of 10 MB.
- **Brand Icons and Logos:** Only include brand icons or logos that aid in navigation and are beneficial to the customer.
- **Prohibited Terms and Logos:** Avoid using terms or logos such as FCKW, CE-certified, WEEE, RoHS, CE-approved, or CE-marked.
- **Quotes and Endorsements:** No quotes or attributions to individuals, customers, or private figures. Up to four quotes or endorsements from publications, public figures, or celebrities are permitted.
- **Competitor References:** Do not reference or compare your products to competitors, whether by specific names or general descriptions.
- **Trademarks and Copyrights:** Acceptable if reasonably sized and part of the product packaging or logo.
- **Awards and Endorsements:** State the date of awards and endorsements, ensuring they are from verified and publicly recognized sources. Do not mention awards older than two years.
- **Use of “Amazon”:** The term “Amazon” is not allowed in content unless referring to AmazonBasics or Amazon-branded products.
- **Marketing Claims:**Avoid boastful claims such as "#1 rated," "top-rated," or "best-selling." All marketing claims, especially those related to regulated products, must be substantiated.
- **Language:**The document’s language must match the consumer's language. For example, Dutch and English are acceptable in the Netherlands.
- **Document Content:** Ensure documents are complete, relevant, and contain useful information such as product features, specifications, and benefits. Blank documents are prohibited.
- **Amazon Branding:** Do not include Amazon headers, logos, or references in documents unless pertaining to AmazonBasics or Amazon-branded products. Avoid phrases like "Buy now," "add to cart," or "shop with us."
- **Shipping Information:** Exclude references to shipping details, such as free shipping or delivery timelines.
- **Promotions:** Avoid time-sensitive offers and promotions with expiry dates. Phrases like "on sale now" or "limited time offer" are not permitted.
- **Watermarks:** Documents must not contain watermarks. Watermarks are visible marks or logos overlaid on images or text.
- **Document Type:** Ensure the document matches the selected type at upload. For example, an SDS cannot be uploaded as a user manual.
- **Accuracy:** The document must accurately reflect the actual product. For instance, a document describing a television should not be used for a phone.
- **Content Restrictions:** Documents must not contain explicit adult content, including nudity, sexually explicit material, or mature themes.
- **Returns and Refunds:** Do not include information about returns or refunds, such as "return the product with shipped address" or "return charges are inclusive."
