## Creating a Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN Report

**Who is this for :** This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is for sellers on Amazon's Seller Central platform who want to download a business report specifically for Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN.

**Objective :**The objective of this SOP is to provide sellers with a step-by-step guide on how to generate and download a Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN Report from Seller Central. This report allows sellers to gain insights into the performance and traffic of their product detail pages, helping them make informed business decisions.

The Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN Report is a valuable tool for sellers on Amazon's Seller Central platform. By following the steps outlined in this SOP, you will be able to access and download this report, which provides comprehensive information about the sales and traffic metrics of your products.

**Instructions:**

1. To generate a Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN Report, navigate to the “Reports” tab on the Top Menu of Seller Central and select “Business Reports” from the Drop-Down Menu.

2. On the next page, find and click the “Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN” link in the left side menu under the Sub-Heading “By ASIN”.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712340997302.png)

3. To customize your report, click the “Columns” button on the left side of the page to open up the Columns Fields available. Select or deselect the columns and information you want to include in your report.

Customize the dates of your report by clicking the calendar icons for the “From” and “To” dates respectively beneath the graph and above the table on the right side of the page and select your desired date range or period.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712341006427.png)

FAQs

1. What is the purpose of the Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN Report?

This report helps sellers analyze the performance of their product listings by providing insights into sales, traffic, and conversion rates. It allows sellers to make data-driven decisions to optimize their listings and improve sales.

2. Where can I find the Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN Report in Seller Central?

You can find this report by navigating to Reports > Business Reports in the top menu of Seller Central. Then, select Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN from the left-side menu under the By ASIN section.

3. What kind of data does this report include?

The report includes key metrics such as sessions (traffic), page views, buy box percentage, units ordered, sales revenue, and conversion rate for each parent ASIN. You can customize the columns to include only the data you need.

4. How can I customize the columns in the report?

Click on the Columns button on the left side of the page to open the column selection menu. From there, you can select or deselect specific data points to be included in your report.

5. What date range options are available for this report?

You can customize the date range by clicking on the calendar icons next to From and To above the table. This allows you to select specific time frames, such as daily, weekly, monthly, or custom periods.

6. In what format can I download the report?

The report can be downloaded in CSV format. Click the Download button above the table on the right side of the page and select CSV from the drop-down menu.

7. Can I schedule this report to be generated automatically?

No, this report is only available for manual download. Amazon does not currently offer an automated scheduling option for the Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN Report.

8. How frequently should I generate this report?

It depends on your business needs. Many sellers generate it weekly or monthly to track performance trends, optimize listings, and make data-driven decisions.

9. What should I do if the report is not generating or downloading?

If you experience issues with generating or downloading the report, try the following:

- Ensure you have the correct permissions to access Business Reports.
- Refresh the page and try again.
- Clear your browser cache and cookies.
- Try using a different browser or incognito mode.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

10. Can I compare multiple Parent ASINs in one report?

Yes, the report includes data for all Parent ASINs in your catalog, allowing you to compare performance across multiple products in a single view.

11. How can I use this report to improve my sales?

By analyzing the data, you can identify which Parent ASINs are underperforming and take action, such as:

- Optimizing product titles, images, and descriptions to improve conversions.
- Running promotions or adjusting pricing to increase buy box percentage.
- Improving advertising strategies to boost traffic and sessions.

12. Why are some columns showing as blank in my report?

If certain columns appear blank, it could be due to:

- Lack of data for the selected date range.
- The ASIN not having enough traffic or sales during that period.
- The column being deselected during customization.

13. Does this report include child ASIN data?

No, this report provides aggregated sales and traffic data at the parent ASIN level. If you need detailed insights at the child ASIN level, you should refer to the Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Child ASIN Report instead.

14. Can I export this report into Excel for further analysis?

Yes, once you download the report as a CSV file, you can open it in Excel or Google Sheets to sort, filter, and analyze the data more effectively.

15. How is "Buy Box Percentage" calculated in this report?

Buy Box Percentage represents the percentage of time your listing was in the Buy Box compared to the total time it was available for purchase. A low Buy Box Percentage may indicate pricing issues or competition from other sellers.

16. Is there a way to see the same data in Seller Central without downloading the report?

Yes, you can view the data directly within Seller Central under Business Reports > Detail Page Sales and Traffic by Parent ASIN without downloading it. However, downloading the CSV allows for deeper analysis.
