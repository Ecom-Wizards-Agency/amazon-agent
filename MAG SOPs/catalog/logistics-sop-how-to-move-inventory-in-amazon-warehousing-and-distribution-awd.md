# Logistics SOP: How to Move Inventory in Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD)

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd

## How to Move Inventory in Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD)

**Who is this for?** This SOP is for Amazon sellers who need to move inventory from Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD) to FBA or off-Amazon locations, including warehouses, wholesalers, and distributors.

**Objective:**This SOP outlines the process for moving inventory from Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD) to FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) or to off-Amazon locations such as your own warehouse, third-party warehouses, wholesalers, or distributors. This workflow includes managing inventory replenishment and ensuring seamless distribution.

**Procedure:**

**1. Access the Move Inventory Workflow**

- **Step 1:** Log in to your **Seller Central** account.
- **Step 2:** Navigate to the **AWD Program Page**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-adidq0iw.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/XN8image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/XN8image.png)

- **Step 3:** Select **Move Inventory** to initiate the process.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-3pjfnt4m.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1yKimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/1yKimage.png)

**2. Choose Distribution Type**

**A. FBA Replenishment (For Amazon Network)**

- If moving inventory to FBA, select **FBA Replenishment** under the **Distribution Type** section.

**B. Off-Amazon Distribution (For External Locations)**

- If moving inventory to off-Amazon locations (e.g., your warehouse, third-party warehouses, wholesalers), select **Off-Amazon Distribution** under the **Distribution Type** section.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-k1182z0d.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/5H7image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/5H7image.png)

**3. Select Origin and Destination**

**A. Moving Within the Amazon Network (FBA Replenishment)**

- Select the **origin warehouse** (the location where your inventory is currently stored).
- Select the **destination** (the FBA warehouse to which your inventory will be moved).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-7gtxs2pb.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/CHBimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/CHBimage.png)

**B. Moving to Off-Amazon Locations**

- Select the **origin warehouse** from the **Move From** dropdown menu.
- Select the **destination** from the **Destination** dropdown menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-7v7k6ya0.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/XN9image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/XN9image.png)

- To add additional channels or destinations, click **Manage Destinations**, then input the **channel** and **destination** information.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-xow69ma1.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/XwDimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/XwDimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-3mm0npzr.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KZVimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/KZVimage.png)

- Click **Save** after entering the destination information.

**4. Select SKUs and Quantity**

- Enter the **SKUs** that you want to move.
- Input the **number of boxes** to move for each SKU.
- Click **Confirm** to proceed with your selections.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-9qbty6gd.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SQ8image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/SQ8image.png)

**5. Review the Move Request**

- After confirming the SKUs oand quantities, click **Review Move Request**.
- On the **Review Move Request** page, verify that all information is correct.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-3yki9rej.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3dzimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/3dzimage.png)

**6. Confirm the Move Request**

- Click **Confirm Move Request** to finalize the process.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-move-inventory-in-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-ra9q9t2a.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/wbTimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/wbTimage.png)

- A **Confirmation Page** will appear once your move request has been successfully completed.

**7. Final Confirmation**

- Ensure that your **move request** has been processed correctly. You will receive a confirmation once the request is complete, and your inventory will begin moving to the designated location.

**Notes:**

- **Auto Replenishment**: If FBA auto replenishment isn't enough to meet expected sales, you can manually request additional inventory to be sent to FBA.
- **Multi-Channel Distribution**: For off-Amazon distribution, ensure all destination channels are correctly added and saved before confirming the move.
