## **Managing Enrolled Vine Products on Seller Central**

**Who is this for:**

- This is intended for sellers who have enrolled their products in the Vine Program on Seller Central and want to manage their enrollments effectively.
- This includes but is not limited to, manufacturers, distributors, and resellers of products marketed on Amazon.

**Objective:**

- To provide step-by-step guidance for managing products enrolled in the Vine Program on Seller Central.
- This aims to help sellers manage the Vine enrollments effectively and efficiently by providing information about enrollment dates, available units, claimed units, Vine reviews, blockers, and enrollment categories.

If you want to manage your products enrolled in the Vine Program on Seller Central, follow these steps:

1. From the top menu of Seller Central, select the "Advertising" tab, and from the drop-down menu, choose "Vine."

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712241556367.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-17T19%3A29%3A44Z&se=2025-01-17T19%3A39%3A44Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=6DZdMMsEhk7eYDjmdaiPsjeCqsvVRykiVJM%2FPPWxm8s%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/nbtimage.png)

2. On the next page, click the "Start Enrolling Products into Vine today!" button at the bottom.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712241562653.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-17T19%3A29%3A44Z&se=2025-01-17T19%3A39%3A44Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=6DZdMMsEhk7eYDjmdaiPsjeCqsvVRykiVJM%2FPPWxm8s%3D)

3. At the bottom of the following page, you will see the enrollment date, the number of units still available for Vine Voices to request, the number of units you enrolled, the number of units Vine Voices claimed, and the number of Vine reviews published for your most recent enrollments.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712241604667.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-17T19%3A29%3A44Z&se=2025-01-17T19%3A39%3A44Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=6DZdMMsEhk7eYDjmdaiPsjeCqsvVRykiVJM%2FPPWxm8s%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/mt1image.png)

4. To find previous enrollments, you can enter the ASIN or SKU in the search field of the dashboard. Additionally, the dashboard provides information about any obstacles encountered during your enrollments. You can verify if your Vine products have been ordered and reviewed or cancel your enrollments. The enrollments are classified into the following categories:

- Concluded: Enrollments that either received all the expected reviews or were enrolled over 90 days ago. At this point, Amazon does not expect additional reviews to be published.
- Reviews in progress: Enrollments for which some expected reviews have been posted.
- Awaiting reviews: Enrollments that have yet to receive any review.
- Canceled by the seller: Enrollments that you have canceled.
- Action required: Blocked enrollments that require corrective action, such as updating the offer/SKU enrolled in Vine, updating some of your product's catalog attributes, or sending more inventory to resolve the block.

5. You can edit specific aspects of your enrollment, such as canceling the enrollment, by clicking the "ASIN link" or "Details" button of enrolled products.

FAQs

**1. Why is it important to actively manage Vine enrollments?**

- Monitoring your Vine enrollments helps you:

  - Track how many units have been claimed by Vine reviewers.
  - Identify if reviews are being posted and how they impact your product’s performance.
  - Spot any blockers or enrollment issues that may prevent products from being reviewed.

**2. Can I edit a Vine enrollment after submitting it?**

- No, once a Vine enrollment is submitted, you cannot edit product details or increase the number of enrolled units. If changes are necessary, your only option is to cancel the enrollment and submit a new one (if eligible).

**3. How long does it take for Vine reviews to be posted after a product is claimed?**

- Once a Vine reviewer receives a product, it typically takes 2–6 weeks for them to leave a review.
- Amazon allows reviewers up to 90 days to submit feedback, but most post within the first 30 days.

**4. Can I re-enroll a product in Vine if the enrollment has already concluded?**

- No, once a Vine enrollment is marked as "Concluded", you cannot re-enroll the same ASIN in the program.

**5. What should I do if my Vine enrollment is marked as "Action Required"?**

- A product may be blocked from Vine due to:

  - Low or no inventory → Restock FBA inventory.
  - Product listing issues → Ensure the ASIN is active and meets Amazon’s guidelines.
  - Incorrect SKU/offer details → Update the catalog attributes in Seller Central.
  - Other errors → Check the "Details" section of the enrollment for specific instructions.

**6. What happens if my enrolled product is never claimed by Vine Voices?**

- If no Vine reviewers request your product, the enrollment remains open until all units are claimed or 90 days have passed.
- If no units are claimed within that period, the enrollment automatically concludes and cannot be re-enrolled.

**7. What should I do if my Vine enrollment dashboard isn’t updating or displaying correctly?**

- If Vine metrics aren’t refreshing or your dashboard isn’t loading:

  - Clear your browser cache and try again.
  - Use a different browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox).
  - Check if there are any delays in Seller Central (Amazon sometimes updates data in batches).
  - If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support.

**8. Do Vine reviews impact my product’s overall Amazon rating?**

- Yes, Vine reviews contribute to your product’s overall review count and rating, just like standard customer reviews. However:

  - They are marked as "Vine Customer Review of Free Product" for transparency.
  - Vine reviewers cannot be influenced or incentivized to leave positive reviews.
