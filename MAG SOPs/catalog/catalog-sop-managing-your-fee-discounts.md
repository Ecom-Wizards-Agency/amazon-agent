## **Managing Your Fee Discounts**

**Who is this for :** This SOP is intended for sellers using Seller Central who want to streamline the process of managing fee discounts. It is suitable for sellers who wish to efficiently update their product prices to meet the discount price thresholds, ensuring they benefit from reduced referral fees.

**Objective :** This SOP aims to help sellers efficiently update and optimize product pricing in order to take advantage of temporary price-based referral fee reductions. This helps sellers offer competitive prices on popular products, attract more customers, and increase sales while maximizing profitability.

1. To access the Fee Discounts page, go to the “Pricing” tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select “View Pricing Dashboard” from the Drop-Down Menu.
2. Click on the Box to the left of the Page that says "Fee Discounts".
3. Fee Discounts are temporary price-based referral fee reductions designed to help you offer competitive prices on popular products.
4. Eligibility for fee promotions is based on the item's total sales price (item price + shipping + gift wrap), which determines the price threshold for each item.
5. Any eligible item sold at or below its price threshold during the promotion will receive the advertised fee discount until the promotion ends.
6. The page displays a list of eligible products, ordered by customer views.
7. Alongside each product, you can either update its pricing to the discount price threshold by clicking the “Update” button, entering the new price in the pop-up box, and clicking the “Update” button, or you can skip editing the product, which will hide the suggested product line for seven days before you can update the price or enroll in Automatic pricing.

FAQs

1. What are Fee Discounts, and how do they benefit me?

Fee Discounts are temporary price-based referral fee reductions that allow you to offer competitive prices on popular products. By meeting the price thresholds during a promotion, you can reduce your referral fees, which increases your profitability while attracting more customers and boosting sales.

2. How do I access the Fee Discounts page in Seller Central?

To access the Fee Discounts page:

- Go to the “Pricing” tab in the top menu of Seller Central.
- Select “View Pricing Dashboard” from the drop-down menu.
- Click on the “Fee Discounts” box on the left side of the page to view eligible products and manage discounts.

3. What determines if a product is eligible for fee promotions?

Eligibility for fee promotions is based on the item’s total sales price, which includes the item price, shipping, and gift wrap. Products must meet a specific price threshold during the promotion to qualify for the advertised fee discount.

4. How do I update product pricing to meet the discount price threshold?

To update your pricing and qualify for the fee discount:

- On the Fee Discounts page, click the “Update” button next to the eligible product.
- Enter the new price in the pop-up box.
- Click the “Update” button to apply the changes.

5. What happens if I don’t update the price for an eligible product?

If you choose not to update the price, the product will be hidden from the Fee Discounts list for seven days. After this period, you can either update the price or opt to use Automatic Pricing to enroll the product in future promotions.

6. Can I use Automatic Pricing to manage Fee Discounts?

Yes, you can enroll products in Automatic Pricing to adjust prices automatically and meet the price threshold for fee promotions. This ensures that your pricing is always competitive without needing to manually update each product.

7. How do I know which products are eligible for Fee Discounts?

The Fee Discounts page will display a list of eligible products, ordered by customer views. This helps you prioritize the products that are most likely to benefit from the promotion.

8. How often can I update the price for a product in the Fee Discounts section?

If you skip editing a product, it will be hidden from the list for seven days. After this period, you can either update the price or activate Automatic Pricing to manage future promotions.

9. How long do Fee Discounts last?

Fee Discounts are temporary, lasting for the duration of the promotion. The specific end date will be provided on the Fee Discounts page.

10. What should I do if my product doesn’t meet the price threshold for Fee Discounts?

If your product doesn’t meet the required price threshold, you can adjust the price manually or enable Automatic Pricing to ensure it qualifies for future promotions. Keeping your prices competitive is key to staying eligible for fee discounts and winning the Buy Box.
