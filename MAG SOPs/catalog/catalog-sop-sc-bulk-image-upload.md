# Catalog SOP: SC Bulk Image Upload

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-sc-bulk-image-upload

## **SC Bulk Image Upload**

**Who is this for:** This is intended for the individuals responsible for managing the Amazon SC system and uploading product images in bulk.

**Objective:** To provide a standardized process for bulk image upload in the Amazon Sales and Catalog (SC) system.

There are **two ways** to upload bulk images: one is through the **Image Uploader**, and the other is via the**Inventory File**.

**Seller Central Bulk Image Uploader**

1. Access the Amazon Seller Central page by clicking the URL: [https://sellercentral.amazon.com/](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/)
2. Once you are logged in, the landing page should look something like this:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-09-17%20at%2010.13.44%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/I7gimage.png)

3. Hover your mouse to **Catalog**, then select **Upload Images**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-05-23_9-35-06(1)(1).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/8Paimage.png)

4. You will be routed to the **Image Uploader** interface. Select **Bulk Image Upload**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684805911522(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DxXimage.png)

5. Rename the images to be uploaded to **ASIN.VARIANT CODE**. This is the same as [VC BULK IMAGE UPLOAD](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-vc-imagevideo-upload). You may refer to this [link](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/GJV4FNMT7563SF5F) on how to rename your images.

- Here are sample naming conventions:

  - ASIN.MAIN (product main image)
  - ASIN.PT01 (1st secondary image)
  - ASIN.PT02 (2nd secondary image)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KEjimage.png)

6. Once done renaming, select all images and create a .zip file.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680214059679(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ptHimage.png)

7. Drag the created **.zip file** to the Bulk Image Uploader. It will show a progress bar while the image is being uploaded.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684806167856(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/QiKimage.png)

8. Once uploading is done, click **Submit images**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684806233771(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/j7Nimage.png)

9. You may click on the [status report](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/imaging/upload/status) to know if your upload is successful or if it encountered an error.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680214220083(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yaVimage.png)

**Uploading Bulk Images via Inventory File**

1. Download the inventory file corresponding to the ASIN that needs to be updated.
2. To identify the correct inventory file, check the category on the product detail page(PDP) of the ASIN.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680803724969(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7tdimage.png)

3. Once identified, go back to the inventory page. Hover over the Catalog tab, then select **Add Products via Upload**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-05-23_9-53-40(1).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LD0image.png)

4. On the Add Products via Upload page, go to the “Download spreadsheet” tab and choose “Download Product Spreadsheet” under “List Products that are not currently in Amazon’s catalog” option.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-06-27%20at%2011.36.15%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Kh8image.png)

5. On the landing page try searching your product on the search bar dialogue box and make sure that you are choosing the correct product by clicking on “Browse via product category”.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-06-27%20at%2011.41.01%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DvUimage.png)

6.Browse the correct subcategory based on how it was displayed on the PDP. i.e.: Home & Kitchen›Kitchen & Dining›Dining & Entertaining›Glassware & Drinkware›Tumblers & Water Glasses.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-06-27%20at%2011.42.23%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bUpimage.png)

7. Scroll down and click the yellow button Generate Spreadsheet. Google Chrome will automatically download the inventory file.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-06-27%20at%2011.43.08%E2%80%AFPM(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/y4fimage.png)

8. Open the downloaded inventory file. This is a protected file. To edit, click**Enable Editing** at the top of the spreadsheet.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop_-sc-bulk-image-upload-image-1ewkwdiv(1).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VCyimage.png)

9. Click the **Template** section of the spreadsheet.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop_-sc-bulk-image-upload-image-1e072h3q(1).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/d3Himage.png)

10. Under the **Product Type** column, click the **dropdown arrow** and select the feed_product_type available. For this example, it is a drinking cup. This will enable all the dropdown options in this row.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop_-sc-bulk-image-upload-image-dj3nnkc5(1).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/mhXimage.png)

1. Fill out the Images columns using IMAGE URLs. You can use the Amazon Backend link or other platforms.

- **Note :** Imgur is no longer used for getting image links

12. After providing the **SKU, Image URLs**, and selecting **PARTIAL UPDATE**, delete the entry under the **Product Typ**e column. Fill out only the SKU and Images columns.

Note: All SKUs (including duplicates) under the same ASIN to be updated must be included in the Spreadsheet.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop_-sc-bulk-image-upload-image-ldbdpb4i(1).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/p8Rimage.png)

- IMPORTANT: Select only Partial Update for any image upload. To know the differences between each option:
- UPDATE - This is used only to update the entire listing entry to the Amazon catalog. All blank attributes in the inventory file will be wiped out from the Amazon system.
- PARTIAL UPDATE - Only those attributes with an entry will be updated in the Amazon system. All blank attributes will remain as is.
- DELETE - This will delete the SKU from the Amazon catalog.

1. Keep in mind that you’re on the correct seller account before uploading the inventory file. You can fill out the Email alert box using your MAG email to receive the processing report on your email. Put a tick on the box to Remember my email address for future alerts. Then click the Upload file button

- Otherwise, you can click and **Download Processing Summary** to analyze which attribute/entry is causing the error.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-05-23_10-12-07(1).jpg)

- If in case the new images are still not displayed after 24hrs, please log a case with Amazon support supplying all the necessary details (batch ID, screenshot of the backend showing the uploaded images, screenshot of the PDP showing old images, and the URL of the PDP).

FAQs

**1. What is the SC Bulk Image Upload process used for?**

- The SC Bulk Image Upload process is designed to efficiently upload multiple product images to Amazon’s Seller Central system, either through the Bulk Image Uploader or an inventory file.

**2. When should I use the Bulk Image Uploader vs. the Inventory File?**

- Bulk Image Uploader: Ideal for uploading multiple images quickly without detailed spreadsheet editing.
- Inventory File: Required when updating image URLs or making partial updates alongside other product details (e.g., SKU adjustments or attribute changes).

**3. How should I name images before uploading via the Bulk Image Uploader?**

- Use the naming convention:

  - ASIN.MAIN for the main image.
  - [ASIN.PT](http://ASIN.PT)01, [ASIN.PT](http://ASIN.PT)02, etc. for additional images.
  - Ensure images are correctly named to match their ASIN and variant code.

**4. What file format should I use for Bulk Image Upload?**

- Images should be compiled into a .zip file before uploading. Ensure the file contains only correctly named images.

**5. How can I verify if my Bulk Image Upload was successful?**

- After uploading, click the status report to check for errors or confirmation of success. If errors are present, the report will identify the specific issues.

**6. What should I do if images do not appear on the PDP after 24 hours?**

- If the new images do not appear after 24 hours:

  - Log a case with Amazon Seller Support.
  - Provide necessary details, including:

    - Batch ID
    - Screenshot of the backend showing uploaded images
    - Screenshot of the PDP showing old images
    - PDP URL

**7. What information must be included in the inventory file for image uploads?**

- Ensure the following fields are correctly filled:

  - SKU
  - Image URLs (must be valid, secure links; avoid platforms like Imgur)
  - Update Type: Set to Partial Update to avoid overwriting unrelated attributes.

**8. What happens if the wrong update type is selected in the inventory file?**

- UPDATE: Overwrites the entire listing, erasing blank attributes.
- DELETE: Removes the SKU from the catalog entirely.

  - Always select PARTIAL UPDATE to update only the specified attributes, such as images.

**9. Can I use the same inventory file for multiple ASINs?**

- Yes, you can include multiple ASINs and their respective SKUs in a single inventory file. Ensure each SKU under the same ASIN is included for updates to apply correctly.

**10. How can I generate an inventory file for specific ASINs?**

- Identify the product category from the PDP.
- Navigate to Catalog > Add Products via Upload > Download spreadsheet.
- Use the Browse via Product Category option to locate the correct subcategory.
- Click Generate Spreadsheet to download the file.

**11. What should I do if the inventory file upload encounters errors?**

- Click Download Processing Summary from the Action dropdown.
- Analyze the error report to identify and resolve incorrect attributes.
- Re-upload the corrected file.

**12. Can I still use Imgur for image URLs in the inventory file?**

- No, Imgur is no longer supported for generating image links. Use secure links from reliable platforms or the Amazon Backend for image URLs.

**13. How do I receive notifications about my inventory file upload status?**

- Enter your MAG email address in the Email alert box during the file upload process. Tick the Remember my email address for future alerts option to save this setting.

**14. How long does it take for images to reflect on the PDP after a successful upload?**

- Images typically reflect within a few hours but may take up to 24 hours. If images are not displayed after this period, log a case with Seller Support.

**15. What should I verify before uploading an inventory file?**

- Before uploading:

  - Confirm you are logged into the correct Seller Central account.
  - Ensure all mandatory fields (SKU, image URLs, update type) are completed.
  - Check the Update Type is set to Partial Update.

**16. Are there specific image requirements for uploading to Seller Central?**

- Yes, images must meet Amazon’s guidelines:

  - Minimum 1000px on the longest side.
  - JPEG, PNG, or TIFF format.
  - No watermarks, text, or logos.
  - White background for the main image.

**17. What does a missing "Action" dropdown in the inventory file upload status indicate?**

- If the "Action" dropdown is missing, it means the file uploaded successfully with no errors.

**18. Can I overwrite existing images using Bulk Image Uploader or Inventory File?**

- Yes, both methods allow you to replace existing images. New images with the same naming conventions or updated URLs will overwrite older ones.
