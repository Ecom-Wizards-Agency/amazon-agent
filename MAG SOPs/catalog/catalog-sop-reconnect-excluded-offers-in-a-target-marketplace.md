# Catalog SOP: Reconnect Excluded Offers in a Target Marketplace

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-reconnect-excluded-offers-in-a-target-marketplace

## **Reconnect Excluded Offers in a Target Marketplace**

**Who is this for :** This is intended for Amazon Sellers who aim to quickly link their offers within a particular target marketplace.

**Objective :** To re-establish the availability and visibility of the offers that were previously removed or unavailable in a specific marketplace. The goal is to restore the listings and make them available for customers to purchase again.

**Instructions:**

1. Log in to the Amazon Seller Central account.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%2010.36.19%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VdDimage.png)

2. Go to Inventory -> Sell Globally

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%2010.36.25%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KKrimage.png)

3. Click the Build International Listings tab

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%2010.36.31%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/CNXimage.png)

4. Select the source marketplace for which you want to reconnect offers. Then click View and edit all exclusions under Product Exclusions.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%2010.36.41%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LHdimage.png)

5. Search the SKU of the listing that needs reconnection in the search box. Tick the box, click the Actions, and select Reconnect offers from the drop-down.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-08%20at%2010.36.49%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yrOpgVimage.png)

6. The reconnection process can take up to 4 hours. To confirm if the listing is successfully reconnected, go to the target marketplace and search for the listing in the Manage inventory, available inventory from the US should match in the target marketplace.

FAQs

1. Why was my offer excluded from a target marketplace?

Offers may be excluded due to pricing discrepancies, policy violations, listing errors, or automated system removals. Checking the Product Exclusions section can help determine the reason.

2. Can I reconnect multiple SKUs at once?

Yes, you can reconnect multiple SKUs by selecting multiple checkboxes under Product Exclusions and using the Actions menu to choose Reconnect offers.

3. How long does it take for an offer to be reconnected?

The reconnection process can take up to 4 hours, but in some cases, it may take longer due to system updates or additional verification.

4. How do I confirm if my offer was successfully reconnected?

Go to Manage Inventory in the target marketplace and check if the available inventory from the US matches in the target marketplace. If it does, the offer is successfully reconnected.

5. What if my offer does not appear after 4 hours?

If the offer is still missing, try the following:

- Refresh the inventory page.
- Check if there are pricing errors or policy violations.
- Ensure the SKU exists in both the source and target marketplaces.
- Contact Amazon Seller Support if the issue persists.

6. Does reconnecting an offer also update pricing and inventory?

Yes, reconnecting an offer restores the listing’s visibility, and pricing/inventory should sync based on your Build International Listings settings.

7. Can I reconnect offers if I don’t have a source marketplace selected?

No, you need to select a source marketplace in Build International Listings before reconnecting offers.

8. Will reconnecting my offer override any previous settings?

No, reconnecting restores availability, but it won’t change pricing, inventory, or fulfillment settings unless Build International Listings is set to automatically update them.
