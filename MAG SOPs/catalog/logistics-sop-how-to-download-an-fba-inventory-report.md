# Logistics SOP: How to Download an FBA Inventory Report

## **How to Download an FBA Inventory Report**

**Who is this for?**This SOP is for Amazon specialists responsible for managing FBA inventory and generating reports to analyze stock levels, sales, and inventory performance.

**Objective:**The objective of this SOP is to guide Amazon specialists through the process of downloading the FBA Inventory Report to effectively track and manage inventory data.

**Introduction to the FBA Inventory Report**

The **FBA Inventory page** and **FBA Inventory Report** enable you to manage your restock, excess, aged, and unfulfillable inventory, helping you maintain healthy inventory levels from a single location. These reports also help you assess fees, monitor critical factors like ASIN sales performance, optimize pricing, and maintain a healthy IPI (Inventory Performance Index) score by improving capacity usage at Amazon’s fulfillment centers.

The following are some of the key metrics included in the FBA Inventory Report that can help you plan your inventory:

- **Seven-day, 30-day, 60-day, and 90-day sales and units shipped**
- **Your list prices**
- **Shipment quantities:** working, shipped, and receiving

**Procedure:**

**Step 1: Log in to**[**Seller Central**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/)

**Step 2: Navigate to FBA Inventory**

1. From the **Main Menu**, go to **Inventory** and select **FBA Inventory**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9Znimage.png)

- **Note:** If you cannot see the **FBA Inventory** option in the menu, follow the next sub-step:

  - Go to the **FBA Dashboard** first.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/lMbimage.png)

- In the **Plan Inventory** section, click **View FBA Inventory**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/j9Simage.png)

**Step 3: Download the Inventory Report**

1. Once in the **FBA Inventory** section, look for the **Download Inventory Report** option.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DMAimage.png)

1. Click the **Download Inventory Report** button.

  - **Note:** You will be presented with two format options for downloading the report:

    - **.csv Format**: This will allow you to open the report in Excel or another spreadsheet program.

      - If you choose to download the report in .csv format, please note that this will **drop any leading zeros** in the MSKUs, ASINs, and FNSKUs.
    - **.txt Format**: This will download the report in a text file format. You can copy the data into your own sheet for review.

      - This format preserves all data as text, and you can copy and paste it into a spreadsheet for further analysis.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bQqimage.png)

3. **Monitor the Report Status**

- Once you’ve initiated the request, the **Report Status** will appear below the report request button.
- It will show the status as **“In Progress”** initially, and once the report is ready, it will change to **“Download”**
- Once ready, you can now download the report.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/OhOimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Hasimage.png)
