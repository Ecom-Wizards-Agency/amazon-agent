## How to Change Legal Entity

**Who is this for :** This is intended for Amazon sellers who want to change their legal entity.

**Objective :** To help you know how to update the legal entity and what to do if a seller can’t update the legal entity.

A legal entity is a company or organization that has legal rights and responsibilities, for example, the right to make contracts and the responsibility to pay debts. Amazon requires that sellers input their legal entities in their seller central account for tax purposes. Whatever legal entity shown in the seller central will be taxed by Internal Revenue Service (IRS) through Amazon.

Some sellers put their name as their legal entity at the beginning of their Amazon seller enrollment. Maybe because they don’t own an LLC or starting out small. Once they receive their own LLC or if their business becomes bigger (Corporation), they need to update their legal entity for tax reassignment.

**REMEMBER:**A seller can only update the legal entity through the main account access and not through user permissions.

**How to Change a Legal Entity/Legal Business Name?**

1. Go to Settings and click Account Info.
2. Under Business Information, click Legal Entity.
3. Enter your Legal Business Name and click Update.[2]

**What if the Seller can’t Update the Legal Entity?**

The validation process can take up to eight weeks from when Amazon receives your information, either electronically or via mail if you checked the box that states "I have received my EIN/SSN within 60 Days" while taking the tax interview.

FAQs

#### 1. Why would I need to change my legal entity on Amazon Seller Central?

You might need to change your legal entity if your business has grown from an individual or sole proprietorship to an LLC, corporation, or other business structure. Updating your legal entity ensures that your tax information is correct for IRS purposes and aligns with your business’s official structure.

#### 2. How can I change my legal entity/ legal business name on Amazon Seller Central?

To update your legal entity:

1. Go to the Settings menu and select Account Info.
2. Under Business Information, click Legal Entity.
3. Enter your new Legal Business Name and click Update.

#### 3. Can I update the legal entity if I only have user permissions?

No, you must be the main account holder with full access to update the legal entity. You cannot update it through user permissions.

#### 4. What documents are needed when submitting the request to update the legal entity?

You may need to provide the following documents:

- W-9 Form
- Business Account Application
- 575 G Notice from the IRS
- Bank account in the legal entity name
- A credit card in the legal entity name
- Utility bill in the legal entity name

#### 5. How do I proceed if I still cannot update the legal entity?

Once your tax information interview is reopened, you can proceed to reapply for the new legal entity by following the steps outlined earlier for changing your legal entity. Make sure to include all required supporting documents.

#### 6. How long does it take for the changes to take effect?

The process for updating your legal entity can take up to 8 weeks from when Amazon receives your information. You will be notified once the update is complete, or you may need to follow up with Seller Support or Seller Executive Relations if issues persist.
