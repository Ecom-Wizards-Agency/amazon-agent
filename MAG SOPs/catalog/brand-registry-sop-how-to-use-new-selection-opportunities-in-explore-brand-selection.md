# Brand Registry SOP: How to Use New Selection Opportunities in Explore Brand Selection

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection

## How to Use New Selection Opportunities in Explore Brand Selection

Who is this for?

To use this feature, you need to:

- Be a Brand Representative through Brand Registry
- Be internal to the brand (not a third-party agency)
- Be actively involved in managing your brand’s listings on Amazon

Objective:

This guide walks you through how to use the New Selection Opportunities feature in Explore Brand Selection. If you’re a brand representative, this tool can help you quickly find and list products that aren’t yet live on Amazon or aren’t connected to your brand’s offer.

Step 1: Get Started

- Go to [Explore Brand Selection](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/selectiongaps/) in Seller Central
- If you haven’t already, opt in on the [enrollment page](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/selectiongaps/enrollment)

NOTE: Opting in doesn’t guarantee you’ll get recommendations—it depends on availability. Also, this opt-in only applies to *New Selection Opportunities*, not *Review Listing Updates*, which is always on.

Step 2: Understand the Layout

There are two main tabs:

1. New Selection Opportunities – where you’ll find listing suggestions (focus of this SOP)
2. Review Listing Updates – where you can see changes made to your current catalog

Step 3: Check for Recommendations

- When new recommendations are ready, you’ll see a homepage card notification.
- Click into the New Selection Opportunities tab and check out three sections:

A. New to Amazon

- These are products that aren’t currently on Amazon but may be available on your website or elsewhere publicly.
- Amazon builds these based on external sources (no website integration needed).
- Updates twice a year.
- It can take up to 30 days after opting in to see suggestions here.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-u9nr1ie6.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jU4image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/jU4image.png)

B. Has Other Offers

- Products already on Amazon, but not listed by your brand.
- “Offers on Amazon” shows how many sellers are already offering that product.
- Refreshes daily—so counts may change or be slightly off.
- This section still updates even if you later unenroll.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-k26odh0j.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/8n4image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/8n4image.png)

C. Clarification Needed

- These are products Amazon found that are missing just a few required fields to complete the listing.
- Most of the listing info is pre-filled—you just need to add missing attributes like price or inventory.
- This section refreshes every 30 days.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-lup02y10.png)

Step 4: Review Product Info

When browsing recommendations, keep an eye on these helpful metrics:

- Monthly Demand Signal: A score from 0–1,000 showing how much potential a product may have. (Use it for ranking—not as a sales forecast.)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/owSimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/owSimage.png)

- Offers on Amazon: Number of existing seller offers (for *Has Other Offers* only).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-denza15o.png)

Step 5: Take Action

A. To list a product:

- Click Sell this product (available on *New to Amazon* and *Has Other Offers* tabs).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-nx0wnm7h.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ANuimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/ANuimage.png)

- The product will be removed from your list after you list it.

B. To filter or sort:

- Use filters above the list to narrow by brand, product type, or monthly demand signal.
- Click on column headers or use the Sort by dropdown to organize results.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-9lsrsiln.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/W32image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/W32image.png)

C. To bulk list:

- Click Download recommendations to export them into an Excel file.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-npgttnbr.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Zm5image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Zm5image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-a56qo8da.png)

- Follow the bulk upload steps under Create listings in bulk if you’re adding many products at once.

Step 6: Provide Feedback (Optional but Helpful)

- If you decide *not* to list a product, click the dropdown next to Sell this product and select a reason.
- This helps Amazon improve future recommendations—and removes that product from your suggestions list.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/brand-registry-sop-how-to-use-new-selection-opportunities-in-explore-brand-selection-image-lps8cdnc.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/S9Qimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/S9Qimage.png)

Step 7: Manage Your Listings

- Once a product is listed, go to [Manage All Inventory](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/myinventory/inventory?fulfilledBy=all&page=1&pageSize=25&sort=date_created_desc&status=all) to track or edit your offers.
- You’re free to update any pre-filled content to better reflect your brand’s voice or correct any details.
- If you need to request edits to product detail pages, check out [Suggest changes to your product detail page](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G200335450).

Notes:

- Amazon may automatically add variations (or connect to existing ones) based on what it finds on your brand’s site.
- Monthly demand signals are just **guidance**. Use your own judgment when choosing which products to list.
- Listing products through this tool doesn’t guarantee sales—but it does speed up the process and helps you stay ahead.

**Quick Checklist ✅**

| Task | Done? |
| --- | --- |
| Opted in to New Selection Opportunities | ☐ |
| Checked for product recommendations | ☐ |
| Evaluated key product info (demand, reviews, etc.) | ☐ |
| Listed products or gave feedback | ☐ |
| Downloaded and listed in bulk (if needed) | ☐ |
| Managed new listings in inventory | ☐ |
| Submitted feedback on the experience (optional) | ☐ |
