## Unlink CA/MX Inventory From US Inventory

**Who is this for :** This Catalog SOP is for Amazon sellers who are currently selling in the US, CA, and/or MX marketplaces and want to separate their CA/MX Inventory From US Inventory.

**Objective :** The objective is to delink the inventory of the CA/MX marketplace from the US marketplace on Amazon by following the step-by-step instructions outlined in this SOP.

**Instructions**

1. Go to seller central > Inventory > Sell Globally

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680816234465.png)

2. Click the “Build International Listings” button at the top of the page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680816311425.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/kapimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/kapimage.png)

3. Select the target marketplace to remove and click Remove Connection:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1680816405404.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VObimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/VObimage.png)

4. Tick the correct circle if you would like to remove existing listings in the target marketplace:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop_-de-link-ca_mx-inventory-from-us-inventory-image-ma1j6hxy.png)

5. Then click on the “Remove Connection”

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop_-de-link-ca_mx-inventory-from-us-inventory-image-hchlz0hv.png)

6. Check the target marketplace to see if the connection is removed.

FAQs

**1. What happens when I unlink CA/MX inventory from the US inventory?**

- Unlinking inventory means that your US inventory will no longer be automatically shared with CA/MX. However, existing listings in CA/MX may still remain active unless you choose to remove them.

**2. Does this process delete my active listings in CA/MX?**

- No, removing the inventory link does not automatically delete listings in CA/MX. If you want to remove listings, select the option to remove existing listings when unlinking. Otherwise, they will remain active but without a shared inventory.

**3. What happens to my FBA inventory already stored in CA/MX?**

- Any inventory already shipped to Canadian or Mexican fulfillment centers will remain there. If you no longer want to sell in those marketplaces, you may need to create a removal order to have inventory returned or disposed of.

**4. How long does it take to unlink inventory from CA/MX?**

- Unlinking typically takes a few minutes to an hour, but in some cases, changes may take up to 24 hours to fully reflect in Seller Central.

**5. What if the "Remove Connection" button is missing or not working?**

- If you can’t find the option to remove the connection:

  - Check if you are logged into the correct marketplace account.
  - Ensure you have admin permissions for account settings.
  - Clear cache and try another browser.
  - If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

**6. Can I relink my CA/MX inventory to the US later?**

- Yes. You can re-enable Build International Listings in Seller Central and link the inventory again. However, you may need to manually sync pricing, fulfillment, and shipping settings.

**7. Does unlinking inventory affect my pricing or fulfillment settings?**

- Yes. Since pricing and fulfillment settings can be different for each marketplace, unlinking means:

  - Currency conversions won’t apply automatically.
  - Fulfillment preferences (FBA or FBM) must be set separately for CA/MX.
  - You may need to manually update pricing in CA/MX since Amazon won’t sync it with your US pricing anymore.
