# Catalog SOP: Editing your Vine Enrolled Products on Seller Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-editing-your-vine-enrolled-products-on-seller-central

## **Editing your Vine Enrolled Products on Seller Central**

**Who is this for :** This is for Amazon sellers who have enrolled their products in the Vine Program and need to make changes to their enrolled products such as updating the available units, editing product information, and making other necessary changes.

**Objective :** This aims to guide Amazon sellers on how to edit their products enrolled in the Vine Program on Seller Central.

**If you need to make changes to the products you enrolled in the Vine Program on Seller Central, follow these simple steps:**

1. Go to Seller Central and select “Advertising” from the top menu and choose “Vine” from the drop-down menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%2011.22.16%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/o2vimage.png)

2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click “Start Enrolling Products into Vine today!”

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-31%20at%2011.22.26%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Uwyimage.png)

3. Find the product you want to edit and click on its ASIN link in the appropriate column.

4. On the following page, you can view important details about the product such as the enrollment date, the number of available units for Vine Voices to request, and the number of units enrolled.

5. If you need to change any aspects of your enrollment, make your edits and then click the “Save Changes” button.

By following these steps, you can easily edit the products you enrolled in the Vine Program on Seller Central.

FAQs

**1. Can I change the number of available units after enrolling my product in the Vine Program?**

- Yes, you can edit the number of available units for Vine Voices to request. You can either increase or decrease the available units, depending on your inventory and product availability. Just be sure to save your changes once you’ve updated the quantity.

**2. What specific product information can I edit after enrolling in Vine?**

- You can edit certain product details like the number of available units, but other aspects of product information such as pricing, titles, descriptions, or images may not be editable through the Vine enrollment process. Check with Amazon Seller Central for any limitations on editing these details once a product is enrolled.

**3. Will I need to re-enroll my product in Vine if I make changes to it?**

- No, you do not need to re-enroll your product in the Vine Program after making changes. Any edits to the enrolled product will automatically be reflected in the program once saved.

**4. How do I know if my changes were saved successfully?**

- Once you click the “Save Changes” button, the system should notify you with a confirmation message indicating that the changes were successfully saved. If you do not see this message, try refreshing the page to ensure your updates have been applied.

**5. Is there a limit to the number of units I can enroll in the Vine Program at one time?**

- While there is no specific limit mentioned, it’s important to ensure that the number of available units you edit aligns with your inventory and that you have enough stock to fulfill Vine Voice requests. Review your inventory levels and ensure they are accurate before making changes.

**6. Can I make changes to my Vine-enrolled product if it’s already under review or has received reviews?**

- Yes, you can still make changes to your Vine-enrolled product, but you should be mindful that certain changes could affect how the product is viewed by Vine Voices, especially if they are already reviewing it. Make sure to review Amazon’s guidelines to avoid disrupting the review process.

**7. What should I do if I accidentally clicked "Start Enrolling Products into Vine today!" while editing my product?**

- If you clicked "Start Enrolling Products into Vine today!" by mistake, you can simply navigate back to your Vine dashboard or select a different action within Seller Central. This button is for enrolling new products and should not interfere with the process of editing already-enrolled products.
