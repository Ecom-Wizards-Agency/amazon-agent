# Catalog SOP: Download a Manage FBA Inventory Archive Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-manage-fba-inventory-archive-report

## **Download a Manage FBA Inventory Archive Report**

**Who is this for :** For individuals who are responsible for managing inventory within an e-commerce company that utilizes Amazon's FBA service. It is relevant to warehouse managers, inventory coordinators, or anyone involved in tracking and maintaining stock levels using the FBA platform. The SOP assumes a basic understanding of Amazon's FBA system and its associated terminologies.

**Objective :** To provide step-by-step instructions on how to download and effectively manage the Archive Report for FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) inventory. This SOP aims to assist employees or individuals involved in inventory management within an e-commerce company that utilizes Amazon's FBA service.

**To download a Manage FBA Inventory Archive Report:**

1. Go to the "Reports" Tab on the Top Menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the Drop-Down Menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-IN6HUUSJ.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sIAimage.png)

2. Under the "Inventory" Header on the left side of the page, click on the "Manage FBA Inventory Report - Archived" link.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/download-a-manage-fba-inventory-archive-report-image2.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/IM7image.png)

3. On the next page, choose to download the report in either an Excel or text format by clicking the "Request .csv Download" or "Request .txt Download" buttons, respectively.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/download-a-manage-fba-inventory-archive-report-image3.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/UHmimage.png)

4. In the table below, the "Report Status" Column will display the status as "In Progress".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/download-a-manage-fba-inventory-archive-report-image4.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/I36image.png)

5. Once the report is ready for download, click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" Column to download the report.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/download-a-manage-fba-inventory-archive-report-image5.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Nbfimage.png)

FAQs

1. Who can download the Manage FBA Inventory Archive Report?

Only users with the appropriate permissions in Amazon Seller Central, such as inventory managers or account administrators, can generate and download this report. If you don’t see the report option, verify your account permissions.

2. How often is the Manage FBA Inventory Archive Report updated?

This report does not contain real-time data. It includes historical inventory records, and the update frequency depends on Amazon’s internal processing schedule, which typically ranges from weekly to monthly.

3. How long is the archive report available for download?

Once requested, the report remains available for download for 7 days. After that, a new request must be made to generate a fresh copy.

4. What is the difference between the .csv and .txt file formats?

- .csv (Comma-Separated Values): Best for use in spreadsheet applications like Excel or Google Sheets, allowing for easier data sorting and filtering.
- .txt (Text File): A plain text format, useful for custom scripts, database imports, or manual processing.

5. What should I do if the report does not generate or download?

- Refresh the page and check if the report status updates.
- Ensure pop-up blockers or browser extensions are not preventing the download.
- Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge) or clearing your cache and cookies.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.
