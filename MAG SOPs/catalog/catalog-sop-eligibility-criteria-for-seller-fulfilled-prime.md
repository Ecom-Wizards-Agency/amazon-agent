# Catalog SOP: Eligibility Criteria for Seller Fulfilled Prime

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-eligibility-criteria-for-seller-fulfilled-prime

## **Eligibility Criteria for Seller Fulfilled Prime**

**Who is this for :** Amazon sellers who are interested in offering Seller Fulfilled Prime delivery options to their customers on Amazon should review the eligibility criteria for this program. The seller or their authorized representative who has access to their Amazon seller account should carefully review the criteria and ensure that they meet all the requirements before applying for the program.

**Objective :** To ensure that sellers who offer Prime delivery options meet Amazon's strict performance and delivery standards, which help to maintain a high level of customer satisfaction and trust in the Amazon marketplace.

**To be eligible for Seller Fulfilled Prime, sellers must meet the following requirements:**

1. Maintain an active and in-good-standing account with a Professional selling plan.

2. Use at least one of the approved Buy Shipping Service carriers and schedule carrier pickup time to be at or after 4:00 p.m. local time for Standard or Two-Day Shipping orders (specific requirements may vary by the marketplace).

3. Meet or exceed the following performance standards:

- On-Time Shipment rate of at least 99% on Prime orders
- Use of Buy Shipping Services for at least 99% of Prime orders
- Cancellation rate of no more than 0.5% on Prime orders
- The On-Time Delivery rate of at least 97% on Prime orders where shipping was purchased outside of Buy Shipping Services

4. Be able to offer Free Two-Day Shipping to nearly all addresses in the continental U.S., Free Standard Shipping to addresses in Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. protectorates as well as P.O. Boxes in the continental U.S.

5. Not be able to offer to ship to Ineligible APO/FPO addresses and International destinations.

6. Offer free shipping to Prime members who select slower delivery options, and Free Same-Day and Free One-Day Delivery to Prime members for Same-Day and One-Day Delivery options.

7. Offer all buyers free Standard shipping, while non-Prime members can pay for faster shipping options.

8. Purchase all shipping labels for Prime item orders through Buy Shipping Services.

9. Agree to Amazon charging any returns, refunds, or adjustments related to Prime items to the seller's account.

10. Have a daily order limit for Amazon Prime orders that the seller can reliably ship every day.

11. Remain responsible for pre-order buyer inquiries and product-related inquiries.

**It's important to note that shipping options and geographic location requirements may vary by the marketplace.**

FAQs

**1. How do I apply for Seller Fulfilled Prime once I meet the eligibility criteria?**

**Answer:**Once you meet the eligibility criteria for Seller Fulfilled Prime, you can apply for the program by following these steps:

- Log into your Amazon Seller Central account.
- Navigate to the "Seller Fulfilled Prime" section (found under the "Programs" or "Account Settings" menu).
- Click on "Apply for Seller Fulfilled Prime."
- Fill out the application form, ensuring all required information is accurate.
- Submit the application for review.

After applying, Amazon will evaluate your account and performance. If approved, you will be granted access to the program and can begin offering Seller Fulfilled Prime shipping to eligible customers.

**2. What happens if I don’t meet the performance standards for Seller Fulfilled Prime?**

**Answer:**If you fail to meet any of the performance standards required for Seller Fulfilled Prime, such as not meeting the On-Time Shipment or On-Time Delivery rates, Amazon may take the following actions:

Suspension of Seller Fulfilled Prime benefits: If your performance consistently falls below the required standards, Amazon may temporarily suspend your Seller Fulfilled Prime status.

Warning or Account Review: If your performance is borderline, you may receive warnings or be asked to improve specific areas before Amazon allows you to continue offering Seller Fulfilled Prime.

Revocation of Seller Fulfilled Prime access: Continued non-compliance can result in permanent removal from the program.

You will be notified of any actions taken by Amazon, and you’ll typically be given a chance to improve before any permanent measures are applied.

**3. How do I manage the daily order limit for Seller Fulfilled Prime?**

**Answer:**The daily order limit is based on your ability to fulfill Prime orders consistently. There isn't a fixed order number, but rather a performance-based threshold that varies by seller. To manage your limit:

- Monitor your order volume and ensure you can consistently meet the shipping deadlines for Prime orders.
- Check your limit in Seller Central by going to the Seller Fulfilled Prime section in your account settings. If you're consistently shipping orders on time, Amazon may increase your daily order limit.
- If you find your order volume fluctuates, it’s important to adjust your operations (e.g., hiring more staff or adjusting your shipping hours) to ensure that you remain within your limit and meet performance standards.
- If your daily order limit is too low, or if you cannot fulfill the required number of orders, you may not be eligible for Seller Fulfilled Prime.

**4. Are there any international shipping restrictions for Seller Fulfilled Prime?**

**Answer:**Yes, Seller Fulfilled Prime has geographic restrictions. Sellers can only offer Seller Fulfilled Prime within the continental U.S. (for Free Two-Day and Standard Shipping), and to certain U.S. territories (Alaska, Hawaii, U.S. protectorates, and P.O. Boxes in the U.S.).

Additionally, you cannot offer Seller Fulfilled Prime to APO/FPO addresses or international destinations. The program is not available for shipping outside of the U.S., and shipping to international locations is excluded from the criteria.

For more specific shipping policies and country exclusions, check the details within your Seller Central account, as these requirements may vary by marketplace.

**5. How are returns, refunds, or adjustments for Prime items handled?**

**Answer:**When you sell through Seller Fulfilled Prime, Amazon charges returns, refunds, or adjustments related to Prime items directly to your seller account. These charges may include:

- Return shipping costs for items returned by customers.
- Refunds for Prime orders if the customer returns the product.
- Adjustments in case of fulfillment errors, such as delays or issues with shipping.
- Amazon will charge these costs based on their policies, and they are deducted directly from your seller account balance. Be sure to review the Prime Return Policy and Shipping Cost Structure in your Seller Central account for more specific details.

**6. Can I change my carrier’s pickup time for Seller Fulfilled Prime?**

**Answer:**The carrier pickup time for Seller Fulfilled Prime orders must be scheduled at or after 4:00 p.m. local time for Standard or Two-Day Shipping orders. This is a general guideline set by Amazon to ensure timely processing and shipment of Prime orders.

However, pickup times may vary based on location and the carrier you use. If you are in a location where the 4:00 p.m. cutoff is not feasible, you may need to contact your carrier to discuss available options. In some cases, exceptions may be allowed, but you should ensure that your pickup time is in line with Amazon’s requirements to maintain eligibility for Seller Fulfilled Prime.
