# Catalog SOP: Download a Reserved Inventory Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-reserved-inventory-report

## **Download a Reserved Inventory Report**

**Who is this for :** This is for all individuals and sellers who want to know how to download a Reserved Inventory Report.

**Objective :** This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) will help you understand how you can download a Reserved Inventory Report. The Reserved Inventory report shows a breakdown of units in your inventory that are in reserved status. The report can also help you find more information about the reserved units.

> **Inventory Overview Column Update**
>
> **What's changing:**
>
> •The "Available" category has been replaced by "On-hand", which includes both Available and FC transfer units. These units are considered customer-purchasable.
>
> •As a result, FC transfer units have been removed from the "Reserved" category. Reserved now reflects only FC processing and Customer order units.
>
> These changes apply to all FBA sellers in the US store. No action is required on your part.
>
> **Please note:**
>
> •These updates will be fully reflected in FBA reports by mid-2026.
>
> •Until then, inventory reports may differ from the new Inventory Overview column, as they will continue to classify FC transfer units as Reserved.
>
> We understand this temporary discrepancy may cause some confusion and appreciate your patience as the transition is completed across all systems.
>

**To create a Reserved Inventory Report on Seller Central, follow these steps:**

1. Go to the "Reports" tab on the top menu and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.50.59%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9kqimage.png)
2. Click the "Reserved Inventory" link under the "Inventory" header on the left-hand side of the page.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.51.07%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/EYuimage.png)
3. On the next page, you can download the report in Excel format by clicking the "Request .csv Download" button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.51.15%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/5FZimage.png)
4. If you prefer a text format, click the "Request .txt Download" button.
5. The "Report Status" column in the table below will show "In Progress" until the report is ready.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%203.51.23%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ME5image.png)
6. Once the report is ready, the status will change to "Download".
7. To download the report, click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column. If you don't have any Reserved Inventory, you will see "No Data Available" under the "Report Status" column.

FAQs

1. What is the Reserved Inventory Report, and why is it useful?

The Reserved Inventory Report shows a breakdown of units in your inventory that are in a reserved status. This includes items that are allocated for a specific order or are held for other reasons. This report helps you track and manage inventory that is not available for sale, allowing for better planning and decision-making.

2. How do I access the Reserved Inventory Report in Seller Central?

To download the Reserved Inventory Report:

1. Go to the Reports tab in the top menu of Seller Central.
2. Select Fulfillment from the drop-down menu.
3. Under the Inventory header on the left-hand side of the page, click the “Reserved Inventory” link.

3. What options are available to download the Reserved Inventory Report?

You can download the Reserved Inventory Report in either of the following formats:

- Excel: Click the “Request .csv Download” button.
- Text: Click the “Request .txt Download” button.

4. How can I track the status of my Reserved Inventory Report?

The Report Status column will initially show “In Progress” while the report is being generated. Once ready, the status will change to “Download”. You can then click the “Download” button in the Report Status column to access the report.

5. What happens if I have no Reserved Inventory?

If you have no Reserved Inventory, you will see “No Data Available” under the Report Status column. This indicates that there is no reserved stock in your inventory to report.

6. How long does it take for the Reserved Inventory Report to be generated?

The report may take a few minutes to generate, depending on the size of your inventory. Once the Report Status changes to “Download”, you can download it immediately.

7. Can I view the Reserved Inventory Report for a specific time period?

The Reserved Inventory Report reflects real-time data on your reserved stock, but you can download it at any time to analyze inventory status. If you need data for a specific period, you may need to check reports periodically.

8. Is there any other report related to reserved inventory?

While the Reserved Inventory Report provides detailed insights into reserved stock, you can also explore other inventory management reports available in Seller Central, such as the Manage Inventory Report and FBA Inventory Report, for additional inventory data.

9. What should I do if I encounter an error or issue while downloading the report?

If you experience issues with downloading the report, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, you can contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.
