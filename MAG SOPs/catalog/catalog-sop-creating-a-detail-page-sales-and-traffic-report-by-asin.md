## Creating a Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report by ASIN

**Who is this for :** This is intended for individuals or teams who are registered sellers on Seller Central, the e-commerce platform provided by Amazon. It is designed for those who need to access and download business reports specifically related to Detail Page sales and traffic.

**Objective :** To guide users through the process of generating a detailed business report on Seller Central. By following the provided instructions, users will be able to select the desired columns and information to include in their report, set a specific date range, and successfully download the report in CSV format.

Generating comprehensive business reports is essential for sellers on Amazon as it enables them to gain valuable insights into their Detail Page sales and traffic. These reports can help sellers understand the performance of their products, identify trends, and make informed decisions to improve their business strategies.

By following the instructions below, you will be able to customize your report by selecting specific columns and information, as well as setting a date range according to your requirements. The report will be downloaded in CSV format, which can be easily opened and analyzed using spreadsheet software.

**Instructions:**

1. To generate a report on your Detail Page sales and traffic, go to the "Reports" tab on Seller Central's top menu and select "Business Reports" from the drop-down menu.

FAQs

1. What is the Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report by ASIN used for?

This report provides insights into the sales and traffic performance of individual ASINs (Amazon Standard Identification Numbers). It helps sellers track product visibility, conversion rates, and overall sales performance over a selected time period.

2. Who can access the Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report by ASIN?

Only users with the appropriate permissions in Seller Central can access and download this report. If you don’t see the option, check with your account administrator to ensure you have the correct permissions.

3. Can I download the report in different formats?

No, the report is only available in CSV format, which can be opened in spreadsheet applications like Excel or Google Sheets for further analysis.

4. Why don’t I see the "Detail Page Sales and Traffic" option under Business Reports?

This could be due to insufficient user permissions, report availability restrictions for your marketplace, or temporary system issues. Refresh the page, check your permissions, or try accessing the report later.

5. How often is the Detail Page Sales and Traffic Report updated?

The data in the report is updated daily, but some metrics may take longer to reflect recent changes. If the latest data is missing, try adjusting the date range or checking back later.

6. Can I schedule automatic downloads for this report?

No, Seller Central does not currently offer an automated scheduling feature for this report. You must manually download it each time you need an update.

7. How do I select the most important columns for my report?

Key columns to consider include Sessions, Page Views, Buy Box Percentage, Conversion Rate, Units Ordered, and Ordered Product Sales. The best columns to select depend on the insights you are looking for.

8. Why is my downloaded report missing some data?

Ensure that you have selected the correct date range and all necessary columns before downloading. Additionally, historical data may not be available for all time periods.

9. Can I generate reports for multiple ASINs at once?

Yes, the report includes data for all ASINs in your account by default. You can filter the data after downloading it to focus on specific ASINs.

10. How can I analyze the report effectively?

Use Excel or Google Sheets to filter, sort, and create pivot tables for deeper insights. Look for trends in page views, conversion rates, and sales to optimize product listings and marketing strategies.
