# Catalog SOP: Manual Update of Listing Attributes in Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-manual-update-of-listing-attributes-in-vendor-central

## **Manual Update of Listing Attributes in Vendor Central**

**Who is this for :** Vendors who need to update their product listings on Vendor Central.

**Objective:** To ensure that the product information on Amazon Vendor Central is accurate, complete, and up-to-date.

**Instructions:**

1. Access the account by going to [https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1704358137505.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/h29image.png)

2. Once logged in and you are not redirected to the correct account, click the account name at the left top then click See All to see the complete list of accounts.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-09-21%20at%208.34.45%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fucimage.png)

3. Choose the account that you need to work on.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-09-21%20at%208.35.48%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/QChimage.png)

4. After choosing the correct account go to Items > Catalog

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-09-21%20at%208.36.49%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/z2Jimage.png)

5. Search for the ASIN that you need to update (you can search ASINs in bulk) tick the upper box to select all the listings including the dupe SKUs, then click the “Edit product details”.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/IAVimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1704358731649.png)

6. You will be redirected to the Edit Page. The sample you are trying to update is the Product Name. Go to Product Identity, enter the proposed title in the Item Name box, and hit “Submit and continue”

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1704358766092.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4Fcimage.png)

7. If you see attributes that have been highlighted in red like the image below, it means that those are required to fill out to be able to save the listing changes.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1704358810541.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zp2image.png)

8. If all required attributes are filled out and changes were successfully saved, monitor the changes in the backend and detail page after 15 minutes to check if the updates were successfully pushed through. Sometimes it takes 24 hours for the system to absorb the updates.

FAQs

**1. What is the purpose of manually updating listing attributes in Vendor Central?**

- Manual updates allow vendors to make quick changes to product information, ensuring listings remain accurate, complete, and compliant with Amazon's guidelines.

**2. How can I confirm that I am working in the correct account on Vendor Central?**

- After logging in:

  - Check the account name at the top left corner of the screen.
  - Click See All to view the list of accounts.
  - Select the correct account to proceed with updates.

**3. What should I do if I cannot find the ASIN I want to update?**

- If the ASIN is not appearing in your catalog:

  - Double-check the ASIN for accuracy.
  - Ensure the ASIN is associated with the selected account.
  - If the issue persists, contact the Vendor Central support team for assistance.

**4. Can I update multiple ASINs at the same time?**

- Yes, you can search for ASINs in bulk and select multiple listings. After selecting, click Edit product details to make changes to all selected ASINs simultaneously.

**5. What do the red-highlighted attributes mean on the edit page?**

- Red-highlighted attributes indicate mandatory fields that must be completed before you can save changes. These are essential for ensuring the listing complies with Amazon's requirements.

**6. What should I do if the required attribute information is unavailable?**

- If required information is missing:

  - Verify if the data is available in Seller Central (if applicable).
  - If not, escalate the issue to your point of contact (POC) or the relevant team for further guidance.

**7. Can I update optional attributes while manually editing a listing?**

- Yes, optional attributes can be updated alongside required ones. However, ensure that any changes align with Amazon's guidelines and improve the listing quality.

**8. How long does it take for listing updates to reflect on the product detail page?**

- Changes typically reflect in the backend or product detail page within 15 minutes. However, it can take up to 24 hours for updates to propagate across the system.

**9. What happens if I submit incomplete information when updating a listing?**

- If required fields are left blank, you will not be able to save the changes. Ensure all mandatory attributes (highlighted in red) are completed before submission.

**10. How do I verify if my changes have been successfully saved?**

- After submitting updates:

  - Monitor the backend for changes under Items > Catalog.
  - Check the product detail page to confirm if the updates are live.
  - If changes are not visible after 24 hours, contact Vendor Central support.

**11. Can I revert changes made to a listing manually?**

- No, manual updates cannot be reverted automatically. To restore previous values, you will need to re-enter the original information manually and submit the changes again.

**12. What should I include in the “Item Name” when updating the product title?**

- The Item Name should follow Amazon’s title guidelines and include:

  - Brand name
  - Product type
  - Key attributes (e.g., size, color, material).

**13. Can I update suppressed or inactive ASINs manually?**

- Yes, suppressed or inactive ASINs can be updated manually. However, ensure all required fields are completed to reactivate the listing.

**14. What should I do if updates fail to reflect after 24 hours?**

- If updates are not visible after 24 hours:

  - Verify that all required attributes were completed during the update.
  - Check for backend errors or issues.
  - Contact Vendor Central support with details of the changes you attempted to make.

**15. Are there any restrictions on the types of attributes I can update manually?**

- Some attributes, such as category or product type, may be restricted for manual updates. If these need changes, you may need to submit a case to Vendor Central support for assistance.

**16. Can I add new attributes to a listing manually?**

- No, manual updates are for editing existing attributes. Adding new attributes may require uploading a flat file or escalating to Vendor Central support.

**17. Is it possible to make changes without affecting live listings?**

- No, changes made manually are reflected directly on the live listing. If you’re unsure about an update, consult the POC or relevant team before proceeding.

**18. How do I handle duplicate SKUs when updating a listing?**

- When selecting listings for manual updates, ensure all duplicate SKUs associated with the ASIN are included. This ensures consistent updates across all versions of the product.

**19. What are common errors to avoid when manually updating a listing?**

- Common errors include:

  - Leaving mandatory fields blank.
  - Entering incorrect or unsupported values (e.g., invalid characters or formats).
  - Failing to address red-highlighted attributes.

**20. How often should listing attributes be reviewed for updates?**

- Listing attributes should be reviewed periodically or whenever there are changes to:

  - Product details (e.g., new features, updated descriptions).
  - Amazon policies or guidelines.
  - Market trends requiring optimization of titles, bullet points, or images.
