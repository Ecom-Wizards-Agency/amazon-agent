# Catalog SOP: How to Generate a Fee Preview Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-generate-a-fee-preview-report

## **How to Generate a Fee Preview Report**

**Who is this for :** This is intended for individuals or teams involved in financial planning, cost analysis, or budgeting for selling activities on Seller Central. It is suitable for users who have access to Seller Central and need to generate a Fee Preview Report to estimate the fees and charges associated with their product listings. This SOP will assist users in navigating to the appropriate section, selecting the desired report format, and downloading the report for further analysis and decision-making processes.

**Objective :** To provide instructions for generating a Fee Preview Report. This SOP aims to guide users in accessing the necessary information related to fees and charges on Seller Central and generating a preview report to evaluate and analyze potential costs associated with their selling activities.

**Instructions:**

1. To generate a Fee Preview Report, navigate to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684261549701.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RVTimage.png)
2. Under the "Payments" header on the left-hand side of the page, click on "Fee Preview".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684261773882.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zyhimage.png)
3. On the next page, select the desired report format by clicking the "Request .csv Download" or "Request .txt Download" button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684261859572.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yU9image.png)
4. In the table below, the "Report Status" column will display the status as "In Progress".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684262013432.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/t8Fimage.png)
5. Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to "Download". Click the "Download" button in the "Report Status" column to download the report.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684262054391.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9oQimage.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I find the Fee Preview Report in Seller Central?

You can access the Fee Preview Report by navigating to Reports > Fulfillment, then selecting Payments > Fee Preview on the left-hand side of the page.

2. What information does the Fee Preview Report include?

The report provides estimated fees for product listings, including referral fees, fulfillment fees, storage fees, and other charges associated with selling on Amazon.

3. Can I customize the Fee Preview Report based on specific products?

No, the Fee Preview Report is generated for all eligible listings in your Seller Central account. If you need data for specific products, you will need to filter the downloaded report manually in Excel or a text editor.

4. What file formats are available for downloading the report?

You can download the report in Excel (.csv) format by clicking "Request .csv Download" or in text (.txt) format by selecting "Request .txt Download."

5. How do I track the progress of my report request?

After requesting the download, check the "Report Status" column in the table. The status will first show "In Progress" and update to "Download" when the report is ready.

6. How long does it take for the Fee Preview Report to be generated?

Processing time varies based on the number of SKUs in your account, but most reports are available within a few minutes.

7. What should I do if my Fee Preview Report does not generate or download properly?

If the report does not generate or download successfully:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for further assistance.

8. Does the Fee Preview Report reflect real-time fees?

No, the Fee Preview Report provides an estimate based on current pricing and fulfillment rates. Actual fees may vary due to dynamic changes in Amazon’s pricing structure, promotions, or storage fees.

9. Can I schedule automatic Fee Preview Reports for regular updates?

Currently, Amazon does not offer an option to schedule automated Fee Preview Report downloads. You will need to manually generate and download the report when needed.

10. Who should I contact if I notice discrepancies in the fee estimates?

If you notice discrepancies in the fee calculations, you can reach out to Amazon Seller Support for clarification or review the FBA Fee Schedule and Referral Fee Guidelines in Seller Central.
