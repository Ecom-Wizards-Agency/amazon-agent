# Catalog SOP: How to Generate an Amazon Fulfilled Shipments – Tax Remittance Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-how-to-generate-an-amazon-fulfilled-shipments-tax-remittance-report

## **How to Generate an Amazon Fulfilled Shipments – Tax Remittance Report**

**Who is this for :** This is intended for individuals or teams responsible for managing and monitoring tax remittance for Amazon-fulfilled shipments. It is suitable for users who have access to Seller Central and need to generate tax reports specifically related to fulfilled orders. This SOP will assist users in accurately retrieving and downloading the required tax remittance information for further analysis, compliance, or financial purposes.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions for generating an Amazon Fulfilled Shipments – Tax Remittance Report. This SOP aims to guide users in accessing and downloading the necessary tax-related information related to fulfilled shipments on the Amazon platform.

**To generate a Customer Taxes Report on Seller Central, follow these steps:**

1. Click on the "Reports" tab on the top menu and select "Fulfillment" from the drop-down menu.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/kJYimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-customer-taxes-report-image-1.png)
2. Under the "Sales" header located on the left side of the page, click on "Amazon Fulfilled Shipments – Tax Remittance".![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-customer-taxes-report-image-2.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pUZimage.png)
3. On the next page, select the desired "Event Date" from the available drop-down menu or choose "Exact Dates" from the drop-down menu and enter the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icon.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-customer-taxes-report-image-3.png)

  ![creating-a-customer-taxes-report-image-3.gif](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/creating-a-customer-taxes-report-image-3.gif)
4. If you want to download the report in an Excel format, click the "Request .csv Download" button. If you prefer a text format, click the "Request .txt Download" button.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-customer-taxes-report-image-4.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/lSpimage.png)
5. In the table below, the "Report Status" column will indicate the status as "In Progress".![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-customer-taxes-report-image-5.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rOIimage.png)
6. Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to "Download". Click the "Download" button located in the "Report Status" column to download the report.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/creating-a-customer-taxes-report-image-6.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I find the Amazon Fulfilled Shipments – Tax Remittance Report in Seller Central?

You can find the report by navigating to Reports > Fulfillment, then selecting Sales > Amazon Fulfilled Shipments – Tax Remittance on the left-hand side of the page.

2. What information does the Amazon Fulfilled Shipments – Tax Remittance Report include?

The report contains tax-related details for Amazon-fulfilled orders, including order IDs, taxable amounts, tax collection details, remitted tax amounts, jurisdictions, and other relevant tax data.

3. Can I select a custom date range for the report?

Yes, you can select "Exact Dates" from the drop-down menu and manually enter the "From" and "To" dates using the calendar icon.

4. What file formats are available for downloading the report?

You can download the report in Excel (.csv) format by clicking "Request .csv Download" or in text (.txt) format by selecting "Request .txt Download."

5. How do I track the progress of my report request?

After clicking "Request Download," check the "Report Status" column in the table. The status will first show "In Progress" and update to "Download" when the report is ready.

6. How long does it take for the report to be generated?

The processing time varies based on data volume, but most reports are available within a few minutes.

7. What should I do if my report does not generate or download properly?

If your report does not generate or download successfully:

- Refresh the page and check the "Report Status" again.
- Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
- If the issue persists, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

8. Can I automate or schedule tax remittance reports for regular downloads?

Currently, Amazon does not offer an option to schedule automated downloads for this report. You will need to manually generate and download the report as needed.

9. Who should I contact if I notice discrepancies in the tax amounts?

If you find discrepancies in the tax remittance data, reach out to Amazon Seller Support or consult a tax professional for further guidance.
