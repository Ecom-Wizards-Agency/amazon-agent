# Catalog SOP: Download a Sales Dashboard Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-download-a-sales-dashboard-report

## Download a Sales Dashboard Report

**Who is this for :** This is intended for sellers or individuals responsible for analyzing sales performance on Seller Central. It provides step-by-step instructions on how to generate and download Sales Dashboard Reports

**Objective :** The objective of this SOP is to guide users in accessing and utilizing the Sales Dashboard feature within Business Reports on Seller Central. By following these instructions, users will be able to retrieve up-to-date sales statistics, compare sales data over different periods, and customize their reports to gain valuable insights into their business performance.

The Sales Dashboard on Seller Central provides sellers with a comprehensive overview of their sales performance, allowing them to monitor key metrics, analyze trends, and make data-driven decisions. This feature includes various tools and interactive elements that enable users to filter and compare sales data based on different parameters

**To generate a Sales Dashboard Report, follow these steps:**

1. Go to Seller Central and click on the "Reports" tab in the top menu and select "Business Reports" from the drop-down menu.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eu3image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/eu3image.png)
2. On the next page, click on the "Sales Dashboard" link located in the left-side menu.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pWnimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/pWnimage.png)
3. Choose the date or dates, sales breakdown, and fulfillment channel you want to include in your report using the available drop-down menus above the displayed graphs. Click the "Apply" button after making your selections.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Ax4image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Ax4image.png)
4. Customize your report by selecting or deselecting the comparative views using the options below the graphs.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/438image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/438image.png)
5. To switch between "Graph View" and "Table View", click the appropriate button located in the top left corner of the graphs or table.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/knZimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/knZimage.png)
6. Once you have created the report data you want to see, click the "Download" button in the top right corner of the page to download your report.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yoGimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/yoGimage.png)

FAQs

1. Where can I access the Sales Dashboard Report in Seller Central?

To access the Sales Dashboard Report, go to the Reports tab in the top menu of Seller Central, then select Business Reports from the drop-down menu. On the next page, click the "Sales Dashboard" link in the left-side menu.

2. How do I select the date range for the Sales Dashboard Report?

Above the displayed graphs, you'll find a drop-down menu where you can choose the date or dates for the report. After selecting the desired date range, click the "Apply" button to apply your choice.

3. What sales breakdown options can I include in the report?

In the Sales Dashboard Report, you can choose the sales breakdown you want to include, such as sales by SKU, order status, or category. Select the breakdown from the available options in the drop-down menu above the graphs, and click "Apply" to view your selection.

4. Can I choose the fulfillment channel for the report?

Yes, the Sales Dashboard Report allows you to filter data by fulfillment channel (e.g., Amazon Fulfillment, Merchant Fulfilled, etc.). You can select the fulfillment channel from the available drop-down menu and click "Apply" to include it in the report.

5. How do I compare sales data across different periods?

You can customize your report to compare sales data by selecting or deselecting the comparative views options below the graphs. These allow you to view sales performance comparisons across various time frames (e.g., daily, weekly, monthly).

6. How do I toggle between "Graph View" and "Table View"?

To switch between Graph View and Table View, click the corresponding button located in the top left corner of the graphs or table. This allows you to view the data in a format that suits your analysis.

7. How do I download the Sales Dashboard Report?

After customizing your report data, click the "Download" button in the top right corner of the page. This will allow you to download the report, usually in .csv or Excel format for further analysis.

8. Can I download the report in different formats?

The Sales Dashboard Report is typically available for download in .csv or Excel format, depending on the options available. Check the download settings to confirm the format.

9. What if I need to include or exclude specific data from the report?

You can filter and customize your report by selecting or deselecting the comparative views, sales breakdowns, or fulfillment channels using the drop-down menus and options provided. Click "Apply" after each change to adjust the report according to your needs.

10. Is it possible to schedule automatic generation of the Sales Dashboard Report?

Currently, the SOP does not mention scheduling. If you wish to schedule regular reports, explore the Automated Reports feature in Seller Central, or use other tools available in Business Reports to set up recurring data delivery.

11. Can I view historical data in the Sales Dashboard?

Yes, the Sales Dashboard Report allows you to analyze sales data from historical periods as long as the date range you select is within the data retention period in Seller Central. Ensure you select the correct dates to access historical data.

12. How do I ensure the report is up-to-date when I download it?

Before downloading, make sure you have applied the correct date range, sales breakdown, and fulfillment channel filters, and refresh the page to ensure the data is up-to-date. You can verify that the data looks correct on the screen before clicking the "Download" button.

13. Can I generate reports for specific products or categories in the Sales Dashboard?

Yes, you can filter the Sales Dashboard Report based on specific product categories or individual products by adjusting the sales breakdown filters. Select the category or product from the drop-down menu and click "Apply".

14. What should I do if the data in the Sales Dashboard Report seems incorrect?

If the data appears incorrect, double-check your filters (e.g., date range, sales breakdown, fulfillment channel) to ensure they are correctly set. If the issue persists, consider reaching out to Seller Support for assistance.
