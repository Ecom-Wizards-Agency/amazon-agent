# Catalog SOP: Cancelling a Lightning Deal in Seller Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-cancelling-a-lightning-deal-in-seller-central

## Canceling a Lightning Deal

**Who is this for:** This is intended for sellers on the Seller Central platform who are responsible for managing Lightning Deals.

**Objective:** To guide sellers through the process of canceling a Lightning Deal on Seller Central. To ensure that sellers can efficiently and accurately update their Lightning Deals to meet their business needs and promotional strategies.

Before any cancellation of a running Lightning Deals, it is important to inform, brief details and get client’s approval. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.

1. To cancel a Lightning Deal, go to the “Advertising” tab on the top menu of Seller Central and select “Deals” from the Drop-Down Menu.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/k7pimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/k7pimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-cancelling-a-lightning-deal-in-seller-central-image-1.jpg)
2. On the following page, enter the ASIN or SKU associated with your Deal in the search bar. Select the status of your Deal from the Drop-Down Menu under “Status”, and choose your Lightning Deal from the “Deal Type” Drop-Down Menu, along with the desired date option from the “Date” Drop-Down Menu.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-cancelling-a-lightning-deal-in-seller-central-image-2.jpg)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/41rimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/41rimage.png)
3. Once you have located your Lightning Deal and it is in the Status Mode of “Upcoming”, “Needs Attention” or “Draft”, you can cancel it by selecting “Cancel Deal” from the Drop-Down Menu of the “Edit” button located to the right of the Deal.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/M7mimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/M7mimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-cancelling-a-lightning-deal-in-seller-central-image-3.png)
4. Alternatively, you can cancel the Deal by clicking the “Edit” button located to the right of the Deal, which will open up the Deal page, and then clicking the “Cancel Deal” link located on the top right side of the page.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-cancelling-a-lightning-deal-in-seller-central-image-4.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/catalog-sop-cancelling-a-lightning-deal-in-seller-central-image-5.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hZzimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/hZzimage.png)
5. A Pop-Up Window will appear, giving you the option to either cancel or keep the Deal. To cancel the Deal, click the “Yes, cancel this deal now” button. To keep the Deal, click the “Don’t Cancel” button.

FAQs

#### 1. Can a running Lightning Deal be canceled?

No, once a Lightning Deal has started, it cannot be canceled. Only Upcoming, Needs Attention, and Draft deals can be canceled before they go live.

#### 2. Are there any fees or penalties for canceling a Lightning Deal?

Yes, canceling a Lightning Deal may result in penalties, such as reduced eligibility for future deals or fees depending on the deal type. Check Amazon’s policies before canceling.

#### 3. Will canceling a Lightning Deal affect my ability to run future deals?

Frequent cancellations may impact your eligibility for future Lightning Deals, as Amazon prioritizes sellers with a strong track record of running deals successfully.

#### 4. How far in advance should I cancel a deal before it starts?

It is recommended to cancel at least 24 hours before the deal start time to ensure Amazon processes the cancellation in time.

#### 5. What should I do if the "Cancel Deal" button is missing?

- Ensure the deal is in Upcoming, Needs Attention, or Draft status, as running deals cannot be canceled.
- Check if you have the necessary account permissions to cancel deals.
- If the button is still missing, contact Amazon Seller Support for assistance.

#### 6. Will I receive a confirmation after canceling a Lightning Deal?

Yes, Amazon typically sends a confirmation email once a deal is successfully canceled. You can also check the Deals Dashboard to confirm the status change.

#### 7. Can I cancel a Lightning Deal through Seller Support?

Yes, if you are unable to cancel a deal through Seller Central, you can open a case with Amazon Seller Support and request cancellation. However, this process may take longer.

#### 8. Can a canceled Lightning Deal be reactivated?

No, once a Lightning Deal is canceled, it cannot be reactivated. You will need to create a new deal and submit it for approval.
