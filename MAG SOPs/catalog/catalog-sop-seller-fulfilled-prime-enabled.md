## Seller Fulfilled Prime Enabled

**Who is this for :** This is for Amazon sellers who have been granted eligibility for the Seller Fulfilled Prime program and want to maintain that eligibility or reactivate it after a suspension. It is also for those who want to understand how the program works and what is required of them to maintain eligibility.

**Objective :** To provide guidelines on maintaining eligibility for the Seller Fulfilled Prime program, and how to reactivate or re-enable it if your account gets suspended.

**To maintain enrollment in Seller Fulfilled Prime:**

You must continuously satisfy the [Seller Fulfilled Prime program policy](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G201812300). Performance requirements are monitored and enforced weekly against the minimum performance thresholds. For more information, go to [Seller Fulfilled Prime performance requirements](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/G202072550).

Seller Fulfilled Prime allows you to list your products as Prime-eligible and handle the fulfillment yourself. You can display the Prime badge on the products you already ship directly to customers, offering two-day, one-day, and free standard shipping. There is no fee to enroll in Seller Fulfilled Prime.

1. After 24 hours of being eligible for the Seller Fulfilled Prime program, any SKUs associated with your Prime templates will automatically display the Prime badge for customers. To maintain eligibility for Seller Fulfilled Prime, you must meet the **exact performance requirements** during the Prime trial period.

FAQs

#### 1. What is Seller Fulfilled Prime?

Seller Fulfilled Prime (SFP) allows sellers to offer Prime-eligible products while handling the fulfillment themselves. Sellers can list products with the Prime badge and offer two-day, one-day, and free standard shipping. There is no fee to enroll in the program, but performance requirements must be met to maintain eligibility.

#### 2. How do I know if I am eligible for Seller Fulfilled Prime?

After being eligible for the Seller Fulfilled Prime program, any SKUs associated with your Prime templates will automatically display the Prime badge for customers within 24 hours. Your Prime items will also show guaranteed delivery date messaging.

#### 3. What are the performance requirements to maintain eligibility for Seller Fulfilled Prime?

To maintain eligibility for Seller Fulfilled Prime, sellers must continuously meet Amazon’s performance requirements. These requirements are monitored weekly and include metrics such as:

- On-Time Shipment Rate
- Pre-Fulfillment Cancel Rate
- Valid Tracking Rate For more detailed information, refer to Seller Fulfilled Prime Performance Requirements.

#### 4. What happens if my performance metrics fall below the required levels?

If your performance metrics fall below the required levels, you will lose eligibility for Seller Fulfilled Prime. However, you can still sell those items on Amazon, but they will no longer carry the Prime badge.

#### 5. How do I reactivate Seller Fulfilled Prime if my eligibility is suspended?

To reactivate or re-enable Seller Fulfilled Prime on your account, you must:

1. Correct the issues that led to the suspension.
2. Meet the minimum performance metrics required for Seller Fulfilled Prime.
3. Submit a Plan of Action (POA) to the Seller Performance Team. The POA should include:

  - An explanation of what happened.
  - The reason the issues occurred.
  - How you will prevent these issues from occurring in the future.
4. Request re-enablement of Seller Fulfilled Prime on your account.

#### 6. Can I still sell my products on Amazon without the Prime badge?

Yes, even if your Seller Fulfilled Prime eligibility is suspended, you can still sell your products on Amazon. However, those products will no longer display the Prime badge, and they will not be eligible for the guaranteed delivery date messaging.

#### 7. How can I prevent my eligibility from being suspended?

To prevent suspension:

- Consistently meet the required performance metrics (e.g., On-Time Shipment Rate, Valid Tracking Rate).
- Monitor your performance regularly in the Account Health Dashboard.
- Address any issues promptly and ensure all shipments are processed efficiently.

#### 8. Where can I find more information about Seller Fulfilled Prime?

For additional information, you can refer to Seller Fulfilled Prime Requirements and the Seller Fulfilled Prime performance guidelines available in Seller Central.
