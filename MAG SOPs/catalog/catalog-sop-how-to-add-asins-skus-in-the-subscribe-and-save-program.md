# Catalog SOP: How to Add ASINs/SKUs in the Subscribe and Save program

## **How to Add ASINs/SKUs in the Subscribe and Save program**

**Who is this for?**

Individuals who need to add listings to FBA subscribe and save subscriptions.

**Objective:**

The purpose of this document is to present the updated process for enrolling listings to the SnS subscription.

FBA Subscribe & Save (SnS) lets Amazon customers sign up for recurring, scheduled deliveries of products that they use frequently. It gives customers discounts on eligible products.

Say, for instance, if a customer has subscribed to 4 or fewer products, then he or she receives a discount of 5%. On the other hand, if he or she has subscribed to 5+ products, then the discount is either 10% to 15% based on the product category.

**2 types of SnS:**

- **Sign-up orders** - the moment the buyer initially completes the subscription.
- **Replenishment orders** - based on the delivery frequency the customer has set, will automatically generate for the coming months.

**✍️NOTE: Coupons and promotions will work on top of your SnS-enrolled products resulting in a higher discount than what is already given in the SnS.**

**How to enroll ASINs/SKUs in the SnS program:**

**1. Automatic Enrollment**

- Eligible products are automatically enrolled in the Subscribe & Save program at the default enrollment discount selected in the Manage Products section. By default, this discount is set at 0%.

**2. Additional Tiered Discount**

- An additional 5% tiered discount is applied for customers who have five or more Subscribe & Save subscription orders scheduled to arrive on the same delivery day (e.g., the 1st of the month).

**3. Updating Default Enrollment Discount**

- To streamline future enrollments and increase subscriber acquisition rates from day one, consider updating your default enrollment discount to 5% or 10%. Follow these steps:

1. Navigate to Manage Products.
2. Click on Change default enrollment discount at the top of the page.
3. Set the desired default discount rate.

**Note: Starting June 27, 2024, if you have previously set a default discount for FBA Subscribe & Save products, this setting will also apply to seller-fulfilled products enrolled in the program.**

**4. Troubleshooting Automatic Enrollment**

- **For FBA Products:**

  - Use the self-service product eligibility tool in Manage Products to determine why certain products have not been automatically enrolled.
- **For Seller-Fulfilled Products:**

  - If you believe the product eligibility assessment is incorrect or you need to inquire about eligibility, contact support via the Get Help page. Provide the following details to facilitate resolution:

    - Marketplace ID
    - Merchant ID or Seller ID
    - ASIN
    - SKU

**5. Enabling Automatic Enrollment**

- To ensure automatic enrollment of eligible products, go to Manage Products and confirm that your settings are correctly configured.

⚠️**WARNING: DO NOT REMOVE OTHER PRODUCTS FROM THE SNS PROGRAM.**

**⚠️WARNING: DO NOT REMOVE THE OTHER SKUs FROM THE PROGRAM IF THEY ARE NOT INCLUDED IN THE SKU YOU’VE UPLOADED. Chances are, these SKUs are already existing on the dashboard prior to your upload. If you are unsure, refer the matter to the designated brand manager and ask for further instructions about the other SKUs.**

FAQs

**1. What is the Subscribe and Save (SnS) program?**

- The SnS program allows customers to sign up for recurring, scheduled deliveries of frequently used products. Customers receive discounts, ranging from 5% for fewer than five subscriptions to 10–15% for five or more subscriptions, depending on the product category.

**2. How do I enroll products in the SnS program?**

- Products can be enrolled in two ways:

  - Automatic Enrollment: Eligible products are automatically enrolled with the default discount (typically 0%).
  - Manual Adjustment: Update the default enrollment discount or troubleshoot eligibility issues through the Manage Products section.

**3. What is the default discount for SnS products?**

- The default discount is set to 0% but can be updated to 5% or 10% in the Manage Products section. Adjusting this rate can increase subscriber acquisition from the start.

**4. How can I update the default enrollment discount for future products?**

- Navigate to the Manage Products page.
- Click Change default enrollment discount at the top.
- Set your desired discount rate (e.g., 5% or 10%).

**5. What is the additional tiered discount in SnS?**

- An additional 5% discount applies when customers have five or more SnS subscription orders scheduled for delivery on the same day.

**6. Can coupons and promotions be combined with SnS discounts?**

- Yes, coupons and promotions apply on top of the SnS discount, resulting in a higher overall discount for the customer.

**7. How do I check why a product hasn’t been automatically enrolled in SnS?**

- For FBA products, use the self-service product eligibility tool in the Manage Products section. For seller-fulfilled products, contact Seller Support with the following information:

  - Marketplace ID
  - Merchant ID or Seller ID
  - ASIN
  - SKU

**8. How do I enable automatic enrollment for eligible products?**

- Ensure the automatic enrollment settings are configured correctly in the Manage Products section. Contact Seller Support if issues persist.

**9. What happens if I remove other SKUs from the SnS program by mistake?**

- Removing existing SKUs can disrupt existing subscriptions. Always check with the designated brand manager before removing any SKUs not included in your update to avoid errors.

**10. What is the difference between sign-up orders and replenishment orders in SnS?**

- Sign-up Orders: The first order completed when a customer subscribes to a product.
- Replenishment Orders: Recurring orders based on the delivery frequency chosen by the customer.

**11. Can seller-fulfilled products be enrolled in SnS?**

- Yes, starting June 27, 2024, the default enrollment discount set for FBA products will also apply to seller-fulfilled products. Contact Seller Support for eligibility inquiries if needed.

**12. Where does the SnS option appear on the product detail page (PDP)?**

- The SnS option typically appears below the product price or in the "Subscribe & Save" section of the PDP. Ensure the product is properly enrolled for this feature to display.

**13. What should I do if I’m unsure about removing other SKUs from SnS?**

- If you're unsure, always consult the designated brand manager for guidance before removing any SKUs from the SnS program.

**14. How can I troubleshoot eligibility issues for FBA products in SnS?**

- Use the self-service product eligibility tool in the Manage Products section to identify why certain products haven’t been automatically enrolled. Follow the tool’s recommendations to address the issue.

**15. Are there any restrictions on the type of products eligible for SnS?**

- Yes, only certain products are eligible for SnS. Amazon’s eligibility criteria include being an FBA or seller-fulfilled product and meeting specific category requirements. For seller-fulfilled products, eligibility issues may require Seller Support intervention.

**16. How long does it take for changes to SnS enrollment to take effect?**

- Changes to enrollment, including updating discounts or enabling products, typically take a few hours to reflect on the PDP.
