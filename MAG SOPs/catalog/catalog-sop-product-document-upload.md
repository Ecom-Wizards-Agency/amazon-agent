# Catalog SOP: Product Document Upload

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-product-document-upload

## **Product Document Upload**

**Who is this for?**

All individuals who wish to upload product documents on Seller Central

**Objective:**

To be able to add product documents on Seller Central either via flat file or thru backend

**For full document guidelines, please refer to this**[**guide**](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/quick-reference-guide-product-document-guideline)**.**

**A. FLAT FILE**

**Instructions:**

1. **Access the Amazon Seller Central page by clicking the URL:**[**https://sellercentral.amazon.com/**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/)

- Once there, you will be asked to log in. Log in using your Amazon login details.
- Once you are logged in, the landing page should look something like this:

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eH9image.png)

**NOTE: Update as of March 2024, putting documents in a product is now the same as you would update a file - via flat file.**

1. **On Seller Central's Home page, click the hamburger icon (top left) and go to Catalog > Add products via upload.**

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KPdimage.png)
2. **Click Download spreadsheet.**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3ZMimage.png)

1. **Select Download Product Spreadsheet.**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/E8uimage.png)

1. **Download the flat file depending on the category you want to edit - in this example, we will be using Soap Dish.**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bzkimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/QQ6image.png)

1. **Inside the flat file, fill out the necessary details needed for a PartialUpdate, and look for this item in the flat file.**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KCRimage.png)

1. **Put the URL of the certificate/manual that you want to attach**

- The file should be a PDF
- The URL should be from a publicly-accessible link

  - Example: Save the PDF to the client's Google Drive folder, and get the shareable link ("anyone who has the link can view")

1. **Upload the file in Seller Central. Once the file is uploaded and after the turnaround time, it should appear on the PDP.**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/FgEimage.png)

**B. BACKEND (MANAGE INVENTORY)**

**Instructions:**

1. **Access Inventory Management:**

- From the Inventory drop-down menu in Seller Central, select Manage All Inventory.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PrHimage.png)

1. **Edit SKU Details:**

- Click Edit next to the SKU for which you want to upload documents. This will open a new page in your browser.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0X3image.png)

1. **Select Attribute View:**

- On the new page, choose All attributes from the left-hand menu (it may already be selected).

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iCLimage.png)

1. **Suggest Changes (if applicable):**

- If the option is available, click Suggest changes to product information in the top-right corner of the page.

1. **Go to Compliance Section:**

- Click on the Safety & compliance tab.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/m3Kimage.png)

1. **Complete Mandatory Fields:**

- Ensure all required fields are filled out.

1. **Upload Compliance Documents:**

- Scroll to the Compliance media section and fill in the following details:

  - **Compliance media language:** Select the language code from the drop-down menu. Use the code for the store’s official language (e.g., fr_FR for France). For multilingual documents, select only the official language of the store.
  - **Compliance media content type:** Choose the document type from the drop-down menu. Refer to the "Permitted product documents" section of the help page for descriptions.
  - **Compliance media source location:** Enter a direct download link to the product document. Avoid using shareable links that open the PDF in a browser.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3Ffimage.png)

1. **Add Additional Documents (if needed):**

- Click Add more to include any additional documents for the same SKU.

1. **Save Changes:**

- Click Save and finish to save your updates.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rSyimage.png)

**VALIDATION:**

- Your product documents will be reviewed for compliance with Amazon's guidelines within seven business days. Documents that do not meet these guidelines will be rejected.
- Once approved, it may take up to 24 business hours for the documents to be published on the product detail page of the corresponding SKU.

FAQs

**1. What types of documents can be uploaded to a product listing?**

- Amazon permits uploading the following documents (refer to the Permitted product documents section in Seller Central):

  - Certificates (e.g., safety certifications).
  - User manuals.
  - Installation guides.
  - Compliance-related documents.

**2. What format should the documents be in?**

- Documents must be in PDF format and hosted on a publicly accessible link.

**3. How do I generate a publicly accessible URL for the document?**

- Save the document to a client’s Google Drive or another cloud storage service.
- Set the sharing permissions to “Anyone with the link can view.”
- Copy the shareable link and use it in your flat file or backend submission.

**4. What is the difference between uploading documents via flat file and backend?**

- Flat File: Suitable for bulk uploads. Use a flat file template to attach document URLs for multiple SKUs.
- Backend: Used for individual SKUs. Documents are added via the Manage All Inventory section in Seller Central.

**5. Where do uploaded documents appear once processed?**

- Approved documents are displayed in the Product Detail Page (PDP) under the relevant section, such as "Product Documentation" or "Safety Information."

**6. How long does Amazon take to review and publish uploaded documents?**

- Review Process: Up to seven business days for compliance checks.
- Publication: Once approved, it may take an additional 24 business hours for the documents to appear on the PDP.

**7. Can I upload multiple documents for the same SKU?**

- Yes, you can upload multiple documents for a single SKU. Use the Add more option in the backend or include multiple URLs in the flat file, ensuring each document is relevant.

**8. What should I do if my document upload is rejected?**

- If your document is rejected:

  - Review Amazon’s rejection reason (found in the notification or compliance dashboard).
  - Ensure the document meets the format, content, and language requirements.
  - Correct the issues and resubmit the document.

**9. How do I specify the document language when uploading?**

- In the Compliance media language field, select the appropriate language code (e.g., en_US for the United States, fr_FR for France).
- For multilingual documents, choose the primary language of the store’s marketplace.

**10. Can I upload documents for inactive SKUs?**

- No, documents can only be uploaded for active SKUs. Ensure the SKU is live and available in the inventory before attaching documents.

**11. What is the "Compliance media source location," and how should it be formatted?**

- The Compliance media source location is the URL where the document is hosted.
- Use a direct download link to the document (e.g., PDF links that download directly without opening in a browser).
- Avoid links requiring additional permissions or redirects.

**12. What happens if I leave mandatory fields blank in the flat file?**

- Leaving mandatory fields blank will result in errors during the flat file upload. Ensure all required columns (e.g., Compliance media language, Compliance media source location) are filled correctly.

**13. Can I update or replace an existing document?**

- Yes, you can update or replace existing documents by:

  - Re-uploading a flat file with the new document URL.
  - Editing the SKU via Manage All Inventory in Seller Central.

**14. How do I validate if the document upload was successful?**

- Check the upload status in Seller Central under Catalog > Add Products via Upload > Upload Status.
- Monitor for errors in the processing report (if using a flat file).
- Verify the document on the PDP after the review and publication process.

**15. What should I do if the uploaded document doesn’t appear on the PDP after approval?**

- If the document doesn’t appear after 24 business hours:

  - Double-check the SKU and uploaded details.
  - Contact Amazon Seller Support with your submission details for further assistance.

**16. Are there any restrictions on document content?**

- Yes, documents must comply with Amazon’s content policies. Avoid:

  - Incomplete or misleading information.
  - Non-relevant materials.
  - Documents in unsupported formats or languages.

**17. Can I use shared links from platforms other than Google Drive?**

- Yes, other platforms like Dropbox or OneDrive can be used. Ensure the shared link allows direct download and public access.

**18. How do I troubleshoot errors during flat file uploads?**

- Download the Processing Report for error details.
- Check for missing or incorrectly formatted fields.
- Correct the errors and re-upload the file.

**19. Can I upload documents for products sold in international marketplaces?**

- Yes, but ensure the document language and content align with the target marketplace's requirements. Use the appropriate Compliance media language for each region.

**20. Who should I contact if I encounter issues with document uploads?**

- For unresolved issues, contact Amazon Seller Support via the Help section in Seller Central. Provide details such as:

  - SKU/ASIN.
  - Upload method (flat file or backend).
  - Document URL.
  - Error messages (if applicable).
