## Creating a Sales and Traffic Report by Date

**Who is this for:** This is designed for sellers or individuals responsible for managing business reports on Seller Central. It provides step-by-step instructions on how to download a Business report.

**Objective:**To guide users in accessing and generating business reports on Seller Central. By following these instructions, users will be able to retrieve valuable sales data, analyze performance trends, and make informed business decisions based on the insights gained from these reports.

- Business reports play a vital role in understanding the sales performance and overall health of your business on Seller Central. These reports provide valuable insights into key metrics and data that can be used to identify trends, evaluate product performance, and optimize your selling strategies.

This SOP will guide you through the process of accessing and downloading business reports on Seller Central. Whether you need to review sales data for a specific date, analyze performance by ASIN, or explore trends over time, these instructions will help you generate and customize reports to meet your specific requirements.

**To generate a Sales Dashboard Report, follow these steps:**

1. Go to Seller Central and click on the "Reports" tab in the top menu and select "Business Reports" from the drop-down.

FAQs

1. Where can I access the Sales and Traffic Report by Date in Seller Central?

To access the Sales and Traffic Report by Date, go to the Reports tab at the top menu of Seller Central, and select Business Reports from the drop-down menu. On the next page, click on the "Sales Dashboard" link in the left-side menu.

2. How do I choose the date or dates for the Sales and Traffic Report?

Under the Sales Dashboard, you can select the desired date or dates for your report using the drop-down menus located below the displayed graphs. This will allow you to filter the report data by specific time periods.

3. How do I customize the time frame for viewing the report?

You can view the report by day, week, or month using the "View" button. Additionally, you can zoom in on specific periods (7 days, 1 month, 3 months, 1 year, or 2 years) by selecting the appropriate time period to narrow down the view of your data.

4. How do I customize the columns in my Sales and Traffic Report?

Click on the SHOW/HIDE COLUMNS option on the right-hand side of the screen. This allows you to enable or disable specific metrics you want to include in your report. Once you’ve made your selections, click "Save Columns" to apply the changes.

5. How do I download the Sales and Traffic Report?

Once you’ve customized the report, click the "Download" button to download the Sales and Traffic Report in CSV format. This will allow you to further analyze the report in spreadsheet applications like Excel or Google Sheets.

6. Can I choose the specific product metrics to include in my Sales and Traffic Report?

Yes, you can customize the columns to include specific metrics such as sales volume, traffic, conversion rates, etc., by using the SHOW/HIDE COLUMNS option. This allows you to tailor the report to focus on the metrics that matter most to your analysis.

7. How do I analyze trends over time using the Sales and Traffic Report?

You can analyze trends over time by customizing the date range and timeframe view (day, week, or month). Use the zoom period (7D, 1M, 3M, 1Y, or 2Y) to zoom into specific time periods and see how sales and traffic metrics have changed over time.

8. Is there a limit to the amount of data I can include in the Sales and Traffic Report?

There is no specified limit for the Sales and Traffic Report. However, keep in mind that selecting longer time frames (e.g., 2 years) might result in larger data sets, which can be more difficult to analyze and may affect the performance of your report generation.

9. Can I generate reports for specific ASINs or categories?

Yes, the Sales and Traffic Report can be customized by selecting specific ASINs, categories, or other filters available in the Business Reports section. This allows you to focus on particular products or product categories to analyze their individual performance.

10. How do I know if my Sales and Traffic Report has been successfully generated?

After clicking the Download button, the CSV file will be available for download. You can check the download folder or browser notifications to ensure the report is successfully downloaded.

11. Can I schedule the Sales and Traffic Report to be generated automatically?

Currently, the SOP does not specify a scheduling feature for this specific report. If you need regular reports, you may need to manually generate and download them or explore other options available in Seller Central for automating report delivery.

12. What if I don’t want to include certain columns in my report?

You can choose to hide specific columns in the report by clicking on SHOW/HIDE COLUMNS and deselecting the metrics you do not wish to include. Once you’re satisfied with the selection, click "Save Columns" to apply the changes.

13. Can I compare multiple time periods in the Sales and Traffic Report?

Yes, you can compare sales data across different periods by using the zoom feature (7D, 1M, 3M, 1Y, 2Y) to visualize trends and performance over time. You can also adjust the date range to compare data for different periods.
