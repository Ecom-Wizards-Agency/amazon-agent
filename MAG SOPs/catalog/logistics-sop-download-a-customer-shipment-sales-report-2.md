# Logistics SOP: Download a Customer Shipment Sales Report

## **Download a Customer Shipment Sales Report**

**Who is this for :** For individuals who are responsible for generating and analyzing customer shipment sales reports within an organization. It provides step-by-step instructions to help them effectively retrieve the necessary information for sales analysis and decision-making purposes.

**Objective:** To outline the process for downloading a Customer Shipment Sales Report, ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and adherence to established guidelines.

**Instructions**

1. From the Seller Central home page, click the 3-line bars located on the left side. Then, select "Reports" and choose "Fulfillment."

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.52.32%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/n7Zimage.png)
2. Click the “Customer Shipment Sales” link located under the “Sales” header to the left of the page.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.52.38%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6Ewimage.png)
3. On the Customer Shipment Sales page, click on the "Download" tab. Select the “Event Date” or choose “Exact Dates” and enter the “From” and “To” dates respectively from the calendar icon to the right of each.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.52.45%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zsrimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.52.51%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/lYLimage.png)
4. Click the “Request .csv Download” button to download the report in an Excel format or click the “Request .txt Download” button to download the report in a text format.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.52.58%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/u6Uimage.png)
5. The “Report Status” column will indicate the status as “In Progress” in the table below.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-15%20at%2012.53.04%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/OHNimage.png)
6. Once the report is ready for download, the status will change to “Download”. Click the “Download” button located in the “Report Status” column to download your report.

FAQs

1. How far back can I retrieve data in the Customer Shipment Sales Report?

Amazon typically allows access to data for the last 90 days. If you need older data, you may need to generate multiple reports for shorter date ranges or check other sales reports in Seller Central.

2. Can I schedule automatic downloads of the Customer Shipment Sales Report?

No, Amazon does not currently provide an option to schedule automatic downloads for this report. However, you can use Amazon’s Selling Partner API (SP-API) to automate report retrieval.

3. What type of data is included in the Customer Shipment Sales Report?

This report provides details such as order ID, SKU, ASIN, quantity shipped, shipping method, fulfillment center, destination, and sales revenue. It helps sellers track shipped orders and analyze sales trends.

4. Why is my report download failing?

If your download fails, try the following:

- Refresh the page and request the report again.
- Use a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
- Clear your browser cache and cookies.
- Reduce the date range if the report is too large.
- Check if you have the necessary Seller Central permissions.

5. What’s the difference between the Customer Shipment Sales Report and the Date Range Sales Report?

- Customer Shipment Sales Report focuses on orders that have been shipped, including fulfillment center details and shipping methods.
- Date Range Sales Report provides a broader overview of all sales, including pending, canceled, and completed orders.

6. Does this report include canceled or refunded orders?

No, the report only includes shipped orders. To check refunds or cancellations, use the Payments or Returns Reports in Seller Central.

7. Can I filter the report by specific ASINs or SKUs?

No, Amazon does not allow filtering before downloading. However, after downloading the report in CSV format, you can filter the data using Excel or Google Sheets.

8. How often is the Customer Shipment Sales Report updated?

The report is updated daily, but there may be a delay of 24–48 hours for the latest shipments to appear.

9. Why don’t I see any data in my downloaded report?

If your report is empty, check the following:

- Ensure the selected date range includes periods when shipments occurred.
- Verify that the correct fulfillment channel (FBA or FBM) is active.
- Confirm that your seller account has had recent sales activity.

10. Can I download the report in a format other than CSV or TXT?

No, Amazon only provides this report in CSV (Excel format) or TXT (text format). You can convert it to other formats after downloading using spreadsheet or database tools.
