# Catalog SOP: Fulfilling Merchant Fulfilled Orders via Email Notification

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-fulfilling-merchant-fulfilled-orders-via-email-notification

## **Fulfilling Merchant Fulfilled Orders via Email Notification**

**Who is this for :** This is for All individuals who want to fulfill their merchant-fulfilled orders via email notification.

**Objective :** To ensure that all individuals know how to fulfill their merchant-fulfilled orders via email notification.

**Instructions:**

1. You will receive an email notification when an order is placed for your Merchant Fulfilled item.

2. Access Seller Central by clicking the links provided at the bottom of the email.

3. Print the packing slip by selecting “Print Order Packing Slip” next to the order number on the order page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-06%20at%2010.38.18%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1dSimage.png)

4. To purchase and print shipping labels, click the “Buy Shipping” button on the Order Detail Page next to the order.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-06%20at%2010.38.25%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7L6image.png)

5. Alternatively, you can manage your shipping through your preferred carrier and print your own labels instead.

6. Once the order is packed, go to the Order Detail Page, select the order, and click the “Confirm Shipment” button at the top. Enter the shipping and tracking details.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-02-06%20at%2010.38.33%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Fcaimage.png)

7. Once the shipment is confirmed, Amazon will notify the buyer, charge them for the order, and transfer the payment to your Seller Central account.

FAQs

1. What should I do if I don’t receive an email notification for a new order?

- Check your spam or junk folder first. If the email isn’t there, verify that your notification settings in Seller Central are enabled. You can also check your orders directly in Seller Central under the "Orders" tab.

2. Can I fulfill my order without using the email notification?

- Yes, you can log in to Seller Central anytime, navigate to "Orders," and process the fulfillment without relying on the email notification.

3. What if the links in the email notification don’t work?

- Ensure you are logged into the correct Seller Central account. If the links are still unresponsive, try accessing Seller Central manually by visiting Amazon Seller Central and navigating to your orders.

4. Do I have to use Amazon’s “Buy Shipping” option?

- No, you can use your preferred carrier to manage shipping and print your own labels. However, using Amazon’s “Buy Shipping” ensures compliance with Amazon’s shipping policies and may offer discounted rates.

5. What happens if I forget to confirm shipment in Seller Central?

- If you don’t confirm shipment, Amazon won’t notify the buyer, charge them, or release the payment to your Seller Central account. This could also impact your seller performance metrics.

6. Can I edit shipping or tracking details after confirming shipment?

- No, once a shipment is confirmed, you cannot modify the tracking details. If there’s an issue, you may need to contact the buyer and/or Amazon Seller Support.

7. How long do I have to ship the order after receiving the email notification?

- The handling time depends on the shipping settings you’ve configured in Seller Central. Be sure to check your estimated shipping time to avoid late shipments.

8. Will Amazon automatically send the tracking number to the buyer?

- Yes, once you confirm shipment and enter the tracking details, Amazon will notify the buyer and update their order status.
