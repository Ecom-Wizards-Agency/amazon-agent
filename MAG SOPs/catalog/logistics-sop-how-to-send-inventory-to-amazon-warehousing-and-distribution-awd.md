# Logistics SOP: How to Send inventory to Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD)

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/logistics-sop-how-to-send-inventory-to-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd

## How to Send inventory to Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD)

**Who is this for?**This SOP is for team members responsible for preparing and sending shipments to Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD).

**Objective:**This SOP outlines the process for creating and shipping a shipment using **Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD)**. The goal is to ensure that team members understand the steps involved in preparing and sending shipments to an AWD distribution center, including adding inventory, selecting shipping methods, and tracking shipments.

**IMPORTANT NOTE:** Make sure the shipment will comply with Amazon Warehousing and Distribution Product Eligibility page. You can refer to this article to check the eligibility - [Supplemental Article: Maximizing Efficiency with Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/supplemental-article-maximizing-efficiency-with-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd)

**Procedure:**

1. **Accessing the AWD Program Page**

- **Log in to Seller Central:**

  - Access your Amazon Seller Central account.
- **Navigate to AWD Program Page:**

  - From the **Menu**bar, select **Inventory > Warehousing and distribution (AWD)** page to begin the shipment process.

- **Add Inventory:**

  - Select the inventory you want to send by choosing from your available SKUs. Alternatively, you can upload a file with your inventory details.

- **Choose Ship From Address:**

  - Select the location from which the shipment will be sent (Ship From Address).
- **Select Preferred Ship To Region:**

  - Choose the desired destination region for the shipment.

1. **Reviewing Shipment Details**

- **Select the shipping date**

  - Enter the date you would like to start the shipment
- **Choose a Shipping Method:**

  - Select the shipping method, shipping mode and shipping carrier based on your preferences or Amazon’s recommendations.

    - If using an Amazon Partnered Carrier, Amazon will automatically choose the lowest-cost carrier for you and provide available shipping dates. If you prefer to use a different carrier, click “**Change Carrier**” to modify the selection.
- **View Shipping Destination Details:**

  - After selecting the inventory and shipping preferences, review your shipping destination details.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-send-inventory-to-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-vhdslkdr.png)

- **Review shipping fees**

  - Review all fees before clicking “**Accept charges and confirm shipping**”

    [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/lOsimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/lOsimage.png)

1. **Printing Box Labels**

- **Print Serialized Ship Container Codes (SSCC) Box Labels:**

  - Print the required SSCC labels for your shipment. Each box must have a unique label.
- **Apply Labels:**

  - Attach each label flat and uncovered to the outside of each box to prevent delays or rejections.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/logistics-sop-how-to-send-inventory-to-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd-image-mxapvfky.png)

1. **Pallet Information**

  - Input any relevant pallet information if your shipment requires palletization.
2. **Shipping the Products**

- **Send Shipment to AWD Distribution Center:**

  - Once all the information is confirmed, you are ready to ship your products to an AWD distribution center.

- **Track Your Shipment:**

  - After your shipment has been dispatched, you can track the status by returning to the AWD program page and clicking “**Track Shipments**”.

FAQs

**1. What is AWD?**AWD is a third-party logistics program that provides long-term, upstream bulk inventory storage at a cost-effective rate. The program also offers an automatic replenishment option to Prime-ready fulfillment centers. Currently, AWD is available only in the US.

**2. What is the eligibility for using AWD?**For information on product eligibility, you can refer to this article to check the eligibility - [Supplemental Article: Maximizing Efficiency with Amazon Warehousing and Distribution (AWD)](https://myamazonguy.document360.io/docs/supplemental-article-maximizing-efficiency-with-amazon-warehousing-and-distribution-awd)

**3. What are the workflow steps for creating a shipment in AWD?**The steps for creating a shipment are as follows:

- Choose inventory to send
- Confirm shipping
- Print box labels
- Confirm carrier (for Amazon partnered carriers and Seller Managed Carrier only)
- Print pallet labels (for pallet shipments with an Amazon partnered carrier only)
- Tracking details (for pallet shipments with an Amazon partnered carrier only)

**4. Can I schedule a delivery appointment at AWD distribution centers?**No, Amazon does not allow general public deliveries. Only professional carriers are allowed to make delivery appointments at distribution centers through Carrier Central. For more information, visit Book a Delivery Appointment.
