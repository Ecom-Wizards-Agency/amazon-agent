# Catalog SOP: Product Authenticity Customer Complaint

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-product-authenticity-customer-complaint

## **Product Authenticity Customer Complaint**

**Who is this for :** This is for sellers on Amazon who receive customer complaints regarding the authenticity of their products. It provides a framework for the seller to investigate the issue, take corrective actions, and appeal to Amazon.

**Objective :** This standard operating procedure (SOP) outlines the steps to be taken when a customer complains about the authenticity of a product purchased from the seller. The SOP provides guidelines to investigate the root cause of the complaint, take corrective actions to prevent future complaints, and submit an appeal to Amazon.

This issue happens when a customer complains about the authenticity of the product they received. The complaint will be displayed on the [account health page](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/performance/dashboard).

Review the performance notification received for this specific issue. Try to find as [much information as possible](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/performance/notifications).

**Causes of Inauthentic Complaints:**

1. Damaged box/packaging.
2. Incomplete product information, mainly the origin of the product.

**Documents that may be asked by Amazon:**

1. Product packaging images, all sides of it. Make sure the barcode is clearly shown.
2. Manufacturer Invoice.
3. Brand Authorization letter if you're a reseller of the product.

**Disclaimer:**

1. If you only get 1 inauthentic complaint, there's a possibility that Amazon will ask you to acknowledge the violation in account health and the issue will be cleared.
2. If you get constant inauthentic complaints from many customers, Amazon may ask you to explain by providing a plan of action (PoA).

You can use the following template for most issues. Please note that these are templates and should be updated accordingly. If it is not accepted the first time, talk to the account health team and try to find more details about the problem. Update the appeal accordingly and submit it again.

MAG’s template

Root causes of the complaints about the authenticity of our items:

1. We received some items in defective packaging and repackaged them in generic boxes. Since the packaging did not have the brand name, buyers complained about them as being inauthentic.
2. We have not checked all products we received from the manufacturer. Some products have mass production defects. That caused some of the complaints about the product being inauthentic.

We have never sold inauthentic products to our customers. Please see the attached invoices. We understand our mistakes have caused the customer complaints and we are completely responsible for them.

Corrective actions we have taken to prevent future complaints:

1. We responded to all customer complaints and issued a full refund or free replacement.
2. We audited our catalog to ensure all listings are solid in the proper condition and all of them have proper packaging. We made sure all packages have the correct brand name on them.
3. We as a company have reviewed all of Amazon’s policies, paying special attention to the [Best Practices in Product Authenticity and Quality](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/202010130?ref=srsl_gsbp_pq), [Amazon Anti-Counterfeiting Policy](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/201165970?ref=srsl_pol_pq) and the [Amazon General Seller Best Practices](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/200587810?ref=srsl_bppa_pq).

The steps you have taken to prevent the issue going forward:

1. We will never use generic boxes as packaging again in the future. We have ordered more customized packaging from the manufacturer to make sure we can use them if any of the items' packaging is damaged.
2. We will check every unit we receive from the manufacturer and make sure it is not damaged or defective. We have a better quality control process with the manufacturer that will ensure any of our products are not defective. Manufacturer will check every item prior to shipping them out to us.
3. We take all responsibility for this incident and we have learned from this issue. We confirm this will never happen again in the future.

We are confident that the information provided is sufficient in addressing the issue which caused the complaint and addressing our plan to correct the issue and to make sure it does not happen again. We appreciate your support to help us keep selling on Amazon.

FAQs

1. What is a Product Authenticity Customer Complaint?

A Product Authenticity Customer Complaint occurs when a customer reports that the item they received is not genuine or does not match the expected brand or packaging. This can impact Account Health and lead to listing removal or account suspension if not addressed properly.

2. How do I check if my listing has been flagged for an authenticity complaint?

Go to Seller Central > Performance > Account Health > Product Authenticity Customer Complaints to view flagged ASINs and details of the issue.

3. What are the common causes of authenticity complaints?

- Damaged or repackaged items (e.g., generic boxes instead of original brand packaging).
- Mass production defects that make items look different from expected.
- Incomplete product details (e.g., missing manufacturer information or branding).
- Selling unbranded products listed under a branded ASIN.
- Supplier issues where products are sourced from unauthorized distributors.

4. What documents might Amazon request to verify authenticity?

- Product packaging images (showing all sides, including barcodes).
- Manufacturer invoices (showing purchase history and supplier details).
- Brand Authorization Letter (if you are a reseller of the product).

5. How do I appeal a Product Authenticity Complaint?

1. Identify the root cause by reviewing the flagged ASIN and customer complaints.
2. Gather supporting documents such as invoices and product images.
3. Submit a Plan of Action (POA) explaining the issue, corrective actions, and preventive measures.
4. Monitor your case in Seller Central for updates from Amazon.

6. What should be included in my Plan of Action (POA)?

Your POA should include:

- Root cause analysis (why the complaint happened).
- Corrective actions taken (how the issue was fixed).
- Preventive measures (steps to ensure it doesn’t happen again).
- Supporting evidence (invoices, brand authorization, packaging images).

7. How can I prevent future authenticity complaints?

- Never repackage items in generic boxes—use only original branded packaging.
- Verify all products upon arrival from suppliers to ensure they match listings.
- Source only from authorized manufacturers or distributors.
- Update product detail pages to accurately reflect what customers will receive.
- Train warehouse staff to identify and reject damaged or questionable stock.

8. What if my appeal gets denied multiple times?

- Contact Amazon Account Health Support via Performance > Account Health > Call me now for further clarification.
- Revise your POA with additional details and supporting documents.
- Request an internal inventory check to verify the authenticity of your stock.

9. Can I relist a product after an authenticity complaint?

Yes, but only if:

- You provide sufficient evidence proving authenticity.
- The product meets Amazon’s Anti-Counterfeiting Policy and Product Authenticity Guidelines.
- You have updated packaging and supplier verification to avoid repeat issues.
