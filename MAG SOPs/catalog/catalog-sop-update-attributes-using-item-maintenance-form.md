## Update Attributes Using Item Maintenance Form in Vendor Central

**Who is this for :** The vendor may request from the Amazon vendor support team regarding specific attribute updates by filing a ticket and using the Item Maintenance Form.

**Objective :** To ensure that the product information on Amazon is accurate, optimized, and up-to-date.

**Additional Compliance Note:**

Quebec Bill 96 French Language Requirements (Effective June 1, 2025)

**What are the new requirements?**

- **Signage:**
  All signage (outside and inside) that shows business names or trademarks must be **exclusively or predominantly in French**.
- **Packaging:**
  Product packaging sold in Quebec must include **French translations of all generic terms and product characteristics**, even if these terms are part of a registered or pending trademark.
- **Trademark Status:**
  A trademark with a formal application at the Canadian Intellectual Property Office (CIPO) is treated as registered. If unregistered, French translations are mandatory.
- **Compliance Deadline:**
  Products manufactured before June 1, 2025, may be sold until June 1, 2027. After this, only packaging meeting French language requirements can be sold.

Consequences of Non-Compliance

The Office québécois de la langue française (OQLF) enforces these rules and can issue fines from **$3,000 up to $90,000 per day for repeated offenses**.

What You Need to Do

1. Review all your Quebec-targeted product packaging and signage for French language compliance.
2. Add French translations for all descriptive terms and product information on packaging.
3. Verify trademark registration/application status and translate if necessary.
4. Plan inventory and packaging updates to meet the June 1, 2027 deadline.
5. Consult with your legal or compliance team if you have questions.

FAQs

1. **Which attributes can I update using the Item Maintenance Form?**

- The Item Maintenance Form allows vendors to request updates for specific product attributes like size, color, and other catalog-related details. However, not all attributes are editable through this form. If you’re unsure whether an attribute can be updated, refer to the Vendor Central help documentation or contact Vendor Support for clarification.

1. **What should I do if the Item Maintenance Form does not open or is not working properly?**

- If the form doesn’t open or isn’t downloading correctly, ensure that your browser allows pop-ups and macros. If you continue having trouble, try clearing your browser’s cache or use a different browser. You can also contact Vendor Support for assistance if you continue to encounter issues with the form.

1. **How do I know which ASINs to enter on the Data Sheet Tab?**

- You should enter the ASINs that are relevant to the attributes you want to update. You can find these ASINs in your product listings within Vendor Central. If you need to update multiple ASINs, you can list them all in the Data Sheet Tab, one per row. If you’re unsure which ASINs to update, refer to your product catalog or ask Vendor Support for guidance.

1. **What should I do if my attribute update request is denied or not granted?**

- If your request is denied, you can respond to Vendor Support with additional context or clarification regarding the change. If necessary, you can reopen the case by submitting a new support ticket with a more detailed explanation or updated information. Always ensure your request meets Amazon’s policies and guidelines to improve the likelihood of approval.

1. **Are there any size or format requirements for submitting the Item Maintenance Form?**

- The Item Maintenance Form should be saved as a standard Excel or CSV file. Make sure that the file isn’t too large—large files may have trouble uploading. If you encounter issues uploading the form, try reducing the file size or splitting the file into smaller parts before submitting.

1. **What happens after I submit my case with the Item Maintenance Form?**

- After submitting your case, you will receive a case ID that you can use to track the status of your request. Vendor Support typically responds within an hour, but the resolution process may take longer depending on the complexity of the issue. You will be notified via email or in Vendor Central when a response is available. If further action is required, you may need to reply to the support team.

1. **What should I do if I realize I made a mistake on the Item Maintenance Form after submitting it?**

- If you make a mistake after submitting the form, you can respond to the case by providing a corrected version of the form. In your response, explain what changes you made and why. If the case is still under review, Vendor Support will update the request accordingly. If the case has been closed, you may need to submit a new case with the corrected form.
