# Advertising SOP: Setting Up a Sponsored Display Campaign

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-setting-up-a-sponsored-display-campaign

## **Setting Up a Sponsored Display Campaign**

**Who is this for?**

This SOP is intended for **PPC Specialists** looking to set up and optimize Sponsored Display campaigns.

**Objective:**

To guide users through the setup of a Sponsored Display campaign that drives traffic to product detail pages, supports awareness and consideration goals, and reaches relevant audiences both on and off Amazon.

**Step-by-Step Process**

**Step 1: Define Campaign Goals**

- Determine the **primary objective**:

  - Awareness
  - Consideration
  - Purchase
- Identify which **performance metrics** you’ll use to measure success (e.g., reach, CTR, conversions).

**Step 2: Create the Campaign**

- Log in to your **Amazon Advertising Console**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/F6jimage.png)

- Click **“Create Campaign”** and choose **Sponsored Display**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6ouimage.png)

- Click **Continue**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/WQkimage.png)

**Step 3: Set Campaign Settings**

- Enter a **campaign name** that’s clear and descriptive. You can refer to this SOP for popper campaign naming convention - [Advertising SOP: Naming Conventions and Creation Guidelines](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-naming-conventions-and-creation-guidelines)
- Choose a portfolio if you have one
- Set a **start date** (and an **end date** if campaign is seasonal).

  - *Recommended: Leave end date open for ongoing campaigns.*
- Set your **daily budget**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LN4image.png)

**Step 4: Create an Ad Group**

- Name your **ad group** to reflect the product group or targeting tactic.**(You can use the product’s ASIN here ex: B0xxxxxxxx)**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/kiBimage.png)

**Step 5: Customize Bidding Strategy**

- Choose a bidding strategy based on your campaign goals:

  - **Reach**
  - **Page Visits**
  - **Conversions**
- Amazon will automatically adjust bids based on relevance, but you can manually update them at any time.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Ankimage.png)

**Step 6: Select an Ad Format**

- Choose between:

  - **Image Format** (auto-generated from the product detail page, with optional custom branding)
  - **Video Format** (upload your own product- or brand-focused video)
- Customize image ads with:

  - Brand logo
  - Headline
  - Lifestyle image (optional)
- Choose the campaign’s landing page

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zj8image.png)

**Step 7: Choose Your Products**

- Add **10 or more retail-ready products** to maximize visibility.

  - Ensure all products have high-quality images, descriptions, and are in stock.

    ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/cn7image.png)

**Step 8: Select Your Targeting Strategy**

- Choose **one** targeting type per campaign:

  - **Contextual**: Targets shoppers currently browsing products or content related to selected categories or keywords.
  - **Remarketing Audiences**: Re-engages users who have previously viewed, purchased, or interacted with relevant products or content.
  - **In-Market Audiences**: Targets users actively considering purchases based on recent shopping behavior within a specific category.
  - **Interest & Lifestyle Audiences**: Reaches users based on long-term interests and lifestyle indicators from their shopping and entertainment habits.

**![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-setting-up-a-sponsored-display-campaign-image-8dmmntyc.png)**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/IAximage.png)

**Step 9: Provide Targeting Details**

- Based on your targeting choice:

  - **For Contextual Targeting**:

    - Select relevant **categories**, **brands**, or **products**.
  - **For Audiences**:

    - Refine by **brand**, **price range**, **star rating**, or **shipping eligibility**.

**Step 10: Launch the Campaign**

- Review all settings and creatives.
- Click **Launch Campaign** when ready.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-setting-up-a-sponsored-display-campaign-image-t57y3ohp.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/cKvimage.png)
