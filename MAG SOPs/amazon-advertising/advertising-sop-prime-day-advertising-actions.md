## **Amazon Advertising Go-To Actions on Prime Day**

**Who is this for?**Ad POCs that are applying changes to the ad account for Prime Day

**Description:** This SOP (standard operating procedure) will give you a step-by-step guide on how to set up an ad account for prime day.

**OVERVIEW**

Before Prime Day (specified below), we will be applying the strategies below to increase sales. They are:

**On the day before Prime Day:**

1. Take a bulk backup to revert it back to the original state
2. Boost Bids

a. Boost on-target ACOS and below keywords by **20%**

b. Boost “keywords a bit above ACOS target” by **5% to 15%** - Ad POC decision-making required

1. Increase Daily Budgets

a. Increase the budgets by **100%** on the campaigns that are below target ACOS

b. Increase the budgets by **50%** on the campaigns that are **slightly above** the target ACOS - Ad POC decision-making required

1. Add Budget Rule - Performance (Optional)

a. Add a budget rule to increase by **100%** on the campaigns that are less than target ACOS plus 10%(margin of error).

b. Add a budget rule to increase by **50%** on the campaigns that are greater than target ACOS plus 10%(margin of error). - Ad POC decision-making required

**After Prime Day:**

1. Revert everything back after Prime Day

**Action item #1: Backup the Original State**

- Download a bulk file.
- SAVE a copy of this bulk without any change so that we can revert back. You can save it to the client’s ad backup sheet and rename it as “Client Name - Prime Day 2024 Backup”

Note: Make sure you are in the right account by checking on the upper right side of your screen.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eERimage.png)

**Action item #2: Boosting the Bids**

- Go to the targeting tab and filter your targets by ACOS and make sure your date range is at least 15 days. In this example, our target ACOS is 20%.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vSIimage.png)

- Select all and boost all bids by **20%**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7Nhimage.png)

- Remove your filter and revert it by ACOS >= 20%. You should apply your target ACOS here, not 20%

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ggaimage.png)

- Now we will check the KEY targets that are not on target but bringing many sales. This part requires your evaluation and you should decide as you see fit.

  - Make sure you applied your ACOS >= since we already boosted below target ACOS targets and we don’t want to boost them again.
  - Sort your sales by highest to lowest
  - Those keywords are the keywords that are important to your account and they are not on target

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1wgimage.png)

- Now depending on your experience with the account, current ACOS, or the importance of the keyword, you should boost the targets by 3%-10%. We don’t have strict rules here since it might be different for each account. Decide which targets are a good fit and action in bulk or one-by-one manually.
- We are done with boosting the targets. Now we are moving on to the next action, boosting the daily budgets.

**Action item #3: Boosting the Daily Budgets**

- Go to the Budgets tab and apply less than the target ACOS filter and make sure your date range is 15 days.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/5Otimage.png)

- Select all the campaigns and increase the daily budget by 100%.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/NFDimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ZgQimage.png)

- Revert your filter and see the targets that are above the target ACOS and find out your best-selling campaigns by sorting them out by sales

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/POrimage.png)

- Decide which campaigns are important for the account and have a decent ACOS. Select those and increase the daily budget by 50%.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/33aimage.png)

**Action item #4: Add Budget Rule****(Optional)**

- Add a budget rule to increase by 100% on the campaigns that are less than or equal to target ACOS plus 10%(margin of error).

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1mhimage.png)

- In budget rule under type choose Performance then select the start and end date for Prime day. Next, in Performance metrics you need to determine what is the ACOS goal and add 10% for margin of error. In this account, the ACOS goal is 30% and we have to add 10% for margin of error. Lastly, Increase the budget by 100% and click add budget rule

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/BX3image.png)

- You may also put budget rule to ACOS greater than or equal to ACOS Goal plus 10% by 50% - Ad POC decision-making required

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/OAcimage.png)

**Action item #5: Revert Everything Back After Prime Day**

- So the Prime Day is over and we will revert our bids and budgets back. To do that, we will need to bulk what we downloaded and saved in the beginning of this SOP.
- Locate your bulk, download and upload it to the account without any change.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/OmLimage.png)

- Congratulations, the account is back to its original!

- Then, add filters and under **“Entity”**select only**“Keyword”**and**“Product Targeting”**

- Go to the**“ACOS”**column and filter your targets by ACOS. In this example, our target ACOS is 25%

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hdZimage.png)

Note: Make sure to put “%” symbol when you filter in excel

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GY8image.png)

- Once filtered, sort it form A to Z (Largest to Smallest)
- Add 2 new columns with label “Old Bid” and “New Bid.” Under Old Bid copy all the bids from column AB.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ojWimage.png)

- Then, all targets with less than the ACOS Goal boost all bids by 20% by selecting their old bid and multiply it by 1.20

**Formula = Old bid x 1.20**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/B1Qimage.png)

- Remove your filter in ACOS column by clicking on the drop down button of ACOS and do a filter ACOS greater than or equal to ACOS Goal. You should apply your target ACOS here.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/11Nimage.png)

Note: Make sure to put “%” symbol when you filter in excel

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Cmaimage.png)

- Now we will check the KEY targets that are not on target but bringing many sales. This part requires your evaluation and you should decide as you see fit.

  - Make sure you applied your ACOS >= since we already boosted below target ACOS targets and we don’t want to boost them again.
  - Sort your sales by highest to lowest

Note: Do not forget to copy the old bid from column AB to column of Old bid

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/MaMimage.png)

Those targets are important to your account and they are not on target

- Now depending on your experience with the account, current ACOS, or the importance of the keyword, you should boost the targets by 3%-10%. We don’t have strict rules here since it might be different for each account. Decide which targets are a good fit and action in bulk or one-by-one manually.
- We are done with boosting the targets. Now we are moving on to the next action, boosting the daily budgets.

**Action item #3: Boosting the Daily Budgets**

- Remove first all filters in the bulk file. Then filter again the headers. And under “Entity” select only Campaign.

- Also remove the **“paused”** under column R and S
- Go to the **“ACOS”** Column and apply less than the target ACOS Goal.
- Add a column with label “Old Budget” and “New Budget” in the last part.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/37Yimage.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/TeJimage.png)

- Clear your filter in ACOS column and filter it greater than or equal to ACOS goal and see the campaigns that are above the target ACOS and find out your best-selling campaigns by sorting them out by sales.

FAQs

- **Q1: What should I do if I need to boost bids for specific targets?**

  - **A1:**Filter by ACOS and focus on keywords not meeting the target but driving high sales. Boost their bids by 3-10%, depending on the sales importance and current ACOS.
- **Q2: What should I do if I need to boost bids for specific targets?**

  - **A2:**Filter by ACOS and focus on keywords not meeting the target but driving high sales. Boost their bids by 3-10%, depending on the sales importance and current ACOS.
- **Q3: How do I boost the daily budgets for campaigns?**

  - **A3:**Apply filters to campaigns with ACOS below the target and increase budgets by 100%. For campaigns slightly above the target ACOS, increase the budget by 50%.
- **Q4: What is the purpose of the Budget Rule, and how should I set it up?**

  - **A4:**The Budget Rule automatically adjusts the campaign budget based on performance. Set it up to increase the budget by 100% for campaigns with an ACOS under target plus a 10% margin, and by 50% for campaigns with an ACOS over the target plus a 10% margin.
- **Q5: What if I’m managing a large account?**

  - **A5:**The process involves downloading a backup, applying changes to the campaign settings in bulk, and uploading the updated bulk file for changes. After Prime Day, revert to the original backup.
- **Q6: Can I adjust the bids and budgets for individual campaigns or should it be done in bulk?**

  - **A6:**While bulk adjustments are recommended, you can also adjust individual campaigns based on their importance or sales performance.
- **Q7: How do I apply bid adjustments in the bulk file?**

  - **A7:**After downloading the bulk file, filter by ACOS and adjust bids manually by multiplying the old bid by 1.20 for under-target campaigns or adjust according to performance for those above the target ACOS.
- **Q8: How do I ensure the correct adjustments are made for my campaigns?**

  - **A8:**Sort the campaigns by sales and check that the ACOS is accurate for each campaign before applying the bid and budget adjustments.
- **Q9: How do I save and upload the bulk file with changes?**

  - **A9:**After making all adjustments to bids and budgets in the bulk file, save the file and upload it back to the account to implement the changes.
- **Q10: How do I handle changes if the client has specific needs?**

  - **A10:** Adjust decisions like the margin of error for ACOS and the budget increases based on client-specific goals or performance metrics.
