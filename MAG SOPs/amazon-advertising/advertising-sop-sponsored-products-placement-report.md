# Advertising SOP: Sponsored Products Placement Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-sponsored-products-placement-report

## **Sponsored Products Placement Report**

**Who is this for:**Intended for marketers, advertising specialists, and e-commerce professionals who are responsible for managing and optimizing advertising campaigns for products on Amazon. By following this SOP, these individuals can gain a better understanding on how to leverage Sponsored Products Placement Reports to improve their advertising strategies and drive more sales for their products.

**Objective:**The objective of this SOP is to provide step-by-step guidelines for creating and analyzing Sponsored Products Placement Reports on Amazon. The report analysis will help advertisers identify the best placements for their ads, optimize their bids, and increase the overall effectiveness of their advertising campaigns.

**STEPS:**

Step 1: Click on the three-line bar located on the top left side of Amazon seller central homepage, then select "Advertising," and choose "Campaign Manager".

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vuximage.png)

Step 2: Click the "Measurement & Reporting" icon and then select "Sponsored ads reports"

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/buBimage.png)

Step 3: Click on the "Create report" button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/S8Vimage.png)

Step 4: Choose "**Sponsored Products**" as the campaign type and select "**Placement**" as the report type. You can also choose the time unit and date range for the report.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KYEimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6IMimage.png)

Step 5: Label your report and schedule a delivery time if necessary. It's recommended to have a regular monthly report to keep track of the performance of the campaigns.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6iBimage.png)

Step 6: Click on the "Run Report" button.

![image](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXf26e7t35HS1ecDNWM9MZnRF71Fr0nDH1dd6HdD9iXBI4nVMsnbBqgqrFWWNkuDbKa2alvgpMQmBev0Auh0c3L2DGaw6xohxwyurOdXOwNZ9b57kpty7NY-O5MVtDRR3SEan5g9?key=oyTqdY8sVVM24bwjUs-ehWqK)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2ZVimage.png)

Step 7: Download the report and upload it to a google Sheet

Step 8: Select all the data and create a Pivot Table

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/J88image.png)

Step 9: Build the pivot table. We will build it like this:

- Set Placement as rows
- Under values, we should put:

  - SUM of Spend
  - SUM of 7-Day Total Sales
  - SUM of Impressions
  - SUM of clicks
  - SUM of 7 Days Total Orders (#)

This way, we can build some KPIs by placement such as

- ACOS = Spend/Sales
- CTR = Clicks/Impressions
- CVR = 7 Days Total Orders (#)/Clicks

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6I2image.png)

We will be able to understand how we are performing in terms of ACOS, CTR, and CVR in the different advertising placements.

FAQs

##### **FAQs**

**Steps to Access and Create a Report**

- **Q1:** **How do I access the Sponsored Products Placement Report?**

  - **A1:**

    - Click on the three-line bar at the top left of the Amazon Seller Central homepage.
    - Select "Advertising" and choose "Campaign Manager."
    - Click the "Measurement & Reporting" icon and select "Sponsored Ads Reports."
    - Click the "Create Report" button.
- **Q2:** **What should I choose when creating a report?**

  - **A2:**

    - Choose "Sponsored Products" as the campaign type.
    - Select "Placement" as the report type.
    - Set your preferred time unit and date range for the report.
- **Q3:** **How do I schedule a report?**

  - **A3:** Label your report and schedule the delivery time if necessary. It's recommended to set a recurring monthly report to consistently track campaign performance.
- **Q4:** **How do I generate the report once set up?**

  - **A4:** After setting up the report parameters, click "Run Report" to generate the file.

Building a Pivot Table

- **Q5:** **How do I build a Pivot Table to analyze the report data?**

  - **A5:**

    - Open the generated report and select all the data.
    - Create a Pivot Table using the selected data.
- **Q6:** **How should I set up the Pivot Table?**

  - **A6:**

    - Place "Placement" as the row field.
    - Under the values section, include the following:

      - SUM of Spend
      - SUM of 7-Day Total Sales
      - SUM of Impressions
      - SUM of Clicks
      - SUM of 7 Days Total Orders (#)
- **Q7:** **What key performance indicators (KPIs) can I analyze from the Pivot Table?**

  - **A7:** From the Pivot Table, you can calculate the following KPIs for each placement:

    - **ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sales)** = Spend / Sales
    - **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** = Clicks / Impressions
    - **CVR (Conversion Rate)** = 7 Days Total Orders (#) / Clicks
- **Q8:** **What insights can I gain from these KPIs?**

  - **A8:** By analyzing these KPIs, you can evaluate the performance of your campaigns across different advertising placements. This helps in understanding which placements are generating the most efficient sales, clicks, and conversions, allowing you to optimize your advertising strategy accordingly.
