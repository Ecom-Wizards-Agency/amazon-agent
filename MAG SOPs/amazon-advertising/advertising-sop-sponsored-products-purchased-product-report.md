# Advertising SOP: Sponsored Products Purchased Product Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-sponsored-products-purchased-product-report

## **Sponsored Products Purchased Product Report**

**Who is this for:**This SOP is intended for marketers, advertising specialists, and e-commerce professionals who are responsible for managing and optimizing advertising campaigns for products on Amazon. By following this SOP, these individuals can gain a better understanding of how to leverage Sponsored Products Purchased Product Reports to improve their advertising strategies and drive more sales for their products.

**Objective:** To provide a step-by-step guide for generating a Sponsored Products Purchased Product Report.

**STEPS:**

**Step 1:** Access the Campaign Manager

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/26.jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zapimage.png)

**Step 2:** Click on the "Measurement & Reporting" icon from the left side of the page and then select "Sponsored ads reports".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/23.jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ZcBimage.png)

**Step 3:** Click on the "Create report" button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/24.jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/BL8image.png)

**Step 4:** Select "Sponsored Products" as the Campaign type, and choose "Purchased product" as the report type. You can generate a CSV file for download that will contain all campaigns, depending on the time unit and date range you choose.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/29(1).jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pYZimage.png)

You can label your report pull in this step and schedule the time of delivery. It's recommended to schedule a recurring monthly report for regular updates on converting keywords in the campaigns.

**Step 5:** Click on the Run Report button to generate the report. Download the report and upload it to a Google Sheet

**Step 6:** Select all the data and create a Pivot Table

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/30.jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/xBHimage.png)

**Step 7:** Build the pivot table. We will build it like this:

- Set Targeting as rows
- Set Advertised ASIN as rows
- Set SUM of 7-Day Other SKU Sales as values
- Set SUM of 7-Day Other SKU Orders as values

This way, we will be able to identify how many orders are receiving each target depending on the advertised ASIN.

FAQs

##### **FAQs**

Generating the Report

- **Q1: How do I access the Sponsored Products Purchased Product Report?**

  - **A1:**Navigate to the **Campaign Manager** in Amazon Seller Central.
  - Click the **Measurement & Reporting** icon on the left side and select **Sponsored Ads Reports**.
- **Q2:****What options should I select when creating the report?**

  - **A2:**Choose **Sponsored Products** as the campaign type.
  - Select **Purchased Product** as the report type.
  - Specify the desired time unit and date range for the report.
- **Q3:****Can I schedule the report for regular updates?**

  - **A3:**Yes, you can schedule a recurring monthly report to stay updated on converting keywords and their performance over time.
- **Q4:****How do I download the report?**

  - **A4:** Click the Run Report button, wait for it to generate, and then download the CSV file for analysis.

Analyzing the Report

- **Q5: How should I prepare the data for analysis?**

  - **A5:**Upload the downloaded CSV file to a Google Sheet.
  - Create a Pivot Table from the data.
- **Q6:****What structure should the Pivot Table have?**

  - **A6: Rows**: Add **Targeting** and **Advertised ASIN**.
  - **Values**: Include the **SUM of 7-Day Other SKU Sales** and **SUM of 7-Day Other SKU Orders**.
- **Q7:****What insights can be gained from the Pivot Table?**

  - **A7:**The Pivot Table helps identify how many orders each target generates for the advertised ASIN. This insight allows you to focus on high-performing targets and optimize or discontinue underperforming ones.

Best Practices

- **Q8: Why is it recommended to schedule recurring reports?**

  - **A8:**Regular reports ensure timely updates on campaign performance, allowing you to adapt strategies based on the most recent data.
- **Q9: How can the insights from this report improve ad campaigns?**

  - **A9:**By identifying top-performing targets and their impact on orders and sales, you can allocate budgets more effectively and refine your targeting strategy for better ROI.
- **Q10: What should I do if some targets underperform?**

  - **A10:**Analyze the context of underperformance—consider adjusting bids, updating keywords, or pausing low-performing targets to improve overall campaign efficiency.
