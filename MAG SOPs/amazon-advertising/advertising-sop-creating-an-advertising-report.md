# Advertising SOP: Creating an Advertising Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-creating-an-advertising-report

## **Creating an Advertising Report**

**Who is this for?**This document is designed to assist Amazon Sellers who require guidance on accessing their Advertising report and viewing their Ad campaigns.

**Objectives:**To utilize the diverse set of tools provided by Amazon and measure the performance of advertising campaigns effectively, obtain valuable insights, and leverage comprehensive ad metrics for promoted products. By leveraging this information, the aim is to enhance marketing and advertising strategies for improved results.

**Instructions:**

1. To generate an Advertising Report, access '**Advertising**' from the general menu of your Seller Central, and then choose '**Campaign Manager**.'

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712332950389.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1EZimage.png)

2. If there's a column you want to include in your report, click the "**Columns**" button to the right of the page.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712332954276.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6U8image.png)

3. Once done, go back to the left side of the same page, then select the **Measurement & Reporting** icon.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712332958194.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/5U2image.png)

4. Then, there's another menu option that will show up. This time, click on the '**Sponsored Ads Report**'

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712332961973.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Z59image.png)

5. Click the "**Create Report**" button to run your Advertising Report.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712332966282.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/HwIimage.png)

6. Under the "**Co****nfiguration**" section on the next page, select the **Campaign Type**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712332971133.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Fe5image.png)

7. From the available drop-down Menu, select the **Report Type**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oG8image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712332990097.png)8. Choose the **Time Unit** you want to use for the report. It's either "Summary" or "Daily."

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RrIimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333002723.png)
9. Select the **Report Period** from the available options.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333007431.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/xTnimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333011251.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0XGimage.png)

10. If you want to change the automatically populated report name, type in your preferred name under the "**Report Settings**" heading.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333015169.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jDmimage.png)

11. Select the **Delivery** option, or schedule recurring deliveries, if required.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333018395.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/AwRimage.png)

12. Click the "**Run Report**" button on the top right side of the page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333022403.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/c5Bimage.png)

13. Wait until the status of your report says '**Completed**' then hit on the download option.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333026708.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Nx3image.png)

14. Take note that you have the option to create a duplicate of the report by selecting "Create Copy" from the "Action" button. If necessary, you can also remove the report entirely by choosing "Delete" from the "Action" button. To return to the previous page and access the reports link again, follow Steps 3 and 4.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333030454.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/XPzimage.png)

15. Download your report by clicking the download icon in the second column of the table under the column title "**Download**."
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712333035453.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/TDwimage.png)

**Additional Note:**Clicking on the Report Name takes you to the details of when the report was created, and you can download the report again from this page by clicking the download icon in the "Download" column on the far right side of your report table.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/V9pimage.png)

FAQs

##### **FAQs**

Generating Advertising Reports

- **Q1: How do I access the Advertising Report section in Seller Central?**

  - **A1:** Navigate to the "Advertising" section in the general menu of your Seller Central account, then select "Campaign Manager."
- **Q2: How can I include specific columns in my report?**

  - **A2:** Click the "Columns" button on the right side of the Campaign Manager page to select the columns you want in your report.
- **Q3: Where do I find the option to create an Advertising Report?**

  - **A3:** Select the "Measurement & Reporting" icon on the left side of the page, then choose "Sponsored Ads Report" from the subsequent menu.

Configuring and Running Reports

- **Q4:** **What options are available under the "Configuration" section when creating a report?**

  - **A4:** You can configure the following:

    - Campaign Type
    - Report Type (selected from a dropdown menu)
    - Time Unit (either "Summary" or "Daily")
    - Report Period
- **Q5: Can I customize the report name?**

  - **A5:** Yes, under "Report Settings," you can type in your preferred name instead of using the default report name.
- **Q6: How do I schedule recurring report deliveries?**

  - **A6:** Use the "Delivery" option to schedule recurring deliveries if required.

Managing Completed Reports

- **Q7:** How do I know when my report is ready?

  - **A7:** Once the report status changes to "Completed," it will be available for download.
- **Q8:** How can I download my completed report?

  - **A8:** Click the download icon in the "Download" column of the table or in the report details page under the "Download" column.
- **Q9:** What actions can I perform on existing reports?

  - **A9:** You can:

  - Duplicate the report by selecting "Create Copy" from the "Action" button.
  - Delete the report by choosing "Delete" from the "Action" button.
- **Q10:** Can I access the report details after it's created?

  - **A10:** Yes, clicking on the Report Name will show the details of when the report was created. From this page, you can also re-download the report.
