# Quick Reference Guide: Uploading Keywords and Bids via File in PPC Campaigns

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/quick-reference-guide-uploading-keywords-and-bids-via-file-in-ppc-campaigns

## **Uploading Keywords and Bids via File in PPC Campaigns**

**Who is this for:**This guide is for PPC specialists and campaign managers who want to streamline the process of uploading multiple keywords and bids into their campaigns.

**Objective:**This guide shows how to quickly upload keywords and bids to a PPC campaign using an Excel file instead of manually entering them one by one.

**Steps:**

1. During campaign setup, locate the **Upload** option (found in the **Products** section).

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/qrBimage.png)

1. Click **Download the CSV Template** to get a predefined file format, or **use your own Excel file**.

  - The required columns in the file are: **Keyword**, **Match Type**, and **Bid**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yg8image.png)

1. In the Excel file, list the following:

  - **Keyword**: The keyword you want to target.
  - **Match Type**: Choose the match type (e.g., Exact, Phrase).
  - **Bid**: Enter the desired bid for the keyword.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PWFimage.png)

1. Once the file is ready, save it.
2. In the Product section during campaign creation, click the **Upload File** button, select your file, and click **Open**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/5Ljimage.png)

1. After uploading, the keywords and bids will be automatically populated in your campaign setup.
