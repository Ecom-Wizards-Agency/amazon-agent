## **STR Keyword and ASIN Harvesting**

**Who is this for:**This SOP is intended for individuals or teams involved in advertising campaigns for products on Amazon. This may include marketers, advertising specialists, and e-commerce professionals.

**Objective:** The objective of the SOP is to provide guidelines for effective keyword and ASIN harvesting to optimize product visibility, increase traffic, and ultimately drive sales.

Finding and adding new keywords and products for targeting from existing campaigns

Keyword and ASIN harvesting is an essential part of optimizing Amazon advertising campaigns. This SOP outlines two methods for harvesting keywords and ASINs: through the Campaign Manager module and the Advertising Reports page.

**Campaign Manager**

1. Go to the Advertising menu and select Campaign Manager.
2. Navigate to the ad group you want to review.
3. Click on the "Search terms" tab to see the actual search queries that customers inputted before clicking on the ad and purchasing the product.
4. Set the date frame to a maximum of 65 days, apply appropriate filters, and review the list of keywords and ASINs. Transfer the relevant ones to manual keyword and product targeting campaigns. The same tab exists for manual targeting campaigns where you can see the Customer's search terms that different keywords triggered depending on their match type.

**Harvesting:**

Open the CSV file and review the headline that shows all important metrics for the performance of your campaigns. One of the columns is labeled **"Customer Search Terms"** and contains the **actual search queries**that the **customer made before clicking on the ad**, or the ASIN code of the last product page that the customer visited before making the purchase. Filter the metrics to find good converting keywords that align with your advertising goals

**Follow these steps to exclude the ASIN Targets so that we don’t have any exact search terms as targets**

1. Click on **Targeting**Column

2. Select Text Filter **“Does not Contain”**

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-keywords-and-asin-harvesting-image10.jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/seBimage.png)

3. Write **asin=”**to exclude the **exact search terms ASINs**

**Follow these steps to get the profitable good search terms to graduate into new campaigns**

1. In **Orders** column apply a filter and “remove 0 & 1 order"
2. In **CVR**column apply a filter to “greater than or equal to 10%"
3. Now we’ve got the right **search terms**to graduate into new campaigns, go ahead and either**create new campaigns**for new harvested search terms or add search terms as targets in **existing campaigns** .
4. To avoid duplicate keywords, please use the [Deduplicator Tool](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-deduplicator-tool) or VLOOKUP before launching

FAQs

Keyword Harvesting through Campaign Manager

- **Q1:** **How do I harvest keywords and ASINs using Campaign Manager?**

  - **A1:**

    - Go to the Advertising menu and select Campaign Manager.
    - Navigate to the desired ad group.
    - Click on the "Search terms" tab to view customer search queries.
    - Set a date range of up to 65 days and apply appropriate filters.
    - Review the list of keywords and ASINs and transfer relevant ones to manual keyword and product targeting campaigns.
- **Q2:** **What kind of search terms can I expect to see in Campaign Manager?**

  - **A2:** You'll see the actual search queries customers entered before clicking on the ad, along with the ASIN code of the product page the customer visited before making a purchase.

Keyword Harvesting through Advertising Reports

- **Q3: How do I create a report to harvest keywords using the Advertising Reports page?**

  - **A3:**

    - Go to Advertising > Campaign Manager > Reports.
    - Click on "Create report."
    - Select Sponsored Products for the Report Category and Search term for the report type.
    - Choose the time unit and date range, label the report, and schedule the delivery time.
    - Click "Run Report."
    - After the report is ready, download the CSV file.
- **Q4:** **How should I review the CSV file to find relevant keywords?**

  - **A4:** Open the CSV file and look for the "Customer Search Terms" column. This contains the actual search queries or ASIN codes. Filter metrics to find converting keywords that align with your campaign goals.

Excluding ASINs and Filtering Profitable Keywords

- **Q5:** **How do I exclude ASINs from the harvested search terms?**

  - **A5:**

    - Click on the "Targeting" column in the CSV file.
    - Apply the Text Filter “Does not Contain.”
    - Write `asin=` to exclude exact ASIN search terms.
- **Q6:** **How do I filter for profitable keywords with a good ACOS?**

  - **A6:** Apply a filter to the Total Advertising Cost of Sales (ACOS) column, setting it to "less than or equal to 35%." A 30-35% ACOS is often the break-even range for products.

Using Harvested Keywords and ASINs

- **Q7: What should I do with profitable search terms once identified?**

  - **A7:**

    - Identify the profitable search terms with a low ACOS.
    - You can either create new campaigns with these harvested search terms or add them to existing campaigns.
    - Upload the updated file with the Bulk sheet uploader to implement the changes.
- **Q8:** **How often should I repeat this keyword and ASIN harvesting process?**

  - **A8:** Regularly repeat this process to stay updated with converting keywords and ASINs that can enhance your advertising campaign performance.
