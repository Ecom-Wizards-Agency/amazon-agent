# Advertising SOP: How to find ASIN-based ACOS and Conversion Rate in Ad Account

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-how-to-find-asin-based-acos-and-conversion-rate-in-ad-account

## **How to find ASIN-based ACOS and Conversion Rate in Ad Account**

**Who is this for?**

- This is for individuals seeking to analyze and optimize the performance of their Amazon Advertising campaigns on an ASIN-by-ASIN basis.

**Objective:**

- The primary goal of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide for individuals looking to assess ASIN-based advertising performance on Amazon. Specifically, it aims to guide users in finding the Advertising Cost of Sales (ACOS) and Conversion Rate for each ASIN in their advertising account.

**Procedure:**

1. Log in to Seller Central and locate the **Campaign Manager**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RNZimage.png)

1. Once in the Campaign Manager dashboard, click on**Products** tab.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hs3image.png)

1. In the Products Tab, each product is listed individually with its corresponding ASIN and product performance.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gWVimage.png)

1. If the Conversion Rate and ACOS columns are not visible, customize the columns to add them.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Advertising-SOP-How-to-find-ASIN-based-ACOS-and-Conversion-Rate-in-Ad-Account-4.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/xBUimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gsAimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Advertising-SOP-How-to-find-ASIN-based-ACOS-and-Conversion-Rate-in-Ad-Account-5.png)

1. Once the columns are customized, analyze and record the ACOS and Conversion Rate for each ASIN. Review the ACOS of each campaign for each ASIN to identify specific campaign contributions to overall ASIN performance. This will help you understand the effectiveness of your advertising strategy at the product-specific level.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PlQimage.png)

**Conclusion:**

By following these steps, you will be equipped with valuable insights into the performance of your ASIN-based advertising efforts on Amazon. This information will help you identify Bestselling ASINs within your advertising account, strategize your ads game plan, empower you to make informed decisions, and ultimately enhance the overall effectiveness of your Amazon Advertising campaigns.

FAQs

##### **FAQs**

- **Q1: How do I analyze the ACOS and Conversion Rate for each ASIN?**

  - **A1:** Once the columns are visible, review the ACOS and Conversion Rate for each ASIN to understand the performance of each campaign and identify contributions to overall ASIN performance.
- **Q2:** **Why is it important to analyze ACOS and Conversion Rate for each ASIN?**

  - **A2:**Analyzing these metrics helps you understand the effectiveness of your advertising strategy at the product level, identify Bestselling ASINs, and optimize your ads for better results.
