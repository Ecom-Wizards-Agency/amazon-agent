# Advertising SOP: How to Download Advertising Invoice

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-how-to-download-advertising-invoice

## **How to Download Advertising Invoice**

**Who is this for :** This is for individuals who would like to know how to download their advertising invoice on Amazon Seller Central.

**Objective :** This SOP aims to provide a step-by-step guide on how to download your Advertising Invoice to your Amazon Seller Central Account.

**How It Works:**

Step 1: To download an Advertising Invoice, On your Seller Central Home Page click the "Campaign Manager" tab.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-22%20at%204.17.48%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/kOMimage.png)

Step 2: Once you are on the Campaign Manager click the "Administration" icon and select "Sponsored Ads Billing".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-DIF6KGGV.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/M5mimage.png)

- Once you click the "Sponsored Ads Billing" you will be directed to the Sponsored Ads Billing Dashboard. Sponsored Ads Billing Dashboard will show your statement balance, Current billing period, your Payment methods, and your Billing Activities.
- Billing Activity will show you a table of your advertising invoices sorted from the most recent to the oldest. If you want to change the date range or period for the invoice you are looking for, click on the "Custom" button located at the top right of the table.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202025-01-22%20at%204.18.02%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fiWimage.png)

Step 3: To view the details of a specific invoice, click on the invoice number link under the "Invoice number" column.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-KN4OW3TN.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Fevimage.png)

- Once you click the invoice number you will be directed to a page that shows a detailed breakdown of your advertising invoice. This will show you the advertising charge for each portfolio and also for each campaign.

Step 4: Download the advertising invoice. To download the invoice, click on the download icon located to the right of the invoice number. Once you download the file, you will receive a detailed PDF copy of your Sponsored Ads Invoice.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-WS9O3OL7.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gQJimage.png)

FAQs

**FAQs**

- **Q1: What information is available on the Sponsored Ads Billing Dashboard?**

  - **A1:**The Sponsored Ads Billing Dashboard provides:

    - Statement Balance
    - Current Billing Period
    - Payment Methods
    - Billing Activities, which displays a table of your advertising invoices sorted from the most recent to the oldest.

- **Q2: Can I filter the date range for invoices?**

  - **A2:** Yes. To change the date range or period:

    - Click on the Custom button located at the top right of the Billing Activity table.

- **Q3: How can I view the details of a specific invoice?**

  - **A3:** Locate the invoice you want in the Billing Activity table.
  - Click on the invoice number link under the "Invoice" column.
  - You will be directed to a page showing a detailed breakdown of:

    - Advertising charges for each portfolio.
    - Advertising charges for each campaign.

- **Q4: How do I download an advertising invoice?**

  - **A4:** Find the invoice you want to download in the Billing Activity table.
  - Click the download icon to the right of the invoice number.
  - A PDF file of your Sponsored Ads Invoice will be downloaded, containing a detailed breakdown of charges.

- **Q5: What format will the downloaded invoice be in?**

  - **A5:** The invoice will be downloaded as a PDF file.

- **Q6: What details are included in the downloaded invoice?**

  - **A6:** The downloaded invoice includes:

    - A detailed breakdown of your advertising charges.
    - Charges by portfolio and campaign.
