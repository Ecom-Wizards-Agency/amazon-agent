# Advertising SOP: How to Convert Up & Down Bidding Strategy Account to Down Only

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-how-to-convert-up-down-bidding-strategy-account-to-down-only

## **How to Convert Up & Down Bidding Strategy Account to Down Only**

**Who is this for?**
Individuals who need to change the campaigns' bidding strategies from "up and down" to "down only" and adjust the bids according to the new bidding strategy.

**Objective:**
The purpose of this document is to explain how to change the bidding strategy in the whole ad account to down only and adjust the bids accordingly.

**Overview**

1. Download a bulk file
2. Keep a copy of this bulk file as a backup
3. Change the “Bidding Strategy” column to down only
4. Update all bids according to the CPC values
5. Upload your new bulk
6. Monitor the sales the next day and make sure to not lose sales

**Instructions**

1. Download the bulk file for the last 7 or 14 days according to how big the account/spending is on the account. If you think that your account is a small one and 7 days of spending/orders data won’t be enough, then go with 14 days or even 30 days. Make your judgment here.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bxhimage.png)

2. Open the bulk file and filter by bidding strategy

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-how-convert-up-and-down-bidding-strategy-account-to-down-only-image2.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LjJimage.png)

3. Change every "Up & Down" to "Down Only"

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-how-convert-up-and-down-bidding-strategy-account-to-down-only-image3.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2Ufimage.png)

4. Now copy those changed rows and create a new excel file for them. Make sure you add an "update" value to every row in the "Operation" column. Otherwise, you'll see an error when you try to upload the file.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/swQimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/EqWimage.png)

5. Now we successfully changed the bidding strategies. However, now the bids are probably lower than they should be. Here is why; let’s say that we were bidding $1 and with up & down, our CPC was averaging around $1.5. This means that when we convert our bidding strategy to down-only and don't change the bid to at least $1.5, we are adjusting that target from $1.5 to $1.0. We don’t want to do that because this will cause lost sales. So we want to adjust the bids according to the CPC values so we are not missing any opportunity.

6. Filter the old up & down type bids. To do that, you need to extend the bidding type column to targets. The bidding strategy column is normally only available on campaign and ad groups, but we want to find out which bids are being affected by our down-only change. Therefore, extend those newly change down-only rows to the bid level. This will help us to filter those keywords targets in the next step.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/TG2image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-how-convert-up-and-down-bidding-strategy-account-to-down-only-image6.png)

7. Now we can filter the targets that will be affected by our down-only change.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vmXimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-how-convert-up-and-down-bidding-strategy-account-to-down-only-image7.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7XPimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-how-convert-up-and-down-bidding-strategy-account-to-down-only-image8.png)

8. Here are the targets and the CPC values that we need to match.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-how-convert-up-and-down-bidding-strategy-account-to-down-only-image9.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SYxBm4image.png)

9. Match every bid a bit higher or a bit lower than the CPC (+/-10%). We want to make sure the bid is at least at CPC value if the ACOS is not super high. We are not optimizing here, we are just switching the bidding strategy so try not to mind the high ACOS values for now. We will deal with them if our down-only switch is successful.

10. Copy your updated bid rows and paste them to the bulk file that you will be uploading to Amazon.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/advertising-sop-how-convert-up-and-down-bidding-strategy-account-to-down-only-image10.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/slYimage.png)

***Note :****If the bulk upload gives you an error, you can delete the bidding strategy columns for “Keyword” rows.*

*Congratulations, you changed the bids from up and down to down only. NOW, monitor the account tomorrow and make sure we are not losing sales. If we are losing sales, you can always use your backup and go back or use the bulk comparison tool to find out which targets are losing traffic and fix them.*

FAQs

- **Q1: What must be added to avoid upload errors?**

  - **A1:**Add the value "update" to the "Operation" column for all modified rows in the new file.
- **Q2: Why do bids need to be adjusted after changing to "Down Only"?**

  - **A2:**With "Up & Down," average CPC may exceed the initial bid. If bids aren't adjusted to match CPC, the new "Down Only" strategy could result in lower bids, potentially causing lost sales.
- **Q3: How do I adjust the bids after the change?**

  - **A3:**Extend the bidding strategy column to targets, identify affected bids, and adjust them to match CPC values (+/-10%). Ensure the new bid is at least the CPC value unless ACOS is extremely high.
- **Q4: What should be done with the updated bid rows?**

  - **A4:**Copy the updated bid rows and paste them into the bulk file you will upload to Amazon.
- **Q5: What if the bulk upload results in an error?**

  - **A5:**Remove the "Bidding Strategy" column for “Keyword” rows in the file and retry uploading.
- **Q6: What should I do after completing the process?**

  - **A6:**Monitor the account the following day to ensure no significant loss in sales. Use the backup file or bulk comparison tool to identify and fix targets that are losing traffic if necessary.
- **Q7: What precautions should I take before starting the process?**

  - **A7:** Always keep a backup of the bulk file to revert changes if the new strategy impacts performance negatively.
