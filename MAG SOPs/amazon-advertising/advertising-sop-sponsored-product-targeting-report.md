# Advertising SOP: Sponsored Product Targeting Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/amazon-advertising/page/advertising-sop-sponsored-product-targeting-report

## **Sponsored Product Targeting Report**

**Who is this for:**

This SOP is intended for marketers, advertising specialists, and e-commerce professionals who are responsible for managing and optimizing advertising campaigns for products on Amazon. By following this SOP, these individuals can gain a better understanding of how to leverage Sponsored Product Targeting Reports to improve their advertising strategies and drive more sales for their products.

**Objective:**

To provide a step-by-step guide for generating a Sponsored Product Targeting Report.

**STEPS:**

Step 1: Access the Campaign Manager

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-PPSAUBKD.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ZXAimage.png)

Step 2: Click on the "Measurement & Reporting" icon from the left side of the page and then select "Sponsored ads reports".

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/aptimage.png)

Step 3: Click on the "Create report" button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/24.jpg)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/g1Uimage.png)

Step 4: Choose the following options in the report settings:

- Campaign type: Sponsored Products
- Report type: Targeting
- Time unit: Select the appropriate time frame for the report.
- Reporting period: Choose the date range for the report.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-WALG1N5W.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4K0image.png)

The report will now be generated and can be downloaded as a CSV file. The Sponsored Products Targeting Report will provide detailed information about the targeting methods used in your campaigns, including the keywords, ASINs, and product categories that have triggered your ads. This data can help you optimize your campaigns and adjust your targeting strategy to improve performance.

FAQs

##### **FAQs**

- **Q1: How do you navigate to the Sponsored Ads Reports?**

  - **A1:** Click on the "Measurement & Reporting" icon from the left side of the page and then select "Sponsored ads reports."
- **Q2: Which campaign type is relevant for this report?**

  - **A2:** The campaign type relevant for this report is "Sponsored Products."
- **Q3: What report type should you select?**

  - **A3:** You should select the "Targeting" report type.
- **Q4: How should the time frame for the report be selected?**

  - **A4:** Choose the appropriate time unit and reporting period based on the desired date range for the report.
- **Q5: What format is the report generated in?**

  - **A5:** The report is generated in CSV format.
- **Q6: What information does the Sponsored Product Targeting Report provide?**

  - **A6:** The report provides detailed information about targeting methods, including the keywords, ASINs, and product categories that triggered your ads.
- **Q7: How can the data in the report help improve advertising campaigns?**

  - **A7:** The data can help optimize campaigns by adjusting targeting strategies to improve performance.
