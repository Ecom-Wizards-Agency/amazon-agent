# General SOP: How to Switch Tabs While Presenting in Google Meet

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-how-to-switch-tabs-while-presenting-in-google-meet

## **How to Switch Tabs While Presenting in Google Meet**

**Who is this for?**

Applies to all individuals using Google Meet in a web browser (preferably Google Chrome) who must switch between tabs while presenting.

**Objective:**

This SOP outlines the steps to switch to a different tab using the “Share tab instead” feature in Google Meet to ensure smoother, more efficient presentations.

**Prerequisites**

- You must be using **Google Chrome** (other browsers may not support this feature).
- You must already be in a **Google Meet** session.
- You must have at least two browser tabs open.

**Procedure**

**Step 1: Join or Start a Google Meet**

- Go to[https://meet.google.com](https://meet.google.com/) and join or start a meeting.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-how-to-switch-tabs-while-presenting-in-google-meet-image-ckgoh8iq.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-how-to-switch-tabs-while-presenting-in-google-meet-image-ti44cmd8.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4Dpimage.png)

**Step 2: Click “Present Now”**

- At the bottom of the Meet screen, click **“Present now.”**

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/W0Kimage.png)

**Step 3: Choose “A Tab”**

- From the options that appear, select **“A Tab.”**
- A window will appear showing all your open browser tabs.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-how-to-switch-tabs-while-presenting-in-google-meet-image-sdxxdtw5.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/uioimage.png)

**Step 4: Select the Initial Tab**

- Click on the tab you want to share.
- If you are sharing a presentation with an audio, make sure to enable **“Also share tab audio”**
- Click **“Share.”**
- You are now sharing that specific tab in the meeting.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-how-to-switch-tabs-while-presenting-in-google-meet-image-nqmem3cq.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ZPUimage.png)

**Step 5: Switch to Another Tab Using “Share tab instead”**

- Go to the tab you want to switch to.
- At the top of the screen (in that tab), a blue banner will appear that says:

  - *“Stop Sharing or* *Share this tab instead.”*
- Click the **“Share this tab instead”** button.
- The presentation will switch immediately to the new tab.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-how-to-switch-tabs-while-presenting-in-google-meet-image-53crdtba.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/UkYimage.png)

**Step 6: Confirm Switch**

- Verify in the meeting that the new tab is being shared (you may see a **“Sharing this tab to meet.google.com”** indicator).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-how-to-switch-tabs-while-presenting-in-google-meet-image-hnpfaozi.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/D9Jimage.png)

**Tips and Best Practices**

- Close unnecessary tabs to avoid confusion or accidental sharing.
- Mute notifications from messaging apps (e.g., Slack) to prevent interruptions.
- Use tab names or icons to organize content logically before the meeting.

**Troubleshooting**

| Issue | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| “Share tab instead” not appearing | Ensure you're using Google Chrome and sharing a tab, not a window. |
| Audio not sharing | When sharing a tab, check “Share tab audio” at the bottom of the selection window. |
| Others can't see the new tab | Make sure you clicked the blue “Share this tab instead” button. |
