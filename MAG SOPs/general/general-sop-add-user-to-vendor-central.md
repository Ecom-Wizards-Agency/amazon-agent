# General SOP: Add User to Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-add-user-to-vendor-central

## **How to Add User to Vendor Central**

**Who is this for?**This is intended for individuals who will add a user to their Vendor Central.

**Objective:**This document is used to guide Amazon Sellers on how to add a user to their Vendor Central Accounts.

**Instructions**

1. Under Settings, please click “Manage Permissions”.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9mCimage.png)

2. Click “Add another person”

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/WtXimage.png)

3. Please fill out the blanks, and tick all permissions except on banking so we can consult in all areas, as seen on the screenshots below.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Gqnimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/request-vendor-central-invite-image-n5gr3200.png)

4. Click “Send Invitation”

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/request-vendor-central-invite-image-9w87322y.png)

FAQs

Printing Certificates

- **Q1: What is the GS1 Certificate used for?**

  - **A1:** The GS1 Certificate verifies the authenticity and ownership of your GS1 barcode numbers. It is often required by retailers and platforms to confirm compliance.
- **Q2: How can I access the GS1 Certificate?**

  - **A2:** You can log into your GS1 account on their official website, navigate to the appropriate section, and download the certificate directly.
- **Q3: What file format is the GS1 Certificate available in?**

  - **A3:** Typically, the GS1 Certificate is available as a PDF.
- **Q4: Do I need to print a physical copy of the GS1 Certificate?**

  - **A4:** It depends on the requirements of the entity requesting it. In most cases, a digital copy suffices, but some organizations may require a printed version.

Vendor Central Account Management

- **Q1: What permissions should I assign to the new user in Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:** Assign all permissions except for banking permissions. This allows the user to consult in all other areas without granting access to sensitive banking information.
- **Q2: Can I add multiple users to Vendor Central at the same time?**

  - **A2:** Yes, you can add multiple users. You will need to repeat the "Add another person" process for each user.
- **Q3: How will the new user receive access to Vendor Central?**

  - **A3:** Once you click “Send Invitation,” the new user will receive an email with instructions to set up their account and access Vendor Central.
- **Q4: Can I revoke or change permissions for a user after adding them?**

  - **A4:** Yes, you can manage user permissions under the “Manage Permissions” section in Vendor Central at any time.
- **Q5: What should I do if the invitation email is not received?**

  - **A5:** Check the recipient’s spam/junk folder. If the issue persists, verify the email address entered and resend the invitation.
