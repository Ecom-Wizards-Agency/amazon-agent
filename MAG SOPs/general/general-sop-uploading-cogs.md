# General SOP: Uploading COGs

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-uploading-cogs

## **Uploading COGs**

**Who is this for:** This SOP is for Amazon sellers who need to upload their Cost of Goods (COGS) for accurate tracking and reporting of profitability.

**Objective:** To provide a step-by-step SOP for Amazon sellers on how to upload Cost of Goods (COGS) to ensure accurate tracking and reporting of profitability.

**Steps:**

1. Go to the Profitability tab on the Merchant Spring account and click on "Product Profit".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1724188926824.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fRpimage.png)
2. Select "Download Template". The COGS Template will include all active SKUs.
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1724188976574.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/HfVimage.png)
3. Fill out the CSV file with the appropriate COGS and ShippingCost values for each SKU.
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1724189258686.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6qcimage.png)
4. Make sure to only make changes on the "cogs" and "ShippingCost" columns. Only input numbers. "ShippingCost" will only be for FBM products. If you accidentally input an amount for an FBA product, it will be disregarded by the system.
5. When uploading the COGS file, you'll be asked to select an effective date. This would indicate that the COGS file you're uploading will be applied from that date onwards.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1724189464578.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9Aaimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1724189492502.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6Cnimage.png)
6. You can select a past date as the effective date.
7. If you select today's date as the effective date, the COGS will only be applied from today onwards and won't show up in historical reports.

By following these steps, Amazon sellers can ensure accurate tracking and reporting of their profitability by uploading their Cost of Goods (COGS).

FAQs

Access and Navigation

- **Q1: Where can I upload my Cost of Goods (COGS)?**

  - **A1:** You can upload your COGS in the “Profitability” tab under the “Product Profit” section of your Merchant Spring account.
- **Q2: How do I download the COGS template?**

  - **A2:** Click on “Download Template” in the “Product Profit” section. The downloaded template will include all active SKUs.

Editing the COGS Template

- **Q3: What columns can I edit in the COGS template?**

  - **A3:** You can edit only the “cogs” and “ShippingCost” columns. Do not make changes to any other columns.
- **Q4: Can I enter text in the “cogs” or “ShippingCost” columns?**

  - **A4:** No, only numerical values should be entered in the “cogs” and “ShippingCost” columns.
- **Q5: Should I add shipping costs for FBA products?**

  - **A5:** No, the “ShippingCost” column is only for FBM (Fulfilled by Merchant) products. Any amounts entered for FBA products will be disregarded by the system.

Uploading the Template

- **Q6: What is the purpose of selecting an effective date when uploading the COGS file?**

  - **A6:** The effective date determines when the uploaded COGS values will start being applied.
- **Q7: Can I select a past date as the effective date?**

  - **A7:** Yes, you can select a past date, and the system will apply the uploaded COGS to historical data from that date onwards.
- **Q8: What happens if I select today’s date as the effective date?**

  - **A8:** If you select today’s date, the COGS will only apply from today onwards and won’t appear in historical reports.

Troubleshooting

- **Q9: What should I do if I accidentally edit columns other than “cogs” or “ShippingCost”?**

  - **A9:** Redownload the template, re-enter the COGS and ShippingCost values, and ensure you only make changes to the designated columns.
- **Q10: What if the system rejects my uploaded file?**

  - **A10:** Check that your CSV file only contains numerical values in the “cogs” and “ShippingCost” columns and that no other columns were modified. If the issue persists, contact the support team for assistance.
