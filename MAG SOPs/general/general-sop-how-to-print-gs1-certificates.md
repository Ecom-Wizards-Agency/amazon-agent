# General SOP: How to Print GS1 Certificates

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-how-to-print-gs1-certificates

## **How to Print GS1 Certificates**

**Who is this for :** To individuals who need to print GS1 Certificates for reference purposes.

**Objective :** The purpose of this document is to help individuals on printing their GS1 Certificates

**Instructions**

1. To print your GS1 Company Prefix or GS1 US GTIN certificate, first, make sure you have Adobe Acrobat Reader installed on your computer:
2. Visit the home page of [GS1 US Data Hub](https://www.gs1us.org/tools/gs1-us-data-hub), login, and click the Print Identifier Certificate link under the "Identifier Tools" area:

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/5QMimage.png)

3. The myGS1 (US) page displays. Click the header "Identifier Certificates."

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/EWNimage.png)

4. You can search for a specific GS1 Company Prefix or GS1 US GTIN if you have multiple prefixes and identifiers. When you see the identifier in the results, click the "Download" link to open the certificate as a PDF file in Adobe Acrobat Reader.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/J8eimage.png)

5. Save the file to your computer for future reference.

FAQs

**1. Why do I need a GS1 Certificate?**

- A GS1 Certificate proves that your company owns the GS1-issued barcodes (GTINs or UPCs). This is often required for:

  - Amazon Brand Registry and GTIN exemptions.
  - Retailers who need verification of barcode ownership.
  - Internal record-keeping for compliance.

**2. Does every GS1 user have access to a GS1 Certificate?**

- Only companies that have purchased a GS1 Company Prefix or individual GS1 GTINs can download a certificate. If you received barcodes through a third party, you may not have access.

**3. What information is included in a GS1 Certificate?**

- A GS1 Certificate contains:

  - The company’s registered name.
  - The GS1 Company Prefix or individual GTINs.
  - The date of issuance.
  - Verification that the barcodes were obtained directly from GS1.

**4. What should I do if the "Print Identifier Certificate" link is missing?**

- If you do not see the "Print Identifier Certificate" link in GS1 Data Hub:

  - Ensure you are logged in with the correct GS1 account.
  - Verify that your GS1 membership is active and in good standing.
  - If the link is still missing, contact GS1 US Support for assistance.

**5. What if I cannot download or open my GS1 Certificate?**

- Ensure you have Adobe Acrobat Reader installed to open the PDF file.
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
- Disable pop-up blockers, as they may prevent the download.
- If the issue persists, clear cache and cookies or try a different device.

**6. What if I forgot my GS1 login credentials?**

- Click "Forgot Password" on the GS1 US login page to reset your credentials.
- If you no longer have access to the registered email, contact GS1 US Support to update account details.

**7. Do GS1 Certificates expire or need to be renewed?**

- GS1 Certificates do not expire, but your GS1 membership must remain active.
- If your membership expires, you may lose access to the certificate and your barcodes could become invalid.

**8. Is a GS1 Certificate required for Amazon Brand Registry?**

- Yes, Amazon often requires a GS1 Certificate to verify barcode ownership for:

  - Brand Registry applications.
  - GTIN exemptions (to prove that barcodes are registered to your brand).
  - If Amazon rejects your certificate, ensure it matches your Seller Central account's business name.
