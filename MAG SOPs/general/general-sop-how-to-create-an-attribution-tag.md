# General SOP: How to Create an Attribution Tag

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-how-to-create-an-attribution-tag

## **How to Create an Attribution Tag**

**Who is this for :** For Individuals who need to create and monitor their attribution tag

**Objective:** To provide step-by-step instructions for Amazon users on how to create and monitor Amazon attribution.

**What is Amazon attribution Tag :** Amazon Attribution is an advertising and analytics measurement solution that gives marketers insight into how their non-Amazon marketing channels perform on Amazon.

**Steps:**

1. Go to Amazon Seller Central and access the Campaign Manager from the drop-down Menu.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vL1image.png)

2. Once on the Campaign Manager page, Click on the “Measurement and Reporting” tab on the left side of the page then click on Amazon Attribution.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/nxSimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/xlximage.png)

3. On the Amazon attribution page, you’ll see if they have existing Amazon attribution campaigns and their metrics. To create a new campaign click on “Create new campaign”

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RD9image.png)

4. To create a campaign manually which is much simpler choose **“Create Manually”**

5. At the bottom of the page, 3 sections will pop up which are campaign settings, Products, and Ad group.

6. For the Campaign name, input your desired campaign name “Note: Naming is very important as we can create several attributions for the same ASIN especially when you are working with multiple 3rd parties advertising platforms such as TikTok, Facebook, Youtube, Instagram, and all other social media platforms. (For example, you want to create an attribution tag for an IG account, and you have a tik tok account that you want to use an attribution tag. You need to create a separate Attribution/Campaign for both to separately monitor the performance of your ASIN from 2 different platforms.) For this example, I am naming it “Age Of Sage - IG - B0BGQDQ7GW”

7. On the products section select the product that you want to be included in the advertisement.

- **IMPORTANT : When Creating the attribution links its important to include more than just one product. Attribution link tracks the purchase of the products included in this section, and sometimes people end up buying a different one that we intended.**

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/general-sop-how-to-create-an-attribution-tag(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7Aeimage.png)

8. For the ad group name simply name it with your desired name, ad group name could be very helpful when you are working with multiple 3rd party advertising accounts and influencers on the same social media platform. In this example, I'll simply name it “AOS - IG - .B0BGQDQ7GW”

9. For Publisher Dropdown - If you cannot find the publisher from the list, simply choose “NEW” and enter the name of the publisher. For this example, we’ll choose IG(Instagram)

10. Channel - Select the appropriate channel for the attribution tag. (Video, Social, Email, Display, and search) In this case, we’ll choose Social.

11. Click-Through URL - this is the destination URL of the ad when a user clicks through. To get this simply go to the **child ASIN** that you want to advertise. (Important: The link should always start with https://). Just copy and paste the link to the space provided for Click-Through URL.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/UIfimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jCGimage.png)

12. Once everything is filled out you can either click on “create” to create the campaign/Amazon attribution. Or add a new ad group if you are looking to create multiple ad groups at a time.

13. Once Created it will provide you with a receipt notification and the Attribution Tag/Link which you can use on your social media posts and other advertising outside Amazon.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Yomimage.png)

You can provide this link to your clients and can advise them to use link-shortener apps such as Bitly.

To monitor the performance of each attribution tag, you can simply go back to the attribution manager>Click on the Campaign name to see each group’s individual metrics.

FAQs

General

- **Q1: What is an Amazon Attribution Tag?**

  - **A1:** Amazon Attribution is an advertising and analytics measurement solution that provides marketers with insights into how their non-Amazon marketing channels perform on Amazon.

Steps and Procedures

- **Q2: Where can I access the Amazon Attribution tool?**

  - **A2:** You can access the Amazon Attribution tool by going to Amazon Seller Central, navigating to the Campaign Manager from the drop-down menu, and then selecting the “Measurement and Reporting” tab on the left side of the page.
- **Q3: What are the key sections required when creating an Amazon Attribution campaign manually?**

  - **A3:** When creating an attribution campaign manually, the three main sections required are:

    - Campaign settings (e.g., Campaign name).
    - Products (select the products to be advertised).
    - Ad Group (name the group to help organize your campaigns).
- **Q4: Why is naming an attribution campaign important?**

  - **A4:** Naming is crucial because multiple attributions may be created for the same ASIN, especially when using multiple third-party platforms like TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube. Using descriptive names helps monitor the performance of each platform separately.

Tips for Optimizing Attribution Tags

- **Q5: Should I include only one product when creating an attribution link?**

  - **A5:** No, it is recommended to include more than one product when creating the attribution link. This ensures that if a customer purchases a different product than the one intended, it will still be tracked.
- **Q6: What should I do if I can’t find the desired publisher in the dropdown menu?**

  - **A6:** If the desired publisher is not listed, choose “NEW” from the publisher dropdown menu and manually enter the publisher's name.
- **Q7: How do I choose the right channel for an attribution tag?**

  - **A7:** Select the channel based on the type of ad. Options include Video, Social, Email, Display, and Search. For example, if you're running a campaign on Instagram, choose "Social."

Monitoring and Adjustments

- **Q8: How can I monitor the performance of my attribution tags?**

  - **A8:** To monitor performance, go to the Attribution Manager, click on the campaign name, and review individual metrics for each ad group.
- **Q9: Can I create multiple ad groups within a single attribution campaign?**

  - **A9:** Yes, you can add multiple ad groups at the time of campaign creation to manage and track performance across different publishers or platforms.

Best Practices

- **Q10: Why should I use a link shortener for attribution tags?**

  - **A10:** Using link-shortening apps like Bitly makes the attribution tag more user-friendly and easier to share on social media or other marketing channels.
- **Q11: What is the importance of the "Click-Through URL" in the attribution tag?**

  - **A11:** The Click-Through URL is the destination link of the ad when users click through. It should always begin with "https://," and the correct URL must be copied from the child ASIN you intend to advertise.
