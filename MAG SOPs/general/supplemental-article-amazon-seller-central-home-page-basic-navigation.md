## **Amazon Seller Central Home Page Basic Navigation and Shortcuts**

**Who is this for:** For Specialist or anyone new to Amazon Seller Central dashboard.

**Objective:** To familiarize with Seller Central homepage navigation, shortcuts and basic functions, the end part is mostly a surprise that most specialist either didn’t know or ignores.

**Utilizing these useful dashboard features can make account management and account health monitoring multiple folds easier.**

[**HOMEPAGE**](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/home)**:**

- Default landing page upon account selection.
- Provides minimal but important metrics pertaining to the account through card widgets and shortcut links to each attribute pages
- Immediately provides priority actions to take including suppression and deactivation warnings
- Main navigation console

- Language Selection

**Main Menu**

Opens left tray which holds hierarchical structured shortcuts categorized accordingly to purpose. Note that Amazon changes the groupings from time to time:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/amazon-seller-central-home-page-basic-navigation-main-menu.gif)![amazon-seller-central-home-page-basic-navigation-main-menu.gif](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/amazon-seller-central-home-page-basic-navigation-main-menu.gif)

- **Catalog** - Links to Listing Attributes and Documents Addition, Updating and Uploads including images, videos and documents
- **Inventory** - Links to Manage Inventory, FBA and shipments dashboards
- **Pricing** - Links to pricing management and automation options
- **Orders** - Links to manage orders FBA and FBM, order reports, returns and SAFE-T claims
- **Advertising** - Links to Campaign Manager (Ideally open this with new tab as it loads to campaign console), A+ Content Manager, Creator connections, coupons, deals and promotions consoles.
- **Stores** - Link to Brand Store management and insights (this also opens from campaigns console)
- **Growth** - Links to different Amazon suggested growth programs
- **Reports** - Primary link to different report types
- **Payments** - Links to payment dashboard
- **Performance** - Links to Account Health and Performance Contributing Factor dashboards
- **Apps and Services** - Links to Amazon recommended 3rd party Apps
- **B2B**- Links to Business to Business central, dashboard and opportunities
- **Brands** - Links to brand registered exclusive features and analytics
- **Learn** - Links to Seller university, Forums and News

- Widget card that shows Global marketplace current-day-so-far Sales with simple line graph.
- When expanded there is an option to toggle between table or graph showing each marketplace sales.
- Shortcut link to current selected marketplace Business Report by clicking “Go to Sales Dashboard”
- Alternatively shortcut link to any other available marketplaces dashboard by clicking the value either of each respective “Order product sales” or “Units” without the need to use Account and Marketplace Selection.

FAQs

General Navigation

- **Q1: What is the default landing page after logging into Amazon Seller Central?**

  - **A1:**The default landing page is the **Homepage**, which provides key metrics through card widgets, shortcut links, and priority action alerts such as suppression and deactivation warnings.
- **Q2: How do I quickly return to the Seller Central Homepage?**

  - **A2:** Click the **Seller Central Home Button** on the main bar to redirect to the homepage dashboard.

Tools and Shortcuts

- **Q3: How do I use the Search Tool effectively?**

  - **A3:**The Search Tool allows you to navigate Seller Central for dashboards, reports, articles, or tools. You can search for less accessible features like the ASIN Merge Tool or Reserved FBA Inventory Tool.
- **Q4: How can I customize the Favorites/Bookmark bar?**

  - **A4:**Hover over a menu option, click the bookmark icon that appears on the right, and the option will be added to the Favorites bar. You can arrange the favorites in your preferred order.
- **Q5: What is the purpose of the Quick Access Links section?**

  - **A5:** Quick Access Links consolidates shortcuts from the main menu in one view, allowing you to middle-click or control-click links to open them in a new tab for faster navigation.

Metrics and Widgets

- **Q6: How can I view sales metrics across marketplaces without switching accounts?**

  - **A6:** Use the **Sales Card** widget on the homepage. Expand it to toggle between table and graph views, or middle-click individual marketplace values to open each in a new tab.
- **Q7: What does the Account Health Widget Card display?**

  - **A7:** This widget shows the account health score or status for each marketplace and provides quick links to respective account health dashboards.
- **Q8: What is the difference between the Buyer Messages Card and Global Marketplace Messages?**

  - **A8:** The **Buyer Messages Card** shows a per-marketplace summary of shopper messages with SLA breakdowns and direct links. The **Global Marketplace Messages** link provides a collective view of messages from all marketplaces.

Actions and Recommendations

- **Q9: What are Actions Indicators, and why are they important?**

  - **A9:** Actions Indicators prioritize outstanding or upcoming actions critical to account health, such as pending shipments, listing issues, and deactivation risks.
- **Q10: What is the purpose of Recommended Actions?**

  - **A10:**Recommended Actions include widgets with Amazon's suggestions for growth programs and features that enhance account health and performance.

Tips and Bonus Features

- **Q11: How can I access the User-Friendly Help Hub?**

  - **A11:** Switch to a marketplace other than the US or Canada (e.g., Mexico), scroll to the homepage bottom, and click on "Help" to access the User-Friendly Help Hub.
- **Q12: Where can I find tools to suggest changes to my product detail page?**

  - **A12:**Use the **Suggest Changes to Your Product Detail Page** tool, available through Amazon's Help resources.
- **Q13: How do I manage global promotions sales?**

  - **A13:** Use the **Global Promotions Card**, which shows sales data from promotion tools like Coupons and Deals. This widget only links to the selected marketplace’s Coupons and Deals Dashboard.
