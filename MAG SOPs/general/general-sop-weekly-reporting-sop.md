# General SOP: Weekly Reporting SOP

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-weekly-reporting-sop

## **Weekly Reporting SOP**

**Who is this for?**: For Amazon sellers or team members who are responsible for preparing weekly reports to monitor their business performance and make informed decisions based on the insights provided.

**Objective**: To ensure consistent and effective reporting that helps them understand their performance metrics and make informed decisions based on the insights provided in the weekly report.

**Instructions:**

1. Create a Google sheet with base fields as shown below or click this [file](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1UE5OdOWOtLJkkGgu5G-_OcJIIq_zkatz/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=117170874180750576810&rtpof=true&sd=true).![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688141515999.png)

[Client Name - Weekly Sales and Adverstising Report.xlsx](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/attachments/26)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/r5Dimage.png)

2. If this is the first time you are creating a weekly report, provide the data from the previous 4 weeks. The date range should be from Sunday to Saturday. Analyzing this data will help you identify patterns and trends in the performance metrics. See the sample weekly dates below:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688142250244.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fLqimage.png)

3. Insert a new column to the left by right-clicking on the column with the most recent date.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688062109135.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/OKIimage.png)

4. Provide the date for the new column corresponding to the current week you will be reporting on. In this example, I have dated the new column with the week 06/11-06/17.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688062391524.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/scaled-1680-/lVgimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/lVgimage.png)

5. Now we can fill in the chart for the new column. All of these stats can be found on the Seller Central account.

- From the Seller Central homepage, select the three-line bar on the left side and hover over "**Reports**." Then, click on "**Business Reports.**"
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688061613802.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Njeimage.png)
- Click on “**Sales and Traffic**” on the left-hand side of the page.
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688061735858.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Y6Qimage.png)
- Change the date range to the week you will be reporting on. In the example, we will be reporting on 06/11-06/17 so we need to change the date range on the sales and traffic report to match 06/11-06/17.
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688063200736.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/CPdimage.png)
- Get the data of **Revenue**, **Sessions**, **Unit Session Percentage** and **Units Sold**. Then paste it into the weekly reporting sheet.

  **Revenue: *Ordered Product Sales*
  Sessions: *Sessions Total*
  Unit Session Percentage: *Unit Session Percentage*
  Units Sold: *Units Ordered***
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688063319152.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/F5mimage.png)
- [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/scaled-1680-/fDVimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/fDVimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688063360070.png)
- To find **Ad Spend**, **Ad Revenue**, **Impressions**, **CTR**and **ACoS**, hover over “**Advertising**” and click on “**Campaign Manager**”.
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688063514431.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pAyimage.png)
- This will display the advertising stats. Ensure to adjust the date range to match the week you are currently working on. In the provided example, the week is 06/11-06/17, so you should update it to 06/11-06/17 and apply the changes.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688063861214.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ch3image.png)

- Now that the date range has been changed we can copy and paste the **Ad Spend**, **Ad Revenue**, **Impressions**, **CTR**and **ACoS**onto the weekly reporting sheet.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688064111779.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/j7timage.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/scaled-1680-/SV8image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/SV8image.png)

- Now you need to calculate the True ACoS. This is done by dividing the Ad Spend by the Revenue.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/scaled-1680-/bhSimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/bhSimage.png)

6. Once complete, the table will look like this.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688143550492.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/scaled-1680-/9P8image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2026-02/9P8image.png)
