# General SOP: How to do a Viral Launch

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-how-to-do-a-viral-launch

## **How to do a Viral Launch**

**Who is this for:**This is intended for Amazon sellers who want to increase their product rankings and sales by conducting a viral launch.

**Objective:** To outline the steps required to conduct a viral launch for a product, including setting up a promotion, monitoring stock levels and sales, and ensuring compliance with Amazon's policies.

Here are some best practices to consider when doing a Viral Launch

**Competitive Pricing**

Create a 90% off promotion. Check [Creating a Percentage Off Promotion SOP](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-creating-a-percentage-off-promotion) for the complete steps and guidelines. When you set up the promotion, consider setting a "Max Order Quantity" directly from within your Seller Central account.

1. Go to Inventory and then select Manage All Inventory

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684772168198.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bZKimage.png)

2. Find the ASIN where the promotion is created. Click Edit.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/step%205%20viral%20launch.jpg?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4dsimage.png)

3. Go to Offer tab

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684772284460.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/B09image.png)

4. Find the Max Order Quantity attribute and put 1 on its box. If there are duplicates, change both to 1 for the duration of the Viral Launch.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684772532470.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KZRimage.png)

5. Click Save and finish.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684772849730.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/tlqimage.png)

6. Monitor stock levels daily during the promotion. Once the promotion end change the MFN Max Order Quantity back to the original.

**Ensuring full listing optimization and readiness**

1. Product Title: Create a clear product title that includes relevant keywords.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687338105728.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ldgimage.png)

2. Product Features and Descriptions: Write compelling and informative bullet points and product descriptions. Highlight the key features, benefits, and specifications of your product. Use clear language, proper formatting, and bullet points for easy readability. Incorporate relevant keywords naturally, but avoid keyword stuffing.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/muIUr3image.png)

3. Product Images: Use high-quality product images that showcase your product from multiple angles. Follow Amazon's image guidelines, such as using a white background and adhering to the specified dimensions and file formats. Include lifestyle images if possible, demonstrating the product's use or benefits.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687338360068.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/isCimage.png)

4. Backend Search Terms: Utilize the backend search terms section to include additional relevant keywords that may not fit in the visible listing content. These keywords help improve your product's discoverability in Amazon's search results. Be specific, don't use a trademark or brand name. and maximize the 250 characters.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687338394765.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Zbgimage.png)

5. Product Variation Listings: If your product has variations (e.g., size, color), create separate listings for each variation. Optimize each variation listing with specific titles, images, and descriptions that accurately reflect the respective product variant.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687338640550.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oiiimage.png)

6. Enhanced Brand Content or A+ Content: If you have brand registry and access to Enhanced Brand Content (EBC) or A+ Content, take advantage of this feature to create more visually appealing and detailed product descriptions. Use engaging multimedia, including videos and comparison charts, to enhance your listing.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687338794225.png?sv=2022-11-02&spr=https&st=2025-01-16T19%3A54%3A01Z&se=2025-01-16T20%3A04%3A01Z&sr=c&sp=r&sig=XxfjkZtMJOhAZ7PxRYw7pYXKgHcWLbc72SmugN9U0Io%3D)

**Promotions and social media launches**

- Social Media Promo Code: Select the social media platform(s) where you want to promote the code. Consider your target audience and which platforms they are most active on.
- Advertise the Promo Code: Publish your content on the chosen social media platform(s) and include the promo code in the caption or graphic. Encourage users to share, tag friends, or comment to increase engagement and reach.

**Advertising best practices**

1. Keyword Research: Conduct thorough keyword research to identify relevant and high-performing keywords for your products. Use tools like Amazon's Keyword Tool or third-party keyword research tools to discover keywords that have high search volume and low competition.

2. Targeted Campaigns: Create targeted advertising campaigns based on your goals and product offerings. Use different campaign types, such as Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, and Sponsored Display, to reach different audiences and maximize your visibility.

3. Ad Placement: Monitor and analyze the performance of your ads across different placements (e.g., top of the search, product detail pages) and adjust your bidding strategy accordingly. Focus on placements that generate higher click-through rates (CTRs) and conversions.

4. Bidding Strategy: Set competitive bids for your keywords based on their performance and the value of each click to your business. Test different bidding strategies, such as automatic bidding or manual bidding, to find the most effective approach for your campaigns.

5. Ad Creative: Create engaging and visually appealing ad creative that showcases your product's unique features and benefits. Use high-quality product images and compelling ad copy to capture shoppers' attention and drive clicks.

FAQs

1. What is a Viral Launch, and why is it important for my product?

A Viral Launch is a marketing strategy that helps boost a product's ranking and visibility on Amazon by offering deep discounts and driving traffic through promotions and advertising. It is essential for quickly increasing sales volume and improving the product's ranking on Amazon search results.

2. How do I set up the 90% off promotion for a Viral Launch?

To set up the promotion, navigate to Manage All Inventory in Seller Central, find your ASIN, and adjust the Max Order Quantity under the Offer tab to 1. Make sure to monitor stock levels daily during the promotion. Once the promotion ends, revert the Max Order Quantity back to its original setting.

3. How can I ensure my product listing is optimized before launching?

To optimize your listing, make sure your product title includes relevant keywords, create clear and compelling bullet points and descriptions, use high-quality images (following Amazon's guidelines), and optimize backend search terms. If eligible, use A+ Content for a more engaging product description.

4. What if I don’t have Enhanced Brand Content or A+ Content?

If you don’t have access to Enhanced Brand Content (EBC) or A+ Content, focus on creating a high-quality standard product listing. You can still optimize your title, bullet points, images, and search terms to make the listing attractive and informative for potential customers.

5. How should I advertise my Viral Launch on social media?

For advertising, choose the appropriate social media platforms where your target audience is most active. Share your promo code in posts or graphics and encourage engagement through shares, tags, and comments to boost visibility.

6. How do I ensure my product has the Buy Box during a Viral Launch?

To hold the Buy Box, make sure your product information is accurate (e.g., dimensions, materials, and colors), and your customer service metrics (e.g., feedback score, fulfillment method) meet Amazon’s standards. Incorrect product details can lead to negative reviews and affect Buy Box eligibility.

7. How do I do keyword research for a Viral Launch campaign?

Conduct keyword research using tools like Amazon’s Keyword Tool or third-party tools to identify high-volume, low-competition keywords. Incorporate these keywords into your product listing and ad campaigns to ensure your product is visible to the right audience.

8. How should I set up targeted advertising campaigns for my Viral Launch?

Set up targeted advertising campaigns based on your product goals. Use different Amazon ad types such as Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, and Sponsored Display to reach specific customers. Monitor performance and adjust ad placement, bidding strategy, and creative elements for optimal results.

9. What’s the best bidding strategy for a Viral Launch?

Use a combination of automatic and manual bidding strategies. Start with automatic bidding to gain insight into which keywords and placements perform best, then adjust to manual bidding to optimize spend based on performance.

10. How do I monitor and adjust my campaign performance during the Viral Launch?

Track your campaign performance by analyzing click-through rates (CTR) and conversion rates across different ad placements. Adjust your bids and focus on the most effective placements to increase visibility and conversions.
