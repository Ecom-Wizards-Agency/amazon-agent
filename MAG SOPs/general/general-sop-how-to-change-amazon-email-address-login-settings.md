# General SOP: How to Change Amazon Email Address Login Settings

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-how-to-change-amazon-email-address-login-settings

## **How to Change Amazon Email Address Login Settings**

**Who is this for?**Amazon users who wish to change their email address login settings for their Amazon account.

**Objective:**To provide step-by-step instructions to Amazon users on how to change their email address login settings, ensuring that they can access their Amazon account using their new email address without any issues.

**1.**Go to your Seller Central Account and access your Seller Central account (or the client account, if you're doing this on behalf of a client).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1681139070771.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2oyimage.png)

**First Phase - Submit Ticket to Amazon**

**2.**Once you've logged into Seller Central, locate and click the **"Help"** > " **Get help and resources"**button in the top right-hand corner of the page.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/lQiimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-06-05%20at%2010.43.23%E2%80%AFPM.png)**3.**You will be redirected to this page below. Click "My issue is not listed".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-NSPZ7SH6.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/uJyimage.png)

**4.**Enter your Case Details, then Continue. Be as direct as possible, as longer cases will increase the potential margin for error in your response. The more specific the case filed, the more specific your answer will be.

***Note: If your question is about a specific ASIN, make sure to include that ASIN in the case.***

In this case, since we are trying to update an email address for a Seller's Account we need to let Amazon know that we are making this change so they don't reject it. The following text is sufficient for this case:

*"Hi Support,*

*I would like to let you know that I will be updated the email for this account. Thanks, "*

**5.**Once you're finished filling out the dialogue box, click "Continue."

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-06-05%20at%2010.44.58%E2%80%AFPM(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/NX7image.png)

**6.**Choose "Account Related"

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-06-05%20at%2010.46.13%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2TSimage.png)

The system will prompt you to enter your contact information and this will allow you to receive email updates as the case is submitted and the case progresses. We have rarely seen anyone respond to the case when we select the "Phone" option, so Email is preferable here.

**7.** Enter your email address and then click the "**Send**" button to finish submitting your case.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1681138380132.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KKQimage.png)

**Second Phase - Login Settings 'Change Email'**

Return to your Seller Central main page which can be done by clicking on the Amazon Seller Central logo in the top left part of the page.

**8.**Hover your cursor over "**Settings**" and then click "**Login Settings**."

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1681138488969.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/8voimage.png)

**9.**You will be redirected to the '**Login & Security**' page. Go ahead and hit '**Edit**' inside the box labeled "**Email**"

Clicking on this will take you to the page where you'll enter the new email address.

**10.**From this page, enter the desired '**New email address** and click '**Continue**'

After you enter the email address desired you will be redirected to a page that asks for an OTP. This is a One-Time-Password that will be sent to your new email address. Without leaving this page access your email and find the code.

**11.** Once you have the OTP code, enter it in the box and click the yellow "**Continue**" button.

**12.**Click the "**Continue**" button, if the OTP was correct then you should be good to go.

Your login email should be updated to the **new** email.

FAQs

General Process

- **Q1: What should I do if I cannot find the "Help" > "Get help and resources" button in Seller Central?**

  - **A1:** If the "Help" > "Get help and resources" button is missing, check that you are logged into the correct Seller Central account. If it still doesn’t appear, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. You can also directly access the help section via [Seller Central Help Center](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help).
- **Q2: Can I update my email address without submitting a ticket to Amazon?**

  - **A2:** No, submitting a ticket to Amazon ensures they are aware of the change and helps avoid potential account-related issues or rejections during the email update process.

Case Submission

- **Q3: What details should I include in my case when submitting the ticket to Amazon?**

  - **A3:** Be specific and concise. Mention that you are updating the email address associated with the account, and if applicable, include relevant details such as the ASIN or any supporting context.
- **Q4: Why is email the preferred contact method when submitting the case?**

  - **A4:** Email is preferred because it ensures you receive timely updates and responses from Amazon. The "Phone" option is rarely responded to for such cases.

Technical Issues

- **Q5: What if I don’t receive the OTP (One-Time Password) to my new email address?**

  - **A5:** Check your spam/junk folder and ensure you entered the correct email address. If the OTP is still not received, try resending it. If the issue persists, contact Amazon Support for assistance.
- **Q6: What happens if the OTP I enter is incorrect?**

  - **A6:** You will be prompted to re-enter the OTP. If it continues to fail, ensure you’re using the latest code sent to your email and not an older one.

Account Settings

- **Q7: Can I update my login email address through the Amazon app?**

  - **A7:** No, this process must be completed through the Amazon Seller Central website. The app does not provide access to login settings.
- **Q8: What if I accidentally logged out before completing the email update?**

  - **A8:** Log back into Seller Central using your current email address, navigate to "Settings" > "Login Settings," and restart the email update process.

Post-Update

- **Q9: How can I confirm that my email update was successful?**

  - **A9:** After completing the steps and entering the OTP, log out and attempt to log back in using the new email address. Successful login indicates the email update was successful.
- **Q10: Will changing my email address affect my account settings or order history?**

  - **A10:** No, changing your login email address will not affect your account settings, order history, or any other account data.
