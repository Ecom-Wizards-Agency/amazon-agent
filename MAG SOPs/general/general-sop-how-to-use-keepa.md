# General SOP: How To Use Keepa

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-how-to-use-keepa

## **How To Use Keepa**

**Who is this for?:** Intended for individuals who frequently shop on Amazon and are interested in optimizing their online shopping experience. It is suitable for both casual shoppers and professional resellers who want to track product prices, understand market trends, and utilize price alerts to make well-informed buying decisions.

**Objective:**To provide step-by-step instructions on how to effectively use Keepa, a web-based browser extension, for tracking Amazon product prices, analyzing sales rank trends, and setting price alerts. This SOP aims to help users leverage Keepa's features to make informed purchasing decisions, identify favorable pricing opportunities, and save money.

**Introduction:**

Keepa is a web-based browser extension that serves as an Amazon price tracking tool. It enables users to monitor the prices of millions of products across various Amazon marketplaces. By installing Keepa, you gain the ability to track price fluctuations, observe sales rank trends, and gauge the speed at which products are sold. Additionally, Keepa offers the convenience of setting up price alerts to help you save money when prices drop.

**Step 1: Account Setup**

- Create a Keepa account by visiting the Keepa website (keepa.com)
- Fill in the required information and complete the registration process.
- Verify your email address to activate your Keepa account.

**Step 2: Installing Keepa Browser Extension**

- Open your preferred web browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, etc.).
- Search for "Keepa browser extension" in your browser's search engine.
- Locate the official Keepa extension for your browser and click on the installation link.
- Follow the prompts to add the extension to your browser.
- Once installed, you should see the Keepa icon in your browser's toolbar.

**Step 3: Navigating Keepa's Product Pages**

- Visit an e-commerce website, such as Amazon.
- Search for a product you want to track using Keepa.
- On the product page, you will see a graph and additional information provided by Keepa.
- Analyze the graph to understand the product's price history, sales rank, and other relevant data.
- Scroll down to view additional information and statistics about the product's pricing, availability, and sellers.

**Step 4: Customizing Keepa's Settings**

- Click on the Keepa icon in your browser's toolbar to access the extension.
- Select "Settings" from the drop-down menu.
- Customize the settings according to your preferences.

a. Choose your preferred country and marketplace.

b. Adjust the graph's time range and resolution.

c. Enable or disable specific data points, such as sales rank, price, and offers.

d. Set up notifications for price drops or availability changes.

e. Explore other settings and options available to personalize your experience.

**Step 5: Tracking Products and Setting Price Alerts**

- While on a product page, click on the Keepa icon in your browser's toolbar.
- Select "Add to Tracking" to start monitoring the product.
- Optionally, set a desired price or a target price range to receive price alerts.
- Keepa will track the product's price and notify you when it meets your criteria.
- Access your tracked products by clicking on the Keepa icon and selecting "Tracked Products."

**Step 6: Analyzing Product Trends and Insights**

- Use Keepa's graphs and data to gain insights into product pricing and trends.
- Analyze the price history to identify patterns, seasonal fluctuations, and price trends.
- Compare prices offered by different sellers to find the best deals.
- Utilize Keepa's filters and sorting options to refine your search results and narrow down your product choices.
- Take advantage of Keepa's additional features, such as wishlist synchronization and price history exports.

Note: Keepa's functionality and features may vary over time, so it's advisable to refer to the official Keepa documentation or website for any updates or changes.

FAQs

Account Settings

- **Q1: How do I change the country or marketplace in Keepa?**

  - **A1:** You can change the country or marketplace by clicking on the Keepa icon in your browser’s toolbar, selecting "Settings," and then choosing your preferred country and marketplace under the customization options.

Account Setup

- **Q2: Can I track a product without creating a Keepa account?**

  - **A2:** No, you need to create a Keepa account to track products and receive price alerts. The account enables you to monitor your tracked products across devices.

Tracking Products

- **Q3: How do I remove a product from my tracking list in Keepa?**

  - **A3:** To remove a product, click on the Keepa icon in your browser’s toolbar, go to "Tracked Products," find the product you wish to remove, and click the delete or remove option next to it.

Price Alerts

- **Q4: Can I set up multiple price alerts for the same product in Keepa?**

  - **A4:** Yes, you can set multiple price alerts for a product. Simply select different target prices or price ranges to receive notifications when they meet your criteria.

Product Insights

- **Q5: How do I analyze price history using Keepa?**

  - **A5:** On a product page, view the graph provided by Keepa, which displays the price history over time. You can adjust the time range and resolution for a more detailed analysis of price trends, fluctuations, and patterns.

Limitations

- **Q6: Can Keepa track prices on websites other than Amazon?**

  - **A6:** No, Keepa primarily tracks prices on Amazon. However, it covers multiple Amazon marketplaces, allowing you to track products across different countries.

Product Insights

- **Q7: What is the significance of sales rank data in Keepa's graphs?**

  - **A7:** Sales rank data shows the relative popularity of a product on Amazon, helping you understand how well it sells compared to other products in the same category. Keepa displays this data alongside pricing trends for better purchasing decisions.

Wishlist Features

- **Q8: How can I sync my Keepa wishlist with my Amazon account?**

  - **A8:** To sync your Keepa wishlist with your Amazon account, go to Keepa's settings and enable wishlist synchronization. This feature allows you to easily track items you’ve added to your Amazon wishlist directly in Keepa.

Pricing and Plans

- **Q9: Are there any limitations to the free version of Keepa?**

  - **A9:** The free version of Keepa offers basic functionality, including price tracking and basic alerts. For advanced features, such as extensive data points and historical analysis, you may need to upgrade to a paid plan.

Data Export

- **Q10: How do I export price history data from Keepa?**

  - **A10:** You can export price history data by visiting a product's Keepa page, scrolling to the relevant graph, and clicking on the export option to download the data in a format of your choice.
