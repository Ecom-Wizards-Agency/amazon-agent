# General SOP: Helium 10 Follow-Up Tool

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-helium-10-follow-up-tool

## **Helium 10 Follow-up Tool**

**Who is this for? All individuals who want to use Helium 10 Follow-up tool.**

**Objective:**To provide step-by-step instructions on how to set up automated email deliveries to Amazon buyers using Helium 10's Follow-Up tool.

**What is *Follow-Up*?**

Helium 10’s *Follow-Up* tool is used for setting up automated email deliveries to Amazon buyers. It is set up through a seller’s account on Helium 10 and can be set up with a variety of templates and triggers. One template, for example, can be made to send whenever a buyer’s order is delivered. Another can be set to send a few days after a buyer receives their order, requesting reviews on their experience.

The *Follow-Up*tool’s dashboard looks like this:

- **Blue Icon** - Tells you how many messages have been sent in the currently selected time period.
- ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bv5image.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/helium-10-follow-up-tool-image-z13cordk.png)
- **Yellow Icon** - Tells you how many orders have been shipped in the currently selected time period.
- **Green Icon** - Gives you the percentage of Follow-Up emails that have been opened by buyers in the currently selected time period.
- **Red Icon** - Gives you the percentage of buyers that have opted out of Amazon 3rd party messaging in the currently selected time period.

**Setting Up a *Follow-Up*Automation:**

Navigation icons inside the *Follow-Up*tool are located on the left-hand side of the screen.

**Step 1: Create the email template:**

1. You can either a.) create a brand new template from scratch or b.) use one of the premade templates. If you include a request for a review from the buyer in this email template the premade template is recommended, as Amazon is extremely sensitive to how this request is made.

  - To create a brand new template from scratch, click the New Email Template Icon in the top right corner.
  - To create a template from a premade template, hover your mouse over the desired template and then press “Duplicate Template.” This will create a template that you can then edit.
2. This will open a window where you can edit or create your template. The tone you’re going for in this email should be professional and direct. Avoid informal language and unnecessary graphics or links.
3. On the left-hand side of the template editor, there is a series of Amazon template tags that you can insert into your template. These make it easy to reference the buyer’s product and order, among lots of other options.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/helium-10-follow-up-tool-image-bx9du1dp.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/WAfimage.png)

4. Make sure your message includes a subject line and all the appropriate details. Make sure to always thank the buyer for their order.

5. As you’re wrapping up your template pay close attention to the window on the right labeled “Message Preview.” This window shows you how your message will appear to the buyer reading the email. Make sure all the links are displaying appropriately and that there aren’t any glaring formatting issues.

6. Once you’ve finished, check if there are any adjustments needed.

**Step 2: Setting Up the Automation**

1. Enter the Automation section by clicking the “Automation” icon on the left-hand side of the screen.
2. Like the email template, you can either create a brand new automation with your parameters or select from a list of templates. Automation closely resembles a flowchart
3. Each Automation will have two sections connected at the top: a trigger and a filter list:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/helium-10-follow-up-tool-image-mi4hxbo3.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/FlRimage.png)

4. Each can be changed simply by clicking on the bubble.

5. Trigger options include Email Opened, Order Shipped, Order Delivered, Order Refunded, or Order Returned

6. Filters are useful for making sure the email template only reaches the right customer. A common filter to have on most automation is one that excludes refunded orders.

7. You will add actions to the automation flowchart by clicking the + button underneath the trigger bubble. These action buttons will appear red.

8. For this example, we’re using automation that sends an email template to buyers 7 days after their order is delivered. The finished automation looks like this:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/helium-10-follow-up-tool-image-ga4pf2rr.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4YRimage.png)

9. Always make sure that the correct email template is plugged into the correct automation. Fixing this simply involves going into the outdated automation and changing the Send Mail action to send the correct template.

10. Check back in a day or two to make sure that the automated emails are sent correctly.

FAQs

General Information

- **Q1: What is the purpose of the Helium 10 Follow-Up tool?**

  - **A1:** The Helium 10 Follow-Up tool allows Amazon sellers to set up automated email deliveries to buyers. It can be used for tasks such as confirming order deliveries, requesting reviews, and communicating important order information.
- **Q2: What types of templates can be created with the Follow-Up tool?**

  - **A2:** You can create email templates from scratch or use premade templates. Premade templates are recommended when including review requests, as Amazon is strict about how these are formatted.

Dashboard Functionality

- **Q3: What do the icons on the Follow-Up tool dashboard represent?**

  - **A3:**

    - **Blue Icon:** Number of messages sent in the selected time period.
    - **Yellow Icon:** Number of orders shipped in the selected time period.
    - **Green Icon:** Percentage of Follow-Up emails opened by buyers.
    - **Red Icon:** Percentage of buyers who have opted out of Amazon 3rd-party messaging.

Email Template Creation

- **Q4: How can I create a new email template from scratch?**

  - **A4:** To create a new email template:

    - Click the "New Email Template" icon in the top right corner.
    - Use the editor to draft your template with a professional and direct tone.
    - Add appropriate Amazon template tags for personalization, such as buyer and product details.
    - Ensure the email includes a subject line, expresses gratitude to the buyer, and is formatted correctly.
- **Q5: How can I preview my email template?**

  - **A5:** The "Message Preview" window on the right side of the editor shows how your email will appear to the buyer. Use it to check formatting, links, and overall presentation.

Automation Setup

- **Q6: What is the role of triggers and filters in automation?**

  - **A6:**

    - **Triggers:** Initiate the automation based on specific actions, such as "Order Delivered" or "Order Shipped."
    - **Filters:** Ensure the email is sent to the right customer by excluding scenarios like refunded orders.
- **Q7: How do I set up an automation to send an email after order delivery?**

  - **A7:**

    - Open the "Automation" section and create a new automation.
    - Select "Order Delivered" as the trigger.
    - Add filters, such as excluding refunded orders.
    - Use the "+" button to add actions, such as sending a specific email template.
    - Ensure the correct email template is connected to the automation.

Best Practices and Troubleshooting

- **Q8: How do I ensure compliance with Amazon’s messaging guidelines?**

  - **A8:** Use premade templates when requesting reviews to adhere to Amazon’s strict policies. Avoid informal language, unnecessary graphics, or unauthorized links.
- **Q9: What should I do if automated emails are not being sent?**

  - **A9:** Check the automation setup to ensure:

    - The correct email template is linked.
    - Triggers and filters are configured properly.
    - The automation is activated.
- **Q10: How can I monitor the success of my automated emails?**

  - **A10:** Use the dashboard to track metrics like email open rates (Green Icon) and opt-out rates (Red Icon). Adjust templates or automation settings based on these metrics to improve performance.
