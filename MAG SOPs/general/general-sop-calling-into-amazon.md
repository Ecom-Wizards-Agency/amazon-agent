# General SOP: Calling Into Amazon

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/general-sop-calling-into-amazon

## **Calling Into Amazon**

**Who is this for?** This is for individuals who would like to request a call from Amazon selling partner support.

**Objective:** The objective of this SOP is to provide clear instructions and guidelines to the reader on how to effectively call Amazon Selling Partner Support, ensuring that all necessary information is provided and that the issue is resolved efficiently and in a timely manner. By following the steps outlined in this SOP, the reader will be able to navigate the Amazon Selling Partner Support system with ease and maximize their chances of achieving a successful resolution.

**Follow these steps to call Amazon Selling Partner Support:**

**1.**Login to your Seller Central account and click “Help” on the top right corner

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/G3timage.png)

**2.**Click the "Get help and resources" button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/izMimage.png)

**3.** Select your issue from the list on the landing page in this instance “Title, description, bullets update”.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iGkimage.png)

**4.**Then Select the category for you issue.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/I0Zimage.png)

**Reminder: If "Sponsored Ads and Stores" was selected, please proceed to 6.A to 6.E, otherwise proceed on 5.A - 5.B**

**5.A** Once you have selected the category, put a description of your issue on the dialogue box then click review and submit.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Vplimage.png)

**5.B** Once you have clicked “Review and submit” additional options will appear. Update the “Short description” dialogue box then choose “Phone” tab as your contact method and place your number then click “Call me now”.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jcximage.png)

**6.A**On your Campaign Manager Home page click the “?” on the top right corner and choose “Support center”

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/y4Kimage.png)

**6.B**On the landing page scroll down and click “Contact us”.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/UtOimage.png)

**6.C**On the following page, choose the correct type of campaign and issue you are reporting and click “Phone call”. Also choose your preferred language.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9cXimage.png)

**6.D**Afterwards fill out all the necessary fields.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/I7Uimage.png)

**6.E**Click on "Request Phone Call" to submit your request for assistance.

FAQs

General Process

- **Q1: What should I do if I cannot log in to my Seller Central account to access the "Help" section?**

  - **A1:** If you're unable to log in to your Seller Central account, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" option on the login page. If that doesn't resolve the issue, contact Amazon's general support team through their customer service portal for further assistance.

Issue Categories

- **Q2: What should I do if my issue is not listed in the pre-defined categories on the landing page?**

  - **A2:** If your issue doesn't fit any of the listed categories, select the closest related category and provide a detailed description of your problem in the dialogue box. Amazon support will direct your query to the appropriate team.
- **Q3: Can I report multiple issues in a single request?**

  - **A3:** It’s recommended to address one issue per request to ensure clarity and quicker resolution. For multiple issues, submit separate requests for each problem.

Contact Method

- **Q4: What should I do if the "Phone" contact method is unavailable?**

  - **A4:** If the "Phone" tab isn’t available as a contact method, choose an alternative option such as email or chat. You can also revisit the page later, as the phone option may depend on the type of issue or region-specific support availability.

Sponsored Ads and Campaigns

- **Q5: What happens if I accidentally select the wrong campaign type in the Campaign Manager support flow?**

  - **A5:** If you choose the wrong campaign type, go back to the previous page and select the correct one. Ensure all details align with your specific campaign to prevent delays in resolution.
- **Q6: How can I confirm that my "Request Phone Call" submission for a Sponsored Ads issue was successful?**

  - **A6:** After submitting your request, you will receive a confirmation message or email. If you do not receive confirmation, double-check your submission details and try again.

Language and Accessibility

- **Q7: Is phone support available in all languages?**

  - **A7:** Amazon Selling Partner Support offers assistance in multiple languages, but availability may vary based on your region and the specific issue. Select your preferred language during the request process to see available options.
- **Q8: Can I request a callback if I am located outside the country associated with my Seller Central account?**

  - **A8:** Yes, you can request a callback from outside your country, but ensure the phone number you provide is active, reachable, and includes the correct country code.

Follow-ups

- **Q9: What should I do if I miss the callback from Amazon support?**

  - **A9:** If you miss the callback, Amazon support may attempt to call you again. If not, repeat the steps to request another call or use an alternative contact method.
- **Q10: Can I track the status of my issue after submitting a request?**

  - **A10:** Yes, you can track the status of your request through the "Case Log" section in your Seller Central account. Updates from Amazon support will also be sent to your registered email.
