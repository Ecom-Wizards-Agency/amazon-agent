# Brand Management SOP: NARF Pitch

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/brand-management-sop-narf-pitch

## **NARF Pitch (North America Remote Fulfillment - Pitch)**

**Who is this for :** This is for sellers who wants to diversify sales and increase brand presence on North America Amazon Marketplaces.

**Objective :** To outline the process for applying to NARF, My Amazon Guy's responsibilities, Client's responsibilities, and the Pros why we recommend launching on the Amazon North America Marketplaces.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/79Eimage.png)

**Introducing Remote Fulfillment with FBA**

**What is Remote Fulfillment?**

Amazon has a program that makes selling products across North America seamless. This new program is called Remote Fulfillment with FBA. We think this would be an excellent opportunity to break into international markets and would like to get this set up for you.

Remote Fulfillment with FBA lets you sell to customers in Canada and Mexico without sending inventory to those countries. When you enroll in the program and create offers on Amazon.ca and Amazon.com.mx, Fulfillment by Amazon will use your inventory in US fulfillment centers to fulfill orders across the border directly to the customer through Amazon Services LLC.

Customers pay import duties on the items they order, taking ownership of them after the shipping company picks them up from the US fulfillment center. Returns go directly to the US and are subject to the [FBA customer returns policy](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/G200379860?language=en_US&ref=ag_G200379860_cont_5P3CDUHZWSZLXLB).

For more information on the program, follow this [link](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/help-page.html?itemID=5P3CDUHZWSZLXLB)

**Remote Fulfillment Fee Structure:**

**How Do I Turn on Remote fulfillment? (My Amazon Guy will set it up.)**

1. Your account must be enabled for FBA Export and have a North American Unified Account.
2. [https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/ssof/configuration/index.html/ref=xx_fbasettings_dnav_xx](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/ssof/configuration/index.html/ref=xx_fbasettings_dnav_xx)
3. When we set this up for you, we will let you know if you need to enable these features. Certain features require the admin login to do.
4. We will let you know when this service has been enabled and activated.

**Prohibited Items:**

Some items, such as most consumable and topical products, are prohibited from sale across borders.

**Currency Changes:**

Amazon will automatically calculate the currency changes, though we are seeing them mark the products up a little high, so after we launch, we recommend you review the pricing to your comfort level.

**Language Changes:**

The Mexican listing titles will auto-transfer to Spanish. If sales occur, we will spend time optimizing them further.

**Advertising:**

We also recommend turning on advertising for items we load to Canada using USA FBA stock.

**Approval:**

- Please respond with your approval for us to launch your items in the new markets and start Canadian advertising.
- (We can launch with no ads as well; please specify if this is your preference, however).
