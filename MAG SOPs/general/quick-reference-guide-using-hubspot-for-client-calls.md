# Quick Reference Guide: Using HubSpot for Client Calls

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/general/page/quick-reference-guide-using-hubspot-for-client-calls

## **Using HubSpot for Client Calls**

**Who is this for?**

This guide is specifically for Brand Managers and Project Managers who need to communicate directly with clients, track important details, and ensure accurate follow-up. By using HubSpot, you can make client calls more efficient and ensure you never miss key information.

**Objective**: This guide outlines the process of making client calls through HubSpot, utilizing JustCall integration for seamless call recording and transcription.

**Steps to Make a Client Call via HubSpot:**

1. **Access HubSpot**:

  - Log in to your [**HubSpot**](https://app.hubspot.com/login) account.
2. **Navigate to Client’s Profile**:

  - Go to the **Companies** tab.
  - Search and select the **Client Name** for the call you need to make.
3. **Go to the Call Tab**:

  - Inside the client’s profile, locate the **Call Tab** (usually on the top section of their profile).
4. **Make the Call**:

  - Click on **Make a Phone Call** within the Call Tab.
5. **Call via JustCall**:

  - HubSpot will automatically route the call through **JustCall**, the integrated app for voice communication.
6. **Call Recording & Transcription**:

  - After the call is made, HubSpot will record and transcribe the conversation automatically.
  - You can find the recording and transcription in the **Call History** section under the client’s profile.

**Benefits of Using HubSpot for Calls:**

- **Seamless Communication**: Easily make calls directly from HubSpot without switching between multiple apps.
- **Call Recording**: HubSpot records every client call for easy reference.
- **Call Transcription**: Transcriptions allow you to quickly find key details from the conversation and ensure nothing is missed.
- **Backtrack Key Information**: The recorded files help you backtrack and retrieve important information you might have missed during the call.

**Reminder**: Always ensure you are logged into HubSpot and have access to the necessary permissions for making calls.
