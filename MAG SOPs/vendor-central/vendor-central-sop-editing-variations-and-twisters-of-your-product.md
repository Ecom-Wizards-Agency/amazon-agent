## Editing Variations and Twisters of Your Product

**Who is this for?** Intended for Amazon sellers who have access to Vendor Central and want to learn how to create variations for their products.

**Objective:** To provide guidance and options on how to update a variation in Vendor Central, in order to enable sellers to effectively manage their product variations and enhance their online retail experience.

**Instructions:**

1. To edit the variations of your products, you have two options available.

2. Firstly, if you just need to update the variation name of the existing listing, you can edit it in the same way that you edit your products.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712240372243.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/snkimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/snkimage.png)

3. Go to "Catalog" under the "Items" tab.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712240377113.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Xlkimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Xlkimage.png)

4. Look for the ASIN that needs a variation name update and click "Edit product details" and find the variation attributes, edit, and save with the new value.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712240382321.png)

5. If the update is needed to add another variation or add another child ASIN, you can download the "Variation Sheet". To download, Click **Support**.

6. Scroll down and find the **View resource center** button.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712240391094.png)

You can update the variations for all your ASINs as per the instructions on the template. Also, if you want to add to an existing variation family then include the parent ASIN in the template.

FAQs

Editing Variations

- **Q1: How can I update the variation name for an existing product in Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:**To update the variation name, go to "Catalog" under the "Items" tab, find the ASIN needing an update, and click "Edit product details." From there, you can edit the variation attributes and save with the new value.

Adding Variations

- **Q2: What should I do if I need to add another variation or child ASIN to an existing product listing?**

  - **A2:** To add another variation or child ASIN, download the "Variation Sheet" by going to Support, searching for “Variation Template,” and clicking on the "Download the Variations spreadsheet" link. Follow the instructions in the template to make the necessary updates.

Downloading Templates

- **Q3: Where can I find the "Variation Sheet" to download the template for updating product variations?**

  - **A3:** You can find the "Variation Sheet" by going to the Support page, scrolling down to find the “View resource center” button, and searching for “Variation Template.” Click the “Go” button, then download the template from the search results.

Updating Variation Families

- **Q4: How do I include the parent ASIN when updating variations in the Variation Sheet?**

  - **A4:** If you want to add to an existing variation family, include the parent ASIN in the Variation Template as per the instructions provided in the sheet.

Submitting Variations

- **Q5: What should I do after completing the Variation Sheet?**

  - **A5:** After completing the Variation Sheet, go to the Help page, open a support case by clicking on "Contact Us," select "Manage My Catalog," and choose "Product Linking." Then, compose an email, attach the Variation Template, and submit it to Amazon for processing.

Contacting Amazon

- **Q6: How should I describe my issue when contacting Amazon for variation updates?**

  - **A6:** When contacting Amazon, provide detailed information in the "Describe your issue" section, explaining that you are editing variations. Include the subject line, attach the variation template, and click "Submit."

Variation Processing

- **Q7: Will Amazon update the landing pages with the new variations after I submit the Variation Template?**

  - **A7:** Yes, Amazon’s internal teams will update the twisters on your product landing pages based on the variations provided in the submitted Variation Template.

Bulk Editing Variations

- **Q8: Can I edit multiple ASINs' variations at once?**

  - **A8:** Yes, the Variation Sheet allows you to update variations for multiple ASINs at once, depending on the category and the specific variation instructions in the template.

Support Contact

- **Q9: How do I contact Amazon support if I have an issue with the variation updates?**

  - **A9:**To contact Amazon support, go to the Help page, click on "Contact Us," and select "Product Linking" under the "Manage My Catalog" section. From there, you can send an email describing the issue with the variation updates.
