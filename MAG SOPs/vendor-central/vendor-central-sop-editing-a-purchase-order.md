# Vendor Central SOP: Editing a Purchase Order

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-editing-a-purchase-order

## Editing a Purchase Order

**Who is this for**: This is intended for individuals responsible for managing purchase orders on the Vendor Central platform.

**Objective**: To provide clear instructions on how to edit a Purchase Order (PO) on the Vendor Central platform.

**If you need to make changes to Purchase Orders that have already been confirmed,**
1. Go to your Vendor Central Dashboard and click on “Orders” in the top menu and then select “Purchase Orders”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vvlimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/vvlimage.png)

2. Under the “Confirmed Purchase Orders” section, choose the Purchase Orders you want to edit, either one at a time or in bulk, and click “Open Selected POs”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Iliimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Iliimage.png)

3. Select individual items or all items and then click “Update Selected Items” at the bottom left of the table.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-editing-a-purchase-order-image3.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DgMimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/DgMimage.png)

4. On the next page, you can either make individual selections or perform a bulk edit on all items.

a.) To perform **bulk edit**, select all items and click the "Bulk Edit" button.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1tbimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/1tbimage.png)

b.) Choose the product availability from the drop-down menu and the expected ship date, then click the button "Apply to selected rows below".![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-editing-a-purchase-order-image5.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/c83image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/c83image.png)

c.) For**individual selection**, click on the “Availability” cell and select the availability from the drop-down menu.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-editing-a-purchase-order-image6.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Kglimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Kglimage.png)

d.) Click on the “Expected Ship Date” cell and select your date. You can also modify the quantities of each line item in the “Accepted Quantity” cell, or leave it as is. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-editing-a-purchase-order-image7.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2Vwimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/2Vwimage.png)

e.) You can also cancel your PO line items by selecting the appropriate explanation or reason for the cancellation or rejection from the “Availability” drop-down menu. If you choose to cancel the line item, you'll notice that its quantity will be reduced to zero.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-editing-a-purchase-order-image8.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6nBimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/6nBimage.png)

5. Once you're satisfied with all of your edits, click the “Submit” button in the bottom left corner of the page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-editing-a-purchase-order-image9.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3cAimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/3cAimage.png)

6. You can also export this information to Excel before submitting it by clicking the “Export this data to Excel for your records” button above the line items table.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-editing-a-purchase-order-image10.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LLwimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/LLwimage.png)

Don't forget to check the costs of each item to ensure that they're accurate and that your system's Sales Order values match those of the Vendor Central Purchase Orders. All confirmed Purchase Orders must be edited within four days of being issued by Vendor Central.

FAQs

Accessing Purchase Orders

- **Q1: Where can I find confirmed purchase orders to edit?**

  - **A1:** Navigate to your Vendor Central Dashboard, click on “Orders” in the top menu, and then select “Purchase Orders.” Under the “Confirmed Purchase Orders” section, choose the POs you want to edit

Bulk and Individual Editing

- **Q2: Can I edit multiple items within a purchase order simultaneously?**

  - **A2:** Yes, you can perform a bulk edit by selecting all items and clicking the "Bulk Edit" button. From there, you can update product availability, expected ship dates, and other details for all selected items.
- **Q3: How do I make changes to individual line items within a purchase order?**

  - **A3:** For individual edits, click on the relevant cell (e.g., “Availability” or “Expected Ship Date”) and update the details as needed. You can also adjust quantities in the “Accepted Quantity” cell or leave them unchanged.
- **Q4: Is it possible to cancel specific line items in a purchase order?**

  - **A4:** Yes, you can cancel specific line items by selecting an appropriate reason or explanation from the “Availability” drop-down menu. Once canceled, the quantity for that line item will be reduced to zero.

Exporting Data

- **Q5: Can I export purchase order details before submitting edits?**

  - **A5:** Yes, you can export the data to Excel by clicking the “Export this data to Excel for your records” button above the line items table. This helps you maintain a record of the edits.
- **Q6: What details are included in the exported Excel file?**

  - **A6:** The file typically includes product SKUs, quantities, costs, availability, expected ship dates, and any other relevant purchase order information.

Submission and Verification

- **Q7: What happens if I forget to submit my changes after editing?**

  - **A7:** Any changes made will not take effect until you click the “Submit” button in the bottom left corner of the page. Make sure to submit before exiting the page.
- **Q8: Can I make further edits to a purchase order after submission?**

  - **A8:** No, once a purchase order is submitted, further changes cannot be made on the platform. You will need to contact Amazon Vendor Support for assistance.
- **Q9: How soon must confirmed purchase orders be edited?**

  - **A9:** All confirmed purchase orders must be edited within four days of being issued by Vendor Central.

Cost Verification

- **Q10: What should I verify regarding costs before submitting edits?**

  - **A10:** Ensure that the costs of each item are accurate and match your system's Sales Order values with those of the Vendor Central Purchase Orders.
