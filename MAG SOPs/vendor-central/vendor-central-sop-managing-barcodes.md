# Vendor Central SOP: Managing Barcodes

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-managing-barcodes

## Managing Barcodes

**Who is this for**:

- This is designed for vendors or sellers who manage barcodes for their products on Amazon.
- This SOP is relevant to vendors who have products listed on Amazon and need to ensure accurate and up-to-date barcode information.

**Objective**:

- To guide vendors in managing barcodes effectively for their products on Amazon.
- To ensure that vendors understand the process and requirements for managing barcodes effectively, ultimately maintaining accurate and reliable product identification on the Amazon platform.

An external product ID, also known as a barcode, is a unique record used to identify a product. Amazon accepts UPC, EAN, and GTIN. Once an external ID is submitted in Vendor Central, changes cannot be made.

If you need to add, change, or delete an external ID, contact Amazon Vendor Support and provide proof of ownership such as a GS1 certificate for UPC ownership.

FAQs

- **Q1: What types of external product IDs does Amazon accept?**

  - **A1:** Amazon accepts the following types of external product IDs: UPC, EAN, and GTIN.
- **Q2: Can I modify an external product ID after it has been submitted in Vendor Central?**

  - **A2:** No, once an external product ID is submitted in Vendor Central, changes cannot be made. If a change is necessary, you must contact Amazon Vendor Support.
- **Q3: How can I update or delete an external product ID for my product on Amazon?**

  - **A3:** To update or delete an external product ID:

    1. Navigate to the Amazon Vendor Central homepage.
    2. Click on the **Support** link at the top of the page.
    3. Select **Contact Us** at the bottom of the page.
    4. On the "What can we help you with?" page, choose **Manage My Catalog** > **Item ID Update (EAN, SKU, UPC, Model #, ISBN)**.
    5. Click **Still need help?** and select the **Send an email** link.
    6. Provide the required information, including Subject, ASIN, New ID, and a GS1 certificate, and submit your request.
- **Q4: What documentation is required to make changes to an external product ID?**

  - **A4:** You need to provide proof of ownership, such as a GS1 certificate for UPC ownership, to make changes to an external product ID.
- **Q5: Why might the external product ID in my catalog not match the one listed on the Amazon website?**

  - **A5:** Discrepancies between the external product ID in your catalog and the one listed on the Amazon website may occur due to system synchronization or manual data entry errors. Contact Amazon Vendor Support for clarification and assistance.
- **Q6: What should I include in the email to Amazon Vendor Support when requesting an ID update?**

  - **A6:** Include the following details in your email:

    - Subject
    - ASIN
    - New ID (EAN, SKU, UPC, model #, or ISBN)
    - GS1 certificate
    - A description of the issue
- **Q7: Where can I find more information about product IDs and barcodes on Amazon?**

  - **A7:** For more information, visit the help section on Vendor Central at this link: [Product IDs and Barcodes Help](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/hz/vendor/members/support/help/node/GBEZ3ATBV5FQF5XP).
