# Vendor Central SOP: Editing Images of Your Product Listings

## Editing Images of Your Product Listings

**Who is this for:**

- This is intended for vendors and sellers who manage product listings on the Amazon platform.
- It is relevant for individuals or teams responsible for maintaining and optimizing the visual aspects of product listings, such as marketing managers, content creators, or anyone involved in the process of editing and updating images on the Amazon platform.

**Objective:**To provide guidance on editing images of product listings on the Amazon platform.

- Before uploading any changes, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.

**Instructions:**

1. Go to the Items tab on the top menu and select "Catalog" from the drop-down menu.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/B7Qimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/B7Qimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-12%20at%209.57.54%E2%80%AFPM.png)
2. Click "Manage images".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685449117369(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/76Dimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/76Dimage.png)
3. Select the products for which you want to edit the images.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4Ndimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4Ndimage.png)
4. On the same page, upload the new images and replace the old versions by selecting the relevant suffix for the newly uploaded image.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-12%20at%2010.02.31%E2%80%AFPM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/wyTimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/wyTimage.png)
5. If the update is not successful even after you upload new images, you need to contact Amazon via a case and request the removal of the specific image by providing its current name.

**To contact Amazon Vendor Support:**

1. Visit the Support Center on your Vendor Central Dashboard

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GJvimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/GJvimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-12%20at%2010.03.26%E2%80%AFPM.png)
2. Click "Contact Us" button at the bottom of the page.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685450368591(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/As3image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/As3image.png)
3. Under "Manage My Catalog", select "Images Inquiries".

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1X6image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/1X6image.png)
4. Click "Still need help".

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bT2image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/bT2image.png)
5. Fill out the necessary information, describe the issue, and click "Send".

  ![image](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/loading.gif)
6. A case ID will be generated, continue to monitor the changes and reply to the case if necessary.

FAQs

- **Q1: What should I do before uploading any image changes to my product listings?**

  - **A1:** Before uploading any changes, it is important to communicate and get the client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or Fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.
- **Q2: How do I access the "Manage Images" section in Vendor Central?**

  - **A2:** Go to the "Items" tab on the top menu, select "Catalog" from the drop-down menu, and then click "Manage images."
- **Q3: How do I select the products to edit images for?**

  - **A3:** On the "Manage Images" page, select the products for which you want to edit the images.
- **Q4: How do I upload new images to replace old ones?**

  - **A4:** On the "Manage Images" page, upload the new images and replace the old versions by selecting the relevant suffix for the newly uploaded image.
- **Q5: What should I do if my image update is not successful?**

  - **A5:** If the update is not successful, you need to contact Amazon via a case and request the removal of the specific image by providing its current name.
- **Q6: How do I contact Amazon Vendor Support to resolve image issues?**

  - **A6:** To contact Amazon Vendor Support, visit the Support Center on your Vendor Central Dashboard, click the "Contact Us" button at the bottom of the page, select "Images Inquiries" under "Manage My Catalog," click "Still need help," fill out the necessary information, and click "Send." A case ID will be generated.
- **Q7: What should I do after submitting a case to Amazon regarding image issues?**

  - **A7:** After submitting a case, monitor the changes and reply to the case if necessary to resolve the issue.
- **Q8: Do I need to provide any specific information when contacting Amazon about image issues?**

  - **A8:** Yes, when filling out the case form, be sure to describe the issue in detail and provide any necessary information, including the name of the image that needs to be removed.
