## Adding Video to Your Product Listing

**Who is this for:** Intended for individuals who are responsible for managing their product listings and wish to incorporate video content. It provides guidance to individuals who are involved in the process of creating high-quality videos that effectively showcase their products.

**Objective:**To create compelling and visually appealing videos that capture and hold customers' attention, resulting in increased engagement with the products and ultimately driving them to make a purchase. By leveraging the potential of videos to outperform static images and text, this SOP aims to guide individuals in creating captivating video content that effectively showcases the product's features and benefits, encourages viewer engagement, and maximizes the conversion rate of potential customers into buyers.

**Instructions:**

1. Navigate to the "Items" tab in the top menu of Vendor Central, then select "Upload and Manage Videos" from the Drop-Down Menu.

FAQs

Video Quality

- **Q1: What should I do before uploading a video to the product listing?**

  - **A1:** Ensure that your video effectively showcases the product’s features and benefits, as this will help capture the customers' attention and improve engagement. Make sure the video quality is high, as it will be reviewed by Amazon before approval.

Navigation

- **Q2: How do I access the "Upload and Manage Videos" option in Vendor Central?**

  - **A2:** Navigate to the "Items" tab in the top menu of Vendor Central, then select "Upload and Manage Videos" from the drop-down menu.

Video Upload

- **Q3: What information do I need to provide when uploading a video?**

  - **A3:** You will need to provide the video file, a Video Title, the ASIN(s) that the video relates to, and select an appropriate thumbnail before submitting the video.

Submission Process

- **Q4: How do I submit the video after entering the necessary details?**

  - **A4:** Once all the necessary information has been entered, click the "Submit" button located at the bottom right side of the page to finalize the upload.

Review and Approval

- **Q5: What happens after I submit the video?**

  - **A5:** After submitting, your video will be reviewed and processed by Amazon. Once approved, it will be available for customers to view on your product detail page.

Multiple Videos

- **Q6: Can I upload multiple videos for a single product?**

  - **A6:** Yes, you can upload multiple videos, but each video must be relevant to the specific ASIN it is associated with.
