## Producing a Real-Time Sales Report

**Who is this for :** This is designed for vendors on the Vendor Central platform who are interested in accessing real-time sales data for their products. It is particularly useful for sellers running campaigns or seeking to measure the impact of content or image changes on their product's sales performance.

**Objective :** To provide step-by-step instructions on how to access the Amazon Brand Analytics Dashboard, navigate to the Real-Time Sales Report, and extract valuable information about the hourly activity, ordered revenue, and ordered units for a specific ASIN.

- In order to gain insights into your product's real-time sales performance on Amazon, you can utilize the Real-Time Sales Report available in the Amazon Brand Analytics Dashboard. This report allows you to track hourly activity, revenue, and order units for a specific ASIN over the past 24 hours.

**Instructions**

1. To access your Amazon Brand Analytics Dashboard, go to your Vendor Central Dashboard and click on the "Reports" tab. From the drop-down menu, select "Retail Analytics."

FAQs

**1. How often does the Real-Time Sales Report update?**

- The Real-Time Sales Report updates hourly, but there may be a slight delay of 1-2 hours before the latest data appears.

**2. How far back can I access historical real-time sales data?**

- Currently, the report only provides data for the past 24 hours. If you need long-term sales trends, use the Sales Diagnostic Report under Retail Analytics instead.

**3. What do the key metrics in the report mean?**

- Hourly Activity – Tracks customer purchasing patterns within each hour.
- Ordered Revenue – The total revenue generated from orders within each hour.
- Ordered Units – The total number of units sold within each hour.
- By analyzing these metrics, vendors can determine peak purchasing times and measure sales trends.

**4. What should I do if data is missing or incorrect?**

- If your report has missing or incorrect data:

  - Ensure that the correct ASIN is entered in the report search field.
  - Wait for the next hourly update (some data may have a short delay).
  - Compare with other Vendor Central reports to check for inconsistencies.
  - If issues persist, contact Amazon Vendor Support.

**5. How can I use this report beyond campaign tracking?**

- The Real-Time Sales Report is also useful for:

  - Adjusting inventory levels – Ensure stock availability for peak hours.
  - Optimizing pricing – Test pricing adjustments based on sales fluctuations.
  - Improving product pages – Analyze how content or image updates impact immediate sales.

**6. Can I filter real-time sales data by region or fulfillment type?**

- No, the Real-Time Sales Report currently does not support filtering by region, fulfillment method (FBA vs. FBM), or sales channel. It only provides ASIN-specific real-time sales data.

**7. Can I export or automate the Real-Time Sales Report?**

- No, Amazon does not currently allow automated scheduling or downloads for this report. However, you can manually export data by copying the displayed figures into your own tracking system.
