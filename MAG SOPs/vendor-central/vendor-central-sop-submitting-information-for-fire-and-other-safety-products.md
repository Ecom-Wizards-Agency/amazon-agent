# Vendor Central SOP: Submitting Information for Fire and Other Safety Products

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-submitting-information-for-fire-and-other-safety-products

### Submitting Information for Fire and Other Safety Products

**Who is this for:** This is intended for suppliers or vendors who plan to provide fire and other safety products, specifically smoke alarms, smoke detectors, and carbon monoxide alarms, to Amazon.

**Objective:** To provide clear instructions to suppliers who intend to supply smoke alarms, smoke detectors, or carbon monoxide alarms to Amazon. To obtain certification from a nationally recognized testing laboratory, demonstrating that the supplied products meet the appropriate UL standards (UL 217 for smoke alarms, UL 268 for smoke detectors, and UL 2034 for carbon monoxide alarms).

If you plan to supply smoke alarms, smoke detectors, or carbon monoxide alarms to Amazon, your products must be certified to the appropriate UL standard: UL 217 for smoke alarms, UL 268 for smoke detectors, and UL 2034 for carbon monoxide alarms. Amazon defines smoke alarms as products that issue a local audible or visual alarm to indicate the presence of smoke, usually found in homes. Smoke detectors, on the other hand, issue a signal to a fire alarm control panel as part of a larger fire safety system, typically found in commercial environments.

To supply these products to Amazon, please submit the following information to**lifesafetyequipment@amazon.com:**

- Your company name and vendor ID
- Your contact information, including email address and phone number
- A list of ASINs you will be supplying

Documented certification from a nationally recognized testing laboratory that your product is certified under the applicable UL standard:

- UL 217 for smoke alarms
- UL 268 for smoke detectors
- UL 2034 for carbon monoxide alarms
- All applicable documents if your product is a multifunction device
- A certification that all materials submitted are true, authentic, and accurate.

FAQs

- **Q1: What types of products require certification under UL standards to be supplied to Amazon?**

  - **A1:** Products such as smoke alarms, smoke detectors, and carbon monoxide alarms require certification under specific UL standards:

    - **UL 217** for smoke alarms
    - **UL 268** for smoke detectors
    - **UL 2034** for carbon monoxide alarms
- **Q2: What is the difference between a smoke alarm and a smoke detector according to Amazon?**

  - **A2:** Amazon defines smoke alarms as devices that issue a local audible or visual alarm to indicate the presence of smoke, typically used in homes. Smoke detectors, however, send a signal to a fire alarm control panel as part of a larger fire safety system, usually found in commercial environments.
- **Q3: What information must I provide to Amazon to supply fire and safety products?**

  - **A3:** Suppliers must submit the following information to [**lifesafetyequipment@amazon.com**](mailto:lifesafetyequipment@amazon.com):

    - Company name and vendor ID
    - Contact information (email address and phone number)
    - A list of ASINs being supplied
    - Certification from a nationally recognized testing laboratory (e.g., UL 217, UL 268, or UL 2034)
    - Relevant documentation for multifunction devices, if applicable
    - A certification confirming all submitted materials are true, authentic, and accurate
- **Q4: What is a multifunction device, and what documentation is required for it?**

  - **A4:** A multifunction device combines multiple safety functions (e.g., a smoke alarm with a carbon monoxide detector). Documentation must demonstrate that the device meets all relevant UL standards for each function it performs.
- **Q5: Who certifies that fire and safety products meet UL standards?**

  - **A5:** Certification must be obtained from a **nationally recognized testing laboratory**. This ensures the products comply with applicable UL standards.
- **Q6: Where should I submit the required documentation for fire and safety products?**

  - **A6:**All required documentation should be sent to [**lifesafetyequipment@amazon.com**](mailto:lifesafetyequipment@amazon.com).
- **Q7: Why is it important to provide accurate and authentic information in the submission?**

  - **A7:**Providing true, authentic, and accurate information ensures compliance with Amazon's requirements and maintains the integrity of safety standards for customers.
- **Q8: What happens if I fail to provide certification for my fire and safety products?**

  - **A8:** If certification is not provided, Amazon may reject the products, preventing them from being listed or sold on their platform.
