# Vendor Central SOP: Submitting an Advance Shipment Notification (ASN)

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-submitting-an-advance-shipment-notification-asn

## Submitting an Advance Shipment Notification (ASN)

**Who is this for**:

- This SOP is intended for individuals responsible for managing shipments on the Vendor Central platform.
- This is crucial for individuals handling shipments and ensuring compliance with Amazon's requirements.
- It is suitable for users who need to submit ASNs for their purchase orders, whether the carrier is assigned by Amazon (if collect) or an appointment is scheduled for receipt (if prepaid).

**Objective**: To provide instructions on how to submit an Advance Shipment Notification (ASN) on the Vendor Central platform.

To submit an Advance Shipment Notice for Amazon, you need to ensure that your Purchase Order has been assigned a carrier by Amazon (if collect) or have an appointment scheduled with Amazon for receipt (if prepaid).

1. Access your Vendor Central Dashboard and select “Shipments” from the “Orders” Drop-Down Menu located at the Top Left-Hand Corner.

2. Look for the PO and click “Submit ASN" found from the "View Details" drop-down.

3. Select “Submit ASN and Complete Shipment” from the “Action” Drop-Down Menu located to the Right of the PO you wish to submit an ASN.

4. Choose the PO and click “Next”.

5. Check all the information from your Routing Request on the next two pages, and then click “Next” and “Next” again.

6. Fill out all relevant information regarding the shipment date, type, volume, weight, carrier, and tracking or PRO number on the third page, then click “Next”.

7. On the following page, select the Tracking Number you have just entered on the previous page alongside each Amazon container code from the Drop-Down Menu and click “Next”.

8. If all information on the following page is correct, click “Submit”.

9. On the final page, you have the option to print the carton labels, which fulfill all Amazon’s labeling requirements, or use them as an example to ensure that the labels generated from your system contain all the required information.

10. You can also print the Bill of Lading for this shipment and create an invoice from this page if you choose to invoice the purchase order before it has been collected.

FAQs

General Process

- **Q1: What is an Advance Shipment Notification (ASN)?**

  - **A1:** An Advance Shipment Notification (ASN) is a document submitted on Amazon Vendor Central to inform Amazon of shipment details, including carrier information, tracking numbers, and shipping labels.
- **Q2: Why is submitting an ASN important?**

  - **A2:** Submitting an ASN ensures compliance with Amazon’s requirements, enables efficient tracking, and prevents shipment rejections or delays during the receiving process.

Access and Navigation

- **Q3: How do I access the "Shipments" section in Vendor Central?**

  - **A3:** Log in to Vendor Central, navigate to the "Orders" tab in the top left-hand corner, and select "Shipments" from the dropdown menu.
- **Q4: Where can I find the option to submit an ASN for a specific purchase order?**

  - **A4:** Locate the desired PO in the "Shipments" section and click “Submit ASN” from the "View Details" dropdown.

Preparation

- **Q5: What prerequisites must be met before submitting an ASN?**

  - **A5:** Ensure that your PO has an assigned carrier (if collect) or an appointment scheduled with Amazon (if prepaid). Confirm all shipment details, such as volume, weight, carrier, and tracking numbers.
- **Q6: Can I submit an ASN for multiple POs at once?**

  - **A6:** No, you must submit ASNs for each PO individually by selecting them and providing the required shipment details.

Shipment Details

- **Q7: What information is required when filling out an ASN?**

  - **A7:** You need to provide shipment date, type, volume, weight, carrier, tracking number (or PRO number), and container codes for Amazon.
- **Q8: What should I do if my tracking number is incorrect?**

  - **A8:** Edit the tracking number on the ASN page before submission. If the ASN has already been submitted, you must contact Amazon support to correct the information.

Labels and Documentation

- **Q9: Can I use my own system-generated carton labels instead of Amazon’s labels?**

  - **A9:** Yes, as long as your system-generated labels contain all required information and meet Amazon’s labeling standards.
- **Q10: How do I print carton labels and the Bill of Lading?**

  - **A10:** After submitting the ASN, you’ll be prompted to print the carton labels and the Bill of Lading on the final page.
- **Q11: What is the purpose of the Bill of Lading?**

  - **A11:** The Bill of Lading is a document that serves as a receipt for the shipment and includes details about the shipment contents, origin, and destination.

Troubleshooting

- **Q12: What should I do if the "Submit ASN" option is not available?**

  - **A12:** Refresh the page or verify that the PO is eligible for ASN submission. If the issue persists, contact Amazon Vendor Central support.
- **Q13: What happens if I submit an incorrect ASN?**

  - **A13:** Contact Amazon support immediately to resolve any issues with the incorrect ASN to avoid shipment delays or rejection.
