# Vendor Central SOP: Enabling a Vine Account on Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-enabling-a-vine-account-on-vendor-central

## Enabling a Vine Account on Vendor Central

**Who is this for**:

For Amazon sellers who want to enroll their products in the Vine Program and need to create a Vine account on Vendor Central.

**Objective**:

To provide a step-by-step guide on how to enable a Vine account on Vendor Central.

1. The first step in enrolling a product on Vine is to create a Vine account.
2. To create a Vine account, hover the Drop-Down Menu of the “Merchandising” Tab and select “Amazon Vine.”

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/IlAimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/IlAimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1720631841318.png)
3. Once on the next page, click on the “Start enrolling products into Vine today" button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-05-26_8-47-43(1).jpg)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/l6Vimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/l6Vimage.png)
4. A pop window will appear with a list of updates with Amazon Vendor Central Vine program. Click "Ok" to close the window.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/2023-05-26_8-48-34(1).jpg)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/MWtimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/MWtimage.png)
5. You can proceed now to enroll products in the Vine Program. You may follow [Catalog SOP: Enrolling a Product in the Vine Program on Vendor Central.](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/catalog/page/catalog-sop-enrolling-a-product-in-the-vine-program-on-vendor-central)

FAQs

- **Q1: How do I enable a Vine account on Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:** To enable a Vine account, hover over the “Merchandising” tab in Vendor Central, select “Amazon Vine,” then click the “Start enrolling products into Vine today" button.
- **Q2: What happens after I click the “Start enrolling products into Vine today” button?**

  - **A2:** A pop-up window will appear with a list of updates related to the Amazon Vendor Central Vine program. Click "Ok" to close the window and proceed with the account setup.
- **Q3: Is there any additional setup required after closing the pop-up window?**

  - **A3:** Once the pop-up window is closed, you can proceed with enrolling products in the Vine Program, as detailed in the Catalog SOP for enrolling products.
- **Q4: How do I begin enrolling products in the Vine Program after enabling the account?**

  - **A4:** After enabling your Vine account, you can follow the Catalog SOP on “Enrolling a Product in the Vine Program on Vendor Central” to start enrolling products.
