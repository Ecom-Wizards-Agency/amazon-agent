## Fulfilling Orders in Bulk for Direct Fulfillment

**Who is this for:** This SOP is applicable to **vendors**and **sellers**who want to streamline their order processing workflow, increase efficiency, and save time when fulfilling a high volume of **Direct Fulfillment orders** on the **Amazon platform.**

**Objective:**

- To provide **vendors**and **sellers**with a standardized process for fulfilling orders in bulk for **Direct Fulfillment**.
- To streamline the fulfillment process, reduce manual effort, and ensure accurate tracking of shipments for **Direct Fulfillment orders**.

If you have a large number of Direct Fulfillment orders to process, Excel can be used to confirm the bulk shipment of over 100 orders at once.

**Steps :**

1. Navigate to the “**Orders**” tab on the top menu of Vendor Central, select “**Direct Fulfillment Orders**” from the Drop-Down Menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-12%20at%2010.34.35%E2%80%AFPM.png)
2. Click the "**Select All**" link on the left side.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Vendor-Central-SOP-Fulfilling-Orders-in-Bulk-for-Direct-Fulfillment-2(1)(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sEeimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/sEeimage.png)
3. **Download the shipment template** file for one or all warehouses by clicking the "**Available actions**" dropdown, and then open it in **Excel**.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Vendor-Central-SOP-Fulfilling-Orders-in-Bulk-for-Direct-Fulfillment-3(1).png)

4. In the **Tracking Number column**, enter the **tracking ID** for each shipment.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Vendor-Central-SOP-Fulfilling-Orders-in-Bulk-for-Direct-Fulfillment-4(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yFaimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/yFaimage.png)
5. In the '**Please input Yes to confirm shipment**' column, enter "Yes," "yes," or "Y." Note that the field is **not case sensitive.**

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2KKimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/2KKimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Vendor-Central-SOP-Fulfilling-Orders-in-Bulk-for-Direct-Fulfillment-5(1).png)
6. Be sure to enter "**Yes**" in the confirmation column; leaving it blank will only store the tracking ID without confirming shipment of the order.
7. Save the file in **.csv** format only and do not change the pre-selected language.

**Important:** Do not delete or rearrange the order of the columns, and only make changes in the fields for tracking number, shipping cost, and shipped column. Ensure that the items in the order have dimensions; if dimensions are missing, add them in the in-Order Details and update the catalog.

8. Once the spreadsheet is completed, upload it to the system.

FAQs

**1. Can all vendors use Direct Fulfillment, or is it restricted to specific accounts?**

- Not all vendors have access to Direct Fulfillment. Eligibility depends on your vendor agreement with Amazon. Check your Vendor Central settings or contact Amazon support to confirm your eligibility.

**2. Is there a maximum number of orders I can process in a single bulk upload?**

- Amazon allows bulk uploads for over 100 orders, but the system may have file size limitations. It’s recommended to keep CSV files under 50MB to prevent upload issues.

**3. What should I do if my bulk upload fails?**

- Go to Vendor Central > Orders > Direct Fulfillment Orders > Upload Status to check for errors.
- Click "View Errors" to download an error report.
- Correct any issues (e.g., missing tracking numbers, incorrect formatting) and re-upload the file.

**4. How can I verify that my bulk shipments were processed successfully?**

- After uploading your file:

  - Navigate to Orders > Direct Fulfillment Orders to check the status of your shipments.
  - Use the Tracking Number to confirm the shipment’s progress.
  - If the order is successfully processed, its status will update to Shipped.

**5. Can I cancel or edit a shipment after submitting the bulk upload?**

- Once a shipment is confirmed, it cannot be edited. If you need to make changes, you must contact Amazon Vendor Support and follow their cancellation process.

**6. Are all shipping carriers supported, or do I need to use specific ones?**

- Amazon supports select carriers that can provide valid tracking numbers. Ensure your chosen carrier is listed in Vendor Central > Direct Fulfillment Settings to avoid processing errors.

**7. What happens if my product catalog is missing dimensions?**

- Missing dimensions can cause errors in Direct Fulfillment orders. Before uploading shipments, update missing dimensions under Vendor Central > Items > Catalog Management to prevent processing issues.

**8. Will I receive a confirmation after a successful bulk shipment upload?**

- Yes, Amazon typically sends a confirmation email for processed shipments. You can also check the Upload Status page in Vendor Central to confirm successful submissions.
