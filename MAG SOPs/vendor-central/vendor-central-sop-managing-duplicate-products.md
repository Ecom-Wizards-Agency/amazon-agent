## Managing Duplicate Products

1. Listings of the same product in different sectors of the Amazon marketplace are called Duplicate Products.
2. Amazon prohibits the creation of Duplicate Products as they negatively impact the sales potential of the original retailer, dilute reviews, and confuse customers.
3. Duplicate product pages in the Amazon catalog create confusion for customers and make it difficult for them to make purchasing decisions.
4. If you have Duplicate Products in your inventory, you can merge them to create a single product page.
5. To merge product pages, the products must have the same product identifier (UPC, EAN, or ISBN), package quantity, product brand, and physical attributes (color, size, fragrance).
6. If the product pages have the same attributes, you can request Amazon to merge them by opening a case.
7. To open a case, download the Merge Form from the Support Center on Vendor Central, find it under the "Product Management" header,

FAQs

- **Q1: What are Duplicate Products on Amazon?**

  - **A1:** Duplicate Products refer to listings of the same product in different sectors of the Amazon marketplace. These listings can negatively impact the sales potential of the original retailer, dilute reviews, and confuse customers.
- **Q2: Why are Duplicate Products prohibited on Amazon?**

  - **A2:** Duplicate Products create confusion for customers and make it difficult for them to make purchasing decisions. They also negatively impact sales and dilute product reviews.
- **Q3: How can I merge Duplicate Products into a single product page?**

  - **A3:** To merge Duplicate Products, the products must have the same product identifier (UPC, EAN, or ISBN), package quantity, product brand, and physical attributes (color, size, fragrance). If these attributes match, you can request Amazon to merge them by opening a case.
- **Q4: How do I open a case to merge Duplicate Products?**

  - **A4:**To open a case, download the "Merge Form" from the Support Center on Vendor Central under the "Product Management" header. Then, choose "Merge duplicate product pages" and fill out the form with the relevant information.
- **Q5: Where do I find the "Merge Form" to request merging Duplicate Products?**

  - **A5:** The "Merge Form" can be found in the Support Center under the "Product Management" section. Choose “Merge duplicate product pages” and download the form from the landing page.
- **Q6: What should I do after filling out the "Merge Form"?**

  - **A6:** After completing the form, go to the "Contact Us" button at the bottom of the Support Center on Vendor Central, select your business group, and choose "Duplicates" under "Manage My Catalog." Then select "Product linking (Variations, Twister, Newer Version, Duplicates, Edition, Titleset)" and click “Send an email.”
- **Q7: How do I request Amazon to merge my Duplicate Products?**

  - **A7:** Open a case by submitting the form and request Amazon to remove or merge the Duplicate Products, matching them to your ASIN.
- **Q8: What happens after I submit a case to merge Duplicate Products?**

  - **A8:** After submitting the case, Amazon will review your request and take action to remove or merge the Duplicate Products based on your provided information.
