# Vendor Central SOP: Producing an Order History Report

## Producing an Order History Report

**Who is this for?:** For individuals involved in the generation of order history reports within an organization.

**Objective:** To outline the step-by-step process for generating a comprehensive report that provides an organized and detailed record of past customer orders. The SOP ensures consistency, accuracy, and efficiency in producing the report, thereby facilitating data analysis, decision-making, and customer service improvements.

Order History report displays all types of orders such as fulfilled, partially filled, cancelled, and backordered.

**Steps :**

1. To access your Order History Report, navigate to the “Orders” tab on your Vendor Central dashboard and select “Purchase Orders” from the drop-down menu.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/A8yimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/A8yimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-16%20at%203.02.33%E2%80%AFAM.png)
2. Click on the link labeled "PO History" on the next page.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Rkpimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Rkpimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-16%20at%203.08.25%E2%80%AFAM.png)
3. Choose the data that you wish to generate then click on “Submit”

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dHEimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/dHEimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-16%20at%203.09.03%E2%80%AFAM(1).png)
4. You can either download a list of the Purchase Orders as single Purchase Orders with a total value by clicking the "Export data for your records" button at the top of the table. You can also select individual Purchase Orders and download each line item on each Purchase Order by clicking "Open Selected POs" at the bottom of the table.

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3O2image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/3O2image.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-16%20at%203.12.17%E2%80%AFAM.png)
5. The Order History Report is useful for reconciling Purchase Orders with invoices, chargebacks, shortage claims, payment remittances, credit memos, and other documents. You can sort the report by month, PO, ship window, ASIN, and more.
6. The report provides a grand total of what was sold to or shipped to Amazon over a given period of time, as it indicates shortages and cancelled Purchase Order items. Note that the report only shows Purchase Orders with a "Closed" status, meaning they have been receipted by Amazon, and not outstanding Purchase Orders, Orders in Transit, or those prior to receipt.

FAQs

**1. How often does the Order History Report update?**

- The report updates daily, but some order statuses (e.g., shortages and cancellations) may take 24-48 hours to reflect.

**2. How far back can I access historical order data?**

- Amazon allows vendors to retrieve order history data for up to 24 months. If you need older records, consider downloading reports regularly for archiving.

**3. What do the key metrics in the Order History Report mean?**

- Shortages – Items that were ordered but not received by Amazon.
- Backorders – Orders Amazon placed but could not be fulfilled immediately.
- Cancellations – Orders that were placed but later canceled by either party.
- Closed Orders – Purchase orders Amazon has fully processed and marked as complete.

**4. What should I do if an order is missing from the report?**

- If your report is missing an expected order:

  - Ensure the correct date range and filters are applied.
  - Check whether the order is still open or in transit (only closed orders appear in this report).
  - Compare with other Vendor Central reports, such as the Operational Performance Dashboard.
  - If issues persist, contact Amazon Vendor Support.

**5. How can I use the Order History Report beyond reconciling invoices?**

- The report is also useful for:

  - Demand forecasting – Identify order trends over time.
  - Supplier performance evaluation – Track fulfillment rates and shortages.
  - Inventory planning – Adjust stock levels based on order frequency and demand.

**6. Can I automate or schedule the Order History Report?**

- No, Amazon does not currently offer automated scheduling for this report. However, you can manually download it as needed for regular analysis.

**7. Why does the report only show "Closed" purchase orders?**

- Amazon includes only Closed purchase orders because they are fully processed and reconciled. Orders in Transit or Outstanding Orders may still change, making them unreliable for historical reporting.
