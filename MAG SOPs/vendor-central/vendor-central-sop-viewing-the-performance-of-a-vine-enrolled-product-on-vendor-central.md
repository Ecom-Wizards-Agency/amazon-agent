## Viewing the Performance of a Vine-Enrolled Product on Vendor Central

**Who is this for?**This is intended for sellers who have enrolled their products in the Vine Program on Vendor Central and want to check the performance of their enrolled products. This includes but is not limited to, manufacturers, distributors, and resellers of products marketed on Amazon.

**Objective**:

The objective is to provide step-by-step guidance for viewing the performance of a product enrolled in the Vine Program on Vendor Central. This aims to help sellers determine the effectiveness of their enrollment in the Vine Program by providing metrics such as units received, Vine reviews, and average Vine rating.

**Steps on how to check enrolled product in the Vine Program is performing on Vendor Central:**

1. To check the performance of a product enrolled in the Vine Program, go to Vendor Central and select “Amazon Vine” from the “Merchandising” Tab's Drop-Down Menu in the Top Menu. Please see the image below for your reference.

FAQs

- **Q1: How do I access the performance metrics for a product enrolled in the Vine Program?**

  - **A1:** To access performance metrics, go to Vendor Central, select “Amazon Vine” from the “Merchandising” tab’s drop-down menu in the top menu.
- **Q2: What performance metrics can I view for a Vine-enrolled product?**

  - **A2:** You can view metrics such as "Cost Per Claimed Unit," "Units Claimed," and "Units Requested" to assess the performance of your product enrolled in the Vine Program.
- **Q3: What does "Cost Per Claimed Unit" refer to in the Vine Program performance metrics?**

  - **A3:** "Cost Per Claimed Unit" refers to the cost associated with each unit that has been claimed by Vine Voices as part of the Vine Program.
- **Q4: What is the significance of the “Units Claimed” metric?**

  - **A4:**"Units Claimed" refers to the number of units that Vine Voices have claimed for review as part of the Vine Program. This helps you understand how many units have been distributed for review.
- **Q5: How can I track the progress of my Vine-enrolled products?**

  - **A5:** By clicking on the “Details" link for each product, you can view the performance metrics of Vine-enrolled products, including the number of units requested, claimed, and other relevant data.
- **Q6: How do I know if my product has been concluded to Vine Voices?**

  - **A6:**If your product has been concluded to Vine Voices, it will show up in the "Concluded" section after you click on the “Details" link.
- **Q7: How can I use the performance metrics to make decisions about future Vine enrollments?**

  - **A7:**The metrics like "Units Claimed" and "Cost Per Claimed Unit" will give you insight into the effectiveness of your product's enrollment in the Vine Program. If the numbers meet your expectations, you may decide to continue or expand your enrollment; if not, you may adjust your strategy.
