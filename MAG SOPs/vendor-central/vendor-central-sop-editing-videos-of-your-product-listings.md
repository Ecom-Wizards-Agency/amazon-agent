## Editing Videos of Your Product Listings

**Who is this for**:

- This is intended for vendors and sellers who utilize videos as part of their product listings on Amazon.
- It is relevant for individuals or teams responsible for managing and editing video content, such as marketing managers, content creators, or anyone involved in maintaining and improving the visual aspects of product listings on the Amazon platform.

**Objectives:**

- To provide guidance on editing videos of product listings on the Amazon platform.
- To explain the process of accessing and managing videos through the Vendor Central interface, including options for deleting videos and making changes to existing uploads.

Before uploading any changes, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.

**Steps for Editing Videos for Your Product Listings:**

1. To manage or edit your videos, navigate to the "Upload and Manage Videos" option from the "Items" tab on the top menu of Vendor Central.

2. If you want to delete a video, click the "delete" button next to the relevant video.

FAQs

- **Q1: What should I do before uploading or editing videos on my product listings?**

  - **A1:** Before uploading or editing videos, it is important to communicate and get the client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or Fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.
- **Q2: How do I access the "Upload and Manage Videos" option in Vendor Central?**

  - **A2:** To manage or edit your videos, navigate to the "Upload and Manage Videos" option from the "Items" tab on the top menu of Vendor Central.
- **Q3: How do I delete a video from my product listing?**

  - **A3:** To delete a video, click the "delete" button next to the relevant video.
- **Q4: How can I make changes to an existing video upload?**

  - **A4:** To make changes, click the "Manage" button next to the video. From there, you can update the title, add more ASINs, and change the thumbnail as needed.
- **Q5: What should I do after making changes to a video?**

  - **A5:** After making the necessary updates, click "Save and Submit" to apply the changes to your video.
- **Q6: Can I add multiple ASINs to a single video?**

  - **A6:** Yes, you can add more ASINs to a video by updating the video details through the "Manage" option.
- **Q7: How do I update the thumbnail for a video?**

  - **A7:** To update the thumbnail, click the "Manage" button next to the video and change the thumbnail as needed before saving the changes.
- **Q8: What happens after I click "Save and Submit" for video updates?**

  - **A8:** After clicking "Save and Submit," the updates will be processed, and the video details, including title, ASINs, and thumbnail, will be updated accordingly.
