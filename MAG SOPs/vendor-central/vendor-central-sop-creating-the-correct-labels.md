# Vendor Central SOP: Creating the Correct Labels

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-creating-the-correct-labels

## Creating the Correct Labels

**Who is this for:**

This is intended for individuals or teams responsible for preparing and shipping products to Amazon's fulfillment centers.

**Objectives:**

- To provide clear instructions on creating and applying correct labels to shipments destined for Amazon's fulfillment centers.
- To ensure that the shipment details are accurately represented on the labels, facilitating efficient processing and receipt of the shipments by Amazon.

1. In order for Amazon to receive your shipment effectively at their fulfillment centers, it is required that you apply clear and accurate labels to each carton (package) containing specific shipment details.
2. The shipping (ASN) label must include the shipment level information of the ASN/BOL number in the text and barcode. You can print the shipping (ASN) labels using the label printing option in Vendor Central or create your own.
3. Even if you use the provided AMZNCC/SSCC labels and affix them to each package, you must still print and apply ASN labels on each small parcel shipment and palletized shipment.
4. When placing pallet labels, ensure they are positioned squarely (not at an angle) on two sides of the pallet on the forklift entry sides. If the pallet is wrapped in plastic, the pallet labels must be placed on the outside of the plastic wrap.

FAQs

General Process

- **Q1: Why are correct labels important for shipments to Amazon?**

  - **A1:** Accurate labels ensure that Amazon can process, track, and receive shipments efficiently at their fulfillment centers, avoiding delays or rejections.
- **Q2: Who is responsible for creating and applying the labels?**

  - **A2:** The responsibility lies with individuals or teams preparing and shipping products to Amazon's fulfillment centers.

Label Requirements

- **Q3: What information must the shipping (ASN) label include?**

  - **A3:** The shipping (ASN) label must include shipment-level information such as the ASN/BOL number in both text and barcode format.
- **Q4: Do I need to apply both AMZNCC/SSCC labels and ASN labels?**

  - **A4:** Yes, even if you use AMZNCC/SSCC labels, you are still required to print and apply ASN labels to each small parcel shipment and palletized shipment.

Pallet Labeling

- **Q5: How should pallet labels be placed?**

  - **A5:** Pallet labels must be positioned squarely (not at an angle) on two sides of the pallet, specifically on the forklift entry sides. If the pallet is wrapped in plastic, the labels must be applied on the outside of the wrap.
- **Q6: Can I apply labels inside the plastic wrap on palletized shipments?**

  - **A6:** No, labels must always be applied on the outside of the plastic wrap for clear visibility during processing.

Label Printing Options

- **Q7: Where can I print shipping (ASN) labels?**

  - **A7:** You can print shipping (ASN) labels directly from the label printing option in Vendor Central or create your own labels that meet Amazon’s requirements.
- **Q8: What are the key specifications for self-created labels?**

  - **A8:** Self-created labels must include clear text and scannable barcodes for shipment details such as ASN/BOL numbers and comply with Amazon’s labeling guidelines.

Troubleshooting and Compliance

- **Q9: What happens if labels are missing or incorrect?**

  - **A9:** Missing or incorrect labels can result in delays, rejections, or additional fees for reprocessing shipments at Amazon’s fulfillment centers.
- **Q10: How do I ensure compliance with Amazon’s labeling requirements?**

  - **A10:** Regularly review Amazon’s labeling guidelines, double-check all shipment details before printing, and ensure labels are applied securely and visibly.

Additional Considerations

- **Q11: Are there specific tools Amazon recommends for label creation?**

  - **A11:** Amazon provides a label printing option in Vendor Central, but you can also use compatible software or systems that generate compliant labels with scannable barcodes.
- **Q12: Can I reuse old labels for new shipments?**

  - **A12:** No, each shipment must have unique and updated labels that reflect the current ASN/BOL and shipment details.
