## Submitting an Invoice from an Advance Shipment Notification

**Who is this for:**This is intended for individuals responsible for managing invoices and payments on the Vendor Central platform.

**Objective:**To provide instructions on how to submit an invoice based on an Advance Shipment Notification (ASN) in Vendor Central. To ensure accurate and consistent invoice creation and submission based on ASNs.

**Instructions:**

1. To create an invoice based on an Advanced Shipment Notification, go to the **"Payments"** tab on your Vendor Central dashboard and select **"Invoices"** from the dropdown menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-19%20at%2010.23.35%E2%80%AFPM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/HjCimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/HjCimage.png)
2. Click on **"Create Invoice"**

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-19%20at%2010.24.49%E2%80%AFPM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PNZimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/PNZimage.png)
3. Search shipments from the last 3 days. If the shipment was created more than 3 days ago, select shipments created in the last 31 days instead.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688167902521(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Bxhimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Bxhimage.png)
4. Find the ASN you want to invoice and tick its check box.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687976003777(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gueimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/gueimage.png)
5. Scroll down, click PO details to double-check if the chosen ASN and PO numbers are correct, then click on the **"Create Invoice"** button.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687978667724(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ATqimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/ATqimage.png)
6. This will take you to the invoice payment page and this is what it looks like.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687976924562(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/aa7image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/aa7image.png)
7. On this page, enter the invoice date and invoice number (must be unique and must never be reused).

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687977120870(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Ccyimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Ccyimage.png)
8. Make sure all line items on your invoice and packing list for this Purchase Order are included on this invoice for Vendor Central. Check that the relevant tracking number is included in each line item as applicable and that all unit costs are correct.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687977551313(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LoNimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/LoNimage.png)
9. Include any relevant miscellaneous charges.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687977372650(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Y3Qimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Y3Qimage.png)
10. Review the total invoice amount, tick the acknowledgment box, and click "Submit".

FAQs

**1. Do I need an Advance Shipment Notification (ASN) to submit an invoice?**

- Yes, an ASN must be created and linked to a Purchase Order (PO) before an invoice can be submitted in Vendor Central. If your shipment does not have an ASN, create one first before proceeding with the invoice submission.

**2. What file formats are accepted for invoices?**

- Invoices are submitted directly within Vendor Central—there is no need to upload a separate file. However, you may be required to attach supporting documents, which should be in PDF format.

**3. How long do I have to submit an invoice after creating an ASN?**

- Amazon typically allows invoice submission within 30 days of ASN creation, but this can vary by agreement. Check your Vendor Central settings for specific timeframes.

**4. What should I do if my invoice submission fails?**

- If your invoice fails to submit:
- Check for missing or incorrect fields (e.g., duplicate invoice number, incorrect PO details).
- Ensure the ASN is fully processed before submitting the invoice.
- Review the error message and correct any issues before resubmitting.

**5. How can I track the status of my submitted invoice?**

- Go to Vendor Central > Payments > Invoices, then search for the invoice using the invoice number or PO number. The status will indicate whether it is pending, approved, or rejected.

**6. What should I do if my invoice is rejected?**

- If Amazon rejects your invoice:

  - Check the rejection reason in Vendor Central > Payments > Invoices.
  - If there is an error, correct the details and resubmit a new invoice with the correct information.
  - Contact Amazon Vendor Support if the rejection reason is unclear.

**7. Can I edit an invoice after submission?**

- No, once an invoice is submitted, it cannot be edited. If changes are needed, you must cancel the invoice and submit a new one with the correct details.

**8. What types of miscellaneous charges can I include on my invoice?**

- Amazon only allows certain additional charges, such as freight fees and handling charges, if they are pre-approved under the terms of your vendor agreement. Unauthorized charges may result in invoice rejection.
