## Adding a Single Item to Vendor Central

**Who is this for?**Typically falls under the responsibility of the vendor or supplier.

**Objective:** Adding a single item to Vendor Central can be an effective way to test the market demand for a new product or validate its potential before expanding your catalog. It allows you to gauge customer response, gather feedback, and make data-driven decisions about further product expansion.

**Instructions:**

1. Go to the Items tab on the top menu and select "Add Products" from the drop-down menu.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4CVimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4CVimage.png)![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Image%2010-10-24%20at%203.19%E2%80%AFAM.jpg)

2. Click “Select product type” button.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bB2image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/bB2image.png)

3. Choose the categories that are suitable for your product and click “Continue”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/S2ximage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/S2ximage.png)

If your product type is not listed, it might require approval, be restricted, or be under review. In that case, list your product under other suitable categories and contact Amazon through your cases to inquire about when and how you can list your product in the desired category.

If you mistakenly ticked a category, you can remove it at the bottom of the pop-up block. Make sure to review your chosen categories before clicking "Continue".

4. Enter the product IDs (ASIN, UPC, EAN, or GTIN) into the block below the category button to see how many of these products are already listed with Amazon. Click "Submit" afterward.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/8Yyimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/8Yyimage.png)

5. The products that are already listed on Amazon using the product IDs you inserted will appear on the right side of the page. Click the "Continue to next step" button.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pNvimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/pNvimage.png)

6. Complete all product information on the next page under each tab heading, namely: Supplier Description, Product, Identity, Pricing, Safety and Compliance, Product Details, and Shipping.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/KAFimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/KAFimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686134294597.png)

7. Once all information is filled out, click the "Submit and Continue" button.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/okWimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/okWimage.png)

**Note that you need to relist your product under each individual Supplier Code you have.**

8. Confirm your added listing by going to the Catalog page.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jSDimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/jSDimage.png)

FAQs

- **Q1: How can I add a single item to Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:** To add a single item, go to the **Items** tab, select **Add Products** from the drop-down menu, choose the appropriate product category, fill in the product details, and click **Submit and Continue** to finalize the listing.
- **Q2: What should I do if my product type is not listed under the available categories?**

  - **A2:** If your product type isn’t listed, you can either list the product under a suitable alternative category or contact Amazon through **Your Cases** to inquire about approval or restrictions for the desired category.
- **Q3: How do I verify whether the product is already listed on Amazon?**

  - **A3:** Enter the product IDs (ASIN, UPC, EAN, or GTIN) in the designated field to check if the product is already listed. If it is, the existing listing will appear on the right side of the page.
- **Q4: What information do I need to provide when adding a single item?**

  - **A4:** Complete all required product details under the following tabs: **Supplier Description**, **Product**, **Identity**, **Pricing**, **Safety and Compliance**, **Product Details**, and **Shipping**.
- **Q5: How do I confirm that my product has been successfully added?**

  - **A5:** Once you’ve submitted the product details, go to the **Catalog** page to confirm that your listing has been added successfully.
- **Q6: Do I need to relist my product for each Supplier Code?**

  - **A6:** Yes, you must relist your product under each individual **Supplier Code** associated with your Vendor Central account.
