## Setting up Business Pricing Discounts

**Who is this for :** Setting up Amazon Business Pricing Discounts is typically the responsibility of the Amazon Seller or Vendor Central account owner within an organization. The specific individuals involved may vary depending on the organizational structure and the role assigned to managing Amazon accounts.

**Objective :** The objective of setting up business pricing discounts is to establish a pricing strategy that targets business customers and encourages them to make purchases by offering them discounted prices.

- Before implementing business pricing discounts, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.
- Vendor Central can be used to manage quantity discounts for Amazon Business customers, including setting up, editing, and canceling discounts.

FAQs

**1. Who is eligible to set up Business Pricing Discounts on Amazon?**

- Only Amazon Business sellers and vendors can create business pricing discounts. If you don’t see the "Business Discounts" option in Vendor Central, check your account settings or contact Amazon support.

**2. Is there a minimum or maximum discount I can set?**

- The maximum discount you can offer is 50% off Amazon’s cost. Amazon does not enforce a minimum discount, but competitive pricing is recommended to attract business customers.

**3. Can I set tiered discounts based on quantity purchased?**

- Yes, Amazon Business allows quantity-based discounts, where bulk buyers get greater savings. You can specify price breaks for different order quantities when setting up discounts.

**4. Will my business pricing discounts affect other promotions like Subscribe & Save or Coupons?**

- No, business pricing discounts do not apply to Subscribe & Save, Lightning Deals, or Coupons. These programs run independently, and business customers will only see the business price if they qualify.

**5. Can I update multiple business pricing discounts at once?**

- Yes, you can bulk update discounts by uploading a new Excel spreadsheet with updated pricing. Any new discount submission overwrites the previous one for the same ASIN.

**6. How long does it take to remove or adjust a discount?**

- Price adjustments or removals typically take up to 48 hours to reflect on Amazon. To remove a discount, delete the discount percentage or number of units in the spreadsheet and select the correct option from the Remove Discounts column.

**7. Does setting up a business pricing discount impact Buy Box eligibility?**

- No, business pricing discounts do not directly impact Buy Box eligibility for standard retail customers. However, competitive pricing can increase your chances of winning the Business Buy Box, which is separate from the regular Buy Box.

**8. Will I be notified if my pricing spreadsheet contains errors?**

- No, Amazon does not send notifications for errors. You must manually check the status under "Check submission or see all uploads" in Vendor Central and click "View Errors" if the upload fails.

**9. How can I track the performance of my business pricing discounts?**

- Go to Vendor Central > Business Reports to monitor sales performance, conversion rates, and revenue impact of your business pricing discounts. Analyzing this data helps refine your pricing strategy over time.
