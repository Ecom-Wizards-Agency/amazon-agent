## Applying for the Manufacturer Validation Program (MVP)

**Who is this for :**

- This is intended for vendors who have access to Vendor Central and have received an invitation or notice regarding the Manufacturer Validation Program (MVP).
- It is relevant for vendors who want to simplify the import process, minimize document submissions, and achieve approval for Direct Import and NASP for their catalog.

**Objective :** To provide clear instructions on how to apply for the Manufacturer Validation Program (MVP) in Vendor Central.

**Instructions**

1. MVP is a program that streamlines the import process for vendors, reducing document submissions by 90% through compliance verification of a sample of your catalog.

2. This exclusive program is invite-only and eligible vendors will see a notice in their Business Advisor on Vendor Central under the "Required Actions" or "Recommendations" sections.

3. If you have received an invitation email or been directed to the MVP page on Vendor Central by Business Advisor, click "Support".

FAQs

**1. How long does the MVP approval process take?**

- The approval process can take 2-4 weeks, depending on the volume of applications and the complexity of document verification. You can track your application status under Vendor Central > Support > Case Log.

**2. Can I apply for MVP if I haven’t received an invitation?**

- No, MVP is invite-only. If you haven’t received an invitation but believe you qualify, you can contact Amazon Vendor Support and inquire about eligibility.

**3. What types of documents do I need to submit?**

- Amazon typically requests:

  - Compliance certificates (e.g., safety certifications, product testing reports).
  - Country of origin documentation.
  - Product labeling and packaging details.
  - Import/export compliance forms.
  - Amazon may request additional documents based on the ASINs submitted for verification.

**4. What should I do if I encounter issues while applying?**

- If you experience submission errors:

  - Ensure all required fields are completed.
  - Check that document file formats comply with Amazon’s requirements.
  - Clear your browser cache or use a different browser.
  - If the issue persists, submit a support case through Vendor Central > Support > Contact Us > Product Compliance > MVP.

**5. Does MVP approval apply to my entire catalog or just the ASINs reviewed?**

- MVP approval is granted based on the sample ASINs submitted. However, once approved, your entire catalog may benefit from the streamlined import process.

**6. Do I need to renew my MVP status?**

- Amazon may periodically reassess compliance to ensure continued eligibility. Keep all compliance documents up to date, as Amazon may request revalidation.

**7. How can I track my MVP application status?**

- You can monitor your case status under Vendor Central > Support > Case Log. If your case is marked as pending additional information, review the case details and submit the requested documents.
