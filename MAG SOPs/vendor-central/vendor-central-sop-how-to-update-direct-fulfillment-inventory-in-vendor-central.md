# Vendor Central SOP: How to Update Direct Fulfillment Inventory in Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-how-to-update-direct-fulfillment-inventory-in-vendor-central

## **How to Update Direct Fulfillment Inventory in Vendor Central**

**Who is this for?** This SOP is intended for specialists managing Direct Fulfillment inventory through Vendor Central.

**Objective:** The objective of this SOP is to guide specialists through the process of keeping the client’s inventory levels accurate and up-to-date, ensuring optimal product availability and customer satisfaction.

**I. How to Access Your Direct Fulfillment Inventory**

1. Log into [**Vendor Central**](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/ap/signin?clientContext=131-3531479-1652724&openid.pape.preferred_auth_policies=SinglefactorWithPossessionChallenge&openid.return_to=https%3A%2F%2Fvendorcentral.amazon.com%2F&openid.identity=http%3A%2F%2Fspecs.openid.net%2Fauth%2F2.0%2Fidentifier_select&openid.assoc_handle=amzn_vc_us_v2&openid.mode=checkid_setup&intercept=false&language=en_US&openid.claimed_id=http%3A%2F%2Fspecs.openid.net%2Fauth%2F2.0%2Fidentifier_select&pageId=amzn_vc_us_auth&openid.ns=http%3A%2F%2Fspecs.openid.net%2Fauth%2F2.0).
2. In the main menu, click on **Items**, then select [**Direct Fulfillment Inventory**](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/hz/vendor/members/df/inventory/home?ref_=vc_xx_subNav). This will bring up a list of all your available products.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/QyLimage.png)

Here, you can see detailed information about your products, along with easy options to filter and update your inventory.

**II. How to Update Your Inventory**

1. **Updating a Single Product**

- Go to the [**Direct Fulfillment Inventory**](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/hz/vendor/members/df/inventory/home?ref_=vc_xx_subNav) page.
- Find the product you want to update and head to the **Availability** column.
- Manually enter the number of units currently available for that product.
- Hit **Save** to update the inventory.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/nfFimage.png)

1. **Updating Multiple Products**

If you need to update several products but not your entire catalog:

- Select the products you want to update.
- From the **Available actions** drop-down menu, choose **Download inventory**.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-how-to-update-direct-fulfillment-inventory-in-vendor-central-image-owzrgfyj.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vJiimage.png)

- Follow the instructions in the download document to make your updates.
- Once updated, go back to the **Direct Fulfillment Inventory** page and click **Upload inventory** to upload your updated file.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6fYimage.png)

1. **Updating All Your Products**

If you’re updating a large number of products:

- Click **Update with a spreadsheet** on the Direct Fulfillment page.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/EVIimage.png)
- Download the provided spreadsheet template.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jUtimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6P2image.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/t69image.png)

- Update the inventory data in the spreadsheet, and save it as a **CSV file**.
- Upload the updated CSV back to the **Direct Fulfillment Inventory** page to apply the changes.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DFWimage.png)

1. **Updating via EDI/API**

For those using **EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) or API (Application Programming Interface)** integration, you can directly update your inventory in real-time via Amazon’s systems. Just make sure your integration is set up correctly.

**III. Marking Products as Obsolete or Reactivating**

- To mark products as **obsolete**, select the product(s) you want to take off the market.
- Under the **Available actions** drop-down, choose **Mark obsolete**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/wk2image.png)

- If you want to reactivate a product, just select it and choose **Mark active**.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7Gpu3Simage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-how-to-update-direct-fulfillment-inventory-in-vendor-central-image-aswfh8e2.png)

**IV. Updating Product Costs or List Prices**

If you need to update the cost or list price for a product:

- Navigate to [**Edit item costs**](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/vendor/members/cost).

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hvnimage.png)

- For more help, you can refer to the [**Update costs help page**](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/hz/vendor/members/support/help/node/G201371510) for more details.

**V. How to Update Package Dimensions and Weight**

1. **Update Dimensions and Weight for One Product**

- From the **Direct Fulfillment Inventory** page, click on the product name to open its **Item details page**.
- On the Item details page, click **Edit**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-how-to-update-direct-fulfillment-inventory-in-vendor-central-image-cvg9rsjr.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Rvqimage.png)

- Update the package’s **width**, **height**, **length**, and/or **shipping weight**.
- Hit **Save** to apply the changes.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-how-to-update-direct-fulfillment-inventory-in-vendor-central-image-yu7f961d.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Nuoimage.png)

**2.Update Dimensions and Weight for Multiple Products**

- Go to the **Direct Fulfillment Inventory** page and click **Update with a spreadsheet**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-how-to-update-direct-fulfillment-inventory-in-vendor-central-image-v9xb99n0.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ykXimage.png)

- Download the **multi-box file**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-how-to-update-direct-fulfillment-inventory-in-vendor-central-image-yx26cmut.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/OrIimage.png)

- Update the package dimensions and weight in the downloaded file and save it.
- Return to **Update with a spreadsheet** and upload the updated file.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-how-to-update-direct-fulfillment-inventory-in-vendor-central-image-rr46ih4e.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/FXNimage.png)

**NOTE**: For some **single-box ASINs**, Amazon may automatically update and lock package dimensions based on carrier feedback. However, you’ll still be able to update the **package weight**.

**IMPORTANT NOTES:**

**Suppressed Due to Inventory Issues**

- **Excess Inventory**: If a product is suppressed because Amazon has too much stock, you don’t need to take any action. Once the inventory balances out, Amazon will automatically review the situation.
- **Missing Cost Information**: If your product is suppressed due to missing cost data, you’ll need to add the cost in the [**Edit item costs**](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/vendor/members/cost) section.

**Handling Logistics Constraints**

If there’s an issue with your production or logistics that impacts delivery time:

- We recommend temporarily setting your inventory for those affected products to **zero** until the issue is fixed. This will prevent customers from placing orders for products you can’t deliver on time.
