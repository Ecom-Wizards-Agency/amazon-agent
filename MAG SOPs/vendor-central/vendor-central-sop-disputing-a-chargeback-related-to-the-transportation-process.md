# Vendor Central SOP: Disputing a Chargeback Related to the Transportation Process

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-disputing-a-chargeback-related-to-the-transportation-process

## Disputing a Chargeback Related to the Transportation Process

**Who is this for**:

- This is intended for vendors who have access to Vendor Central and need to dispute chargebacks specifically related to the transportation process.
- It is relevant for vendors who have received chargebacks for non-compliance related to Pickup Accuracy, No Show, Import Shipment Late Booking, Import PO On-Time Non-Compliance, and Import Documents Late Delivery.

**Objective**:

- To provide vendors with a clear process for disputing chargebacks related to the transportation process in Vendor Central.
- To inform vendors about the specific documentation requirements for each type of chargeback related to the transportation process, ensuring they provide the appropriate evidence to support their dispute.

1. Go to Vendor Central's "Reports" tab on the top menu and select "Operational Performance" from the drop-down menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1720668130270.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/1Plimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/1Plimage.png)

2. On the top page, click on the "View All" link to go to the chargeback in detail page. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684884326886.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/X41image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/X41image.png)

3. Locate for non-compliance related to the Transportation Process on the transaction page. These may include Pickup Accuracy (no penalty fees charged at this time), No Show, Import Shipment Late Booking, Import PO On-Time Non-Compliance, and Import Documents Late Delivery.

4. On the next page, select the Chargeback(s) you want to dispute and click on "Dispute" button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684884582292.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YyIimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/YyIimage.png)

5. Fill in all the relevant information in the "Dispute Summary" text box, explaining why you think Amazon's assessment that this is non-compliance is incorrect. 6. Upload the documentation required to support your dispute by clicking the "Add Attachments" button below the "Dispute Summary" text box. Locate the relevant document and select it for upload to the dispute. 7. Click on the "Submit Dispute" button at the top right of the page to submit your dispute for these types of Chargebacks. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684884652737.png)

**Notes:**

- You can only dispute the same Chargeback twice. If a Chargeback has been disputed and rejected twice, it is no longer eligible for dispute.
- You have 30 days from the date the Chargeback was raised in Vendor Central to dispute it.

Documentation Required for Disputes of Chargebacks relating to Confirming or Editing Purchase Orders:

**For the No-Show Chargeback:**

- Provide the Amazon Delivery Record or the carrier Proof of Delivery for the delivered goods. Both must be date-stamped by Amazon and show the FC address and the Inbound Shipment Appointment (ISA) number.

**For Import shipment late booking chargeback:**

- Provide a screenshot of the origin service provider's booking system (VMS) with the confirmed PO's Earliest Vendor Ship Date, and/or a screenshot of the origin service provider's booking system (VMS) with the submitted booking.

**For Import PO on-time non-compliance chargeback:**

- Provide a screenshot of the origin service provider's booking system (VMS) showing the confirmed PO Earliest Vendor Ship Date and Latest Vendor Ship Date, and/or a screenshot of the VMS with the Estimated Cargo Delivery Date.

**For Import documents late delivery chargeback:**

- Provide a copy of the email to the origin service provider showing the "sent" date, a copy of the fax or courier receipt showing the delivery date to the origin service provider, and a screenshot of the origin service provider's booking system (VMS) showing the Document Receive Date.

FAQs

- **Q1: How do I dispute a chargeback related to the transportation process?**

  - **A1:**To dispute a transportation-related chargeback, go to the "Reports" tab in Vendor Central, select "Operational Performance," and click on "View All." Locate the relevant chargeback under the transportation process section, select the chargeback you want to dispute, and click the "Dispute" button.
- **Q2: What chargeback types can be disputed under the transportation process?**

  - **A2:**The chargebacks that can be disputed under the transportation process include Pickup Accuracy, No Show, Import Shipment Late Booking, Import PO On-Time Non-Compliance, and Import Documents Late Delivery.
- **Q3: How many times can I dispute the same chargeback related to transportation?**

  - **A3:** You can dispute the same chargeback only twice. After two disputes, the chargeback is no longer eligible for dispute.
- **Q4: What documentation do I need to dispute a No Show chargeback?**

  - **A4:**For a No Show chargeback, provide the Amazon Delivery Record or the carrier Proof of Delivery for the delivered goods. These documents must be date-stamped by Amazon, showing the FC address and the Inbound Shipment Appointment (ISA) number.
- **Q5: What documentation is required for disputing an Import Shipment Late Booking chargeback?**

  - **A5:** For this chargeback, provide a screenshot of the origin service provider's booking system (VMS) with the confirmed PO's Earliest Vendor Ship Date and/or a screenshot of the VMS with the submitted booking.
- **Q6: How do I dispute an Import PO On-Time Non-Compliance chargeback?**

  - **A6:** To dispute this chargeback, provide a screenshot from the origin service provider's booking system (VMS) showing the confirmed PO Earliest Vendor Ship Date and Latest Vendor Ship Date, and/or a screenshot of the VMS with the Estimated Cargo Delivery Date.
- **Q7: What is the deadline for disputing a chargeback related to transportation?**

  - **A7:** Chargebacks must be disputed within 30 days from the date they were raised in Vendor Central.
- **Q8: How do I submit documentation for my dispute?**

  - **A8:** To submit supporting documentation, click the "Add Attachments" button below the "Dispute Summary" text box, locate the relevant document on your computer, and select it for upload.
- **Q9: Can I dispute a chargeback if I don’t have the required documentation?**

  - **A9:** It is highly recommended to provide the necessary documentation to support your dispute. Without the required documentation, your dispute may not be successful.
- **Q10: What happens if my dispute is rejected?**

  - **A10:** If your dispute is rejected, you may not dispute it further. You will need to accept the chargeback, but you can review the reason for rejection and potentially work with Amazon support for clarification or future prevention.
