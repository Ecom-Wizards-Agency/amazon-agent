## Producing a Market Basket Analysis Report

**Who is this for:**

This is intended for Amazon vendors who want to gain insights into consumer behavior and identify potential product opportunities for their brands.

**Objective:**

To guide Amazon vendors in accessing and utilizing the Market Basket Analysis report on Vendor Central. By following the provided steps, vendors will be able to generate a report that identifies the most frequently purchased products in conjunction with their own, allowing them to discover comparable products and opportunities for new product development or bundling.

The Market Basket Analysis report on Vendor Central is a valuable tool for Amazon vendors seeking to understand consumer behavior and identify potential growth opportunities for their brand. This SOP will walk you through the process of accessing this report effectively.

**Instructions:**

1. From your Vendor Central Dashboard, navigate to the "Reports" tab in the top menu then select “Brand Analytics”

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-11-20%20at%202.53.57%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/23Himage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/23Himage.png)
2. In the Brand Analytics section, click on "Market Basket Analysis" to access the report.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-11-20%20at%202.55.24%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/56timage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/56timage.png)
3. Once the Market Basket Analysis report page loads, you can filter the data by the following criteria:

  1. **Reporting Range:** Choose the range of data you wish to view (e.g., Weekly, Monthly, or Quarterly).
  2. **Combinations of Products Displayed:** Select how many products you'd like to see in the report.
  3. **Brand:**Filter by your specific brand, if applicable.
  4. **ASIN Search:** If you want to focus on specific ASINs, you can filter based on the ASINs you're interested in.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-11-20%20at%202.57.31%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/FGOimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/FGOimage.png)
4. The table in the report includes the following information:

  1. The ASIN most frequently purchased with your product
  2. Percentage of orders that contain both your product and the #1 purchased product, compared to the total number of orders that contained at least two different items including your product
  3. The ASIN of the product that is second most frequently purchased with your product
  4. Percentage of orders that contain both your product and the #2 purchased product, compared to the total number of orders that contained at least two different items including your product
  5. The ASIN of the product that is third most frequently purchased with your product
  6. Percentage of orders that contain both your product and the #3 purchased product, compared to the total number of orders that contained at least two different items including your product

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687372099969(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dP8image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/dP8image.png)
5. This report can help you identify comparable products and opportunities for new product development or bundling.
6. To view the report based on your selected data criteria, click "Apply."

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687372409404(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/71zimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/71zimage.png)
7. Once you have selected your preferred views, you can download the report by clicking on the "Download" button located in the top right-hand corner of the page.

FAQs

- **Q1: What is the Market Basket Analysis report and why is it useful?**

  - **A1:** The Market Basket Analysis report is a tool on Amazon Vendor Central that provides insights into consumer behavior by showing the most frequently purchased products in conjunction with your own. It helps vendors identify comparable products, opportunities for new product development, and bundling opportunities.
- **Q2: How do I access the Market Basket Analysis report in Vendor Central?**

  - **A2:** From your Vendor Central Dashboard, navigate to the "Reports" tab in the top menu, then select "Brand Analytics." In the Brand Analytics section, click on "Market Basket Analysis" to access the report.
- **Q3: What filters can I apply when viewing the Market Basket Analysis report?**

  - **A3:** You can filter the report by the following criteria:

    - **Reporting Range**: Choose between Weekly, Monthly, or Quarterly data.
    - **Combinations of Products Displayed**: Select how many products you want to see in the report.
    - **Brand**: Filter by your specific brand, if applicable.
    - **ASIN Search**: Focus on specific ASINs of interest.
- **Q4: What information is included in the Market Basket Analysis report?**

  - **A4:** The report includes:

    - The ASIN most frequently purchased with your product and the percentage of orders that contain both products.
    - The ASINs of the second and third most frequently purchased products with your product, along with their respective percentages of orders containing both items.
- **Q5: How do I view the Market Basket Analysis report based on my selected criteria?**

  - **A5:** After applying your preferred filters, click "Apply" to view the report based on your selected data criteria.
- **Q6: Can I download the Market Basket Analysis report?**

  - **A6:** Yes, after applying your preferred views, you can download the report by clicking on the "Download" button located in the top right-hand corner of the page.
- **Q7: How can the Market Basket Analysis report help with product development?**

  - **A7:** The report helps identify products frequently bought together with your own, providing insights into comparable products and opportunities for new product development or bundling strategies.
