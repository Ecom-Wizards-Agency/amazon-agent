# Vendor Central SOP: Cancelling a Dispute

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-cancelling-a-dispute

## **Cancelling a Dispute**

**Who is this for?:** This SOP is relevant to vendors who have identified valid evidence supporting Amazon's claim, discovered inaccuracies in their submitted documentation, or for any other reason require the cancellation of a dispute they have initiated.

**Objective:**

- To guide vendors in the process of cancelling a dispute they have created on Amazon.
- To provide vendors with a clear process for requesting the cancellation of a dispute, ensuring they provide a reason for the cancellation request.

To cancel a dispute you have created for any reason, such as discovering evidence that Amazon's claim was valid or realizing you submitted incorrect documentation, follow these steps:

1. Go to the Dispute Management Page by selecting "Dispute Management" from the "Payments" tab on the top menu of your Vendor Central Dashboard.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/A0wimage.png)

2. Under the "Disputes Summary" heading, click on "All Active Disputes" and find the specific dispute you wish to cancel from the table of disputes at the bottom of the page.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9bwimage.png)

3. Click on "Request Cancellation" located to the right of the dispute line under the "Actions" column.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iEXimage.png)

4. In the pop-up window that appears, provide a reason for your cancellation request and click "OK" at the bottom right corner of the window.

FAQs

**1. Can all disputes be canceled, or are there restrictions?**

- Most disputes can be canceled, but some dispute types may be locked once Amazon has started reviewing them. If the "Request Cancellation" button is unavailable, the dispute may be too far along in the process to cancel.

**2. Is there a deadline for canceling a dispute?**

- There is no fixed deadline, but once Amazon has processed the claim or issued a decision, the dispute may no longer be cancelable. It’s best to act quickly if you need to cancel.

**3. Can I undo a dispute cancellation if I made a mistake?**

- No, once a dispute is canceled, it cannot be reinstated. If you still believe there is an issue, you will need to submit a new dispute with the correct information.

**4. How can I check if my cancellation request has been processed?**

- After submitting the request, check the Dispute Management page under "All Disputes". The dispute status should update to "Canceled" once the request is processed.

**5. Will canceling a dispute affect my ability to submit future claims?**

- No, canceling a dispute does not impact your ability to submit future disputes. However, frequent cancellations without valid reasons may raise red flags with Amazon.

**6. What should I do if the "Request Cancellation" button is missing?**

- If the cancellation option is unavailable, the dispute may already be under review. In such cases, you can contact Amazon Vendor Support through Vendor Central > Help > Contact Us > Payments & Disputes for further assistance.

**7. Can I cancel multiple disputes at once?**

- No, disputes must be canceled one at a time. There is no bulk cancellation option available in Vendor Central.
