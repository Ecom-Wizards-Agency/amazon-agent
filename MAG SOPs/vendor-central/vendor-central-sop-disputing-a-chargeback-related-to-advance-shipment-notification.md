# Vendor Central SOP: Disputing a Chargeback Related to Advance Shipment Notification

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-disputing-a-chargeback-related-to-advance-shipment-notification

## Disputing a Chargeback Related to Advance Shipment Notification

**Who is this for:**

- This is intended for vendors who have access to Vendor Central and need to dispute chargebacks specifically related to Advance Shipment Notification (ASN).
- It is relevant for vendors who have received chargebacks for non-compliance related to Carton Content Accuracy and ASN Accuracy.

**Objective:**

- To provide vendors with a clear process for disputing chargebacks related to Advance Shipment Notification (ASN) in Vendor Central.
- To inform vendors about the specific documentation requirements for each type of chargeback related to ASN, ensuring they provide the appropriate evidence to support their dispute.

1. Navigate to the "Reports" tab on the top menu of Vendor Central, and select "Operational Performance" from the drop-down menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Vendor-Central-SOP-Disputing-a-Chargeback-Related-to-Advance-Shipment-Notification-1.png)

2. On the top page, click on the "View All" link to go to the chargeback in detail page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Vendor-Central-SOP-Disputing-a-Chargeback-Related-to-Advance-Shipment-Notification-2.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VcPimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/VcPimage.png)

3. Locate the non-compliance related to Advance Shipment Notifications (ASN). Choose from the following two options:

- Carton Content Accuracy
- ASN Accuracy

4. On the next page, select the chargeback(s) you want to dispute and click on "Dispute" button.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Vendor-Central-SOP-Disputing-a-Chargeback-Related-to-Advance-Shipment-Notification-3.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LqWimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/LqWimage.png)

5. Fill in all necessary information in the "Dispute Summary" text box, explaining your reasons for disputing this Chargeback and why you don't agree with Amazon's assessment that this is non-compliance.
6. Attach the required documents to support your dispute by clicking "Add Attachments" beneath the "Dispute Summary" text box. Locate each relevant document and upload it to the dispute.
7. Click on the "Submit Dispute" button located at the top right side of the page to submit your dispute for these types of Chargebacks.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Bvdimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Bvdimage.png)

**Important notes to remember:**

- You can only dispute a Chargeback twice. If a Chargeback has been disputed and rejected twice, it is no longer eligible for dispute.
- You have 30 days from the date the Chargeback was raised in Vendor Central in which to dispute it.

**Documentation Required for Disputes of Chargebacks Relating to Advance Shipment Notifications (ASN):**

- For Carton Content Accuracy Chargebacks:
- For item labels on case packs/master packs, provide clear images that show a face open carton, inner packs, and individual sellable units of the ASIN. These images should illustrate how the items are packed inside the carton for shipping. Ensure that all barcodes are clearly visible in the images.
- For wrong items received compared to the information in the ASN, provide a clear image of the barcode for the missing item.
- For ASN Accuracy Chargebacks:
- Provide a screenshot of the ASN in Vendor Central, or
- Provide a text copy/XML of the EDI 856 transmission that shows the ASN was submitted before the shipment was received.
- In case of a missing expiration date, indicate the type of carton-compliant labels used (SSCC/Barcode Packing slip/GTIN14/AMZNCC).

FAQs

General Process

- **Q1: What is the purpose of disputing a chargeback related to ASN?**

  - **A1:** Disputing a chargeback related to ASN allows vendors to challenge non-compliance chargebacks regarding Carton Content Accuracy or ASN Accuracy by providing the required evidence and documentation.
- **Q2: How do I begin the process of disputing a chargeback related to ASN?**

  - **A2:** Navigate to the "Reports" tab in Vendor Central, select "Operational Performance," and click "View All" to access the chargeback details. Choose the chargeback related to ASN, either Carton Content Accuracy or ASN Accuracy, and proceed to dispute it.

Steps and Navigation

- **Q3: How do I locate a specific ASN-related chargeback?**

  - **A3:** On the chargeback detail page, locate the non-compliance related to ASN, and choose from the two types: Carton Content Accuracy or ASN Accuracy.
- **Q4: Can I dispute multiple ASN-related chargebacks at once?**

  - **A4:** Yes, you can select multiple ASN-related chargebacks and dispute them together.

Dispute Process

- **Q5: What should I include in the "Dispute Summary" when disputing an ASN-related chargeback?**

  - **A5:** Provide a detailed explanation of why you disagree with Amazon’s assessment, addressing the specific ASN-related issue (either Carton Content Accuracy or ASN Accuracy). Ensure your explanation is clear and well-supported.
- **Q6: How do I upload supporting documentation for an ASN chargeback dispute?**

  - **A6:** Click the "Add Attachments" button below the "Dispute Summary" text box, then upload the relevant images or documents that support your dispute.
- **Q7: What do I do after I’ve entered all the necessary information and uploaded my documents?**

  - **A7:** After filling out the dispute summary and uploading your supporting documentation, click the "Submit Dispute" button at the top right of the page to submit the dispute.

Supporting Documentation

- **Q8: What documentation is required for a Carton Content Accuracy chargeback?**

  - **A8:** Provide clear images that show:

    - A face-open carton.
    - Inner packs.
    - Individual sellable units of the ASIN.
    - Barcodes that are clearly visible for all items.
      Additionally, if the wrong items were received compared to the ASN, provide a clear image of the barcode for the missing item.
- **Q9: What documentation is needed for an ASN Accuracy chargeback?**

  - **A9:** Submit a screenshot of the ASN in Vendor Central or provide a text copy/XML of the EDI 856 transmission that confirms the ASN was submitted before the shipment was received. If the expiration date is missing, indicate the type of carton-compliant labels used (SSCC/Barcode Packing slip/GTIN-14/AMZNCC).

Submission and Review

- **Q10: Is there a time limit for disputing an ASN chargeback?**

  - **A10:** Yes, you must submit your dispute within 30 days from the date the chargeback was raised in Vendor Central.
- **Q11: Can I dispute the same ASN chargeback multiple times?**

  - **A11:** No, you can only dispute a chargeback twice. After two rejections, the chargeback is no longer eligible for dispute.

Best Practices

- **Q12: How can I improve my chances of a successful ASN chargeback dispute?**

  - **A12:** Ensure your documentation is clear, accurate, and timely. Double-check that the evidence you provide directly supports the dispute and addresses the specific chargeback issue, whether it is related to Carton Content Accuracy or ASN Accuracy.
- **Q13: What should I do if my dispute is rejected twice?**

  - **A13:** If your dispute is rejected twice, the chargeback cannot be disputed further. Review the reasons for rejection carefully to identify any areas of improvement for future ASN compliance.
