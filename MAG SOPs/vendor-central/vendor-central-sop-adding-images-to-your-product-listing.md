# Vendor Central SOP: Adding Images to Your Product Listing

## Adding Images to Your Product Listing

**Who is this for**:

- This is for sellers and vendors on Amazon who need to add images to their product listings.
- The target audience includes sellers and vendors who have access to Vendor Central and want to enhance their product listings by including relevant and visually appealing images.

**Objective**:

- To guide sellers and vendors in successfully adding images to their product listings on Amazon.
- To improve the visual representation of products, attract customer attention, and enhance the overall shopping experience.

Before uploading any images, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.

**Instructions**:

1. Navigate to the "Items" tab in the top menu of Vendor Central, then select "Upload Images" from the Drop-Down Menu.

2. Search the ASIN you want to upload images for.

3. You can click the "Live images" link to check the ASIN's current images.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685442470529.png)

4. On the right side of the page, upload your own images under "Images submitted by you" by clicking Upload for each main, front, side, back, or image variant. For the naming convention guidelines please read [https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/hz/vendor/members/support/help/node/G201310030](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/hz/vendor/members/support/help/node/G201310030)

5. Select an image from your computer and click Open. This will submit the image to that variant.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685751395672.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/tMDimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/tMDimage.png)

6. To delete an image, click the trash bin icon under the image variant. Once deleted, a new image can be uploaded to that variant. Images shared between child products will need to be deleted individually from each ASIN. Main image, Front, Side, Back, and PT01 images can only be replaced, not deleted.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1713824677769.png)

7. Once uploaded, check the Submission status to review the status of your uploaded images, and resolve any failed uploads.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685442737263.png)

8. If your image is rejected, Vendor Central will notify you. Review the Amazon image requirements to determine why the image was rejected, make necessary changes, and then re-upload the image.

FAQs

Approval Process

- **Q1: What should I do before uploading images to the product listing?**

  - **A1:** Before uploading any images, it's crucial to communicate with your client and get their approval first. Ensure you include the approval proof, such as a screenshot or Fireflies call link, as Task Details or as a Comment.

Navigation

- **Q2: How do I access the "Upload Images" option in Vendor Central?**

  - **A2:** Navigate to the "Items" tab in the top menu of Vendor Central, then select "Upload Images" from the drop-down menu.

Image Types

- **Q3: How can I check the current images for an ASIN before uploading new ones?**

  - **A3:** You can click the "Live images" link to view the ASIN’s current images.
    **Category:** Image Review
- **Q4: What types of images can I upload for a product listing?**

  - **A4:** You can upload images for the main image, front, side, back, and image variants.

Image Naming

- **Q5: How should I name my images before uploading them?**

  - **A5:** Follow the specific naming convention guidelines provided in Amazon's help documentation, accessible at [this link](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/hz/vendor/members/support/help/node/G201310030).

Image Management

- **Q6: What happens if I want to delete an image from the listing?**

  - **A6:** To delete an image, click the trash bin icon under the image variant. Once deleted, you can upload a new image for that variant. If images are shared between child products, you need to delete them individually from each ASIN. Main images, Front, Side, Back, and PT01 images can only be replaced, not deleted.

Image Submission Status

- **Q7: How can I track the status of my uploaded images?**

  - **A7:** After uploading, check the Submission status to review the status of your images. If any uploads fail, resolve the issue and re-upload the image.

Image Rejection

- **Q8: What should I do if my image is rejected?**

  - **A8:** If your image is rejected, Vendor Central will notify you. Review Amazon's image requirements, make the necessary changes, and then re-upload the image.
