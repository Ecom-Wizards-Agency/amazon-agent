## Producing a Repeat Purchase Behavior Report

**Who is this for :** This is intended for vendors on Amazon who are responsible for analyzing customer behavior and brand loyalty in order to develop effective marketing campaigns and strategies.

**Objective :** To guide vendors in accessing and generating a Repeat Purchase Behavior Report on Amazon Vendor Central. By following the provided steps, users will be able to filter the report based on specific criteria and download the necessary data. The information obtained from this report will help them gain insights into customer behavior, assess brand loyalty, and utilize the findings to enhance marketing campaigns, improve product pages, and create targeted promotional activities.

In order to gain a competitive edge in the e-commerce market, it is crucial to understand customer behavior and cultivate brand loyalty. Amazon Seller Central provides a comprehensive set of tools and reports to assist sellers and vendors in this process. One such report is the Repeat Purchase Behavior Report, which offers valuable data on customer orders, repeat customer share, and repeat ordered product sales.

FAQs

**1. How often does the Repeat Purchase Behavior Report update?**

- The report updates weekly, monthly, and quarterly, depending on the time frame selected. Some data points may have a slight lag of a few days before they appear in Vendor Central.

**2. How far back can I access historical repeat purchase data?**

- Amazon allows vendors to retrieve repeat purchase data for up to two years. If you need older reports, consider downloading and storing them periodically for reference.

**3. What does Repeat Customer Share mean, and why is it important?**

- Repeat Customer Share represents the percentage of total orders from returning customers.
- A high percentage suggests strong brand loyalty, while a low percentage may indicate the need for retention strategies like promotions or bundling.

**4. What should I do if data is missing or incorrect?**

- If your report has missing or incorrect data:

  - Ensure that the correct date range and filters are applied.
  - Check if the report is still updating (some data may have a delay).
  - Compare with other reports, like Sales Diagnostic Reports, for inconsistencies.
  - If the issue persists, contact Amazon Vendor Support.

**5. Can this report help with more than just marketing strategies?**

- Yes! Beyond marketing, vendors can use the insights for:

  - Product development – Identify high-retention products and develop similar offerings.
  - Inventory planning – Ensure adequate stock for products with high repeat purchases.
  - Pricing strategies – Adjust pricing to maximize customer lifetime value.

**6. Can I filter repeat purchase data by region or customer demographics?**

- No, Amazon does not currently provide customer demographic data in this report. However, you can analyze by category, ASIN, and brand to spot trends in repeat purchases across different product lines.

**7. Can I automate or schedule Repeat Purchase Behavior Report downloads?**

- No, Amazon does not currently allow automated downloads for this report. However, you can manually download it as needed by navigating to Reports > Brand Analytics > Repeat Purchase Behavior.
