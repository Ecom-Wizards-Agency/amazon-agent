# Vendor Central SOP: Tracking the status of your Catalog Feed Submission

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-tracking-the-status-of-your-catalog-feed-submission

## Tracking the status of your Catalog Feed Submission

**Who is this for?:** Tracking catalog feed submission is typically the responsibility of the person or team managing the catalog management or e-commerce operations within an organization. This role may vary depending on the organizational structure, but the tasks associated with tracking catalog feed submission can be carried out by E-commerce Manager and Catalog Specialist.

**Objective:** The objective of tracking catalog feed submission status is to monitor the progress and outcome of your catalog feed submission process. It helps ensure that your product data is successfully processed by the platform or marketplace you are submitting to.

There are two ways to access submission status reports:

**I. Through your CSP's software**

- After submitting your product content to your Catalog Service Providers (CSP), log in to their software and check if your submission has been validated.
- If validation fails, contact your CSP to resolve any issues and resubmit the content until it's validated.
- You can find the submission status report under "State 1" in the "Reading Your Submission Status Report" section of this SOP.

**II. Through Amazon Vendor Central**

Once your content has been validated and published on Amazon, Vendor Central generates a submission acknowledgment report and sends a notification to your CSP's software.

- To access this report, go to the "Manage Catalog Feeds" page under the "Items" tab on the top menu of Vendor Central.
