# Vendor Central SOP: Notifying Amazon of a Product Recall

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-notifying-amazon-of-a-product-recall

## Notifying Amazon of a Product Recall

**Objectives:**This SOP outlines the step-by-step process for notifying Amazon of a product recall in **Vendor Central**. It aims to ensure efficient and consistent communication, minimize risks to customer safety, and maintain regulatory compliance. Amazon strictly prohibits the sale of any product that has been recalled by the manufacturer, brand owner, or a government regulatory agency.

**Who is this for?**This SOP is designed for the **Product Safety** **and Compliance Team, Vendor Central Account Managers, and relevant stakeholders** involved in managing product recalls within the organization. It is specifically tailored to those responsible for notifying Amazon of a product recall through Vendor Central.

1. A recall can be initiated by regulators such as the **USDA, FDA, CPSC, Health/Transport Canada**, in partnership with the recalling entity (manufacturer, brand owner, or supplier).
2. The recalling entity can also directly inform retailers and other supply chain partners of the product issue before or without the involvement of regulators.
3. In any case, Amazon immediately remove product offers related to the recall from the website and notifies customers who purchased the product during the recall period.
4. Amazon also instructs all retail teams to collaborate with the vendor to remove the inventory and provide reinstatement instructions.
5. If a manufacturer-driven recall needs to be reported, Go to "**Help**" on the upper right corner of the screen then scroll down and click the "**Contact Us**" button at the bottom of the "**Support**" page on **Vendor Central**.

7. Ensure all relevant documents are attached and include the following information:

- Reason(s) for the recall
- Affected product ASINs/titles
- Start date of the recall
- Vendor's customer-facing contact information (if it's a safety recall)
- Inventory disposition, either return or destroy
- If return, provide the return address and authorization number (if required)
