## Canceling a Product Enrolled in the Vine Program on Vendor Central

**Who is this for:**

All individuals who want to cancel their current Amazon Vendor Central Vine enrollment.

**Objective:**

To be able to cancel a product currently enrolled in Amazon Vendor Central Vine program.

**Steps to Cancel a Product Enrolled in the Amazon Vine Program on Vendor Central**

1. Go to Vendor Central and hover over the Drop-Down Menu of the “Merchandising” Tab and select “Amazon Vine.”

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jwXimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/jwXimage.png)

2. On the list of products currently enrolled in Vine, click the "Stop" button.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Iziimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Iziimage.png)

3. You may also click the "Details" button to view the current status of the Vine enrollment before the cancellation.

FAQs

- **Q1: How do I cancel a product enrolled in the Vine Program on Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:** To cancel a product's enrollment in the Vine Program, go to Vendor Central, hover over the “Merchandising” tab, select “Amazon Vine,” and click the "Stop" button next to the product you wish to cancel.
- **Q2: Can I view the status of a product before canceling its Vine enrollment?**

  - **A2:** Yes, you can click the "Details" button next to the product to view its current status before proceeding with the cancellation.
- **Q3: What happens when I click the "Stop" button for a product enrolled in Vine?**

  - **A3:** Clicking the "Stop" button will cancel the product's current enrollment in the Vine Program, preventing further reviews from Vine Voices.
- **Q4: Is there any confirmation or additional action required after clicking "Stop"?**

  - **A4:**The cancellation process is initiated by clicking the "Stop" button, but there may be a confirmation screen depending on Vendor Central's interface to ensure you want to proceed with the cancellation.
