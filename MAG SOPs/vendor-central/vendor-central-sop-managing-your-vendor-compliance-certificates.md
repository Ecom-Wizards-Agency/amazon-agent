## Managing Your Vendor Compliance Certificates

**Who is this for :** This SOP is designed for procurement and supply chain teams responsible for managing vendor relationships and compliance within an organization. It provides a clear framework and guidelines for effectively handling vendor compliance certificates to mitigate risks and maintain regulatory compliance.

**Objective :** This SOP aims to establish a standardized process for monitoring and maintaining vendor compliance certificates to ensure regulatory and contractual obligations are met.

1. Access the Vendor Compliance Certificates by clicking on “Product Certificates” in the “Items” tab on the top menu of Vendor Central.

FAQs

**1. What types of compliance certificates do I need to submit?**

- The required compliance certificates depend on your product category. Common examples include CPSIA certificates for children’s products, FCC certifications for electronic devices, and FDA approvals for food and supplements. Check your ASIN requirements under Vendor Central > Product Certificates.

**2. How will I know if a compliance document is missing, rejected, or expiring?**

- Amazon sends email notifications and alerts in Vendor Central > Product Certificates under the Pending Your Action section. You should regularly check this page to stay compliant.

**3. How long does Amazon take to review submitted compliance documents?**

- Amazon typically processes compliance document submissions within 5–10 business days, but processing times may vary depending on the volume of submissions.

**4. What should I do if my compliance document is rejected?**

- Click "Resubmit" under Rejected Compliance Documents to see the rejection reason. Make the necessary corrections and resubmit the document. If you believe the rejection was in error, you can open a case with Amazon Vendor Support for clarification.

**5. What file formats and sizes are accepted for compliance document uploads?**

- Amazon accepts PDF, JPEG, and PNG formats for compliance document uploads. Files should not exceed 10MB to ensure smooth processing.

**6. What happens if I don’t submit the required compliance documents?**

- Failing to provide the necessary compliance documents may result in product delisting, purchase order delays, or account suspension. Amazon enforces strict compliance requirements to meet regulatory and safety standards.

**7. Can I track previously submitted compliance documents?**

- Yes, you can view your past submissions in Vendor Central > Product Certificates. Look under the ASIN-specific compliance section to check which documents are on file.

**8. Can I upload multiple compliance documents at once?**

- No, Amazon requires vendors to submit compliance documents individually for each ASIN. However, if multiple ASINs require the same document, you can use the bulk submission feature in Vendor Central.
