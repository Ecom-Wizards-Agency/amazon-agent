# Vendor Central SOP: Editing Product Costs

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-editing-product-costs

## Editing Product Costs

**Who is this for:**

- This is intended for vendors and sellers who sell their products on the Amazon platform.
- It is relevant for individuals or teams responsible for managing and updating product costs, such as financial managers, inventory managers, or anyone involved in pricing and cost management on the Amazon platform.

**Objective:** To provide guidance on editing product costs for Amazon through the Vendor Central Dashboard.

Before editing product costs, it is important to communicate, and get client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.

**Instructions:**

1. To edit your products' costs to Amazon, go to the "Items" tab on the top menu of your Vendor Central Dashboard and choose "Edit Item Costs" from the drop-down menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685453026949.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/HYFimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/HYFimage.png)

2. Select the appropriate spreadsheet to download based on your needs: either "Download costs for all available ASINs" or "Specify costs to update". Under the latter option, select your criteria from the drop-down menu and type in the relevant codes. Check or uncheck the "Download entire family (if applicable)?" box as needed.

6. You can now enter the new cost and effective date into the relevant columns. You may also provide additional information, such as a reason for the cost increase or an HTS or tariff code if applicable.

7. After completing the spreadsheet, save it and return to the "Edit Item Costs" page.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685453806224.png)

8. Under "Step 3 - Upload Costs" at the bottom of the page, click the gray "Choose File" button and upload your spreadsheet.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685453958317.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/e9Qimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/e9Qimage.png)

9. Keep in mind that Amazon's processing time for your request can range from 30 days to several months.

FAQs

- **Q1: What should I do before editing product costs in Amazon Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:** Before editing product costs, it is important to communicate and get the client’s approval first. Include the approval proof (screenshot or Fireflies call link) as Task Details or as a Comment.
- **Q2: How do I access the "Edit Item Costs" option in Vendor Central?**

  - **A2:** To access the "Edit Item Costs" option, go to the "Items" tab on the top menu of your Vendor Central Dashboard and choose "Edit Item Costs" from the drop-down menu.
- **Q3: What options are available when downloading the product cost spreadsheet?**

  - **A3:** You can choose either "Download costs for all available ASINs" or "Specify costs to update." If you select "Specify costs to update," you will need to choose your criteria and input the relevant codes.
- **Q4: How do I enable editing in the downloaded spreadsheet?**

  - **A4:** After opening the downloaded Excel file, select the "Cost" tab at the bottom and click the "Enable Editing" button at the top of the page.
- **Q5: What information can I add when editing the product cost?**

  - **A5:** You can enter the new cost, effective date, and any additional information, such as a reason for the cost increase or an HTS or tariff code if applicable.
- **Q6: What should I do after editing the cost spreadsheet?**

  - **A6:** After completing the spreadsheet, save it and return to the "Edit Item Costs" page in Vendor Central. Then, click the "Choose File" button under "Step 3 - Upload Costs" to upload your edited spreadsheet.
- **Q7: How long does it take for Amazon to process product cost changes?**

  - **A7:** Amazon’s processing time for your request can range from 30 days to several months.
- **Q8: What happens if I don’t provide approval proof when editing product costs?**

  - **A8:** It is essential to provide approval proof before editing product costs. Failure to do so may delay or hinder the processing of your request.
