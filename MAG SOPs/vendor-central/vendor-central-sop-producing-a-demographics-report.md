## Producing a Demographics Report

**Who is this for?**

Intended for individuals involved in conducting demographic studies or generating demographic reports, the SOP provides a standardized framework to ensure consistency and reliability in producing demographic reports across various projects and organizations.

**Objective:**

To outline the process and guidelines for producing a comprehensive demographics report. This report aims to provide an accurate and detailed analysis of the demographic characteristics of a particular population or target group.

**To access your demographic report from the Amazon Brand Analytics dashboard, please follow these steps:**

1. Go to the “Reports” tab on your Vendor Central dashboard and select “Brand Analytics” from the dropdown menu.

FAQs

**1. How often does the Demographics Report update?**

- The report updates monthly, and data is typically available by the 15th of the following month.

**2. How far back can I access historical demographic data?**

- Amazon allows vendors to retrieve demographic data for up to 24 months. If you need older data, consider downloading reports regularly for long-term tracking.

**3. What do the demographic categories mean in the context of my business?**

- Age – Helps determine which age groups are most engaged with your brand.
- Household Income – Indicates purchasing power and potential pricing strategies.
- Education Level – May influence product positioning and messaging.
- Gender – Helps tailor product descriptions, ads, and imagery.
- Marital Status – Can indicate preferences for gifting or household-related products.

**4. What should I do if data is missing or inconsistent?**

- If demographic data appears incomplete or incorrect:

  - Ensure the correct time frame and filters are selected.
  - Check if your ASINs have enough sales volume to generate reliable demographic insights.
  - Some data points may not be available due to privacy restrictions or low sample sizes.
  - If issues persist, contact Amazon Vendor Support.

**5. How can I use this report beyond marketing?**

- You can use demographic insights to:

  - Refine pricing strategies by analyzing income-based purchasing behavior.
  - Plan inventory by identifying trends in repeat purchases among specific demographics.
  - Improve product development by tailoring features and packaging to key customer segments.
  - Optimize ad targeting by creating audience-specific campaigns.

**6. Can I automate or schedule demographic report downloads?**

- No, Amazon does not currently offer automated scheduling for demographic reports. However, you can manually download them as needed for periodic analysis.

**7. How does Amazon collect demographic data, and how accurate is it?**

- Amazon gathers demographic insights from customer account information, browsing behavior, and purchase history. While this data is aggregated and anonymized, it provides directional insights rather than exact details about individual buyers.
