# Vendor Central SOP: Submitting a Routing Request

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-submitting-a-routing-request

## Submitting a Routing Request

**Who is this for**:

- This is intended for individuals responsible for managing shipments on the Vendor Central platform.
- This is crucial for individuals handling shipments, as it provides step-by-step instructions to navigate through the Routing Request submission process.

**Objective**:

- To provide instructions on how to submit a Routing Request on the Vendor Central platform.
- To ensure that the Routing Request process is carried out accurately and efficiently.

**Instructions:**

1. Go to the "Orders" tab located in the top left-hand corner, and select “Shipments”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yDvimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/yDvimage.png)

2. Click on “Create new shipment” on the next page.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/qKlimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/qKlimage.png)

3. Select the PO Number for which you want to create the Routing Request by clicking the “Add purchase order to shipment” button.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/TEEimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/TEEimage.png)

4. Once the button “Add purchase order to shipment” was clicked a confirmation that it was added will show up.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ztdimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/ztdimage.png)

5. Once done with the PO selection, click “Continue to step 2”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/camimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/camimage.png)

6. Make sure that the “Number of Cartons” fields are correctly filled out and that the Warehouse Address in the “Ship From Location” is correct. Click “Continue to step 3”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DRJimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/DRJimage.png)

7. Schedule pickup. Click the date and choose your requested pick update. Review your shipment contents and click Confirm and submit shipment.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7Chimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/7Chimage.png)

8. After confirmation, you can track, edit, print labels, and submit your ASN on the [Shipments page](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/kt/vendor/members/afi-shipment-mgr/shippingqueue).

FAQs

General Process

- **Q1: What is a Routing Request?**

  - **A1:** A Routing Request is a process on Amazon Vendor Central used to schedule and coordinate the pickup and delivery of shipments for purchase orders.
- **Q2: Why is submitting a Routing Request important?**

  - **A2:** Submitting a Routing Request ensures that shipments are scheduled, tracked, and delivered efficiently, reducing delays and ensuring accurate handling of orders.

Access and Navigation

- **Q3: How do I access the "Shipments" section on Vendor Central?**

  - **A3:** Log in to Amazon Vendor Central, navigate to the "Orders" tab in the top left-hand corner, and select "Shipments" from the dropdown menu.
- **Q4: What should I do if I cannot find the "Create new shipment" button?**

  - **A4:** Ensure you are in the "Shipments" section under the "Orders" tab. If the button is still not visible, refresh the page or contact Amazon support.

Purchase Order Selection

- **Q5: How do I add a purchase order to a shipment?**

  - **A5:** Click the "Add purchase order to shipment" button, and a confirmation will appear once the PO has been successfully added.
- **Q6: Can I add multiple purchase orders to a single shipment?**

  - **A6:** Yes, multiple purchase orders can be added to a single shipment, provided they are being sent to the same destination.

Shipment Details

- **Q7: What information do I need to provide in the "Number of Cartons" fields?**

  - **A7:** You must accurately input the number of cartons included in the shipment. This ensures proper tracking and handling of your shipment.
- **Q8: How do I verify the "Ship From Location"?**

  - **A8:** Double-check that the Warehouse Address listed in the “Ship From Location” field matches your shipping origin before proceeding.

Scheduling and Submission

- **Q9: How do I schedule a pickup for my shipment?**

  - **A9:** On the scheduling step, click the date field and select your preferred pickup date. Review the shipment contents and click "Confirm and submit shipment."
- **Q10: Can I edit the shipment details after confirmation?**

  - **A10:** Yes, after confirmation, you can track, edit, print labels, and submit your Advance Shipment Notification (ASN) from the Shipments page.

Troubleshooting

- **Q11: What should I do if I selected the wrong pickup date?**

  - **A11:** You can edit your shipment details, including the pickup date, on the Shipments page before the shipment is finalized.
- **Q12: Why is the confirmation message not showing after I add a purchase order to the shipment?**

  - **A12:** Refresh the page or retry adding the purchase order. If the problem persists, check for system updates or contact Amazon support.
