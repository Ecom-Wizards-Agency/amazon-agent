# Vendor Central SOP: Creating A+ Content in Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-creating-a-content-in-vendor-central

## Creating A+ Content in Vendor Central

**Who is this for?**

This SOP applies to all individuals or teams responsible for creating and managing A+ content for Amazon product listings.

**Objective:**

This SOP provides a step-by-step guide for creating and managing A+ content on Amazon to enhance product listings, drive customer engagement, and improve brand awareness. It outlines the process of adding enhanced product descriptions, A+ brand stories, using AI tools for content generation, and applying content to ASINs.

**IMPORTANT NOTE:**Please make sure you have read the Quick Reference Guide: Vendor Central A+ Content Guidelines below before creating your A+ content.

**Procedure**

Creating A+ Content in Vendor Central

##### Creating A+ Content in Vendor Central

**1. Accessing A+ Content Manager**

- Navigate to [**A+ Content Manager**](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/hz/vendor/members/aplus/content-manager) under **Merchandising** in your Amazon Vendor Central account.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-creating-a-content-in-vendor-central-image-j5wifqe1.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/n18image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/n18image.png)

**2. Creating A+ Content**

- Click on **Start creating A+ content**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-creating-a-content-in-vendor-central-image-tdd628rm.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oTeimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/oTeimage.png)

- Choose the type of A+ content you want to create:

  - Basic

    - Create up to 5 modules to highlight your product's features with enhanced content.
  - Brand Story

    - Share your brand’s history, values, and more. Include your logo, brand picture, and description, and answer questions about your brand. This content appears in a new section above the Enhanced Product Description.
- Follow the on-screen prompts to:

  - Name your content

    : Provide a clear name for the A+ content project.
  - Choose the language

    : Select the language for the content (e.g., English, Spanish).
  - Choose content modules

    : Select from available modules (e.g., enhanced product descriptions, brand stories).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-creating-a-content-in-vendor-central-image-nnrlqhfc.png)

**Note**: Save your content as draft to avoid losing changes. You can stop at any step and resume later by saving your progress as a draft.

#### **4. Populate Content**

- Text and Image Slots

  : Populate both text and image fields. It is recommended to include both to provide customers with a complete understanding of the product.
- Images

  : Either select images from your

  Media Library

  or upload new images. The

  Media Library

  allows for easy image management, categorization, and searching.

  - Ensure your images meet the size limitations specified in each template.
  - If your images are too large, they will be resized automatically to fit the maximum allowed size.

**Note**: A+ content does not support HTML tags.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iYQimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/iYQimage.png)

#### **5. Alt-Text for Images**

- Provide

  alt-text

  for all images used in the A+ content. Alt-text should be a simple description of the image (e.g., “Blender on kitchen counter next to fruit and yogurt”).
- Alt-text aids visually impaired customers and improves product visibility in search results.
- If using an image from the

  Media Library

  , you can use existing alt-text or modify it.

#### **6. Assigning ASINs to A+ Content**

- Add products to your A+ content by selecting the appropriate ASINs from the

  ASIN management section

  .

  - You can apply A+ content to parent ASINs, child ASINs, or both within an ASIN family.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-creating-a-content-in-vendor-central-image-9soos2q6.png)

- If applying content to multiple ASINs (over 1,000), use the

  bulk upload feature

  to upload a spreadsheet with ASINs.

**7. Creating Language Variations**

- If needed, create language variation drafts for different markets (e.g., Spanish for US customers).
- Automatic translations are provided by Amazon for languages where no content has been published.
- You can review, edit, or delete automatic translations as needed.
- Once content is ready, submit for review.

**8. Review and Submission**

- Review the final draft of the A+ content to ensure all information is accurate and complete.
- Check that all ASINs are properly applied to the content.
- Submit the content for Amazon’s approval.

**Note**: The system allows a maximum of 20 pending submissions at a time.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/peuimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/peuimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/vendor-central-sop-creating-a-content-in-vendor-central-image-952l8gw9.png)

**9. Approval and Publication**

- Once submitted, Amazon will review the A+ content. If compliant with Amazon’s policies, the content will be approved and published on the product detail page.

**Best Practices**

- Ensure content is accurate and free of misleading claims.
- Use high-quality images and detailed product descriptions to enhance customer experience.
- Regularly review A+ content to keep it up to date with product changes.
- Use the GenAI feature wisely and customize the output to match your brand’s voice.
- Ensure that the content complies with

  Amazon’s

  Intellectual Property

  and

  Listings

  policies

  to avoid removal.

Quick Reference Guide: Vendor Central A+ Content Guidelines

##### Quick Reference Guide: Vendor Central A+ Content Guidelines

A+ Content on Amazon allows sellers to enhance their product listings with additional images, detailed descriptions, and brand stories to increase customer engagement and boost sales. However, it's important to follow specific guidelines regarding image quality, text formatting, claims, and content restrictions to ensure your content is compliant and effective. Proper adherence to these rules will help avoid content rejection and maintain a positive product listing experience.

**General Guidelines**

- **Rights**: Ensure you have the necessary rights to the images and text used.
- **Quality**: Content should be clear, useful, and free of grammatical or formatting errors.
- **Unique Content**: Avoid reusing images from the product page. A+ content should showcase unique aspects of the product or brand.
- **Rejection Risk**: Non-compliance with guidelines can lead to rejection or removal of ASINs.

**Image & Text Formatting**

- **Supported File Types**: .jpg, .bmp, .png (RGB colorspace only).
- **File Size & Quality**: Images must be under 2 MB, at least 72 dpi resolution, and must not be blurry or low-quality. No animated images (e.g., .GIFs).
- **Image Alt-Text**: Must describe the image and be useful for screen readers. Avoid irrelevant or misleading descriptions.
- **Logo**: Only one brand logo allowed. Partner logos or symbols are allowed only if logical or beneficial.
- **Text Formatting**:

  - Spell out numbers under 10.
  - Use proper grammar, punctuation, and consistent style (serial/Oxford commas).
  - Capitalize each major word in a header
  - Bold or italic formatting should only be used for headings or emphasis on select words.
  - No HTML tags or text written in unauthorized languages, except as part of the brand identity.

**Claims & Awards**

- **Awards**: Mention only awards from the last 2 years. Include the awarding body and year in the text.
- **Certifications**: Must be substantiated with the certifying body, study, or publication details and the year.
- **Environmental Claims**: Avoid claims like “eco-friendly” or “biodegradable.” Claims such as “recyclable” must be substantiated with evidence.
- **Prohibited Claims**:

  - Satisfaction guarantees (“100% satisfaction”).
  - Health-related claims (e.g., cures or treatments for diseases).
  - Boastful claims like “#1 rated” or “best-selling.”

**Content Restrictions**

- **Warranty/Guarantee**: No warranty or guarantee info allowed, except for warranty cards directly related to the product.
- **Shipping Details**: Do not include shipping info, QR codes, or personal information (e.g., phone numbers, email).
- **Quotes & Endorsements**: Only up to four quotes, from well-known public figures or publications, with proper attribution (author, date, title).
- **Competitor References**: Do not compare products to competitors. Comparisons can only be made with products from the same brand.
- **Promotional Language**: Do not include pricing, promotional offers, or phrases like "buy now" or "cheap."
- **Time-Sensitive Info**: Avoid terms like “new,” “latest,” “on sale now,” or holiday references, except in the Brand Story section.
- **Trademarks & Copyrights**: Use only if already on product packaging or as part of the logo. Remove standalone symbols.
- **Hyperlinks**: External links or attempts to redirect to other sites (including other products) are prohibited.
- **Customer Service References**: Do not include contact information for customer service (e.g., “contact us for issues”).
- **Mimicking Amazon**: Do not replicate Amazon logos, detail page headings, or other elements.
- **Adult Content**: No adult content or references to criminal activity are allowed.

**Note**: Failure to comply with these guidelines can lead to content rejection or removal of your ASIN. Ensure all content is accurate, compliant with Amazon policies, and properly formatted for the best results.
