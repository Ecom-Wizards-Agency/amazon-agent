Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-creating-a-pick-list

## Creating a Pick List

**Who is this for:** An Amazon seller may request an Open Listings Report to gain to obtain detailed data and insights about the open listings on Amazon.

**Objective:** To facilitate efficient order fulfillment and streamline the picking process in a warehouse or fulfillment center. A pick list provides a systematic guide for warehouse staff to locate, gather, and pack the items required to fulfill specific purchase orders.

**Instructions:**

1. Within the "Purchase Orders" section, select the "Confirmed Purchase Orders" tab. This will show you all the purchase orders that have been confirmed and are ready for fulfillment.
2. In the list of Confirmed Purchase Orders, check the boxes in the left column next to the POs you want to generate the pick list for.
3. After selecting the POs, click on the "Open selected POs" button to open the specific purchase orders.
4. In the PO Items list, check the boxes next to the items you want to include in the pick list.
5. Once you've selected the items, click the "Create pick list from selected items" button. This will generate a pick list for the items you've selected

FAQs

#### General Process

- **Q1: What is a pick list?**

  - **A1:** A pick list is a systematic guide used in a warehouse or fulfillment center to help staff locate, gather, and pack items required to fulfill specific purchase orders.
- **Q2: Why is generating a pick list important?**

  - **A2:** Generating a pick list improves order fulfillment efficiency, reduces errors, and streamlines the process of gathering and packing items for shipment.

#### Access and Navigation

- **Q3: How do I access the "Purchase Orders" section in Amazon Vendor Central?**

  - **A3:** Log in to Amazon Vendor Central, navigate to the top menu, hover over or click the "Orders" tab, and then select “Purchase Orders.”
- **Q4: What should I do if I cannot log in to Amazon Vendor Central?**

  - **A4:** Ensure your credentials are correct. If you still encounter issues, reset your password or contact Amazon Vendor Central support for assistance.

#### Selection Process

- **Q5: How do I know which purchase orders are ready for fulfillment?**

  - **A5:** In the "Purchase Orders" section, navigate to the "Confirmed Purchase Orders" tab. This tab lists all purchase orders that have been confirmed and are ready for fulfillment.
- **Q6: Can I generate a pick list for multiple purchase orders at once?**

  - **A6:** Yes, you can. Check the boxes in the left column next to the purchase orders you want to include, then click on "Open selected POs" to open them collectively.
- **Q7: What if I accidentally select the wrong items in a purchase order?**

  - **A7:** You can deselect the items by unchecking the boxes before generating the pick list. If the list has already been created, you will need to regenerate it with the correct selections.

#### Pick List Creation

- **Q8: How do I create a pick list from selected items?**

  - **A8:** After selecting the items in the "PO Items" list, click the "Create pick list from selected items" button. This will generate the pick list.
- **Q9: What format will the pick list be in once generated?**

  - **A9:** The pick list is typically generated as a printable document or downloadable file. The format depends on Amazon Vendor Central’s settings.

#### Troubleshooting

- **Q10: What should I do if the "Create pick list" button is not visible?**

  - **A10:** Ensure you have selected the items from the PO Items list. If the issue persists, refresh the page or contact Amazon support.
- **Q11: Why can’t I see any confirmed purchase orders?**

  - **A11:** This might occur if no purchase orders have been confirmed yet. Check with your team to ensure orders have been processed or refresh the page to update the list.
- **Q12: Can I edit a pick list after it has been created?**

  - **A12:** No, you usually cannot edit a generated pick list. If changes are needed, you must create a new pick list by selecting the correct items.
