## Correcting Purchase Order Shipping Information

**Who is this for**:

- This is intended for vendors who have a contractual agreement with Amazon and are responsible for providing shipping information for their Purchase Orders.
- It applies to vendors who do not use EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) for submitting their shipment information and need guidance on the specific information requirements for their shipments.

**Objective**:

- To provide clear guidelines on the specific shipping information requirements from vendors to ensure smooth processing and receipt of shipments by Amazon.
- To provide the correct shipping information in a timely manner, thereby avoiding delays and ensuring compliance with Amazon's shipping guidelines

1. In order to process and receive your shipments, Amazon has specific information requirements.
2. Depending on whether you agreed to Collect or Prepaid freight terms in your contract with them, you may need to provide slightly different information at different times.
3. The following table outlines the information that Amazon requires from you and when it needs to be provided.
4. Note: This table does not apply to EDI vendors who submit their shipment information through the EDI 753 (carrier request from a vendor to Amazon)/EDI 754 (carrier assignment back to vendor from Amazon) and EDI 856 (ASN from a vendor to Amazon) specifications.

FAQs

General Process

- **Q1: What is the purpose of providing correct shipping information to Amazon?**

  - **A1:** Providing accurate shipping information ensures that Amazon can process and receive shipments efficiently, avoiding delays, rejections, or compliance issues.
- **Q2: Who needs to follow these guidelines?**

  - **A2:** These guidelines apply to vendors who have a contractual agreement with Amazon and do not use EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) for submitting shipment information.

Shipping Information Requirements

- **Q3: What specific shipping information does Amazon require?**

  - **A3:** Shipping information requirements vary based on your freight terms (Collect or Prepaid). The information may include carrier details, shipment dates, tracking numbers, and carton or pallet specifications.
- **Q4: How do freight terms (Collect or Prepaid) affect the shipping information I need to provide?**

  - **A4:** - **Collect Freight Terms:** Amazon assigns the carrier, and you need to coordinate shipment details accordingly.

    - **Prepaid Freight Terms:** You arrange the carrier and provide details about the appointment scheduled with Amazon.
- **Q5: When should the required shipping information be submitted to Amazon?**

  - **A5:** Shipping information must be submitted promptly to align with the agreed shipment timelines in your contract. Specific deadlines vary depending on freight terms and shipment schedules.

Corrections

- **Q6: What should I do if I submit incorrect shipping information?**

  - **A6:** Contact Amazon Vendor Support immediately to update or correct the shipping information. Delays in corrections can disrupt the shipment process.
- **Q7: Can I make corrections after submitting my shipment details in Vendor Central?**

  - **A7:** Yes, you can update certain details in Vendor Central, depending on the shipment stage. For critical changes, you may need to contact Amazon support.

Exclusions for EDI Vendors

- **Q8: Do these guidelines apply to EDI vendors?**

  - **A8:** No, EDI vendors submit shipment information through EDI 753, 754, and 856 specifications, which have separate requirements and processes.
- **Q9: Can a vendor switch from manual processes to EDI for shipping information submission?**

  - **A9:** Yes, switching to EDI is possible, but you must coordinate with Amazon to implement and test the EDI specifications before transitioning.

Troubleshooting and Compliance

- **Q10: What happens if I fail to provide the correct shipping information on time?**

  - **A10:** Delays or inaccuracies can result in shipment rejections, penalties, or disruptions to the receipt process at Amazon fulfillment centers.
- **Q11: How do I ensure compliance with Amazon’s shipping guidelines?**

  - **A11:** Regularly review Amazon's shipping requirements, confirm freight terms in your contract, and use the Vendor Central platform to accurately submit all required details.
