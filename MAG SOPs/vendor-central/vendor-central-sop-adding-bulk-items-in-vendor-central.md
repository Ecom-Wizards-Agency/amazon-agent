# Vendor Central SOP: Adding Bulk Items in Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-adding-bulk-items-in-vendor-central

## Adding Bulk Items in Vendor Central

**Who is this for:** Typically falls under the responsibility of the vendor or supplier.

**Objective:** Adding items or listing in bulk allows you to save time and effort compared to individually adding each item or listing. This is particularly beneficial when you have a large number of items to add.

**Instructions:**

1. Go to the Items tab on the top menu and select Add Products from the drop-down menu.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/mk8image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/mk8image.png)

2. On the next page, click “Select product type” and choose the most appropriate categories for your products.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gx5image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/gx5image.png)

3. You can choose up to 20 product types of categories.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/10Gimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/10Gimage.png)

4. Once product type/s is/are selected, click “Continue”

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RU4image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/RU4image.png)

5. Then click “Download a blank template”

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LM4image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/LM4image.png)

6. You will be redirected to [https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/listing/download-status](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/listing/download-status). Look for the category template that you have requested and click “Download”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bZ9image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/bZ9image.png)

7. Open the dowloaded file.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/kJXimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/kJXimage.png)

8. Click “Enable Editing” and go to the category template tab.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/f3f2ee4c-0da0-4ea9-8932-ef770b1eac47.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/M1aimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/M1aimage.png)

9. Row 6 or grayed row is just a sample of how you should fill out each attribute.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/f533a6d8-027d-4078-8849-680279d8ee97.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/cNpimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/cNpimage.png)

10. Make sure to fill out each attribute or the required attributes which texts are highlighted in red color to avoid flat file upload errors.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/15e82b9a-e8da-446e-ba44-9d11b139f904.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hvuimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/hvuimage.png)

11. Save the file. Go back to Bulk product submissions, go to “Upload your spreadsheet” tab.

[https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/listing/upload](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/listing/upload)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dEHimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/dEHimage.png)

12. Drag or drop the file and click “Upload file”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ttBimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/ttBimage.png)

13. Monitor the status of your upload in “Spreadsheet upload status": [https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/listing/status](https://vendorcentral.amazon.com/listing/status)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ZYFimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/ZYFimage.png)

14. To confirm that the upload is successfully processed, the status should be tagged as “Done”.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dkjimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/dkjimage.png)

15. Confirm your added listing by going to the Catalog page.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Vvqimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Vvqimage.png)

FAQs

- **Q1: How can I add items in bulk on Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:** To add items in bulk, go to the **Items** tab, select **Add Products** from the drop-down menu, choose the appropriate product type, download the relevant template, fill it out, and upload it back through the **Bulk product submissions** section.
- **Q2: How do I download the category template for my products?**

  - **A2:**After selecting the product type and clicking "Continue," choose the "Download a blank template" option. You’ll be redirected to the **Download Status** page, where you can download the requested template.
- **Q3: What should I do after downloading the category template?**

  - **A3:** Open the file, enable editing, and fill out the required attributes highlighted in red in the category template tab. Ensure all mandatory fields are completed to avoid upload errors.
- **Q4: How do I upload the completed template?**

  - **A4:** Save the completed file, navigate to the **Bulk product submissions** section, go to the “Upload your spreadsheet” tab, and drag or drop the file. Then, click **Upload file**.
- **Q5: How can I monitor the status of my bulk upload?**

  - **A5:**Check the **Spreadsheet upload status** page. The status of your upload should eventually be tagged as **Done** to confirm successful processing.
- **Q6: How do I confirm the items have been added successfully?**

  - **A6:** Once the upload status shows as "Done," verify your added listings by visiting the **Catalog** page on Vendor Central.
