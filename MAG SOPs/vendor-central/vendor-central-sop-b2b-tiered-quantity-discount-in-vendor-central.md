# Vendor Central SOP: B2B Tiered Quantity Discount in Vendor Central

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-b2b-tiered-quantity-discount-in-vendor-central

## B2B Tiered Quantity Discount in Vendor Central

**Who is this for?:** This is intended for individuals who want to create a B2B tiered quantity discount in Amazon Vendor Central.

**Objective:** To establish a standardized process for implementing and managing tiered quantity discounts in the B2B (Business-to-Business) context within the Vendor Central platform. The SOP aims to ensure consistency, transparency, and efficiency in offering and applying quantity-based discounts to B2B customers.

**Video :**[https://www.loom.com/share/592dd22e8adb4fa3a5c416ebab307d45](https://www.loom.com/share/592dd22e8adb4fa3a5c416ebab307d45)

**Instructions:**

1. Go to **Merchandising** → **Business Discounts**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Pricing.jpg)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fZrimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/fZrimage.png)

2. Click **Business Discount Manager** → **Add business discounts** / **Edit business discounts**.

- **Add business discounts** - Use this option to Add business discounts.
- **Edit business discounts** - Use this option to Edit existing business discounts.

**NOTE:** Business Discount Manager does not support deleting business discounts.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/WUnimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/WUnimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1720622318350(1).png)

3. Choose between**Quantity discount** or **Single unit discount**.

- **Quantity discount** - Drive multi-unit purchases by offering discounts to all Amazon Business customers who make higher volume single purchases of an ASIN. A quantity discount is applied when a business customer purchases the minimum quantity for that quantity discount. If you set up a quantity discount of 5% off a minimum of 20 units, Amazon will offer at least the same discount to an Amazon Business customer when they buy a minimum of 20 units in a single purchase.
- **Single unit discoun**t - Offer an exclusive discount to all Amazon Business customers who buy one or more units of an ASIN in a one-time purchase.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SRximage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/SRximage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1720622340122(1).png)

4. Choose between **Fund on units directly supplied by Fund** or me on units supplied by me or a distributor.

- **Fund on units directly supplied by me** - Select this option:

  1) If you are a distributor

  2) If you are a manufacturer who does not want to fund quantity discounts on products that distributors supply to Amazon
- **Fund on units supplied by me or a distributor** - If you are a manufacturer, you can select to fund a quantity discount on units of an ASIN that you or a distributor supply to Amazon. To set the discount type to money off, select Fund on units supplied by me or the distributor. You will be responsible for paying the per-unit funding for all units sold via Amazon Business while the business discount remains active.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LxKimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/LxKimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1720622361346(1).png)

5. Choose between "Choose by vendor code". (Select up to 5 to make bulk updates) or "Choose by brand name". (Select up to 5 to make bulk updates) .

- Choose by vendor code. (Select up to 5 to make bulk updates) - A vendor code is an alphanumeric code that represents a relationship between Amazon and a vendor. This option lists all child vendor codes.
- Choose by brand name. (Select up to 5 to make bulk updates) - Amazon brand name for owning the brand. Amazon considers a brand to be a name that represents a product or set of products. Products from the same brand share a common name, logo, or other identifying mark that appears on the product or its packaging, and distinguishes those products from similar products that do not belong to that brand.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1720622381745(1).png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/q7Aimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/q7Aimage.png)

6. Choose between **Yes**or **No**.

- Yes - Use this option to set the same discount on all ASINs you selected using Business Discount Manager. Note, this is only valid when you select 5 or fewer vendor codes or brand names.
- No - Use this option to set different discounts for each ASIN using a pre-populated Excel template

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4dyimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4dyimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1720622399375(1).png)

7. Add the details on Tier Quantity and Tier Discount then Click Submit.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Q89image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Q89image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1720622420913(1).png)

8. Scroll down below to check on your active discounts.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/56nimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/56nimage.png)

FAQs

- **Q1: How do I access the Business Discount Manager in Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:** To access the Business Discount Manager, go to the "Merchandising" tab, then select "Business Discounts."
- **Q2: Can I delete business discounts once they are created?**

  - **A2:** No, the Business Discount Manager does not support deleting business discounts. However, you can edit existing discounts.
- **Q3: What is the difference between a Quantity Discount and a Single Unit Discount?**

  - **A3:** A Quantity Discount offers a discount when a business customer purchases a minimum quantity of an ASIN in a single purchase, whereas a Single Unit Discount offers an exclusive discount to all Amazon Business customers for purchasing one or more units in a one-time purchase.
- **Q4: How do I choose the type of units for funding a quantity discount?**

  - **A4:** You can choose between:

    - "Fund on units directly supplied by me" if you're a distributor or a manufacturer not wanting to fund quantity discounts on products supplied by distributors to Amazon.
    - "Fund on units supplied by me or a distributor" if you're a manufacturer who wants to fund quantity discounts on products supplied by either you or a distributor.
- **Q5: Can I apply the same discount across multiple ASINs at once?**

  - **A5:**Yes, you can either choose "Choose by vendor code" or "Choose by brand name" to make bulk updates to up to 5 ASINs, setting the same discount for all selected ASINs.
- **Q6: What happens if I choose "No" when setting the discount for multiple ASINs?**

  - **A6:** If you choose "No," you will be required to set different discounts for each ASIN using a pre-populated Excel template.
- **Q7: How do I set the tiered quantity and tier discount?**

  - **A7:** After selecting the relevant options, enter the details for Tier Quantity and Tier Discount, then click "Submit" to save your settings.
- **Q8: How can I check the status of my active discounts?**

  - **A8:** After setting the discount, scroll down to the section where you can view all your active discounts.
