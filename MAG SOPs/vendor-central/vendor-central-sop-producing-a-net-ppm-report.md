# Vendor Central SOP: Producing a Net PPM Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-producing-a-net-ppm-report

## Producing a Net PPM Report

**Who is this for:**This is designed for vendors who have access to the Amazon Retail Analytics Dashboard and need to generate a Net PPM (Pure Product Margin) Report.

**Objective:**

- To guide vendors in producing a Net PPM Report through the Amazon Retail Analytics Dashboard.
- To provide instructions on how to access and utilize the report to analyze the profitability of their ASINs.

**Instructions:**

1. To access your Amazon Retail Analytics Dashboard, go to the "Reports" tab on your Vendor Central Dashboard and choose "Retail Analytics" from the dropdown menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-15%20at%2011.52.47%E2%80%AFPM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/MV7image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/MV7image.png)
2. You will see an overview of your Retail Analytics Dashboard.
3. Select "Net PPM".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-15%20at%2011.54.16%E2%80%AFPM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/o1nimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/o1nimage.png)
4. Use the Time frame to filter your data and also you can choose the type of view you want to use. Once done then click "Apply".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-16%20at%2012.00.06%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/tE9image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/tE9image.png)
5. If you also want to show the percentage report of "Prior Period" or "Year over Year" just click its corresponding check box and click "Refine Results".

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-16%20at%2012.01.20%E2%80%AFAM.png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/QfEimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/QfEimage.png)
6. The percentage of the Report Period along with the Prior Period and Year over Year will now show in the (NetPPM) Net Pure Product Margin report.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712246203813(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/10gimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/10gimage.png)
7. Reports can be sorted in ascending or descending by clicking the arrow beside the report.
8. After adjusting to your desired views, you can download the report by clicking the "Download" button in the top right corner of the page.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712246221846(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Deeimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/Deeimage.png)
9. Then click "View and manage your downloads."

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/wvBimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/wvBimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712246227392(1).png)
10. Click "Download" next to your requested report.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712246234161(1).png)

  [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bO1image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/bO1image.png)
11. Open the file, with the given information, you can easily determine which of your ASINs are the most profitable, and investigate why some others may not be generating much profit. This can help you develop strategies to improve profitability.

FAQs

**1. How often does the Net PPM Report update?**

- The Net PPM Report updates monthly, but some data points may have a slight delay of up to a few days before they appear in Vendor Central.

**2. How far back can I access historical Net PPM data?**

- Amazon allows vendors to retrieve Net PPM data for up to 24 months. If you need older reports, consider downloading and archiving them regularly for future reference.

**3. What do the key metrics in the Net PPM Report mean?**

- Net PPM (%) – The percentage of profit after deducting Amazon fees, cost of goods, and allowances.
- Prior Period Comparison (%) – Shows how your margin has changed compared to the previous reporting period.
- Year-over-Year Change (%) – Compares your current margin to the same period in the previous year to track profitability trends.

**4. What should I do if my report data is missing or incorrect?**

- If your Net PPM data is missing or doesn’t seem accurate:

  - Check that the correct date range and filters are applied.
  - Ensure that all ASINs have complete cost and fee data in Vendor Central.
  - If the issue persists, contact Amazon Vendor Support to investigate.

**5. How can I use the Net PPM Report beyond tracking profitability?**

- The Net PPM Report is useful for:

  - Identifying underperforming ASINs and deciding whether to discontinue or optimize them.
  - Adjusting pricing strategies to maximize margins.
  - Evaluating product costs and looking for ways to reduce expenses.
  - Planning marketing and promotions based on profitable vs. low-margin items.

**6. How does Amazon calculate Net PPM?**

- Net PPM is calculated by factoring in:

  - Revenue per ASIN
  - Amazon fees (storage, fulfillment, referral fees, etc.)
  - Cost of goods (COGS) provided by the vendor
  - Allowances and chargebacks
  - It does not include advertising costs, so vendors should analyze PPM alongside ad spend for a full profitability picture.

**7. Can I automate or schedule Net PPM Reports?**

- No, Amazon does not currently allow automated scheduling for Net PPM Reports. However, you can manually download the report on a monthly basis to track profitability trends over time.
