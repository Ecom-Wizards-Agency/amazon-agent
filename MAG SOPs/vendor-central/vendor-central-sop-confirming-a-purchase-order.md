# Vendor Central SOP: Confirming a Purchase Order

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/vendor-central/page/vendor-central-sop-confirming-a-purchase-order

## Confirming a Purchase Order

**Who is this for?**: This is intended for individuals responsible for managing purchase orders on the Vendor Central platform.

**Objective:**To provide step-by-step instructions on how to confirm a Purchase Order (PO) on the Vendor Central platform.

**To confirm your Purchase Orders, follow these steps:**

1. From your Amazon Vendor Central homepage, select "Purchase Orders" from the "Orders" menu item.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0z3image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/0z3image.png)

2. If there is a number other than (0) beside "Confirm New POs" in the top left corner of your Purchase Orders Dashboard under "Action Items", click on it.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gDlimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/gDlimage.png)

3. Select the box next to the purchase orders that you want to confirm and click Open selected POs.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fngimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/fngimage.png)

4. On the next page, you can either make individual selections or perform a bulk edit on all items.

a.) To perform **bulk edit**, select all items and click the "Bulk Edit" button.

b.) Choose the product availability from the drop-down menu and the expected ship date, then click the button "Apply to selected rows below".

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/mk3image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/mk3image.png)

c. For**individual selection**, select the box next to the purchase orders that you want to confirm. Double-click on the yellow field under "Availability" to choose a suitable acknowledgment code, listed below. Your acknowledgment code will affect Amazon's buying decisions.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684431130886.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/QR7image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/QR7image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684431289766.png)

5. Before submitting, you may also export this data to Excel by clicking the "Export this data to Excel for your records" button above the line items table.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zRRimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/zRRimage.png)

Verify that the costs of each item are correct and match your system's Sales Order values against those of the Vendor Central Purchase Orders. All Purchase Orders must be verified within 12 hours of being issued by Vendor Central.

6. After making all necessary edits, click on the "Submit" button in the bottom left corner of the page.

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/JpEimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/JpEimage.png)

FAQs

Navigating the Vendor Central Platform

- **Q1: How can I locate the "Purchase Orders" section in Vendor Central?**

  - **A1:** From your Amazon Vendor Central homepage, select "Purchase Orders" under the "Orders" menu item.

Bulk Editing Purchase Orders

- **Q2: Can I edit multiple purchase orders at once?**

  - **A2:** Yes, you can perform bulk edits by selecting all items in the purchase order and clicking the "Bulk Edit" button. Then, choose the product availability and expected ship date from the drop-down menu and apply the changes to the selected rows.
- **Q3: Is there a way to revert changes made during bulk editing?**

  - **A3:** Vendor Central does not provide an explicit undo function. Double-check your selections before applying bulk edits. If changes have been submitted, you may need to contact Amazon support to rectify errors.

Exporting Data

- **Q4: Can I download the purchase order details for record-keeping?**

  - **A4:** Yes, you can export the data to Excel by clicking the "Export this data to Excel for your records" button above the line items table.
- **Q5: What information is included in the exported Excel file?**

  - **A5:** The Excel file typically contains details such as product SKUs, quantities, costs, and expected ship dates.

Verification and Timelines

- **Q6: What should I verify before submitting a purchase order?**

  - **A6:** Verify the costs of each item and ensure they match your system's sales order values. Also, confirm the availability and expected ship dates for accuracy.
- **Q7: How soon must purchase orders be verified after being issued?**

  - **A7:** All purchase orders must be verified within 12 hours of being issued by Vendor Central.

Submitting Purchase Orders

- **Q8: What happens if I forget to submit changes after editing?**

  - **A8:** Any changes made will not take effect until you click the "Submit" button in the bottom left corner of the page. Be sure to submit your edits before leaving the page.
- **Q9: Can I make changes to a purchase order after submission?**

  - **A9:** Once a purchase order is submitted, changes cannot be made directly on Vendor Central. You will need to contact Amazon Vendor Support for assistance.
