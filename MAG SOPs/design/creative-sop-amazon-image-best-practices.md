# Creative SOP: Amazon Image Best Practices

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/design-XyS/page/creative-sop-amazon-image-best-practices

## **Amazon Image Best Practices**

**Who is this for?**This is intended for individuals who need to produce, select, or create high-quality images for product pages and want to enhance their product image content.

**Objective:**The purpose of this document is to provide comprehensive best practices for producing, creating, or selecting quality images that effectively represent the appearance and usage of the product. It also includes recommendations for image sizing and content.

Amazon maintains strict guidelines for product photos. It is essential to review their guidelines here before proceeding. By adhering to the following best practices, you can optimize your listings and improve customer engagement. You can view the guidelines [HERE](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/gp/help/1881?language=en_US&ref=ag_1881_cont_16881).

**Main/Hero Image:**

- Background: Main images should have a pure white background with RGB color values of 255, 255, and 255. This white background seamlessly integrates with the Amazon search and product detail pages, providing a clean and professional appearance.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sUqimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686745851192.png)
- Authentic Product Representation: Main images must be professional photographs of the actual product. Graphics, illustrations, mockups, or placeholders are not allowed. The images should accurately depict the item without any excluded accessories or props that could confuse customers.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686745702611.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GTLimage.png)

- Avoid Text, Logos, and Graphics: Main images should not include any text, logos, borders, color blocks, watermarks, or other graphics over the product or in the background. The focus should be solely on showcasing the product itself.
- Single Product View: Main images must display a single view of the product. Avoid multiple angles or perspectives in the main image, as it can cause confusion for customers.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686745619972.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rfximage.png)

- Complete Product Visibility: The main image should show the entire product that is being sold. Ensure that no parts of the product are cut off or obscured by the image frame, except in the case of jewelry such as necklaces.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686744853344.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zphCUKimage.png)

- Packaging Exclusion: Main images should feature the product outside of any packaging. Boxes, bags, or cases should not be visible in the image unless they are an essential part of the product itself.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686745276976.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/CZFimage.png)
- Human Model Posture: Avoid using images of human models that are sitting, kneeling, leaning, or lying down. However, it is encouraged to showcase different physical mobilities using assistive technology such as wheelchairs or prosthetics.
- Model Display for Women's and Men's Clothing: Main images for women's and men's clothing must show the product being worn on a human model to provide a visual representation of how it looks when worn.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686738354621.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hoVimage.png)
- Flat Photography for Apparel and Accessories: For multi-pack apparel items and accessories, they should be photographed flat, without being worn or modeled.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Gk9image.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686745121872.png)
- Shoe Orientation: Main images of shoes should display a single shoe facing left at a 45-degree angle. This angle provides a clear and consistent view of the product.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686745538995.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Mgfimage.png)

**Guidelines for All Product Images**

- Accurate Representation and Matching Product Title: Product images must provide an accurate representation of the item being sold. They should effectively showcase the product's features, appearance, and functionality. The images must also align with the product title. They should depict the specific item described in the title, enabling customers to easily associate the visuals with the product they are considering.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686746089066.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/BGpimage.png)
- Use a pure white background (RGB 255, 255, 255). Failure to do so may result in rejection by Amazon.
- The product should occupy at least 85% of the image area. This ensures that customers can clearly see the item and its details without unnecessary distractions.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686738217337.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/kYhimage.png)

- Optimal zoom experience and File Size Limit: To enhance sales, it is recommended that image files use 1600 x 2000 px for mobile-dominant traffic and 2000 x 2000 px for desktop-dominant traffic ,based on customer session behavior. Ideally, the resolution should be 2500 x 2500 pixels, but a minimum of 1000 x 1000 pixels is acceptable. Images must also not exceed 10,000px on the longest side. Keeping the file sizes within this limit ensures efficient loading and optimal performance on the Amazon platform.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686744239026.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YiRimage.png)

- Image Quality: Images must be clear, sharp, and free from blurriness, pixelation, or jagged edges. This ensures that customers can view the product details accurately and make informed purchasing decisions. There is a large difference in quality between the following images:

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/amazon-image-best-practices-image-eogmbsbg.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Kb3image.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/nViimage.png)

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686744293921.png)
- Appropriate Content: Images must not contain nudity or have any sexually suggestive elements. Additionally, avoid displaying leotards, underwear, and swimwear for kids and babies on human models..
- Avoid Amazon Logos or Trademarks:

  Images must not include any Amazon logos, trademarks, or variations thereof that may cause confusion. This includes words or logos containing terms such as AMAZON, PRIME, ALEXA, or the Amazon Smile design.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1686738399320.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eAcimage.png)
- Acceptable image formats include JPEG (.jpg or .jpeg), TIFF (.tif), PNG (.png), or GIF (.gif). JPEG is the preferred format. Please note that our servers do not support animated .gifs.

FAQs

Main/Hero Image

- **Q1: What background should be used for main images on Amazon?**

  - **A1:**Main images should have a pure white background with RGB values of 255, 255, 255.
- **Q2: Can illustrations or mockups be used as main images?**

  - **A2:** No, main images must be professional photographs of the actual product, not graphics, illustrations, mockups, or placeholders.
- **Q3: Are logos or watermarks allowed on main images?**

  - **A3:**No, main images should not include text, logos, borders, color blocks, watermarks, or other graphics.
- **Q4: Can the main image display multiple angles or perspectives of a product?**

  - **A4:** No, the main image must show a single view of the product to avoid customer confusion.
- **Q5: Should the main image include the product packaging?**

  - **A5:** No, the main image should display the product outside its packaging, unless the packaging is an integral part of the product.
- **Q6: What are the requirements for clothing products in the main image?**

  - **A6:** Women's and men's clothing must be shown worn on a human model, while multi-pack apparel and accessories should be photographed flat.
- **Q7: How should shoes be displayed in the main image?**

  - **A7:**Shoes should be oriented to face left at a 45-degree angle for a clear and consistent view.

Secondary Images

- **Q8: How many secondary images are recommended for a product listing?**

  - **A8:** It is recommended to include 3-5 secondary images to enhance the product’s appeal.
- **Q9: What content should secondary images showcase?**

  - **A9:** Secondary images should capture the product in use, highlight functionality, provide additional angles, and can include lifestyle photos or group shots of product lines.
- **Q10: Are infographics allowed in secondary images?**

  - **A10:** Yes, infographics can be used in secondary images to highlight key differences between your product and competitors.

General Product Image Guidelines

- **Q11: What percentage of the image area should the product occupy?**

  - **A11:** The product should occupy at least 85% of the image area for clear visibility.
- **Q12: What resolution is recommended for Amazon product images?**

  - **A12:** Image resolution should ideally be 2500 x 2500 pixels, with a minimum of 1000 x 1000 pixels and a maximum of 10,000px on the longest side.
- **Q13: Are animated GIFs supported for product images?**

  - **A13:** No, animated GIFs are not supported. Acceptable formats include JPEG, TIFF, PNG, and non-animated GIFs, with JPEG being preferred.
- **Q14: Can product images include Amazon logos or trademarks?**

  - **A14:** No, images must not include Amazon logos, trademarks, or any variations that could cause confusion.
- **Q15: What content is prohibited in product images?**

  - **A15:** Images must not include nudity, sexually suggestive elements, or depictions of leotards, underwear, or swimwear for children on human models.
- **Q16: What are the requirements for image quality?**

  - **A16:** Images must be clear, sharp, and free from blurriness, pixelation, or jagged edges to ensure accurate representation of product details.
