## **Creating A+ Content and Brand Story Modules**

**Who is this for?**

This SOP is for Amazon sellers, marketers, designers, and account managers aiming to enhance product listings with A+ Content and Brand Story modules.

**Objective:**

To design and implement high-quality A+ Content and Brand Story Modules that enhance product pages, engage customers, and drive sales.

> **Important Reminder:**
>
> **For Brand Story images****, any image containing crawlable text will** **not** **display the uploaded alt-text on the Product Detail Page (PDP). Instead, it will show as having an** **empty alt attribute** **on the PDP, even if alt-text was provided in the backend.**
>
> **New Alt Text Update for Brand Stores**
>
> - You can now add alt text to shoppable images in Amazon Brand Stores
> - Character Limit: Max 125 characters
> - Alt Text Process: Same as the current process used for A+ Content alt text
>
> **UPDATE**: Amazon now automatically generates alt-text for all images in A+ Content, including Brand Story, using AI technology. Selling partners no longer need to manually input alt-text or keyword images, as these are auto-generated for accessibility purposes and do not impact search results. Due to this update, manually added alt-text may not reflect or display as expected. No action is required from the seller, and existing content continues to function as intended.
>
> **NOTE**: Not all contents are affected so we may still continue to add alt-text until further notice

**Step 1: Understand A+ Content Basics**

1. **A+ Content Types:**

  - **Basic Modules:**

    - Modules include logos, text, and images in various combinations.
    - Suitable for showcasing brand and product features.
  - **Premium Modules:**

    - Advanced layouts featuring videos, interactive elements, and carousels.
2. **General Specifications:**

  - Follow the specific dimensions, character limits, and format requirements listed below for each module type.
  - Images must meet minimum pixel requirements to ensure clarity.

Brand Story Modules

#### **Brand Story Modules**

1. **Brand Carousel Background:**

  - Locked in the top position, mandatory.
  - Will be displayed behind all of the modules submitted on the content

  - Image Specs: 1464px x 625px for desktop; 463px x 625px for mobile.
  - Headline: 30 characters.
  - Body Text: 130 characters.

**Step 4: Develop Content**

1. **Gather Visuals:**

  - Ensure all images meet resolution and dimension requirements.
  - Align branding elements like logos and color schemes.
2. **Write Copy:**

  - Headline and body text must adhere to specified character limits.
  - Focus on concise, impactful messaging.
3. **Follow Recommended Layout:**

A+ Content Layout

#### **A+ Content Layout**

[**(Visual Example Found Here)**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1bwjBrja8JlMHdVtTMFda99KYWgpVzld6/view)

Recommended sequence for organizing A+ Content:

- Standard Image & Dark Text Overlay
- Standard Image Header With Text
- Standard Single Image & Sidebar
- Standard Image Header With Text
- Standard Four Image & Text
- Standard Image Header With Text
- Standard Comparison Chart

1. **Standard Image & Dark Text Overlay**: Use as a narrow header image for logos, banners, or graphic headers. Leaving the text area blank ensures it won’t block the image.
2. **Standard Image Header with Text**: Large graphic callout ideal for promoting products with text below the image.
3. **Standard Single Image & Sidebar**: Best for logo placement or smaller graphics with brand info on the side and product details in the center.
4. **Standard Four Image & Text:** Perfect for presenting product benefits, features, instructions, or ingredients with relevant icons or graphics.
5. **Standard Comparison Chart:** Position at the bottom (7th module). Compare up to 6 products using 10 metrics, with options for highlighting, checkmarks, or text.

Brand Story Layout

#### **Brand Story Layout**

Recommended modules for creating a compelling brand narrative:

**SAMPLE: Age of Sage - Brand Story Design**[**(Preview Found Here**](https://www.amazon.com/Fruity-Scent-Natural-Soap-Women/dp/B09C6N41QY?ref_=ast_sto_dp&th=1&psc=1)**)**

1. **Brand Carousel Background:** Large graphic background image aligned with the brand's theme to establish trust and loyalty.
2. **Brand Logo & Description**: Highlight the brand's identity and introduce what sets it apart or how it started.
3. **Brand Focus Image**: Showcase a company photo or top-selling product to refocus attention on what customers should purchase.
4. **Brand Q&A**: Address frequently asked questions to share the brand's story, mission, or uniqueness.
5. **Brand ASIN & Store Showcase**: Encourage customers to explore the catalog by showcasing top sellers or linking to the brand store.

**NOTE**: For both A+ Content and Brand Story, limit the number of modules to 3-5 for clarity and effectiveness.

**Step 5: Upload and Verify**

1. **Upload Process:**

  - Use Amazon’s A+ Content Manager to place modules in the chosen sequence.
  - Ensure all fields are filled accurately.
2. **Validation**:

  - Preview content to ensure compatibility across devices.
  - Verify adherence to specifications for image and text quality.

**Step 6: Analyze and Optimize**

1. **Monitor Metrics:**

  - Track customer engagement (e.g., clicks, dwell time).
2. **Refine Content:**

  - Use performance data to update visuals and copy.

**Pro Tips**

1. **For Brand Story:**

  - Use the Brand Carousel Background to create an impactful first impression.
  - Highlight top-selling products in the Brand Focus Image module.
2. **For A+ Content:**

  - Place the Standard Comparison Chart at the bottom for a strong closing.

Basics of A+ Content

  - **A1:** A+ Content is divided into two types:

    - **Basic Modules:** Include logos, text, and images in various combinations to showcase brand and product features.
    - **Premium Modules:** Offer advanced layouts with videos, interactive elements, and carousels.
- **Q2: What are the general specifications for A+ Content?**

  - **A2:** Follow specific dimensions, character limits, and format requirements for each module type. Ensure images meet minimum pixel requirements for clarity.

Module Specifications

- **Q3: What are the specifications for the Standard Company Logo module?**

  - **A3:** Minimum image size: 600px x 180px.
- **Q4: What are the specifications for the Standard Comparison Chart module?**

  - **A4:**

    - Products: 3-6 comparison products.
    - Image size: 150px x 300px minimum.
    - Metrics: 1-10 metrics with a title limit of 100 characters.
- **Q5: What are the requirements for the Premium Full Video module?**

  - **A5:**

    - Video resolution: 960:540 minimum for main video, 1464:600 for preview.
