# Design Tier Q&A

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/design-XyS/page/design-tier-qa

### **Design Tier Q&A**

- **How do our design tiers work?**

  - Each tier includes the number of designs, photos, and copy that can be delivered each month. If you select design tier 2, each month you can receive up to
- 2 Designs (2 of any of the below)

  - A design is any of the following:

    - A+ Content
    - Brand Story
    - Brand Store
- +8 photo designs for secondary images
- +6 copy updates on 6 ASINs

  - Titles/bullets

- The tiers also dictate how many design projects can be worked on at the same time. Example: Design tier 4 = 2 concurrent projects. Work will not start on a 3rd project until you as the client have signed off on a project fully. This is to encourage clients to give sturdy and specific direction early in the creative process to save resources and limit revisions. It rewards clients with faster usable deliveries too. Copy & photos are not limited to active projects. Definition: Active: What is currently being worked on. Cannot exceed active project count simultaneously or must upgrade design tier. Requested: The number of projects that can be requested or worked in a given month. Design: A+ content, a brand store, or a brand story. Copy: Title, Bullets, and Description Images: Graphics, infographics, images, above the fold outside of A+ content. (Secondary images) Videos: Editing a video for a detail page or advertisement.

Example:

- Client XYZ: Tier 3 (can have the following in one month)

  - 3 Designs (2 A+, one Brand Story) (Brand Store, 1 A+, Brand Story) ( 3 total) - only 1 can be actively assigned at a time
  - 15 Images (That is 15 individual images (.png, .jpeg, etc.)) (You can spread those 15 out however) - 7 can be actively assigned
  - 9 Copy (That is 9 ASINs worth of copy - Title, Bullets, Description) - 4 can be actively assigned
  - 0 Videos

**Where do I find what tier my client has?**

- Hubspot - creative tier column

**What is Defined by Requested Designs?**

- Designs are (A+ Content, Storefront, or Brand Story Builds)
- Requested Designs are how many each tier allows to be requested per month
- The # of active can be assigned and worked on (depending on their tier)

**What are Requested Images?**

Note: Each image limitation is by individual image (infographic).

- Images are info-graphics, lifestyle, main image, etc
- Requested images is how many images can be requested per month.
- 1 = image not 1 image stack
- The # of active images can be assigned based on the design tier

**What are Requested Copy?**

- Requested copy is listing optimizations for:

  - Title, Bullet Points, Description
  - (A+ copy does NOT fall into this category)
- Requested copy is how many optimizations can be requested per month
- Allotted number = (X) amount ASINs worth of copy - Title, Bullets, Description
- The # of active copy can be assigned based on the design tier

**What are Requested Videos?**

- Requested videos are MAG created inhouse videos for advertising or the product detail page
- Requested video is how many videos can be requested per month

**Premium A+ Content Eligibility:**

- If Yes, we can create Premium A+ content
- If No, we cannot create Premium A+ content

**Active Designs/Images/Copy**

- Active (designs/images/copy), dictates how many active deliverables can be worked on at a given time during a month
- ACTIVE = actively being worked on by a designer (once content is assigned to another team for upload, it is not considered active and a new design can be assigned out)

**Q: Do these numbers roll over into the next month (ex: only used 1 design and 5 image tasks when they were allotted 3 designs and 15 images - do the additional 2 designs and 10 images get added onto the allotted amount for the next month)?**

**A:**No - the limit is monthly whether they are all used or not. Encourage clients to give precise and direct feedback to move through designs more efficiently. Contracts are “for up to X designs per month” (This is better for the client!!) The design tiers are "REQUESTS" per month with contract language **"UP TO" X amount

**Q: Does MAG create A+ Content crawlable text and would this be considered part of “Requested Design” or “Requested Copy”?**

Ex: if a client is just looking for help creating crawlable text for their current A+ Content?

**A:** Yes MAG creates crawlable text.

**Q: In order to track images correctly, should I create one ticket per each image, one ticket per group of images corresponding to one asin or one ticket per images corresponding to a parentage?**

Ex. I want to use the 7 active image slots across 3 different ASINS ( 3 main images, 2 infographics, and 2 lifestyle).

**A:** 1 task per ASIN - note how many images are needed in the task (ie. 3 main images, 2 infographics, and 2 lifestyle). Monitor image count on ASIN tracker Q: How will templated designs for infographics / A+ work for the design limit? Ex: Stack of 7 images are made in one task, but 10 other style variations will use the same images just replacing the product.

**A:** 1 image is 1 image regardless of a template (work on combination images showing size options and color variations). Use your best judgment and reach out to your AD if there are specific requests.
