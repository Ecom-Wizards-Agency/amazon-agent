# Walmart SOP: Adding Multiple Items by Matching Walmart's Catalog

## **Adding Multiple Items by Matching Walmart's Catalog**

**Who is this for?** This process is applicable to Walmart sellers who wish to add items in bulk by matching them to Walmart's catalog, or those adding new products that aren’t listed on Walmart.

**Objective:** This SOP provides step-by-step instructions for adding multiple items to your catalog by matching them to existing products listed on Walmart.com using the bulk upload method in Seller Center.

**Steps to Match Walmart's Catalog**

**Step 1: Get Started**

1. **Navigate to your Catalog:**

  - Log in to Seller Center.
  - Go to the **Catalog**section.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-adding-multiple-items-by-matching-walmarts-catalog-image-2de13ixg.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/lJTimage.png)

1. **Select the "Add items" button:**

  - Choose Upload in bulk under Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/STBimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6QWimage.png)

1. **Click on the “Match the catalog” button:**

  - Select the fulfillment type for your products.
  - If you are selling in multiple markets, choose the markets where you want to apply the changes.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-adding-multiple-items-by-matching-walmarts-catalog-image-hnz60h1r.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yjOimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3ZYimage.png)

**Step 2: Download and Complete the Template**

1. **Download the Template:**

  - After selecting “**Match the catalog**,” download the template provided by Walmart.
  - The template contains sections categorized as:

    - **Required**: These fields must be filled out to avoid data errors.
    - **Conditional/Required for Specific Conditions**: These fields are necessary for specific conditions like item status (e.g., Remanufactured, Pre-Owned).
    - **Optional**: Additional details like extra images or sales restrictions.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/MN7image.png)

1. **Fill Out the Required Sections:**

  - **Additional Image URL**: You can upload extra images for an item. Just ensure they comply with the [Product Detail Page: Image Guidelines & Requirements](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-Image-guidelines-&-requirements).
  - **Sales Restrictions**: Fill out if certain states or zip codes prohibit the sale of the product.

**Important Notes:**

- Do not modify the data in the first six rows of your template.
- Failing to provide required information will result in data errors..

**Step 3: Upload the Template**

1. **Upload the Completed Template:**

  - After filling out the template, go to the Catalog section in Seller Center.
  - Select Add items and then Upload in bulk.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-adding-multiple-items-by-matching-walmarts-catalog-image-ijlcvts6.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0VSimage.png)

1. **Drag and Drop or Browse:**

  - Drag and drop your completed file onto the browser window under Upload, or select the Browse link to locate the file on your computer.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-adding-multiple-items-by-matching-walmarts-catalog-image-t9lfidg7.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/RaUimage.png)

1. **Check the Upload Status:**

  - After uploading, check the [Activity Feed](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/list-items) in Seller Center to ensure the upload was successful.
  - Updates can appear within 15 minutes, but may take up to 4 hours. To track the status of your item setup, refer to [Track item setup or maintenance activity in Seller Center.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20setup/Track-item-setup-or-maintenance-activity-in-Seller-Center)

FAQs

**Q1: How do I get started with adding items?**

**A1**:

1. Log in to Seller Center.
2. Navigate to the "Catalog" section.
3. Click the “Add items” button.
4. Choose "Upload in bulk" under "Add thousands of items using a spreadsheet."
5. Click the “Match the catalog” button and select the appropriate fulfillment type.

**Q2: What information do I need to fill out in the template?**

**A2**: The template has three sections:

- **Required:** These fields must be completed to avoid data errors.
- **Conditional/Required for Specific Conditions:** Fields required for certain conditions, like item status (e.g., Remanufactured, Pre-Owned).
- **Optional:** Additional information like extra images or sales restrictions.

**Q3: Can I upload additional images for my items?**

**A3**: Yes, you can add additional images. Make sure they meet the guidelines in[Walmart’s Product Detail Page: Image Guidelines & Requirements.](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Item%20setup/Item%20content,%20imagery,%20and%20media/Product-detail-page:-Image-guidelines-&-requirements)

**Q4: What should I do if my product has sales restrictions?**

**A4**: If your product is prohibited from being sold in certain states or zip codes, make sure to fill out the Sales Restrictions section in the template.

**Q5: What happens if I modify the data in the first six rows of the template?**

**A5**: Modifying the first six rows of the template will lead to data errors. Always leave these rows unaltered.

**Q6: How do I upload the completed template?**

**A6**:

1. Go to the Catalog section in Seller Center.
2. Click “Add items” and then “Upload in bulk.”
3. Drag and drop the completed file into the upload section or select "Browse" to locate the file on your computer.

**Q7: How can I check if my file uploaded successfully?**

**A7**: After uploading, check the Activity Feed in Seller Center to monitor the upload status. It can take up to 4 hours for updates to appear, but they may show within 15 minutes.

**Q8: What if I can’t find my item in Walmart’s catalog?**

**A8**: If you can’t find a match, consider using the "Full Setup" template to add new items to your catalog. This process allows you to add items that aren’t listed on Walmart.com.

**Q9: How long does it take for my items to be added after uploading?**

**A9**: Updates can take anywhere from 15 minutes to 4 hours to appear in Seller Center, depending on the system's processing time.
