## **Walmart Seller Center Account Audit**

**Who is this for?:**This is done by Walmart specialists or by various professionals or teams, depending on your resources and requirements.

**Objective:**The primary objective of auditing a Walmart seller account is to ensure its overall health, compliance, and optimal performance on the Walmart marketplace.

**Instructions:**

1. To audit Walmart settings, click settings and check each section under the Settings.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/X3ximage.png)

2. If there is missing information, inform the seller about the details that are missing and need to be supplied, and notify them of the needed actions
