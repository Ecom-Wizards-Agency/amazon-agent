# Walmart SOP: Create a Single Item in Walmart Manually (Seller Fulfilled Type)

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-create-a-single-item-in-walmart-manually-seller-fulfilled-type

## **Create a Single Item in Walmart Manually (Seller Fulfilled Type)**

**Who is this for?**For individuals who want to create new listings on their Walmart Seller Central account.

**Objective:** Successfully create a listing in Walmart manually and know all the required attributes to be able to create the listing.

Required Attributes:

- SKU
- Product ID (only UPC, EAN, GTIN, ISBN are allowed)
- Product ID Type
- Product Name
- Description (not required but good to have)
- Key Product Features (not required but good to have)
- Brand
- Selling Price
- Main Image URL
- Secondary Images (not required but good to have)
- Package Weight (lbs) if in a different weight unit please convert lbs.
- Restriction Type (this attribute is category specific)

**Instructions:**

1. Log in to Walmart seller central account. Go to [https://seller.walmart.com/](https://seller.walmart.com/)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1710429524059(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/qMjimage.png)

2. Go to **“Items”** under the Product Catalog. And click the drop down arrow in the upper right corner then click on **“Add Items”**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%203.40.50%E2%80%AFAM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/atmimage.png)

3. You will be redirected to the [Add page](https://seller.walmart.com/item/add-items?returnPATH=%2Fitems-and-inventory%2Fmanage-items). Click the **“create a new item”** link.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1710429532168(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Hhlimage.png)

4. Choose the most appropriate category for the product. Enter the Item identifier (Product ID), Fulfillment, and Item Name.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1710429541427(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/CJHimage.png)

5. Once the mentioned attributes are supplied, complete the required information in **ITEM CONTENT, IMAGERY & MEDIA**, and **OFFER** sections.

- **ITEM CONTENT**: SKU, Product Description, Brand. Supply key features if the listing has.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684190273349(1).png)

- Key features or bullet points.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1710429549889(1).png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dLXimage.png)
- **IMAGERY** AND **MEDIA**: Here you have the option to select the images from your device or import images by URL.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1684190340697(1).png)

- **OFFER:** Restriction Type (other categories do not require this field), Selling Price, and Shipping Weight (lbs).

6. Double check the information and once complete click the submit changes button. A feed ID will be generated.

FAQs

1. What does "Product ID" mean, and how do I obtain it?

The Product ID refers to the unique identifier for the product, such as a UPC, EAN, GTIN, or ISBN. This is crucial for identifying your product in Walmart’s catalog. You can obtain the Product ID from:

- Manufacturers (UPC/GTIN codes are often provided with the product).
- GS1 or other GTIN providers.
- Ensure the Product ID you enter matches what’s on the packaging or product specifications.

2. How do I determine the appropriate Product ID Type for my item?

The Product ID Type is a dropdown field where you select the type of identifier:

- UPC for a universal product code.
- EAN for European product codes.
- GTIN for a global trade item number.
- ISBN for books. Choose the correct identifier based on the type your product uses.

3. Where do I find the "Restriction Type" for my product?

The Restriction Type is a category-specific field that determines whether your product has any legal, safety, or compliance restrictions.

- To find the Restriction Type, select the correct product category during the product creation process.
- Walmart may have specific restrictions for categories such as health, beauty, food, or electronics. If the field does not appear, it means your category does not have any restriction requirements.

4. What are some common errors that can occur during the upload process?

Common errors during listing creation include:

- Invalid Product ID: Ensure your Product ID is correctly formatted (UPC, EAN, GTIN).
- Missing Product Images: Images must meet Walmart’s minimum resolution (1000x1000 pixels).
- Incorrect Weight Formatting: Make sure the shipping weight is entered in pounds (lbs).
- Category Mismatch: Verify the product is in the correct category. If errors occur, download the error report from the Activity Feed, review it, and re-upload the file after fixing the issues.

5. How can I cross-check the listing details before submitting?

Before submitting your listing:

- Check the product category and ensure it is correct.
- Verify the product ID (UPC, EAN) matches the correct format.
- Confirm image URLs: Ensure product images meet Walmart's specifications (1000x1000 pixels).
- Review the title and description for accuracy and clarity.
- Ensure that the product price and shipping weight are correct.

6. How do I create a listing for variants or multipacks?

If your product has variants (e.g., different sizes or colors), you should:

1. Use the parent-child relationship to group variants under a parent SKU.
2. Ensure each child variant has its own SKU and is listed with the correct variant attributes (color, size, etc.).
3. If you are uploading a multipack, specify the number of items per pack under the product description.

7. What image size and format does Walmart require for product listings?

Walmart requires the main image to be at least 1000x1000 pixels for a clear zoom-in feature.

- Image format should be JPEG or PNG.
- Ensure the image has a white background and displays the product clearly, without additional text or watermarks.

8. What should I do if my listing isn't showing on the Product Detail Page (PDP) after 10 minutes?

If your product isn’t showing after 10 minutes:

1. Check the Activity Feed for Feed ID status to ensure the file uploaded successfully.
2. Allow up to 24 hours for the changes to appear.
3. If the listing is still missing, contact Walmart Support for further investigation.

9. What happens if I leave out non-required fields like the product description or key features?

While not required, fields like Product Description and Key Features are important for improving your listing's visibility and customer appeal.

- Missing Product Descriptions may affect your listing’s ranking and discovery on Walmart.com.
- Key Features help highlight the product’s selling points and provide customers with relevant information to make purchasing decisions.

10. How can I monitor the upload status for my new listing?

To monitor the upload:

1. Go to Activity Feed in the Seller Center and search using the Feed ID.
2. If the upload has errors, download the error report and resolve the issues.
3. If the upload is successful, verify the changes on the PDP by clicking the listing and selecting View on Walmart.com.
