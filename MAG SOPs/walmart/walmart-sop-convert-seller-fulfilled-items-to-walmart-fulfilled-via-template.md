# Walmart SOP: Convert Seller-Fulfilled Items to Walmart-Fulfilled via Template

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-convert-seller-fulfilled-items-to-walmart-fulfilled-via-template

## **Convert Seller-Fulfilled Items to Walmart-Fulfilled via Template**

**Who is this for?:** To convert seller-fulfilled items to Walmart-fulfilled items on the Walmart Marketplace, Walmart specialists or the seller themselves are responsible for making this transition.

**Objective:** Converting seller-fulfilled items to Walmart-fulfilled items is a process that involves using Walmart's fulfillment service known as Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS). The main objective is to help sellers store their products in Walmart's fulfillment centers and have Walmart handle the packaging, shipping, and customer service for those items. This allows sellers to offer customers fast and reliable shipping options and leverage Walmart's brand recognition and customer trust.

Instructions:

1. Log in to Walmart seller central account. Go to[https://seller.walmart.com/](https://seller.walmart.com/)![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712242974708.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/JXEimage.png)

2. Navigate to Catalog and select all items you want to convert to Walmart-fulfilled. You can also filter by fulfillment type to find all seller-fulfilled items.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-TEB59TWI.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GiPimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-FOKWT9B9.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/E2ximage.png)

3. Next, In the actions bar, select More actions and then Convert to WFS.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-8E7KRBLG.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0rzimage.png)

4. Review the selections, and click Confirm if the selections are correct

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-KKDFUS4N.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/w9yimage.png)

5. Click Go to Activity Feed and view the Downloads Tab. Download the template and fill in the blank fields.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-A1UB5DPP.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eGYimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-NV5GVHTB.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/S41image.png)

6. Open the file and complete all attributes under the required columns. Check each sheet to ensure all required attributes or information are filled out accurately.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-DRGO3XRT.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/aBrimage.png)

7. In the **Trade Item Configurations** Tab, ensure the following required attributes are completed: **SKU**, **Country of Origin**, **Orderable GTIN**, **Each GTIN**, and **Dimensions** (Depth, Width, Height, and Weight).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-OD4FCKTO.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vPAimage.png)

1. Once you’ve filled out the template, save the file. Then, return to **Catalog**, select **Add Items**and click **Upload in Bulk**. Upload your file by either dragging it under the **Upload a Spreadsheet** heading or selecting it from your computer.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-6OHV33T0.png)![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-Y834SR5R.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-QG62Z1PI.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/D4fimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YHMimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/aYoimage.png)

9. Click Activity Feed under Catalog to monitor the upload and check whether the new listing was sucessfully processed or if any errors occurred.![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-LNBT4J1R.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SnCimage.png)

10. If there are no issues, your item will be published in as quickly as 15 minutes or up to 4 hours. Our system will also check for errors — such as missing attributes, wrong data type, invalid check digit — within a few seconds. If there are any, the error report will automatically download in your browser.

In the report, you’ll see the number of errors and descriptions in columns A and B. The cells that contain errors will also be highlighted in yellow. Correct the errors in the report and reupload it in the Activity Feed.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-G9WXO3O7.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/PLVimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-40670SJ0.png)
