# Walmart SOP: How to Appeal an Account Suspension or Suppression on Walmart Marketplace

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-how-to-appeal-an-account-suspension-or-suppression-on-walmart-marketplace

## **How to Appeal an Account Suspension or Suppression on Walmart Marketplace**

**Who is this for?**

This document applies to Amazon specialists who are tasked with handling Walmart account suspensions, suppressions, or terminations for clients. It is designed to provide clear, actionable steps to appeal the decision when eligible, and ensure that the client's account is in good standing.

**Objective:**

This SOP is intended to guide Amazon specialists managing a client's Walmart account through the appeal process if their account is suspended, suppressed, or terminated. It provides step-by-step instructions on how to submit an appeal and the actions needed to reinstate the account, ensuring compliance with Walmart Marketplace policies.

**Policy Overview**

Sellers on Walmart Marketplace are expected to adhere to the [**Seller Performance Standards**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards) and the [**Marketplace Retailer Agreement**](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt7029afb2a0cd3919/Comprehensive_Walmart_Marketplace_Program_Retailer_Agreement.pdf?branch=test2). If they fail to comply, their account may be suspended or suppressed. The seller will receive an email detailing the reason for the suspension, and if applicable, the steps they can take to appeal.

**Key Points:**

- **Suspended Accounts**: Eligible for appeal if the suspension is linked to performance issues or policy violations.
- **Terminated Accounts**: Once terminated, appeals are not possible.

**Appeal Process**

**Step 1: Review Client’s Performance Metrics**

1. **Go to the**[**Performance Page**](https://seller.walmart.com/performance/order-and-fulfillment) in the Seller Center.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-how-to-appeal-an-account-suspension-or-suppression-on-walmart-marketplace-image-fg4p6zr0.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/x9eimage.png)

1. Review your client’s performance metrics, such as order defect rate, late shipment rate, etc., to identify why the account was suspended.
2. Understanding the root cause will help tailor the appeal and prevent similar issues in the future.

**Step 2: Create a Business Plan of Action**

1. **Prepare a business plan** that clearly outlines the violation, corrective actions, and steps to prevent future issues.
2. If the suspension is related to the [**Marketplace Retailer Agreement**](https://azure-na-assets.contentstack.com/v3/assets/blta7903c6b840b702d/blt7029afb2a0cd3919/Comprehensive_Walmart_Marketplace_Program_Retailer_Agreement.pdf?branch=test2), include details about how the client will ensure compliance moving forward.
3. **Include supporting documentation** where applicable, such as:

  - **Warehouse images** or stock documentation.
  - **Invoices** from distributors or suppliers (must be within two months old).
  - **Intellectual property documents** if relevant.
4. These documents help verify that the client has inventory available and is in compliance with Walmart’s policies.

**Step 3: Submit the Appeal**

1. Navigate to the **Seller Center** and click the **Help** button at the top.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-how-to-appeal-an-account-suspension-or-suppression-on-walmart-marketplace-image-1zydd24x.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fxQimage.png)

1. Select **Contact Support** and follow the instructions to submit the appeal.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-how-to-appeal-an-account-suspension-or-suppression-on-walmart-marketplace-image-25v9vfd3.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Sf5image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-how-to-appeal-an-account-suspension-or-suppression-on-walmart-marketplace-image-ny2fiomv.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oCdi54image.png)

1. Be sure to attach the business plan and any necessary documents.
2. Wait for Walmart’s response, as appeals are processed in the order they are received.

**Step 4: Wait for the Appeal Decision**

- Walmart will review the appeal and notify you about the decision.
- **Note**: There is no guarantee that the account will be reinstated after submitting the appeal.

**Things to Keep in Mind**

- **Appeals don’t guarantee reinstatement**: While submitting an appeal is a crucial step, it does not automatically guarantee that the client’s account will be reinstated.
- **Account reinstatement**: If the account is reinstated, the client must continue following the action plan to improve their performance and avoid future violations.
- **Compliance**: Submitting an appeal confirms that the seller agrees to follow Walmart’s [**Seller Performance Standards**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Performance/Seller-performance-standards) and [**Marketplace Retailer Policies**](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Account/Walmart-Marketplace-seller-retailer-policies).

FAQs

**Q1: My client’s selling privileges were reinstated. What should we do now?**

- If the client’s privileges were reinstated, continue to follow the steps in the **business plan of action** to improve performance. Regularly monitor the client’s metrics to ensure continued compliance and avoid future violations.

**Q2: How long will it take to process the appeal?**

- Walmart reviews appeals in the order they are submitted, and the processing time can vary. Stay in communication with the client and check for updates regularly.

**Q3: My client’s account was terminated. Can we appeal?**

- Unfortunately, once an account is terminated, there is no option to appeal, and the account will be permanently deactivated.
