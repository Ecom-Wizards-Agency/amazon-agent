# AI SOP: Creating Product Listing with ChatGPT

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/ai/page/ai-sop-creating-product-listing-with-chatgpt

## **Creating Product Listing with ChatGPT**

**Who is this for?**

Intended for the team members responsible for managing product listings on Amazon and other e-commerce platforms.

**Objective:**

- To provide guidelines to the team members on conducting product listing with ChatGPT.
- To ensure that the product listings are accurate and comprehensive, and effectively communicate the features and benefits of the product to potential customers.
- Log in to your OpenAI account. If you don't have an account, you can sign up for free.

**Ask ChatGPT to write a Product Listing Prompt:**

1. Create an Amazon listing title of 150-170 characters for [SAMPLE PRODUCT NAME] utilizing the keywords [KW1, KW2, KW3 ...]

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/JTYimage.png)

2. Create a list of 5 compelling and inquisitive bullet points using 200-220 characters, incorporating the given keywords [INSERT KW1, KW2, KW3 ...]. The aim is to persuade the reader and spark their interest.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zjgimage.png)

3. Can you develop a launch plan for my upcoming product, scheduled for July 1, 2023? The plan should include actionable steps, a timeline, backup plans, scheduled breaks, and methods for collecting user feedback.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vBIimage.png)

4. Compose a user-friendly product description for Amazon containing 1000-1500 characters, utilizing the provided keywords**[INSERT KW1, KW2, KW3 ...].** The description should be easy to comprehend and engaging.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GYYimage.png)

5. Write a list of five product titles that are both compelling and SEO-friendly for**[SAMPLE PRODUCT NAME]** listings on Amazon.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/A6Nimage.png)

6. Could you please provide me with 10 benefits of using**[SAMPLE PRODUCT NAME]**?

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/jnaimage.png)

7. Please write a list of five Amazon listing titles that are both compelling and SEO-friendly for your [describe your product].

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/oCZimage.png)

8. Produce a product description for a home improvement item that is both captivating and distinctive, and contains practical instructions on its usage and how it can enhance a customer's home. Additionally, make sure to include relevant SEO keywords.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gQOimage.png)

9. Create an Amazon product title that accurately describes the product, including all of its characteristics such as size, color, and other relevant features: [SAMPLE PRODUCT NAME]

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/00aimage.png)

10. Please develop ten Amazon-optimized title variations for my product, which is currently titled [SAMPLE PRODUCT NAME]

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Amhimage.png)

11. What are some images that could be added to an Amazon listing for [SAMPLE PRODUCT NAME]?

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/E0Himage.png)

12. Compose a product description and 5 bullet points for an Amazon listing that is selling [SAMPLE PRODUCT NAME].

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/NA2image.png)

13. Write a product description and five bullet points for an Amazon listing that sells [SAMPLE PRODUCT NAME]. Please ensure that the following details are included in the bullet points:

- Set of 12 brushes
- Three different sizes: small, medium, and large
- Comes with a plastic case

  Also, please mention the following keywords:
- Designed for beginners
- High absorption rate
- Consistent flow
- No shedding
- No flaking

Format the bullet points in the following manner:

PRODUCT BENEFIT: Detailed description.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/b3Mimage.png)
