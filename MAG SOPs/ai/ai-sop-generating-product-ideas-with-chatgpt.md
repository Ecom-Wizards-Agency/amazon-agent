## **Generating Product Ideas with Chat GPT**

**Who is this for?** For individuals who are looking to create or expand their product lines, and who want to use technology to generate new ideas.

**Objective:** To provide a systematic approach to generating new product ideas through prompts, with the goal of helping individuals or teams come up with innovative and feasible product ideas.

- Log in to your OpenAI account. If you don't have an account, you can sign up for free.

**Ask ChatGPT to write a Product Ideas Prompt:**

1. Provide a list of small and lightweight products that are not fragile, electronic, or food.
