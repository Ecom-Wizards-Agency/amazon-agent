## **Conducting Keyword Research with ChatGPT**

**Who is this for :**

Intended for individuals who are involved in Amazon product marketing, particularly those responsible for optimizing product listings for search engines and improving search rankings.

**Objective :**

To guide the user on how to conduct effective keyword research for Amazon products. The SOP outlines the steps to generate a list of relevant keywords for a given product, how to use these keywords as broad match modifiers, and how to generate a list of relevant search terms using a product link.

- Open a web browser and navigate to OpenAI's GPT-3 Playground: https://beta.openai.com/playground/
- Log in to your OpenAI account. If you don't have an account, you can sign up for free.

**Ask ChatGPT to write a Keyword Research Prompt:**

**1.**Generate a list of common search terms or keywords for the product listed below, excluding any brand names: **[INSERT AMAZON PRODUCT TITLE]**
