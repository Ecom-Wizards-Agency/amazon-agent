# AI SOP: Bonus ChatGPT Prompts

## **Bonus ChatGPT Prompts**

**Who is this for?:**

The bonus prompts are intended for individuals who are responsible for managing and improving the performance of Chat GPT.

**Objective:**

The objective is to create and implement a set of bonus prompts for Chat GPT that cater to the diverse interests and preferences of users, enhancing user engagement and satisfaction during conversations with the AI model.

- Log in to your OpenAI account. If you don't have an account, you can sign up for free.

  Ask ChatGPT to conduct Bonus Prompts:

1. Begin by stating "Hey ChatGPT, I need assistance in creating an Amazon Store."

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-6DHCHV14.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/WTcimage.png)

2. Identify the most popular [PRODUCT NAME] products priced between $30 and $80.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-AHWCZ5HS.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LKEimage.png)

3. Discover 5 creative and innovative ways to reduce shipping costs.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-V35TP9LV.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VSdimage.png)

4. Compose a letter to Amazon Seller Central addressing a seller [HIJACKER NAME] who is selling counterfeit products on my ASIN [PASTE ASIN] listing.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-UQQYV974.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/IH9image.png)

Explore the various applications of Dall-E, such as generating custom images for product pages, brand storefronts, and Amazon Posts.

5. Develop a template for reaching out to influencers to promote my [PRODUCT NAME] product.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-EA3KFCXA.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/b3Aimage.png)

6. Recommend kitchen and cooking YouTube channels with under 30k subscribers, displaying the subscriber count.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-SEC02WBZ.png)
