# Business Analysis: Helium10 Market Tracker

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/business-analysis/page/business-analysis-helium10-market-tracker

## **Helium 10 Market Tracker**

**Who is this for :** All individuals who want to use Helium 10 Market Tracker.

**Objective :** To be able to successfully use the Market Tracker in Helium 10.

Helium10’s Market Tracker tool allows you to track your competitors on a daily basis and compare your sales to your overall market’s sales. Market Tracker lets you create your own market by choosing the competitor ASINs and your own ASINs that make up your market, as well as choosing your top 3-5 keywords to track.

Market Tracker works in U.S., Canadian, German, Spanish, Italian, French, and British marketplaces.

**How to Set Up Market Tracker**

1. Click on the market tracker on Helium10’s Tools > Market Tracker ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Market.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/2ohimage.png)
2. Click Create a New Market
  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1683156551160.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/bUIimage.png)
3. Enter any market name, and select your corresponding marketplace.
4. Add up to 5 of your highly-relevant keywords for your specific market. Keywords with higher search volumes work best here.
5. Start creating your market by adding competitor ASINs, as well as your own ASINs. Start with 5+ competitor ASINs; once Helium10 creates your market, you will get competitor recommendations to add to your market. Your total market sales are the sum of all the ASINs you add to your market, so the more ASINs you add, the more accurate your market is.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/create.JPG)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YVhimage.png)

6. Click on Create Market. Your market will then take up to 1 day to populate and become ready.

7. After your market is populated, change your product filter to Tracked Products.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/helium10-market-tracker-image-054go84p.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Rn1image.png)

8. Find your product(s) and select This Is My Product

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ocMimage.png)

9. Once your products are selected, your market share will be populated in the modules below:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/business-analysis-helium-10-market-tracker.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vV1image.png)

10. Market Tracker does not pull past data; your data will start from the day you set up Market Tracker.

FAQs

1. Why should I use Helium 10 Market Tracker?

Market Tracker helps you monitor competitor activity, track your market share, and analyze trends over time. This allows you to adjust your pricing, keywords, and product strategies to stay competitive.

2. How do I choose the best keywords for Market Tracker?

Select keywords that:

- Have high search volume to track the most competitive terms.
- Are directly relevant to your product category.
- Include a mix of short-tail (broad) and long-tail (specific) keywords.

3. Can I edit or update an existing market after setting it up?

Yes. You can add or remove ASINs, update keywords, and refine your market as needed by navigating to Market Tracker > Select Your Market > Edit Market.

4. How do I analyze the data provided by Market Tracker?

- Market Share % – Shows your product’s percentage of total market sales.
- Total Market Sales – The estimated revenue of all tracked ASINs.
- Competitor Performance – Helps identify which competitors are gaining or losing sales.

5. Does Market Tracker work in all Amazon marketplaces?

No, Market Tracker currently supports the U.S., Canada, Germany, Spain, Italy, France, and the U.K. It does not work in other regions.

6. What should I do if Market Tracker is not displaying data or recommendations?

- Ensure you’ve added at least 5 competitor ASINs to generate market data.
- Wait up to 24 hours for the data to populate.
- Try refreshing the page or re-adding ASINs if no data appears.

7. Is Market Tracker available on all Helium 10 subscription plans?

No, Market Tracker is available only on certain Helium 10 plans. Check your subscription level to confirm access.

8. Can I export Market Tracker data for reports?

Yes. You can download and export data by clicking on the Export CSV button within the Market Tracker dashboard.

9. How accurate is the sales data in Market Tracker?

Market Tracker provides estimated sales data based on Helium 10’s algorithms. It is not an exact reflection of real Amazon sales but offers a useful benchmark.

10. How often should I check my Market Tracker data?

For best results, review your Market Tracker data weekly to monitor competitor movements and adjust your strategies accordingly.
