# Catalog SOP: Creating a Sales and Orders by Month Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/business-analysis/page/catalog-sop-creating-a-sales-and-orders-by-month-report

## **Creating a Sales and Orders by Month Report**

**Who is this for :** This is for sellers on the Seller Central platform who need to download a Sales and Orders by Month Report for their business.

**Objective:** To provide step-by-step instructions on how to generate and download a Sales and Orders by Month Report on Seller Central. This report can provide valuable insights into the sales and order trends of a business over a specific time period.

The Sales and Orders by Month Report is a powerful tool for sellers on Seller Central to track their business performance and analyze sales and order data over time. This SOP will guide you through the process of accessing the report, customizing the fields, adjusting the date range, and downloading the report in CSV format. By following these instructions, you will be able to generate comprehensive reports that can aid in decision-making and help you gain valuable insights into your business's sales and order trends.

**Instructions:**

1. To generate a Sales and Orders by Month Report, go to the "Reports" tab in Seller Central and select "Business Reports" from the dropdown menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-M6V3QS5X.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pVwimage.png)
2. On the next page, choose "Sales and Orders by Month" under the "Other" subheading in the left-hand menu.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685029952183.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/86cimage.png)
3. Choose the date or dates you want to include in your report using the available drop-down menus below the displayed graphs.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685031839651.png)

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VAJimage.png)
4. Click the "SHOW/HIDE COLUMNS" button on the left side of the page to access the available fields and select the ones you want in your report.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685030236698.png)

  ![Creating a Sales and Orders by Month Report.gif](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/creating-a-sales-and-orders-by-month-report.gif)
5. If you prefer to hide the graph, click the "Graph" icon in the top right corner of the chart. To show it again, click the same icon.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685030302519.png)

  ![Creating a Sales and Orders by Month Report 1.gif](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/creating-a-sales-and-orders-by-month-report-1.gif)
6. To customize the date range, drag the scrollbar or click the calendar icons under the graph or above the table.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685030750982.png)

  ![Creating a Sales and Orders by Month Report 3.gif](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/creating-a-sales-and-orders-by-month-report-3.gif)
7. Zoom in on a specific period using the 7D, 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, or 2Y buttons on the left side of the graph.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/cnXimage.png)
8. Once you're satisfied with the report settings, click the "Download" button and select "CSV" from the dropdown menu to download your report.

  ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685031067033.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/pZ9image.png)

FAQs

General Report Generation

- **Q1: What is the "Sales and Orders by Month" report used for?**

  - **A1:** The report provides insights into your sales and order trends over a specified time period. It is useful for tracking business performance, identifying trends, and aiding in decision-making.
- **Q2: Can I generate this report for a custom date range?**

  - **A2:** Yes, you can customize the date range using the calendar icons or scrollbar under the graph or above the table.
- **Q3: Is it possible to hide the graph while viewing the report?**

  - **A3:** Yes, you can hide the graph by clicking the "Graph" icon in the top right corner of the chart. You can click the icon again to show it.

Customizing Report Fields

- **Q4: How can I customize the fields displayed in the report?**

  - **A4:** Click the "SHOW/HIDE COLUMNS" button on the left side of the page to access and select the desired fields for your report.
- **Q5: Are there any restrictions on the fields I can add to the report?**

  - **A5:** No, you can select from all the available fields displayed in the "SHOW/HIDE COLUMNS" menu to suit your reporting needs.

Report Download

- **Q6: In what format can I download the report?**

  - **A6:** The report can be downloaded in CSV format.
- **Q7: Is it possible to preview the report before downloading?**

  - **A7:** Yes, you can adjust the settings, including the date range and fields, to preview the report on the screen before downloading it.

Troubleshooting

- **Q8: Why can't I see the "Sales and Orders by Month" option in the "Business Reports" menu?**

  - **A8:** Ensure you are navigating to the correct subheading, "Other," in the left-hand menu of the "Business Reports" page. If the option is still unavailable, you might not have the necessary permissions or access in Seller Central.
- **Q9: What should I do if the report fails to download?**

  - **A9:** Check your internet connection, ensure pop-ups are not blocked in your browser, and verify that you have selected a valid date range. If the issue persists, contact Seller Central support.

Additional Features

- **Q10: Are there shortcuts for viewing data for specific periods?**

  - **A10:** Yes, you can use the 7D, 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, or 2Y buttons on the left side of the graph to zoom in on predefined periods.
- **Q11: Can I share the downloaded report with my team?**

  - **A11:** Yes, once downloaded as a CSV file, you can share the file via email or upload it to shared drives for team access.
