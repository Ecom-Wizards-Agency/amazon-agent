# Walmart SOP: Generating a Buy Box Report

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-generating-a-buy-box-report

## **Generating a Buy Box Report**

**Who is this for**? This SOP is intended for Amazon specialists responsible for managing product listings and analyzing Buy Box performance to optimize product competitiveness.

**Objective**: The objective of this SOP is to guide users through the process of generating and downloading a Buy Box report. This report provides insights into your products that have competed for the Buy Box, helping you analyze performance and make improvements.

**Procedure**

**Step 1**: **Navigate to Catalog**

- Log in to your [Walmart Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) account.
- From the Seller Center dashboard, go to your **Catalog** section.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/XdKimage.png)

**Step 2**: **Access the Reports Page**

- Click the **Download View** button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rmQimage.png)

- A pop-up window will appear. Click **Go to the Reports Page** to continue.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-generating-a-buy-box-report-image-8hkxoomu.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rtgimage.png)

**Step 3**: **Locate the Buy Box Report**

- On the **Reports Page**, go to the **On Request Reports** section.
- From the dropdown menu, select **Buy Box Report**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rvgimage.png)

**Step 4**: **Request the Report**

- After selecting the **Buy Box Report**, click **Request Report**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-generating-a-buy-box-report-image-6noo29wf.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GSUimage.png)

**Step 5**: **Monitor Report Progress**

- To track the status of the report, go to the **Report Activity** section.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vnfimage.png)

**Step 6**: **Download the Report**

- Once the report is ready, it will appear in the **Download Section**.
- Click **Download** to access the Buy Box report file.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/wYMimage.png)

FAQs

**1. What is a Buy Box report?**

The Buy Box report provides an overview of your products that have competed for the Buy Box, including details such as SKUs, whether you won the Buy Box, item price, and shipping price.

**2. Who should use this guide?**

This guide is intended for Amazon specialists responsible for managing product listings and analyzing Buy Box performance to optimize product competitiveness.

**3. How do I generate a Buy Box report?**

To generate the Buy Box report, follow these steps:

- Navigate to your **Catalog** in Seller Center.
- Click the **Download View** button and select **Go to the Reports Page**.
- On the **Reports Page**, select the **Buy Box Report** from the dropdown menu in the **On Request Reports** section.
- Click **Request Report** to generate the report.

**4. Where can I track the status of the report?**

You can track the progress of your report under the **Report Activity** section. Once the report is ready, you can download it from the **Download Section**.

**5. How do I download the Buy Box report?**

Once the Buy Box report is generated, go to the **Download Section** in the **Report Activity** area. Click **Download** to access the report file.

**6. What information is included in the Buy Box report?**

The report includes key details such as:

- SKUs of products that competed in the Buy Box
- Item ID
- Product Name
- Product Category
- Whether you won the Buy Box for each product
- Seller Item price
- Seller Shipping price
- Buybox Item Price
- Buybox Ship Price
