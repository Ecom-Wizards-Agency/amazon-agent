# Walmart SOP: Reactivating Items in Seller Center

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-reactivating-items-in-seller-center

## **Reactivating Items in Seller Center**

**Who is this for?**This procedure is intended for Amazon Specialists responsible for managing product listings and ensuring items are accurately reflected on the platform.

**Objective:**This SOP outlines the steps for reactivating retired or archived items in Seller Center, either individually or in bulk. This guide ensures that items are successfully relisted on Walmart.com and accurately reflect their updated lifecycle status.

**I. Reactivate Items Individually**

**Step 1: Get Started**

- Log in to [Seller Center.](https://login.account.wal-mart.com/authorize?responseType=code&state=U5NQMHKH6Y&scope=openId&nonce=HTYRJ2FNRT&clientType=seller&redirectUri=https://seller.walmart.com/aurora/v1/auroraScCoreService/rest/login/sso/hook&clientId=66620dfd-1f3f-479b-8b9c-e11f36c5438b)
- Navigate to your **Catalog** in Seller Center.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9C4image.png)

- Select **Filters**, then under **Lifecycle**, choose **Retired** and **Archived**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vyWimage.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/SlZimage.png)

- Use the SKU, Item ID, Item Name, GTIN, or UPC to search for the item you want to reactivate.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-reactivating-items-in-seller-center-image-jfm16vuq.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Jkfimage.png)

**Step 2: Reactivate the Item**

- Once the item is located, click the three horizontal dots to the right of the item.
- Select **Edit item** from the dropdown.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Umtimage.png)

- On the **Item Edit** screen, scroll down to the **Site Experience** field.
- Update the **Site End Date** to a future date.
- Click **Submit** in the bottom right corner to save your changes.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Uvvimage.png)

**II. Reactivate Items in Bulk**

**Step 1: Get Started**

- Navigate to your **Catalog** in Seller Center.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-reactivating-items-in-seller-center-image-etsvti7r.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Vhfimage.png)

**Step 2: Download & Complete the Template**

- At the top right of the screen, select **Update items** from the dropdown menu and choose either **Update with file** or **Update with GTINs**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7Fwimage.png)

- Download the provided template.

  - If using the **Update all attributes** template, make sure to complete the product category, product type, and fulfillment method fields.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zJLimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-reactivating-items-in-seller-center-image-3krldr4w.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/9Xnimage.png)

- Find the **Site End Date** column and update the date to a future date to reactivate each item.

  ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iHuimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-reactivating-items-in-seller-center-image-zgkxluyx.png)

**Step 3: Upload the Template**

- Navigate back to your **Catalog** in Seller Center.
- Select **Update items** and choose **Update with file** to upload the template.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/odCimage.png)

- Select **Update all attributes** from the dropdown menu to upload and apply your changes.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/NBoimage.png)

FAQs

**Q: How can I verify that my item has been reactivated?**

- After the submission is processed, the **Lifecycle** status of the item will change from **Retired** or **Archived** to **Active**.

**Q: What happens if I forget to update the Site End Date?**

- If the **Site End Date** is not updated to a future date, the item will not be reactivated. It will remain in its **Retired** or **Archived** state. Ensure that you update the Site End Date to a future date to reactivate the listing.

**Q: Can I reactivate multiple items at once using the bulk method?**

- Yes, you can reactivate multiple items at once by updating the **Site End Date** for each product in the bulk template. Once the template is completed and uploaded, all items with updated dates will be reactivated simultaneously.

**Q: How long does it take for changes to be reflected in Seller Center?**

- The reactivation process may take some time to reflect in Seller Center. Typically, the changes should appear within 24 to 48 hours after submission, depending on the volume of changes and processing times.

**Q: Can I reactivate items that are still in an active state?**

- No, you can only reactivate items that are marked as **Retired** or **Archived**. Items that are already active cannot be reactivated using these steps.

**Q: What if I encounter an error when uploading the bulk file?**

- If an error occurs, review the template for any missing or incorrect information (such as missing product category, fulfillment method, or Site End Date). Ensure that the file format is correct and that all required fields are completed. You may need to fix the issue and re-upload the file.

**Q: Can I bulk-reactivate items using the Update with GTIN method?**

- Yes, you can use the **Update with GTINs** option to reactivate items in bulk by including their GTINs in the template and updating the **Site End Date** to a future date for each item.
