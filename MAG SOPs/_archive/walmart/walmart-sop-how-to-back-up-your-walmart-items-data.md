# Walmart SOP: How to Back Up Your Walmart Items Data

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-how-to-back-up-your-walmart-items-data

## **How to Back Up Your Walmart Items Data**

Who is this for?: This backing up Walmart items data can be performed by a Walmart specialist or teams within the organization.

Objective: The objective of backing up your Walmart items data is to ensure the preservation and protection of critical product information, inventory details, and other essential information related to your Walmart items.

**Instructions:**

1. Log in to Walmart seller central account. Go to https://seller.walmart.com/ ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1694699684131.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LV0image.png)

2. Determine the GTIN or UPC of the item that needs to be backed up. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1690915733339.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/eb3image.png)

3. Once GTIN is confirmed, choose Update items under Manage Items. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1694699710413.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/sEwimage.png)

4. Choose the fulfillment type, select By GTIN Match, input the item GTINs in the box, and click the Match button.
![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1690915781878.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7xaimage.png)

5. Once you select Match, the system will verify the product IDs provided and if there are issues with the entries, you will see an error. Possible issues that will result in an error:

- Submitting items that are in more than 7 categories at once.
- Entering invalid GTINs*.
- Entering more than the 5,000 GTIN limit.

6. It may take time to process your request, depending on the number of items and categories you submitted, click refresh if needed. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1694699734402.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ZBjimage.png)

7. Once your submitted GTINs are processed, you can now download the GTIN Match file. ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1694699748867.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/EMrimage.png)

8. All item attributes are available in the downloaded GTIN Matchfile and are ready to back up.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1694699786622.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ayuimage.png)

FAQs

1. What data is included in the GTIN Match file backup?

- The GTIN Match file backup includes product attributes, such as the product title, description, price, inventory details, and other essential product information. However, it does not include images or other media. It’s important to manually back up images if needed.

2. Why might my GTINs fail to match during the backup process?

- GTINs might fail to match due to several reasons:

  - Invalid GTINs: Ensure the GTIN entered is valid and properly formatted.
  - Exceeding Category Limits: You can only submit items from 7 categories at a time. If you exceed this, the system will throw an error.
  - Exceeding GTIN Limit: Walmart allows up to 5,000 GTINs per submission. If you have more than 5,000 GTINs, you will need to split them into smaller batches.

3. How do I fix errors when submitting GTINs?

- Invalid GTINs: Double-check the GTIN format (UPC, EAN, ISBN) and confirm they’re correct.
- Exceeding Category Limits: Divide your items into smaller groups, each containing products from no more than 7 categories.
- Exceeding GTIN Limit: Split the submission into multiple smaller batches, each containing no more than 5,000 GTINs.

4. How do I verify the integrity of the GTIN Match file after downloading it?

- After downloading the GTIN Match file, open the file and review the contents to ensure all product details are present. Check for missing or incorrect entries by cross-referencing the file with the actual product listings in Walmart Seller Central.

5. How often should I back up my Walmart items data?

- It’s recommended to back up your Walmart items data on a monthly basis or whenever there are significant changes to your product listings (such as adding new products or updating existing ones). Regular backups ensure that you have the most up-to-date product information in case you need to restore or reference it.

6. Where should I store the backup GTIN Match file for safety?

- Store the backup in a secure location such as cloud storage (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox, or AWS) for easy access and retrieval. You may also choose to save it on an external hard drive for additional protection.

7. Can I back up other product data such as images or reviews?

- The GTIN Match file only includes product attributes. To back up images, descriptions, or reviews, you will need to manually download or export them separately. Use Walmart’s Media Management or Reporting Tools to gather such data.

8. How do I download the GTIN Match file once it’s processed?

- After your GTINs are processed, go to the Download tab in the WFS Inventory section. Click on the link to download the GTIN Match file, which will be available as an Excel file.

9. How do I handle errors during the GTIN match download process?

- If you encounter an error during the download process, refresh the page and try again. If the issue persists, check the GTIN format or the number of GTINs being processed. For persistent errors, contact Walmart support.
