## **How to Manage Duplicate Listings in Seller Center**

**Who is this for?**This SOP is for all Seller Center users who manage listings and ensure product information is accurate. It outlines how to identify, review, and resolve duplicate listings.

**Objective:**This SOP provides a guide to managing duplicate listings in Seller Center, helping you address issues caused by incorrect product identifiers and maintain your account’s good standing.

**Procedure**

**Step 1: Get Started**

1. Log in to [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home).
2. Navigate to the [**Pending review**](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review?filters=%257B%2522filters%2522%253A%257B%2522filters%2522%253A%255B%257B%2522field%2522%253A%2522status%2522%252C%2522op%2522%253A%2522equals%2522%252C%2522values%2522%253A%255B%2522ACTION_NEEDED%2522%252C%2522PROHIBITED%2522%252C%2522IN_REVIEW%2522%255D%257D%255D%257D%252C%2522tab%2522%253A%2522wfsCompliance%2522%252C%2522filterTab%2522%253A%2522All%2522%257D) page.
3. Select the **Potential duplicates** tab.

**Pro Tip:**To receive notifications about duplicate items, follow these steps:

- Navigate to **Notifications**.
- Select **Settings**.
- Scroll to the **Items** section and check the box next to **Duplicate items identified**.

**Step 2: Review Potential Duplicates**

1. On the [**Pending review** page](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review?filters=%257B%2522filters%2522%253A%257B%2522filters%2522%253A%255B%257B%2522field%2522%253A%2522status%2522%252C%2522op%2522%253A%2522equals%2522%252C%2522values%2522%253A%255B%2522ACTION_NEEDED%2522%252C%2522PROHIBITED%2522%252C%2522IN_REVIEW%2522%255D%257D%255D%257D%252C%2522tab%2522%253A%2522wfsCompliance%2522%252C%2522filterTab%2522%253A%2522All%2522%257D), locate the item identified as a duplicate.
2. Click **Review duplicate** next to the item. This will redirect you to the **Review duplicates** page.
3. On the **Review duplicates** page, you will see the duplicated items alongside the original item.

**Step 3: Submit Decision**

1. Carefully review the duplicated items listed.
2. Choose one of the following options:

  - **Mark as unique** if the items are different.
  - **Confirm duplicate** if the items are the same.
3. Once satisfied with your decision, click **Submit**.
4. After submission, the status will be updated on the [**Pending review** page](https://seller.walmart.com/catalog/pending-review?filters=%257B%2522filters%2522%253A%257B%2522filters%2522%253A%255B%257B%2522field%2522%253A%2522status%2522%252C%2522op%2522%253A%2522equals%2522%252C%2522values%2522%253A%255B%2522ACTION_NEEDED%2522%252C%2522PROHIBITED%2522%252C%2522IN_REVIEW%2522%255D%257D%255D%257D%252C%2522tab%2522%253A%2522wfsCompliance%2522%252C%2522filterTab%2522%253A%2522All%2522%257D).

**Notes**

- Use the **All** filter to view items that still require action, are under review, being processed, or have errors.

FAQs

**1. Why should I merge duplicates?**

Creating duplicate listings violates Walmart’s Marketplace policy. Failure to resolve duplicates may result in the removal of listings, account suspension, or account termination. To learn more about [Walmart’s policy on duplicate listings](https://marketplacelearn.walmart.com/guides/Policies%20&%20standards/Product%20listings/duplicate-listings-policy), visit the relevant policy page.

**2. I didn’t receive the email notification. What should I do?**

You will receive a reminder notification 10 days before your item is unpublished. Ensure that you take action within 10 days to avoid further account-related actions.

**3. I didn’t receive any notifications. What should I do?**

To receive notifications for potential duplicate items, ensure that you have opted in to receive them. You can adjust this setting under **Notifications** in Seller Center. If you are still not receiving notifications, contact Support via the **Help** button in the Seller Center menu bar.

**4. I believe my item was merged in error. What should I do?**

If you believe an item was merged incorrectly or wish to appeal the decision, contact Support through the **Help** button in Seller Center.

**5. How can I send notifications to a new user or a user without a Seller Center account?**

As an admin, you can add a new user in Seller Center, and they will need to subscribe to relevant notifications. To send notifications to users without a Seller Center account, follow the steps outlined in[**Managing notifications in Seller Center**](https://myamazonguy.document360.io/docs/walmart-sop-how-to-manage-notifications-in-seller-center).
