# Walmart SOP: Protecting Your Brand from Unauthorized Sellers on Walmart Marketplace

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-protecting-your-brand-from-unauthorized-sellers-on-walmart-marketplace

## **Protecting Your Brand from Unauthorized Sellers on Walmart Marketplace**

**Who is this for:**This procedure is designed for Walmart Marketplace sellers, brand owners, and authorized resellers who want to secure their accounts and ensure that only approved sellers are listing their products.

**Objective:**This SOP outlines the necessary steps for securing your Seller Center account and preventing unauthorized sellers from listing your products on Walmart Marketplace. It covers the process of enabling two-factor authentication (2-step verification) for enhanced security and utilizing the Walmart Brand Portal to protect your intellectual property and control brand representation.

**Introduction**

Unauthorized sellers can compromise your brand’s reputation by listing counterfeit or infringing items. To prevent such risks, it’s essential to implement multi-factor authentication (2-step verification) for your account security and manage brand ownership through the Walmart Brand Portal. These measures will help you monitor and control who sells your products.

**Enabling 2-Step Verification for Enhanced Security**

Two-step verification (2SV) adds an additional layer of security to your Walmart Seller Center account, reducing the chances of unauthorized access.

**Step 1: Activate 2-Step Verification**

- Log in to **Seller Center** and go to the **Settings** tab.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/vQTimage.png)

- Under**Administrator Options**, click on **2-Step Verification** and toggle it on.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Dg8image.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/AJLimage.png)

- Confirm your decision to enable 2-step verification. Only users with admin-level access can activate this feature.

**Step 2: Choose Your Authentication Method**

After enabling 2SV, each user on the account must select their preferred authentication method. The available options include:

- **Authenticator App:** Use an app (e.g., Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator) to generate a temporary code that changes every 30 seconds.
- **Email Verification:** Enter a one-time code sent to the email associated with your account.
- **Text Message Verification:** Receive a code via SMS (Note: this option is available only for U.S. phone numbers).

**Utilizing the Walmart Brand Portal to Protect Your Brand**

The [**Walmart Brand Portal**](https://brandportal.walmart.com/register) provides tools to manage intellectual property and control who is authorized to sell your products. By registering your brand, you can monitor your sellers and report any infringement or unauthorized listings.

**Step 1: Register Your Brand**

To use the Brand Portal, your brand must have a registered trademark with the **United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO)**. Once registered, you can assign roles to manage your brand’s representation.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/cr7image.png)

**Step 2: Assign Roles for Brand Management**

- **Acting Brand Owner:** You can designate a single acting brand owner who will have the authority to manage product listings and content for your brand.
- **Authorized Resellers:** Multiple resellers can be granted permissions to sell your brand’s products, but they won’t have the ability to edit product content if an acting brand owner is assigned.

**Step 3: Track and Report Unauthorized Sellers**

> **Before Submitting a case, Make sure :**
>
> You have only selected the seller who recently joined the listing.
>
> You have **NOT chosen** the “Remove all sellers” option.
>
> The request is not being submitted on a Friday or the day before a holiday after 12 PM EST

- Regularly monitor product listings for any unauthorized sellers.
- In the **Brand Portfolio** section of the Brand Portal, you can view and manage all active sellers of your products.
- If you identify an unauthorized seller, you can report them directly via the Brand Portal or by contacting the seller using the details available on the product listing page.

Strategies to Prevent Unauthorized Sellers

#### **Strategies to Prevent Unauthorized Sellers**

Although Walmart allows multiple sellers to list the same product, there are proactive steps you can take to limit unauthorized listings and control who sells your products.

**Step 1: Regularly Review Your Listings**

- Continuously monitor your product listings to ensure they are only listed by authorized sellers.
- Use the **Brand Portfolio** to stay updated on all sellers selling your products on Walmart Marketplace.

**Step 2: Ensure Account Security**

- Enable **2-step verification** for all users within your Seller Center account to prevent unauthorized access.
- Regularly review user roles and permissions to ensure only trusted individuals have access to your account.

**Step 3: Contact Unauthorized Sellers**

- If you find an unauthorized seller, contact them directly through the contact details listed on the product page.
- Be polite but firm in requesting the removal of your product listings if they are not authorized to sell your brand’s items.

FAQs

- **How can I report an unauthorized seller?**

  You can report unauthorized sellers via the [**Walmart Brand Portal**](https://brandportal.walmart.com/register) or directly reach out to them using the contact details available on the listing.
- **Can Walmart intervene in unauthorized reseller issues?**

  Walmart does not directly manage conflicts between brand owners and unauthorized resellers. You will need to resolve these issues independently, either through direct communication or legal channels if necessary.
- **What if an unauthorized seller continues to list my products?**

  Continue to communicate with the unauthorized seller. If they persist in listing your products, escalate the matter through legal means if necessary. Walmart also allows reporting violations through the [**Brand Portal**.](https://brandportal.walmart.com/register)
- **Can I block all other sellers from listing my brand’s products?**

  Walmart operates on a unified catalog system, meaning multiple sellers can list the same product. While you can’t block other sellers entirely, you can monitor and control who has the right to sell your products by managing your resellers through the [**Brand Portal**.](https://brandportal.walmart.com/register)
