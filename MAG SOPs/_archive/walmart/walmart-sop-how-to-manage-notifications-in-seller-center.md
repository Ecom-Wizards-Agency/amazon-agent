## **How to Manage Notifications in Seller Center**

**Who is this for?**This SOP is for all Seller Center users, especially admins and those managing notifications for important updates like alerts and catalog changes.

**Objective:**This SOP provides a simple guide to managing notification settings in Seller Center, helping you stay informed about critical updates and take timely action.

**Procedure**

**Step 1: Get Started**

1. Log in to [Seller Center.](https://seller.walmart.com/home)
2. Navigate to the **Notifications Settings** page.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/K3Qimage.png)

**Step 2: Choose Your Preferred Notifications**

1. On the [**Notifications Settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/account/notification-settings) page, review the list of available notifications.
2. Select the checkboxes next to the types of notifications you want to enable. These can include alerts about catalog changes, order updates, and other critical business notifications.

**Step 3: Choose Delivery and Frequency**

1. Decide how you want to receive notifications:

  - **In Seller Center**: Notifications will appear in your Seller Center account.
  - **Email**: Notifications will be sent to your registered email address.
  - **Both**: Receive notifications in both Seller Center and via email.
2. **Note**: If any option is greyed out, it means it cannot be modified at this time.
3. After selecting your preferences, click **Save** in the bottom right corner of the page to confirm your changes.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-how-to-manage-notifications-in-seller-center-image-vz5x3b08.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rawimage.png)

**Step 4: Add Additional Recipients (Optional)**

1. If you are an **admin user**, you can add additional recipients to receive notifications.
2. If the recipient doesn’t have a Seller Center account, follow the appropriate process to ensure they are subscribed to relevant notifications.

FAQs

**1. How do I address a notification?**

To address a notification, simply select it from the list in Seller Center. You will be redirected to the appropriate dashboard where you can take action or gather more information.

**2. Are notifications automatically enabled?**

No, notifications are not enabled by default. You must modify your [**Notifications Settings**](https://seller.walmart.com/settings/account/notification-settings) to select the notifications you wish to receive.

**3. Are the notification types listed in Seller Center exhaustive?**

No, the types of notifications listed may not cover all alerts. You may receive additional notifications based on the Walmart tools or programs you have enabled.

**4. Can I set up notifications for newly generated orders?**

Yes, you can enable notifications for newly generated orders as part of your notification settings.
