# Walmart SOP: Requesting Review Removal on Walmart Marketplace

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-requesting-review-removal-on-walmart-marketplace

## **Requesting Review Removal on Walmart Marketplace**

**Who is this for?**This SOP is intended for Walmart Marketplace sellers who need to request the removal of an irrelevant or inappropriate review from their product or seller listings.

**Objective:**This SOP outlines the steps for submitting a request to remove a product or seller review on Walmart Marketplace that meets specific removal criteria. The process ensures consistent handling of review removal requests in compliance with Walmart's guidelines.

**Overview**

Walmart allows sellers to request the removal of certain reviews on their Marketplace listings if those reviews meet specific removal criteria. This SOP provides guidance on how to identify eligible reviews and submit a request for removal.

**Eligibility Criteria for Review Removal**

A review may be eligible for removal if it meets one of the following conditions:

- **Incorrect Star Rating:** The star rating does not match the sentiment expressed in the review. For example, if a review states the seller is excellent but the rating is two stars, this may qualify for removal.
- **Inappropriate Feedback:** The review contains inappropriate or offensive language such as profanity, slander, or other vulgar comments that violate Walmart’s policies.
- **Walmart System Issue:** The review is a result of a Walmart system issue and is not the fault of the seller. This type of request requires supporting evidence to substantiate the claim.

**Review Removal Process**

**Step 1: Identify the Review for Removal**

- Review the feedback to confirm it falls under one of the eligible criteria.
- Ensure the review is not a "Ratings Only" review, as these cannot be removed without a comment.

**Step 2: Gather Supporting Evidence (If Necessary)**

- If requesting removal due to a Walmart system issue, gather any evidence that supports your claim (e.g., screenshots or documentation of the system error).

**Step 3: Submit a Removal Request**

- Log in [to **Seller Center**.](https://seller.walmart.com/home)
- Click the **Help** button in the Seller Center menu.
- Select **Contact Support** and submit a request to remove the review, specifying the reason it meets the eligibility criteria for removal.
- Attach any supporting evidence as needed.

**Step 4: Wait for Response**

- You can expect a response from Seller Support within **five to seven business days**. If further clarification is needed, you may be contacted by Seller Support.

**Important Notes**

- **Time Limitation:** Walmart cannot remove reviews older than one year, unless the content contains offensive material (e.g., profanity or slander).
- **Negative Reviews:** Walmart will not remove a review simply because it is negative. Removal requests must meet one of the specified criteria.
- **Syndicated Reviews:** Walmart can only remove reviews originally posted on [Walmart.com](http://walmart.com). Reviews from third-party sites or syndicated reviews cannot be removed.
- **Request Denials:** If your request is denied, the review likely does not meet the removal criteria. You will receive an explanation from Seller Support. For more details, refer to the communication from Seller Support.

**FAQs**

- **How long will it take to get a response to my removal request?**

  You should expect a response within **five to seven business days**. If needed, contact **Support** via Seller Center for further assistance.
- **Can I request the removal of old reviews?**

  Walmart can only remove reviews older than one year if they contain offensive content such as profanity or slander.
- **What if a review is damaging to my business but doesn't meet the removal criteria?**

  Walmart will not remove a review simply because it negatively impacts your business. It must meet one of the criteria specified in this SOP.
- **Can reviews added through syndication be removed?**

  No, Walmart can only remove reviews posted natively on Walmart.com. Reviews obtained through third-party syndication cannot be removed.
- **What should I do if my review removal request is denied?**

  Review denials typically occur when the review does not meet the specified criteria. Consult the correspondence from Seller Support for a detailed explanation.
