# Walmart SOP: Managing Item Enrollment in Walmart-fulfilled Incentives Program

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-managing-item-enrollment-in-walmart-fulfilled-incentives-program

## Managing Item Enrollment in Walmart-fulfilled Incentives Program

**Who is this for?**

This SOP is intended for Amazon specialists who need to manage product enrollment in Walmart-funded incentives program. It outlines both individual and bulk item management via Seller Center.

**Objective:**

This SOP is for managing the enrollment and unenrollment of items in Walmart-funded incentives program. It helps Walmart sellers selectively choose which products they want to include in the program to stay competitive with pricing, while also ensuring they maintain profitability.

**Procedure**

**Step 1: Access the Incentives Page**

1. **Log in** to Seller Center with your credentials.
2. Navigate to the **Pricing** section.
3. Click on the **Incentives** page to open the incentives management tool.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-managing-item-enrollment-in-walmart-fulfilled-incentives-program-image-z8m7drep.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6xWimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/6xWimage.png)

**Step 2: Choose Your Items to Enroll or Unenroll**

You can manage enrollment individually or in bulk, depending on your preferences.

**A. Enroll or Unenroll Individual Items**

1. Under the **Eligible**tab, review the list of eligible items that may be enrolled in Walmart-funded incentives program.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-managing-item-enrollment-in-walmart-fulfilled-incentives-program-image-ewlmjo26.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/xi4image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/xi4image.png)

1. Select the products you want to **enroll**.
2. Click the **Enroll** button under the **Action** column or when selecting multiple products, click the **Enroll**button on top.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-managing-item-enrollment-in-walmart-fulfilled-incentives-program-image-kbqvmn4r.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/4drimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/4drimage.png)

1. Confirm the **enrollment**. These products will appear in the **Active**tab.
2. If you wish to **un-enroll a**ny items, just go to the Active tab, select the product you want to unenroll, and click unenroll under the **Action**column..

**B. Enroll or Unenroll in Bulk**

1. On the **Incentives** page, click the **Manage incentives** button in the top right corner.
2. Select **Manage Walmart-funded incentives** from the dropdown menu.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-managing-item-enrollment-in-walmart-fulfilled-incentives-program-image-dv99b9ky.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/LVXimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/LVXimage.png)

1. Click **Generate file** to download the most recent list of items. (This may take up to 15 minutes.)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yV0image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/yV0image.png)

1. Click the **cloud symbol** to download the bulk template.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-managing-item-enrollment-in-walmart-fulfilled-incentives-program-image-0c4pzqnu.png)

1. Open the template and navigate to the **Walmart-funded Incentives Enrollment** column. Set the status to **Opt-in** or **Opt-out** for each item.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-managing-item-enrollment-in-walmart-fulfilled-incentives-program-image-huzdirbu.png)

1. Click **Submit** to finalize the enrollment or unenrollment.

To track Walmart-funded incentives applied to your orders, use the [**Payments Report**](https://seller.walmart.com/payments/statements/period). Filter by the **Total Walmart Funded Savings Program** column for detailed information on the incentive amount.

FAQs

**Do Walmart-funded incentives expire?**

- Yes, Walmart-funded incentives may expire at Walmart’s discretion. If they do, the item price will revert to the last submitted price.

**What happens if items are unenrolled from Walmart-funded incentives?**

- Once an item is unenrolled, it will no longer participate in the program for future sales and will disappear from the **Eligible** tab until you re-enroll it.

**Will I receive incentives for all items I opt-in?**

- No, Walmart-funded incentives are not guaranteed for every enrolled item. Walmart will decide whether to apply the incentives.

**How does the Referral Fee adjustment affect my payment?**

- Your payment remains unchanged even though the customer price is reduced due to Walmart-funded incentives. You will receive the same net amount as if the item were sold at its original price.

**What happens if I apply a promotion to an item?**

- The price used for Walmart-funded incentives will reflect the promotional price.

**How can I tell if a customer order includes an item with Walmart-funded incentives?**

- Orders will display a **Price Incentive** pop-up, which indicates the incentive program applied.

**How do I ensure MAPs (Minimum Advertised Prices) aren’t violated?**

- You can exclude MAP-restricted items from Walmart-funded incentives by using the bulk template. Add more SKUs to the exclusion list if necessary.

**How do I turn off Walmart-funded incentives?**

- To turn off Walmart-funded incentives for all items, refer to the **Walmart-funded Incentives: Program Enrollment SOP**.

**How do I access my Payments Report?**

- To learn how to access your **Payments Report**, visit the **Payment Statements and Transactions** page in Seller Center.
