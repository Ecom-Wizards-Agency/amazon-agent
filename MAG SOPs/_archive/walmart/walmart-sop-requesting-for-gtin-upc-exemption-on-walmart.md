# Walmart SOP: Requesting for GTIN/UPC Exemption on Walmart

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-requesting-for-gtinupc-exemption-on-walmart

## **Requesting for GTIN/UPC Exemption on Walmart**

**Who is this for?:** This SOP is intended for sellers on Walmart's online marketplace who need to request a GTIN/UPC exemption for their products. It provides step-by-step instructions on how to submit a case to Walmart support in order to list items that do not have a standard GTIN or UPC assigned to them.

**Objective:** The objective of this SOP is to guide sellers in the process of requesting a GTIN/UPC exemption on Walmart. By following these instructions, sellers will be able to submit a case to Walmart support, provide relevant information, and potentially obtain approval to list their products without a GTIN/UPC.

Requesting a GTIN/UPC exemption on Walmart serves a specific purpose for sellers. In some cases, certain products, particularly those that are unique or custom-made, may not have a GTIN/UPC assigned by the manufacturer. This SOP aims to assist sellers in navigating the process of requesting an exemption, enabling them to list their products on Walmart's marketplace without a standard GTIN/UPC.

**Instructions:**

1. Log in to your Walmart seller central account by visiting [https://seller.walmart.com/](https://seller.walmart.com/).

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/A9Dimage.png)

2. Click on "**Help**" and select "**Contact Support**".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-XAGTMAM0.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/GYNimage.png)

3. Click on “**Issue Categories**”.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-OQQF0M3B.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/WoAimage.png)

4. Choose "**Items and Inventory**" from the “**Select a category**”.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-YVVQKZE9.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/dN9image.png)

5. Select "**Request exemption**".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-5WJ5EPR9.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Rcsimage.png)

6.Then choose "**UPC exemptions**".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-CQU0LV83.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/yOfimage.png)

7. If you still need help, proceed with case creation, select "**Get in touch with us**".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-KJNHMJ5C.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6Mrimage.png)

8. Choose your preferred method of communication with Walmart support. We usually select the option to open the case via Email since call and live chat have specific time restrictions.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-FK4NKVWY.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fKWimage.png)

9. Fill out and answer the following questions to submit the case:

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-F0KKTT9Y.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YbAimage.png)

I. Subject line: Provide a clear and concise subject line.

II. Which categories are you requesting UPC exemptions for?

- Ensure to determine the most appropriate category of the product by following [Steps 1-4 from the Walmart SOP: Create Bulk Items in Walmart SOP](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-create-multiple-items-in-walmart-via-flat-file-seller-fulfilled-type).

III. Are you in direct contact with a Walmart Category Manager or Specialist? If so, please share their name and email address.

IV. Are you a brand/manufacturer, retailer/reseller or both of the items without UPCs

V. Please provide the reason for this exemption request, along with an explanation of why the items don't have GS1-approved UPCs/GTINs.

VI. Which brand(s) will require UPC exemptions?

VII. Do you expect to have UPCs/GTINs for these items in the future?

VIII. How many items in your catalog are in the categories requested, and what fraction of them will need UPC exemptions?

IX. If granted, what is the expected annual sales on Walmart that will be generated by the items with the UPC exemptions?

X. Please send links for your website and 5-10 items (per subcategory) that you are proposing to list without UPCs

XI. Please provide description of the issue.

10. Once you have filled out all the required information, click on "**Submit**".

11. To monitor or follow up on your case, click on "**Help**" and select "**Your cases**".

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-EH7R48IW.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/L56image.png)

12. Find the case ID in the history and click on the ID link to read Walmart support's reply.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-3984MVAA.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/p0gimage.png)

13. If the case is not yet resolved, respond to the case accordingly and provide any additional information or clarification as requested by Walmart support. If the case is resolved and Walmart support has approved the request, you need to set up the item using the normal bulk item setup templates. In the template, you need to select GTIN for the Product ID Type and enter CUSTOM for the Product Identifier.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1688040351632.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/afFimage.png)
