# Walmart SOP: Convert Seller-Fulfilled Items to Walmart-Fulfilled Manually

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-convert-seller-fulfilled-items-to-walmart-fulfilled-manually

## **Convert Seller-Fulfilled Items to Walmart-Fulfilled Manually**

**Who is this for?:**To convert seller-fulfilled items to Walmart-fulfilled items on the Walmart Marketplace, Walmart specialists or the seller themselves are responsible for making this transition.

**Objective:**Converting seller-fulfilled items to Walmart-fulfilled items is a process that involves using Walmart's fulfillment service known as Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS). The main objective is to help sellers store their products in Walmart's fulfillment centers and have Walmart handle the packaging, shipping, and customer service for those items. This allows sellers to offer customers fast and reliable shipping options and leverage Walmart's brand recognition and customer trust.

**Instructions:**

1. Log in to Walmart seller central account. Go to [https://seller.walmart.com/](https://seller.walmart.com/)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1YJDGJK5.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Zf9image.png)

2. Navigate to **Catalog** in the main menu and search for the item to convert using **SKU**, **Item ID**, **Item Name**, **GTIN**, or **UPC**. You can also filter by **Fulfillment** to display all seller-fulfilled items.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-GGK3O7QD.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3iNimage.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-Q041O6UD.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/H42image.png)

3. Click the three dots under the **Actions**tab, then select **Fulfill with WFS** from the options.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-ICFGQ3BQ.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/DFPimage.png)

4. Fill in the required information under the following sections for your Walmart-fulfilled item: **Required to List, Required for Walmart's Supply Chain** (only complete relevant Trade Items), **Required for Walmart Fulfillment Services,**and **Conditional/Required for Hazardous Materials** (only complete relevant Hazmat attributes). Ensure all attributes marked with a red asterisk (*) are completed accurately.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-YZUPOOSQ.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/xYNimage.png)

5. Once all required attributes are filled out, click the **Submit**button.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Envimage.png)

6. Click the **Activity Feed** under **Catalog**to monitor the upload and see if the new listing is successfully processed or if there is an error. If there is an error, go to the**Error Report**tab and click **Download**to review and fix the error.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-N6BDBRKO.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rkXimage.png)

7. If your item contains a battery, chemical, or is classified as hazardous material (hazmat), it will be listed as **Pending Review**. Walmart's review process typically takes up to 3 business days. If the item is tagged as prohibited, you may need to submit a **Safety Data Sheet**or contact **Partner Support**to provide any required documents. For more details about Walmart's Trust and Safety Policies, visit[Walmart Seller Help](https://sellerhelp.walmart.com/s/?language=en_US).

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-I27S2QFV.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/63yimage.png)

8. If there are no issues with the item, a **WFS Eligible** tag will appear on your item.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-XWC1QHCD.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/370image.png)
