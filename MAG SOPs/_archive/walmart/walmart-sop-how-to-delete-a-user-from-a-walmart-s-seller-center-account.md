# Walmart SOP: How to Delete a User from a Walmart’s Seller Center Account

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/walmart/page/walmart-sop-how-to-delete-a-user-from-a-walmarts-seller-center-account

## **How to Delete a User from a Walmart’s Seller Center Account**

**Who is this for?** This guide is for Seller Center account administrators who need to manage and delete user access to their Seller Center account.

**Objective**: This SOP outlines the steps to delete a user from your Seller Center account. Only users with **admin-level access** can delete users. If you do not have admin access, please reach out to your account administrator for assistance. This process is final, and once a user is deleted, they will lose access to your account and all associated data.

**Procedure:**

**Step 1 – Get Started**

- Log in to your [Seller Center](https://seller.walmart.com/home) account.
- Navigate to the **Settings** tab in the top right corner of the page.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ErLimage.png)

- Under the **Administrator options**, select **People and Permissions**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-how-to-delete-a-user-from-a-walmarts-seller-center-account-image-9whd2rjw.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/rzRimage.png)

**Step 2 – Manage Users**

- In the **People and Permissions** section, you’ll see a full list of users who currently have access to your Seller Center account.
- Locate the user you want to delete from the list.
- Click on the **three vertical dots menu** next to the user’s name.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-how-to-delete-a-user-from-a-walmarts-seller-center-account-image-2o7r9hmk.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/gzrimage.png)

**Step 3 – Delete**

- From the menu that appears, select **Remove user**.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3egimage.png)

- A pop-up window will appear, prompting you to confirm that you want to delete the user.
- To confirm, select **Yes, remove**.

FAQs

**1. What happens to the data associated with the user’s account after it’s deleted?**Once a user is deleted, their access to your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted. This action is final, so ensure you no longer need the user’s access before proceeding with the deletion.

**2. What should I do if I do not have admin-level access?**If you do not have admin-level access, contact your account administrator to help you with the deletion process.
