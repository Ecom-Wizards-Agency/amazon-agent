# Walmart SOP: How to Enroll in Walmart-funded Incentives Program

## How to Enroll in Walmart-funded Incentives Program

**Who is this for?**

This process is for Amazon specialists who are managing Walmart accounts. You'll be helping Walmart sellers to either enroll or opt out of the Walmart-funded incentives program, ensuring their products are priced to stay competitive in the marketplace.

**Objective:**

This SOP provides clear, step-by-step instructions for Amazon specialists on how to manage Walmart-funded incentives for Walmart Marketplace accounts. By following this procedure, you’ll ensure that Walmart sellers are set up to take full advantage of Walmart-funded incentives, which help keep their product prices competitive.

**Procedure**

**Step 1: Access the Incentives Page**

1. **Log in to Seller Center** using the Walmart account you’re managing.
2. In the **Pricing** section, navigate to the **Incentives page**.

1. Click on the **Manage incentives** button located in the top right of the page.
2. From the dropdown, select **Manage Walmart-funded incentives**.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-how-to-enroll-in-walmart-funded-incentives-program-image-50d6w9jx.png)

**Step 2: Turn the Program On or Off**

1. On the Walmart-funded incentives page, look for the toggle button at the top right corner.
2. To **activate the program**:

  - Switch the toggle to the **ON** position. This will automatically enroll all eligible products in the Walmart-funded incentives program.
3. To **deactivate the program**:

  - Switch the toggle to the **OFF** position. This will remove all products from receiving incentives.

**Pro Tip:** If a seller only wants to participate in the program for specific items (and not their entire catalog), this can be done by managing each product individually. For more details, point them to the **Walmart-funded incentives: Manage item enrollment** guide.

FAQs

**Do Walmart-funded incentives expire?**

- Yes, Walmart-funded incentives can expire at Walmart’s discretion. If this happens, the item price will revert to the seller’s last submitted price.

**What happens if an item is unenrolled from Walmart-funded incentives?**

- When an item is unenrolled, it will no longer receive any current or future Walmart-funded incentives. The item will be removed from the **Active** or **Eligible** tabs until it’s opted back in.

**How does the Referral Fee adjustment affect the seller’s payment?**

- Even if the price displayed to the customer is lower than the seller’s original price, the seller will still receive the same payment as if the item were sold at the original price. The price difference is covered by Walmart, so it doesn't affect the seller’s net payment.

**What happens if I apply a promotion to an item?**

- If the seller applies a promotion to an item, the price under the Walmart-funded incentives program will reflect the current promotional price.

**How can I tell if a customer order contains an item receiving Walmart-funded incentives?**

- Customer orders with items that have Walmart-funded incentives applied will show a **Price Incentive** notification in a pop-up window, indicating which incentive program is applied.

**How do I ensure that MAP (Minimum Advertised Price) requirements aren’t violated?**

- If the seller needs to exclude MAP-restricted items, use the **bulk template process** to remove them from the incentives program. You can also manually add specific SKUs to the exclusion list as needed.
