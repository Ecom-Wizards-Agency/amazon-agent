## Catalog Performance Report in Walmart Seller Center

**Who is this for?**

This SOP applies to amazon specialists and advertising specialists overseeing the performance of products in Walmart Seller Center. It covers how to generate, analyze, and track product performance using the **Catalog Performance Report** and the **Search Insights** tool.

**Objective:**

To provide valuable insights into how products are performing in Walmart's search results. This guide helps specialists use the report to optimize product listings and improve search visibility.

How to Download a Catalog Performance Report?

1. **Access the**[**Search Insights Dashboard**](https://seller.walmart.com/analytics/search-insights)

  - Navigate to the **Search Insights** section in **Seller Center** to see how your products are being found by customers on [Walmart.com.](http://walmart.com)

How to Analyze Item Performance?

1. **View Performance of Individual Items**

  - To analyze the performance of a specific product, click on the **item name** within the report. This will allow you to see detailed insights for that product.

    [![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/791image.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/791image.png)

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-catalog-performance-report-in-walmart-seller-center-image-oasih9fm.png)

1. **Review Key Metrics for the Selected Item**

  - After selecting an item, you will be able to view:

    - **Recommended Keywords:** Suggestions for adding keywords to your product title for better visibility.
    - **Item's Search Performance:** Insights into how the item is performing in search results.
    - **Customer Search Keywords (Top Search Queries for This Item):** The keywords that customers are using to find your product.
    - **Sales Rank:** The product's rank within its category based on sales data from the past six months.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-catalog-performance-report-in-walmart-seller-center-image-fqbkq5e8.png)

[![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/iOgimage.png)](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/iOgimage.png)

1. **Understand Sales Rank**

  - **Sales Rank Score:** This score shows how your product ranks compared to others in the same product type. A **lower sales rank** indicates higher performance.
  - **Eligibility for Sales Rank:** Items must be listed for at least one month and have had at least one sale in the past six months to be eligible for a sales rank.

How to Track Keyword Performance?

1. **Implement Recommended Keywords**

  - Based on the **recommended keywords** provided in the report, update your product title and description as necessary.
2. **Monitor Performance Impact**

  - Use the **Search Performance Impact** insights to measure the performance of your product before and after implementing keyword recommendations.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/walmart-sop-catalog-performance-report-in-walmart-seller-center-image-5p1mi5ou.png)

- Track the **14-day performance change** by comparing metrics before and after keywords are added to the product title.

1. **Monitor Weekly Data Changes**

  - Be aware that **data refreshes weekly**, meaning your product’s performance might fluctuate due to market trends, competition, or updates to your listings.

FAQs

1. **Why can’t I see some of my items in the Search Insights report?**

  - Items that do not have any performance history (traffic or sales data) will not appear in the report.
2. **Why is my item’s performance showing as low, even though it’s performing well individually?**

  - The search funnel performance is based on how your item compares to competing products in the same subcategory. Even if your item is doing well on its own, it may still need improvements to stay competitive.
3. **Why does the data change every week?**

  - The Search Insights report is updated weekly to reflect trends and changes in product performance, so you may see fluctuations in your data. This is normal and reflects the competitive nature of the marketplace.
4. **Why don’t some items have a sales rank?**

  - A sales rank is only available for items in **featured categories** and those that meet the criteria of being listed for at least a month and having at least one sale in the last six months.
5. **How can I further improve my product’s search performance?**

  - In addition to implementing keyword recommendations, consider using [**Walmart Sponsored Search**](https://marketplace.walmart.com/sponsored-search-ads/) ads, improving your product listings with better keywords, and ensuring your titles and descriptions are optimized for broader search terms.
