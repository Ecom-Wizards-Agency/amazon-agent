# SEO SOP: How to Find Relevant Keywords in Magnet

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/seo/page/seo-sop-how-to-find-relevant-keywords-in-magnet

## **How to Find Relevant Keywords Using Helium 10 Magnet**

**Who is it for :** This SOP is for individuals who are searching for coincidences and areas of improvement between the content you are providing (ads, A+ content, TBD, etc.) and what people are searching for currently.

**Objective**: This SOP will help you to add value to your account by determining what people are searching on Amazon (it can be Google or Walmart too), understanding the competition, and determining trends (by seasonality) and how they affect your products too.

**Instructions / Steps:**

1. Log in to Helium 10.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/fSSimage.png)

2. Click on Tools.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/7mmimage.png)

3. Click on Magnet.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/6zsimage.png)

4. Click on Find Suggestions.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/mHiimage.png)

5. Input the most relevant keyword for the ASIN.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/MuHimage.png)

6. Click on Get Keywords

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Edvimage.png)

7. Sort the search volume from highest to lowest.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/VlDimage.png)

8. Click on Search Volume Trend and analyze which keywords have more than 100% on search volume trends.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/h1Simage.png)

9. Look at the Search Volume data and identify the ones that have more than 500 search volumes monthly.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YAyimage.png)

10. Identify the keywords that comply with the two requirements:

- + 100% on Search Volume Trend
- + 500 on Search Volume Monthly

11. Download the report, mark or highlight the relevant keywords, and share them with your advertising team, PPC POC, or creative team to take into consideration the content you are creating (Title, BP, etc).

12. To see how the main keyword is doing at a specific time, click on the Search Volume graph icon.![Click on highlight](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/sop---how-to-find-relevant-keywords-in-magnet-image-n5u7h3pj.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/YFIimage.png)

13. Sort the Search Volume by 1 year, 30 days, and 90 days to identify the seasonality it affects your products.

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/hsVimage.png)

**Important Note:** Make sure you are looking at all the scenes when determining insights, do not base every behavior on sales. Compare with your data from previous periods/years as well as unique situations (OOS, budget adjustment, etc).

FAQs

General Overview

- **Q1: What is the purpose of this SOP for using Helium 10 Magnet?**

  - **A1:** The purpose is to help individuals identify relevant keywords, analyze search trends, and understand competition to improve the alignment between provided content and current customer searches on platforms like Amazon, Google, or Walmart.

Prerequisites

- **Q2: What tools or data are required before using Helium 10 Magnet?**

  - **A2:** You need access to Helium 10, relevant ASINs, and data from previous periods or unique situations (e.g., out-of-stock events, budget adjustments).

Steps to Find Keywords

- **Q3: How do you start the process in Helium 10 Magnet?**

  - **A3:**

    - Log in to Helium 10.
    - Click on Tools.
    - Select Magnet.
    - Click on "Find Suggestions."
    - Input the most relevant keyword for the ASIN.
    - Click on "Get Keywords."
- **Q4: How do you sort and analyze keywords?**

  - **A4:**

    - Sort the search volume from highest to lowest.
    - Click on "Search Volume Trend" and analyze keywords with over 100% trend growth.
    - Look for keywords with a search volume exceeding 500 monthly.
- **Q5: What criteria should keywords meet to be considered relevant?**

  - **A5:** Keywords should meet the following criteria:

    - Over 100% on the Search Volume Trend.
    - Over 500 search volumes monthly.
- **Q6: How do you finalize and share keyword findings?**

  - **A6:**

    - Download the report.
    - Mark or highlight relevant keywords.
    - Share them with your advertising team, PPC point of contact, or creative team for consideration in content creation (e.g., title, bullet points).

Advanced Analysis

- **Q7: How can you analyze seasonality using Helium 10 Magnet?**

  - **A7:**

    - Click on the Search Volume graph icon for a specific keyword.
    - Sort the Search Volume data by 1 year, 30 days, and 90 days to identify seasonal effects on the product.
- **Q8: What factors should you consider when analyzing data?**

  - **A8:** Ensure you review all relevant contexts, including:

    - Sales data.
    - Data from previous periods or years.
    - Unique situations such as out-of-stock events or budget adjustments.

Reporting

- **Q9: How do you share insights from your analysis?**

  - **A9:** After finalizing the report, share the marked keywords and insights with the relevant teams to enhance advertising strategies and content creation.
