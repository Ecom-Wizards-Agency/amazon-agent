# SEO SOP: How to Construct Alt-Text and Add Them to A+ Content Images

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/seo/page/seo-sop-how-to-construct-alt-text-and-add-them-to-a-content-images

## **How to Construct Alt-Text and Add Them to A+ Content Images**

**Who is this for :** For individuals who wish further enhance their organic rankings and visibility on Amazon.

**Objectives:**

- To help enhance the search engine of your listings;
- To land on page 1 of Amazon;
- To help boost conversions, sales, traffic, and organic rankings

> **Important Reminder:**
>
> **For Brand Story images****, any image containing crawlable text will** **not** **display the uploaded alt-text on the Product Detail Page (PDP). Instead, it will show as having an** **empty alt attribute** **on the PDP, even if alt-text was provided in the backend.**
>
> **New Alt Text Update for Brand Stores**
>
> - You can now add alt text to shoppable images in Amazon Brand Stores
> - Character Limit: Max 125 characters
>
> Alt Text Process: Same as the current process used for A+ Content alt text
>
> Marketplace Limitation
>
> Alt-Text No Longer Available in EU Marketplaces
>
> Amazon has removed the option to add Alt-Text to A+ Content image modules in all European (EU) marketplaces, including:
>
> - United Kingdom (UK)
> - Germany (DE)
> - France (FR)
> - Italy (IT)
> - Spain (ES)
> - and other EU locales
>
> *This means the text box to insert alt-text is no longer visible or accessible in the A+ Content Manager for these marketplaces.*
>
> Alt-Text Still Available in:
>
> - United States (US)
> - Canada (CA)
> - Mexico (MX)
> - Other non-EU marketplaces (verify before use)

**II. Constructing the Alt-Text**

As mentioned above, make use of the **most recent MKL no matter what SEO Phase**. Check the client’s folder and look for SEO spreadsheets. If you find a completed Phase 1 SEO worksheet, use it. However, it’s better to use the Phase 3 SEO worksheet if there are any.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1712157478041.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/ujRimage.png)

Access the worksheet and go to the Master Keyword List (MKL) tab. As much as possible, pick those keywords with high search volume and high MAG Score. Process them into H10 Frankenstein, and apply some filters as shown in Figure 6.

Then, look out for prohibited, branded, and duplicate keywords. Take them out and select only relevant keywords. Also, the ideal character length should be between 90-100.

Our goal is to improve the search engine of the client’s listings, thus constructing a different set of Alt-Text for each content is a must. Once Alt-Text is constructed, paste them into column G of your worksheet. Include as well the character count for each set of Alt-Text.

**III. Applying the Alt-Text**

(Before getting to this stage, ensure that the proposed Alt-Text is approved for upload by the client, and once approved make sure that screenshot of the approval or fireflies link is commented in the Asana Task).

Go to A+ Content Manager and access the content to which you are trying to apply the Alt-Text. Click on edit and paste the new Alt-Text. Refer to Figures 9 and 10.

**Important Note:**

Do not apply Alt-Text to images in the Standard Comparison Chart module anymore. Amazon has recently updated its system so that Alt-Text added to comparison chart images will not reflect, as these are now considered decorative images.

**IV. SOP Update Guidelines & Best Practices**

To further enhance the quality and effectiveness of Alt-Text creation, please take note of the following:

**A. Mobile and Desktop Version Alt-Text**

- Important: The mobile and desktop versions of A+ Content images must have separate sets of Alt-Text.
- This is to ensure Amazon indexes both versions appropriately for keyword relevance and user experience.

**B. Alt-Text Do’s and Don’ts**

**Do’s:**

- Do use high-volume, relevant keywords from the Master Keyword List (MKL)
- Do maintain a character count between 90–100 characters
- Do create unique Alt-Text per image (avoid duplication)
- Do focus on benefits and features aligned with product USPs

**Don’ts:**

- Don’t use branded keywords or prohibited terms
- Don’t keyword stuff or use unrelated terms
- Don’t repeat the same Alt-Text across multiple images

**C. If the MKL Lacks Sufficient Keywords**

If the MKL doesn’t provide enough relevant keywords, please refer to these additional tools and SOPs:

- [Magnet](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/seo/page/seo-sop-how-to-find-relevant-keywords-in-magnet) – For identifying high-volume keywords not present in the MKL
- [Google Translate](https://myamazonguy.document360.io/docs/seo-sop-how-to-find-additional-keywords-using-spanish-translation-of-keywords?highlight=Google%20Translate) – For creating spanish words, to be used specifically in US marketplace.

FAQs

Preparing the Worksheet

- **Q1: How do I identify the total number of approved A+ Content for the worksheet?**

  - **A1:** Click on the A+ Content Manager, select the approved content, and copy the content name, ASINs, and item names. Paste them into your worksheet.
- **Q2: How do I document existing Alt-Text from the content?**

  - **A2:** Count the number of images in each A+ Content, copy the existing Alt-Text, and paste them into your worksheet along with their character counts.

Constructing the Alt-Text

- **Q3: What is the ideal source for finding keywords when constructing Alt-Text?**

  - **A3:** Use the most recent Phase 3 SEO worksheet or the Phase 1 worksheet if Phase 3 is unavailable. Prioritize the Master Keyword List (MKL) tab.
- **Q4: Which keywords should be selected for Alt-Text construction?**

  - **A4:** Choose keywords with high search volume and high MAG Score. Avoid prohibited, branded, and duplicate keywords.
- **Q5: What is the recommended character length for Alt-Text?**

  - **A5:** The ideal character length is between 90-100 characters per Alt-Text.
- **Q6: Why should a different set of Alt-Text be created for each content?**

  - **A6:** Each content requires unique Alt-Text to optimize the search engine performance of individual listings.

Applying the Alt-Text

- **Q7: What should be done before uploading the Alt-Text to A+ Content?**

  - **A7:** Ensure that the Alt-Text is approved by the client. Document the approval through a screenshot or Fireflies link and comment on the relevant Asana Task.
- **Q8: How do I apply Alt-Text to A+ Content images?**

  - **A8:** Access the A+ Content Manager, edit the content, and paste the Alt-Text into the appropriate fields.
- **Q9: What precautions should be taken when editing images in product recommendations or Standard Comparison Charts?**

  - **A9:** Download and secure the images before editing and applying the Alt-Text to prevent them from being lost if removed from the system.
- **Q10: How long does it take for Amazon to approve the content after submitting Alt-Text changes?**

  - **A10:** Amazon typically takes at least 24 hours to review and approve the content.
