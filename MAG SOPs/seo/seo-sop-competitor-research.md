# SEO SOP: Competitor Research

Source: https://sop.myamazonguy.com/books/seo/page/seo-sop-competitor-research

## **SEO SOP : Competitor Research**

**Who is this for:**

- To individuals who seek to provide a high-level overview of how your competitors are faring on the Amazon marketplace.

**Objectives:**

- To provide a high-level overview of how our clients’ competitors are faring on the Amazon marketplace
- To know where our clients’ listings stand in the competition
- To determine the monthly data of a specific product category, including competitor prices, search ranks, sales, reviews, average ratings, revenues, fees, and more

**WHEN DO WE CONDUCT COMPETITOR RESEARCH?**

- After onboarding a new client
- An existing client launches a new product
- We want to check the competition for a product / category.
- Upon client’s request

**Process**

1. Identify which ASINs / products you need to work on.For this example we will use wooden soap tray as our target listing.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1687459624762(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/0hximage.png)

1. Using the main keyphrase of the product, look for competitors on Amazon.

- Important: Open the product detail page when performing this step. It is important to have the right product knowledge to determine the right search query to be used.
- Ensure that you are conducting the search in the correct Amazon marketplace. The delivery address should also be in the same region covered by the marketplace.
- You may use Amazon’s filtering tool to customize and narrow down the search results when conducting specific competitor research.

1. Once the results page renders, click on the Helium 10 extension on your browser and run Xray.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/image-1685473382207(1).png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/EcLimage.png)

1. On the Xray page, scroll down and click on Load More Results to include the 2nd page search results.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%209.10.59%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/q31image.png)

1. Go through the products to determine which ones are relevant to the client’s product. Tick all the products that are not relevant (cannot be considered as competitors). Click on the trash icon to remove the ticked products.

  - Tip: You may use Xray’s filtering options to customize and narrow down the results.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%209.14.47%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/D1Iimage.png)

1. After removing the irrelevant products, click on the download CSV report button to download the results to your computer.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%209.19.21%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/J7jimage.png)

1. Open your report [template](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1yUYGLUp81l-lGXT1yoAOw1C65tq8zb7HF4CPO9oxl50/copy). Rename the file using the following format: “Client Name - Competitor Research Report”

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%209.21.32%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/3AVimage.png)

1. Prepare / clean up the list by performing the following:

  - Remove the URL (C) and Image URL (D) columns

    - New columns might show up on the right most part, make sure to check this as well and remove if necessary.

    ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%209.23.22%E2%80%AFPM.png)

    ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/zU6image.png)
  - Remove duplicate BSRs

    ![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%209.27.53%E2%80%AFPM.png)

    ![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/27rimage.png)
  - Remove listings that have no revenue and BSR data
  - Sort the BSR data in ascending order

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%209.30.15%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/qMcimage.png)

1. From the sheet, transfer the list to your competitor research template. Use paste as values when performing this step to maintain the template’s formatting.

![image](https://files.document360.io/d9e9b7a2-6758-4c75-97d3-36a6877048cf/Images/Documentation/Screenshot%202024-08-20%20at%209.32.50%E2%80%AFPM.png)

![image.png](https://sop.myamazonguy.com/uploads/images/gallery/2025-08/scaled-1680-/Z5limage.png)

1. Name the report’s tab and the summary table's header using the search query used to look up competitors.
2. Double check the following before delivering the report to the client:

  - The competitors / products included in the report are relevant to the listing you are researching on

    - Most of the unhighlighted listings contain the keyword bamboo, which is classified as wood. This gives us an idea that the said keyword can be used as the second or alternate format rule.
    - From here, we can then remove those irrelevant listings (i.e. ceramic, plastic, glass, etc.) that were left unhighlighted. Additionally, we can narrow down the list of competitors by applying the next set of format rules by using the keywords soap and tray and removing all irrelevant (or unhighlighted) listings.
    - To further remove irrelevant listings, take a look at one of the unhighlighted listings and see what keywords are used. In this case, the keywords dispenser and white were the common words used in their listings, thus we shall be using these keywords as the next format rules.

FAQs

- **Q1: What is the main purpose of conducting competitor research on Amazon?**

  - **A1:**The main purpose is to provide a high-level overview of how competitors are performing on the Amazon marketplace, including tracking competitor prices, sales, reviews, ratings, and other relevant metrics, to help determine how a client’s listings stand in the competition.
- **Q2: When should competitor research be conducted?**

  - **A2:** Competitor research should be conducted after onboarding a new client, when an existing client launches a new product, to check the competition for a product/category, or upon a client’s request.
- **Q3: How do I ensure I am conducting competitor research in the correct Amazon marketplace?**

  - **A3:** Make sure that the delivery address is set to the same region as the marketplace you are conducting research in.
- **Q4: Why is it important to open the product detail page before performing a search on Amazon?**

  - **A4:** Opening the product detail page helps ensure that you have the right product knowledge to determine the correct search query to be used for competitor research.
- **Q5: How do I remove irrelevant competitors from the search results when using Helium 10's Xray tool?**

  - **A5:** After reviewing the results, tick all products that are irrelevant (not competitors), and click the trash icon to remove them from the list.
- **Q6: What should I do if duplicate data or irrelevant listings appear in the competitor research report?**

  - **A6:** Remove any duplicate BSRs, listings with no revenue or BSR data, and irrelevant listings (such as ceramic, plastic, or glass products).
- **Q7: How should I format the competitor research report before delivering it to the client?**

  - **A7:** Ensure that the report includes only relevant competitors, uses the correct keywords for narrowing the search, and that all formatting rules have been applied to remove irrelevant listings. The report should be named with the client's name and the search query used.
- **Q8: What is the role of keywords like "bamboo," "soap," and "tray" in competitor research?**

  - **A8:** Keywords like "bamboo," "soap," and "tray" are used as format rules to narrow down the list of competitors, helping to exclude irrelevant listings (such as those made of ceramic, plastic, or glass) and ensuring the results are more targeted and relevant.
- **Q9: What should I do if I find new columns in the report template during cleanup?**

  - **A9:** Check the new columns for unnecessary data and remove them if necessary to ensure the final report is clean and relevant.
- **Q10: How do I handle results that appear on the second page of the search in Helium 10's Xray tool?**

  - **A10:** After running Xray, click on "Load More Results" to include data from the second page of search results, ensuring all potential competitors are considered.
- **Q11: What if there are <10 competitors despite using different keyphrases related to the product, can I proceed and gather keywords?**

  - **Yes, as long as we have 5 competitors at least.**
