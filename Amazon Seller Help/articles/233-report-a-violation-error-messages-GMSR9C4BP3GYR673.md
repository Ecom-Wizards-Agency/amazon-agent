# Report a Violation error messages


These are the most common error messages during the Report a Violation process and what they mean.

Reporting a violation

Error message	What it means and next steps
You do not have any trademarks registered with one of the trademark offices connected to your account.	

Notices of potential trademark infringement can only be submitted with trademarks that are enforceable in the jurisdiction of the selected store, such as USPTO for amazon.com.

If the error message states that you don’t have any trademarks registered with a specific trademark office that is connected to your account, go to Manage intellectual properties in Brand Registry and click Connect a trademark.

You have already reported all the selected items (e.g. offer, image, complete ASIN).	

This error indicates that you have already submitted an infringement for the same reason.

To follow up on a previously reported item, go to Brand Registry support, choose Escalate previously submitted issue, and select Escalate an infringement related issue.

…selected ASIN(s) do not contain the reported trademark and will be excluded from your report.	

Ensure that the ASIN that you are reporting for potential trademark infringement contains the reported trademark. If you the selected ASIN contains your trademark, indicate whether the trademark is in the text or in the product images of the item.

If you conducted a test buy and received a product that used your trademark on the product or packaging but was not authentic, include the associated order ID when you submit the report.

Because an increasing number of your recent reports of intellectual property violations were not accepted and found to be invalid, you are temporarily required to conduct a test purchase for reported items and provide the associated order number.	

Because you allege that a specific seller or multiple sellers have offers that infringe upon your intellectual property, you must substantiate this claim by making a test purchase for each offer you report.

If you believe there are illegally manufactured, re-copied, or unlawfully obtained copies of your work being offered for sale, submit the notice as an ASIN-level claim.

If you want to submit a report against individual third-party sellers, submit it with a test purchase from the seller of the product that you allege to be infringing.

After you’ve received the item and confirmed it is infringing, you can submit a report with the order ID by clicking Yes when asked Have you purchased the item and confirmed that the product infringes upon your intellectual property?

Note: Once a product has been legally purchased or acquired by another owner, it may subsequently be sold and resold without restriction.

You have alleged that one or more specific seller’s offer infringes upon your design right. You will need a test purchase for each offer you are reporting to substantiate this claim. If you believe the entire product/ASIN infringes (not just a specific seller’s offer) your design right, go back and select the ASIN(s) to report.
You can’t select the complete ASIN, because it’s connected to you or your brand.	

If you're unable to select an ASIN in the Report a Violation tool, and the error message says, "The ASIN is connected to you or your brand," you won't be able to select and report the complete ASIN because it contains your own offer.

We recommend that you only select and report offers that are infringing.

The ASIN is connected to you or your brand.

You can review the excluded ASIN(s) in the offer summary. If you choose to include any of the excluded offer(s), please select them using the following checkboxes. Given the reported trademark in on the product detail page associated with the complete ASIN, to report the complete ASIN, click 'Back' below the offer summary to return to search results and select the complete ASIN.

	

We allow other sellers to list their items for sale against ASINs as long as they aren't infringing on any intellectual property. If you still want to report a counterfeit infringement because your trademark is on a product detail page, you must ensure that the entire ASIN is selected, not specific offers.

To proceed with your report, go to the search results page and select the ASIN that you want to report.

Your report was not accepted. The selected product or products may not meet the necessary acceptance criteria.

Review any issues and update your report to ensure it includes the required information for acceptance.

	

Your report or part of your report was not accepted because the selected product or products may not meet the necessary criteria for acceptance.

For copyrighted content, it must be your intellectual property, or content for which you are the owner or authorized agent.

Resubmit the report with a publicly accessible direct link to content that is your intellectual property, or to content for which you are the owner or authorized agent.

To learn more about other error messages not listed above, go to Brand Registry error messages.

If your error still cannot be solved, contact us and select Technical issue or use Amazon's Seller Assistant.
