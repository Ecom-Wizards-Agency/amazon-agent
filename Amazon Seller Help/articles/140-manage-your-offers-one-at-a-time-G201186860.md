# Manage your offers one at a time


The Manage Inventory page provides you with tools for searching, viewing, and updating your product and inventory listing information.

For more information, go to Manage Inventory.

To learn more about viewing, copying, deleting, or closing listings, go to the following pages:

View and sort your inventory by status, SKU, product name, and more by using View your inventory.
Change your product information quickly and easily by using Edit a listing.
Use the Copy listing feature to create a new detail page by using Copy a listing.
Delete a listing from your seller account by using Delete a product.
Close a listing by using Close a listing.

For more information on relisting, managing pricing, viewing your sales history, and setting page preferences, go to the following pages:

Relist a product by using Relist a product that is Inactive (Closed) - Inactive Listings.
Find reasons for blocked listings by using Reasons for Blocked listings.
Add images and video to your listing by using Add product images and video.
Change the category of a product by using Change a product’s category.
Manage product pricing by using Manage pricing.
View your sales history by using Sales history.
Customize your page preferences by using Set your Manage Inventory page preferences.
Address opportunities in your listings
Address additional opportunities by doing the following:
Keep your listings discoverable- Make sure your listings are visible to customers in their search results.
Improve your listings.
