# Spotlight communication features


Spotlight helps communicate different features and protections that may become available for your account in Brand Registry. You can receive new feature announcements, product or service eligibility notifications, and supporting content within Brand Registry—all from one easy-to-access place.

View Spotlight

The Spotlight widget will automatically display the latest announcement for you to review and act, as needed, at the first login session.

To view your Spotlight:

Click the Spotlight icon to view all content available to you.
Click any of the content calls to action to resolve it or to learn more.
Click the close icon at the top right corner to exit the Spotlight widget.

Note:
New content will be indicated by a red badge overlaying the Spotlight icon in the header navigation with a blue border around the content.
Unread content will be indicated by a blue background.
Content that has been read will be indicated by a white background.
To remove the blue background for unread content, you must view each piece of content in the Spotlight widget.
Spotlight error messages

If you encounter a functional error on the Spotlight widget, you’ll likely see an error message that will prompt you to refresh the page. If refreshing the page does not solve your issue, sign out and then sign back into your Brand Registry account.
