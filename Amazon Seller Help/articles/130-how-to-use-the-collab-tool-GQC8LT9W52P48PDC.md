# How to use the Collab tool


As a Brand owner, follow the below steps to approve, reject, and revise design submissions.

How to approve designs

To access your Collab Dashboard, log into your Seller Central account.
To review a submitted design, click the Review tab and then click Review in the right column.
To approve a design, click Approve on the top right.

You can choose to add comments for the designer. This is not mandatory. However, it is highly recommended, as the feedback will guide designers towards generating designs that are likelier to get approval.

How to reject designs

To access your Collab Dashboard, log into your Seller Central account.
To review a submitted design, click the Review tab and then click Review in the right column.
To reject a design, add your comments for the designer to understand the reason for rejection and use the feedback to influence future submissions.
On adding your comments, click Reject on the top right. When prompted, enter the ‘Reject reason‘. Ensure to choose the appropriate one.

How to request revisions

To access your Collab Dashboard, log into your Seller Central account.
To review a submitted design, click the Review tab and then click Review in the right column.
To request revisions, enter your request in the comment box. Be sure to be as specific as possible. On adding your comments, click Revision on the top right.

As a designer, follow the below steps to submit and withdraw designs and submit revisions.

How to submit designs

To access your Collab Dashboard, log into your Seller Central account.
Click Add New Design on the top right of the page.
On this page, complete the following fields:
Brand (that you are submitting a design for)
Design Title
Products your design will be placed on
Upload your design using the design template
Click Next and you will be prompted to confirm that you are ready to submit your design to the brand. Once you confirm, the submission is complete.

How to withdraw designs

To access your Collab Dashboard, log into your Seller Central account.
Locate the design you would like to withdraw, and click Edit in the right column.
Click Withdraw on the top right of the page.

How to submit revisions

Access your Collab Dashboard by logging into your Seller Central account.
Locate the design you would like to upload a revised version of, and click Edit in the right column.
Click Revise on the top right of the page.
Upload your revised template and add comments to highlight the revisions made along with any pertinent notes to the Brand to consider while reviewing the updated design.
