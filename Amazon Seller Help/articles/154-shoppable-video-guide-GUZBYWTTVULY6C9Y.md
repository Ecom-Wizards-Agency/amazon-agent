Shoppable videos can demonstrate your product in action, describe your product, and tell your unique brand story. When used effectively, videos can result in higher conversion rates and increased sales. You can upload videos of your product that will display next to images of the product.

Videos provide the following key benefits:

Increased customer confidence through product demonstrations
Better understanding of product size, features, and functionality
Reduced product returns through clearer expectations
Enhanced detail page engagement
On average, sellers who added videos to their detail pages observed a *23.8% increase in sales compared with an ASIN without a video (*Amazon internal historical data, January through June 2024).

To learn more, watch Benefits of adding a video to a product listing.

Rights and infringement

Your videos cannot infringe the intellectual or other property rights of third parties. Post only your own content or content that you have permission to use. Unless you have obtained permission from all relevant third parties, do not play music or include images, artwork, or other audiovisual works in your videos.

Each video must be associated with at least one ASIN.
US-Canada shared listings sync automatically, but no other marketplace sharing is supported. Note that we constantly experiment to deliver the best customer and seller experience. As part of this testing, some videos may appear on detail pages of shared global listings during the experimentation process.

Best practices

Shoppable videos must have clear product visibility, high-quality audio, proper lighting, professional presentation, and accurate product information.

Upload videos

Before you upload a video, ensure the following requirements are met:

Seller account has been active for more than three months (brand-registered or non-brand registered).
Product ASINs are created and listed.
Video files are in MOV or MP4 format, up to 1080p quality (max 5GB). Go to Video file requirements for more information.
Compliance with our Community guidelines.

Method 1: Upload through the Upload and Manage Videos tool

To use the tool, follow these steps:

Go to Catalog and select Upload and Manage Videos.
Click Upload video.
Fill in the required fields:
Title: Display name for the video
ASINs: Select the relevant products
Language: Select the supported language associated with your video.
Thumbnail: Auto-generated or upload custom (JPG, PNG, GIF, or BMP under 5MB)
Click Submit.

Method 2: Upload through the Image Manager dashboard

To use the tool, follow these steps:

Go to Catalog and select Upload images.
Select your product in the Image Manager tool.
Click Videos on the product detail pane.
Click Upload video.
Fill in the required fields:
Title
Thumbnail is auto generated, or you can choose to replace with a JPEG, PNG, GIF, or BMP under 5MB. 1920 x 1080 pixels at 16:9 is recommended
Click Submit.

Important: Bulk upload of up to 15 videos at once is supported.

Variations

If you want a video to be uploaded to a selected set of ASINs, list them in the ASINs field during upload. This will map the video to only the specified ASINs. Don't activate the variation extension feature.

To extend a video to all variations of the mentioned ASINs, activate the variation extension feature by setting the Include ASINs product variations toggle to the “on” position.

Language support

You can upload videos in multiple languages. When you upload videos, select the language used in the video under Language of video. The drop-down list will include all languages that are supported in that store.

Thumbnails

A thumbnail will be automatically generated for the video. If you decide to replace it, click the thumbnail to upload an image. You must use a high-quality image in either JPEG or PNG format recommend, under 5MB in size. The image should be high-resolution with clear visibility.

Processing time

Videos uploads and updates will take the following amount of time to process:

Initial review: 1-7 business days
Publication to detail page: within three days after approval
ASIN variation updates: up to three days for changes

Note: Review and publication timelines may be longer before and during holiday shopping seasons.

Video Metrics Dashboard

Trends Calculations: Trends are calculated differently based on the metric type. We use averages for percentage metrics (like view duration percentage). We use sums for numerical metrics (like product clicks). The comparison is always made between the current period and the previous equivalent period.

Trend Graph Lines: Trends compare your currently selected date range to the equivalent previous period. You can change the date range using the dropdown button at the top of the dashboard.

A green color indicates that the metric has a positive trend.
A red color indicates that the metric has a negative trend.
No color indicates the metric is unchanged.

For return rate, a decrease in return rate will result in a green trend line to indicate fewer returns and an increase in return rate will show a red trend line to indicate an increase in returns.
