# Unlock marketing opportunities


Your products could be eligible for complimentary marketing opportunities throughout the year. We're always looking for exciting new products to showcase to customers, and we want to make sure your products get the spotlight they deserve.

ASIN eligibility

Review your newly launched and top-performing products to ensure that they meet the marketing readiness eligibility criteria noted below. If specific ASINs don’t meet the criteria, take the necessary steps, such as updating your listings. The more ASINs that meet these criteria, the higher the chances that your products might be selected.

ASINs that meet the marketing readiness criteria will be considered for placements in ongoing and seasonal events, such as Customers' Most Loved, Everyday Essentials, New Arrivals, Valentine’s Day, and Back to School. Some ASINs may even be selected for inclusion in Amazon social media and influencer content.

Brand quality
--Brand registered	Required

Note: While we do our best to feature eligible products, we can’t guarantee that your products will be included in events. Requirements are subject to change at any time at our discretion.
Optimize customer engagement

Effectively engage with your customers during key events throughout the year by using the 2026 marketing calendar (in English). This calendar shows Amazon events that align with your deals strategies, advertising campaigns, and seasonal content.

Note: This calendar is only an overview and is subject to change at any time.

To optimize your products for success, review the calendar and the tips below:

Manage your inventory: Ensure that your top-selling products are available to buy during key events by having a minimum of five units in stock and Prime shipping offers.
Optimize listings: Enhance your product listings with bullet points, relevant keywords, captivating lifestyle imagery, and videos to help customers understand your products.
Update seasonal content: Refresh your A+ Content and brand storefront to engage customers with timely and relevant information.
Create deals: We recommend that you create deals of 20% or more for the event that you choose to participate in. To create event-specific deals, select Advertising from the drop-down menu in Seller Central and then select Deals. Deals typically are refreshed weekly.
Start advertising: Invest in advertising campaigns to help increase customer awareness, engagement, and conversion. Advertising one to two weeks before, during, and a few days after the event helps to maximize the traffic halo effect generated from the event.

Explore the Growth opportunities and Advertising tabs in Seller Central frequently to identify recommendations that can help increase discoverability and growth for your brand and products.
