# Listing quality guidelines


High-quality listings help customers find, evaluate, and purchase your products. A quality listing consists of seven key components that work together to create effective listings on Amazon:

Title

The product title is the first thing customers see in search results. An effective title clearly describes your product with essential information to identify, differentiate, and compare products making it easier for them to make decisions.

To learn more, go to Product title requirements and guidelines.

Bullet points

Bullet points highlight the main features and benefits of your product. They help customers quickly determine if the product meets their needs by focusing on the top features customers need to know in a consistent, easy-to-scan format.

To learn more, go to Product bullet points requirements.

Attributes

Product attributes are specific facts about your product that help with search and filtering. Complete attributes improve search visibility and enable customers to filter results based on their preferences, which provides essential information for purchase decisions.

To learn more, go to Attributes guide.

Images

Product images visually represent your product to customers. High-quality images show the product accurately and professionally, meeting our technical requirements while helping customers evaluate the product before purchase.

To learn more, go to Product image guide.

Video

Shoppable videos demonstrate your product in action and tell your brand story. Videos increase customer confidence through demonstrations of features and functionality.

To learn more, go to Shoppable video guide.

A+ Content

A+ Content enhances your product detail pages with rich marketing content. This premium content showcases product features and communicates brand value, which reduces customer questions and strengthens your brand presence across desktop and mobile experiences.

To learn more, go to A+ Content guide.

Search

Search optimization helps customers discover your products through keywords and browse categories. Accurate product classification and relevant search terms increase your product's visibility in search results, which can lead to increased sales.

To learn more, go to Search optimization.
