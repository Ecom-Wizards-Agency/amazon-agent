# Merch Collab


Merch Collab is Amazon's licensing program, where brands collaborate with qualified designers and manufacturers to create the world’s largest selection of branded merchandise for customers. The program makes licensing a simple, one-click process. Brands can simply approve and promote products, while Amazon tracks sales and pays out royalties.

For more information, go to Merch Collab.
