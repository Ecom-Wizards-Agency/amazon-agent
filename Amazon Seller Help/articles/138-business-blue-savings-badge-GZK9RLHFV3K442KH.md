# Business Blue Savings Badge

Frequently asked questions

The Business Blue Savings Badge displays savings percentage messages (like "Save 5%") that highlight savings to high potential business customers on Amazon Business store. These badges make your offers easier to find when business customers search for products. When you offer competitive business prices and quantity discounts, your products can earn the Business Savings Blue Badge (BSBB) on Amazon Business. Products with this badge improve the discoverability of your Business Discounts. This increased visibility helps because Business to Business (B2B) buyers are three times more likely to buy an item after viewing its product page than B2C (Business to Customer) buyers.¹

Where the badge appears

Search results pages
Business Home page - in select featured selections
Savings related pages

Badge eligibility criteria requirements

Products need to meet these minimum requirements to earn the badge:

You must have a Professional selling account. Check your current account type if you’re not sure and upgrade if required.
You must add single-unit business prices of at least a 5% discount off standard price or add quantity discounts of at least a 3% discount on the first tier.
Products must have at least a 4-star rating.

Products eligibility for Business Savings Blue Badge for single-unit Business Price

To find out if any of your items are eligible for the Business Savings Blue Badge for single-unit Business Price, follow the steps below:

First, ensure seller eligibility (see above).
In Seller Central, go to Manage Inventory page and then click Preferences.
Select Business Price and, at the bottom of the page, click Save changes.
In the Pricing and shipping column: click Set/amount link next to the yellow hazard icon and Business price, to open the Business Price action panel. Check the Business Savings Blue Badge eligible column status of your ASIN to see if you’ve matched the badge for single-unit business price.

Note: Even if you add single unit Business Prices lower than 5% discount but if your Quantity Discounts first tier has 3% discount, then you will get the blue badge on Quantity Discounts.

Check if you have the Business Savings Blue Badge on Amazon Business (B2B customer view)

You can check if you have Business Savings Blue Badge through search page on the Amazon Business store. Create a free Amazon Business buyer account to access Amazon Business store. You will be asked for business information during registration to verify your business. When eligible, the badge displays your savings percentage across multiple locations: search results, Business Home Page - in featured selections.

While not all listings get the badge, you can still offer and view business prices and quantity discounts available only to B2B buyers. Manage these B2B discounts easily in Seller Central through automated business price rules, the Manage Inventory Business Price Action Panel for individual items, or via bulk uploads, apps, and APIs alongside your standard pricing updates.

Frequently asked questions
Why isn't my Business Blue Savings Badge (BSBB) showing on Amazon Business despite eligible status in Seller Central?

While the Business Price Action Panel in Manage Inventory, Seller Central shows badge eligibility for single-unit business prices, this is just one component of the complete requirements and doesn't guarantee that your offers will get the badge. The badge's visibility on Amazon Business depends on meeting several criteria simultaneously. For single-unit purchases, you need at least a minimum 5% discount and a 4-star product rating.

Seller Central doesn't show badge eligibility for Quantity Discounts currently, so if the Business Price Action Panel in Manage Inventory doesn't show eligibility, it doesn't mean you don't have the BSBB for the quantity discount in a certain ASIN. For quantity discounts, you need a minimum 3% discount on the first tier and maintain the 4-star rating requirement.

Products can qualify for the badge through either discount type independently, displaying messages like "Save 5% on 10+ units" to attract business customers. To optimize your badge visibility, ensure your products meet all eligibility requirements while maintaining profitable margins across both single-unit business prices and quantity discount offerings. For more information, go to how does feedback and rating work.

How different discount types affect badge eligibility?

Products can qualify for the badge differently depending on discount type. Your product might earn the badge for quantity discounts (with a 3% discount) even if it doesn't qualify for its single-unit business price (which needs 5% discount).

To increase your chances of earning the badge, focus on creating meaningful discounts that meet or exceed the minimum thresholds while maintaining your profitability.

Note: A Business Blue Savings Badge is at the ASIN level. It is possible that if you have multiple variations for the same product, you may be Business Blue Savings Badge eligible for some of them and not the others. This is because each variation is a separate ASIN.
