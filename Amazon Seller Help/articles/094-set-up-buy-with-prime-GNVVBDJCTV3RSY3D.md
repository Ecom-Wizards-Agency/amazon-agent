# Set up Buy with Prime


Grow your ecommerce website by offering Prime shopping benefits, including fast, free delivery and a trusted checkout, as well as Reviews from Amazon, and more.

To get started, go to Professional Seller Central account, and then review the information in the Requirements FAQ section.

There are two ways to set up Buy with Prime on your site, depending on your ecommerce provider:

If you have a Shopify site, go to Sign up for Buy with Prime
Otherwise, go to Set up Buy with Prime widget

On either page, click the "+" icon to expand the Sign up from Seller Central section.
