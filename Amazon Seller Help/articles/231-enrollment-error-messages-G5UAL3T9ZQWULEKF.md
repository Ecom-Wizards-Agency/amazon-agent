# Enrollment error messages


These are the most common error messages you might encounter during the brand enrollment process and how to solve them.

Enroll a brand

Error message	What it means and next steps
The trademark number you have entered does not follow the required format in accordance with the trademark office selected. Below, in the "Examples" section you can check the accepted formats for this office in particular. Refer to the examples provided and make sure that the number matches in order to continue the application process. Note that each office has its particular requirements and formats, and the selection can affect the validation of the trademark number. For more information, visit the following Help Page.	

Each trademark office has its own way of registering a trademark, and they provide a number with a particular format.

For specific information regarding the office where your trademark was registered, go to Country-specific trademark information.

Check if your trademark number follows the correct format required by the relevant trademark office. If necessary, submit a new application.

This trademark number is already enrolled in Brand Registry. If you'd like to be affiliated with this brand, use this form to share your contact information with the current Administrator.	

This error message indicates that your brand is already enrolled in Brand Registry, and that you don’t have to enroll it again. Note the following:

Once a brand is approved for one store, it will be registered for all regions. You don’t have to enroll the brand for each store individually.
If you want to enroll a brand that is not part of your Brand Registry account, contact the Administrator of that brand to be assigned with a protection role. To do this, try to enroll the brand with a trademark registered in Brand Registry by entering your registration or serial number, click Verify, and click Use this form on the displayed error message.

We can't find this number in the trademark office that you selected. If you've confirmed the number and office are correct and still receive this message, continue with the rest of the application. Once submitted, we'll manually review your application, which may take additional time.	

This error occurs when we can't find your trademark in the selected office's database.

You received this error because of one or more of the following reasons:

• The trademark was recently registered, but it's not yet reflected on the site. Note: Some trademark offices may take 1-3 weeks to make records publicly searchable.

• The trademark registration/application number you entered is incorrect.

• The trademark office you selected is incorrect.

Ensure that you have entered the correct trademark registration/application number and selected the correct trademark office before re-enrolling.

If your trademark was recently filed and you've confirmed the number and office are correct but still receive this message, continue with the rest of the application. Once submitted, we'll manually review your application, which may take additional time

There was a problem while creating your brand application.

Note: The error includes an alphanumeric string which starts with ''REF'' and ends in ''-3".

	

This error message indicates that you had another browser tab or window with an open session of Seller Central or Brand Registry after you started to fill out the application in Brand Registry.

Close all of your browser tabs or windows, and create a new Brand Registry application to start the enrollment process for your brand.

Create a Brand Registry account

Error message	What it means and next steps
You indicated you're a new customer, but an account already exists with the email address [email].	

You've previously created an Amazon account in any Amazon store.

You’ll receive the Email address already in use error message if you attempt to use the same email address on more than one Amazon account.

If the error occurs when you’re trying to create a new Amazon account:

Attempt to sign into the Amazon account with your password. If you’ve forgotten your password, you can reset your password .
If you're still unable to sign in and you're certain you've never created an Amazon account using this email address, try to create an account using:
Another email address you own.
An email alias. Check with your email provider if they support creating an email alias with your email account.
Your mobile phone number.

For more information, go to Manage an email address already in use error .

To learn more about other error messages not listed above, go to Brand Registry error messages.

If your error still cannot be solved, contact us and select Technical issue or use Amazon's Seller Assistant.
