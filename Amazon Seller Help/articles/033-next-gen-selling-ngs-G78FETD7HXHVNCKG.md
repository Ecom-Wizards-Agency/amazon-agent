# Next Gen Selling (NGS)

Next Gen Selling (NGS) replaces previous friction from the classic Seller Central experience with a redesigned, simplified experience. With integrated workspaces, contextual recommendations, and visualized KPIs, NGS eliminates previous challenges with tab-switching, data re-entry, and menu hunting. The new Seller Central provides a personalized dashboard that surfaces what matters most to your business. Visualized KPIs (sales, conversion, ad spend) provide a comprehensive view of business performance, while timely alerts and contextual recommendations ensure critical actions are never overlooked. Instead of complex navigation, NGS anticipates brings the right information to you, so you spend less time searching and more time acting on opportunities.

Who uses NGS

If you’re new to Amazon and not already active in other Amazon stores under your account, you'll be redirected to NGS as your default selling experience after you register and are verified.

NGS is becoming the default selling experience for all sellers. As part of this transition, existing sellers will begin to see the new Seller Central as their default view when signing in. This transition will occur in phases across marketplaces.

If NGS isn’t your default experience currently, you can access it at any time. Scroll to the top of any Seller Central page and click the New Seller Central toggle. This will then open the Next Gen Selling experience.

Navigation in NGS

NGS uses a new navigation model that groups related actions and tools into workspaces, instead of relying on traditional top-level menus and drop-down menus. This layout helps you find tools faster and complete tasks more efficiently.

While the structure differs from the classic Seller Central experience, the core features and functionality remain the same. In the top navigation bar, common actions are organized under Manage and Create.

Use Manage to view or update listings, inventory, orders, or payments. Use Create to add products, set up deals or promotions, or launch campaigns. If you can’t find a specific page, you can use the search bar at the top of the page to locate it at any time.

Switching from or opting out of NGS

Only the primary seller account can change the default selling experience and opt out of Next Gen Selling. To switch your default experience back to classic Seller Central, follow these steps:

Go to the top right corner of any Next Gen Selling page.

Click the three vertical dots next to the Help icon.

Select Default selling experience and choose Seller Central.

Confirm the change when prompted.

To return to Next Gen Selling, scroll to the top of any Seller Central page and click the New Seller Central toggle.
Accessing classic Seller Central

Visiting classic Seller Central doesn’t opt you out of the new Seller Central interface. To go back to the classic Seller Central experience from the new version, follow these steps:

Go to the top right corner of any page.

Click the three vertical dots next to the Help icon.

Select Seller Central.

Classic Seller Central will open in a new tab.
