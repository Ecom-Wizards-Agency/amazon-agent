A+ Content enables brand owners and authorized resellers to enhance their product detail pages with rich marketing content, which includes enhanced product descriptions and brand storytelling.

This comprehensive tool helps you showcase product features, communicate brand value, and provide detailed information that helps customers make informed purchases. The A+ Content Manager provides a structured way to create, manage, and publish enhanced content across product listings.

A+ Content provides the following benefits:

Increases product discoverability and conversion rates through enhanced detail pages
Reduces customer questions and negative reviews by proactively providing detailed product information
Strengthens brand presence with consistent messaging and visual storytelling
Improves the customer experience with product content that’s optimized for both desktop and mobile viewing

Note: A+ Content is available at no additional cost for basic features. Premium features may have associated fees based on eligibility criteria. To avoid rejection and delays in publishing, review all content against our A+ Content guidelines before submission.

Types of A+ Content

We offer three distinct types of A+ Content for product detail pages:

Basic A+ Content appears in the product description section, and provides access to modules for custom headers, images, text layouts, and product comparison charts with a rich-text editor.

Premium A+ Content enhances the product description section with advanced features such as interactive hotspots, multiple videos, enhanced comparison charts, larger images, carousels, and Q&A modules.

A+ brand story content, which appears in the From the brand section, supports one module with up to 19 pre-formatted cards in a carousel format. It features full-screen backgrounds, image and text cards, and links to products and brand stores. Brand story can be used alongside either basic or premium A+ Content, though these features are not supported for Books, Music, Video, or DVD categories.

Create basic A+ Content:

Follow these steps to create basic A+ Content:

Go to Advertising in the Vendor Central menu, select A+ Content Manager, and then click Start creating A+ content.
Name your content project, select the language, and choose the content modules. Be sure to save your progress frequently.
To add your content, enter product descriptions in the text fields and upload images to Media Library. Ensure you add alt-text for each image. Preview the content using the desktop and mobile views.
To apply the content, first search for ASIN families, and then select the target ASINs.
You can use bulk upload for more than 1,000 ASINs (10,000-ASIN limit per upload).

Create premium A+ Content

Follow these steps to create premium A+ Content (if eligible):

Verify your eligibility. Go to the A+ Content Manager and check the status of your submitted projects and the last published date. You must have the following to be eligible:
A published brand story for all catalog ASINs
Five or more approved A+ Content projects in past 12 months
Set up any enhanced features, which includes the following:
Configure interactive hotspots
Add multiple video modules
Create enhanced comparison charts
Set up carousel modules
Implement Q&A sections

After submission, the review period may take up to seven business days, and publication will be complete within 24 hours after approval. Content will only be viewable on language-specific pages. We recommend that you maintain copies of all submissions for reference.

Guidelines and best practices

To ensure your content is of the highest quality, we recommend the following:

Include unique content that doesn't duplicate the image gallery.
Focus on product features and benefits rather than promotional language.
Make sure all content adheres to your brand’s aesthetic and voice, while remaining professional.

For images, ensure that you upload high-resolution files (minimum 72 dpi), submit in RGB color space (.jpg, .bmp, or .png formats), and keep files under 2MB each.

Publication best practices

We recommend that you take the following actions when you publish your content:

Give the content a final review, and ensure that you check the applied ASINs, verify language variations, and review all content in the preview mode.
After you submit your content, allow up to seven business days for approval. Be sure to monitor the approval status and address any rejection reasons.
After your content is published, ensure the following within 24 hours:
All ASINs are live and display correctly
Confirm language variations
Test all interactive elements

We recommend that you take the following documentation and maintenance steps:

Record all applied ASINs.
Document any custom modules.
Save template configurations.
Schedule regular content reviews.

Note: A maximum of 20 pending submissions are allowed at once. Save your work frequently as drafts to prevent loss of progress.

Troubleshooting steps

If your content does not display, take these steps:

Verify that the content status is Approved.
Check the ASIN status under Applied ASINs.
Confirm that the language selection is correct.
Ensure that you’ve waited 24 hours after approval
If the content is still not visible, contact Selling Partner Support or use Amazon's Seller Assistant.

If your content is rejected, take these steps:

Review the rejection reasons in A+ Content Manager.
Check against the A+ Content guidelines listed above.
Make the required corrections.
Resubmit for approval.

Note: A+ Content features are not yet supported for products sold through Seller Central in the Books, Music, Video, or DVD (BMVD) categories. A+ Content is not the same as Amazon Business enhanced content, which is a tool that lets you upload separate file documents to the detail page.
