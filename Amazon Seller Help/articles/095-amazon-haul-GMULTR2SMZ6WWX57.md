# Amazon Haul

Note: For now, Amazon Haul is operating on an invitation-only basis. If you want to be added to the interest list, contact interested-sellers@amazon.com

Amazon Haul is a dedicated shopping experience in applicable Amazon stores that allows customers to shop low-priced fashion, home, and lifestyle products. You can sell your low-priced selection in the product types that are listed in Selection allowed in Amazon Haul.

Products that are sold in Amazon Haul are offered in their own shopping experience. This new experience provides visibility for your low-cost selection, making it easier for customers to find and shop for these products.

Amazon Haul orders are fulfilled by Amazon. Inventory is often stored in a fulfillment center that is operated by one of our third-party logistics service providers located in China.

Amazon Haul Direct Fulfillment leverages Haul-dedicated fulfillment centers to deliver purchases directly to customers, bypassing storage in local Amazon fulfillment networks.

Amazon Haul Distributed Fulfillment is a program within Amazon Haul that extends visibility for ASINs enrolled in FBA and stored within the local Amazon fulfillment network to customers in the Amazon Haul store. Amazon Haul Distributed Fulfillment ASINs have inventory stored within the Amazon Fulfillment Network of the region of purchase, and orders are fulfilled from this domestically stored inventory.

Note: Seller fulfillment is not yet available.

Amazon Haul is currently available in the stores listed on the Haul program page. We will inform you about the launch of new stores. For each of your products, Amazon will evaluate which product will be made available in the new stores. We may request you to provide further information (for example, compliance documentation, updated packaging) to enable us to sell your product in other stores.
