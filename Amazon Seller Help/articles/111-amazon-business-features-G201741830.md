# Amazon Business features


Amazon Business provides access to exclusive benefits and features to help grow your sales to business customers.

The following features are available to sellers as part of Amazon Business:

Amazon Tax Exemption program
Business-Only Offers
Business Prices and Quantity Discounts
Manage quotes
Business-only selections
Feeds
Order reports
Payment options
Seller Credential program
Fee discounts
