FBA Enrollment Opportunities is a tool that analyzes your catalog and recommends which ASINs to enroll in FBA.

How FBA Enrollment Opportunities work

FBA Enrollment Opportunities can help you identify which ASINs to enroll in which FBA programs. The tool uses a machine-learning system to identify products in your catalog that will benefit from an FBA program.

To improve our tools, we encourage you to provide feedback.

Benefits of FBA Enrollment Opportunities

The recommendations tool is designed to help guide your selling decisions. Recommendations can help you find key information about an ASIN, such as the price of the Featured Offer.

Our machine-learning system is constantly building on existing data to offer increasingly accurate and comprehensive recommendations most of the time.

Limitations of recommendations

If you find errors in your recommendations, contact Selling Partner Support or use Amazon's Seller Assistant. We read every comment and use your feedback to guide our improvement efforts.

Find more data

Because we can't access your cost data, recommendations don't factor in your costs. Therefore, in response to feedback from sellers like you, we've made it possible to download all the data points for each of your recommendations. Note that higher costs for certain channels may be offset by an increase in sales.

If you would like to access the FBA revenue calculator, click into the product dimensions listed under the column "FBA fees". It will redirect you to the integrated FBA revenue calculator, which allows for a comprehensive view of fulfillment costs. In the revenue calculator, you can also compare your fulfillment channel costs versus FBA, forecast costs for the holiday season and non-holiday season months.

When to act on recommendations

We recommend you act on recommendations at the time that they’re listed. If you wait to act, make sure to go back and check that the ASIN is still recommended for the respective program.

Use FBA Opportunities
Go to Inventory and select Opportunities

On the FBA Enrollment Opportunities page, the following options are displayed.
Recommendations
All Products

Select > to view the analysis for the required product’s past performance and opportunities for up to the past 12 months. You can also view customer ratings, best sellers rank, competing offers, featured offer, and applicable promotion conditions.

To download the data for your recommendations, select Download all results on the top right.

To enroll ASINs into FBA, select the required product. Under the Action column, select Enroll in FBA. You can also select multiple ASINs and select Enroll Multiple at the top right of the recommendation list.

PanEU FBA sales forecast

This resource is intended for international expansion in German, Spanish, French and Italian stores only. It provides a six-month sales forecast and ASIN availability for fulfillment through these Amazon stores. You can also enroll in the program directly from the FBA Enrollment Opportunities page:

If your account is eligible for Pan European FBA (PanEU) and hasn't yet enabled at least two of the following countries (Germany, Spain, Italy and France): select the View PanEU settings (opening in a new tab) option and enable any 2 of EU4 stores.
If your account is eligible for PanEU: enroll by selecting the Enroll Now option. On the next page, by checking the small box, you can enroll in Build International Listing (BIL) for new destinations and continue with enrollment.

Frequently asked questions
How does the 'Why this ASIN' enhanced explainability feature work?

Click the 'Why this ASIN' option to open a side panel that provides instant visibility into the recommendation logic. The panel shows detailed ASIN analysis including Prime vs. self-fulfilled performance comparisons, speed and visibility advantages through FBA, and competitive data - all without leaving your current page. This enhanced view goes beyond basic rationales (Delivery Promise, High Demand, Low Competition) by providing specific performance metrics, historical data, and market analysis that support each recommendation.

How can I apply additional filters to my recommendations?

There are four ways to further categorize your recommendations:

The first way is by going to the recommendation type drop-down and selecting the filter of your choice.
The second way is filtering by Brand Name in the following drop-down menu on the page.
The third way is by filtering only by recommendations that meet the "Promotion Eligible" criteria.
The fourth way is by selecting the "All Filters" tab on the right to include all desired filters at once.

How do I provide feedback on an ASIN?

Select one or more ASINs and click Feedback on the upper right-hand corner.

How often do recommendations get updated?

Recommendations are refreshed daily on the FBA Enrollment Opportunities page. Make sure to download the data file for each new set of recommendations.

How do I use the "Potential sales lift" column?

The Potential sales lift for a recommendation estimates the incremental sales you could have gained over 90 days if you had taken this action, on this product, 90 days ago. It is based on a wide range of factors, including but not limited to seasonality, selling price, and existing fulfillment solution.

What kind of information am I looking for in the "All Products" tab?

Until now, FBA Enrollment Opportunities only displayed recommended ASINs. You can now view the entire MFN (merchant-fulfillment network) catalog to find recommendations, promotions and selection for FBA eligibility. You can also perform detailed analysis of your entire catalog for FBA enrollment.
