You can now access the Compliance Services Store (CSS), a network of third-party compliance service providers on Service Hub, Sell Globally, PanEU Portal, and account health dashboard (AHD). These help you explore, compare, and connect with experts who can provide you with third-party services to meet local obligations when selling in Europe.

Compliance Services Store on Service Hub

Through Service Hub you can discover, compare, and connect with experts who can provide you with services to meet local obligations when expanding into Spain, France, Germany, Italy, Sweden, Poland, Netherlands, Belgium or United Kingdom.

Available services:

VAT (Value Added Tax) Registration and Filing
Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) registration and filing

Access compliance services on Service Hub:

Sign into Seller Central.
Go to the Service Hub page.
Select the region you want to sell in the drop down.
Select country you want to sell in the drop down.
Select the services you want to your compliance requirements.
Select confirm to view packages.
Select learn more to select packages.
Select continue to view proceed to contact collection.
Submit contact information (name, phone, and email)

Third-party service providers should respond within two business days. After you sign up on Seller Central, you will pay the third-party service provider directly.

Manage your services:

Monitor compliance status directly in Service Hub page
Receive automatic updates on order completion
View detailed status for services

Compliance Services Store on Sell Globally

If you are already selling in the United States or Canada, go to Sell Globally North America page to discover third-party service provider options for your expansion to United Kingdom, Germany, France, Italy, and Spain. When selecting a third-party service provider, you can filter based on the countries you plan to operate in to find a suitable provider.

You can benefit from competitively priced bundles for Amazon sellers, including five services for up to 20 ASINs:

Value Added Tax (VAT) registration and filing
Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) registration and filing
EU Responsible Person (RsP)
Listing translation
Labeling

Access CSS on Sell Globally at Seller Central:

Sign in to Seller Central.
Select Inventory, then select Sell globally.
Go to the Launch in Europe widget and select Explore plans.
Select View service packages.
Review and select providers.
Submit contact information (name, phone, and email).

Third-party service providers should respond within two business days. After you sign up on Seller Central, you will pay the third-party service provider directly.

Manage your services:

Monitor compliance status directly in Sell Globally page
Receive automatic updates on order completion
View detailed status for services

Compliance Services Store on PanEU Portal

If you are selling in at least one store in Europe, you can now access a network of third-party service providers directly through your Pan-EU Portal. This integration helps you discover, compare, and connect with experts who can provide you with services to meet local obligations when expanding into Spain, France, Germany, Italy, Sweden, Poland, Netherlands, Belgium, or United Kingdom.

Available services:

Value Added Tax (VAT) registration and filing
Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) registration and filing

Access CSS on PanEU Portal:

Sign into Seller Central.
Go to the Pan-EU Portal Portal and select Marketplace enrollment.
Select Fix issues on compliance alerts.
Choose your remediation path:

If you have a VAT/EPR number, select update VAT to enter your existing information.
If you need help with how to obtain a VAT/EPR number, select Explore plans to obtain VAT or Explore plans to obtain ERN.
Compare list of services and providers.
Select Learn more to see the provider detail page.
Select Continue to proceed with the sign-up process.
Submit contact information (name, phone, and email).

Third-party service providers should respond within two business days. After you sign up on Seller Central, you will pay the third-party service provider directly.

Manage your services:

Monitor compliance status directly in the Pan-EU Portal
Receive automatic updates on completed orders
View detailed status for services

Additionally, you can monitor your compliance status directly in the Pan-EU Portal. At the bottom of the “Tax Information” section, you will find an informative text, indicating Click here to check status. The system automatically updates when third-party service providers complete services.

CSS on account health dashboard

If you have an active Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) compliance issue in your account health dashboard (AHD), you can now access compliance services directly from the issue alert. This new remediation path helps you discover, compare, and connect with third-party service providers who can help you resolve your compliance issues and meet local regulatory obligations when selling in Europe.

Available services:

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) registration and filing

Access CSS on AHD:

Go to your AHD in Seller Central.
Locate your EPR or EU Responsible Person compliance issue under Product Policy Compliance.
Select Submit on the compliance alert to open the Compliance Services widget.
Choose your remediation option:

Select Do you already have an EPR registration number (ERN)? to enter your existing EPR registration number (ERN).
Select Do you need help getting an EPR registration number (ERN)? to discover independent providers who can help you obtain an EPR registration number (ERN).
Explore available service providers and packages.
Select learn more to view third-party service provider details, pricing, and services.
Select Continue to proceed with the sign-up process.
Confirm your contact information (name, phone, and email) and select Continue.
Review your order confirmation, your selected third-party service provider, services covered, and total plan cost.

Third-party service providers should respond within two business days. After you sign up on Seller Central, you will pay the third-party service provider directly.

Manage your services:

Monitor compliance resolution status directly in the account health dashboard
View detailed status for your compliance issue resolution

Service costs:

Service costs vary among providers across European marketplaces. You can view and compare individual service pricing and multi-country bundles before making any commitment. You can view real-time pricing updates as you customize your selection and clearly distinguishing between one-time and recurring fees. This transparency helps you choose options that best fit your budget and expansion needs. Service fees will be collected and invoices will be shared directly by the third-party service provider.

Frequently asked questions

What is Amazon’s role in the Compliance Services Store?

Amazon provides the interface and technology to help sellers explore and connect with a network of third-party service providers. While we do not directly offer these services, it enables easier access and discovery of third-party service provider. Services are provided by third-party service providers and are subject to terms and conditions.

Who are the third-party service providers, and what is their role?

Amazon connects sellers directly with third-party service providers who support specific regulatory needs. Once you choose a third-party service provider, you will contract directly with them for their services.

The third-party service provider services will include but will not be limited to the following:

Determine which documents need to be submitted to the relevant authorities
Ensure all required documents are complete
Review information and documents for accuracy and completeness
Submit necessary forms and provide requested services to complete the process

Third-party service providers handle these tasks to simplify your operations and ensure compliance.

Can I switch third-party service providers after I have subscribed to one?

Yes, you can switch, however, there might be a transfer cost hence it’s important to review the cancellation or transfer policies set by your current provider, as they may have specific terms regarding switching or contract duration. As this is an annual subscription, opting out mid-way may not make you eligible for a refund. We recommend checking with your third-party service provider before making any changes.

What happens if my third-party service provider exits the Amazon program?

If you have signed up with VAT, EPR, or RsP through one of the channels mentioned on this help page, we will communicate to you in advance if your third-party service provider is exiting and ensure a smooth transition to a new third-party service provider.

What documents do I need to provide when using compliance services?

The required documents depend on the compliance service and your target destination store. Generally, third-party service provider may request business registration certificates, tax identification numbers, and transaction records. Each third-party service provider will specify the exact documents needed before service confirmation.

How can I track my order status?

Select Go to Order Tracking to view your order status.

Why am I receiving an email asking for feedback and will my service provider see my individual feedback?

You're receiving this email because you've recently used services from a third-party provider through our Compliance Services Store, Sell Globally, or PanEU Portal. We value your experience and want to ensure providers are meeting seller expectations. Providers receive aggregated feedback trends and ratings, and your feedback will be published anonymously on our pages to help other sellers to make informed decisions.
