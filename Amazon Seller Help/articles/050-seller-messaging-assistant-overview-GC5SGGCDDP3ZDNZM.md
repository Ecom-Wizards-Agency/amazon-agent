Introducing the Seller Messaging Assistant (SMA)

The Seller Messaging Assistant (SMA) is a conversational assistant that helps buyers quickly get instant answers to common questions about their orders and products. It uses Amazon's systems to provide instant, relevant information across a range of inquiry types. SMA is designed to improve the pre-purchase and post-purchase experience for buyers and reduce repetitive contacts for sellers.

How SMA helps buyers

SMA helps buyers get answers to their most common questions, both before and after making a purchase. For pre-order questions, SMA can pull information like Product Details, Warranty coverage, Return policies, and Pricing directly from Amazon and any seller-provided data. For post-order inquiries, it handles topics like Where's my stuff ?, Order cancellations, Returns, Invoices, and Product information.

How SMA benefits sellers

For you, SMA reduces the time and effort you must manage your buyer messages by automatically resolving repeatable inquiries. It lowers contact volume and response burden, especially for high-frequency topics like delivery updates and return eligibility. You can provide a faster experience to buyers while focusing on more complex or high-value interactions. SMA acts as the first layer of support in the Buyer-Seller Messaging system, providing a consistent and fast experience for buyers across supported topics. By resolving contacts before they reach you, it helps avoid unnecessary back-and-forth and ensures messages that do reach you are more actionable. This leads to a better experience for both parties and improves overall efficiency.

How Buyers access SMA

Buyers can interact with SMA from multiple touchpoints across the Amazon shopping experience. These include the 'Contact Seller' in Order Details, Your Order page, and the Seller Profile page where they can ask questions about an item before buying or an order they've placed. In each case, SMA attempts to answer the buyer's question automatically and only escalates to you when necessary.

Topics SMA Supports

SMA supports a growing set of topics, using information pulled from various Amazon systems and seller configurations. For pre-order inquiries, it can provide answers about product descriptions, compatibility, warranties, shipping timelines, and return windows. For post-order inquiries, it supports shipment tracking, delivery status, cancellations, return initiation, invoice downloads, and more.

How sellers can configure SMA

Learn more about how you can use SMA by Configuring Auto-Responses in SMA to match your specific products and services, and provide Buyers with tailored customer support. By investing in clear and complete configurations, you can enable SMA to handle inquiries successfully on your behalf.
