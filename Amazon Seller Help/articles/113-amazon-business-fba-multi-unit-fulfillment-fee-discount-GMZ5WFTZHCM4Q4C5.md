# Amazon Business FBA multi-unit fulfillment fee discount

On this page

Amazon Business gives you the opportunity to save on FBA fulfillment fees for multi-unit business orders with qualifying business prices or quantity discounts. This program is available to all Amazon sellers on a Professional selling plan who have offers that meet the eligibility requirements.

For more information, including methods for managing business prices and quantity discounts in your listings, go to Business prices and quantity discounts.

FBA fulfillment fee discount eligibility

This program is applicable to Amazon sellers on a Professional selling plan who use Fulfillment by Amazon.

The fee discount is available for small-standard and large-standard size tiers. Large bulky and extra-large products are excluded. For more information about product size tiers, go to Product size tiers.

The fee discount applies only on purchases made by Amazon Business customers. For information on how to identify business orders, go to Amazon Business frequently asked questions.

To qualify for the fee discount, you must provide a 3% or greater discount off the standard price using business pricing, quantity discounts, or both. The business price or quantity discount must apply to the quantity ordered. For example, a 3% quantity discount on orders of 10 or more units will qualify for the fee discount on an order of 12 units but will not qualify for reduced fees on an order of 5 units.

The required 3% discount off the standard price can be met with a 3% or greater business price discount, a 3% or greater quantity discount for the quantity ordered, or a combination of business pricing and quantity discount that results in a total 3% or greater discount off the standard price.

FBA fulfillment fee discount

For qualifying orders, the per-unit fulfillment fee discount amount is shown in the following table. The fee discount amount for each qualifying product is based on the product’s size band and the quantity of that product purchased in a single order. The final fulfillment fee amount charged will depend on the applicable standard FBA fulfillment fee rate for the product plus any other applicable fulfillment fee discounts.

View the discounted FBA fulfillment fee
Follow these steps to see the fulfillment fee discount for a transaction:
Sign into Seller Central.

Go to Reports, Payments, and then Transaction view.

Click on the number in the Total column of the transaction.

The total fulfillment fee discount appears in the line, "Discount on FBA per unit Pick & Pack fee." To find the per-unit fee discount amount, divide this value by the Item quantity value.
