What is PanAmericas?

PanAmericas bridges the gap between Remote Fulfillment and full in-country operations, giving you in-country FBA performance without the complexity and capital requirements of traditional cross-border expansion.

Proactive inventory transfer leads to faster delivery times. We handle imports from US to Canadian Fulfillment Centers, enabling Prime badging for eligible products.
Transform your cross-border selling experience. Reach Canadian customers with the same ease as the US store while we manage logistics, customs clearance, and duty payments.
Hands-free logistics and placement of inventory. Focus on growth, while we handle the complexity.

Why do sellers choose PanAmericas?

4-8x potential sales lift: On average, sellers have seen 4-8x potential sales lift for ASINs in-country FBA compared to ASINs enrolled in Remote Fulfillment during the same period. Faster eligibility windows and Prime Badge eligibility help you increase sales and drive repeat purchases.
Lower fees than Remote Fulfillment with FBA: Expand into Canada's $40B+ ecommerce market with fulfillment fees lower than Remote Fulfillment with FBA. PanAmericas combines the conversion benefits of domestic fulfillment with competitive pricing that improves your bottom line.
80% Amazon handles: Focus on developing products and delighting customers instead of managing cross-border complexity, low risk with no Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) requirements. Amazon handles 80% of the operational work.

How PanAmericas works

Simple signup. Automatic transfers. Seamless fulfillment.

*Check eligibility of high demand ASINs.
*Enroll your products and complete requirements. Accept the program policy, register as an NRI, sign up for CARM and check inventory forecasting.
Amazon handles the rest. We automatically transfer inventory, clear customs, and fulfill orders from Canadian Fulfillment Centers. In transit products are available for sale.

*These features are coming soon.

No upfront duty payments. Amazon handles custom duties and taxes when your inventory crosses the border, then recovers these costs with a pass-through chargeback fee when your products sell. You can opt out any eligible items from the program at any time.

PanAmericas fees

Simplify cross-border selling with competitive, transparent fulfillment fees.

When you sell through the PanAmericas program, you'll pay two types of fees:

PanAmericas Fulfillment Fee: Instead of US FBA fees, you will be charged the PanAmericas fee at the time of sale.
Chargeback Fee: It's not an extra charge; you're just reimbursing Amazon for the import duties costs to get your inventory into Canada quickly.

PanAmericas Chargeback Fee

What is the chargeback fee?

Amazon pays customs duties upfront when your inventory enters Canada. Once your products sell, Amazon recovers these duty costs through the chargeback fee. GST is handled separately and recovered directly from the Canadian government.

Example

ASIN B0001: Wireless Bluetooth Headset

Each product receives an HTS (Harmonized Tariff Schedule) code that determines the duty rate. For this example:

HTS Code: 8518.30 (headphones and earphones, whether or not combined with a microphone)
Duty Rate: 4.9%
Cost of Goods: $75.00 (trailing 30-day list price)

Calculation: Chargeback Fee = Cost of Goods × Duty Rate $75.00 × 4.9% = $3.68 per unit

When each unit sells, the Net Proceeds pricing rule adds $3.68 to the offer price which is applied as a chargeback fee.*

Enrolling in the program

To participate in PanAmericas, you'll need to complete these one-time steps:

Step 1: Check eligibility

You must not be registered for Goods and Services Tax (GST) in Canada.
