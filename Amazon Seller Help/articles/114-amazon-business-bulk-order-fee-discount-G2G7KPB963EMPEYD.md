Amazon Business gives you the opportunity to save on high-value business orders with qualifying business prices or quantity discounts. This program is available to all Amazon sellers on a Professional selling plan who have offers that meet the eligibility requirements.

For more information, including methods for managing business prices and quantity discounts in your listings, go to Business prices and quantity discounts.

Bulk order fee discount eligibility

This program is applicable to Amazon sellers on a Professional selling plan.

The fee discount is available for the products listed in the "Bulk order discounted fee rates" table below.

The fee discount applies only on purchases made by Amazon Business customers, where the order value for a single product is greater than $1000 (item price multiplied by quantity ordered). For information on how to identify business orders, go to Amazon Business Frequently Asked Questions.

To qualify for the fee discount, you must provide a 3% or greater discount off the standard price using business pricing, quantity discounts, or both. The business price or quantity discount must apply to the quantity ordered. For example, a 3% quantity discount on orders of 10 or more units will qualify for the fee discount on an order of 12 units but will not qualify for reduced fees on an order of 5 units.

The required 3% discount off the standard price can be met with a 3% or greater business price discount, a 3% or greater quantity discount for the quantity ordered, or a combination of business pricing and quantity discount that results in a total 3% or greater discount off the standard price.

Request for Quote orders do not require business pricing or quantity discounts to qualify for the fee discount.

Bulk order fee discount

For qualifying orders, standard referral fee rates are applied to the portion of the product order value up to $1000, then discounted referral fee rates are applied to the portion of the product order value above $1000 (if lower than standard referral fee rate).

Discounted referral fee rates are listed in the following table, based on the product’s fee category. Products in fee categories not listed in the table are ineligible for this fee discount program. Product order value is based on the item price multiplied by the ordered quantity for an individual product and in a single order.

Fee calculation example

Fee category: Office Products

Standard referral fee rate: 15% of total sales price

Item price: $250.00

Quantity ordered: 500

Product order value = Item price multiplied by quantity ordered = $125,000

Standard referral fee amount = product order value multiplied by fee rate = $18,750

Bulk order referral fee calculation:

Sales price	Referral fee rate	Example referral fee calculation
For the portion of the product order value up to $1,000.00	15%	$1,000 × 15% = $150
For the portion of the product order value from $1,000.01 to $5,000.00	12%	$4,000 × 12% = $480
For the portion of the product order value from $5,000.01 to $10,000.00	10%	$5,000 × 10% = $500
For the portion of the product order value from $10,000.01 to $50,000.00	7%	$40,000 × 7% = $2,800
For the portion of the product order value from $50,000.01 to $100,000.00	6%	$50,000 × 6% = $3,000
For any portion of the product order value from $100,000.01 and above	5%	$25,000 × 5% = $1,250
Referral fee amount (actual fee charged)	$8,180

Bulk order fee discount amount = $18,750 - $8,180 = $10,570

View the discounted referral fee

Follow these steps to see the referral fee discount for a transaction:

Sign into Seller Central.

Go to Reports, Payments, and then Transaction view.

Click on the number in the Total column of the transaction.

The total referral fee discount amount will be displayed in the line 'Discount on referral fee,' or may appear in multiple lines including 'Discount on shipping commission' and 'Discount on gift wrap commission' for applicable orders. For detailed fee calculation, click on any discount value.
Note: If the order qualifies for additional referral fee discounts, those may also be included in the ‘Discount on Referral Fee on Item Price’ line, which may increase the total discount amount.
