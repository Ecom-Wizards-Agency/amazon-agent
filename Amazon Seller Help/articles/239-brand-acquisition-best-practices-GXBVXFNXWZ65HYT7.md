Brand acquisition best practices

Learn how to manage Administrator permissions, roles, and brand protection during brand acquisitions to ensure a smooth transition process.

Follow these guidelines to manage Administrator permissions, role assignments, and brand benefits during brand acquisitions. These protocols ensure a smooth transition between previous and new brand Administrators.

Administrator transfer protocol

Transfer Administrator permissions through Brand Registry's User permissions before completing the brand acquisition. This ensures continuous access to brand management tools. If the previous Administrator did not assign roles, refer to the Brand Registry FAQ: The current Administrator has left the company or is no longer associated with the brand. How can I update the Administrator?

Managing brand roles

Brand Administrators must understand both protection and selling roles, as each includes distinct permissions and benefits. Conduct regular role audits to maintain current permissions and prevent unauthorized access. This helps preserve brand integrity across sales channels and protects your market position.

Maintaining trademark registration

Review your trademarks in Brand Registry through Managing intellectual properties to verify their registration status. Current trademark registration ensures continued access to brand protection tools and benefits. Verify ownership information updates with country-specific IP offices following brand acquisitions.
