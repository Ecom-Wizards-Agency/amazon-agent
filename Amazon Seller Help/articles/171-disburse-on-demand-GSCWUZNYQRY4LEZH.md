# Disburse on Demand


Disburse on Demand lets you request a disbursement of your available funds once every 24 hours. You can choose between your automated settlement cycle or Disburse on Demand to access funds in your account balance.

Request a disbursement

In Seller Central, select Payments dashboard.
In the Funds Available box, select Pay Now or Disburse Now.
Your disbursement request will be processed.
Note: Disburse on Demand lets you request disbursement of your account balance. However, delivery date reserves process normally, with funds moving to your account balance after delivery date plus 7 days.

Troubleshooting Pay now

If you don't see the Pay now or Disburse now button, verify that:

Your account has funds available for disbursement.
You haven't requested a disbursement in the last 24 hours.
You don't have a disbursement in process.

To view your past and current disbursements:

Go to the Payments dashboard.
Select Disbursements.
Note: If your account has reserve settings, you are still eligible for Disburse on Demand. Contact Selling Partner Support or use Amazon's Seller Assistant to review your account's reserve settings. Reserve settings are specific to each store.
