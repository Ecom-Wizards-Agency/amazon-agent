# Role assignment error messages


These are the most common error messages you can encounter during the protection or selling role assignment process and how to solve them.

Adding a role

Error message	What it means and next steps
This role invitation is under review. We appreciate your patience as we work to complete this review. Please note that you will not be able to withdraw the invitation during this time.	

This role invitation requires additional review and cannot be withdrawn until the review is completed.

You will be notified by email once the review is complete.

Some of my brands are not visible on Manage selling roles or Connect a selling partner account.	We will gradually expand Manage access to Vendor Central accounts to all brands. Access to some of your brands may not be available yet, but will become available as the rollout progresses.
You already have an open invitation for this email address. Please wait for the user to respond before sending another invitation.	

This indicates that there is an already open invitation for the user that you're trying to assign to a protection role. Resend the invitation link to that user or wait for the user to respond to your invitation before sending a new one.

In case you own both Brand Registry accounts (the Administrator and the to-be-added user), log out and ensure you log in using the credentials of the user that you want to add.

You can review your open invitations in Brand Registry by going to User permissions and selecting Open invitations.

The email is already connected to one of your brands. You can add permissions to other brands in Manage user permissions.

You already have a protection role in this brand.

If you are the Administrator of the brand, go to User permissions to manage existing protection roles.

If you intended to receive selling benefits, go to Manage selling accounts to assign a selling role to your seller account, or to assign a role to another seller.

If you are not the Administrator of the brand, please get in contact with them to receive a protection or selling role as needed.

Your request to be added as an authorized user cannot be processed at this time because your account requires additional review. Once the review is complete you will be notified of our decision. Please expect a response within 5 to 7 calendar days.	

Brand Registry must review your account before you can be assigned a Brand Registry protection role.

Once the review has been completed, you'll be notified of the decision. The review will take between five to seven calendar days.

To review your case log for updates, go to the case dashboard.

Your user addition request has been submitted. Our team is reviewing the request and will reach out to you if additional information is needed.
Your connection requests for this brand have reached the maximum limit. Please contact Brand Registry support with error code 6790, your merchant token, and the brand name you are trying to connect.	We cannot increase or remove the limit for selling benefits invitations. The limit automatically resets at the start of each month. Once reset, you can connect additional sellers on Brand Registry.
This brand has been locked by Amazon. Please contact your account manager for support to make your requested changes.	This indicates that a protection role cannot be assigned in your brand without the help of your Amazon account manager. Contact your account manager for support to make your requested changes.
Your request to add an authorized user cannot be processed at this time. Your account requires additional review before you can invite users to the brand. Once the review is complete you will be notified of our decision. Please expect a response within three to five calendar days.	Brand Registry is currently reviewing your account. This review must be completed before you can invite users to the brand. Once the review has been completed, you'll be notified of the decision.

To learn more about other error messages not listed above, go to Brand Registry error messages.

If your error still cannot be solved, contact us and select Technical issue or use Amazon's Seller Assistant.
