# Amazon Lending Automated Repayment terms

Automated Repayment Terms

When you accepted the financing agreement (“Financing Agreement”) governing a loan or other financial product you obtained via Amazon Lending (“Financing”) that required use of the Automated Repayment Service, you agreed to be bound by this agreement (the “Automated Repayment Terms”). These Automated Repayment Terms apply to your use of the Automated Repayment Service provided by Amazon Payments, Inc. (“API”) which is described more fully on the Seller Facing FAQs in Seller Central and in your Financing Agreement. These Automated Repayment Terms supplement the Amazon Services Business Solutions Agreement (the “BSA”) and any other Amazon seller services agreement that governs your offering of products for purchase through www.Amazon.com (the “Site”). All capitalized terms used but not defined in these Automated Repayment Terms have the meanings assigned in the BSA.

Descriptionof Automated Repayment Service. The Automated Repayment Service allows you to make payments (each, a “Financing Payment”) directly from your Sales Proceeds to the provider of the Financing (the “Provider”). Enrollment in the Automated Repayment Service may not be available for all Financings and the Provider will inform you if your Financing qualifies for the Automated Repayment Service.
Enrollment. You enroll in the Automated Repayment Service by selecting a Financing that requires use of the Automated Repayment Service and executing the Financing Agreement.
Irrevocability. The Automated Repayment Service is an optional service provided to you by API, but you acknowledge and agree that once enrolled the Automated Repayment Service is irrevocable for the term of the Financing Agreement and that your enrollment in the Automated Repayment Service enabled the Provider to offer you better financing terms under the Financing Agreement. When your Financing Agreement terminates, your enrollment in the Automated Repayment Service will automatically terminate.
Financing Payments

Financing Agreement Information. When you enter into the Financing Agreement and throughout the term of the Financing, the Provider will send us your Financing Agreement information; including (i) the Financing Payment amounts (which may be fixed or variable and may include past due payments, late fees, or other charges), (ii) the Financing Payment due dates, (iii) outstanding principal balance, interest, and finance charges, if any, and (iv) any other material terms of or changes to the Financing Agreement.
Timing of Financing Payments. At the time your Sales Proceeds are scheduled for disbursement, if you have any Financing Payments due, we will transfer all or some of your Sales Proceeds (if any) to the Provider. If your Sales Proceeds exceed the amount of your Financing Payments then due, we will disburse the remaining Sales Proceeds to your Bank Account. If your Sales Proceeds are less than or equal to the amount of your Financing Payments then due, we will transfer the entirety of your Sales Proceeds to the Provider and we will not disburse any Sales Proceeds to your Bank Account. You will be solely responsible for ensuring the Provider receives a full and complete Financing Payment during any payment cycle. If you do not have Sales Proceeds on any applicable disbursement date, or any Sales Proceeds disbursements are insufficient to pay a Financing Payment (“Incomplete Financing Payment”), you must pay any amount that remains outstanding directly to the Provider. API has no responsibility to make a complete Financing Payment and no liability with respect to any fees or charges incurred due to a partial payment or non-payment of a Financing Payment. If you made an Incomplete Financing Payment through the Automated Repayment Service and do not make any additional payments directly to the Provider, your next Financing Payment may include late fees and any past due portion of your Financing Payments.
Making Financing Payments Outside of the Automated Repayment Service. If you make an Incomplete Financing Payment or if you want to make any unscheduled Financing Payments (including paying off your Financing), you must coordinate with and directly pay the Provider. The Automated Repayment Service allows you to make Financing Payments on the Seller Proceeds disbursement dates. You cannot use the Automated Repayment Service to make any payments separate from those disbursement dates. You are solely responsible for ensuring the Provider receives a full and complete Financing Payment during each Financing Agreement payment cycle.
Late Fees. If your Financing Payment due dates and your scheduled disbursement dates for your Sales Proceeds do not fall on the same day, your Financing Agreement should include a grace period to ensure that you are not charged a late fee, if such Financing Agreement includes late fees, for the period between your Financing Payment due date and your next scheduled disbursement date for your Sales Proceeds as long as you pay the full outstanding amount before the end of that grace period. For example, if your Financing Payment due date is the first of the month and the next scheduled disbursement date for your Sales Proceeds is on the tenth of that month, your Financing Payment will be made on the tenth and you will not incur a late fee as long as your Sales Proceeds are sufficient to pay the full outstanding amount due.
Errors in Financing Payments. If you believe a Financing Payment made through the Automated Repayment Service was (i) calculated incorrectly, (ii) paid before the due date, or (iii) otherwise inaccurate, immediately notify us at lending-partner@amazon.com.
Payment History. When you make a Financing Payment through the Automated Repayment Service, we will update the Financing page on Seller Central to reflect the amount and timing of the Financing Payment. We will also make available on Seller Central summaries of all Financing Payments made through the Automated Repayment Service. Except as required by Law, you are solely responsible for compiling and retaining records of all transactions and other data associated with your use of the Automated Repayment Service. You will notify us of any error in your Financing Payments transaction history within 60 days of the date that a transaction appears (or you believe should have appeared).
Limits. We may delay, suspend or reject a Financing Payment in our reasonable discretion, including, without limitation, if we suspect any transaction subjects us to financial or security risk or is unauthorized, fraudulent, suspicious, unlawful, in violation of these Automated Repayment Terms or the Financing Agreement, subject to dispute, or otherwise unusual. We will not be liable for any loss you may incur if we exercise these rights, or for compensation, reimbursement, or damages of any kind in connection with any suspension or termination of the Automated Repayment Service.
Your Authorization. You authorize us:

To receive Financing Agreement information from the Provider and rely on such information when processing your Financing Payment.
To hold funds on your behalf and transmit your Sales Proceeds to the Provider in payment of your Financing Payments.
Our role. We are not a party to your Financing Agreement. We are not extending you any credit or financing in connection with these Automated Repayment Terms or collecting funds on behalf of the Provider. We are not responsible for making a complete Financing Payment or liable for any fees or charges you incur due to an Incomplete Financing Payment or non-payment of a Financing Payment. The Automated Repayment Service is a regulated money transmission service performed on your behalf solely by API pursuant to its regulatory authorizations. Additional information is available here.
