# Report alleged trademark infringements via common law


Amazon Brand Registry is a global service that helps you protect your brand’s intellectual property (IP). Users who are designated as a Rights Owner or a Registered Agent can use the Report a Violation tool to file infringement claims for their registered trademarks.

In general, you cannot submit a Report a Violation report for pending trademarks or their equivalent. However, in our United States, Canada, and South Africa stores, Brand Registry users with Rights Owner or Registered Agent roles can leverage common law rights to file infringement reports for unregistered and registered trademarks.

Common law rights are trademark rights established when you start using a trademark in commerce with particular goods and services. Common law rights are limited to the specific geographic area where you use your trademark.

To learn more about protection roles and how they are assigned, go to Brand Registry protection roles.

If you believe that your unregistered trademark is being infringed upon the United States, Canada, or South Africa, follow these steps:

Go to Report a Violation.
Search for the ASIN that is potentially infringing your intellectual property or trademark.
Select complete ASIN or click Show offers and select a specific offer from a different seller.
Click Report selected and choose Trademark infringement.
Select A product or its packaging has my trademark on it.
Choose the brand that is being infringed from the list.
For Which of this brand’s trademarks do you believe is being infringed? you must select Not one of these trademarks.
Next, click No for Is the trademark registered?
After answering No, enter the following information:
Date of the trademark’s first use in commerce. Note that the reported first use date must be prior to the date that the allegedly infringing product was offered for sale in our US, Canada, or South Africa store.
An example of the trademark as used in commerce.
Information about the goods or services for which the trademark is used.
Important: For common law submissions, you are required to complete a test buy. If you’ve purchased the item and confirmed that your trademark is on it, select Yes. If you select No, you won’t be able to submit the report.
Enter the order ID or IDs and add more information about the infringement in the text box.
When adding information about the infringement, be specific. While you can reinforce previous and new details, we strongly recommend that you include the following:
Pending trademark number, if available.
The date of the first use of your trademark in commerce.
The number of products using your legitimate trademark that were sold in the US, Canada or South Africa store.
The number of customers who are currently buying your products under your trademark.
Evidence of a large advertising campaign in one of the recognized stores or advertising material that uses your trademark, if possible.
Website or store links that show your trademark actively being used in the sale of your products.
Add your contact information and click Submit.
