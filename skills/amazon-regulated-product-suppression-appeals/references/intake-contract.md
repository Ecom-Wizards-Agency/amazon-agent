# Intake Contract

Do not draft the appeal conclusion until the case record is complete enough to identify the issue and evidence gaps.

## Required Case Fields

- Client slug and legal seller name
- Brand and marketplace
- Exact Amazon notice and notice date
- Case IDs and chronological case history
- Affected ASINs, SKUs, variation roles, and product identifiers
- Product category and product type
- Requested outcome
- Current pack status

## Required Source Inventory

- Current title, bullets, description, Item Highlights, images, A+ Content, and videos
- Current backend attributes or fresh Category Listings Report
- Packaging for every relevant variation and all physical sides
- Manual, instructions, inserts, and every QR-linked destination
- Manufacturer and supply-chain records relevant to the issue
- Test reports, certificates, registrations, clearances, exemptions, COAs, or GMP records cited by the case
- Catalog correction files and Amazon processing summaries
- Post-update frontend and backend verification evidence

## Evidence Classes

Tag every source as one of:

- `amazon_notice`
- `amazon_catalog_record`
- `amazon_processing_record`
- `seller_record`
- `manufacturer_declaration`
- `independent_test`
- `regulator_source`
- `packaging_or_label`
- `manual_or_insert`
- `linked_content`
- `other`

## Claim Register

Every material statement in the appeal must have:

- Claim ID
- Exact proposed statement
- Evidence class
- Source filename
- Page, section, field, or record locator
- Reviewer
- Verification status
- Notes or limitation

If a statement has no evidence, mark it unsupported and remove it from the appeal or leave the pack at `Evidence incomplete`.
