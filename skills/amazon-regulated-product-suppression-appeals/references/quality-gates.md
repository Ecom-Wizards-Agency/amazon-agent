# Quality Gates

## Evidence

- Every material statement points to a named source and locator.
- Manufacturer, laboratory, regulatory, and legal assertions come from the relevant source.
- Declarations use the exact legal relationship and identify the signer, authority, date, and supporting records.
- Test evidence maps the tested item to the affected finished products.
- Limitations and unresolved facts remain visible.

## Catalog

- Every affected ASIN and SKU is mapped consistently.
- The correction file contains only intended SKUs and fields.
- `Prepared`, `Uploaded`, `Processed`, and `Verified` are used as distinct states.
- A successful processing summary supports `Processed`.
- Frontend and backend evidence supports `Verified`.

## Cross-Surface Consistency

- Title, bullets, description, Item Highlights, images, A+, video, search terms, and backend attributes use the approved standard.
- Packaging, labels, manual, inserts, and QR-linked content do not contradict the listing.
- Every variation uses the same approved claim standard while preserving factual quantity differences.

## Preventative Controls

- Corrective actions are complete, specific, and already performed.
- Preventative measures name owners, review stages, stop rules, training, verification, and record retention.
- The SOP and training guide match the actual workflow the team will follow.

## Document Hygiene

- No unresolved placeholders exist in final submission documents.
- No comments, tracked changes, hidden text, custom properties, personal author metadata, unintended external relationships, or client leakage remain.
- Every DOCX and PDF renders cleanly.
- Every attachment appears in the evidence index with its purpose.

## Approval

- The pack validator reports no errors.
- Remaining warnings are summarized for Victor.
- Victor's approval is recorded before the status becomes `Approved for manual submission`.
