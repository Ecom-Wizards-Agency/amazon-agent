# Module Selection

## Core Modules

Always include:

- `01-appeal`
- `02-technical-evidence-and-claim-register`
- `09-evidence-index`
- `10-submission-checklist`

Include `04-catalog-correction-and-processing-proof` whenever seller-controlled catalog content or attributes contributed to the suppression.

Include `05-compliance-sop` and `06-training-guide` when Amazon requests preventative measures, the appeal follows a denial, or the failure exposed a repeatable process gap.

Include `07-manual-review` or `08-packaging-and-label-review` whenever those surfaces contain relevant language, instructions, labeling, or linked content.

Include `03-manufacturer-declaration` only when the manufacturer can truthfully confirm a material product, component, supply-chain, testing, or labeling fact.

## Issue Modules

### Supplements

Review Supplement Facts, ingredients, serving information, directions, warnings, structure or function claims, disease claims, COAs, GMP evidence, manufacturer records, and consistency across label and catalog. Do not invent a Supplement Facts panel or infer GMP status.

### Cosmetics And Serums

Review cosmetic intended use versus drug claims, ingredient declarations, directions, claims, packaging, serum labels, applicator language, and linked content. Do not create a Drug Facts panel unless the product is actually marketed and supported as an OTC drug.

### Devices And Application Tools

Review intended use, mechanical characteristics, clearance or exemption position, model and component mapping, test method, tested sample, manufacturer relationship, instructions, warnings, and claims. Do not infer that a technical measurement alone decides regulatory classification.

### Other Restricted Products

Build requirements from Amazon's exact notice and current first-party policy. Add only the documents needed to prove the requested facts and completed corrections.

## Routing

- Catalog correction file: `amazon-catalog` or `amazon-flatfilepro-compliance`
- FlatFilePro browser mapping: `amazon-flatfilepro-upload-mapper`
- Seller Support or Account Health wording: `amazon-communications`
- General error diagnosis: `amazon-troubleshooting`

This skill owns the cross-document evidence architecture and final pack validation.
