# Creator Reply Playbook

Starter templates for drafting Creator Connections replies. All drafts follow the SKILL.md gate: present the batch, **stop before send**, send only what the operator approves (or what a `_local/local-permissions.md` standing permission covers). Client-specific tone tweaks and approved variants live in `_local/creator-connections/local-notes.md`. Check there first and prefer the client's own wording.

## Tone rules

- Friendly, brief, and specific: creators skim. One ask or answer per message.
- Always name the actual product and campaign; never send a template with placeholders left in.
- Never promise commission changes, payment terms, or free products beyond what the campaign/operator authorized.
- No claims about the product that the listing itself doesn't make (compliance: same claim rules as listing copy).
- Match the thread's language/marketplace.

## Templates by thread type

**Sample request (creator qualifies):**
> Hi {creator first name}, thanks for your interest in {product}! We'd love to send you a sample. Could you confirm your full name and shipping address? Once we have it, we'll ship within {X} business days and drop the tracking here.

**Sample request (needs qualification):**
> Hi {creator first name}, thanks for reaching out about {product}! Could you share where you'd feature it (storefront, video, social) and a link to a recent example? We'll confirm the sample right after.

**Address received → confirm shipment (only after shipment is actually confirmed):**
> Thanks {creator first name}! Your {product} sample is on the way, sent {date}. Excited to see what you create. If anything's unclear about the product, just ask.

**Content submitted:**
> This looks great, {creator first name}. Thank you! We've logged your {content type} for {product}. {Optional: one specific positive note.} Looking forward to the results.

**Question about the product:**
> Answer factually from the live listing only. If the answer isn't on the listing, check with the operator before replying.

**Follow-up (no response after sample):**
> Hi {creator first name}, just checking in. Did the {product} sample arrive okay? Whenever your content is live, drop the link here so we can track it on our side.

**Decline (politely, when the operator decides not to proceed):**
> Hi {creator first name}, thanks so much for your interest in {product}. We're not able to move forward with this collaboration right now, but we'd be happy to keep you in mind for future campaigns.

## What never gets a drafted reply

- Threads skipped by the status filter (declined/expired/closed).
- Threads flagged for manager review (ambiguous product, multi-product, unusual requests). Those get a decision first, then a draft.
- Anything involving payment disputes, legal language, or off-Amazon deals; escalate to the operator.
